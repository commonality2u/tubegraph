## Starting Despite Discomfort

One of the biggest hurdles is the feeling of being uncomfortable or "hung up" when speaking on camera <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:16">[00:00:16]</a>. The key to [[facing_fears | overcoming this]] is simply to start <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>. Initial videos may be of poor quality, but continuous practice leads to improvement <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:23">[00:00:23]</a>.

### Find Your Method

Through consistent practice, you will develop a better sense of how to deliver content to the camera <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>. While some challenges may persist, consistent effort allows you to figure out a method that feels comfortable and yields good results <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:35">[00:00:35]</a>.

Your specific process for getting comfortable might differ from others, but the only way to discover your own effective method is by actively engaging in the practice <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:44">[00:00:44]</a>.
