This perspective aligns with a passion for [[Self Sovereignty and Financial Independence | Financial Freedom]] and [[Self Sovereignty and Financial Independence | Financial Rights]], which are considered foundational to nearly every human right <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>. This passion was a primary driver for entering the crypto space <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>. The belief is that if finances are liberated, individuals can allocate their money to causes they genuinely care about, rather than being controlled by governing bodies that own the money <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:59">[00:00:59]</a>.
