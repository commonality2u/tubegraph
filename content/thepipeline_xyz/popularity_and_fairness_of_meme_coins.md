## Reasons for [[reasons_for_investing_in_meme_coins | Investing in Meme Coins]]

Frustrated with existing market conditions, many users are withdrawing their participation from traditional crypto markets to seek out more "honest" alternatives <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:25">[00:00:25]</a>. This shift in sentiment has directly contributed to the rise of [[meme_coins | meme coins]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:31">[00:00:31]</a>.

The primary appeal of [[meme_coins | meme coins]] stems from their perceived fairness:
*   They are seen as having "no insiders" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:32">[00:00:32]</a>.
*   They offer a "very Level Playing Field" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:34">[00:00:34]</a>.

People are largely buying [[meme_coins | meme coins]] because they are "sick of getting dumped on by insiders" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:51">[00:00:51]</a>.
