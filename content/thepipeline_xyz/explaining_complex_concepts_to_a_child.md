### Explaining Monad to a Child

One approach to [[explaining_monad_to_a_child|explaining Monad to a child]] (specifically a 5-year-old) involves comparing it to a common childhood experience, such as playing games <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>:

> "You know how you can't play too many games at the same time? Monad makes that possible for people who are developing that game." <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:19">[00:00:19]</a>
