A [[hot_wallets_vs_cold_wallets | hot wallet]] is characterized by being "live" and continuously connected to the internet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>. This is a key [[differences_between_hot_and_cold_wallets | difference]] compared to a [[hot_wallets_vs_cold_wallets | cold wallet]], which stores private keys offline <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:08">[00:00:08]</a>.

## Convenience of Hot Wallets

The primary advantage of [[hot_wallets_vs_cold_wallets | hot wallets]] is their ease of use <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:58">[00:00:58]</a>. They facilitate quick transactions, making it easy to move funds on-chain and off-chain <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:05">[00:01:05]</a>. Many traders, particularly "trench traders," utilize [[hot_wallets_vs_cold_wallets | hot wallets]] due to their speed and the reduced friction compared to [[hot_wallets_vs_cold_wallets | cold wallets]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>. This convenience highlights their role in enabling fluid [[Technology and User Experience in Crypto Wallets | user experience]] for active participants in the crypto space.

## Risks of Hot Wallets

Despite their convenience, [[hot_wallets_vs_cold_wallets | hot wallets]] carry inherent [[security_concerns_and_solutions_in_blockchain_ecosystems | security]] risks because their connection to the internet makes them more vulnerable to compromise by hackers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:15">[00:01:15]</a>. Unlike [[hot_wallets_vs_cold_wallets | cold wallets]] where private keys are offline and thus untouchable by online threats <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:08">[00:00:08]</a>, [[hot_wallets_vs_cold_wallets | hot wallets]] are more susceptible to being compromised <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:15">[00:01:15]</a>.

## Best Practices

Given the higher risk of compromise, it is a [[best_practices_for_wallet_security | best practice]] to avoid keeping too much cryptocurrency in a [[hot_wallets_vs_cold_wallets | hot wallet]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:12">[00:01:12]</a>. For more secure storage, especially for larger amounts, [[hot_wallets_vs_cold_wallets | cold wallets]] are recommended as they are significantly more secure <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>.
