## Proposed Gadget: Emotional State Reminder
This [[gadget_invention_ideas | gadget invention idea]] involves a device worn on a person's head <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>. Its primary function would be to remind the wearer when they are experiencing negative emotions or anger <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:23">[00:00:23]</a>. The motivation behind this [[gadget_invention_ideas | gadget]] is the observation that sometimes anger in others is not outwardly visible, despite being present <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>.
