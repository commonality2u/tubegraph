## Overcoming Barriers to Sharing

### Humility

Many humble individuals feel reserved about "bragging" about their work <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:11">[00:00:11]</a>. However, it is necessary to [[overcoming_humility_to_promote_your_business | overcome this feeling]] and share what you are doing <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>.

### Fear of Idea Theft

A common concern is that sharing an idea publicly might lead to it being stolen <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>. However, if an idea is truly good, it's likely someone else is already working on it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:57">[00:00:57]</a>. The advantage lies with the first person to openly discuss and share their work <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:01">[00:01:01]</a>.

## Key Benefits of Public Sharing

### Building Trust

To attract any market, particularly in sectors like crypto, people need to be able to trust you <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:29">[00:00:29]</a>. Public sharing helps in establishing this trust.

### Establishing Authority

The individual who first begins discussing and sharing their ideas publicly will establish themselves as an authority or an expert on the subject <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:01">[00:01:01]</a>. The quicker you start sharing, the better <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>.

### Attracting a Market

Sharing your message and product is fundamental to attracting any type of market <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:02">[00:00:02]</a>.

## How to Start Sharing

If you're unsure where to begin, consider advice from figures like Gary Vaynerchuk:

> "If you don't know where to start just start documenting just start vlogging and documenting what you're doing because there are things that you can do with that later or just talk about what you're working on." <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[00:00:37]</a>
