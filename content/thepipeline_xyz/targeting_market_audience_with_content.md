When businesses create content, a primary consideration is determining the intended audience <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:18">[00:00:18]</a>. Content can take various forms, including written, audio, or video <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:14">[00:00:14]</a>.

A key purpose for content creation is to specifically target a business's existing market, audience, and customer base <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:22">[00:00:22]</a>. This contrasts with other goals, such as attracting talent or gaining a [[using_content_to_gain_a_competitive_advantage | competitive advantage]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>. Therefore, creators must differentiate who the content is being made for <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:20">[00:00:20]</a>.

Businesses produce content for various [[reasons_businesses_create_content | reasons]], including to [[attracting_talent_and_partners_with_content | attract people]] who might want to work for or with the company <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:08">[00:00:08]</a>. This is distinct from content aimed purely at the consumer market <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:22">[00:00:22]</a>.
