## Dynamics and Characteristics of Crypto Twitter

[[Social_media_and_community_marketing_in_crypto | Crypto Twitter]] exhibits unique dynamics that influence market perception and individual trading strategies:

### Public Trading and Authenticity
Successful traders on CT often share their positions and updates in real-time, which builds credibility because it's difficult to fake <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:53">[00:04:53]</a>. Publicly "calling stuff before it happens" is seen as a genuine display of insight, unlike faking screenshots or private messages <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:00">[00:05:00]</a>. This public visibility has led to successful calls on various assets like Solana, AVAX, Luna, DFK, and Axie <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:22">[00:05:22]</a>.

### Conviction and Bias Flipping
*   **Strong Conviction** Users often tweet with high conviction, which can be entertaining to observe <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:44">[00:07:44]</a>.
*   **Overcoming Bias** A significant challenge for traders on CT is "flipping bias"â€”changing a market stance (e.g., from bearish to bullish) when the market shifts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:26">[00:11:26]</a>. This is difficult because the general sentiment on the "timeline" often leans strongly in one direction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:30">[00:11:30]</a>.
*   **Drowning Out Noise** To navigate this, it's advised to stick to one's own process and not over-rely on or counter-trade sentiment from others, as it can be mentally taxing and lead to losses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:46">[00:11:46]</a>. Journaling trades helps maintain a clear thought process, especially when emotions are involved <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:27">[00:12:27]</a>.

### Social Signaling and Narrative Trading
[[Social_media_and_community_marketing_in_crypto | Social signaling]] plays a powerful role in asset pricing, sometimes causing projects to be valued far higher than their fundamental utility <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:16">[00:16:16]</a>.

The CEO's ability to "convince people that his coin was worth a lot" through social means demonstrates this <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:57">[00:16:57]</a>. The speculative premium on altcoins due to how they trade is a significant factor <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:35">[00:17:35]</a>.
*   **Distinguishing Value** The intertwining of price action and perceived value makes it challenging to distinguish between legitimate projects (like Solana with its [[Crypto_Ecosystem_Developments | fundamental value]]) and those purely driven by hype or social influence <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:00">[00:18:00]</a>. ### "Normie Perception" and Mass Adoption
A significant [[Challenges_and_opportunities_in_crypto_gaming_and_social_platforms | challenge for crypto]] is overcoming the "normie perception" that equates it with scams, especially for concepts like NFTs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:36:25">[00:36:25]</a>.
