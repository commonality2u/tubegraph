## Role and Perceptions of [[crypto_twitter | Crypto Twitter]]

[[crypto_twitter | Crypto Twitter]] serves as a central hub for discussions and information exchange within the cryptocurrency community <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:31">[00:08:31]</a>. It's where trends emerge, insights are shared, and market sentiments are often shaped <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:31">[00:08:31]</a>. For example, Anom's calls on Solana were widely discussed on [[crypto_twitter | Crypto Twitter]], with many looking to him for guidance on the platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:31">[00:08:31]</a>.

However, the nature of information on [[crypto_twitter | Crypto Twitter]] can be challenging. It's noted that unless one is "deep in the weeds" and understands which accounts have good reputations, it's difficult to vet people and projects, making it hard for new retail investors to get reliable information <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:28">[00:22:28]</a>. The community, while welcoming to those who know where to look, can appear intimidating to newcomers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:18">[00:23:18]</a>. ## Social Media's Impact and Personal Experiences

The broader [[the_relationship_between_crypto_and_social_media | relationship between crypto and social media]] is complex. While platforms like [[crypto_twitter | Crypto Twitter]] facilitate connection among like-minded individuals globally <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:33:30">[00:33:30]</a>, there's a growing concern about the negative effects of social media. This includes declining attention spans, consumerism, and a perceived loss of genuine human engagement and connection due to technology adding a "middleman" to interactions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:26:13">[00:26:13]</a>.

Pancak, despite being proficient in [[social_media_marketing_in_crypto | social media marketing in crypto]], expressed a bearish view on social media as a whole, looking forward to a day when he can delete all accounts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:27:58">[00:27:58]</a>. He noted the addiction many have to platforms like TikTok, leading to younger generations prioritizing fame over traditional careers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:29:32">[00:29:32]</a>.

The hosts and guests acknowledge the immersive nature of the crypto space, particularly during bull markets, which can lead individuals to spend excessive hours online <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:30:17">[00:30:17]</a>. It's stressed that maintaining real-life connections and being present away from technology is crucial, even when making significant money in the market <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:30:51">[00:30:51]</a>. For those who've achieved "generational wealth," the constant pursuit of more profits can become a self-defeating personality trait <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:31:15">[00:31:15]</a>.
