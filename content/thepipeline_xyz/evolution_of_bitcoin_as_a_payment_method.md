## Bitcoin's Original Payment Thesis

Around 2013, it was a widely held belief that [[Bitcoin and large investments | Bitcoin]] would serve as a primary method for payments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:12">[00:00:12]</a>. This "payment thesis" for Bitcoin was a common thought process at the time <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>.

## Evolution of the Payment Thesis

While [[Bitcoin and large investments | Bitcoin]] is not currently viewed in the same way regarding payments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:16">[00:00:16]</a>, the underlying thesis of crypto for payments has evolved and largely played out through the emergence of stablecoins <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:19">[00:00:19]</a>.

## Importance of Conviction and Adaptation

In the broader context of crypto, maintaining conviction and continuing to build is crucial, even when challenging events occur that might cause doubt <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:24">[00:00:24]</a>. While it's essential to [[Adapting to changing markets in crypto | adapt]] as markets change <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:36">[00:00:36]</a>, individuals should remain true to the core beliefs and thesis of why they are involved in the space <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:40">[00:00:40]</a>.
