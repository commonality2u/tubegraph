## Enhancing Credit and Lending in DeFi

### Verifiable Credit Solutions
Accountable is building a new wave of verifiable credit, aiming to prove that undercollateralized lending can be done right, unlike the situation in 2022 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:15">[00:00:15]</a>. The platform introduces a data verification system that acts as a trust layer, enabling communication between lenders and borrowers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:47">[00:03:47]</a>. This system uses API connectors, including those run in SGX for hardware guarantees and ZK TLS for increased trust <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:14">[00:04:14]</a>. Accountable's own solution, the Signed API, is envisioned as a future industry standard for sensitive data sharing <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:27">[00:04:27]</a>. This approach allows lenders to calculate risk-reward and potentially drop collateral requirements, while transparent borrowers can secure lower rates <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:46">[00:04:46]</a>. A capital formation layer is also being built to match lenders and borrowers in a privacy-preserving manner, with [[development_and_technical_innovation_in_blockchains_like_solana_and_monad | Monad]] playing a role in settlement <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:40">[00:05:40]</a>. The goal is to decentralize the traditional centralized credit desk model <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:50">[00:06:50]</a>.

### Decentralized Lending Exchanges
Amalgam is developing a decentralized lending exchange focused on "set it and forget it capital efficiency" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:05">[00:15:05]</a>. Recognizing issues with Uniswap V3's capital efficiency, Amalgam recomposes DeFi into a single protocol <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:07">[00:16:07]</a>. It integrates a lending protocol directly into a V2-style DEX, allowing dormant assets to be lent out, potentially increasing capital efficiency by 60% <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:39">[00:16:39]</a>. The protocol offers feature parity with Uniswap and Aave, enabling shorts, longs, and leveraged market making strategies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:00">[00:17:00]</a>. Its autonomous, oracle-free design supports lending on any asset, including long-tail assets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:27">[00:17:27]</a>.

## Risk Management and Hedging

### Credit Default Swaps for Pegged Assets
Cork Protocol is introducing novel DeFi primitives: Credit Default Swaps (CDS) for pegged assets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:50:25">[00:50:25]</a>. This innovation addresses the layers of unpriced and unhedged risk introduced by the expansion of pegged assets like stablecoins and LSTs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:50:41">[00:50:41]</a>. Cork creates oracle-free, fully collateralized, trustless CDS (depeg swaps) that enable risk pricing and hedging for traders, institutions, and protocols <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:52:05">[00:52:05]</a>. This allows for liquidation-free lending pools and enables users to earn yield on pegged assets without depeg risk, unlocking institutional capital <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:54:47">[00:54:47]</a>.

### Risk-Managed Credit Vaults
RISK Finance has developed risk-managed credit vaults that democratize market making <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:25:11">[03:25:11]</a>. Liquidity providers deposit capital into these vaults, earning rewards and returns from trading strategies run by market makers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:25:23">[03:25:23]</a>. Market makers can execute high-frequency, sophisticated strategies across multiple exchanges simultaneously, leveraging the liquidity provided by users without counterparty risk to their own books <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:25:44">[03:25:44]</a>. This lowers the cost of liquidity for exchanges and creates more efficient order books, overcoming limitations of traditional market maker models <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:26:40">[03:26:40]</a>. [[development_and_technical_innovation_in_blockchains_like_solana_and_monad | Monad]] is seen as an ideal platform due to its ability to support high-frequency strategies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:27:01">[03:27:01]</a>.

## Advanced Trading and Capital Efficiency

### Hybrid AMM/Order Book Exchanges
Drake Exchange is building a hybrid AMM/Order Book model that combines the strengths of both approaches <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:05:08">[01:05:08]</a>. This model, designed to run on [[development_and_technical_innovation_in_blockchains_like_solana_and_monad | Monad]] due to its 10K TPS, aims to be the first fully on-chain order book <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:06:00">[01:06:00]</a>. It includes advanced trading features like cross-collateral deposits, margin collateral swap, and customizable perpetual indexes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:06:30">[01:06:30]</a>. A tokenized funding rate vault allows users to deposit USDC and earn a delta-neutral yield by automating short positions to collect funding rate payments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:06:43">[01:06:43]</a>. The architecture features an on-demand auction liquidity layer for market maker competition, an order book layer for limit orders, and an AMM layer for backstop liquidity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:07:31">[01:07:31]</a>.

### AMM Rehypothecation
Hyperplex introduces AMM rehypothecation, a mechanism to utilize the "idle" 98% of liquidity in AMMs that sits unused at a single tick <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:36:01">[01:36:01]</a>. By wrapping existing AMM positions, Hyperplex allows users to choose how they want to rehypothecate, earning additional yield from auto-compounding or by lending liquidity to other protocols <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:36:42">[01:36:42]</a>. This approach enables earning yield from various options or derivatives platforms that borrow gamma from the liquidity, creating a composite yield for LPs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:39:18">[01:39:18]</a>. The platform aims to solve the liquidity constraint for derivatives platforms by providing instant access to deep liquidity for any token with an AMM pair <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:39:55">[01:39:55]</a>.

### Unlimited Leverage with No Liquidations
Infinity Pools offers a revolutionary approach to leverage trading with unlimited leverage, no liquidations, no counterparty risk, and no oracles <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:16:09">[01:16:09]</a>. Instead of directly borrowing cash, users borrow liquidity backed by a stablecoin like USDC at their hypothetical unwind price point <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:48:26">[01:48:26]</a>. This borrowed liquidity, functioning like a limit order, guarantees the ability to unwind positions at a specific price, even if the asset price drops to zero <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:49:30">[01:49:30]</a>. Infinity Pools aims to enable significant leverage on high-market cap assets (e.g., 5,000X on ETH/wBTC), pegged assets (e.g., 10,000X), and lower leverage on long-tail assets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:51:27">[01:51:27]</a>. Crucially, a single Infinity Pools liquidity pool can power four different markets—spot, overcollateralized borrowing, options, and leverage swaps—without fragmenting liquidity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:52:20">[01:52:20]</a>.

### Turbocharging On-chain Activity
Sauce aims to turbocharge on-chain activity by creating a CEX-like experience for advanced traders <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:39:31">[0:39:31]</a>. The platform focuses on building a 10x user experience with one-click cross-chain transactions into any asset <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:50:52">[03:50:52]</a>. Idle funds can automatically go into yield-earning protocols <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:11:09">[04:11:09]</a>. A core innovation is transaction automation within trusted execution environments (TEEs), enabling private DCAs and off-chain limit orders, which address concerns about public transaction visibility <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:40:38">[03:40:38]</a>. This infrastructure is designed to be more powerful and efficient than existing account abstraction solutions, offering advanced trading types that can be written in code and secured within the TEE <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:41:35">[03:41:35]</a>. Sauce also aims to simplify onboarding to new ecosystems like [[development_and_technical_innovation_in_blockchains_like_solana_and_monad | Monad]] with one click from an existing wallet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:42:44">[03:42:44]</a>.

### Agnostic Execution Network
Symphony is building the first agnostic execution network to make DeFi usable, scalable, and accessible <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:52:16">[03:52:16]</a>. It addresses friction points like complex gas management, multiple wallets, and fragmented liquidity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:52:30">[03:52:30]</a>. Symphony enables users to transact across all chains, unifying liquidity and performing cross-chain trades in seconds, eliminating the need for bridges or gas management <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:55:17">[03:55:17]</a>. The platform uses a network of permissionless solvers to find the best liquidity and fees across different perpetual exchanges and chains, sharding large trades across multiple protocols <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:56:12">[03:56:12]</a>. Trades are opened under the user's smart account (ERC-4337 based wallet), ensuring full custody <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:56:49">[03:56:49]</a>. Symphony aims to bring billions of liquidity to chains like [[development_and_technical_innovation_in_blockchains_like_solana_and_monad | Monad]] on day one by allowing instant access to perpetual exchanges across other ecosystems <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:59:01">[03:59:01]</a>.
