## Explaining Monad to a Child

One way to [[explaining_monad_to_a_child | explain Monad]] is to compare it to a car upgrade <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>.
> "Monad is like if you go from a normal car to a Tesla because it's really fast and also it's really efficient" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>.

This analogy highlights key features like speed and efficiency in terms a child can easily grasp <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:47">[00:00:47]</a>.
