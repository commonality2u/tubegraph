## Overcoming Obstacles to Sharing

Many individuals, particularly those who are more humble, may feel reserved about promoting their work <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:11">[00:00:11]</a>. However, this is a sentiment that must be overcome <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>, highlighting the need for [[overcoming_humility_to_promote_your_business | overcoming humility to promote your business]] effectively.

Another common concern is the fear of having ideas stolen if shared publicly <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:51">[00:00:51]</a>. However, if an idea is truly good, it is likely that someone else is already working on something similar <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:57">[00:00:57]</a>.

## Benefits of Sharing Your Message

Sharing what you are doing offers several key [[benefits_of_sharing_your_ideas_publicly | benefits]]:

*   **Building Trust** To attract any market, people need to be able to trust you, especially in sectors like crypto <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:29">[00:00:29]</a>.
*   **Establishing Authority** The first person to talk about and share their ideas publicly often establishes themselves as an authority or the expert on the subject <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:01">[00:01:01]</a>. The sooner you start communicating, the better <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>.

## How to Begin Sharing

A simple way to start is by documenting your work <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>. As Gary Vaynerchuk suggests, if you're unsure where to begin, start vlogging and documenting what you're doing, or simply talk about what you're working on <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[00:00:37]</a>. This approach can be a [[creative_content_ideas_for_business_promotion | creative content idea]] and provides material that can be utilized later <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:45">[00:00:45]</a>.
