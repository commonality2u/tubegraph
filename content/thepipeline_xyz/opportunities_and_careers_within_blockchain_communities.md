Key advice for projects is to hire individuals who are "crypto native" and, specifically, "Monad native" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:32">[00:11:32]</a>. This is because community members already possess a deep understanding of the project's lore, history, and culture, which provides significant value to any project <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:45">[00:06:45]</a>. The most successful teams have actively embraced the community's culture and hired its members <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:42">[00:11:42]</a>.

It is considered "beautiful" that one can join a crypto community and secure "legitimate job opportunities" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:02">[00:12:02]</a>. Staying active and engaged in the community is described as one of the easiest ways to "maximize your luck surface area" and increase chances of success <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:10">[00:13:10]</a>. This proactive approach allows members to contribute in unique ways across various platforms like Discord, Telegram, and Twitter, fostering broad appeal and potential for professional growth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:30:16">[00:30:16]</a>.

Projects benefit from having a deeply engaged ecosystem with strong relationships between members and projects <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:50">[00:10:50]</a>. The community's history, including the evolution of its Discord and Telegram chats (from invite-only to public, and the implementation of "purges" to maintain engagement), highlights the dynamic nature of [[building_community_within_blockchain_ecosystems|community building and engagement in crypto spaces]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:21:48">[00:21:48]</a>. This continuous adaptation and focus on high-quality interaction contribute to a self-sustaining pool of talent.

## General Advice for Success

*   **Be Nice and Help Others**: Actively helping people without expecting an immediate reward often leads to future recognition and rewards <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:38:24">[00:38:24]</a>.
*   **Don't Be Afraid to Be "Cringe"**: Taking risks and expressing ideas, even if they seem unconventional, can lead to the most impactful outcomes, particularly in content creation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:44:17">[00:44:17]</a>.
*   **Value Your Team and Connections**: The quality of the team you work with significantly impacts enjoyment and success <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:42:01">[00:42:01]</a>. Finding smart people with good intentions to collaborate with is invaluable <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:42:40">[00:42:40]</a>. This aligns with the idea that "if you want to go fast go alone, if you want to go far go together" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:42:32">[00:42:32]</a>.
*   **Focus and Dial In**: In a noisy and rapidly evolving space, it's crucial to find your niche, stick to your expertise, and avoid chasing every new trend <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:43:16">[00:43:16]</a>. Find your people and your "thing" and focus on it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:43:39">[00:43:39]</a>.
