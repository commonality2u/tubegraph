## Key Concerns for Users

*   **Lack of Early Access**: Even when legitimate opportunities arise, average users often lack the chance to participate early <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:15">[00:00:15]</a>.
*   **Growing Resentment**: This dynamic, where new coins are created in a perceived unfair manner, generates significant resentment among crypto users <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:18">[00:00:18]</a>. This frustration leads some users to disengage from this type of market <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>.
