Valery Solovey recorded a special edition of his program on April 12, focusing on the escalating situation in Russia and offering advice for personal survival. He warns that the situation is developing "more alarmingly, faster, fleeting, and worse" than previously expected <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:40">[00:01:40]</a>. This includes the potential imposition of martial law on a general national scale, rendering existing legal protections meaningless and allowing authorities to "do whatever they want" with citizens' lives, property, and savings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:59">[00:01:59]</a>. He describes the beginning of "military serfdom" as a "monstrous social regression" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:50">[00:02:50]</a>.

The coming year is projected to be one of "severe, constant, permanent stress," with life, property, and savings under "not just constant and also a growing threat" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:00">[00:06:00]</a>.

## Key Priorities for Survival

In this challenging environment, Solovey outlines two main strategic priorities:
1.  **Preserving your own life and health**, along with the life and health of family and friends <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:02">[00:10:02]</a>.
2.  **Preserving property, modest savings, and business** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:10">[00:10:10]</a>.

He emphasizes that escaping "without losses" is impossible for individuals or families <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:31">[00:10:31]</a>, and advises accepting that "losses are inevitable" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:52">[00:10:52]</a>. The focus must be on these two main priorities, with everything else, such as apartment repairs or vacation plans, becoming secondary and often needing to be ignored due to limited time and energy <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:17">[00:11:17]</a>.

## Emotional Resilience

The prolonged and intensifying stress of the situation means that it will be "impossible or extremely difficult to survive alone" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:10">[00:07:10]</a>. People need support, a means to share fears and concerns, and the ability to cry in the literal sense to reduce personal stress <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:28">[00:07:28]</a>.

### Building Support Networks
*   **Family and Friends:** A "very important role" in building emotional balance and mental stability belongs to women, as men may more easily fall into prostration or panic <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:51">[00:07:51]</a>.
*   **Shared Burden:** Stress needs to be shared, as "together this knife will be easier if we carry it" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:54">[00:08:54]</a>.
*   **Core Needs:** A "safe haven," "reliable friends or an extensive family that can come to the rescue," and a "spare person" are the desired minimum <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:42">[00:19:42]</a>.

### Stress Relief Techniques
Solovey suggests traditional methods for stress relief:
*   **Alcohol:** Only "very moderately" to avoid intensifying depression <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:11">[00:09:11]</a>.
*   **Sedatives:** Recommended for acute reactions to reduce severity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:17">[00:09:17]</a>.
*   **Physical Activity:** Sex, sports, and walks in the fresh air <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:26">[00:09:26]</a>.
*   **Companionship:** Pets, "including children" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:33">[00:09:33]</a>.
*   **Calmness:** The primary advice is "calmness, the main thing calmness," or at least the "absence of hysteria and panic" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:28">[00:19:28]</a>.

## Financial and Legal Resilience

Solovey advises careful thought and rational, consistent action that benefits oneself and loved ones, rather than fighting or running instinctively <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:31">[00:08:31]</a>.

### Navigating Mobilization and Property Risks
*   **Avoid Risks:** It's important to "avoid those places and situations where and when the risks for you increase," particularly regarding "getting in for the good" (detention) by law enforcement <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:35">[00:13:35]</a>.
*   **Mobilization Evasion:** While no one has been criminally or administratively prosecuted for evading previous mobilization, future risks are assessed as minimal, but "they can demonstratively punish someone" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:12">[00:13:12]</a>.
*   **Summons and Databases:** Do not be fooled by the delayed "single register of military personnel" for summonses; existing databases are integrated enough to "comb the public" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:56">[00:13:56]</a>. The claim of a 20-day grace period after a summons is a "lie" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:28">[00:14:28]</a>.
*   **Immediate Consequences:**
    *   Accounts and bank cards will be blocked automatically, even if not explicitly stated in law <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:44">[00:14:44]</a>. This is seen as the "most effective" measure in documents reaching the president's desk <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:52">[00:14:52]</a>.     *   Mobilized individuals will be sent directly to assembly points and then to the fields, and their families will be expected to supply equipment like bulletproof vests, shifting provision to "self-sufficiency" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:35">[00:15:35]</a>. ### Property Protection Measures
*   **Cash Reserves:** Always have a sufficient amount of cash for operational freedom <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:33">[00:16:33]</a>.
*   **Bank Accounts:** Money can be withdrawn or transferred to trusted relatives and friends <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:26">[00:16:26]</a>.
*   **Vehicles:** A general power of attorney for a car is useless; the car will be impounded <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:02">[00:17:02]</a>.
*   **Business:** Transferring a business is difficult and sometimes impossible; losses are inevitable <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:24">[00:17:24]</a>. It is "better to lose a little money than a precious life" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:45">[00:17:45]</a>.
*   **Legal Counsel:** For business and property protection, seeking recommendations from lawyers to develop a quick implementation scheme is highly recommended. Always have a lawyer's phone number <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:56">[00:17:56]</a>.
