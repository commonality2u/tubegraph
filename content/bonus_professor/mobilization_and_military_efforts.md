## Future of the Military

Looking ahead, the experience of the Russian-Ukrainian confrontation and the current state of the Russian armed forces strongly suggest a future transition to a fully contract-based, professional army <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:41">[00:18:41]</a>. This indicates a potential shift from conscription-based models to a force composed entirely of professional soldiers.
