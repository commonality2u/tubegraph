This influence extends from historical involvement to current day-to-day governance and future political transitions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="14:25:00">[14:25:00]</a>.

## Current Influence and Key Figures
Nikolai Platonovich Patrushev, Secretary of the Security Council of Russia, is highlighted as a key figure in the [[russian_political_dynamics_and_power_consolidation | consolidation of power]] on external tracks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:40:00">[01:40:00]</a>. Despite a purported "bad irritation and disbelief" towards Belarusian President Lukashenka, Patrushev is not currently prepared to quarrel with remaining allies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:17:15">[01:17:15]</a>. Patrushev and those behind him are anticipated to play a role in the official announcement regarding the end of the "president" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="15:24:00">[15:24:00]</a>.

Andrei Belousov, a high-ranking government official, is also noted for actively assisting "the junta" in preparing illegitimate actions and has "always gravitated" towards the security forces and a tough economic course <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="29:12:00">[29:12:00]</a>.

## Historical Context and Methodology
The perspective that the KGB seized power and initiated the collapse of the USSR, and is now destroying Russia, is dismissed as an "obviously incorrect methodology" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="13:52:00">[13:52:00]</a>. It is argued that the KGB was not in power at the time of the USSR's collapse and "suffered a lot" from it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="13:59:00">[13:59:00]</a>. However, the current role of the FSB and security agencies is described as holding "political authority" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="14:15:00">[14:15:00]</a>.

It is asserted that no other country in the world trusts intelligence and counterintelligence to govern, with Russia being the sole exception <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="14:27:00">[14:27:00]</a>. This situation is attributed not to "evil intent" but to "built-in defects" in their thinking <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="14:37:00">[14:37:00]</a>.

## Future and Reform
In the context of a "radical change of power" in [[political_situation_in_russia | Russia]], a comprehensive cleaning of the "crooked security agencies" is deemed necessary <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:23:00">[10:23:00]</a>. This process is projected to take approximately two years based on "realistic plans and estimates" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:29:00">[10:29:00]</a>.
