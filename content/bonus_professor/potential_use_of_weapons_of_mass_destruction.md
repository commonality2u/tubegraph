## Potential Escalation Mechanisms

Several triggers could lead to the [[potential_use_of_nuclear_weapons | use of WMD]] or broader escalation:
1.  **Interruption of Western Arms Supplies** There is a temptation for Russia to interrupt Western logistical chains of arms supplies to Ukraine, possibly using [[potential_use_of_nuclear_weapons | tactical nuclear weapons]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:05">[00:07:05]</a>. The strike could occur on Ukrainian territory or a neighboring country, and this option is under [[discussion_of_nuclear_weapons_usage | discussion]] in the West <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:21">[00:07:21]</a>.
2.  **Strikes on Russian Territory** The Ukrainian side, or opposing forces, might strike military and civilian targets in Russian regions adjacent to Ukraine <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:44">[00:07:44]</a>. Ukraine is expected to receive missiles capable of reaching Russian military airfields <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:08">[00:08:08]</a>.
3.  **Terrorist Attacks** Terrorist attacks in Moscow and other large Russian cities could serve as another trigger <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:20">[00:08:20]</a>. Hints about such possibilities are observed in official Russian media <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:33">[00:08:33]</a>.
