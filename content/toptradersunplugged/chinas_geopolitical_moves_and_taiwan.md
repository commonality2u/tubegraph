## Taiwan's Strategic Importance and US Influence
Taiwan, a critical player in semiconductor technology, has seen significant investment in the United States. The president of TSMC, a major chipmaker, recently signed an agreement for an additional $100 billion investment in the US, supplementing $65 billion already invested <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="43:00">[43:00]</a>. This move is perceived by some as the US compelling or inviting Taiwan to produce chips domestically, which could potentially alter the US's commitment to defending Taiwan should a conflict arise <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="43:22">[43:22]</a>. This highlights [[chinas_role_and_vulnerability_in_semiconductor_technology | China's role and vulnerability in semiconductor technology]] and the intricate [[uschina_relations_and_geopolitical_dynamics | US-China relations and geopolitical dynamics]].
