## Unique Benefits
The specific correlation profile offered by [[trend_following_investment_strategies | trend following]] is considered unique <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:48">[00:00:48]</a>. It can not only enhance the returns of traditional investment portfolios but also simultaneously reduce portfolio risk <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>.

## Nature of Trends
Trends do not move in a straight line <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:56">[00:05:56]</a>. They can look very different from each other, requiring investors to give them "a bit of space to move around" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:00">[00:06:00]</a>. In the [[trend_following_investment_strategies | trend following]] world, trends are understood not to last forever; they may persist for a few months, take a pause, and then resume <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:59:33">[00:59:33]</a>.
