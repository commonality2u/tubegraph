## Tax Shelters Cost More Than They Save
Tax shelters, while seemingly saving money, often incur greater costs in other resources <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:11">[00:02:11]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:17">[00:02:17]</a>. ### Cost of Discovery and Conversion

### Cost of Upkeep
Even with a legitimate structure, ongoing attention and resources are needed for upkeep <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:05">[00:04:05]</a>. This often means trading valuable attention for less valuable monetary savings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:07">[00:04:07]</a>.

The speaker questioned if that time could have been used to make 30% more money <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:32">[00:04:32]</a>.
*   **Example 2: Missed Life Events**: Another friend missed their baby's birth to meet residency requirements for tax purposes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:03">[00:05:03]</a>.
*   **Example 3: Captive Insurance**: A relative engaged in a captive insurance arrangement, which required constant allocation of attention and funds to remain compliant, adding no value to the business <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:17">[00:05:17]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:32">[00:05:32]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:47">[00:05:47]</a>.
