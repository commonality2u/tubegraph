However, about 5% are described as "hair-raising" and involve observations made by multiple witnesses using different technologies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:02">[00:01:02]</a>. Despite these intriguing accounts, the "gold standard" in the search for [[existence_of_intelligent_life_forms | intelligent life]] does not consist merely of observing flying saucers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:14">[00:01:14]</a>.

The gold standard—repeatable, multiple evidence using various methods that confirms contact with a technologically advanced civilization—has not been achieved <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:24">[00:02:24]</a>.

## Theoretical Concepts of Advanced Civilization Travel

While tangible evidence of [[alien technology]] is missing, theoretical physics explores concepts that could enable interstellar travel for advanced civilizations.

### Faster-Than-Light Travel: Warp Drives and Wormholes

Hypersonic ships observed traveling faster than light are currently not believed to be possible with our understanding <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:40">[00:02:40]</a>. However, "warp drive" may be possible, distinguishing two types:
*   **Alcubierre Drive:** This method involves squeezing space-time to create a shortcut, allowing movement faster than light without violating Einstein's theory of relativity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:04">[00:03:04]</a>. Miguel Alcubierre discovered a solution to Einstein's equations that permits this <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:39">[00:03:39]</a>.
*   **Wormhole:** This involves cutting a hole in space-time to create a direct shortcut between two points <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:21">[00:03:21]</a>.

The primary obstacle to building these drives is the immense energy required, specifically Planck energy <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:17">[00:04:17]</a>. Human accelerators can only reach trillions of electron volts, whereas Planck energy is 10^19 billion electron volts (one with 19 zeros) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:27">[00:04:27]</a>.

### Planck Energy and Space-Time Manipulation

Planck energy is described as the largest energy in the universe, where the universe itself loses stability <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:02">[00:13:02]</a>. When space is heated to unimaginable temperatures exceeding Planck energy, it becomes unstable, leading to the formation of "space-time foam"—bubbles within space-time, most of which appear and disappear <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:16">[00:13:16]</a>. This concept, formulated by Stephen Hawking, suggests that worlds could begin to form after exceeding Planck energy, potentially even being the environment of a Type 3 civilization using it as a gateway for space travel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:42">[00:13:42]</a>. The Big Bang itself could have been a bubble that reached Planck energy and expanded <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:02">[00:14:02]</a>.

## Civilization Types and Their Capabilities

Physicists categorize civilizations based on their energy mastery, offering a framework for discussing [[life_beyond_earth_and_the_search_for_extraterrestrial_life | extraterrestrial life]] and potential technological capabilities:

*   **Type 0 Civilization:** Humanity is currently a Type 0 civilization, with no defined place on the scale <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:09">[00:05:09]</a>. Our technological level is approximated at 0.7 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:22">[00:05:22]</a>.
*   **Type 1 Civilization:** This civilization has mastered all the energy of its home planet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:14">[00:11:14]</a>. It controls everything on the planet, including utilizing all light from its sun, controlling earthquakes and volcanoes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:20">[00:11:20]</a>. A Type 1 civilization can traverse its solar system <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:25">[00:05:25]</a> and might colonize several planets in its region, but flying saucers are unlikely to come from them due to the limitations of approaching light speed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:30">[00:12:30]</a>.
*   **Type 2 Civilization:** This civilization uses the power of both its planet and its sun, harnessing all the sun's energy to power its machines <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:31">[00:11:31]</a>. The Federation of Planets in *Star Trek* is an example of a Type 2 civilization <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:52">[00:11:52]</a>. While understanding stars, Type 2 civilizations still struggle to approach the speed of light needed for universal travel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:44">[00:12:44]</a>.
*   **Type 3 Civilization:** This is a galactic civilization <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:59">[00:11:59]</a>. Such a civilization travels along galactic routes, can manipulate black holes (like in *Star Wars*) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:02">[00:12:02]</a>, and may have access to Planck energy <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:47">[00:04:47]</a>. Calculations suggest they could have the energy to bend space-time and potentially even build a time machine <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:52">[00:04:52]</a>. A Type 3 civilization would be approximately 100,000 years more advanced than Earth, developing exponentially to reach Planck energy within that timeframe <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:33">[00:08:33]</a>.

## Why Advanced Civilizations Might Not Visit or Interact

If [[existence_of_intelligent_life_forms | aliens]] exist, the question arises as to why they might not visit us <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:50">[00:08:50]</a>. It is suggested that a Type 1 or even Type 2 civilization would have difficulty reaching Earth; only a Type 3 civilization would likely be capable of such a feat <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:20">[00:08:20]</a>.

### The "Deer Analogy"

The speaker posits that if we met intelligent beings from outer space, they might consider humanity boring <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:37">[00:09:37]</a>. Drawing an analogy to humans trying to talk to a deer, the deer might initially seem interested but then get bored because the conversation is one-sided and offers nothing <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:14">[00:09:14]</a>. Similarly, humanity may have nothing of value to offer an advanced civilization; gold and silver would likely be useless to them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:57">[00:09:57]</a>. Such beings might study us out of curiosity, analyze us, and then eventually get bored due to a lack of "profit" or meaningful interaction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:14">[00:10:14]</a>. Just as humans don't spend vast amounts of time interacting with animals to learn their secrets, advanced aliens might not heavily invest in studying humanity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:32">[00:10:32]</a>. This perspective aligns with [[cultural_and_psychological_factors_in_belief_in_aliens | cultural and psychological factors in belief in aliens]], as it attempts to explain the Great Filter or Fermi Paradox.

In summary, while the theoretical possibility of advanced [[alien technology]] and even time travel exists for highly advanced civilizations, the current [[exploration_and_research_of_extraterrestrial_life_and_ufos | exploration and research of extraterrestrial life and UFOs]] yields no hard, tangible, or repeatable scientific evidence of alien visitation or technology on Earth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:04">[00:07:04]</a>.
