The Samsung Galaxy S10 series, released in 2019, includes the [[samsung_galaxy_s10_models_overview | Samsung Galaxy S10]], S10+, and S10e, offering notable improvements in battery and storage capabilities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:18">[00:00:18]</a>.

## Battery Enhancements
The batteries in the Galaxy S10 models are "significantly bigger" compared to previous generations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:04">[00:05:04]</a>.
*   The [[samsung_galaxy_s10_models_overview | Galaxy S10]] features a 3,400 mAh battery <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:07">[00:05:07]</a>.
*   The [[samsung_galaxy_s10_models_overview | Galaxy S10 Plus]] is equipped with a 4,100 mAh battery <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:09">[00:05:09]</a>.

### Charging Capabilities
The phones support various charging methods:
*   Fast charging <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:44">[00:07:44]</a>
*   Fast wireless charging <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:46">[00:07:46]</a>

### Wireless Power Share
A new feature, "Wireless Power Share" (also known as reverse wireless charging), is included <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:59">[00:07:59]</a>. This allows the [[samsung_galaxy_s10_models_overview | Galaxy S10]] to wirelessly charge other devices at 4.5 watts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:12">[00:08:12]</a>. While considered "gimmicky" for emergency phone charging, it is practical for charging accessories like the new Galaxy Buds, which feature a Qi wireless charging case <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:24">[00:08:24]</a>.

## Storage and RAM Configurations
All models feature a Snapdragon 855 processor <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:16">[00:05:16]</a>.

### Galaxy S10
*   Starts at $900 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:22">[00:05:22]</a>.
*   Base configuration: 8GB of RAM and 128GB of storage <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:26">[00:05:26]</a>.
*   Available in an 8GB RAM / 512GB storage version <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:28">[00:05:28]</a>.

### Galaxy S10 Plus
*   Starts at $1,000 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:34">[00:05:34]</a>.
*   Base configuration: 8GB of RAM and 128GB of storage <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:36">[00:05:36]</a>.
*   Higher-end options include an 8GB RAM / 512GB storage version <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:43">[00:05:43]</a>.
*   A premium model features a ceramic back (instead of glass) and offers a "ridiculous" 1TB storage option with 12GB of RAM <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:40">[00:05:40]</a>.

### Expandable Storage
All [[samsung_galaxy_s10_models_overview | Samsung Galaxy S10]] phones continue the tradition of offering expandable storage via microSD <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:02">[00:06:02]</a>. This allows for a total memory of up to 1.5 terabytes (1TB internal + 0.5TB microSD card) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:06">[00:06:06]</a>.
