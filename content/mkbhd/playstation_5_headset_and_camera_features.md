The [[playstation_5_accessories_overview | PlayStation 5]] comes with a suite of accessories, including a dedicated headset and an HD camera, designed to integrate seamlessly with the console's aesthetic and features <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:55">[00:08:55]</a>.

## Pulse 3D Wireless Headset

The new Pulse 3D Wireless Headset features a distinct, minimal design, sporting a white and black color scheme that matches the console's aesthetic <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:02">[00:09:02]</a>.

### Key Features
*   **3D Audio:** Supports 3D audio in compatible games <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:08">[00:09:08]</a>.
*   **Microphones:** Includes dual microphones for improved audio quality and noise cancellation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:10">[00:09:10]</a>.
*   **Battery Life:** Offers an estimated 12-hour battery life <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:16">[00:09:16]</a>.
*   **Charging:** Features USB-C charging <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:34">[00:09:34]</a>.
*   **Connectivity:** Can connect wirelessly to the console or other devices via a USB dongle <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:40">[00:09:40]</a>. It also has a 3.5mm headphone jack for wired connection to the controller <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:36">[00:09:36]</a>.

### Design and Comfort
The headphones are described as "crazy" and "pretty massive" yet "very unique looking" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:21">[00:09:21]</a>. They are constructed almost entirely from plastic <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:44">[00:09:44]</a> and are lightweight <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:01">[00:10:01]</a>.
*   **Headband:** Features a unique dual-band design with a sliding rubber band at the bottom for an adaptable fit <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:49">[00:09:49]</a>.
*   **Ear Cushions:** The ear cushions are thick, soft, and comfortable <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:57">[00:09:57]</a>.
*   **Controls:** All controls, including USB-C charging, microphone mute, sliding power, and an LED indicator, are conveniently located on the left ear cup <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:05">[00:10:05]</a>.

## HD Camera

The HD Camera is expected to be the least purchased first-party accessory <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:37">[00:10:37]</a>. However, it is likely the preferred camera for streamers due to its seamless integration with the [[PlayStation 5 unboxing and design | PlayStation 5 system]] and its matching aesthetic <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:40">[00:10:40]</a>. This accessory can also be related to [[camera_performance_and_features | general camera features]] or [[camera_technology_in_sony_phones | Sony camera technology]].

### Specifications and Design
*   **Resolution:** It is a 1080p camera <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:48">[00:10:48]</a>.
*   **Stand:** Features a built-in white stand that allows users to adjust the camera's angle, enabling it to point up when placed below a TV or down when placed above <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:50">[00:10:50]</a>.
