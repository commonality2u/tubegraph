## Specifications and Features

*   **Processor**: Snapdragon 670 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:25">[00:03:25]</a>
*   **Memory**: Up to 6 GB RAM <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:27">[00:03:27]</a>
*   **Storage**: 128 GB <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:29">[00:03:29]</a>
*   **Operating System**: Android 9.0, customized into a UI called "Infinite OS" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:31">[00:03:31]</a>
*   **Sensors**: Includes cameras and sensors at the front and top, plus a gyroscope to track orientation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:42">[00:03:42]</a>. The AI engine leverages the Snapdragon 670 chip <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:07">[00:06:07]</a>.
*   **Projection**: Can project onto a wall or downward onto a flat surface like a desk or table <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:50">[00:03:50]</a>. It automatically calibrates and sharpens the image <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:01">[00:04:01]</a>.
*   **Touch Screen Projection**: Features a 10-point multi-touch element, enabling a giant [[features_of_highend_android_tablets | tablet]]-like experience on any flat surface <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:12">[00:04:12]</a>. The responsiveness is impressive <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:21">[00:04:21]</a>. A limitation is the need to avoid casting a shadow on the area one wishes to touch <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:31">[00:04:31]</a>.
*   **Portability**: Equipped with a built-in battery, making it portable for use without a direct power source <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:01">[00:05:01]</a>. It can turn any surface into a large gaming controller <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:10">[00:05:10]</a>.
*   **Price**: $999 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:14">[00:06:14]</a>.
