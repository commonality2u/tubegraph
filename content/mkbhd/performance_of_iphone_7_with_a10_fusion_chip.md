## A10 Fusion Chip and RAM

The iPhone 7 and 7 Plus are equipped with the new A10 Fusion chip <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:55">[00:03:55]</a>. The iPhone 7 has 2 gigabytes of RAM, while the iPhone 7 Plus comes with 3 gigabytes of RAM <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:00">[00:04:00]</a>.

## Benchmarking Performance

Running a quick test with Geekbench 4 confirmed the capabilities of the A10 Fusion chip <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:55">[00:03:55]</a>. The final score was noted as "pretty great," especially the multi-core score <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:04">[00:04:04]</a>. The multi-core performance of the iPhone 7's A10 Fusion chip was observed to be above that of Snapdragon 820 devices <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:09">[00:04:09]</a>.

Overall, devices running on the new chip are "a little bit faster" and "a little bit smoother" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:49">[00:03:49]</a>.
