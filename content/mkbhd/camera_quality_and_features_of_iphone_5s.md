## Overall Camera Experience
The [[iphone_camera_capabilities_and_features | iPhone camera]] is described as a "very likable camera" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:07:07">[10:07:07]</a>. On a scale of one to ten, its quality is rated around an eight, and its ease of use (control) is also an eight <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:58:08">[09:58:08]</a><a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:02:08">[10:02:08]</a>. Users can quickly open the camera by swiping up from a shortcut in the corner and immediately begin taking pictures <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:09:09">[10:09:09]</a>.

## Speed and Performance
The [[iphone_5s_design_and_build_quality | iPhone 5s]] camera is characterized as very fast <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:14:04">[10:14:04]</a>:
*   **Photo Capture & Processing** It's fast to take photos and process shots <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:21:04">[10:21:04]</a>.
*   **HDR** Taking HDR photos is quick, meaning a still subject isn't always necessary <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:24:02">[10:24:02]</a>.
*   **Aperture** The aperture is wide open, believed to be F 2.0 or 2.2 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:30:08">[10:30:08]</a>. While this can make images slightly softer than the [[performance_and_hardware_specifications_of_iphone_5s | iPhone 5]], it's beneficial for low-light conditions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:35:05">[10:35:05]</a><a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:39:07">[10:39:07]</a>.
*   **Video Modes** It can record video at 120 frames per second, which can then be slowed down to 30 frames per second for a smooth slow-motion effect <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:41:09">[10:41:09]</a>.

## Image Quality
Photos taken with the [[iphone_5s_design_and_build_quality | iPhone 5s]] are generally solid, with accurate and extremely colorful rendering <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="11:11:09">[11:11:09]</a>. Detail is largely preserved, though it might be slightly softer than the [[performance_and_hardware_specifications_of_iphone_5s | iPhone 5]] due to the wider aperture <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="11:16:04">[11:16:04]</a><a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="11:18:03">[11:18:03]</a>.

### Low-Light Capabilities
A highlight of the [[iphone_x_camera_quality_and_features | iPhone 5s]] camera is its low-light performance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:55:04">[10:55:04]</a>. It "can essentially see in the dark," an area where many other camera phones fall short <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:57:08">[10:57:08]</a>.

Overall, in terms of photo and video quality and user control, the [[iphone_6_camera_performance_and_features | iPhone 5s]] is considered among the best smartphones for photography <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="11:26:07">[11:26:07]</a>.
