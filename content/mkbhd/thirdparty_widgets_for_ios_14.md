With the public release of [[widgets_on_ios_14 | iOS 14]], users can now customize their iPhone home screens, a feature that feels new to Apple users despite being available on other platforms for years <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:09">[00:00:09]</a>. This has led to the emergence of a dedicated ecosystem of third-party widget applications <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:50:44">[04:50:44]</a>.

## Benefits and Features
Developers are actively creating beautiful, functional, and "glanceable" widgets for [[widgets_on_ios_14 | iOS 14]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:34:44">[03:34:44]</a>. These apps have their own user interfaces for customizing widgets to display information at a glance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:00:44">[05:00:44]</a>.

Information that can be displayed includes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:05:44">[05:05:44]</a>:
*   Health information (e.g., from Apple Watch)
*   News tickers
*   Stocks
*   Weather
*   Date and time
*   Battery status
*   Calendar events

Users can extensively customize the appearance of these widgets, including colors and gradients <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:17:44">[05:17:44]</a>.

## Notable Third-Party Widget Apps
*   **Timepage**: Offers a minimal and aesthetically pleasing weather widget <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:51:44">[03:51:44]</a>. While Timepage is primarily a productivity app, its weather widget is a popular choice <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:55:44">[03:55:44]</a>.
*   **Hayweather widget**: Provides various sizes and customization options for weather information <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:06:44">[04:06:44]</a>.
*   **Google widget**: A useful widget offering a quick search bar, voice search, incognito text search, and Google Lens functionality for image-based searches <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:10:44">[04:10:44]</a>.
*   **Customization Apps**:
    *   Color Widgets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:31:44">[04:31:44]</a>
    *   Widgery <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:31:44">[04:31:44]</a>
    *   Widgetsmith <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:31:44">[04:31:44]</a>

## Pricing Considerations
Many third-party widget apps offer basic customization features for free <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:36:44">[05:36:44]</a>. However, advanced options like changing specific colors or adding certain functions often require a paid upgrade, which can be a one-time charge or a subscription <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:40:44">[05:40:44]</a>.
