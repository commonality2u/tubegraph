## Capabilities and [[use_cases_for_ai_in_business_automation | Use Cases for AI in Business Automation]]

AI agents can automate various business processes, allowing users to initiate complex tasks with simple prompts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:32">[00:00:32]</a>. Key capabilities demonstrated include:

### Retail Arbitrage
An [[benefits_of_ai_agents_in_business_operations | AI agent]] can initiate a business by:
- Messaging individuals on platforms like Facebook Marketplace <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:15">[00:00:15]</a>.
- Logging replies in a spreadsheet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>.
- Identifying grossly undervalued items (e.g., pizza ovens) by comparing prices across different marketplaces (e.g., Facebook Marketplace and eBay) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:19">[00:01:19]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>.
- Logging details of promising finds, including titles, locations, conditions, and links, into a provided spreadsheet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:04">[00:02:04]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:38">[00:05:38]</a>.
This process can lead to significant profit, with the potential to build a six or seven-figure business through arbitrage <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:07">[00:03:07]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:16">[00:03:16]</a>. The ultimate vision for this [[deploying_ai_agents_for_business_automation | AI-powered automation]] involves the agent performing price research, making offers, listing items with collected photos, handling payments, and even coordinating direct shipping from the original seller to the end buyer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:08">[00:10:08]</a>.

### Research and Information Gathering
[[building_and_using_aipowered_tools_for_business_processes | AI tools]] can conduct research on specific topics or criteria <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:35">[00:00:35]</a>. For instance, an AI agent can:
- Search for caterers in a specific area (e.g., Dallas) on specialized websites (e.g., TheKnot.com) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:01">[00:13:01]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:04">[00:13:04]</a>.
- Fill out contact forms with specific details (e.g., event type, number of guests, desired menu) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:43">[00:14:43]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:00">[00:15:00]</a>.
- Send messages to vendors to request quotes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:14">[00:12:14]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:42">[00:14:42]</a>.
This can save significant time, allowing users to wake up to dozens of quotes without manual phone calls <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:18">[00:13:18]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:26">[00:13:26]</a>. The AI can also generate random, logical data when needed, such as a tentative wedding date <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:22">[00:16:22]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:48">[00:16:48]</a>.

### Cold Email Outreach
AI agents can be used for cold email campaigns <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[00:00:37]</a>. If provided with a spreadsheet containing emails, first names, and a message, the agent can send customized cold emails <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:33:47">[00:33:47]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:33:55">[00:33:55]</a>. This requires logging into the user's email account within the AI's browser <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:34:06">[00:34:06]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:34:09">[00:34:09]</a>.

### Product Sourcing and Sample Requests
For businesses involved in product sourcing, an AI agent can:
- Navigate e-commerce sites (e.g., AliExpress, Banggood.com) to find trending items <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:12">[00:22:12]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:56">[00:25:56]</a>.
- Message vendors to request free samples to be shipped <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:22">[00:22:22]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:27">[00:22:27]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:48">[00:25:48]</a>.

This capability means a user could wake up to tracking numbers for dozens of free samples <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:45">[00:22:45]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:47">[00:22:47]</a>.

## Challenges and [[benefits_and_challenges_of_ai_integration_in_business | AI Integration in Business]]

Despite the vast [[evaluating_ai_capabilities_for_business_growth | capabilities of AI for business growth]], several challenges exist:

### Inconsistent Performance and Bugginess
The results of AI agents can be "hit and miss" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:51">[00:06:51]</a>. For example:
- An agent might fail to make offers on Facebook Marketplace on behalf of the user, despite having done so previously <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:24">[00:06:24]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:32">[00:06:32]</a>. This often indicates a need for more precise prompting <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:18">[00:07:18]</a>.
- Websites might be inaccessible to the AI agent, potentially due to geo-restrictions or other limitations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:56">[00:22:56]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:01">[00:23:01]</a>.
- While the AI can navigate websites, it might sometimes default to less optimal search engines like Bing <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:13">[00:24:13]</a>.
- The AI may struggle to perform tasks at extremely high speeds or refuse specific tones (e.g., "rude tone") if not aligned with its programming <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:52">[00:20:52]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:21:02">[00:21:02]</a>.

### Prompting Nuances and User Expectations
Users often need to learn how to effectively "prompt" the AI agent to achieve desired outcomes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:19">[00:07:19]</a>. While the AI can learn from user actions (e.g., taking control to show it how to click), it may not always interpret complex or nuanced instructions immediately <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:10">[00:15:10]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:26">[00:15:26]</a>. Treating the AI bluntly and directly, continuously commanding it, is suggested for optimal learning <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:32:35">[00:32:35]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:32:40">[00:32:40]</a>. Giving it a specific goal (e.g., "submit three quote requests per minute") can also be an effective prompting strategy <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:26">[00:20:26]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:39">[00:20:39]</a>.

### Cost and Early Adoption
The cost of AI tools, such as a $200/month "Pro ChatGPT plan" for "Operator", can be perceived as expensive by those who don't understand its full capabilities or have specific ideas for its use <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:05">[00:00:05]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:30:03">[00:30:03]</a>. However, for those who invest time and effort into learning the nuances of the technology, it represents a significant opportunity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:30:10">[00:30:10]</a>. Many people are likely "waiting till it gets better," but early adoption allows for a deeper understanding of its evolving capabilities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:30:37">[00:30:37]</a>.

The current state of AI agents, while sometimes buggy, is seen as a glimpse into Artificial General Intelligence (AGI), where machines can "go rogue" and figure things out on their own, mimicking human problem-solving <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:41">[00:24:41]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:00">[00:25:00]</a>. This represents one of the greatest "arbitrage moments" in business history, combining billions of internet-connected users, global knowledge bases (like ChatGPT), and autonomous bots capable of executing tasks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:28:07">[00:28:07]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:28:35">[00:28:35]</a>. As AI technology advances, future iterations are expected to be faster and more integrated, potentially adding features like voice communication directly into platforms like Operator <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:30">[00:14:30]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:44">[00:18:44]</a>.
