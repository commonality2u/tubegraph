## Understanding "Type Beats"

"Type beats" are a subgenre within hip-hop and rap where producers create instrumental tracks designed to emulate the style of popular artists, such as a "Kendrick type beat" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:01">[00:11:01]</a>. There is substantial search volume for these types of keywords <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:14">[00:11:14]</a>, indicating a high demand from aspiring artists looking for sounds similar to established styles <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:08">[00:11:08]</a>.

## Leveraging AI for Production

Historically, a producer might create around 10 "type beats" in a day <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:54">[00:11:54]</a>. However, with the advent of [[innovations_in_ai_image_generation_and_design | AI models]] for beat production, it's now possible to generate thousands of beats, significantly reducing the time and effort traditionally required <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:01">[00:12:01]</a>. This technology is continually improving, offering increasingly decent outputs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:19">[00:13:19]</a>. This represents a major disruption to traditional music production methods <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:16">[00:13:16]</a>.

## Monetization Strategies

Several avenues exist for monetizing [[shortterm_moneymaking_opportunities_with_ai_technology | AI-generated music]]:

1.  **Direct Sales and Licensing** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:32">[00:11:32]</a>:
    *   Create a marketplace to sell beats <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:06">[00:12:06]</a>.
    *   Beats can be licensed for a specific period (e.g., $29 for a year), meaning no one else can use that beat during that time <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:16">[00:15:16]</a>.
    *   Full rights to a beat can be purchased at a higher price (e.g., $295 instead of $29) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:28">[00:15:28]</a>.
    *   Many artists buy batches of beats to record multiple tracks, increasing the chances of one going public <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:34">[00:15:34]</a>.
2.  **YouTube Channel & Ad Revenue** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:19">[00:12:19]</a>:
    *   Post beats to YouTube after registering them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:13">[00:12:13]</a>.
    *   Earn ad revenue and streaming revenue when people listen to the beats <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:26">[00:12:26]</a>.
    *   Include links in the description to the marketplace where users can license or buy the beat <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:29">[00:12:29]</a>.
    *   YouTube typically ranks instrumentals higher in search results, leading to more views and potential sales <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:44">[00:14:44]</a>.
    *   Some producers have YouTube channels with 100,000+ subscribers, generating consistent revenue from new releases <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:51">[00:14:51]</a>.
3.  **Google Ads / Funnel Optimization** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:43">[00:13:43]</a>:
    *   Bid on keywords related to type beats to show YouTube videos as ads <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:47">[00:13:47]</a>.
    *   Direct interested users from the ad to the YouTube video for listening, and then to the purchase link <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:58">[00:13:58]</a>.
