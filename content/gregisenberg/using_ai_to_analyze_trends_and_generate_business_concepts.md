The user tasked Manis to act as an AI co-founder to create a business that could generate $1 million in 18 months <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:32">[00:01:32]</a>. The specific interest was in niche AI agent or AI startups not present in Y Combinator's (YC) latest batch, and utilizing Reddit to discover trends for business ideas <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:52">[00:01:52]</a>. The ideal outcome included an entire business plan and a Minimal Viable Product (MVP) design <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:53">[00:02:53]</a>.

### Business Concept Generation and [[integration_of_ai_in_business_plan_development | Business Plan Development]]

Manis proposed "Smart Console AI," an [[automated_business_creation_using_ai | AI-powered micro-consulting platform for SMBs]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:40">[00:20:40]</a>.
The concept includes:
*   **Problem**: SMBs need expert consulting but cannot afford traditional services <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:24">[00:24:24]</a>.
*   **Solution**: AI-powered consulting agents specializing in key business functions at a fraction of the cost ($49-$500/month) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:31">[00:24:31]</a>.
*   **Validation**: Supported by discussions on Reddit highlighting cost barriers, the need for specialized AI tools, and information overload among small business owners <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:34:09">[00:34:09]</a>.
*   **MVP**: The proposed MVP was broad (marketing, operations, financial, strategy consultants), which the user noted would need to be more niche for a true startup <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:35:25">[00:35:25]</a>.

## Manis's Strengths and Limitations

Manis demonstrates significant "horsepower" compared to tools like ChatGPT, Perplexity, or Claude, attributed to its multi-agent browsing capabilities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:40">[00:09:40]</a>. It functions like an AI co-worker, providing documents for review and displaying live task progress <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:11">[00:10:11]</a>. It can multitask and listen to new prompts while already working on a task <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:53">[00:15:53]</a>.

However, Manis is not a "one-prompt" solution <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:15">[00:19:15]</a>. Users need to provide precise prompts and "push back" on the AI to get more specific or data-backed insights <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:39:11">[00:39:11]</a>. It's compared to a "junior employee" that requires management, mentoring, and "taste" from the user to achieve optimal results <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:39:47">[00:39:47]</a>. Additionally, caution is advised when downloading files, especially from unknown sources <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:37:00">[00:37:00]</a>.
