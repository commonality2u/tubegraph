## Identifying App Ideas

Identifying which [[building_apps_with_ai_tools | AI apps are popping]] and why, along with strategies for user acquisition, is crucial <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:06">[00:00:06]</a>.

### Unique AI App Ideas
One approach is to conceptualize a completely new idea for an AI app <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:26">[00:04:26]</a>.
*   **Verse App:** This app transforms a Spotify playlist into a visual experience, creating "vision boards" with beautiful covers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:34">[00:01:34]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:48">[00:02:48]</a>. It recently went viral on TikTok <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:03">[00:02:03]</a> and reached the 8th spot in the App Store <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:56">[00:03:56]</a>. Its marketing flow was embedded within the app's concept, encouraging sharing <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:35">[00:02:35]</a>.

### Adding "AI Sizzle" to Existing Apps
A more accessible strategy is to identify popular existing apps and enhance them by adding an "AI sizzle" to capture existing demand <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:44">[00:04:44]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:10">[00:05:10]</a>.
*   **Examples:**
    *   Calorie counters or sleep sound apps existed, but adding AI makes them more valuable, leading to higher click-through rates and downloads <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:35:02">[00:35:02]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:35:31">[00:35:31]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:35:40">[00:35:40]</a>. Many older apps that previously didn't work effectively (e.g., photo-based calorie counters) now work with AI <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:36:30">[00:36:30]</a>.
    *   [[building_niche_ai_apps | Building niche AI apps]] for specific audiences, like a "Bible for women" app based on a general Bible chat app, or localizing apps for different countries (e.g., "plant AI for Canadians") <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:46:08">[00:46:08]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:46:46">[00:46:46]</a>. This is analogous to dating apps like Tinder creating niche versions for specific demographics <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:46:02">[00:46:02]</a>.

### Trend Spotting

*   **App Figures:** A tool that helps search keywords and identify new opportunities for app ideas <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:51">[00:17:51]</a>.
*   **General Trend Software:** Use tools like Google Trends or Glimpse to identify trending topics outside of the App Store <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:01">[00:20:01]</a>. For example, the Umax app idea came from the "looksmaxing" trend on Reddit and TikTok <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:21:56">[00:21:56]</a>. Similarly, the text-to-speech app was developed when it was identified as a trend not yet saturated in the App Store <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:55">[00:13:55]</a>.
*   **Automated Tracking:** Set up automations to receive notifications about new apps ranking in the top of the App Store categories, indicating emerging opportunities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:21:24">[00:21:24]</a>.

## App Growth Strategies

There are three main ways to grow a mobile app:
1.  **TikTok Organic / Short-form Video Content:** Utilizing platforms like TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts for free traffic <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:47">[00:08:47]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:08">[00:24:08]</a>.
    *   **Umax (Looksmaxing app):** Developed by a 21-22 year old who taught himself to code with ChatGPT <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:45">[00:07:45]</a>. They grew by having small creators promote the app on TikTok and Instagram through viral videos <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:30">[00:08:30]</a>.
    *   **KAI (AI Calorie Tracking app):** Created by a 17-year-old, this app generates approximately $400,000 in monthly revenue and saw 200,000 downloads last month <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:27:41">[00:27:41]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:28:52">[00:28:52]</a>. They scaled through organic TikTok and Instagram by finding micro-influencers to create simple, native content that integrates the app <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:29:50">[00:29:50]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:31:02">[00:31:02]</a>. The strategy involves creating many videos, understanding what goes viral, and replicating successful formulas <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:32:32">[00:32:32]</a>.
    *   Content should feel organic and can even omit direct links, as users often search for the app name directly in the App Store <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:31:17">[00:31:17]</a>.

2.  **Paid Ads:** Running effective ad campaigns on platforms like Facebook, TikTok, and Google Ads <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:06">[00:09:06]</a>.
    *   **PictureThis (Plant Identifier app):** This app makes $7 million in monthly revenue <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:34:31">[00:34:31]</a>. They use paid ads and are diligent about optimizing every detail to achieve profitability <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:37:13">[00:37:13]</a>.
    *   **High Volume Testing:** Successful companies test thousands of ad creatives monthly to find the perfect angle <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:37:27">[00:37:27]</a>. For example, PictureThis has tested around 30,000 videos <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:37:37">[00:37:37]</a>.
    *   **AI for Ad Creation:** AI tools like Arc Ads enable the creation of hundreds or thousands of ad variations quickly and cost-effectively, eliminating the need for actors or complex productions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:38:38">[00:38:38]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:39:48">[00:39:48]</a>. Arc Ads allows users to write a script, select AI actors from a library, and generate videos in minutes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:40:28">[00:40:28]</a>. These can then be further edited with screenshots and AI-powered background removal <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:41:51">[00:41:51]</a>.
    *   **Localization:** Once an ad works in one market (e.g., the US), it can be localized and scaled to other countries and languages using AI tools <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:43:07">[00:43:07]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:43:53">[00:43:53]</a>. A Turkish studio makes $8 million monthly revenue by localizing ads for various apps <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:43:23">[00:43:23]</a>.

3.  **App Store Optimization (ASO) / Apple Search Ads (ASA):** Optimizing an app to rank highly in App Store search results for specific keywords <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:19">[00:09:19]</a>.
    *   **ASO is the "art of being number one"** when users type a keyword in the App Store <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:22">[00:13:22]</a>.
    *   **Strategy:** Find keywords with sufficient search volume but low competition <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:43">[00:13:43]</a>.
    *   **Example:** A text-to-speech app grew quickly organically because it targeted an under-served keyword in the App Store <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:19">[00:14:19]</a>.
    *   **Remone Holding:** This company excels at identifying "weird keywords" like "fan noise" or "anime girlfriend AI" that have demand but not much competition <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:31">[00:15:31]</a>. They often repeat successful app ideas with different keywords or marketing angles <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:00">[00:16:00]</a>.
    *   **Techniques:** Include keywords in the title, optimize conversion rates on the App Store page, maximize reviews, and leverage the initial launch boost given by Apple <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:42">[00:17:42]</a>. Some companies also run paid ads not just for profitability, but to drive traffic and reviews that improve ASO rankings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:02">[00:19:02]</a>.

4.  **Virality (Product-Led Growth):** Designing the app with embedded invite flows or sharing mechanisms that encourage users to spread the word <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:43">[00:09:43]</a>.
    *   The Verse app is a strong example, as its core functionality creates shareable visual content from Spotify playlists <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:56">[00:02:56]</a>.
    *   Virality acts as a multiplier effect on marketing efforts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:49">[00:11:49]</a>. For instance, an app could require users to invite friends to unlock features or improve their experience <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:06">[00:11:06]</a>.
    *   AI can potentially help kickstart virality by enabling highly targeted ads to niche audiences (e.g., specific high schools), which is difficult to scale without AI <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:43">[00:12:43]</a>.

## Key Success Factors
*   **Simplicity:** Apps should be designed to be straightforward, guiding users directly to the core feature and then to payment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:05">[00:08:05]</a>.
*   **Team Structure:** Clearly separating roles for product development and growth can lead to success <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:01">[00:06:01]</a>.
*   **Lean Operations:** A profitable app business doesn't necessarily require a large team <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:07">[00:07:07]</a>.
*   **Learning to Code with AI:** Individuals can learn to code and build successful apps using AI tools like ChatGPT in a matter of weeks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:15">[00:07:15]</a>. This enables individuals to quickly [[building_a_startup_using_ai_tools | build a startup using AI tools]] and even reach millions in yearly revenue <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:28">[00:07:28]</a>.
