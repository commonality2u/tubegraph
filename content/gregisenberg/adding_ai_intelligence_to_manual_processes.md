## The "Export Button Theory"

Every "export button" in software represents a potential business opportunity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:48:00">[02:48:00]</a>. When a user clicks export, they are essentially signaling that the current software doesn't meet their needs, requiring them to take data elsewhere for manual work <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:59:00">[02:59:00]</a>. Each such export points to a workflow breakdown, a pain point, manual labor that could be **[[automating workflows using AI | automated]]**, and a potential feature worth $10,000 to $30,000 per month <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:09:00">[03:09:00]</a>.
