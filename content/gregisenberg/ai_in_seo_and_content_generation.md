## Strategies for Leveraging AI in SEO and Content

A key strategy involves identifying keywords with high search intent but low keyword difficulty using tools like Ahrefs. These are terms with scores between zero and five, indicating minimal competition and quick ranking potential for new websites <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:34">[00:02:34]</a>.

Once such keywords are identified, [[ai_in_writing_and_content_creation | AI in writing and content creation]] can be rapidly deployed to generate content <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:13">[00:03:13]</a>. For example, using AI image generators like Stable Diffusion or Midjourney, along with language models, can create vast amounts of niche content <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:51">[00:03:51]</a>.

### Case Study: AI Tattoos

An example shared is an "AI tattoos" website that utilized this approach. By identifying thousands of long-tail keywords related to specific tattoo designs (e.g., "butterfly tattoo," "dragon tattoo"), AI was used to generate images and content, filling pages and building automated directories <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:26">[00:03:26]</a>. This method allowed for the organic growth of the website, quickly generating hundreds of thousands of content pieces that Google favored, leading to significant traffic <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:10">[00:04:10]</a>. The business model also incorporated a tool where users could generate their own AI tattoos, displaying that content on the website, effectively creating user-generated content <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:14">[00:04:14]</a>. This site achieved $10,000 Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) before being sold <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:34">[00:04:34]</a>.

## Humanizing AI for Wider Adoption

For products to be widely adopted, especially by "normies" (regular people), AI needs to be "humanized" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:28:03">[00:28:03]</a>. This means making AI-powered services feel like familiar real-world interactions rather than purely technical APIs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:28:18">[00:28:18]</a>.

*   **Fiverr/Upwork 2.0:** A proposed idea is a platform similar to Fiverr or Upwork, but instead of hiring human freelancers, users "hire" AI bots, Large Language Models (LLMs), or automated workflows to perform tasks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:31">[00:19:31]</a>. For example, a translation service where users upload an Excel sheet and receive a translated version a day later, all processed by AI <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:56">[00:19:56]</a>. This approach makes advanced AI accessible without requiring users to understand programming or APIs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:08">[00:20:08]</a>. Such platforms can cater to larger companies needing automation and reliable outputs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:21:08">[00:21:08]</a>.
*   **Headshot Pro Model:** Headshot Pro exemplifies this by replacing the traditional physical photoshoot with an AI-powered process. Users take selfies at home, and AI acts as the photographer, delivering professional headshots within hours <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:28:41">[00:28:41]</a>. The focus is on replicating the familiar experience, not on the underlying AI <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:29:00">[00:29:00]</a>.
*   **AI in Professional Services:** Another opportunity lies in identifying repetitive, hated tasks in various professions and automating them with AI <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:33:50">[00:33:50]</a>. An example is AI medical scribes: AI can transcribe doctor-patient conversations using Open AI Whisper and summarize them with an LLM, replacing human scribes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:30:20">[00:30:20]</a>. This not only makes the service cheaper but also enables doctors to see more patients and ensures critical information is captured <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:32:45">[00:32:45]</a>. Similar applications can be found in other niches, such as AI note-taking for veterinarians <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:34:47">[00:34:47]</a>.
