## Identifying Content Opportunities with Gummy Search

A tool like Gummy Search can be used to find new content ideas by analyzing trending subreddits and audience discussions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>.

### Finding Trending Communities
*   Navigate to the "trending" section within Gummy Search to identify popular subreddits <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:40">[00:01:40]</a>.
*   Focus on subreddits with 10,000 to 100,000 members, as these often represent newer, trending communities that are in the public consciousness <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:17">[00:02:17]</a>. This answers the "why now" question for content relevance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:28">[00:02:28]</a>.
*   Experiment with different timeframes (daily, weekly, monthly) to uncover unique trends <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:36">[00:03:36]</a>.
*   Consider curated sections for specific niches, such as software developers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:15">[00:04:15]</a>.

### Discovering Problems and Pain Points
Once a target subreddit is selected, Gummy Search can organize discussions into categories like "pain and anger" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:39">[00:06:39]</a>.
*   Analyze common patterns summarized by AI within these discussions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:08">[00:07:08]</a>. This can reveal specific challenges or frustrations users face, which are ripe for content solutions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:00">[00:08:00]</a>.
*   For example, if a community discusses "potty training challenges" or "dogs reluctant to walk outside," these are direct cues for creating relevant content <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:58">[00:07:58]</a>.

### Uncovering Advice and Solution Requests
The "advice and requests" category highlights what products or solutions people are actively seeking <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:08">[00:10:08]</a>.
*   Thousands of people may be asking for product recommendations or guidance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:29">[00:10:29]</a>. This indicates a strong demand for content that addresses these specific needs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:36">[00:10:36]</a>.
*   Observe the number of upvotes and comments to gauge the popularity and urgency of these requests <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:00">[00:11:00]</a>.
*   Topics like "dog breed recommendations" or "dog food recommendations" are direct content opportunities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:18">[00:11:18]</a>.

## Analyzing Top Performing Content
Gummy Search's "top content" section allows you to see what has resonated most with an audience <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:31">[00:18:31]</a>.
*   Identify common patterns and working formats (e.g., long stories, memes) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:40">[00:18:40]</a>.
*   Learn about the community's preferences, such as their appreciation for relatable stories like "Today I saved a dog's life" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:55">[00:18:55]</a>.
*   Use the AI feature to ask direct questions, such as "Do they love memes?" to understand content style <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:18">[00:19:18]</a>.

## Leveraging Creators for Content Marketing
Identifying and analyzing content creators within your niche is a crucial step for [[utilizing_media_and_content_creation_for_brand_leverage | brand leverage]] and [[strategic_content_creation_and_distribution_for_founders | content distribution]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:34">[00:13:34]</a>.

### Finding Influencers
*   Use AI tools like Perplexity.ai to find popular influencers related to your niche (e.g., "25 most popular dog influencers on YouTube") <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:38">[00:13:38]</a>.
*   Refine your search to find educational creators rather than purely entertainment-focused ones if your content aims to solve problems (e.g., "top YouTubers that are educational for dogs") <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:42">[00:14:42]</a>.

### Extracting Insights from Influencer Content
*   Go to a creator's most popular videos <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:12">[00:15:12]</a>.
*   Read through the comments section to understand audience sentiment, questions, and unaddressed needs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:25">[00:15:25]</a>. This provides direct insights into what people are thinking and what additional content or products they might desire <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:40">[00:15:40]</a>.
*   This process helps you "put yourself in their shoes" to understand the audience's perspective <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:49">[00:15:49]</a>.
