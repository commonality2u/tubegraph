*   Access data from external tools [00:27:15](<a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:27:15">[00:27:15]</a>.
*   Send commands or data back to those tools [00:27:19](<a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:27:19">[00:27:19]</a>.
