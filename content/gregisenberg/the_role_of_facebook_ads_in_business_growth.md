## [[building_a_sales_funnel_using_facebook_ads | Building a Sales Funnel Using Facebook Ads]]

[[building_a_sales_funnel_using_facebook_ads | Building a sales funnel using Facebook Ads]] is a critical strategy to convert strangers into customers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:49:49">[00:49:49]</a>. This process involves a series of steps to guide potential customers.

### Anatomy of a VSL (Video Sales Letter) Funnel
A common and effective funnel type is the Video Sales Letter (VSL) funnel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:28:46">[00:28:46]</a>. Here's a breakdown of its components:

1.  **Facebook Ad**: The initial touchpoint, selling the idea of an opt-in <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:30:48">[00:30:48]</a>.
2.  **Opt-in Page**: This page aims to capture an email address by offering something of value, such as a free training video, a downloadable PDF (lead magnet), or a physical book <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:29:41">[00:29:41]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:31:36">[00:31:36]</a>.
    *   **Tools**: Pages can be built using Framer (for aesthetic appeal) or ClickFunnels (for quick testing) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:32:07">[00:32:07]</a>.
    *   **Purpose**: To "sell" the idea of watching the video or consuming the free content, as people have many free options online <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:31:15">[00:31:15]</a>.
3.  **Video (Free Training)**: A training video that subtly acts as an advertisement by showcasing the value of being trained by the business <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:31:20">[00:31:20]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:31:54">[00:31:54]</a>.
4.  **Call Booking (Calendly)**: At the end of the video, instead of directly selling a product, offer a free call with a career coach or sales team member <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:33:05">[00:33:05]</a>. This generates qualified leads for sales.

### Real-World Example & ROI
A company spent $80,000 on Facebook Ads and $6,000 on Google/YouTube ads in one month (totaling $86,000) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:34:51">[00:34:51]</a>. From this, they collected $252,000 in revenue, achieving a 38% return on ad spend (ROAS) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:35:18">[00:35:18]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:35:35">[00:35:35]</a>. This funnel booked 339 calls, with 273 attendees and 65 sales <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:35:42">[00:35:42]</a>.

### Post-Funnel Monetization (Email Marketing)
Leads captured through funnels, even if not immediately monetized, can be nurtured for future campaigns like Black Friday sales <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:38:07">[00:38:07]</a>. This involves extensive email marketing to segments of the email list using tools like ConvertKit <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:38:58">[00:38:58]</a>.

### Conversion Rate Optimization & Copywriting
Funnels require constant tweaking and optimization to improve conversion rates at every step <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:26:46">[00:26:46]</a>. This involves focusing on the overall return on ad spend (ROAS) rather than obsessing over precise attribution for every conversion <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:41:57">[00:41:57]</a>.

#### The "Shadow Funnel"
High-quality [[media_and_content_strategies_for_business_growth | content marketing]], such as a large YouTube channel, acts as a "shadow funnel" that builds trust and makes prospects more comfortable entering a paid ad funnel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:42:24">[00:42:24]</a>.

#### Copywriting Hacks
Effective copywriting is crucial for funnel success <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:43:28">[00:43:28]</a>:
1.  **Status Increase**: Copy should suggest that the product or service will enhance the customer's status or make them more valuable to their peers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:44:42">[00:44:42]</a>.
2.  **Pain vs. Pleasure**: Copy can either focus on moving people *away from pain* (e.g., avoiding negative situations) or *towards pleasure* (e.g., highlighting positive benefits) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:46:15">[00:46:15]</a>.
    *   During economic downturns, focusing on pain avoidance is often more effective <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:47:05">[00:47:05]</a>.

## Websites vs. Funnels
A key distinction:
*   A **website** is like a business card, presenting all available information <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:49:42">[00:49:42]</a>.
*   A **funnel** is a step-by-step process designed specifically to guide a stranger to become a customer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:49:49">[00:49:49]</a>.
