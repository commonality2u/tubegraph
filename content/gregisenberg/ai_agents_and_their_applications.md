## Defining [[ai_agents_and_automation | AI Agents]]

[[ai_agents_and_automation | AI agents]] are designed to perform specific tasks, and their effectiveness is often measured by their ability to:
*   Execute a single clear action <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:16">[00:03:16]</a>.
*   Provide instant feedback <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:18">[00:03:18]</a>.
*   Operate within bounded decisions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:21">[00:03:21]</a>.
*   Offer a zero-friction experience <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:25">[00:03:25]</a>.

"Smart agents" possess additional capabilities, enabling them to think, understand user intent, learn from past mistakes, explain their decisions, and adapt to new situations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:28">[00:03:28]</a>.

## Building Valuable [[ai_agents_and_automation | AI Agents]]

A framework for developing effective [[ai_agents_and_automation | AI agents]] includes:
1.  **Start with one action that matters**: Focus on a single, impactful task <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:46">[00:03:46]</a>.
2.  **Add LLM understanding**: Integrate large language model capabilities to enhance comprehension <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:47">[00:03:47]</a>.
3.  **Build tight feedback loops**: Ensure continuous learning and improvement from interactions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:51">[00:03:51]</a>.
4.  **Learn from every interaction**: Adapt and refine the agent's behavior over time <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:53">[00:03:53]</a>.
5.  **Expand slowly**: Gradually introduce new functionalities and capabilities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:53">[00:03:53]</a>.

## Pricing [[ai_agents_and_automation | AI Agent]] Services

When pricing [[ai_agents_and_automation | AI agent]] services, consider:
*   **Saved Human Input**: Calculate the total cost of human labor being replaced, including salary and "emotional damage" or frustration saved <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:34:54">[00:34:54]</a>.
*   **Discount Factor**: Apply a discount to the saved cost to make the AI solution a "no-brainer" offer, potentially 50% to 80% off the human equivalent <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:35:30">[00:35:30]</a>.
*   **Value-Based Pricing**: If a product is loved and provides significant value, pricing can be adjusted upwards later <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:37:51">[00:37:51]</a>. Customers remember the value, not just the initial price <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:38:44">[00:38:44]</a>.
