## Process and Techniques
The process for generating app assets with AI involves several key steps and techniques:

### Leveraging Existing Assets
A core technique involves taking an existing mascot or primary asset and using AI to generate an "infinite number of secondary assets" based on its style <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:28:22">[00:28:22]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:49:28">[00:49:28]</a>. This ensures visual consistency across the app, for elements like loading screens or empty states <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:49:30">[00:49:30]</a>.

### Prompting for Specifics and Consistency
*   **Detailed instructions:** Provide specific instructions to the AI model, such as adding accessories (e.g., "wired glasses") or placing the character in a scene (e.g., "in front of a laptop") <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:49:37">[00:49:37]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:49:50">[00:49:50]</a>.
*   **Maintaining style:** Emphasize maintaining the "same style" as the original asset <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:49:57">[00:49:57]</a>. This helps create a cohesive visual identity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:50:00">[00:50:00]</a>.
*   **Refinement:** If the AI doesn't get it perfectly the first time, users can provide specific prompts to refine the image, such as "remove the legs" or "make the dog look more like my dog" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:51:14">[00:51:14]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:51:17">[00:51:17]</a>. AI models like GPT-4o get it right 60-70% of the time, which is still considered worthwhile <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:51:12">[00:51:12]</a>.

### Brainstorming with AI
AI can also be used as a brainstorming tool for initial design ideas <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:52:54">[00:52:54]</a>. For example, when creating a mascot for a meeting assistant app, AI suggested a ghost, as ghosts "live in the background" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:53:06">[00:53:06]</a>.

## Examples of Generated Assets

## Tips for Success
*   **Iterate and refine:** Don't expect perfect results on the first try. Continuously prompt and iterate to get the desired outcome <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:51:08">[00:51:08]</a>.
*   **Be specific:** Provide clear and detailed instructions in your prompts to guide the AI towards the desired aesthetic and content <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:51:08">[00:51:08]</a>.
*   **Focus on humanizing the app:** Beyond core functionality, consider using AI to generate "delightful" assets for elements like welcome emails, loading screens, and empty states <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:52:16">[00:52:16]</a>.
*   **Ask AI for ideas:** Use AI to brainstorm concepts for assets based on your app's purpose and target audience <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:52:31">[00:52:31]</a>.
