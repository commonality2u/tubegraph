## Digital Gravity: A New Marketing Paradigm

Digital gravity posits that building a brand involves accumulating "digital mass" on the internet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:03">[00:02:03]</a>. The greater the digital mass, the more influence a brand has within its niche <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:09">[00:02:09]</a>. This mass is built primarily through content and marketing consistently delivered wherever customers are present online <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:14">[00:02:14]</a>.

Instead of a linear funnel where customers enter and exit, actual purchasing behavior is cyclical; people interact with a brand, move away, get "sucked back in," and may complete multiple rotations before making a purchase <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:08">[00:04:08]</a>. The goal is to build enough mass (like a planet) so that customers (like comets) are drawn into the brand's orbit, leading to "touchdowns" (revenue) and a growth flywheel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:39">[00:03:39]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:49">[00:03:49]</a>.
