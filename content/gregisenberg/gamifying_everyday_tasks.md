## Applications of Gamification

### Employee Engagement
A practical application of [[gamification_to_enhance_personal_habits_and_goals | gamification]] involves employee engagement <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:00">[00:12:00]</a>. An employee engagement tool like "15Five" can be used to track performance and reward timely completion of tasks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:00">[00:12:00]</a>.

Examples of incentives include:
*   Receiving a small amount of cryptocurrency, such as a dollar or two in Solana, for filling out weekly reports on time <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:06">[00:12:06]</a>.
*   Getting a high rating from a colleague <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:11">[00:12:11]</a>.
*   Completing all deadlines in project management tools like Jira <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:16">[00:12:16]</a>.

Many actions are trackable via APIs, enabling the creation of leaderboards to show who is completing the most tasks or who might be falling behind <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:17">[00:12:17]</a>. This system can even provide grounds for promotion decisions or staffing adjustments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:27">[00:12:27]</a>. One implementation saw a 77% increase in engagement with these tools <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:31">[00:12:31]</a>.

### Household Chores and Personal Habits
The potential for [[gamification_to_enhance_personal_habits_and_goals | gamification]] extends to personal and household tasks.

*   **Vacuuming with AR**: An Apple Vision Pro demo showed a user vacuuming while collecting virtual coins, similar to playing a Mario game <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:47">[00:12:47]</a>. This could be applied to rewarding children for chores based on the number of coins they collect <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:02">[00:13:02]</a>.
*   **Spousal Tasks**: Everyday tasks, like taking out the trash, can be gamified with micro-rewards, which don't necessarily have to be monetary <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:30">[00:13:30]</a>. Rewards could even come in the form of virtual coins from favorite stores, such as an Amazon coin <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:46">[00:13:46]</a>.

### Product Design and Market Opportunity
The effectiveness of [[gamification_to_enhance_personal_habits_and_goals | gamification]] relies heavily on good product design that evokes a positive "feel" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:05">[00:15:05]</a>. This is akin to the difference in sound when closing a car door on a Rolls-Royce versus a Honda Civic, or the automatic safety features in a Volvo <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:09">[00:15:09]</a>.

While the market for spatial computing devices like the Apple Vision Pro is currently small (e.g., 500,000 units in the first 12 months) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:09">[00:14:09]</a>, it presents an early opportunity to gain experience <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:27">[00:14:27]</a>. The increased effectiveness of developers (4 to 8 times more effective) with [[ai_tools_and_productivity_enhancement | AI co-pilots]] means much of this [[gamification_to_enhance_personal_habits_and_goals | gamification]] software can be built more efficiently, provided there is a clear vision and "taste" in design <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:41">[00:14:41]</a>.
