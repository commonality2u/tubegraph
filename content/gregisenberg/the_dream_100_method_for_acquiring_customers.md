From: [[gregisenberg]] <br/> 

The Dream 100 method is a strategic approach to [[Lead generation and conversion strategies | acquiring customers]] and leads without relying on a large social media following or expensive ads <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:08">[00:00:08]</a>. This method focuses on leveraging the audiences of potential partners or well-known figures <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:30">[00:03:30]</a>.

## Core Principles

The central idea is to identify individuals or organizations who already have access to your target audience <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:33">[00:03:33]</a>. By establishing a connection or partnership with these entities, you can expose your product or service to a relevant and engaged audience <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:26">[00:10:26]</a>. This approach can yield significant results, potentially generating more leads than traditional social media efforts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:53">[00:08:53]</a>.

## Implementing the Dream 100 Method

### Step 1: Create Your Dream 100 List
Begin by compiling a list, typically in a spreadsheet, of people or organizations that you believe are already engaging with your desired audience <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:27">[00:04:27]</a>.

*   **Identify Influencers and Organizations:** Think of individuals (e.g., podcast hosts like Greg) or companies (e.g., Product Hunt) who cater to your niche <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:47">[00:05:47]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:32">[00:06:32]</a>. For a web design agency targeting startup founders, this could include startup community organizers or tech news platforms <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:00">[00:04:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:11">[00:07:11]</a>.
*   **Research:** For each entry, record their main website, social media links, and an estimate of their audience size <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:56">[00:05:56]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:10">[00:06:10]</a>. This serves as a research project to understand your potential partners better <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:17">[00:06:17]</a>.
*   **Scalability:** While the name suggests "100," even a list of 10 or 20 highly targeted individuals can be effective <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:11">[00:09:11]</a>.

### Step 2: Determine Your Value Proposition
Once you have your list, brainstorm what you can offer these individuals or organizations that would provide significant value to them, while also exposing you to their audience <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:17">[00:09:17]</a>.

*   **Paid Collaborations:** Sometimes, the most direct approach is to pay for an advertisement or a collaboration. For example, paying for an ad on a relevant blog can generate substantial traffic <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:42">[00:09:42]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:56">[00:09:56]</a>. Allocating marketing budget (e.g., $10K-$15K) to a direct collaboration with a top person on your list can be more impactful than general branding efforts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:27:37">[00:27:37]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:28:11">[00:28:11]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:29:03">[00:29:03]</a>.
*   **Guest Appearances/Partnerships:** Offer to guest host a podcast, contribute content, or participate in a joint event (e.g., a webinar) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:05">[00:10:05]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:48:11">[00:48:11]</a>. This requires thoughtful pitches demonstrating why your involvement benefits their audience and platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:23">[00:12:23]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:26">[00:12:26]</a>.
*   **Gifting and Personalization:** Gifting can be an underrated tool to gain favor and create a memorable connection. The key is for the gift to be personal and meaningful to the recipient <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:30:45">[00:30:45]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:30:51">[00:30:51]</a>. Even small, thoughtful items can leave a lasting impression <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:32:08">[00:32:08]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:32:13">[00:32:13]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:32:47">[00:32:47]</a>.

### Step 3: Strategic Outreach (Canvas Strategy)
Reaching out to busy, influential people requires a careful approach. The "Canvas Strategy" by Ryan Holiday is recommended <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:34">[00:11:34]</a>.

*   **Be Helpful:** Focus on how you can be helpful to the gatekeepers or those above you in the industry <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:43">[00:11:43]</a>.
*   **Avoid Insulting Requests:** Do not simply ask for access to their audience without offering substantial value in return <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:06">[00:12:06]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:13">[00:12:13]</a>.
*   **Demonstrate Effort:** Even if you have an existing relationship, put effort into your pitch, clearly outlining the value you bring <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:21">[00:12:21]</a>.

## Beyond Dream 100: Community Engagement and Content

### Utilizing Existing Platforms and Groups
Another crucial step is to identify where your audience congregates and actively engage there <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:38">[00:18:38]</a>.

*   **Join Communities:** Find groups on platforms like Discord, School (e.g., [[Building and marketing startup ideas from Reddit insights | Startup Empire.co]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:07">[00:19:07]</a>), Facebook, or even Reddit <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:40">[00:20:40]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:21:25">[00:21:25]</a>.
*   **Deliver Extreme Value:** In these groups, focus on being the most valuable person <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:02">[00:20:02]</a>. Answer questions thoroughly, share free templates, and observe common challenges, all without spamming or directly seeking work <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:05">[00:20:05]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:25">[00:20:25]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:29">[00:20:29]</a>.
*   **Engage with Creators:** Turn on notifications for creators in your niche and provide thoughtful comments on their content <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:26">[00:25:26]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:32">[00:25:32]</a>. This builds trust with the creator and attracts other commenters <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:51">[00:25:51]</a>. Sharing their content also demonstrates genuine care <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:26:27">[00:26:27]</a>.

### High-Value, Low-Quality Content Creation
Even without a personal brand, creating high-value content can attract customers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:40:05">[00:40:05]</a>.

*   **Address Specific Needs:** Use platforms like Quora or Reddit to find frequently asked questions in your niche <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:41:00">[00:41:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:45:36">[00:45:36]</a>.
*   **Create In-Depth Tutorials:** Instead of selling courses, create extremely detailed, long-form video tutorials that solve specific problems <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:40:42">[00:40:42]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:40:48">[00:40:48]</a>. These don't need to be highly polished; "low quality is the new high quality," as long as the effort and value are high <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:42:30">[00:42:30]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:43:49">[00:43:49]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:44:35">[00:44:35]</a>.
*   **Show, Don't Just Tell:** Demonstrate your expertise by showing people how to do the thing you're good at <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:41:19">[00:41:19]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:45:18">[00:45:18]</a>. This can lead viewers to prefer hiring you rather than doing it themselves <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:45:25">[00:45:25]</a>.

This approach prioritizes high-leverage activities that can lead to explosive growth, rather than getting bogged down in minor optimizations or low-impact tasks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:37:37">[00:37:37]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:47:37">[00:47:37]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:48:31">[00:48:31]</a>.
