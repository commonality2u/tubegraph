## Navigating Company Structures and Taxes

Choosing the right legal structure, such as an LLC (Limited Liability Company) versus an S-Corp (S Corporation), can have significant tax implications <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:34:40">[00:34:40]</a>. Forgetting to convert an LLC to an S-Corp at the right time, as one entrepreneur experienced, can lead to hundreds of thousands or even millions of dollars in unnecessary taxes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:35:21">[00:35:21]</a>. This highlights a critical need for accessible, expert advice that bridges the gap between expensive Manhattan lawyers ($1,100/hour) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:36:02">[00:36:02]</a> and basic self-serve platforms <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:36:19">[00:36:19]</a>.

Options for company registration can seem endless, leading to confusion about where to establish a business for tax purposes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:37:21">[00:37:21]</a>.
