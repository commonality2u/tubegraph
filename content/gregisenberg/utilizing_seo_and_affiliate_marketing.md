## Business Idea: Canva YouTube Tutorials to Agency Funnel

## Business Idea: Newsletters for Short-Form Creators

### Leveraging Operations and Content
*   **Content Generation**: Short-form creators record a 30-minute video discussing recent insights or data <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:38:18">[00:38:18]</a>. This video is transcribed and transformed into a newsletter in their voice and vocabulary, then sent for approval <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:38:29">[00:38:29]</a>. This provides a consistent source of unique, original content <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:39:42">[00:39:42]</a>.
