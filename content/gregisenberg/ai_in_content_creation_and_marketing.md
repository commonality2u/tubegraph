## AI as a "First Employee"

A core idea emerging from this shift is that instead of hiring a first employee, businesses, especially small ones, should consider subscribing to services like ChatGPT Pro <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>. This approach offers a significant cost advantage, costing around $200 a month compared to potentially $5,000-$15,000 for a human employee <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:35">[00:00:35]</a>.

The practical benefits are striking:
-   A white paper that cost $35,000 and took three months to create three years ago was recently recreated with deeper research and better quality in about 10 minutes using GPT-3 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>.
-   Tools like ChatGPT Pro, Claude Max, and Notebook LM, costing around $320 per month, can provide the productivity equivalent of hundreds of people <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:26">[00:04:26]</a>.

## Impact on Employment and Business Structure

While some initially feared widespread layoffs, the current narrative suggests a shift towards maximizing the output of existing teams with these tools <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:01">[00:06:01]</a>. However, it's also acknowledged that some level of downsizing or restriction on hiring, particularly for contractors, is occurring <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:09">[00:06:09]</a>.

### Audience Building and Content Generation
For businesses aiming to build an audience, [[automating_content_creation_with_ai | automating content creation with AI]] is crucial due to the need for consistent output to engage algorithms <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:07">[00:10:07]</a>.

1.  **Idea Generation:** ChatGPT Pro can act as a "junior researcher" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:55">[00:13:55]</a>. By prompting it to "scour Reddit" for interesting, validated stories (e.g., posts with many upvotes or comments) within a specific niche, businesses can create a database of engaging content ideas <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:57">[00:12:57]</a>.
2.  **Content Execution:**
    *   **Writing:** AI can help outline and write various content formats, such as long tweets, which a human then edits <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:03">[00:20:03]</a>.
    *   **Video Creation:** There's a significant opportunity for [[leveraging_ai_for_storytelling_and_marketing | script writing]] and faceless video creation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:40">[00:20:40]</a>. Popular content (like a viral tweet) can be fed into ChatGPT to script a 30-second video, which can then be created using tools like Hendra.ai or Arcads <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:21:28">[00:21:28]</a>. This process, termed "vibe marketing," involves figuring out the manual process and then automating it entirely <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:04">[00:22:04]</a>.

### [[Using AI for ad creation and strategy | AI in Ad Creation and Strategy]]
The process of creating Facebook ads, traditionally time-consuming and expensive due to the need for designers, videographers, and multiple variations, is being revolutionized by AI <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:42">[00:23:42]</a>.

-   **Rapid Prototyping:** New [[leveraging_ai_for_ad_creation_and_iteration | imaging tools]] allow for prototyping hundreds of Facebook ads quickly <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:58">[00:23:58]</a>.
-   **Testing and Optimization:** AI can generate ad images for product variations (e.g., different colors) to determine which ones stand out most, a process that used to take weeks or months manually <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:15">[00:24:15]</a>.
-   **Prompt Engineering for Ads:** A key hack is to ask AI (like ChatGPT) to reverse-engineer prompts from existing ad images to recreate their exact look and feel, leading to indistinguishable-from-reality results with perfect text <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:26:43">[00:26:43]</a>.
-   **Video Ads:** Tools like Arcads enable AI-generated video ads with AI actors that appear 100% real <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:27:41">[00:27:41]</a>.

## The Human Element: Curation, Taste, and Prompts

While AI handles much of the heavy lifting, the human role shifts from creation to curation and editing <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:05">[00:16:05]</a>. The founder becomes the "editor" of a vast amount of AI-generated output, deciding what's good, bad, and what to focus on <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:09">[00:16:09]</a>.

-   **Intuition and Taste:** Similar to a music producer like Rick Rubin, successful individuals using AI will rely on their intuition and "taste" to select the best ideas and refine AI outputs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:22">[00:17:22]</a>. This taste can even be informed by AI research, like identifying popular content on platforms like Reddit <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:52">[00:17:52]</a>.
-   **Prompt Engineering:** The quality of AI output is directly proportional to the quality of the prompt <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:44">[00:24:44]</a>. Learning how to effectively teach the AI and add context to its memory is crucial for getting 10x better results <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:10">[00:25:10]</a>.
