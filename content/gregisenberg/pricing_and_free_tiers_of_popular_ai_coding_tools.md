## General [[pricing_strategies_for_ai_startups | Pricing]] Trends

Most of the popular AI coding tools share a similar base [[pricing_strategies_for_ai_startups | pricing]] structure <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="15:20:00">[15:20:00]</a>. The starter or initial tier typically costs between $20 and $30 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="15:23:00">[15:23:00]</a>. Beyond the initial tier, prices can increase, potentially reaching $50, $130, and even higher for more expensive tiers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="15:29:00">[15:29:00]</a>.

Specific examples of base [[pricing_strategies_for_ai_startups | pricing]] include:

A key takeaway for potential users is that all these tools offer "great free tiers" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="15:37:00">[15:37:00]</a>. Some of these free tiers may have daily or weekly usage caps <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="15:41:00">[15:41:00]</a>.

It is highly recommended to leverage these free tiers to "play around" and "explore" different tools <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="15:43:00">[15:43:00]</a>.
