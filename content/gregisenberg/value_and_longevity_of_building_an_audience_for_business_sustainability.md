## The ACP Framework: Audience-First Strategy

The core framework for [[building_an_audience_in_2025_using_the_acp_framework | building an audience in 2025]] is the ACP framework, which stands for Audience, Community, and Product <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:49">[00:01:49]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:56">[00:01:56]</a>.

*   **Audience (A)**: Defined as people in a specific niche who are passionate about a topic and underserved in some way <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:56">[00:01:56]</a>.
*   **Community (C)**: Refers to the "hardcore" segment of your audience <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:01">[00:02:01]</a>.
*   **Product (P)**: What you sell to these people <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:08">[00:02:08]</a>.

The crucial aspect of the ACP framework is the order: start with the audience, then build the community, and finally develop the product <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:17">[00:02:17]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:23">[00:02:23]</a>. This [[importance_and_strategy_of_audiencefirst_approach_for_startups | audience-first approach]] provides numerous benefits:

*   **Confidence and Options**: It offers confidence when releasing products, as there's an existing group of people likely to use it, avoiding the "soul-destroying" experience of launching to zero users <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:36">[00:02:36]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:41">[00:02:41]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:47">[00:02:47]</a>.
*   **Suitability for Bootstrapped Businesses**: While well-funded startups might start with a product, the audience-first approach is essential for bootstrapped businesses due to limited resources for marketing <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:58">[00:02:58]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:10">[00:03:10]</a>.
*   **Organic vs. Paid Attention**: Building an audience organically (free) is preferable to renting attention through paid ads, especially for startups <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:06">[00:17:06]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:14">[00:17:14]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:22">[00:17:22]</a>. An organic approach fosters deep understanding of the audience, leading to better products and more indispensable businesses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:35">[00:17:35]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:41">[00:17:41]</a>.
