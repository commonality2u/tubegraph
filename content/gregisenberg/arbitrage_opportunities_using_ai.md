## What is Operator?

Operator is an AI agent that can perform various tasks online, including starting businesses, conducting research, and performing cold email outreach <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:32">[00:00:32]</a>. The Pro plan costs $200 per month <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:05">[00:00:05]</a>. It can log into accounts, browse websites, log data into spreadsheets, and interact with other users <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:15">[00:00:15]</a>. Multiple operators can run simultaneously with separate subscriptions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:49">[00:08:49]</a>, and even with a VPN if outside the US <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:03">[00:09:03]</a>.

The AI can be instructed to perform specific actions and even shown what to do by taking control of its interface, similar to a Loom recording <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:27">[00:15:27]</a>.

## Case Study: Pizza Oven Arbitrage

An example demonstrates using Operator for retail arbitrage with pizza ovens <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:19">[00:01:19]</a>.

The process involves:
1.  **Finding Undervalued Items:** The AI was prompted to find Gozney pizza ovens on Facebook Marketplace that were "grossly undervalued" compared to eBay listings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>. It was instructed to search within 500 miles of Dallas and log promising findings into a blank spreadsheet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:55">[00:01:55]</a>.
2.  **Identifying Opportunities:** Within two minutes, Operator found a Gozney Rockbox for $250, which was significantly undervalued compared to its typical $500 price, especially with included accessories <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:15">[00:02:15]</a>. It logged details like title, location, condition, and a link to the listing <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:38">[00:05:38]</a>.
3.  **Automated Offers:** The user then instructed Operator to message sellers with a half-price offer, stating cash payment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:00">[00:06:00]</a>. While the messaging feature had some initial hit-and-miss results due to Facebook's API <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:46">[00:06:46]</a>, it demonstrated the potential for automated negotiation.
4.  **Scaling the Business:** The vision for this business involves:
    *   Operator researching eBay for average prices of similar ovens <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:08">[00:10:08]</a>.
    *   Automated lowball offers (e.g., $150 for a $300 oven), especially for items listed for 30+ days <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:17">[00:10:17]</a>.
    *   Once an offer is accepted, the user takes pictures and lists the item on eBay for a fair market price (e.g., $250 buy-now) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:31">[00:10:31]</a>.
    *   Upon sale, payment is collected, the original Facebook Marketplace seller is paid, and the end buyer's address is provided for direct shipping <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:40">[00:10:40]</a>. This creates a highly automated drop-shipping-like model <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:56">[00:10:56]</a>.

This type of arbitrage, finding undervalued products in one market and selling them in another, is not new, but AI automates what was previously done manually or with virtual assistants <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:57">[00:03:57]</a>.

### Product Sourcing for Resale
AI can streamline the process of finding and sourcing products from international markets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:12">[00:22:12]</a>.
*   **Task:** Go to AliExpress (or a comparable site like Banggood) to find trending items and message 10 vendors of one item asking for a free sample to be shipped to Texas <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:12">[00:22:12]</a>.
*   The AI attempted to access AliExpress but encountered issues <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:56">[00:22:56]</a>. It then autonomously searched for "AliExpress alternatives" using Bing <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:00">[00:24:00]</a> and navigated to Banggood.com <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:18">[00:25:18]</a>.
*   It identified trending earbuds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:57">[00:25:57]</a>, drafted a message requesting a free sample <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:48">[00:25:48]</a>, and submitted the request after creating an account by generating a strong password <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:26:51">[00:26:51]</a>.

*   This could lead to receiving tracking numbers for multiple free samples upon waking up <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:45">[00:22:45]</a>, significantly accelerating product research.

## Business Potential and Strategic Insights

AI-driven arbitrage represents one of the "greatest arbitrage moments in business" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:28:07">[00:28:07]</a>. With billions of people online and AI connecting global knowledge with task-performing bots, the potential is immense <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:28:10">[00:28:10]</a>.

Key strategic insights include:
*   **Early Adoption:** While AI tools may seem slow or buggy now, getting hands-on experience allows users to understand their nuances and leverage them effectively as they improve <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:30:38">[00:30:38]</a>.
*   **Prompting Techniques:**
    *   Being blunt and direct in commands to the AI is effective <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:32:37">[00:32:37]</a>.
    *   Giving it a specific goal, such as "submit three quote requests per minute," can influence its speed and efficiency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:29">[00:20:29]</a>. The prompt "move faster" also made it perform tasks more quickly <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:23">[00:17:23]</a>.
    *   Instructing it not to ask for confirmation before submitting forms improved workflow <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:16">[00:16:16]</a>.
    *   More detailed prompts lead to better results, e.g., finding "vegan caterers only in Dallas County" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:31:31">[00:31:31]</a>.
*   **Automation:** The ability for AI to run 24/7 in the background, checking in periodically, offers a significant advantage over human virtual assistants <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:26">[00:25:26]</a>.

### Limitations and Learnings
*   **Stubbornness:** The AI can become "stubborn," saying "no" to certain commands, such as changing tone or language <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:21:02">[00:21:02]</a>.
*   **Website Compatibility:** Some websites (e.g., AliExpress) might be inaccessible to the AI, or their user interfaces might be challenging for it to navigate <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:56">[00:22:56]</a>.
*   **Real-time interaction:** While it can take screenshots of its actions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:31:51">[00:31:51]</a>, the ability to integrate these into external documents like Google Docs for record-keeping is still being explored <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:31:57">[00:31:57]</a>.

Despite current limitations, the demonstrated capabilities suggest a future where AI agents play a crucial role in [[building_a_competitive_ai_business_in_the_modern_market | automating business operations]] and uncovering [[innovative_startup_ideas_using_ai_technology | new arbitrage opportunities]]. The relatively low cost of $200 per month for such power is considered a remarkable value given the potential returns <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:28:37">[00:28:37]</a>.
