## The Export Button Theory of AI Opportunity

A core framework for identifying these opportunities is the "export button theory" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:43">[00:02:43]</a>. When a user clicks an "export" button in software, they are essentially signaling that the current software doesn't meet their needs, and they are taking their data elsewhere to perform manual work <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:54">[00:02:54]</a>.

Each export button represents:
*   A workflow breakdown <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:09">[00:03:09]</a>
*   A pain point <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:10">[00:03:10]</a>
*   Manual labor that could be automated <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:12">[00:03:12]</a>
*   A potential feature worth $10k to $30k a month <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:15">[00:03:15]</a>

This insight suggests that where there's an export button, there's likely a workflow breakdown, a pain point, and manual labor that could be automated, presenting an opportunity to create a valuable feature <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:21">[00:03:21]</a>.

## Five-Step Framework for Finding Opportunities

### 1. Identifying Repetitive Pain Points <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:53">[00:03:53]</a>
Observe how people use enterprise software in their daily work and look for patterns <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:00">[00:04:00]</a>.

Common Pain Patterns and AI Opportunities:
*   **Exporting data to reformat it:** E.g., Salesforce data to Excel to PowerPoint.
    *   **AI Opportunity:** Automatic report generation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:14">[00:04:14]</a>.
*   **Copying and pasting between tools:** E.g., Jira tickets to Slack updates.
    *   **AI Opportunity:** Automated status syncing <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:33">[00:04:33]</a>.
*   **Building the same report weekly:** E.g., a Monday dashboard export.
    *   **AI Opportunity:** Self-updating reports <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:48">[00:04:48]</a>.
*   **Maintaining spreadsheets by hand:** E.g., manual inventory tracking.
    *   **AI Opportunity:** Intelligent inventory system <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:09">[00:05:09]</a>.

To find these pain points, you can ask people how they use software, observe your own daily job tasks, or use online tools like Gummy Search (which analyzes Reddit for pain points), X (formerly Twitter), or Instagram to see what people are complaining about <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:25">[00:05:25]</a>.
