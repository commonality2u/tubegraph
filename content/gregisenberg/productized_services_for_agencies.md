## Core Concepts

Productized services for agencies are defined by their ability to generate business-critical results in a specific niche <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:22">[00:02:22]</a>. The shift with AI means these services are becoming more akin to products than traditional services, enabling rapid scaling <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:46">[00:02:46]</a>. Hundreds of agencies currently utilize this model, earning anywhere from $10,000 to $100,000 per month, with some scaling to over $1,000,000 per month <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:07">[00:02:07]</a>.

1.  **Audience Building:** Start by registering social accounts and building an audience to learn messaging and understand the community <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:30:00">[00:30:00]</a>.
