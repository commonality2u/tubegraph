*   **Time Consumption**: Complex prompts can take 10-15 minutes to process, which is longer than some other AI tools <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:04:00">[05:04:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:07:00">[05:07:00]</a>.
*   **Data Validation Depth**: While Manis identifies trends, it may not automatically provide deep quantitative data (like Google Trends data or subreddit growth metrics) without specific prompting <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="22:45:00">[22:45:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="23:38:00">[23:38:00]</a>. Users need to explicitly ask for this kind of support <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="26:09:00">[26:09:00]</a>.
*   **Prompting Skill Required**: Effective use of Manis requires precise and well-crafted prompts. Users need to know the right questions to ask and guide the AI to get the desired depth of information <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="16:14:00">[16:14:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="39:11:00">[39:11:00]</a>. *   **Overly Ambitious MVPs**: Sometimes, the generated MVP plan might be too broad or ambitious for an initial startup, requiring the user to push back and refine for a more attainable niche <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="35:25:00">[35:25:00]</a>.
