## Limitations and User Interaction

While powerful, Manis AI requires thoughtful user interaction:
*   **Prompting is Key**: Users cannot simply give a broad instruction like "build me a million-dollar business" and expect perfect results; they must provide specific and well-crafted prompts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="16:31:00">[16:31:00]</a>.
*   **Taste and Management**: Success with Manis AI depends on the user's "taste" and ability to ask the "right questions," similar to managing a junior employee <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="39:25:00">[39:25:00]</a>.
*   **Data Validation**: While Manis can identify trends, users may need to explicitly request supporting data (e.g., Google Trends data, subreddit growth metrics) to fully validate its findings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="23:03:00">[23:03:00]</a>.
*   **Time Consumption**: Complex prompts can take 10 to 15 minutes to process <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:01:00">[05:01:00]</a>.
