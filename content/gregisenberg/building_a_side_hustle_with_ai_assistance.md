## What is AI Assistance / Operator?

Operator is an [[building_businesses_around_ai_agents | AI agent]] that can be directed to perform various online tasks. It was demonstrated logging into a Facebook account, messaging people, and logging replies in a spreadsheet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:09">[00:00:09]</a>. It functions like a human interacting with a computer, capable of navigating websites, filling forms, and even generating content <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:59:58">[03:59:58]</a>. Users can instruct the AI using natural language prompts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:34:00">[01:34:00]</a>.

## Potential Side Hustle Applications

### 1. Retail Arbitrage

A significant opportunity identified is [[business_opportunities_using_ai_technologies | retail arbitrage]], which involves buying undervalued products on one platform and selling them for a higher price on another <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:17:00">[03:17:00]</a>.

*   **Process Overview**:
    *   **Research**: The AI can be prompted to find specific items on platforms like Facebook Marketplace that are "grossly undervalued" compared to listings on other sites, such as eBay <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:34:00">[01:34:00]</a>. It can log details like title, location, condition, and direct links to promising items in a spreadsheet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:35:00">[05:35:00]</a>.
    *   **Automated Offers**: The AI can be instructed to make offers (e.g., half price, cash payment) to sellers, especially for items listed for extended periods, as they are more likely to accept lower offers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:17:00">[10:17:00]</a>.
    *   **Fulfillment (Partially Automated)**: Once an offer is accepted, the vision is for the AI to list the item on a different marketplace (e.g., eBay) at a fair market price. Upon sale, the AI could potentially manage payment collection and even provide the end buyer's address to the original seller for direct shipping, minimizing physical handling by the side hustler <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:31:00">[10:31:00]</a>.
*   **Example**: An Operator instance successfully found an undervalued "Gsy Rockbox" pizza oven on Facebook Marketplace for $250, which typically sells for $500, and logged its details <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:15:00">[02:15:00]</a>.
*   **Scalability**: This arbitrage model could potentially scale into a six or seven-figure business by applying the same principles to a wide range of high-ticket undervalued products <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:07:00">[03:07:00]</a>.

### 2. Gathering Quotes for Services

AI agents can automate the tedious process of gathering quotes for various services, saving significant time and effort.

*   **Process Overview**:
    *   **Define Needs**: The user provides specific requirements for the service (e.g., catering quotes for 50 people, steak plus two sides in Dallas) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="11:56:00">[11:56:00]</a>.
    *   **Platform Interaction**: The AI logs into relevant service provider platforms (e.g., TheKnot.com) and navigates to contact forms <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="12:14:00">[12:14:00]</a>.
    *   **Automated Communication**: It fills out contact forms with user details and sends messages requesting quotes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="14:43:00">[14:43:00]</a>. The AI can even adjust the tone of the message (though this feature can be inconsistent) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="15:55:00">[15:55:00]</a>.
    *   **Goal-Oriented Prompting**: Giving the AI a specific goal, such as "submit three quote requests per minute," can encourage faster execution <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="20:26:00">[20:26:00]</a>.
*   **Example**: An AI agent was tasked with finding catering quotes for a fake wedding. It navigated to TheKnot.com, logged into an account, found caterers in Dallas, chose a tentative date, filled out contact forms, and sent messages requesting quotes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="12:03:00">[12:03:03]</a>. Emails from caterers were subsequently received <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="28:57:00">[28:57:00]</a>.
*   **Future Potential**: This concept could extend to voice calling services, where an AI agent could call hundreds of vendors simultaneously and deliver quotes in minutes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="13:35:00">[13:35:00]</a>.

### 3. Sourcing Products and Samples

For those interested in product development or dropshipping, AI can streamline the sourcing process from international suppliers.

*   **Process Overview**:
    *   **Trend Identification**: The AI can be directed to find trending items on e-commerce platforms like AliExpress or its alternatives <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="22:12:00">[22:12:00]</a>.
    *   **Vendor Outreach**: It can then message multiple vendors of a chosen item, asking for free samples to be shipped to a specified location <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="22:22:00">[22:22:00]</a>.
    *   **Account Creation**: If required, the AI can even create accounts on these platforms, including generating strong passwords and processing verification codes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="26:35:00">[26:35:00]</a>.
*   **Example**: An attempt was made to use Operator to find trending items on AliExpress and message vendors for free samples. When AliExpress was inaccessible, the AI independently found and navigated to an alternative site, Banggood.com <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="23:46:00">[23:46:00]</a>. It then found trending earbuds, drafted a message requesting a free sample, and created an account on the site <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="25:55:00">[25:55:00]</a>.
*   **Research Integration**: In the future, users could provide the AI with data sets of trends (e.g., Google Trend data) and instruct it to find products and samples specifically within those trends <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="33:01:00">[33:01:00]</a>.

### 4. Cold Email Outreach

AI can significantly reduce the manual effort involved in cold email campaigns.

*   **Process Overview**:
    *   **Email List**: While the AI cannot directly scrape emails for cold outreach (as of the transcript's date), it can send personalized cold emails if provided with a spreadsheet containing emails, first names, and a message <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="33:30:00">[33:30:00]</a>.
    *   **Customization**: The AI can customize messages using data from the spreadsheet, such as first names <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="33:55:00">[33:55:00]</a>.
    *   **Integration**: It can log into a user's Gmail account within its browser to send these emails <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="34:09:00">[34:09:00]</a>.
*   **Benefit**: This functionality can replace dedicated cold emailing software, potentially saving subscription costs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="34:00:00">[34:00:00]</a>.

## Benefits and Limitations

### Benefits

*   **Automation**: AI agents can perform repetitive, time-consuming tasks in the background, freeing up the user for other activities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:05:00">[04:05:00]</a>.
*   **Efficiency**: They can work continuously, potentially 24/7 (though time-outs might occur) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="17:54:00">[17:54:00]</a>.
*   **Scalability**: The ability to automate processes allows for scaling operations without linear increases in human labor <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:07:00">[03:07:00]</a>.
*   **Learning Capability**: AI agents can learn from user input and demonstrations, adapting to specific instructions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="15:26:00">[15:26:00]</a>. Prompting it to "move faster" or "don't ask before submitting" can influence its behavior <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="17:23:00">[17:23:00]</a>.

### Limitations and Challenges

*   **Buggy/Inconsistent Performance**: The technology is still early, and results can be "hit and miss," with occasional bugs or unexpected behaviors <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:46:00">[06:46:00]</a>.
*   **Platform Specificity**: Some websites or platforms might have restrictions or user interface complexities that the AI struggles to navigate or interact with <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="22:56:00">[22:56:00]</a>.
*   **Prompting Nuances**: Effective use requires learning how to prompt the AI correctly, akin to understanding how to direct a human assistant <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:19:00">[07:19:00]</a>. Blunt and direct commands tend to be more effective <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="32:37:00">[32:37:00]</a>.
*   **Ethical Concerns**: The ability of AI to create accounts and interact on behalf of users raises questions about privacy and potential misuse <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="27:14:00">[27:14:00]</a>.
