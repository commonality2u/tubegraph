Developing consumer apps that resonate with deep-seated human desires and existing community "movements" presents a significant opportunity for growth and high monetization <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:43:33">[00:43:33]</a>. ## Identifying Opportunities: The "Cult-like" Framework
The strategy involves identifying niche groups or ideologies that people are already deeply invested in <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:43:23">[00:43:23]</a>. A useful framework for finding these opportunities revolves around three universal human desires:
*   **Health** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:44:00">[00:44:00]</a>
*   **Wealth** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:44:00">[00:44:00]</a>
*   **Relationships** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:44:00">[00:44:00]</a>

By focusing on these core areas, it's possible to find movements that lack a dedicated product <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:43:27">[00:43:27]</a>.

## Case Studies and Examples

### Looksmaxing: Umax
The "looksmaxing" movement is a prime example of a cult-like community focused on improving physical appearance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:41:16">[00:41:16]</a>. This movement has deep internet roots and is driven by insecurities and desires related to dating and self-improvement <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:42:41">[00:42:41]</a>.

The Umax app capitalized on this by providing an AI-powered tool that:
*   Scans a user's face <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:41:54">[00:41:54]</a>.
*   Shows their "potential" and provides scores <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:42:00">[00:42:00]</a>.
*   Offers tips to improve features like jawline, skin, and hair <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:42:08">[00:42:08]</a>.

Umax has achieved several million dollars in annual revenue, primarily using TikTok for distribution <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:42:24">[00:42:24]</a>.

### Wealth Generation: Crayo
Another movement centers around teaching teenagers how to make money, particularly through platforms like TikTok and YouTube Shorts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:44:52">[00:44:52]</a>. Initially, this involved selling expensive courses on how to clip and mass-post streamer content <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:45:02">[00:45:02]</a>.

Crayo productized this educational component into a software <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:45:32">[00:45:32]</a>. Instead of just teaching how to make videos, the app allows users to create videos automatically <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:45:38">[00:45:38]</a>. This caters to the "cult of young money" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:46:01">[00:46:01]</a> and has enabled Crayo to reach over $5 million in annual revenue <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:44:40">[00:44:40]</a>.

## Distribution Leveraging TikTok
TikTok is identified as the largest "underpriced distribution channel" for consumer apps <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:41">[00:01:41]</a>. Unlike traditional influencer marketing, TikTok's "For You Page" algorithm prioritizes content over follower count, meaning users primarily discover new accounts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:33:47">[00:33:47]</a>.

Key strategies for TikTok distribution:
*   **Carousel/Slideshows**: Despite common perceptions, photo carousels (slideshows) made with tools like Midjourney can go viral and are easier to create than videos <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:16">[00:06:16]</a>.
*   **Focus on Content, Not Influencers**: Instead of paying for an influencer's audience, hire creators as "actors" to make good content, even if they have zero followers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:35:41">[00:35:41]</a>.
*   **Multiple Brand Accounts**: If a content format works, create new brand accounts dedicated to that specific style or creator to help the algorithm serve content to the right audience consistently <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:36:17">[00:36:17]</a>.
*   **Learn from E-commerce**: Tactics from the e-commerce space, especially those leveraging TikTok Shop affiliates, can be adapted to promote apps <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:28">[00:02:28]</a>. These affiliates are skilled at creating engaging, converting content <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:54">[00:18:54]</a>.

### Underpriced Opportunities
Analyzing the TikTok Shop Affiliate Center can reveal products selling in high volumes and the creators promoting them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:15">[00:11:15]</a>. The opportunity lies in building apps that these e-commerce creators can use as a utility within their existing content (e.g., a skincare analysis app for skincare product reviews) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:11">[00:19:11]</a>. This can lead to free marketing as the app helps creators make better content <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:30:38">[00:30:38]</a>.

## Monetization and Product Quality
Many successful consumer apps operate on a subscription model, often charging weekly or annually <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:16">[00:15:16]</a>. The ease of "Double Tap to Pay" on iOS has significantly increased people's willingness to pay for app subscriptions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:43">[00:15:43]</a>. While the price point might be lower than physical products, the volume and profit margins (90%+) are significantly higher due to zero cost of goods <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:21:54">[00:21:54]</a>.

### The Importance of a Good Product
Even with excellent distribution, the product must be high-quality and retentive <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:46:54">[00:46:54]</a>. Before scaling to millions of users, it's crucial to ensure the app retains users and provides value <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:47:27">[00:47:27]</a>. Understanding metrics like Customer Lifetime Value (LTV) and Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) requires a product that users value enough to continue paying for <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:47:57">[00:47:57]</a>. Without retention, it becomes an e-commerce model, selling a product only once <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:48:19">[00:48:19]</a>.
