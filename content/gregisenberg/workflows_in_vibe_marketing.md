## General Workflow Formula
The fundamental process for creating [[creating_aipowered_automation_workflows | AI-powered automation workflows]] in [[introduction_to_vibe_marketing | Vibe Marketing]] involves <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="12:06:00">[12:06:00]</a>:
1.  **Pull Data**: Extracting information from various sources <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="12:06:00">[12:06:00]</a>.
2.  **Transform with AI**: Processing and enriching the data using AI models <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="12:08:00">[12:08:00]</a>.
3.  **Analyze Results**: Interpreting the transformed data for insights <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="12:11:00">[12:11:00]</a>.
4.  **Apply Insights to Strategy**: Using the analysis to inform and refine marketing strategies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="12:11:00">[12:11:00]</a>.
5.  **Create Closed-Loop Strategy with Output**: Building a system that continuously feeds back information and optimizes outputs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="12:13:00">[12:13:00]</a>.

## The Future of Workflows in Marketing
In the next 12 to 18 months, marketing will shift from isolated AI tool usage to interconnected AI systems with shared contexts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="19:57:00">[19:57:00]</a>. This means:
*   **Multiple AI Agents & Workflows**: Managed by "manager agents" trained by human experts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="20:05:00">[20:05:00]</a>.
*   **Marketer Evolution**: Marketers will become orchestrators of these systems rather than individual executors <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="20:16:00">[20:16:00]</a>.
*   **Lean Teams**: Small marketing teams will achieve what previously required much larger teams <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="20:21:00">[20:21:00]</a>.
*   **Scalability**: Adding new marketing channels will be as simple as deploying another agent, without needing new hires <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="20:27:00">[20:27:00]</a>.
*   **Consistent Outputs**: More consistent outputs with fewer feedback cycles <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="20:35:00">[20:35:00]</a>.
*   **Goal-Oriented Systems**: Systems will be designed to achieve specific goals, e.g., "get 50,000 new organic visitors monthly" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="20:40:00">[20:40:00]</a>.
*   **Real-Time Adaptation**: Systems will analyze market gaps and create opportunities instantly, resembling high-frequency trading <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="20:48:00">[20:48:00]</a>.
