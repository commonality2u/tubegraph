## Frameworks for Using AI in Writing

### Starting with a Seed Idea
Most writing processes begin with a seed of an idea, which can then be developed in two main ways <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:25">[00:06:25]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:12">[00:08:12]</a>:
1.  **The idea *is* the main piece**: The entire article focuses on this one specific concept <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:32">[00:08:32]</a>.
2.  **The idea is a *sub-point* within a larger piece**: This seed idea becomes one component of a broader topic <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:55">[00:08:55]</a>.
    *   *Example*: Reed Hastings' rule about banning "Blockbuster" could be one of three "strange rules" from CEOs at the start of their ventures <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:08">[00:09:08]</a>.
