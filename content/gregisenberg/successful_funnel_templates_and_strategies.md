## The Productized Service Opportunity

Successful agencies are building super profitable productized services, often scaling to $50,000-$100,000 a month quite quickly, and some even reaching $1,000,000 a month <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>. This success is achieved by generating "business critical results" in a specific niche <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:22">[00:02:22]</a>.

### Key Elements for Agencies:

*   **Niche Focus**: The most successful agencies pick one industry, ideally one that isn't too "techy" and prefers to outsource marketing efforts (e.g., insurance, manufacturing, law, medical, solar) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:24">[00:02:24]</a>.
*   **Business-Critical Results**: Agencies focus on delivering essential outcomes like recruiting new people or generating new customers, which helps maintain low churn <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:54">[00:03:54]</a>.
*   **Productized Approach**: The goal is to "build once, sell twice" by creating a repeatable service <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:31">[00:04:31]</a>. With AI and tools like Perspective, this allows for highly automated services, potentially run by a single founder with minimal team members and a high-profit margin (e.g., 80% profit on $400,000-$500,000 a month) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:49">[00:02:49]</a>.

### The "Killer Funnel" Concept

The core of this strategy is figuring out one specific funnel concept that works for a chosen industry and offer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:41">[00:04:41]</a>. For example, a lawyer agency found success with a "quizfunnel" approach, achieving 40-50% conversion rates on cold paid traffic, which is exceptionally high <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:47">[00:04:47]</a>.

Once this funnel is optimized, the entire service can be built around it, with AI automating copy and other elements based on onboarding surveys <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:10">[00:05:10]</a>.

## Designing Effective Funnels with Perspective

Perspective focuses on optimizing the user experience for high [[optimizing_conversion_through_funnel_design | conversion]] rates <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:47">[00:11:47]</a>.

### Key Optimization Areas:

*   **Speed and Delightful Experience**: Funnels should load quickly and provide an engaging experience, especially coming from fast-paced social media feeds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:50">[00:11:50]</a>. Pages loading faster than 1 second can convert up to 2.5 times more than those loading longer than 5 seconds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:12">[00:12:12]</a>. Perspective funnels are designed for out-of-the-box fast loading speeds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:43">[00:12:43]</a>.
*   **Interactivity (Quiz Elements)**: Many high-performing funnels use quiz elements to make the experience fun and interactive, leading to higher conversion rates <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:49">[00:12:49]</a>.
*   **Lead Qualification & Personalization**:
    *   Quiz questions help qualify leads, saving sales teams time <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:01">[00:14:01]</a>.
    *   Only qualified leads are sent back to ad platforms (like Meta) as conversion events, allowing AI algorithms to optimize ad delivery to the right audience <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:12">[00:14:12]</a>.
    *   Answers to quiz questions can personalize the offer or subsequent content (e.g., showing a different Video Sales Letter - VSSL) to directly address the user's pain point or desire <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:09">[00:20:09]</a>.
*   **Consistency**: The message in the ad must be consistent with the content on the funnel page <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:17">[00:19:17]</a>. Personalization, such as dynamically changing headlines on the funnel based on ad variables, helps achieve this <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:24">[00:19:24]</a>.
*   **Mobile-First Design**: Given that 95% of paid social traffic is mobile, funnels should be built with a mobile-first approach <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:26:12">[00:26:12]</a>.
*   **Integrated Tools**: Perspective allows for direct setup of [[email_marketing_and_outreach_strategies | emails]], integration with Calendly, and offers various blocks for different content types (images, videos, testimonials, countdowns) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:27:08">[00:27:08]</a>.
*   **Simplified AB Testing**: Perspective simplifies AB testing by allowing users to duplicate pages and automatically split traffic, with statistical significance suggestions for results <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:29:04">[00:29:04]</a>. This enables rapid iteration and optimization, potentially even generating B variants with GPT <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:29:28">[00:29:28]</a>.

## Building and Optimizing Video Sales Letters (VSSLs)

VSSLs are crucial for engaging users. When creating them, Niels focuses on four key areas <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:26">[00:16:26]</a>:

1.  **Hook**: The first 5 seconds must grab attention and make people want to watch <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:48">[00:15:48]</a>.
2.  **Education/Value**: Especially in B2B, provide value to ensure users understand the product and are more likely to convert and retain <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:57">[00:15:57]</a>.
3.  **Proof**: Show testimonials or evidence to establish credibility <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:18">[00:16:18]</a>.
4.  **Natural Progression to Offer**: Seamlessly transition from value provision to the call to action <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:26">[00:16:26]</a>.

In marketing, it's crucial to lead with the desired result for the customer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:39">[00:18:39]</a>.

### Startup Application

For startup ideas, it's recommended to:
1.  Build a social audience and understand messaging <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:29:58">[00:29:58]</a>.
2.  Before writing any code, build a landing page or quiz funnel to further understand potential customers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:30:07">[00:30:07]</a>.
3.  Send free organic social traffic to the funnel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:30:23">[00:30:23]</a>.
4.  Iterate on messaging both on the funnel and social platforms <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:30:30">[00:30:30]</a>.
5.  Gather qualitative insights from quiz results (e.g., how users define themselves, what they click) to optimize messaging and identify pain points <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:31:14">[00:31:14]</a>.

This approach provides a forcing function to become proficient at [[building_a_sales_funnel_using_facebook_ads | building traffic]] and converting it, while understanding the audience, before investing heavily in product development <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:30:53">[00:30:53]</a>. Perspective also includes an integrated CRM to collect data and automate messages <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:32:09">[00:32:09]</a>.
