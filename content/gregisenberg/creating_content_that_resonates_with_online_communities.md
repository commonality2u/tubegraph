## Identifying Content Ideas from Online Communities

A powerful method for discovering content ideas is by analyzing trending discussions and popular content within online communities, particularly on platforms like Reddit <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:07:00">[01:07:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:21:00">[01:21:00]</a>. This approach allows you to observe and identify what truly resonates with people <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="20:27:00">[20:27:00]</a>.

### Using Gummy Search for Content Insights

Utilize [[AI tools for content creation and distribution | AI tools]] like Gummy Search (a free and paid tool) to gain insights into subreddits <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:31:00">[01:31:00]</a>.

1.  **Explore Trending Content**: Navigate to the "Top Content" section within Gummy Search for a chosen audience (e.g., "people into dog food") <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="18:31:00">[18:31:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="18:34:00">[18:34:00]</a>.
2.  **Identify Common Patterns**: Gummy Search uses AI to summarize submissions and identify common patterns in discussions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:08:00">[07:08:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="18:38:00">[18:38:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="18:40:00">[18:40:00]</a>.
3.  **Analyze Content Formats**: Observe the types of formats that perform well within the community, such as long stories or memes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="18:47:00">[18:47:00]</a>. Examples of popular story types include "loss of beloved pet seeking attention" or "today I saved a dog's life" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="18:55:00">[18:55:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="19:02:00">[19:02:00]</a>.
4.  **Understand Community Preferences**: Ask questions directly within Gummy Search (e.g., "do they love memes?") to understand the community's preferences and what they care about <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="18:50:00">[18:50:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="19:18:00">[19:18:00]</a>.
