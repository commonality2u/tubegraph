## The HQ Trivia Precedent

HQ Trivia was a mobile live trivia game that gained immense popularity around 2018-2019 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:15">[00:16:15]</a>. Millions of people played it daily, with a host like Steve Rowski conducting live trivia sessions and giving away money <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:20">[00:16:20]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:29">[00:16:29]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:32">[00:16:32]</a>.

### Why HQ Trivia Failed
Despite its initial success, HQ Trivia ultimately "crashed and burned" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:34">[00:16:34]</a>. The reasons for its downfall were attributed to external factors like the unfortunate death of its founder, growing pains, and drug-related issues, rather than the format itself being ineffective <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:39">[00:16:39]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:50">[00:16:50]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:54">[00:16:54]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:01">[00:17:01]</a>.
