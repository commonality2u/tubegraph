## The Power of Owned Media

Owned media, such as a YouTube channel or podcast, provides a platform where businesses have full control over their content and distribution <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>. This control enables businesses to attract individuals interested in their specific offerings, acting as a direct channel for product and service sales <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:05">[00:00:05]</a>. Operating at a "pro level" in today's market necessitates the use of owned media <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:33">[00:00:33]</a>.

Younger generations are actively leveraging platforms like YouTube to generate revenue, which then subsidizes the development of productized services and SaaS companies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:39">[00:00:39]</a>. This represents a new and dynamic [[media_and_content_strategies_for_business_growth | business model]] that is changing how businesses function <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>.

### Why Owned Media is Essential
*   **Direct Audience Connection:** Attracts people genuinely interested in the same topics as the content creator <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:05">[00:00:05]</a>.
*   **Monetization Potential:** Allows for the creation and sale of products and services directly to the audience <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:12">[00:00:12]</a>.
*   **Geographic Flexibility:** Eliminates the need to be located in traditional business hubs like New York City or San Francisco <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:16">[00:00:16]</a>.
*   **Content Control:** Provides the "rails" to put whatever content is desired <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>.
*   **Sustainable Growth:** Owned media channels allow companies to go from zero to one in 30 days, becoming cash-flow positive and self-sustaining <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:09">[00:20:09]</a>.
*   **Audience Leverage:** Once a significant audience is built, the rules of the game change, enabling cross-promotion and owned distribution across various social channels <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:57">[00:15:57]</a>.

### 1. Cricut SVG Collections SaaS Business
An individual successfully built a SaaS business by offering SVG collections for Cricut users <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:54">[02:54]</a>.
*   **Execution:** The individual leveraged [[tactical_marketing_techniques_for_b2b_and_b2c_startups | Facebook ads]] to acquire emails at 50 cents each, then used a drip nurture sequence to upsell customers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:18">[02:18]</a>.
*   **Technology:** The business was built using off-the-shelf tools like Shopify and its apps <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:49">[02:49]</a>.
*   **AI Integration:** Designs were created using AI tools like Midjourney, prompted to generate black and white images, and then vectorized for SVG files <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:34">[03:34]</a>. Keyword searches drive the creation of collections <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:56">[03:56]</a>.
*   **Monetization:** A "freemium" model is used, where users sign up with their email for free products and pay for premium downloads, charging $3.99 a month <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:25">[04:25]</a>.
*   **Market Opportunity:** With 10 million Cricut users, there's potential to scale this into a massive business <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:28">[05:28]</a>. The business is validated and expected to make money with proper execution <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:38">[05:38]</a>.

### 2. LinkedIn Video Content Agency for Founders

    3.  An agency creates clips from the recordings and schedules them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:36">[07:36]</a>.

*   **Pricing Tiers:**
    *   **Entry-level:** ~$2,500/month for content creation and scheduling <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="0:55">[0:55]</a>.
    *   **Mid-tier:** $5,000-$8,000/month for lead magnets as a service, creating a new lead magnet (e.g., a drip course) monthly <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="0:03">[0:03]</a>.
    *   **High-tier:** Design help (banners, cover art, infographics) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="12:18">[12:18]</a>.
*   **Client Acquisition:** Build your own LinkedIn channel to demonstrate expertise and attract founders. Create a free community for founders trying to grow on LinkedIn, then upsell them to the service <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="12:37">[12:37]</a>.

## Key Principles for Business Growth

### Riding the Wave
*   **Attach to Larger Trends:** Companies should look for larger trends to attach themselves to rather than trying to educate the market <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:08:05">[01:08:05]</a>. This ensures selling something people already want <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:14">[01:14]</a>.
*   **First Mover Advantage:** Capitalize on new platform changes (e.g., LinkedIn's algorithmic feed) to gain early traction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:47">[09:47]</a>.
*   **Adaptability:** Even if a trend shifts, a profitable business with an existing audience can pivot to offer new services <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:15:26">[01:15:26]</a>.

### Content Strategy and Audience Building
*   **Consistency is Key:** The hardest part of content is showing up and doing it consistently, not the hours involved <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:00:26">[02:00:26]</a>.
*   **Define Tone and Style:** Spend time figuring out your unique tone, voice, personality, and content format <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:10:02">[02:10:02]</a>.
*   **Pipeline of Ideas:** Establish a system to generate and curate content ideas, acting more as an editor or conductor than a sole writer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:16:00">[02:16:00]</a>.
*   **Founder Energy:** In the early days, founder energy is crucial to "break the laws of physics" and get into orbit <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:18:00">[02:18:00]</a>. Once established, step back and delegate <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:22:01">[02:22:01]</a>.
*   **Founder-Led Companies:** The trend is towards founders becoming media companies themselves, turning their personal brands into powerful distribution channels <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:17:00">[03:17:00]</a>.
*   **Strategic Audience:** It's not about the quantity of followers, but attracting the *right* followers who have high buying power <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:11:29">[01:11:29]</a>. Niche down to target specific, underserved audiences <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:14:51">[01:14:51]</a>.
*   **Digital Gravity:** Build as much digital mass as possible across the internet to create "digital gravity," leading to increased serendipity and inbound opportunities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:18:18">[01:18:18]</a>.
*   **Authenticity:** Be yourself in your content. Trying to mimic others is exhausting and less effective <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:16:46">[01:16:46]</a>. Embrace differences and polarize to stand out <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:18:45">[01:18:45]</a>.
*   **Ungatekeeping Knowledge:** Sharing specialized knowledge publicly attracts interested individuals and builds reputation and leverage <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:13:03">[01:13:03]</a>.

### Tactical Execution
*   **Scraping and Validation:** Utilize tools like Apollo.io for scraping contact information (e.g., Shopify owners), then validate emails with services like ZeroBounce, and automate outreach via platforms like Smartlead <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:06:20">[01:06:20]</a>. This is a crucial component of [[valuable_outreach_strategies_for_gaining_business_partners | distribution strategy]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:06:45">[01:06:45]</a>.
*   **Affiliate Programs:** Consider incorporating affiliate programs for monetization (e.g., airline kickbacks from Google Flights) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:57:19">[00:57:19]</a>.

### VC Involvement
*   **VCs as Customers:** VC firms want their portfolio founders to be media companies, as it generates deal flow and attention <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:46:00">[00:46:00]</a>. VCs themselves also want to be famous <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:46:32">[00:46:32]</a>.
*   **Selling to VCs:** Pitch content creation services (videos, thumbnails, titles) as a value-add to their portfolio, where the VC firm is the customer, not the founder <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:47:57">[00:47:57]</a>. VCs may pay up to 4x what a founder would <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:48:50">[00:48:50]</a>.
*   **Media Arm Advantage:** Having a [[building_a_media_arm_to_leverage_brand_growth_and_influence | media arm]] allows businesses to launch new ventures rapidly and sustainably <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:19:54">[01:19:54]</a>.
