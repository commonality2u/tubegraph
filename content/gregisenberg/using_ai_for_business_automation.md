## Retail Arbitrage with AI Agents
The concept of retail arbitrage, buying undervalued items in one market and selling them at a fair price in another, can be significantly enhanced by [[ai_agents_in_business_automation | AI agents]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:49">[00:03:49]</a>.

> [!example] Pizza Oven Arbitrage
> An example involved using Operator to find undervalued Gurney pizza ovens on Facebook Marketplace that were willing to ship <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>. The AI successfully identified a Gurney Rockbox listed for $250, which was significantly undervalued compared to its typical $500 price with included accessories <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:17">[00:02:17]</a>. The agent logged details like title, location, condition, and link into a spreadsheet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:45">[00:05:45]</a>.

This type of arbitrage business could potentially scale to six or seven figures <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:07">[00:03:07]</a>. The AI can manage the entire process:
1.  **Research:** Use the operator to research eBay pricing for similar items to determine average market value <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:08">[00:10:08]</a>.
2.  **Offers:** Make offers on behalf of the user, particularly on listings that have been active for an extended period, as sellers are more likely to accept lowball offers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:17">[00:10:17]</a>.
3.  **Resale:** Once an offer is accepted, the user would take pictures and list the item on eBay <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:31">[00:10:31]</a>.
4.  **Drop-shipping:** Upon sale, payment would be collected, the original seller paid, and the item shipped directly from the original seller to the end buyer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:40">[00:10:40]</a>.

This process highlights the potential for a largely [[utilizing_ai_for_scaling_and_automation | automated business]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:56">[00:10:56]</a>. While there were initial challenges with the AI directly messaging sellers on Facebook, it was noted that proper prompting can resolve such issues <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:17">[00:07:17]</a>.

## [[using_ai_for_professional_services_automation | Automating Professional Services]] and Lead Generation
[[ai_agents_in_business_automation | AI agents]] can also automate lead generation and inquiry processes, saving significant time.

> [!example] Wedding Catering Quotes
> An AI agent was tasked with obtaining catering quotes for a 50-person wedding in Dallas, Texas, specifying steak and two sides <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:56">[00:11:56]</a>. The agent navigated to TheKnot.com, searched for caterers, and filled out contact forms to request per-head quotes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:14">[00:12:14]</a>.

This process, typically time-consuming for humans, could allow a user to go to sleep and wake up with a dozen quotes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:18">[00:13:18]</a>. The AI can learn from user interaction; for instance, after showing it how to navigate a website, it can replicate the actions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:27">[00:15:27]</a>. The AI can also understand and apply tone requests (e.g., "over-the-top friendly") and instructions to submit forms without asking for confirmation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:13">[00:16:13]</a>. Even requests like "move faster" seemed to make the AI operate more quickly <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:47">[00:18:47]</a>.

## Sourcing and Sample Acquisition
AI agents can streamline the process of finding and acquiring product samples from international vendors.

> [!example] AliExpress/Banggood Sourcing
> The task was to go to AliExpress, find trending items, and message ten vendors to request free samples to be shipped to Texas <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:12">[00:22:12]</a>. While AliExpress was initially inaccessible, the AI independently found a comparable site, Banggood.com <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:52">[00:23:52]</a>. It then identified trending earbuds, drafted a message, and sent a request for a free sample <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:50">[00:25:50]</a>. The AI was even able to create an account by generating a strong password and entering a verification code provided by the user <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:26:56">[00:26:56]</a>.

This demonstrates the AI's ability to navigate complex websites, identify relevant information, compose messages, and even manage account creation, mimicking human browsing behavior <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:57">[00:24:57]</a>.
