## The ACP Funnel

A recommended strategy for [[monetization_strategies_for_content_creators | monetizing creator content]] beyond ads is the ACP (Audience, Community, Product) funnel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:17">[00:12:17]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:41:24">[00:41:24]</a>:
1.  **Audience**: Build an audience around a specific niche or lifestyle <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:21">[00:12:21]</a>.
2.  **Community**: Convert that audience into a community <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:23">[00:12:23]</a>.
3.  **Product**: Develop products or services tailored for that community <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:23">[00:12:23]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:35">[00:23:35]</a>.

This approach emphasizes understanding the audience deeply to create offerings they genuinely need, rather than solely relying on fluctuating ad revenue <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:41">[00:23:41]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:44">[00:23:44]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:50">[00:23:50]</a>.
