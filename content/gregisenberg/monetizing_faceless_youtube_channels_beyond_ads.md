## Limitations of Ad-Only Monetization
Most people operating [[faceless_youtube_channels_as_a_business_model | faceless YouTube channels]] primarily monetize through advertisements <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:18">[00:10:18]</a>. This approach often leads creators to prioritize maximizing views without necessarily picking a specific niche <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:31">[00:12:31]</a>. While some channels can make significant ad revenue from very long videos, like "The Infographics Show" which earns an estimated $10,000-$15,000 per video due to its length <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:31:48">[00:31:48]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:32:07">[00:32:07]</a>, relying solely on ads means income can fluctuate heavily based on the YouTube algorithm and video performance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:00">[00:24:00]</a>.

## The ACP Funnel: Audience, Community, Product
The recommended approach for [[monetizing_a_youtube_channel_and_podcast_for_30000month | monetization]] is the "ACP funnel": building an audience, converting them into a community, and then creating products for that community <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:17">[00:12:17]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:41:27">[00:41:27]</a>. This contrasts with the ad-centric model, which simply aims for maximum views <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:33">[00:12:33]</a>.

Instead of focusing on generating views, focus on what the most impactful niche is, then create [[challenges_and_advantages_of_creating_faceless_youtube_content | faceless content]] for it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:31">[00:00:31]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:39">[00:12:39]</a>.

*   Survival <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:19">[00:19:19]</a>

*   Digital detox/anti-smartphone communities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:31:31">[00:31:31]</a>
*   Specific video games (e.g., Elden Ring lore) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:32:02">[00:32:02]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:07">[00:25:07]</a>
*   Keto diet communities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:54">[00:22:54]</a>

By understanding the audience within a specific niche through content optimization (e.g., scripts, titles, thumbnails), creators gain insights for product development <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:44">[00:23:44]</a>.
