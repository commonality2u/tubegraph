## The "Operator" AI Agent

Operator is an AI agent that can be prompted to perform various tasks, including starting businesses, conducting research, and sending cold emails <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:35">[00:00:35]</a>. Users pay a $200/month subscription for its Pro plan <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:05">[00:00:05]</a>. The agent can log into user accounts (e.g., Facebook, Gmail), navigate websites, fill out forms, interact with sellers, and log data into spreadsheets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:51">[00:01:51]</a>. It functions by automating web browsing and interaction, much like a human, but at an accelerated pace <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:15">[00:05:15]</a>.

## Use Cases for Automated Arbitrage and Research

### Automated Retail Arbitrage with Pizza Ovens

One demonstration involved using Operator to find undervalued Gozney pizza ovens for retail arbitrage <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>.

*   **The Prompt**: The user instructed Operator to find Gozney pizza ovens on Facebook Marketplace that were "grossly undervalued" compared to similar models listed elsewhere, and were willing to ship to Dallas <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>.
*   **Execution**:
    *   Operator logged into the user's Facebook account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:51">[00:01:51]</a>.
    *   It started searching within 500 miles of Dallas <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:55">[00:01:55]</a>.
    *   It logged promising listings into a provided spreadsheet, including title, location, condition, and link <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:04">[00:02:04]</a>.
    *   Within two minutes, it identified a Gozney Roccbox for $250, which was significantly undervalued compared to its actual $500 value, especially with included accessories <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:15">[00:02:15]</a>.
*   **Challenges and Refinements**: Initially, Operator struggled to directly make offers on Facebook Marketplace on the user's behalf <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:24">[00:06:24]</a>. This highlighted the need for precise prompting and understanding the AI's current limitations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:19">[00:07:19]</a>.
*   **Business Vision**: The ultimate goal is to build a six or seven-figure arbitrage business around this concept <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:07">[00:03:07]</a>. The automated process would involve:
    1.  Operator conducting [[data_analysis_for_identifying_market_opportunities | pricing research]] on platforms like eBay to determine fair market value <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:10">[00:10:10]</a>.
    2.  Making lowball offers on behalf of the user for items listed for extended periods (e.g., 30+ days) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:17">[00:10:17]</a>.
    3.  Once accepted, the user would list the item on a larger marketplace like eBay at a fair market price <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:31">[00:10:31]</a>.
    4.  Upon sale, payment would be collected, and the buyer's address provided to the original seller for direct shipping, minimizing handling <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:40">[00:10:40]</a>. This fully automated process can operate continuously <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:56">[00:10:56]</a>.
