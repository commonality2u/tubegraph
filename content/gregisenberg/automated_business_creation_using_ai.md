### Use Case 1: Retail Arbitrage

One significant application for automated business creation is retail arbitrage, which involves finding undervalued products in one market and selling them at a higher price in another <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:21">[00:01:21]</a>.

*   **Process**:
    *   An AI agent was prompted to search Facebook Marketplace for undervalued "Gozney pizza ovens" within 500 miles of Dallas, specifically looking for sellers willing to ship <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>.
    *   The AI was provided with a blank spreadsheet to log promising listings, including titles, locations, conditions, and links <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:02">[00:02:02]</a>.
    *   Within two minutes, the AI identified a "Gozney Rockbox" listed for $250, noting it was undervalued compared to others, as a new one might cost around $500 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:15">[00:02:15]</a>.
    *   The user then instructed the AI to offer half price in cash for the promising listings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:00">[00:06:00]</a>.
    *   While the AI initially reported being "unable to make offers on your behalf," it eventually did message the listings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:27">[00:06:27]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:53">[00:06:53]</a>.
*   **Business Potential**:
    *   This arbitrage model could scale to a six or seven-figure business by applying the same method to various high-ticket undervalued products <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:07">[00:03:07]</a>.
    *   The AI can find products listed cheaply on platforms like Facebook Marketplace (due to convenience for sellers) and identify opportunities to sell them at fair market value on broader platforms like eBay <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:30">[00:03:30]</a>.
    *   This automates a process often done manually or with virtual assistants <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:03">[00:04:03]</a>.
*   **Vision for Automation**:
    *   The AI would conduct pricing research on eBay to determine average resale prices <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:08">[00:10:08]</a>.
    *   It would then make offers on the user's behalf for undervalued items, particularly those listed for 30+ days <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:17">[00:10:17]</a>.
    *   Once purchased, the user would take pictures and list the item on eBay <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:31">[00:10:31]</a>.
    *   For maximum automation, the vision is to have the Facebook Marketplace seller ship directly to the eBay buyer upon payment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:46">[00:10:46]</a>.

### Use Case 2: Automating Quotes and Research

AI agents can significantly reduce the manual effort involved in gathering quotes for services, demonstrating a powerful application for [[automating_business_processes_with_ai_agents | automating business processes with AI agents]].

*   **Process**:
    *   The AI was instructed to find catering quotes for a hypothetical wedding of 50 people, specifying "steak plus two sides" in Dallas, Texas, by reaching out to caterers on "theknot.com" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:56">[00:11:56]</a>.
    *   The AI successfully navigated the website, searched for caterers, switched the location to Dallas, and proceeded to fill out contact forms <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:01">[00:13:01]</a>.
    *   It drafted messages requesting per-head quotes, including details like the desired menu and guest count <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:42">[00:14:42]</a>.
    *   The AI even chose a tentative wedding date (May 11th) when one wasn't provided, demonstrating smart decision-making <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:22">[00:16:22]</a>.
    *   A prompt to "move faster" seemed to increase the AI's processing speed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:23">[00:17:23]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:44">[00:18:44]</a>.
    *   Emails from caterers confirming date availability and providing sample menus were received <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:30:52">[00:30:52]</a>.
*   **Efficiency**:
    *   This automation saves immense time, as manually calling or contacting hundreds of vendors is highly time-consuming <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:37">[00:13:37]</a>.
    *   The AI can run 24/7, potentially gathering dozens of quotes while the user is away <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:56">[00:17:56]</a>.
    *   The AI can also be prompted to perform specific research, such as finding "vegan caterers only in Dallas County" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:34">[00:19:34]</a>.

### Use Case 3: Sourcing Samples and Cold Outreach

AI can also be used for product sourcing and initial business-to-business (B2B) outreach.

*   **Process**:
    *   The AI was tasked with going to AliExpress, finding trending items, and messaging 10 vendors of one item to ask for a free sample shipped to Texas <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:12">[00:22:12]</a>.
    *   When AliExpress was inaccessible (possibly due to regional restrictions or AI limitations), the AI independently searched for "AliExpress alternatives" and navigated to banggood.com <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:51">[00:23:51]</a>. This highlights the AI's ability to "go rogue" and problem-solve <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:57">[00:24:57]</a>.
    *   It identified "Blitzwolf earbuds" as a trending item and drafted a message requesting a free sample <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:57">[00:25:57]</a>.
    *   The AI successfully created an account on Banggood.com using a provided email and generating a 20-character password, which it then entered <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:26:53">[00:26:53]</a>.
    *   The earbuds were chosen because they were prominently featured and had high ratings, indicating they were trending <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:34:43">[00:34:43]</a>.
*   **[[automating_your_marketing_with_ai | Cold Emailing]] Capability**:
    *   While direct scraping and cold outreach were not supported, the AI confirmed it could send customized cold emails if provided with a spreadsheet containing emails, first names, and message content, by logging into a Gmail account through its browser <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:33:38">[00:33:38]</a>. This represents a cost-effective alternative to dedicated cold emailing software <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:34:00">[00:34:00]</a>.

## Implications and Future Outlook

The development of AI agents capable of performing complex, multi-step tasks across the internet marks a significant shift for [[using_ai_tools for_business_growth | business growth]] and [[building_a_startup_using_ai_tools | building a startup using AI tools]].

*   **Cost vs. Value**: While a $200/month subscription might seem high to some, it's considered an "Arbitrage moment in business" when compared to the potential for generating significant income and saving time <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:28:07">[00:28:07]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:30:03">[00:30:03]</a>. The value is realized by those who invest time to understand and leverage its capabilities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:30:10">[00:30:10]</a>.
*   **Learning and Adaptation**: Early adoption is crucial for understanding the nuances of these tools <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:30:38">[00:30:38]</a>.
*   **Prompting Best Practices**: Treating the AI like a direct, blunt, but effective manager, continually commanding it to perform tasks and observing outcomes, is key to learning what works and what doesn't <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:32:26">[00:32:26]</a>. Giving it a specific goal, especially with a numerical target, can also improve performance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:42">[00:20:42]</a>.
*   **The Future**: The capability for AI to independently navigate, interact, and execute tasks across the internet, even adapting to challenges like inaccessible websites, offers a glimpse into [[automating_business_processes_with_ai | the future of business processes]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:41">[00:24:41]</a>.
