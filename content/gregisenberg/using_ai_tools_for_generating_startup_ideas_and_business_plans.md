### Example Startup Idea: Legal Contract Lifecycle AI Agent

Perplexity suggested an AI agent for legal contract lifecycle management <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:51">[00:09:51]</a>.
*   **Problem**: Corporate legal teams waste significant time on manual contract review and suffer from version control errors, costing enterprises billions annually <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:00">[00:10:00]</a>.
*   **Defensible Solution**: An AI agent that integrates with email, SharePoint, and DocuSign, learns organizational risk thresholds, auto-redlines third-party documents using firm-specific fallback positions, and maintains version lineage with blockchain-style audit trails <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:25">[00:10:25]</a>.
