## What is an AI Receptionist?
An [[ai_agents_for_customer_service_and_scheduling | AI receptionist]] is an [[automated_business_with_ai_assistants | AI]]-driven tool trained on a company's specific data to hold conversations with callers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:45:17">[00:45:17]</a>. These solutions can answer repetitive questions by processing information from sources like a PDF of frequently asked questions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:45:25">[00:45:25]</a>. Additionally, they can integrate with a company's CRM to perform tasks such as scheduling appointments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:45:33">[00:45:33]</a>.
