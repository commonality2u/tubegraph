Ahrefs is primarily used by [[keyword_research_and_seo_optimization_using_ai | SEO]] professionals <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:58">[00:04:58]</a>, but its features offer valuable insights for evaluating market opportunities and understanding category demand <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:03">[00:05:03]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:34">[00:05:34]</a>. ### Key Metrics and Features

1.  **Search Volume**: Ahrefs allows users to input any keyword or term to get a general sense of its search volume on Google <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:06">[00:05:06]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:11">[00:05:11]</a>. This helps gauge general public interest and demand for a product or category <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:34">[00:05:34]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:36">[00:05:36]</a>.
2.  **Keyword Difficulty (KD)**: The tool provides a difficulty score from 0 to 100, indicating how hard it is to rank for a particular term on Google <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:17">[00:05:17]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:22">[00:05:22]</a>. A high KD, like 88 for "electrolytes", suggests significant competition and a challenging environment for new entrants to rank highly organically <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:26">[00:06:26]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:29">[00:06:29]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:17">[00:07:17]</a>.
3.  **Identifying Category Demand and Trends**: By examining search volume over time, users can identify rising or declining [[ai_trends_and_market_analysis | trends]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:36">[00:05:36]</a>. For example, "electrolytes" showed a rising trend over years, while "CBD" saw an explosive peak in 2019 after the 2018 farm bill, followed by a steady decline <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:59">[00:05:59]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:01">[00:06:01]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:11">[00:13:11]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:14">[00:13:14]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:18">[00:13:18]</a>. This decline in CBD coincided with the rise of "Delta 9", a legal THC variant derived from hemp, which has seen explosive growth since 2020 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:42">[00:13:42]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:49">[00:13:49]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:00">[00:14:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:02">[00:14:02]</a>.
4.  **Seasonal Trends**: Ahrefs can reveal seasonality in demand, such as huge pops for "electrolytes" in June and July <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:08">[00:06:08]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:10">[00:06:10]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:12">[00:06:12]</a>.
