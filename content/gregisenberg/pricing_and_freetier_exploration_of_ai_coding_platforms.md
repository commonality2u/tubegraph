## General Pricing Structure

Most of the discussed AI coding tools share a similar base [[monetization_strategies_for_ai_applications | pricing]] model for their starter tiers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="15:20:00">[15:20:00]</a>. These typically range from $20 to $30 for the initial tier, with subsequent tiers increasing to $50, $130, and potentially more expensive options <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="15:23:00">[15:23:00]</a>.

Specific examples include:
*   Lovable: ~$20 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="15:52:00">[15:52:00]</a>
*   Bolt: ~$20 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="15:54:00">[15:54:00]</a>
*   Tempo: ~$30 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="15:55:00">[15:55:00]</a>
*   Replit: Pricing is in a similar range <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="15:57:00">[15:57:00]</a>

## Leveraging Free Tiers for Exploration

A key recommendation for users is to extensively utilize the generous free tiers offered by these AI coding platforms <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="15:38:00">[15:38:00]</a>. Many tools have daily or weekly caps on their free usage, which users can navigate strategically <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="15:41:00">[15:41:00]</a>.

> "All of them have great free tiers. <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="15:38:00">[15:38:00]</a>"
> "Play around that, explore, test all these different tools and then whatever tool makes sense for you, you can then choose to upgrade <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="15:43:00">[15:43:00]</a>."

### Recommended Exploration Strategy

To effectively [[evaluating_ai_tools_for_startup_ideation | evaluate AI tools]], it is suggested that users:
1.  **Use Every Free Tier:** Experiment with each tool's free offering <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="16:20:00">[16:20:00]</a>.
2.  **Build a Simple App:** Create a basic application, such as a to-do list app, as a standard test <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:09:00">[09:09:00]</a>. This allows users to assess:
    *   Ease of use <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:24:00">[09:24:00]</a>
    *   Overall experience <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:26:00">[09:26:00]</a>
    *   Encountered roadblocks and ease of resolution <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:27:00">[09:27:00]</a>
    *   Desired level of control (whether they prefer AI assumptions or explicit instructions) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="19:30:00">[19:30:00]</a>

By testing tools in this manner, users can form their own conclusions before committing to a paid plan <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="16:37:00">[16:37:00]</a>. This hands-on experience helps align individual needs with the strengths of different platforms <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="11:30:00">[11:30:00]</a>.
