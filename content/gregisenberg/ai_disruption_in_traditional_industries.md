## Rebuilding Core Business Tools with AI

There is a strong prediction that every calendar, inbox, and CRM will be rebuilt from scratch within the next three years, not just "AI-enhanced" but "fully rethought" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:37">[00:07:37]</a>. This shift is driven by the desire to integrate all communication channels into a single feed, addressing the current complexity of managing multiple emails and Slack groups <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:56">[00:07:56]</a>.

The email client, for example, is ripe for reinvention. Current email platforms often function as overwhelming to-do lists <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:08">[00:09:08]</a>. New solutions aim to leverage [[ai_applications_in_business | AI applications in business]] and improved design to categorize and prioritize information, creating a more intuitive "dashboard" experience that integrates calendars and other functionalities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:27">[00:09:27]</a>. This represents a massive opportunity in a multi-billion dollar industry <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:38">[00:10:38]</a>.
