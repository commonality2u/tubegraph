## The Transformative Shift in Business with AI <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:25:28">[04:25:28]</a>

The business landscape has undergone significant changes, particularly with the advent of AI and the impact of global events like COVID-19 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:44:03">[04:44:03]</a>. This has led to shifts across product, marketing, and operations:

*   **Product:** Traditionally, launching a product involved raising VC capital, years of development, and hiring software engineers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:03:00">[05:03:00]</a>. Now, with AI, products can be built instantly, at low cost, and with [[creating_ai_employees_for_startups|zero employees]] or engineers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:42:53">[06:42:53]</a>.
*   **Marketing:** The shift has moved from seasonal, big campaigns with large budgets and creative teams <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:30:57">[05:30:57]</a> to an "always-on" storytelling approach <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:54:19">[08:54:19]</a>. Solo entrepreneurs are now pushing into the mainstream by transparently sharing their journey and revenue <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:46:17">[08:46:17]</a>.
*   **Operations:** There's been a cultural shift towards remote working, eliminating the need for physical office spaces and reducing costs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:34:03">[09:34:03]</a>. Businesses can operate more leanly with fewer people, leading to faster development cycles <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:56:06">[09:56:06]</a>.

The overall sentiment is that while AI is a buzzword, its power should not be desensitized, as it allows for immediate startup with zero employees and minimal cost <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:54:09">[06:54:09]</a>.

## Sequential Prompting: A New Technique for AI Utilization <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:37:37">[10:37:37]</a>

Beyond conversational prompting (back-and-forth with ChatGPT) and master prompts (one large prompt for a solid output) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:19:04">[10:19:04]</a>, a third technique, **sequential prompting**, is introduced <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:40:02">[10:40:02]</a>.

Sequential prompting involves using ChatGPT to generate a prompt that will then be used in *another* AI tool to produce a different output <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="11:47:04">[11:47:04]</a>. This creates a chain reaction:
1.  **ChatGPT:** Generates a prompt (e.g., for an image) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="11:47:04">[11:47:04]</a>.
2.  **Another AI tool (e.g., Leonardo AI):** Uses that prompt to generate an output (e.g., an image) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="11:51:30">[11:51:30]</a>.
3.  **Subsequent AI tools (e.g., Cling AI, Eleven Labs):** Can further process that output (e.g., animate the image into a video, add voiceovers) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="12:08:42">[12:08:42]</a>.

This sequential process allows for the creation of complex workflows and products by stitching different AI tools together <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="12:20:20">[12:20:20]</a>. It's recommended to start manually to iterate and refine prompts, then automate the process using tools like Zapier or Make, or by hiring an engineer for more complex integrations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="12:42:07">[12:42:07]</a>.

## Business Ideas and Practical Applications

The core idea is to leverage AI for "full workflows" that can be started with zero employees <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:09:16">[03:09:16]</a>.

### 1. AI-First Personalized Greeting Cards and Stories

This idea builds on the existing market of personalized greeting cards (like Moonpig, a $400 million business) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="28:54:19">[28:54:19]</a>.
*   **Concept:** Create custom, animated stories or greeting cards featuring personalized AI avatars <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="26:10:49">[26:10:49]</a>.
*   **Workflow:**
    1.  **Image Upload:** User uploads an image of a child or person <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="17:42:37">[17:42:37]</a>.
    2.  **ChatGPT Prompt Generation:** A pre-structured prompt (available via newsletter or community) is used in ChatGPT to describe the character from the uploaded image (e.g., character name, age, gender, hairstyle, outfit) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="18:10:04">[18:10:04]</a>. ChatGPT can read the image and generate a consistent character description <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="19:27:07">[19:27:07]</a>.
    3.  **Image Generation (Leonardo AI):** The prompt from ChatGPT is fed into Leonardo AI to generate Disney/Pixar-style images of the character <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="20:07:05">[20:07:05]</a>. Leonardo AI can generate high-resolution images rapidly (e.g., 4 seconds) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="22:09:13">[22:09:13]</a>.
    4.  **Story Generation:** ChatGPT can generate narratives or scenes based on the characters and desired scenarios (e.g., "Kaden the brave explorer celebrated his birthday") <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="32:34:02">[32:34:02]</a>.
    5.  **Video Animation (Cling AI):** Images from Leonardo AI can be uploaded to Cling AI to turn them into short videos (e.g., 5-second clips taking ~4 minutes to generate currently) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="50:00:23">[50:00:23]</a>. Cling AI also has a dub feature for lip-syncing words <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="50:30:17">[50:30:17]</a>.
    6.  **Voiceovers (Eleven Labs):** Voiceovers can be added to videos <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="12:12:04">[12:12:04]</a>.
*   **Monetization:**
    *   Premium card sales (e.g., $5-$10, with 20-30% markup for AI personalization) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="33:11:41">[33:11:41]</a>.
    *   Monthly memberships for unlimited cards <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="33:21:02">[33:21:02]</a>.
    *   Upselling related gifts (flowers, chocolates) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="33:26:07">[33:26:07]</a>.
*   **Tools demonstrated:** ChatGPT, Leonardo AI, Cling AI (intended demo, experienced error), Eleven Labs (mentioned) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="11:29:43">[11:29:43]</a>.
*   **Prototype example:** A user interface (Teeny Tales) was built to demonstrate how users would interact with such a product, allowing them to upload images, input details, select themes, and generate stories or cards <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="36:37:37">[36:37:37]</a>. This front-end design enhances the user experience and perceived value <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="38:54:14">[38:54:14]</a>.

### 2. AI-Generated Personalized Stickers

*   **Concept:** Create customized stickers featuring AI-generated avatars of individuals (e.g., teachers, students) with specific messages <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="52:52:43">[52:52:43]</a>.
*   **Workflow:**
    1.  **Avatar Creation:** Use the same process as above (ChatGPT for prompt, Leonardo AI for image) to create a character <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="53:03:07">[53:03:07]</a>.
    2.  **Kiss-Cut Sticker Style:** Adjust the prompt to generate an image in a "kiss-cut sticker artwork" style with plain backgrounds, ensuring it can be easily cut out <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="55:58:39">[55:58:39]</a>.
    3.  **Background Removal (Remove BG):** Upload the generated image to Remove BG (or use its API) to automatically remove the background, creating a transparent PNG sticker <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:00:02">[01:00:02]</a>.
    4.  **Printing & Fulfillment (Printful):** Integrate with a print-on-demand service like Printful (via its API) to automatically fulfill orders for physical stickers or other products (e.g., clothing, t-shirts) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:01:01">[01:01:01]</a>. This allows for a fully automated fulfillment process <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:01:16">[01:01:16]</a>.
*   **Monetization & Marketing:**
    *   Target schools: Offer personalized "well done" stickers featuring teachers' or students' faces <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="53:14:03">[53:14:03]</a>. This could be an incentive for students and a unique offering for schools.
    *   Gorilla marketing: Find staff photos on school websites, create personalized sticker samples, and mail them to the principals to grab attention <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="57:17:09">[57:17:09]</a>.
    *   Social media: Grab display pictures from Instagram or X, generate AI stickers, and DM them to users to drive interest <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:02:02">[01:02:02]</a>.
*   **Tools demonstrated:** ChatGPT, Leonardo AI, Remove BG, Printful (mentioned) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:01:01">[01:01:01]</a>.
