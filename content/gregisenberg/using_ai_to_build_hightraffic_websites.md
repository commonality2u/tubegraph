## Case Study: Guilty Chef (Recipes)

Omar, a UX/UI designer, created "Guilty Chef" as a side project to generate recipes using AI and technology <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:00">[00:05:00]</a>.

### Problem & Idea
Omar wanted to continue sharing recipes without constantly creating videos <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:56">[00:04:56]</a>. He noticed a gap in the market for a single online directory of recipes from various restaurants worldwide <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:07">[00:06:07]</a>.

### AI & Technology Stack
*   **Webflow:** Used for designing and structuring the website and its content management system (CMS) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:06">[00:04:06]</a>.
*   **Chat GPT:** Used to generate recipes from restaurant menus (e.g., KFC, Chick-fil-A) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:48">[00:05:48]</a>.
*   **Zapier:** Integrates the AI content generation with the website publishing process <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:29">[00:05:29]</a>.
*   **AI Image Generation:** Images on the site are also automatically generated by AI <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:37">[00:06:37]</a>.

### SEO Strategy (Schema)
A crucial element was implementing "schema" (structured data) to inform Google about the content <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:09">[00:07:09]</a>. For a recipe website, "recipe schema" defines properties like cook time, nutritional value, and steps, which AI can write automatically and plug into the site <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:49">[00:08:49]</a>. This allows Google to display "rich snippets" (e.g., ingredients for Taco Supreme directly in search results) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:54">[00:14:54]</a>.

### Results & Monetization
*   Within six months as a side project, Guilty Chef achieved a number one search term ranking on Google <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>.
*   The site is ranking #1 for specific menu items, even above major sites like Trip Advisor <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:35">[00:14:35]</a>.
*   Traffic grew from zero in March to potentially exceeding 100,000 monthly visits by December, with almost no time spent on it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:51">[00:11:51]</a>.
*   Monetization includes paid recipe generation (e.g., $2.99 for 10 recipes) and potential future opportunities like sponsorships, partnerships with food brands, and in-content affiliate advertising <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:23">[00:11:23]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:28">[00:18:28]</a>.
*   The site's content is also being indexed by AI engines like Claude and Chat GPT, leading to Guilty Chef being cited as the primary source for certain recipes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:01">[00:17:01]</a>.

### Automation Potential
The process is so automated that Omar can type a restaurant and menu item, and the entire recipe page is generated and designed within a minute <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:07">[00:09:07]</a>. Further automation could involve an AI agent continually adding new restaurants based on specific criteria <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:12">[00:13:12]</a>.
