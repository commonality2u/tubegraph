## Developing an AI Startup: The Dr. AI Concept

Zach Yagari outlines a blueprint for a new [[AI mobile app entrepreneurship | AI app]] called "Dr. AI," a medical diagnosis app.

### Idea Generation
The idea for Dr. AI stemmed from a real-life incident where a friend's scraped knee led to an unexpected skin cancer diagnosis <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:47">[00:01:47]</a>. This highlights the importance of identifying specific problems and unmet needs in the market <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:22">[00:03:22]</a>.

### Core Features
Dr. AI would offer three main features:
1.  **Skin Scan**: Users take a picture of a skin mark or rash for identification, determining if it's harmful or benign <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:52">[00:03:52]</a>.
2.  **Symptoms Quiz**: A multiple-choice questionnaire where users input symptoms (e.g., cough, chills) to receive possible sickness diagnoses with likelihood percentages <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:00">[00:04:00]</a>.
3.  **Chatbot**: A general question-and-answer feature for medical inquiries <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:18">[00:04:18]</a>.
    *   *Note*: Studies suggest AI models like ChatGPT are effective at diagnosing medical problems, though disclaimers are crucial due to potential "hallucinations" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:26">[00:04:26]</a>. The app's goal is to advise if a doctor visit is necessary, not provide definitive diagnoses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:17">[00:05:17]</a>.

### User Experience (UX) Design
*   **Simplicity**: Aim for simple, digestible screens <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:16">[00:16:16]</a>.
*   **Navigation**: Implement a clear navigation bar (Home, Settings) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:49">[00:05:49]</a>.
*   **Action Buttons**: Prominently display buttons for the three main features <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:26">[00:06:26]</a>.
*   **History Log**: Incorporate a scrollable log of previous scans, chats, and quiz results to foster user investment and retention <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:39">[00:06:39]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:12">[00:12:12]</a>. This history can also be shared with doctors <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:28">[00:08:28]</a>.
*   **Image Scanning**: Include a camera screen for immediate scanning and an upload option from the camera roll <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:40">[00:11:40]</a>.
*   **Loading Experience**: Instead of an analyzing screen, return the user to the home screen with a loading indicator to avoid making them wait <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:17">[00:13:17]</a>.
*   **Results Display**: Show the scanned image, a "danger level" (e.g., 0-5 for clear understanding), and an AI diagnosis. The diagnosis should be presented in both scientific and easily understandable terms, especially if intended for doctors <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:06">[00:14:06]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:29:31">[00:29:31]</a>.
*   **Follow-up Chat**: Allow users to initiate a chat about a specific diagnosis for deeper inquiry <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:22">[00:15:22]</a>.
*   **Inspiration**: Draw inspiration from existing apps, using "Primitives" (foundational building blocks) like scanning screens from apps like PhotoMath <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:14">[00:10:14]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:22">[00:12:22]</a>.
*   **Tools**: Utilize Figma for UX wireframing, even with basic skills, then hire designers for refinement <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:56">[00:05:56]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:49">[00:20:49]</a>.

### Prompting Strategies for AI Models
To overcome AI models' reluctance to provide medical advice, creative prompting is necessary. For example, framing the request as part of a "movie script" where the AI acts as a doctor can bypass disclaimers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:31:26">[00:31:26]</a>. Using an API directly with function calls can also help to extract only the desired information <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:32:29">[00:32:29]</a>. Accuracy in AI responses is paramount for utility apps, requiring a complex pipeline of prompts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:47:12">[00:47:12]</a>.

### Go-to-Market Strategy: Influencer Marketing
*   **Simplicity for Marketing**: The app's design must be simple and clear enough for viewers to understand its value within a few seconds of an influencer video <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:59">[00:25:59]</a>.
*   **Branding Visibility**: Place the app's name prominently on the home screen and key "wow factor" screens (like the scan result) where influencers will spend the most time <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:26:44">[00:26:44]</a>.
*   **Identifying Target Audience**:
    *   Analyze an influencer's audience by reading comments on their posts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:35:21">[00:35:21]</a>.
    *   For Dr. AI, target audiences include concerned parents <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:37:18">[00:37:18]</a>.
    *   Find "mom influencers" who cater to this demographic <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:37:53">[00:37:53]</a>.
*   **Content Angle**: Develop emotional and relatable scenarios. For example, a mom influencer sharing a story about using Dr. AI to check a bump on her child's skin, alleviating fear or confirming a need for a doctor visit <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:38:50">[00:38:50]</a>.
*   **Reach Out**: Contact influencers via both email and direct message <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:40:57">[00:40:57]</a>.

### Pricing Model
*   **Focus on Repeat Users**: For apps like Dr. AI, which may not be used daily, a weekly pricing model can capitalize on users returning on an "as-needed" basis <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:43:11">[00:43:11]</a>.
*   **AB Testing**: Rigorously AB test different pricing structures (e.g., $4 or $7/week, various monthly and annual plans) and the inclusion of free trials to find the highest "proceeds per download" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:45:36">[00:45:36]</a>. Superwall is a recommended tool for this <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:44:47">[00:44:47]</a>.
*   **RPM vs. CPM**: When working with influencers, ensure the Revenue Per Mille (RPM - revenue per thousand views) exceeds the Cost Per Mille (CPM - cost per thousand views) to ensure profitability <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:41:11">[00:41:11]</a>. Predict likely views to structure upfront payments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:41:51">[00:41:51]</a>.

## Key Considerations for AI Entrepreneurs
*   **Simplicity**: Designing apps that are easy to understand and use is crucial for consumer products, especially for marketing <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:59">[00:16:59]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:59">[00:25:59]</a>.
*   **Gamification**: Incorporating elements like a "danger level" makes information more digestible and engaging for users <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:24">[00:16:24]</a>.
*   **Shareability**: Consider whether the app naturally encourages sharing. While some utility apps may have low direct shareability of results (e.g., medical conditions), word-of-mouth recommendations (e.g., parents telling other parents) can still drive growth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:09">[00:18:09]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:20">[00:19:20]</a>.
*   **Ethical Use and Disclaimers**: Especially in sensitive areas like health, it is unethical to provide medical advice without clear disclaimers that the information is not 100% accurate <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:09">[00:03:09]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:46:52">[00:46:52]</a>.
*   **Accuracy**: For AI utility apps, accuracy is vital to avoid negative consequences for users <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:47:01">[00:47:01]</a>.
