## The Paid Ads Strategy

The [[challenges_and_opportunities_in_the_paid_ads_market | paid ads strategy]] involves creating compelling advertisements for platforms like [[google_ads_strategy | Google Ads]], Facebook Ads, and TikTok Ads <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:10:10">[09:10:10]</a>. Companies employing this method are often the largest in terms of revenue <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:04:06">[09:04:06]</a>.

A key principle of successful paid advertising is the sheer volume of ad testing. It is crucial to create and test thousands of ads monthly to identify profitable angles and optimize every detail for conversion <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="37:26:27">[37:26:27]</a>. Historically, only large gaming studios like Candy Crush and King could afford to test such a high volume of creatives <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="38:02:00">[38:02:00]</a>.

### The Role of AI in Ad Creation

The advent of AI has democratized this approach, making it possible for smaller teams or even individual developers to test a massive number of ads <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="37:44:00">[37:44:00]</a>. AI allows for the creation of ads entirely from a computer, eliminating the need for actors or elaborate sets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="38:46:00">[38:46:00]</a>.

> [!info] **AI-Assisted Ad Creation Process**
> 1.  **Script Writing:** Develop compelling ad scripts tailored to the app's features and target audience <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="40:31:00">[40:31:00]</a>.
> 2.  **Actor Selection:** Choose from libraries of AI-generated actors representing various demographics and situations (e.g., people in a gym for a calorie tracking app) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="40:50:00">[40:50:00]</a>.
> 3.  **Video Generation:** Generate videos matching the script with selected actors, often within minutes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="41:19:00">[41:19:00]</a>.
> 4.  **Editing & Integration:** Use video editing tools to add app screenshots, remove backgrounds, and combine elements to create final ads <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="41:51:00">[41:51:00]</a>.
>
> This process allows for testing 20 different ad angles in just an hour, quickly identifying which ones perform best <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="41:39:00">[41:39:00]</a>.

### Localization and Niche Marketing

Once an ad strategy proves successful in an initial market, the next step is to scale by localizing ads for other countries and languages <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="42:56:00">[42:56:00]</a>. While starting in the US market is often recommended due to higher conversion rates for subscription apps, significant opportunities exist by targeting specific regions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="42:37:00">[42:37:00]</a>.

This strategy can also involve niching down existing app ideas for specific audiences <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="45:47:00">[45:47:00]</a>. Examples include:
-   A weight loss app tailored for women <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="45:47:00">[45:47:00]</a>.
-   Dating apps designed for specific demographics like Latinos or gay individuals <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="46:02:00">[46:02:00]</a>.
-   A general Bible chat app being adapted into "Bible for Women" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="46:36:00">[46:36:00]</a>.

This approach of taking a proven app idea and wrapping it for different niches or countries is a powerful way to leverage existing demand and achieve growth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="46:08:00">[46:08:00]</a>.

### Examples of Paid Ad Success

-   **Cway (Turkish Studio):** This company makes approximately $8 million in monthly revenue through various apps, including plant identifiers and utility apps like phone cleaners. They leverage AI to create and test a high volume of ads, iterating on angles and localizing them for different countries and languages <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="34:10:00">[34:10:00]</a>. Their "Picture This" plant identifier app alone brings in $7 million per month <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="34:33:48">[34:33:48]</a>. The success of apps like Picture This and calorie trackers highlights how adding AI to existing concepts can significantly increase their value and advertising effectiveness <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="35:33:00">[35:33:00]</a>.

By combining the power of AI for ad creation and iterative testing with strategic localization and niche targeting, app developers can efficiently capture demand and scale their applications to significant revenue figures.
