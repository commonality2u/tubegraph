## Functionality and Benefits

An [[AI agents in customer service|AI receptionist]] is designed to be trained on a company's specific data, enabling it to engage in natural conversations with callers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:45:15">[00:45:15]</a>. Many calls involve repetitive questions, which an AI can efficiently handle by drawing information from a predefined source, such as a PDF <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:45:22">[00:45:22]</a>.

Key features and advantages include:
*   **Data Training** The AI can be trained on internal data to provide accurate and consistent responses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:45:17">[00:45:17]</a>.
*   **CRM Integration** Advanced systems can integrate with a company's CRM to perform actions like scheduling appointments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:45:33">[00:45:33]</a>.
*   **Cost-Effectiveness** These solutions are generally cheaper than hiring human staff <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:45:57">[00:45:57]</a>.
*   **Improved Efficiency and Quality** [[AI agents and automation|AI automation]] can be more effective than human receptionists, delivering higher quality and more consistent outcomes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:45:51">[00:45:51]</a>.
*   **Low Latency** Modern AI systems have low latency, making conversations responsive and providing a good user experience that can be superior to calling a human at times <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:46:05">[00:46:05]</a>.
