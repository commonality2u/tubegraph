*   **Enhanced Market Research and Sentiment Analysis**
    *   [[evaluating_ai_tools_for_startup_design_and_market_research | AI and other tools]] can quickly scrape and analyze sentiment across platforms like Reddit, forums, and Twitter <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:01">[00:13:01]</a>.
