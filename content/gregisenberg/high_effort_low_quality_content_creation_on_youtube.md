From: [[gregisenberg]] <br/> 

For businesses and individuals looking to generate leads and customers without a strong social media following or extensive advertising spend, a strategy involving "high effort, low quality" content creation on YouTube can be highly effective <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:00">[00:00:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:15">[00:01:15]</a>. This approach focuses on delivering immense value rather than polished production, making it accessible even for those who are not comfortable being a public figure <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:36:34">[00:36:34]</a>.

## The Philosophy
The core idea behind this strategy is that "low quality is the new high quality" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:43:51">[00:43:51]</a>. While the visual or audio production might not be top-tier, the content itself requires significant effort and provides exceptional value <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:43:51">[00:43:51]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:44:07">[00:44:07]</a>. The aim is to make people feel "almost guilty for consuming it" due to its depth and utility <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:40:53">[00:40:53]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:41:41">[00:41:41]</a>.

This method is particularly suitable for those who prefer not to build a personal brand or be the "face" of their content <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:36:34">[00:36:34]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:40:05">[00:40:05]</a>. Instead, the focus remains on the immense value and practical application shared through the content <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:39:18">[00:39:18]</a>.

## Practical Application
To implement this strategy, businesses should focus on creating long, in-depth tutorial videos <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:39:34">[00:39:34]</a>. Examples include:
*   **Long-form tutorials:** Videos that are 11, 12, or even 15 hours long, providing comprehensive guides on a specific topic <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:39:34">[00:39:34]</a>.
*   **"From zero to done" videos:** Sitting at a computer and recording a multi-hour session using a tool like Loom, demonstrating how to complete a task from start to finish with minimal edits <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:41:19">[00:41:19]</a>. For example, demonstrating how to build a responsive landing page in Framer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:41:08">[00:41:08]</a>.
*   **Authenticity over polish:** The videos don't need to "look good" or be professionally produced; a simple setup with a camera pointed at a device, or recording from a laptop, is sufficient <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:42:49">[00:42:49]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:43:09">[00:43:09]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:45:10">[00:45:10]</a>. Many viewers appreciate content they can passively watch and learn from, even if it's not highly edited <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:44:44">[00:44:44]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:45:07">[00:45:07]</a>.

A notable example of this approach is the YouTube channel "Mastering Doom," which has "mastered the art of [[creating_highquality_content_to_attract_customers | creating content]]" that is "ludicrously over-the-top valuable" and not focused on individual personalities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:39:06">[00:39:06]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:39:18">[00:39:18]</a>.

## Content Ideas and Sourcing
To find relevant topics for these valuable videos, actively engage with online communities where your target audience congregates <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:40:58">[00:40:58]</a>.
*   **Community observation:** Join groups on platforms like Discord, School, or Facebook, and observe the challenges and questions people frequently ask <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:29">[00:20:29]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:40">[00:20:40]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:41:02">[00:41:02]</a>.
*   **Quora and Reddit:** Utilize platforms like Quora and Reddit, especially niche-specific Reddit groups, to understand common inquiries <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:21:25">[00:21:25]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:41:00">[00:41:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:45:33">[00:45:33]</a>. Creating videos directly addressing top Quora questions is a highly effective way to provide targeted value <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:45:47">[00:45:47]</a>.
*   **Deliver extreme value:** In these communities, provide "extremely like over-the-top better than everyone else's answers" to questions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:21:55">[00:21:55]</a>. If a question is asked that requires a longer explanation, create a video for it and share the link <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:41:37">[00:41:37]</a>.

## Benefits
*   **Lead generation:** By providing immense value for free, viewers who find the content helpful are naturally inclined to explore the company or individual behind it, leading to inquiries and potential clients <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:39:45">[00:39:45]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:42:25">[00:42:25]</a>.
*   **Building trust:** Consistently delivering high-quality, valuable content builds trust and authority within your niche <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:53">[00:25:53]</a>.
*   **Efficiency:** This method can be a highly efficient way to attract customers without relying on traditional advertising or extensive personal branding <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:21">[00:22:21]</a>.
*   **Scalability for software:** For software companies, especially in SaaS, this approach can effectively "sell software" by demonstrating its use through in-depth tutorials, leveraging high margins without needing a polished appearance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:42:13">[00:42:13]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:42:24">[00:42:24]</a>.

While [[building_and_monetizing_youtube_channels | building and monetizing YouTube channels]] with a personal brand and regular content can bring significant leverage and multiply growth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:36:01">[00:36:01]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:36:10">[00:36:10]</a>, this "high effort, low quality" approach offers a viable alternative for [[faceless_youtube_channels | faceless YouTube channels]] or those just starting out without a significant following <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:21">[00:22:21]</a>.
