### Practical Applications of AI Image Generation

*   **Brand Assets and CPG Design**: ChatGPT 4o is becoming adept at mocking up designs for consumer packaged goods (CPG) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:27">[00:06:27]</a>. For example, it can generate high-quality studio images, like an energy drink can with limbs riding a surfboard, which would have cost thousands of dollars from a design agency just three years prior <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:44">[00:04:44]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:19">[00:05:19]</a>. This capability opens doors for service businesses offering GIFs on demand for e-commerce companies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:46">[00:05:46]</a>.
*   **Web and Mobile App Design**: AI tools can generate intricate gradients, components, and characters, making them invaluable for [[generating app assets using ai tools | generating app assets]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:40">[00:02:40]</a>.
*   **Component Libraries**: Resources like 21st.dev provide component libraries that allow developers to easily grab and implement animations and movements, adding "delightfulness and beauty" to web apps <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:02">[00:11:02]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:31">[00:11:31]</a>. Soon, AI agents are expected to access these libraries directly to place components on web and mobile apps <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:04">[00:12:04]</a>.

## The "Sketching Economy" and AI-First Mobile Apps

The rise of AI has led to the concept of a "sketching economy," where the ability to turn simple sketches into production-ready designs with AI tools makes "taste and ideation" the only scarce resources <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:47">[00:24:47]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:55">[00:25:55]</a>. Sketching, whether literal or through voice notes to brainstorm ideas, is gaining value because AI can quickly refine and bring these concepts to life <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:00">[00:25:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:26:00">[00:26:00]</a>.

This trend supports the shift towards "AI-first mobile apps" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:26:27">[00:26:27]</a>. The next wave of successful apps, potentially reaching $100 million annually, will be built mobile-first with AI integrated from the start <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:26:38">[00:26:38]</a>. This includes highly niche applications that can be tailored to specific user types and carved out into profitable businesses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:27:09">[00:27:09]</a>.

A "really smart strategy" is to rebuild traditional products, such as calendars, inboxes, and CRMs, by integrating AI as an unfair advantage and hiding complexity behind familiar interfaces <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:29:03">[00:29:03]</a>. This could transform the multi-billion dollar industry of these tools <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:38">[00:10:38]</a>.

## The Future of Design and Market Dynamics

The cost of custom AI tools and agents is becoming cheaper than hiring humans, even for small businesses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:30:51">[00:31:01]</a>. This suggests that the ideal combination is a talented designer who is also proficient with AI, enabling them to amplify their client reach <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:31:42">[00:31:42]</a>.

However, AI is creating "winner take most" markets overnight, meaning the window to establish oneself as a go-to solution in a specific vertical might be as short as 6 to 12 months <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:27:43">[00:27:43]</a>. The focus for early adopters should be on simplifying complex AI tools for the masses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:28:44">[00:28:44]</a>.

The concept of a traditional homepage may also disappear as AI enables personalized interfaces that change based on user identity, needs, and context, similar to how the ad market became highly personalized <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:35:45">[00:35:45]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:36:20">[00:36:20]</a>.
