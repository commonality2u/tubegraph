### Agent Swarms

A significant advancement in [[automating_business_workflows_with_ai | AI agent]] capabilities is the concept of "agent swarms" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:20">[00:10:20]</a>. An agent swarm allows an AI agent to duplicate itself and handle multiple tasks in parallel, improving reliability, speed, and cost-effectiveness for large-scale operations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:31">[00:10:31]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:15">[00:11:15]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:20">[00:11:20]</a>.
