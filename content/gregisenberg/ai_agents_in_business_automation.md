## Agent Swarms

A significant development in [[AI agents for business automation | AI agent]] technology is the concept of "agent swarms" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>. An agent swarm allows an [[AI agent]] to duplicate itself and handle multiple tasks reliably, quickly, and in parallel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:15">[00:11:15]</a>. This overcomes limitations of single [[AI agents for business automation | AI agents]] failing or losing coherence over long-term, large-scale tasks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:44">[00:10:44]</a>. For example, instead of one agent sending personalized outreach to 500 people over a long time and potentially failing, an agent swarm can deploy a copy of itself to each lead simultaneously <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:51">[00:10:51]</a>.
