## Disruption vs. Disintermediation

While some might expect AI to simply disrupt existing educational models, a more profound shift, "disintermediation," is anticipated <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:38:16">[00:38:16]</a>. This implies that smart teenagers may bypass traditional formal education altogether <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:38:18">[00:38:18]</a>. Instead, they will focus on building audiences, running experiments, and learning at an accelerated pace <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:38:20">[00:38:20]</a>.

## The Rise of the Creator-Entrepreneur

A notable trend suggests that what young people express as a desire to become "creators" is, in essence, a pathway to becoming entrepreneurs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:38:26">[00:38:26]</a>. This shift indicates that entrepreneurship could become the most popular profession <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:38:31">[00:38:31]</a>. Many students might opt out of college to instead run experiments, engage in [[how_to_effectively_use_ai_tools_in_startups | vibe coding]], and [[strategies_for_building_ai_startups | vibe marketing]], actively seeking to generate income <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:38:45">[00:38:45]</a>.

## The Alpha School Model

An example of this educational transformation is the Alpha School in Austin, Texas <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:39:03">[00:39:03]</a>. In this model, young students engage in only two hours of daily work, learning directly from an AI <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:39:12">[00:39:12]</a>. Students in this program have demonstrated enhanced performance and increased intelligence <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:39:17">[00:39:17]</a>.

Ironically, many of these students become creators, with some starting companies (e.g., food trucks) as early as 14 or 15 years old <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:39:23">[00:39:23]</a>. This suggests that AI could lead to the emergence of "luxury schools" that surpass current private school standards <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:39:37">[00:39:37]</a>.

## Entrepreneurship as Job Security

The notion that being an entrepreneur is a safer career path than traditional employment is gaining traction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:39:54">[00:39:54]</a>. With strong foundational skills, individuals can leverage [[ai_as_a_replacement_for_traditional_workforce | AI]] to remain adaptable and resilient to economic changes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:40:04">[00:40:04]</a>. It is suggested that teaching children to be adaptive, especially from a young age, is crucial in this evolving landscape <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:40:17">[00:40:17]</a>.
