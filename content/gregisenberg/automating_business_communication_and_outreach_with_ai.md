## Retail Arbitrage and Market Communication
One application involves using an AI agent for retail arbitrage, specifically identifying undervalued products on one platform to resell on another <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:19:07">[01:19:07]</a>. An AI agent can:
*   **Search for undervalued items** on platforms like Facebook Marketplace, even specifying search parameters like location and willingness to ship <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:37:00">[01:37:00]</a>.
*   **Log details** of promising listings into a spreadsheet, including title, location, condition, and links <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:02:02">[02:02:02]</a>.
*   **Initiate communication** by attempting to message sellers with an offer, for example, offering half the listed price and stating intent to pay cash <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:59:00">[05:59:00]</a>. While this function can be buggy, it highlights the potential for automated negotiation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:46:00">[06:46:00]</a>.
*   **Scale operations** to build a six or seven-figure business by applying this arbitrage technique to various high-ticket products <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:07:00">[03:07:00]</a>. The automation removes the need for human virtual assistants who traditionally perform these tasks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:00:00">[04:00:00]</a>.

## Streamlining Service Quote Acquisition
Another significant [[use_cases_for_ai_in_business_automation | use case]] is automating the process of gathering quotes for services, which can be time-consuming for individuals or businesses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:37:00">[01:37:00]</a>.
*   An AI agent can be prompted to find catering quotes for an event, specifying details like guest count, menu preferences (e.g., steak plus two sides), and location <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="11:56:00">[11:56:00]</a>.
*   The AI can log into dedicated service platforms (like The Knot.com), search for vendors, and fill out contact forms to request quotes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="12:14:00">[12:14:00]</a>.
*   It can intelligently choose a tentative date if none is provided, as it understands a date is required for the form <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="16:22:00">[16:22:00]</a>.
*   Users can instruct the AI to change the tone of communication (e.g., "over-the-top friendly" or "very rude"), although the AI might have limitations on applying extreme tones or changing languages <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="15:55:00">[15:55:00]</a>.
*   The AI can be commanded to move faster or submit requests at a specific rate, which can significantly speed up the quote collection process <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="17:23:00">[17:23:00]</a>.

## Automated Sourcing and Sample Requests
For product-based businesses, AI can revolutionize the sourcing process, especially for requesting samples from international vendors:
*   An AI agent can navigate e-commerce sites like AliExpress or Banggood.com to find trending items <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="22:12:00">[22:12:17]</a>.
*   It can then message multiple vendors of a chosen item to request free samples to be shipped to a specified address <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="22:22:00">[22:22:00]</a>.
*   If an initial website is inaccessible, the AI can independently search for comparable alternative sites and continue the task <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="23:46:00">[23:46:00]</a>. This self-directed problem-solving capability hints at the [[potential_of_ai_tools_in_business_automation and growth | potential of AI tools]] in more complex scenarios <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="24:57:00">[24:57:00]</a>.
*   The AI can create accounts on new platforms, generate strong passwords, and handle verification codes, further automating the process <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="26:35:00">[26:35:00]</a>.
*   Once logged in, it can find messaging interfaces, draft polite requests, and confirm submission, streamlining the outreach for product samples <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="25:41:00">[25:41:00]</a>.

## Cold Emailing and Prompting Best Practices
Beyond specific examples, AI agents demonstrate broader [[automating_marketing_with_ai | marketing automation]] and communication capabilities:
*   An AI can send [[automating_marketing_with_ai | cold emails]] by processing a provided spreadsheet containing emails and first names, customizing messages for each recipient <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="33:47:00">[33:47:00]</a>. This requires logging into an email account (e.g., Gmail) within the AI's browser <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="34:06:00">[34:06:00]</a>.
*   For effective [[building_and_using_aipowered_tools_for_business_processes | building and using AI-powered tools]], prompting practices are crucial. Treating the AI like a human, but with blunt and direct commands, tends to yield better results <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="32:26:00">[32:26:00]</a>. Giving the AI specific goals, such as a numerical target (e.g., "submit three quote requests per minute"), can also improve performance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="20:32:00">[20:32:00]</a>.
