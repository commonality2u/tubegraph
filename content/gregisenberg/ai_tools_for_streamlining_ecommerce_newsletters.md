## Pain Points and Opportunities
E-commerce businesses often struggle with creating engaging content for their newsletters, particularly in determining how to effectively talk about their products <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:11">[00:11:11]</a>. Another common challenge is the design process of email newsletters, as many e-commerce companies still hardcode their emails, leading to complex and error-prone design work involving "tables within tables" of code <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:45">[00:11:45]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:04">[00:12:04]</a>.

An opportunity exists to create AI-powered solutions that address these challenges <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:14">[00:13:14]</a>.

## Potential AI Applications
AI tools could provide various functionalities to streamline e-commerce newsletters:

*   **Design and Template Modification**
    *   An AI chat feature could help design email newsletters, similar to a tool like Vercel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:27">[00:11:27]</a>.
    *   Users could chat with the AI to insert specific products into predefined templates <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:41">[00:11:41]</a>.
    *   The AI could modify existing email templates to promote products effectively <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:26">[00:12:26]</a>.

*   **Content Generation and Storytelling**
    *   AI could assist in writing newsletters by generating content related to upcoming holidays or events <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:40">[00:12:40]</a>.
    *   It could identify relevant products from a catalog for a specific event <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:01">[00:13:01]</a>.
    *   The AI could then craft a narrative or "storytelling format" around those products <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:46">[00:12:46]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:11">[00:13:11]</a>. This helps businesses present their products from different angles, avoiding repetitive messaging <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:39">[00:15:39]</a>.
