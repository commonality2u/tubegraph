## When to Implement SEO

SEO, unlike other growth channels, offers delayed gratification, meaning it takes a long time to generate traffic <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:52:50">[00:52:50]</a>. Therefore, it is not recommended for a startup's day one <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:52:47">[00:52:47]</a>. David Park advises focusing on it after establishing a steady income stream and achieving some level of product-market fit (PMF) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:58:29">[00:58:29]</a>. Initial traffic can be more quickly generated through channels like influencer marketing and user-generated content (UGC) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:53:02">[00:53:02]</a>.

### Leveraging Other Channels for SEO Benefits
When users see engaging content about a product on platforms like TikTok, they often don't click directly on the link in the bio <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:54:12">[00:54:12]</a>. Instead, they swipe up, go to Google, and search for the product's name <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:54:26">[00:54:26]</a>. This increase in brand name searches organically boosts the product's legitimacy in the eyes of Google, providing a passive SEO benefit <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:54:37">[00:54:37]</a>.

## David Park's Unique SEO Strategy

David Park's approach to SEO was unconventional: he acquired an entire SEO agency to become their sole client, ensuring dedicated attention to Jenny AI's SEO efforts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:52:02">[00:52:02]</a>. This reflects a philosophy of deeply investing in spectacular talent <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:52:25">[00:52:25]</a>.

### Key SEO Tactics
His in-house SEO team employs several strategies:
*   **Targeting Featured Snippets**: Instead of aiming to incrementally climb search rankings, they focus on identifying and creating the perfect answer for featured snippets. This allows them to "leapfrog" directly to the top spot and gain significant visibility <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:53:15">[00:53:15]</a>.
*   **Valuable Keyword Focus**: It's crucial to prioritize keywords that indicate high buying intent, rather than those focused on "free" solutions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:55:37">[00:55:37]</a>. Traffic volume alone can be a vanity metric if it doesn't lead to conversions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:55:15">[00:55:15]</a>.
*   **Accurate ROI Estimation**: Continuously evaluate the return on investment from search engine traffic to ensure profitability <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:55:48">[00:55:48]</a>.
*   **Content and Tools**: Implement various content strategies including creating mini-tools, free tools, and landing pages in multiple languages <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:56:08">[00:56:08]</a>. They also write a lot of content <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:56:17">[00:56:17]</a>.

## Investment in SEO
At scale, Jenny AI dedicates tens of thousands of dollars per month to increasing its SEO presence <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:56:03">[00:56:03]</a>. This substantial investment demonstrates that SEO has become a main pillar of their growth strategy <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:55:58">[00:55:58]</a>.
