## The Rise of AI Agents
AI agents, especially voice-based large language models (LLMs), are demonstrating remarkable capabilities in automating customer interactions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:26">[00:05:26]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:30">[00:05:30]</a>. For instance, in a demo, an AI agent seamlessly handled dental appointment scheduling, including initial calls, setting next appointments, and rescheduling requests <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:00">[00:00:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:09">[00:00:09]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:14">[00:00:14]</a>. These agents are becoming so sophisticated that they may soon be able to close customers directly <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:17">[00:01:17]</a>.

A recent report highlighted the significant impact of AI agents: a company added 700 new AI agents, leading to an estimated $40 million increase in profit <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:32">[00:06:32]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:35">[00:06:35]</a>. These agents managed 2.3 million chats and operated 80-90% faster than human counterparts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:40">[00:06:40]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:42">[00:06:42]</a>.

This presents a huge opportunity for agencies to approach companies with large customer service teams and offer to save them millions per month by implementing AI agents for customer service <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:50">[00:06:50]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:57">[00:06:57]</a>. While a pay-for-performance model based on cost savings is attractive <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:00">[00:07:00]</a>, some experts suggest focusing on the value generated (e.g., X more customers) and charging upfront for potentially higher returns <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:33">[00:07:33]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:44">[00:07:44]</a>.

## New Opportunities for Agencies
The integration of AI creates several new agency models and enhances existing ones:

*   **AI Transformation Agencies**: Just as digital transformation agencies emerged, there's a clear need for agencies specializing in guiding businesses through [[ai_integration_in_traditional_industries | AI transformation]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:52">[00:25:52]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:56">[00:25:56]</a>.
*   **Pay-for-Performance Models**: This model, where agencies only get paid if they deliver results, is gaining renewed interest, especially with AI enabling marketers to better underwrite opportunities due to more data <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:55">[00:01:55]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:03">[00:02:03]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:23">[00:02:23]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:27">[00:02:27]</a>. This includes:
    *   **Lead Generation Agencies**: Agencies driving leads (e.g., through Google Ads) for high-value industries like finance, crypto, or hotels, where clients pay a high cost per acquisition (CPA) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:05">[00:03:05]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:41">[00:03:41]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:00">[00:04:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:06">[00:04:06]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:10">[00:04:10]</a>.
    *   **Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) Agencies**: An agency in Atlanta operates on a pay-for-performance model, taking a percentage of the increased revenue if they double a client's e-commerce conversion rate <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:51">[00:08:51]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:03">[00:09:03]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:07">[00:09:07]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:12">[00:09:12]</a>.
*   **AI-Assisted Content and Programmatic SEO**: [[role_of_ai_in_marketing_and_business_growth | AI-assisted content]] and programmatic SEO are becoming key offerings for agencies, especially within a 1-2 year window before search landscapes shift <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:26:03">[00:26:03]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:26:06">[00:26:06]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:26:10">[00:26:10]</a>.
*   **Creative Agencies**: With AI automating many tasks, the importance of strong, high-quality creative work is becoming an even greater "X-factor" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:26:19">[00:26:19]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:26:22">[00:26:22]</a>. Finding a good creative agency is increasingly difficult, highlighting a market need <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:26:25">[00:26:25]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:26:27">[00:26:27]</a>.

## The Enduring Value of Agencies in an AI World
Despite the rapid advancements in AI, agencies remain a robust business model. Even though they may be perceived as "boring" or "not sexy," agencies can achieve significant profits and valuations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:15">[00:01:15]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:18">[00:01:18]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:28">[00:24:28]</a>. An agency doing $5 million in EBITDA could be valued at $15-20 million, potentially becoming a $100 million business <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:53">[00:23:53]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:55">[00:23:55]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:00">[00:24:00]</a>. For agencies generating $5-20 million in annual profit, that profit itself represents an "exit" opportunity every year <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:04">[00:24:04]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:07">[00:24:07]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:10">[00:24:10]</a>.

AI co-pilots are making developers 4-8 times more effective, enabling agencies to build complex solutions with greater efficiency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:39">[00:14:39]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:41">[00:14:41]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:43">[00:14:43]</a>. This, combined with AI's deflationary effects on service costs, makes agencies an appealing model for building "generational" businesses that compound value over decades <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:20">[00:24:20]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:25">[00:24:25]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:40">[00:24:40]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:42">[00:24:42]</a>. Agencies also serve as effective engines to incubate and launch new software products <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:37">[00:24:37]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:45">[00:24:45]</a>.

Ultimately, agencies that focus on a niche, have a clear vision, and possess "taste" in product design (i.e., creating excellent user experiences) are well-positioned for success in the evolving AI landscape <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:47">[00:14:47]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:50">[00:14:50]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:05">[00:15:05]</a>.
