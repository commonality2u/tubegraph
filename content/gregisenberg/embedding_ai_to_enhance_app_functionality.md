## Approaches to Building AI Apps

There are two primary ways to approach [[building apps with AI tools | AI app development]] <a class="yt-0timestamp" data-t="00:04:23">[00:04:23]</a>:

1.  **Completely New Idea**: This involves conceiving a unique product that leverages AI for a novel purpose <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:29">[00:04:29]</a>.
2.  **Adding an "AI Sizzle"**: This approach focuses on enhancing existing popular app concepts by integrating AI features <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:51">[00:04:51]</a>. This is often an easier path, as it taps into existing demand <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:01">[00:05:01]</a>.

## Case Study: Verse App – A Unique AI Idea

The Verse app, which gained significant traction, transforms Spotify playlists into visual "vision boards" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:34">[00:01:34]</a>. These boards feature beautiful covers, reminiscent of a kid's designed wall with posters <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:45">[00:01:45]</a>.

### Key Insights:

*   **Unique Concept**: The app's idea is highly unique and difficult to replicate consistently <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:19">[00:02:19]</a>.
*   **Embedded Marketing Flow**: Verse embeds its marketing directly into the app's concept <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:35">[00:02:35]</a>. Once a vision board is created, the natural next step is to share it, either via iMessage with a well-designed invite flow or through TikTok <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:54">[00:02:54]</a>. This strategy contributed to its viral success on TikTok and its ranking in the top 8 of the App Store <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:03">[00:02:03]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:56">[00:03:56]</a>.
*   **Product Complexity**: While innovative, Verse is a challenging product to build <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:19">[00:04:19]</a>.

## Case Study: Umax – AI Calorie Tracking App

Umax is an [[innovative_ai_functionalities_in_apps | AI-powered calorie tracking app]] created by a 17-year-old who taught himself to code using ChatGPT <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:48">[00:07:48]</a>.

### Key Aspects:

*   **Simplicity**: The app's design is straightforward, guiding users to its core feature and payment options <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:01">[00:08:01]</a>.
*   **AI Functionality**: Users take a picture of their meal, and the app uses AI to provide calorie information <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:31:58">[00:31:58]</a>. This feature significantly improves upon older calorie tracking apps that lacked effective image recognition <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:36:29">[00:36:29]</a>.
*   **Organic Growth**: Umax grew primarily through organic TikTok marketing <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:22">[00:08:22]</a>. The strategy involved:
    *   **Micro-Influencers**: Partnering with small creators on TikTok and Instagram to promote the app <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:29:50">[00:29:50]</a>.
    *   **Viral Content**: Creating native-feeling content that integrates the app subtly <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:45">[00:24:45]</a>. Often, users close the app and search for its name in the App Store rather than clicking a link <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:31:17">[00:31:17]</a>.
    *   **Founder-Led Content**: The founder actively creates viral videos on TikTok, sometimes filming outside his high school <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:33:08">[00:33:08]</a>.
*   **Impressive Results**: Umax achieved $400,000 in monthly revenue and 200,000 downloads in a single month <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:29:52">[00:29:52]</a>.

## Mobile App Growth Strategies

Four main strategies exist for growing a mobile app <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:44">[00:08:44]</a>:

### 1. Organic TikTok / Short Video Platforms

This strategy involves generating free traffic from platforms like TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:08">[00:24:08]</a>.

*   **Content Focus**: The goal is to create smart, organic-feeling videos that showcase the product and gain views <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:22">[00:24:22]</a>.
*   **Repeatability**: Successful apps find a repeatable way to create content that drives download spikes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:26">[00:25:26]</a>.
*   **VC Analogy**: This method can be compared to venture capitalism, where many content pieces might fail, but one viral hit pays for the rest <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:32:24">[00:32:24]</a>.

### 2. Apple Search Optimization (ASO) & Apple Search Ads (ASA)

ASO is the practice of ranking highly when users search for keywords in the App Store <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:21">[00:19:21]</a>.

*   **Keyword Strategy**: The challenge is to find keywords with sufficient search volume but low competition <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:37">[00:13:37]</a>.
    *   **AI Integration**: Adding "AI" to a keyword can help capture demand if people are interested in the space (e.g., "AI calorie counter" vs. "calorie counter") <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:55">[00:16:55]</a>.
    *   **Trend Research**: Use tools like Google Trends, Ahrefs, and App Figures to identify broader trends and then check App Store competition <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:01">[00:20:01]</a>.
*   **ASO Techniques**:
    *   **Keyword in Title**: Including target keywords in the app's title <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:42">[00:17:42]</a>.
    *   **Conversion Rate**: Optimizing the app page to encourage downloads after a visit <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:01">[00:18:01]</a>.
    *   **Reviews and Growth Rate**: Maintaining a high number of positive reviews and a strong growth rate <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:13">[00:18:13]</a>.
    *   **Launch Boost**: Apple provides an initial boost to new apps; a high conversion rate during this period leads to sustained high rankings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:20">[00:18:20]</a>.
    *   **Paid Ads for ASO**: Running ads, even if not immediately profitable, can drive traffic and reviews, which in turn boosts ASO rankings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:53">[00:18:53]</a>.
*   **Example: Remone Holding**: This company specializes in identifying niche keywords like "fan noise," "green noise," or "anime girlfriend AI" and [[developing_ai_character_apps_with_customization | developing AI character apps with customization]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:31">[00:15:31]</a>. They aren't reinventing concepts but are adept at capturing existing search demand by adding an AI "sizzle" to them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:23">[00:16:23]</a>.

### 3. Paid Ads

This strategy involves running targeted ads on platforms like Facebook, TikTok, and Google <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:06">[00:09:06]</a>.

*   **Iterative Testing**: Success in paid ads requires extensive testing. Companies need to create and test thousands of ad creatives to find profitable angles <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:37:24">[00:37:24]</a>.
*   **AI for Ad Creation**: AI tools (like Arc Ads) have revolutionized this by making it feasible to generate thousands of ad variations from a computer without needing actors or extensive production <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:38:38">[00:38:38]</a>. This capability was previously limited to large gaming studios <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:38:02">[00:38:02]</a>.
    *   **Process**: Write a script, select AI actors/voices, and generate video ads in minutes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:40:28">[00:40:28]</a>. These can then be further edited with app screenshots or background removal <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:41:51">[00:41:51]</a>.
*   **Localization**: Once an ad works in one market (e.g., US), AI can be used to localize it into multiple languages and target other countries, significantly expanding reach <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:43:07">[00:43:07]</a>.
*   **Example: PictureThis (Plant Identifier)**: This app makes $7 million in monthly revenue, largely through paid ads <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:34:33">[00:34:33]</a>. They leverage AI to identify plants from photos <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:35:05">[00:35:05]</a>. They have tested over 30,000 video creatives to optimize their ad performance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:37:37">[00:37:37]</a>.
*   **Example: Turkish Studio (Cleanup, Storage Cleaner, AI Tutor, AI Art Generator)**: This studio generates $8 million in monthly revenue by using AI to create a high volume of ads across different angles and languages for a range of apps <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:43:14">[00:43:14]</a>.

### 4. Product-Led Growth (Virality)

This strategy involves embedding features within the app that encourage users to invite others, creating a multiplier effect on marketing efforts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:43">[00:09:43]</a>.

*   **Invite Flows**: Well-designed invite flows encourage users to share their creations or invite friends <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:02">[00:03:02]</a>.
*   **Case Study: Islands (Social App for College Students)**: A social app for college students, Islands, required 5% of a school to pre-sign up before launching <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:43">[00:10:43]</a>. Before joining, users were prompted to invite at least three people, resulting in an average of 2.7 invites per user and rapid growth from 5% to 20% penetration <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:04">[00:11:04]</a>.
*   **AI's Role**: AI can enable highly targeted initial ad campaigns to acquire the first small percentage of users for niche viral apps <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:43">[00:12:43]</a>. For example, creating specific ads for individual high schools ("Hey, you are Carl Catholic High School! Did you hear about these new things?") is scalable with AI, unlike relying on influencers in every school <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:46">[00:12:46]</a>.

## General Strategies for AI App Ideas

*   **Trend Spotting**: Find trends outside the App Store (e.g., Reddit, TikTok trends like "looksmaxing" that inspired Umax) that can be developed into an app <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:21:56">[00:21:56]</a>. Similarly, look for popular web products (e.g., Suno AI for song creation) and replicate their functionality in a mobile app using APIs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:26">[00:22:26]</a>.
*   **Niche Down Existing Ideas**: Take a proven app concept and specialize it for a specific audience or geography <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:45:51">[00:45:51]</a>.
    *   **Geographic Niching**: Develop localized versions of apps (e.g., "Plant AI for Canadians" with local branding and ads) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:44:42">[00:44:42]</a>.
    *   **Audience Niching**: Target specific demographics with existing app concepts (e.g., a "weight loss app for women," dating apps for Latinos or gay people, or "Bible for women" based on a general Bible chat app) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:45:47">[00:45:47]</a>. This allows for focused marketing and product development.

By combining [[integration_of_ai_in_app_development_and_marketing | AI integration]] with smart growth strategies, developers can create profitable and scalable app businesses.
