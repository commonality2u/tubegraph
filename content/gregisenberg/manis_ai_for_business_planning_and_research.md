## How Manis AI Works

Manis AI operates using a multi-agent browsing capability, which allows it to multitask and perform multiple operations simultaneously <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:51">[00:15:51]</a>. When given a complex prompt for business planning, it typically takes 10 to 15 minutes to generate results <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:04">[00:05:04]</a>. Users can monitor the live progress of Manis AI's tasks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:31:51">[00:31:51]</a>. Additionally, users can take control of Manis's computer to directly interact with applications like VS Code or the browser <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:05">[00:10:05]</a>. The platform also includes "knowledge suggestions" to learn user preferences and apply best practices for future tasks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:30:56">[00:30:56]</a>.

## Limitations and Areas for Improvement

*   **Prompt Engineering Dependency:** Manis AI requires specific and well-crafted prompts. It cannot independently "go wild" to generate optimal results; users need to guide it with clear questions and direction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:31">[00:16:31]</a>.
*   **Data Transparency:** While it identifies trends, it may not always immediately provide raw supporting data like Google Trends data or detailed subreddit growth metrics, requiring further prompting for validation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:03">[00:23:03]</a>.
*   **MVP Scope:** The initial Minimal Viable Product (MVP) proposed by Manis AI might be too broad for a true startup, requiring the user to refine it to a more attainable and niche focus <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:35:25">[00:35:25]</a>.
*   **"Taste" and Management:** Users need to have "taste" and be willing to "push back" on Manis AI's suggestions to refine outcomes, much like mentoring a junior employee <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:39:54">[00:39:54]</a>.
*   **Legal Disclaimer:** While it can review contracts, it is not a substitute for a lawyer, and users should exercise caution with sensitive legal information <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:00">[00:09:00]</a>.
*   **Accessibility and Security:** Manis AI is currently invite-only <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:40:24">[00:40:24]</a>. Downloading and running code from the platform, especially from a foreign server, requires caution and potentially a developer's review <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:37:00">[00:37:00]</a>.
