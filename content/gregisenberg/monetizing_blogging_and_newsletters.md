## Agency Model for Setup and Monetization

Rather than creating a new platform to compete with established services that have raised significant capital <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:24">[00:09:24]</a>, an effective approach is an agency model <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:09">[00:09:09]</a>. This model would involve working on top of existing services like:
*   [[growing_and_monetizing_a_newsletter_using_beehive | Beehiiv]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:37">[00:09:37]</a>
*   Substack <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:37">[00:09:37]</a>
*   WordPress <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:40">[00:09:40]</a>

The service would involve setting up a blog and newsletter for clients, charging a small one-time fee <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:44">[00:09:44]</a>.

### Client Acquisition and Marketing

To acquire clients, strategies include:
*   **Cold Outreach** Utilizing cold email or direct messages (DMs) to identify individuals who lack a blog or newsletter setup and inform them of missed opportunities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:58">[00:09:58]</a>.
*   **Social Credibility** Leverage examples of successful creators, such as [[monetizing_newsletters | Lenny Ritky]], who generates significant income from their blog and newsletter, even on free platforms like Substack <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:11">[00:10:11]</a>.
*   **Content Marketing** Create content discussing how to [[building_an_audience_for_newsletters | build an audience]] and share success stories <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:37">[00:10:37]</a>.
*   **Affordable Offer** Direct potential clients to a low-cost, one-time offer, making it an easy decision for them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:41">[00:10:41]</a>.

### Business Viability

This type of business is characterized by:
*   Low starting costs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:54">[00:10:54]</a>.
*   Potential to be started as a side business <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:53">[00:10:53]</a>.
*   Focus on helping others capitalize on the opportunities presented by blogging and newsletters.
