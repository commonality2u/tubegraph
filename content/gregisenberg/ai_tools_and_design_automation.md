A significant gap in the market exists for an [[optimizing_workflow_with_ai_design_tools | AI graphic designer]] that can adhere to brand style guides (colors, logos, fonts) to generate assets like podcast thumbnails or email headers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:07">[00:23:07]</a>. Such a tool could be a subscription-based SaaS product, potentially priced around $99/month, undercutting existing services like Design Pickle <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:27:04">[00:27:04]</a>. The key is providing source material (brand style guides) to the AI to generate better, constrained outputs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:28:30">[00:28:30]</a>.
