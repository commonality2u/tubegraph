## Prompting Strategies for Ad Content

Effective prompting is crucial for leveraging AI tools in ad creation.

### Using Reference Images

The most important elements for creating good ads are the inspiration images and product images provided to the AI <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:42">[00:02:42]</a>.

*   **Inspiration + Product Imagery**
    A common approach is to provide an inspiration ad that you want to adjust and your own custom product image <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:00">[00:03:00]</a>. The AI can then follow instructions to combine these elements, for example, placing a product image over an inspiration ad with text below <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:13">[00:03:13]</a>. This method helps create consistent and reliable ads <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:09">[00:04:09]</a>.
*   **When to Use Reference Images**
    If your product is relatively new, small, or lacks significant online presence (e.g., millions in ad spend per month), always provide a reference image <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:43">[00:06:43]</a>. For well-known concepts, ideas, or brands (like Nike or Adidas), the AI has a strong understanding and can generate relevant content without a direct reference image <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:22">[00:06:22]</a>.

### Crafting Effective Prompts

*   **Clarity and Directness:** Be very clear and direct in what you want from the AI <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:36">[00:12:36]</a>.
*   **Keywords for Realism:** Use keywords like "ultra realistic" or "photo realistic" to ensure the output is high-quality and not cartoonish <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:34">[00:07:34]</a>.
*   **Detailed Descriptions:** Provide a detailed prose description of your desired image <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:12">[00:08:12]</a>.
*   **Font and Style Adjustments:** For well-known fonts, the AI can have a decent understanding. Users can experiment with specifying font types (e.g., "exo2 bold italic") or color styles <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:20">[00:08:20]</a>.
*   **Aspect Ratios:** Adjustments to aspect ratios (e.g., 1x1, 9x16) can be made directly in the prompt <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:47">[00:08:47]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:37:54">[00:37:54]</a>.
*   **"Generate the Image" Command:** Explicitly stating "generate the image" can prevent the AI from generating text descriptions instead of visuals <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:06">[00:14:06]</a>.
*   **Starting a New Chat for Adjustments:** If the initial prompt doesn't yield desired results, it's better to start a new chat and adjust the prompt there rather than continuing in the same conversation. This prevents the AI from referencing flawed generated images and ensures consistent results <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:46">[00:12:46]</a>.
*   **Leveraging AI Creativity:** For unknown products or companies, giving more vague instructions can allow the AI to leverage its own inbuilt creativity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:28:59">[00:28:59]</a>. Once an initial idea is generated, more specific prompts can be used to refine it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:41:08">[00:41:08]</a>.
