## Key Strategies for App Growth

There are three main ways to grow a mobile app:
1.  **Organic Social Media (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube Shorts)**: This involves creating viral content that organically promotes the app <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:47">[00:08:47]</a>.
2.  **Paid Ads**: Running advertisements on platforms like Facebook, TikTok, and Google Ads <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:06">[00:09:06]</a>. This strategy is often employed by the largest companies in terms of revenue <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:04">[00:09:04]</a>.
3.  **[[aso_and_organic_growth_for_apps | Apple Search Optimization (ASO)]] / Apple Search Ads (ASA)**: Optimizing app store listings and running ads within the App Store search results to capture intent-based demand <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:19">[00:09:19]</a>.
4.  **Product-Led Growth (Virality)**: Embedding an invite flow or shareable features directly into the app's concept, encouraging users to spread the word <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:35">[00:02:35]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:43">[00:09:43]</a>. This acts as a multiplier effect on existing marketing efforts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:49">[00:11:49]</a>.

## Paid Ads Strategy in Detail

Paid ads involve creating compelling advertisements that are distributed across various digital platforms <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:12">[00:09:12]</a>. The goal is to drive installations and engagement by showing ads to users who may not even know they need the app yet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:41">[00:14:41]</a>.

### The Importance of Scale in Ad Testing

For paid advertising to be profitable, it requires extensive optimization <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:37:13">[00:37:13]</a>. It's not enough to run just a few ads; success often comes from testing thousands of creatives each month to find the perfect angle and achieve profitability <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:37:27">[00:37:27]</a>. This rigorous testing was historically common in gaming studios, which could easily generate many ad variations from gameplay footage <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:38:04">[00:38:04]</a>.

### AI's Role in Scalable Ad Creation

The emergence of [[ai_app_marketing_strategies | AI app marketing strategies]] has democratized the ability to create ads at scale <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:38:38">[00:38:38]</a>. Previously, creating thousands of unique ads was extremely costly <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:39:43">[00:39:43]</a>. Now, [[using_marketing_strategies_for_aibased_apps | AI-powered tools]] allow for rapid ad generation without needing actors or extensive production teams <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:38:51">[00:38:51]</a>.

Tools like Arc Ads enable users to:
*   Write a script <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:40:43">[00:40:43]</a>.
*   Select from a library of virtual actors with diverse demographics <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:40:47">[00:40:47]</a>.
*   Generate video ads that match the script in minutes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:41:19">[00:41:19]</a>.
*   Integrate app screenshots and remove backgrounds with AI assistance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:41:51">[00:41:51]</a>.

This process allows app developers to test 20 different ad angles in a single day, quickly identifying winning formats to iterate upon <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:41:39">[00:41:39]</a>.

### Case Study: PictureThis (Cway)

Cway, a Turkish company, exemplifies the success of a paid ads strategy <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:34:10">[00:34:10]</a>. Their plant identifier app, "PictureThis," generates approximately $7 million in monthly revenue, totaling almost $100 million annually <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:34:36">[00:34:36]</a>. This app, initially launched in 2017, saw significant scaling once AI was integrated, making the core product more valuable and boosting click-through rates on ads <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:35:54">[00:35:54]</a>.

The company has tested around 30,000 video creatives to achieve this scale, a feat made possible by leveraging AI for ad generation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:37:37">[00:37:37]</a>.

### Localization and Niche Targeting

Once an ad strategy proves successful in an initial market like the US (where conversion rates for subscription apps are generally higher) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:42:37">[00:42:37]</a>, the next step is to localize ads and apps for international markets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:43:07">[00:43:07]</a>. This involves:
*   **Translating ads**: Tools can generate ads in various languages, such as French or Spanish <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:42:20">[00:42:20]</a>.
*   **Targeting specific audiences**: Taking a proven app idea and niching it down to a particular country or audience, e.g., "Plant AI for Canadians" or "Bible for women" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:46:08">[00:46:08]</a>. This strategy is also used by large companies like Tinder, which creates dating apps for specific demographics (e.g., Latinos, gay individuals) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:46:02">[00:46:02]</a>.

This localized approach, combined with the power of AI to rapidly create and test ads, presents a vast opportunity for new app ventures <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:44:48">[00:44:48]</a>.

## Paid Ads and ASO Synergy

Paid ads can also contribute to [[aso_and_organic_growth_for_apps | ASO and organic growth for apps]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:55">[00:18:55]</a>. Running ads can increase an app's traffic and review count, signaling to Apple that the app is relevant and high-converting, which can lead to better organic rankings in the App Store <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:02">[00:19:02]</a>.
