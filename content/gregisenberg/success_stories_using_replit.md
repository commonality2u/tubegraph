## Featured Success Stories

### Adil Khan and Magic School

Adil Khan, a former school teacher, became bored during the pandemic <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:37:14">[00:37:14]</a>. He learned a little bit of coding and used [[Replit platform overview | Replit]] to create an application called Magic School <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:37:22">[00:37:22]</a>.

Magic School is designed to help teachers use generative AI in a safe and educational way, enabling them to create new assignments and correct existing ones, effectively "supercharging" their abilities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:37:31">[00:37:31]</a>.

The app quickly gained traction after its launch in mid-2023 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:37:50">[00:37:50]</a>. Early in the following year, Khan raised approximately $20 million, demonstrating phenomenal revenue growth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:38:00">[00:38:00]</a>. This story highlights how an individual with no prior engineering background can launch a successful startup and secure significant funding by leveraging [[Replit platform overview | Replit]] to bring their idea to life and scale it rapidly <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:38:15">[00:38:15]</a>.
