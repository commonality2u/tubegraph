## Identifying Creators in a Niche

After identifying a promising niche or community through platforms like Reddit, the next step is to find influential creators within that specific area <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:34">[00:13:34]</a>. Tools like Perplexity.ai can be used to generate lists of popular influencers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:36">[00:13:36]</a>.

For example, to find dog influencers on YouTube, one might prompt the AI with "25 most popular dog influencers on YouTube" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:43">[00:13:43]</a>. This list can then be organized in a Google Sheet for further analysis <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:07">[00:14:07]</a>. It's important to refine searches to find "educational" or "training" focused YouTubers rather than just entertainment-focused ones, to ensure relevance to potential business solutions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:50">[00:14:50]</a>.

## Gaining Insights from Creator Content and Audiences

Once relevant creators are identified, their content and audience engagement can provide invaluable insights for product development and [[growth_strategies_using_creators_and_social_media | marketing strategies]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:21">[00:14:21]</a>.

### Analyzing Top-Performing Videos
*   Navigate to the creator's YouTube channel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:29">[00:14:29]</a>.
*   Sort their videos by "most popular" to identify content that resonates most with their audience <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:12">[00:15:12]</a>. For instance, a video like "Complete guide to Puppy training" can reveal popular topics <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:19">[00:15:19]</a>.

### Understanding Audience Needs Through Comments
*   Review the comments section of popular videos <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:25">[00:15:25]</a>.
*   Even amidst lighthearted comments, deep insights can be found regarding audience needs, thoughts, and unaddressed problems <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:37">[00:15:37]</a>. This process helps entrepreneurs empathize with their target audience and understand their specific pain points <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:49">[00:15:49]</a>.

By observing and writing down these learnings from 20-25 videos, a comprehensive understanding of the market and potential product ideas can be developed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:11">[00:16:11]</a>.
