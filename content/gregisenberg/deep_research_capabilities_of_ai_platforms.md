Deep research in the context of artificial intelligence (AI) platforms refers to their ability to conduct in-depth analysis and provide comprehensive answers, similar to a highly paid junior analyst, rather than just quick responses to prompts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:20">[00:00:20]</a>. This capability can be leveraged to [[using_ai_to_analyze_trends_and_generate_business_concepts | analyze trends and generate business concepts]] and even to build a business from scratch <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:35">[00:00:35]</a>. This article compares the deep research functionalities of OpenAI's ChatGPT Pro and Perplexity AI.

## Testing Methodology

A common prompt was used for both platforms to test their deep research capabilities:
"I want to create a startup. I want it to be an AI agent that focuses on a high-value niche. I want it to be defensible, not just a flash in the pan. I want you to create a zero-to-one playbook of how you'd launch it to get customers too. Give me five ideas with five playbooks on how to grow it. I'm looking for $1 million year one, $3 million year two, $5 million year three." <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a>

Additional constraints and preferences were added during interaction, such as:
*   Open to any industry with the highest likelihood of success <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:39">[00:02:39]</a>.
*   Open to B2B or B2C models <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:58">[00:02:58]</a>.
*   No specific preference for AI applications (e.g., automation, data analysis), again focusing on highest probability of success <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:06">[00:03:06]</a>.
*   Preference for an MVP (Minimum Viable Product) cost of $5,000 or less, with a low-code/no-code approach preferred for non-technical users <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:27">[00:03:27]</a>.
*   Desire for potential cash flow but open to raising VC money if the business could exceed $100 million ARR <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:49">[00:03:49]</a>.
*   Leaning towards viral self-serve growth for market entry, but open to suggestions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:12">[00:04:12]</a>.

## Perplexity Deep Research

Perplexity's deep research product is a newer offering <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:01">[00:06:01]</a>. It's noteworthy for its speed and ability to go through many sources, checking its reasoning as it proceeds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:02">[00:07:02]</a>. It can search through 29 or more sources <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:48">[00:07:48]</a>.

### Example Idea: Legal Contract Lifecycle AI Agent

Perplexity proposed an AI agent for legal contract lifecycle management <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:51">[00:09:51]</a>.
*   **Problem:** Corporate legal teams spend 23% of their time manually reviewing contracts, leading to Version Control errors costing enterprises $2.4 billion annually <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:00">[00:10:00]</a>. Existing CLM solutions are often inflexible <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:16">[00:10:16]</a>.
*   **Defensible Solution:** The AI agent would integrate with email, SharePoint, and DocuSign; learn organizational risk thresholds from historical data; auto-redline third-party papers using firm-specific fallback positions; and maintain version lineage with blockchain-style audit trails <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:27">[00:10:27]</a>.
*   **Defensibility:** It develops institutional memory through continuous exposure to negotiation outcomes, embeds itself in legal workflows (average 14.3 logins/week/user), and requires significant retraining (6-9 months of human experience) to replace <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:29">[00:11:29]</a>.
*   **Growth Playbook:**
    *   **Phase 1 (Niche Validation, 90 days):** Build a Chrome extension to record legal contract review sessions (with permission) for model training <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:20">[00:12:20]</a>. Offer free redline audits for 100 AmLaw 200 firms, targeting mid-market companies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:31">[00:12:31]</a>. Monetization at $2,500/month subscription locked to specific document types <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:08">[00:13:08]</a>.
    *   **Phase 2 (Workflow Embedding, months 4-9):** Integrate with legal ops platforms (e.g., Lexion, Entra) and launch a Clause Library Builder <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:25">[00:13:25]</a>. Implement usage-based pricing at $0.25 per reviewed clause <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:38">[00:13:38]</a>.
    *   **Phase 3 (Enterprise Scaling, months 10-12):** Add SOC 2 Type 2 compliance, develop custom connectors, and upsell to an $8,000/month enterprise tier with custom playbooks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:55">[00:13:55]</a>.
*   **Financial Roadmap:** Forecasts $1.2 million ARR in Year 1, $3.1 million in Year 2, and $5.4 million in Year 3 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:12">[00:14:12]</a>.

Perplexity also offered a detailed [[building_apps_with_ai_tools | MVP implementation plan]] for the legal AI agent, with a budget of $4,800, emphasizing a Chrome extension prototype and core feature build <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:32:35">[00:32:35]</a>.

## ChatGPT Deep Research

ChatGPT Pro's deep research is more expensive ($200/month vs. $20/month for Perplexity Pro) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:44">[00:00:44]</a>, and generally takes longer to generate responses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:00">[00:09:00]</a>. However, it often provides more in-depth, business-plan-like outputs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:14">[00:16:14]</a>.

### Example Idea: AI Sales Outreach Assistant

ChatGPT suggested an AI-powered sales outreach assistant <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:55">[00:15:55]</a>.
*   **Concept:** Acts as a virtual SDR (Sales Development Representative) automating prospect research and personalized outreach (email, LinkedIn) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:27">[00:16:27]</a>.
*   **Target Market:** B2B companies (startups to mid-size firms) needing more pipeline without hiring additional SDRs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:38">[00:16:38]</a>.
*   **Defensibility (Data Network Effect):** Learns from every interaction which messaging yields replies, building a unique data set of what works in each industry <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:53">[00:16:53]</a>. Over time, it develops superior personalization and lead scoring, integrating into CRM and email systems <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:25">[00:17:25]</a>.
*   **MVP Plan ($5,000 or less):**
    *   Focus on a narrow, high-value use case, such as automating follow-up emails to warm leads with templated but AI-personalized content <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:05">[00:18:05]</a>.
    *   MVP as a web app or Chrome extension where salespersons input prospects or connect CRM, and AI generates/sends personalized emails <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:43">[00:18:43]</a>.
    *   **Low-Cost Building:** Use existing AI APIs (e.g., OpenAI GPT-4 for text generation), a no-frills UI with low-code tools, and initially focus on a single channel like email <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:57">[00:18:57]</a>. Implement a manual fallback if the AI is unsure <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:38">[00:19:38]</a>.
*   **Customer Acquisition & Growth:**
    *   **Product-Led Viral Adoption:** Self-serve free tier (e.g., 50 AI-generated emails/month) allows individual reps to try it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:45">[00:20:45]</a>.
    *   **Viral Loop:** Prompt users to share successes internally (e.g., "This meeting was set by AI") <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:21:01">[00:21:01]</a>.
    *   **Referral Incentives:** As more reps use it, shared data improves AI performance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:21:20">[00:21:20]</a>.
    *   **Content Marketing:** Publish case studies and demos (e.g., how "Jenny AI" grew to $5M ARR) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:21:27">[00:21:27]</a>.
    *   **CRM Marketplaces:** List on platforms like HubSpot for distribution <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:21:57">[00:21:57]</a>.
*   **Monetization:** Per-seat pricing or usage-based tiers (e.g., $49/month for solo, $199/month for team, enterprise tiers) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:21">[00:23:21]</a>.
*   **VC Readiness:** This idea is deemed capable of scaling to $100 million ARR, potentially requiring VC funding for further growth beyond $200 million ARR <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:29">[00:25:29]</a>.
