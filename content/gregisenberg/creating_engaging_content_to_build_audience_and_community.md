## Identifying Content Ideas from Online Communities

The process for generating content ideas mirrors the approach used for discovering startup opportunities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:04">[00:19:04]</a>.

### Step 1: Utilize Gummy Search's "Top Content" Feature

Gummy Search can be used to identify content that performs well within specific subreddits or communities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:20">[00:18:20]</a>.

*   Navigate to the "Top Content" section for a chosen audience (e.g., "people into dog food" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:31">[00:18:31]</a>).
*   Observe the best-performing content to identify common patterns <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:38">[00:18:38]</a>.
*   Note the types of formats that work, such as long stories or memes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:45">[00:18:45]</a>. For example, in a dog community, stories like "loss of beloved pet" or "today I saved a dog's life" tend to perform well <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:55">[00:18:55]</a>.

### Step 2: Leverage AI for Content Insights

Tools like Gummy Search act as an "entrepreneurial assistant" for [[aidriven_content_creation_for_brand_growth | AI-driven content creation]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:46">[00:19:46]</a>.

*   **Analyze common patterns:** Gummy Search uses AI to summarize large volumes of submissions and identify common patterns in discussions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:08">[00:07:08]</a>. This can reveal popular themes like "potty training challenges" or "dog struggling with potty trains" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:58">[00:07:58]</a>. These patterns can directly inform [[developing_unique_and_entertaining_content_with_ai_insights | content themes]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:30">[00:08:30]</a>.
*   **Ask direct questions:** Users can directly ask Gummy Search questions about content preferences within an audience, such as "Do they love memes?" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:20">[00:19:20]</a>. While not always directly stating "they love memes," the AI's responses can provide insights into the community's general tone and preferred content types, like "snuggle puddle" or "literal dog pile" comments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:33">[00:19:33]</a>.

By observing and writing down these insights, content creators can develop ideas that genuinely resonate with their target communities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:27">[00:20:27]</a>.
