## AI Agents for Customer Service

One prominent [[ai_startup_ideas | AI startup idea]] is the development and deployment of AI agents for customer service <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:08">[00:05:08]</a>. These agents, often functioning as voice Large Language Models (LLMs), can automate various customer interactions.

### Capabilities and Examples
AI agents can handle a range of tasks, including:
*   Taking initial calls from leads <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:10">[00:00:10]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:00">[00:06:00]</a>.
*   Setting up subsequent calls or appointments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:11">[00:00:11]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:03">[00:06:03]</a>.
*   Managing rescheduling requests <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:14">[00:00:14]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:06">[00:06:06]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:07">[00:06:07]</a>.
*   Potentially closing sales with customers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>.

An example cited includes a demo where "Retail AI" successfully set up dental appointments, even when attempts were made to "throw it off" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:00">[00:00:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:03">[00:00:03]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:20">[00:05:20]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:30">[00:05:30]</a>.

### Impact and Profitability
The financial impact of AI agents can be significant. A recent report from Clara indicated they expect to add $40 million in profit by integrating 700 new AI agents <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:23">[00:00:23]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:29">[00:06:29]</a>. These AI agents managed 2.3 million chats and operated 80% to 90% faster than human agents, highlighting their efficiency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:40">[00:06:40]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:42">[00:06:42]</a>.

### Business Model for AI Agent Startups
Startups entering this space can approach companies with large customer service teams <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:50">[00:06:50]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:53">[00:06:53]</a>. Two primary business models are discussed:

*   **Percentage of Cost Savings**: Offer to save a company a significant amount (e.g., $2 million a month) by implementing AI agents and take a percentage of those savings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:57">[00:06:57]</a>. This can be a helpful starting point to de-risk the venture for the entrepreneur <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:27">[00:08:27]</a>.
*   **Value-Based Charging**: Instead of focusing on cost savings, position the AI agents as a direct value generator (e.g., acquiring more customers) and charge a higher, upfront fee. This approach can capture "four to eight times more" value <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:33">[00:07:33]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:36">[00:07:36]</a>.

Similar to past "digital transformation" trends, these agencies would guide businesses through the process of integrating AI across their operations, including customer service <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:56">[00:25:56]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:58">[00:25:58]</a>.
