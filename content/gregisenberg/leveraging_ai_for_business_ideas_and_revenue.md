Even large organizations are adopting this continuous storytelling technique <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:14:07">[09:14:07]</a>.

## Sequential Prompting: A Key Technique for AI-Driven Workflows

While conversational and master prompts are effective ways to use AI, a third technique, **sequential prompting**, is introduced <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:37:07">[10:37:07]</a>. This involves using one AI tool (like ChatGPT) to generate a prompt that is then used in another AI tool (like Leonardo AI) to produce an output <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="11:17:07">[11:17:07]</a>. This output can then be fed into another AI tool, creating a sequence of connected actions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="12:03:07">[12:03:07]</a>.

For example, to create an AI avatar:
1.  **Generate Prompt with ChatGPT:** Upload an image to ChatGPT and ask it to provide a detailed description of the person (character name, age, hairstyle, outfit, etc.) in a structured prompt format <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="15:25:07">[15:25:07]</a>. This prompt will ensure character consistency across different images <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="19:54:07">[19:54:07]</a>.
2.  **Generate Image with Leonardo AI:** Take the prompt generated by ChatGPT and paste it into Leonardo AI. This tool can create high-resolution images in seconds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="20:07:07">[20:07:07]</a>. Leonardo AI also has an API, allowing for programmatic image generation through a user interface <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="20:59:07">[20:59:07]</a>.
3.  **Animate with Cling AI:** The generated images from Leonardo AI can then be uploaded to Cling AI to turn them into videos. Cling AI also has a "dub" feature for lip-syncing words, allowing characters to speak <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="50:29:07">[50:29:07]</a>. While video generation is currently slower (around 4 minutes for a 5-second clip), this is expected to accelerate significantly <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="51:05:07">[51:05:07]</a>.

Initially, it's recommended to perform this process manually to iterate and refine prompts. Once satisfied, the workflow can be automated using tools like Zapier, Make, or by hiring an engineer for more complex integrations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="12:42:07">[12:42:07]</a>.

### 1. AI-First Personalized Content (e.g., "Moonpig" for AI)

The concept of "Moonpig," a business that creates personalized greeting cards with over $400 million in annual revenue, highlights a huge market for personalized gifts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="28:54:07">[28:54:07]</a>. An AI-first version of this could involve:
*   **Dynamic Character & Scene Creation:** Users upload images, and AI generates characters and scenes.
*   **AI-Generated Stories:** Every card becomes a mini-story <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="32:37:07">[32:37:07]</a>.
*   **AI-Enhanced Templates:** Templates adapt in real-time based on user input (tone, voice).
*   **Video Integration:** Users can add AI-generated voiceovers or short animated videos <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="32:55:07">[32:55:07]</a>.
*   **Revenue Streams:** Premium card sales (with a 20-30% markup due to AI personalization), monthly memberships for unlimited cards, and upsells for matching gifts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="33:04:07">[33:04:07]</a>.
*   **Targeting:** This approach could target birthday invites, personalized stories, or even "memifying moments" that are trending online <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="24:02:07">[24:02:07]</a>.

Such a platform could be built with a user-friendly interface that leads straight into the creation experience, allowing users to quickly generate, edit, and print their personalized content <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="36:59:07">[36:59:07]</a>.
