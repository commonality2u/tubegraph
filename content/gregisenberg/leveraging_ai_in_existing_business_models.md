## The Challenge of AI Business Models: Competition and Value Erosion

Businesses built solely on new AI tools, like the Cal AI app for calorie tracking, face immense competition <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:04">[00:00:04]</a>. Hundreds of similar apps can emerge in a short period, driving prices down to zero and eroding equity value quickly <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:06">[00:00:06]</a>. These "one-off bites" might help someone become a millionaire but are unlikely to create deca-millionaires due to lack of sustainability <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:15">[00:00:15]</a>.

AI often functions as a "sustaining innovation," reducing costs but not necessarily creating lasting competitive advantages <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:49:09">[01:49:09]</a>. For example, using an AI agent to replace an outbound email team in a recruiting business might initially boost margins, but competitors will adopt the same technology within months, leading to price drops and increased market competitiveness <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:13:00">[02:13:00]</a>.

The previous tech landscape was characterized by a finite number of coders and venture capital, limiting competition <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:58:00">[02:58:00]</a>. Now, with AI, there's far more competition across more niches <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:06:00">[03:06:00]</a>. Traditional "moats" like a niche market being too small for venture capital or requiring coding knowledge are no longer sufficient <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:21:00">[03:21:00]</a>.

> [!WARNING] The worst businesses are the ones that are easy to start <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:00:00">[01:00:00]</a>.

## Building a Sustainable AI Business: Data Advantage and Network Effects

To build an AI business that remains competitive and successful for several years, a **data advantage** or a **network effect advantage** is crucial <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:39:00">[00:39:00]</a>.

> [!INFO] **Moats that matter:**
> *   **Network Effects:** A product or service that becomes more valuable as more people use it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:48:00">[03:48:00]</a>. Examples include social networks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:50:00">[03:50:00]</a> and communities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:52:00">[03:52:00]</a>.
> *   **Proprietary Data:** Unique or exclusive data sets that feed AI models <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:39:00">[00:39:00]</a>. However, large language models (LLMs) have "gobbled up the entire internet," making unique data moats challenging to identify as much existing data is already within LLMs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="17:42:00">[17:42:00]</a>.
> *   **Distribution and Brand:** In an era where anyone can create anything with AI, strong distribution and brand become essential moats <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:21:00">[04:21:00]</a>. A personal monopoly built through audience engagement and content consumption provides an unfair competitive advantage <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:40:00">[04:40:00]</a>.
> *   **Security:** Offering the most secure solution for integrating data or APIs can create a competitive advantage, similar to how OnePassword built trust <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="32:42:00">[32:42:00]</a>.

The ultimate value capture in the AI era may go to those who control the devices, as much LLM functionality will eventually be implemented directly into them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="20:21:00">[20:21:00]</a>.

## [[practical_use_cases_for_ai_agents_in_business | Practical Use Cases for AI Agents in Business]]

AI agents are transforming various business functions, making them more efficient and intelligent.

### [[automating_business_processes_with_ai | Automating Business Processes with AI]]

*   **Sales Lead Management:** AI agents can assess sales leads by gathering additional information (e.g., time of inquiry, company size, revenue, employee count) to add intelligence to the process, rather than just boolean scoring <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:29">[00:22:29]</a>. Agents can also be programmed to email leads, with human intervention for high-value prospects <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:11">[00:24:11]</a>. Tools like Gum Loop are used for this <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:26:26">[00:26:26]</a>.
*   **Email Management:** An AI agent can ingest every email, label or archive it based on context, and even draft responses for common interactions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:13:00">[02:13:00]</a>. This improves productivity by handling routine tasks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:35:00">[02:35:00]</a>.
*   **Newsletter Curation:** An AI agent can source news from various local forums and websites, punch it up, and format it into a detailed, fact-checked newsletter, potentially outperforming human-written versions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="16:21:00">[16:21:21]</a>.

### Improving Human Resources

*   **Candidate Screening:** Tools like Manis AI can score job applicants based on defined criteria, significantly reducing the time spent sifting through applications and providing unbiased assessments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:10:00">[02:10:00]</a>.

### Website Enhancement

*   **Automated Website Design Agencies:** An automated agency can use AI tools like Vercel to scrape local businesses, identify those with outdated websites, and then automatically redesign them based on modern design practices <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:46:00">[03:46:00]</a>. The agency could then reach out to business owners with the redesigned site, offer hosting, and charge for ongoing changes, creating a recurring revenue model <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:20:00">[03:20:00]</a>.

### Financial Analysis and Management

*   **Financial Analysis with LLMs (MCP):** Using Model Context Protocol (MCP), LLMs can be fed live financial data from accounting software like Xero or QuickBooks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:50:00">[02:50:00]</a>. This allows for real-time financial queries, analysis, and identification of optimization opportunities, such as tax savings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:08:00">[03:08:00]</a>. The opportunity lies in creating user-friendly, secure MCP servers (e.g., "sCP.com" for "secure Model Context Protocol") that integrate APIs and manage databases for businesses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:31:00">[03:31:00]</a>.

### AI-Powered Lending

*   **Automated Due Diligence:** AI can streamline the loan application process by integrating with financial platforms (e.g., Stripe API) and processing necessary documents (IDs, articles of incorporation) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:14:00">[04:14:00]</a>. This could enable significantly faster loan approvals, such as a $5 million loan in 48 hours <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:48:00">[04:48:00]</a>.
*   **"Bank of Vibe Coding":** An AI-based lending LLM could provide capital to "vibe coders" (those who quickly build apps with AI) in exchange for a percentage of profits <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:34:00">[04:34:00]</a>. The LLM would apply criteria (e.g., successful past projects, social media followers, Stripe integration) to qualify applicants, automating the due diligence that previously required an "army of accountants and lawyers" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:50:00">[04:50:00]</a>.

## Conclusion

While AI makes it easier than ever to start businesses, the intense competition means that only those with strong competitive advantages, such as proprietary data, robust network effects, or strong brands, are likely to achieve long-term sustainability <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:46:00">[04:46:00]</a>. Focusing on these fundamental moats and leveraging AI for [[optimizing_business_operations_with_ai | optimizing business operations with AI]] can lead to successful ventures. The ability to prompt an LLM to build a competing product in a short time suggests that many "easy-to-start" businesses will struggle to maintain their value <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:56:00">[04:56:00]</a>.
