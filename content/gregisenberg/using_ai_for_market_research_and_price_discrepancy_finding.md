## Applications of AI in Business

### [[retail_arbitrage_with_ai | Retail Arbitrage]]

AI can be utilized to identify price discrepancies between different online marketplaces, facilitating [[retail_arbitrage_with_ai | retail arbitrage]] business models. The process involves finding undervalued items on one platform and selling them at a higher, fair market price on another <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:49">[00:03:49]</a>.

**Example: Gozney Pizza Ovens**
An AI operator was tasked with finding Gozney pizza ovens on Facebook Marketplace that were "grossly undervalued" compared to listings on eBay, with the condition that sellers be willing to ship <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>.
The AI, after logging into a Facebook account and setting a search radius (e.g., 500 miles from Dallas), identified a promising Gozney Roccbox for $250, noting it was undervalued given its condition and accessories <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:55">[00:01:55]</a>. This model typically sells for around $500 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:40">[00:02:40]</a>. The AI also logged promising finds into a spreadsheet, including title, location, condition, and a link <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:08">[00:02:08]</a>.

**Automated Arbitrage Workflow Vision**
A potential automated business model could involve the AI:
1.  **Researching Prices:** Performing pricing research on platforms like eBay to establish an average market price for a specific item <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:08">[00:10:08]</a>.
2.  **Making Offers:** Making offers (e.g., half price) on behalf of the user for items listed on Facebook Marketplace, especially for those listed for 30 days or more, as they are more likely to accept lowball offers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:17">[00:10:17]</a>.
3.  **Fulfilling Orders:** Once an offer is accepted, the user would take pictures and list the item on eBay. Upon sale, the AI could manage payment collection, send payment to the Facebook Marketplace seller, and provide the end buyer's address for direct shipping <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:36">[00:10:36]</a>. This process could potentially build a six or seven-figure business <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:07">[00:03:07]</a>.

### Sourcing Services and Quotes

AI can automate the process of gathering quotes for various services, saving significant time and effort.

**Example: Catering Quotes for a Wedding**
An AI operator was prompted to find catering quotes for a wedding of 50 people requesting steak plus two sides in Dallas, Texas, by reaching out to caterers on TheKnot.com <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:56">[00:11:56]</a>.
The AI navigated the website, searched for caterers in the specified area, filled out contact forms, and composed messages requesting per-head quotes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:04">[00:13:04]</a>. The AI even chose a tentative wedding date when one wasn't provided, demonstrating its problem-solving capabilities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:22">[00:16:22]</a>.
This application showcases how AI can accelerate a process that typically involves hundreds of phone calls or manual form submissions, potentially yielding many quotes overnight <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:18">[00:13:18]</a>.

## AI Operator Capabilities and Best Practices

### Customization and Control
*   **Prompting:** Users can prompt the AI with specific goals, such as finding a particular product, requesting quotes, or changing the tone of communication (e.g., "over-the-top friendly," "rude tone") <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:55">[00:15:55]</a>.
*   **Intervention:** Users can take control of the AI's actions at any point to demonstrate a task or guide it through a process <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:00">[00:15:00]</a>. The AI can learn from these manual interventions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:36">[00:15:36]</a>.
*   **Speed Control:** Commands like "move faster" or setting specific goals (e.g., "submit three quote requests per minute") can influence the AI's execution speed, although it may have limitations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:29">[00:20:29]</a>.
*   **Persistence:** The AI can retain instructions across different tasks, such as remembering not to ask for confirmation before submitting forms <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:31:40">[00:31:40]</a>.
*   **Screenshots:** The AI can take screenshots of its actions, which could be used for documentation or auditing <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:31:49">[00:31:49]</a>.
