## Why Focus on Anti-trends?

In the competitive landscape of software and startups, it can be easier to succeed by pursuing [[antitrends_and_humancentric_services | anti-trends]] because everyone else is typically chasing the same dominant trends <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:01:00">[01:01:00]</a>. It provides:

*   **Related Trends:** Helps in brainstorming product ideas by suggesting related concepts (e.g., for "slow living," it might suggest "minimalist living rooms" or "cozy bedroom") <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:58:00">[04:58:00]</a>.
