This could start as a newsletter and evolve into a full platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:22">[00:06:22]</a>.
*   **Implementation**:

    *   **Content**: Focus on longtail keywords like "buy it for life vacuum" or "buy it for life couch" to build dedicated landing pages <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:50">[00:10:50]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:06">[00:11:06]</a>.
*   **Acquisition & Monetization**:
    *   **Traffic**: Leverage Reddit by providing value in posts and subtly linking back to the curated directory <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:28">[00:10:28]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:18">[00:12:18]</a>.
