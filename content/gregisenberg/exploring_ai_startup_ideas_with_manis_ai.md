Based on its research, Manis AI proposed "SmartConsole AI," an AI-powered micro-consulting platform for SMBs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="21:09:00">[21:09:00]</a>. This concept addresses the market gap where small businesses need expert consulting but cannot afford traditional services costing $150-$500 per hour <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="24:24:00">[24:24:00]</a>.
