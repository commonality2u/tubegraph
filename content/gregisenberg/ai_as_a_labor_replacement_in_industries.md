## AI Receptionists
One of the primary areas where [[applications_of_ai_in_recruitment_and_customer_support | AI is replacing labor]] is in receptionist roles <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:46:52">[00:46:52]</a>. Businesses across various sectors are finding it challenging to hire front desk personnel who primarily answer questions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:45:00">[00:45:00]</a>, making [[ai_in_customer_service | AI receptionists]] a compelling solution <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:44:12">[00:44:12]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:45:07">[00:45:07]</a>.

These [[ai_in_customer_service | AI-powered receptionists]] can be:
*   **Trained on specific data** to handle conversations and answer frequently asked questions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:45:15">[00:45:15]</a>, such as those found in a PDF document <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:45:25">[00:45:25]</a>.
*   **Integrated with existing CRM systems** to perform actions like scheduling appointments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:45:33">[00:45:33]</a>.
*   **Deployed in various niches**, including:
    *   Dentist offices <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:44:37">[00:44:37]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:47:15">[00:47:15]</a>
    *   Barber shops (for availability, bookings, queries) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:47:11">[00:47:11]</a>
    *   Restaurants <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:47:16">[00:47:16]</a>
    *   SaaS websites <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:47:18">[00:47:18]</a>
    *   Realtor offices <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:47:19">[00:47:19]</a>
