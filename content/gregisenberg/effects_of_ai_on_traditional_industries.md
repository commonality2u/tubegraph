*   **Enhanced [[impact_of_ai_on_design_and_web_apps | Design]] Capabilities** ChatGPT 4o excels at generating gradients, cool components, characters, and even sprite sheets for games <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:36">[00:02:36]</a>.
