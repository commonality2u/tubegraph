## Core Marketing Principles

When developing a SaaS app, it's crucial to identify the single most important KPI (Key Performance Indicator) for the user and demonstrate their progress on it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:38:00">[02:38:00]</a>. Making this information sharable can turn the product dashboard into a powerful [[Marketing Strategies for B2B Companies | marketing tool]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:25:54">[02:25:54]</a>. For example, displaying collected revenue or saved costs on a dashboard, allowing users to screenshot and "flex" their success, can be highly effective <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:54:10">[02:54:10]</a>.

## Specific Marketing Strategies

Several [[Marketing Strategies for B2B Companies | marketing strategies for B2B companies]] are discussed, including:

### 1. Video-Centric Launches (Especially on X)
The current trend for launching products, especially on X (formerly Twitter), heavily prioritizes video content <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:59:00">[02:59:00]</a>. A well-produced, storytelling video can generate significant views and downloads, even in saturated markets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:51:30">[02:51:30]</a>.
*   **Strategy**: Create beautifully produced videos that showcase the app's benefits and tell a compelling story, such as how the app solved a critical business problem <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:47:00">[02:47:00]</a>. Using a humorous or aggressive brand image (e.g., "sending the mob after people" for invoice collection) can make the video memorable <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:56:00">[02:56:00]</a>.
*   **AI for Video Scripts**: Chat GPT can be used to generate video scripts or marketing campaign ideas by providing it with examples of successful viral videos and asking it to act as a CMO for your product <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:28:00">[03:28:00]</a>.

### 2. Cold Email Campaigns
Cold email remains a viable strategy, particularly for B2B SaaS products <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:39:00">[03:39:00]</a>.
*   **Lead Generation**:
    *   Use tools like Apollo to scrape B2B emails <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:08:00">[03:08:00]</a>.
    *   Hire freelancers on Upwork or Fiverr to find existing lists of target contacts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:17:00">[03:17:00]</a>.
    *   Consider Outscraper for local business leads (though caution is advised for email quality) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:43:00">[03:43:00]</a>.
    *   Find Email (Findymail) can scrape up to 2,000 leads from LinkedIn for a monthly fee <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:59:00">[03:59:00]</a>.
*   **Email Sending Tools**: Instantly or Smart Leads (priced around $50-$100/month) allow purchasing email addresses directly and warming them up to avoid spam filters <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:13:00">[03:13:00]</a>.
*   **Copywriting**: Craft killer copy with engaging subject lines (e.g., "Invoice Paid") <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:57:00">[03:57:00]</a>. Instead of asking for a meeting or video watch, offer immediate value like "Go try it out. You get your first two invoices done for free" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:29:00">[03:29:00]</a>. AB test subject lines and calls to action <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:59:00">[03:59:00]</a>.
*   **Layering**: Cold email can be set up to run passively in the background while focusing on other [[Marketing strategies for B2B companies | marketing channels]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:46:00">[04:46:00]</a>.

### 3. Instagram and TikTok Playbook (Adapting B2C for B2B)
While primarily consumer-focused, the strategies from platforms like TikTok and Instagram can be adapted for B2B growth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:48:00">[03:48:00]</a>.
*   **Account Warming**: Open multiple accounts (e.g., four on TikTok, four on Instagram) and warm them up for 3-4 days by acting like a real human, following, bookmarking, and liking content in the target niche <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:53:00">[03:53:00]</a>.
*   **Content Strategy**: Identify working hooks, videos, and formats in the target niche (e.g., freelancer niche). Slideshows, like "Here's the top five tools that saved my agency," can be effective <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:50:00">[03:50:00]</a>.

### 4. LinkedIn Short-Form Video
LinkedIn is currently a prime channel for video content, offering significant reach <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:07:00">[04:07:00]</a>.
*   **Engagement**: Posting short-form videos consistently (e.g., every day for 14 days) can lead to millions of impressions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:14:00">[04:14:00]</a>.
*   **Content Ideas**: Share stories of how the app helps clients, saved businesses, or acts as an ideal AI agent <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:27:00">[04:27:00]</a>. Include screenshots of the app's dashboard <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:46:00">[04:46:00]</a>.
*   **Link Strategy**: Unlike X, LinkedIn allows effective direct linking within posts. Post content, let it gain traction, then edit the post to add a link at the bottom <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:20:00">[04:20:00]</a>.
*   **Addressing Skepticism**: While some find LinkedIn "cringe," ignoring its hundreds of millions of active users is a missed opportunity for increasing the probability of success <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:49:00">[04:49:00]</a>.

### 5. Hand-to-Hand Combat (Direct Outreach)
This involves targeted, personal outreach to a select group of potential users <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:52:00">[04:52:00]</a>.
*   **Target List**: Create a list of 50-100 target individuals or companies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:56:00">[04:56:00]</a>.
*   **Engagement Strategy**: Enter their "orbit" by consistently commenting on their posts and engaging with their content <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:59:00">[04:59:00]</a>. This is a long-term play (e.g., a month-long) to build familiarity before pitching <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:15:00">[04:15:00]</a>.
*   **Personalized Pitch**: When ready, initiate DMs with a personalized message referencing their work and offering the tool for testing, rather than an immediate pitch-slap <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:31:00">[04:31:00]</a>.

## Getting Started

Before even developing the app, consider building a waitlist page to collect emails, using tools like JotForm, Card, Framer, Tally.so, or Typeform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:02:00">[01:02:00]</a>. This allows for testing market interest before significant development investment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:02:00">[01:02:00]</a>.
