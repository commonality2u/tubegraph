## What are AI Receptionists?
An [[AI Agents and their applications | AI receptionist]] is a system trained on a company's specific data to have conversations with callers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:45:17">[00:45:17]</a>. These systems can answer frequently asked questions by leveraging information provided in formats like PDFs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:45:25">[00:45:25]</a>. They can also integrate with a company's CRM to perform tasks such as scheduling appointments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:45:33">[00:45:33]</a>.

The technology behind these [[AI Agents and their applications | AI-powered]] receptionists is rapidly advancing, with latency becoming so low that the experience is responsive and often better than interacting with a human, especially given their greater knowledge base <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:46:05">[00:46:05]</a>.
