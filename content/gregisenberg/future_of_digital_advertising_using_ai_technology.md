## Prompting Strategies for AI-Generated Ads

Unlike some image models, Chat GPT's multimodal language model means prompting can be straightforward due to its strong understanding of intent and creativity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:22">[00:02:22]</a>.

### The Role of Inspiration Images

The most crucial element in creating effective ads with AI is providing high-quality inspiration images and product images <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:42">[00:02:42]</a>. This method allows for the creation of super consistent and reliable ads by making minor edits or adjustments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:05">[00:04:05]</a>.

*   **Using Reference Ads**: Provide an existing ad as an inspiration source and instruct the AI to adjust it with your product image and desired text <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:00">[00:03:00]</a>.
*   **Well-Known Concepts**: AI models have a strong understanding of well-known brands (e.g., Nike, Adidas) and concepts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:17">[00:06:17]</a>. For such brands, a reference image may not be strictly necessary <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:55">[00:06:55]</a>.
*   **Newer/Smaller Products**: For less established products, always provide a reference image <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:43">[00:06:43]</a>.

### Prompting Without Reference Images

When not using reference images, detailed prompts are key <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:12">[00:08:12]</a>.

*   **Keywords for Realism**: Use terms like "ultra realistic" or "photorealistic" to ensure the generated images look authentic and avoid cartoonish or oddly colored outputs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:34">[00:07:34]</a>.
*   **Font and Style Control**: AI can understand well-known font types (e.g., Exo2 bold italic) and adjust color styles or aspect ratios based on prompt instructions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:20">[00:08:20]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:41">[00:08:41]</a>.
*   **Direct Image Generation**: Specify "generate the image" to prevent the AI from trying to generate text that describes the ad, ensuring consistent image results <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:06">[00:14:06]</a>.

### Best Practices for Prompting

*   **Clarity and Directness**: Be very clear and direct in what you want from the AI <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:36">[00:12:36]</a>.
*   **New Chats for Adjustments**: If an initial prompt doesn't yield desired results, start a new chat for adjustments rather than continuing in the same conversation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:10">[00:13:10]</a>. This prevents degradation of quality, as the AI uses the previously generated image as a reference, which may have flaws <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:03">[00:13:03]</a>.
*   **Managing Expectations**: Complex or vague prompts may require two to three iterations to match user intention <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:31:01">[00:31:01]</a>, while simpler, well-defined prompts with known products can often be "one-shotted" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:31:10">[00:31:10]</a>.
