The process of using Manis to find business opportunities involves several steps, with the user providing clear prompts and guidance:

### 1. Setting the Task
The user instructs Manis to create a business that can generate $1 million in 18 months, focusing on [[startup_ideas_using_ai_technology|AI ideas]], particularly niche AI startups not covered in Y Combinator's latest batch <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>. *   Emergence of [[automating_business_tasks_with_ai|AI agents and automations]] for specialized workflows <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:48">[00:12:48]</a>.

*   Growth of no-code and low-code solutions for AI accessibility <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:06">[00:13:06]</a>.

Manis analyzes Reddit to find trends, pain points, and emerging consumer and B2B needs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:19">[00:14:19]</a>.
