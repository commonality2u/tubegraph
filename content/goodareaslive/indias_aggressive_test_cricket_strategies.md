### Strategic Declarations

A key aspect of [[indias_cricketing_strategy_against_england | India's cricketing strategy]] was their willingness to declare early, even with a relatively small lead of 50-odd runs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:09:58">[07:09:58]</a>. This decision was "result-oriented" and demonstrated an intent to ensure enough time to bowl Bangladesh out twice, despite two days being washed out <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:50:00">[07:50:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:11:00">[08:11:00]</a>. Historically, declarations were often more "punchy," and this aligns with that aggressive mindset <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:15:00">[07:15:00]</a>. Declaring also allowed India to get "two bites of the new ball" by bowling in the last hour of day three and again on day four, taking advantage of difficult batting conditions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:37:37">[09:37:37]</a>.
