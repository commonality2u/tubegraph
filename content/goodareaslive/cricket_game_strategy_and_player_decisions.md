*   **Switching Angles**: Against Dawid Malan, Bumrah had been bowling over the wicket with limited threat <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:49">[00:11:49]</a>. Instead, Bumrah bowled a full and straight delivery <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:31">[00:14:31]</a>, surprising Root with its pace and beating him directly <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:15">[00:15:15]</a>.
