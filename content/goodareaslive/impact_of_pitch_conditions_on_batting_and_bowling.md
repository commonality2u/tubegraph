## Modern Bowling Techniques and the Kookaburra Ball

The Kookaburra ball, with its reinforced seam and extra lacquer, allows for consistent seam movement for longer periods <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:33:56">[00:33:56]</a>. This, combined with faster and more accurate bowlers who utilize techniques like the "wobble ball," contributes to the current batting struggles <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:34:04">[00:34:04]</a>.

The "wobble ball" allows bowlers to maintain an exaggerated seam for a longer period by only seaming one in three deliveries <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:50:07">[00:50:07]</a>. This variation makes it harder for batters to predict movement, similar to natural variation in spin bowling <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:50:32">[00:50:32]</a>. Almost every test bowling attack in the world now includes a wobble ball specialist <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:48:57">[00:48:57]</a>.
