### Fast Bowlers
The effectiveness of fast bowling in Test [[cricket_and_player_performances | Cricket]] relies on more than just raw pace. The ability to combine pace and bounce with lateral movement makes it most dangerous <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:03">[00:02:03]</a>.

Modern fast bowlers, influenced by white-ball cricket, often focus on accuracy and varied deliveries. It's becoming rare to see short and wide balls that lead to cut shots, with more straight deliveries resulting in uppercuts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:12">[00:20:12]</a>.
