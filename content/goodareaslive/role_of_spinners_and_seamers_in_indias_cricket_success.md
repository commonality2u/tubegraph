## India's Best Side Ever?

The current Indian team, particularly due to its bowling attack, is considered by the speaker as "the best Indian side I've ever seen" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:39:21">[00:39:21]</a>. While past eras like the Ganguly era had incredible batting talent, they lacked the depth of a consistently great four-person bowling attack <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:39:42">[00:39:42]</a>. The current team has two great seamers and potentially four great spinners (Ashwin, Jadeja, Axar Patel, and Kuldeep Yadav) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:40:47">[00:40:47]</a>, an unprecedented depth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:41:13">[00:41:13]</a>. The all-round abilities of Jadeja and Ashwin, and the potential inclusion of Hardik Pandya, further enhance the team's flexibility <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:41:17">[00:41:17]</a>. The argument is that while batting provides consistency, bowling wins Test matches <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:42:26">[00:42:26]</a>, and this team's bowling depth is what sets it apart.
