## Influence of White Ball Cricket on Test Bowling
White ball cricket has significantly influenced test bowling, particularly in developing [[cricket_strategies_and_player_techniques | attacking bowling techniques]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:05">[00:19:05]</a>. It has helped bowlers manipulate the ball more effectively and become comfortable with many different kinds of deliveries <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:18">[00:19:18]</a>. A key impact is the increased emphasis on accuracy for fast bowlers, as inaccuracy is heavily penalized in white ball formats <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:35">[00:19:35]</a>. This has led to a "completely different kind of cricket," where traditional shots like the cut shot are less common due to straighter bowling lines <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:27">[00:20:27]</a>.
