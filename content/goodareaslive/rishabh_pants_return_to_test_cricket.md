Returning from a severe car accident, particularly for a Test batter, presents unique physical challenges:
*   Injuries to the back and knees can significantly affect a batter's ability to play long innings or impact their bat path <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:06">[00:20:06]</a>.
*   Len Hutton's famous arm injury, which shortened his reach, reportedly didn't affect his batting <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:42">[00:19:42]</a>. However, Pant's type of injury, involving major body parts like the back, knees, or neck, is different and more likely to affect long innings in Test cricket <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:09">[00:20:09]</a>.
