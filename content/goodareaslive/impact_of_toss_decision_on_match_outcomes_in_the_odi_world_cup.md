## Strategic Considerations for the Toss
The decision at the toss is deemed a "massive factor" for the semi-finals of the World Cup <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="52:46:00">[52:46:00]</a>. One potential advantage of bowling second, particularly in the second innings, is the "prodigious swing under lights" during the early overs (from 0 to 15) in many grounds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="53:07:00">[53:07:00]</a>. This can create a significant challenge for the chasing team.

However, traditionally, it has been easier to bat first in World Cups than to chase <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="53:26:00">[53:26:00]</a>. Unless a team has a very strong reason or specific matchup advantage, batting first is generally preferred <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="53:30:00">[53:30:00]</a>.
