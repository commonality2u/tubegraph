## Debates on Player Comparisons

The competition highlighted the difficulty in comparing players across different eras, even when "adjusted numbers" were provided <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:25">[00:17:25]</a>. Discussions often revolved around "raw numbers" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:18">[00:05:18]</a>, strike rates, averages <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:53">[00:09:53]</a>, and adjusted statistics reflecting different eras <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:05">[00:10:05]</a>.
