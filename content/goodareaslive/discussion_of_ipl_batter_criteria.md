## Selection Criteria

The following filters and metrics were used to compile the list of 64 IPL batters:

*   **Minimum Runs Filter**
    A minimum filter of 1,000 runs was applied to include both older and newer batters fairly <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:34">[00:00:34]</a>.
*   **Impact Score**
    Players with an "impact" score of less than zero were removed from the list <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>.
    *   **Definition of Impact:** Impact measures how much a batter changes the predicted team score <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>. For example, if a batter enters with a predicted score of 160 and hits a six that raises the prediction to 163, their impact on that specific ball is +3 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:51">[00:00:51]</a>.
*   **True Average and True Strike Rate**
    Players whose "true average" and "true strike rate" were negative were also excluded <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:59">[00:00:59]</a>.
    *   This rule was specifically noted for players like RV (whose true average and true strike rate were negative) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:59">[00:00:59]</a> and Jack Kalispo <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:11">[00:01:11]</a>.
    *   The purpose was to remove individuals with a negative true average and true combination <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:31">[00:10:31]</a>.
