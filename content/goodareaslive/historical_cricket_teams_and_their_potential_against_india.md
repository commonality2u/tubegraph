## Criteria for Success Against India
To assess which historical teams could challenge the current Indian side, five key factors were established [00:11:47]:
1.  **Fast Spinners:** Spinners who bowl quicker are more effective against Indian batters, especially on rank turners [00:13:30]. Slow spinners tend to struggle [00:14:02].
2.  **Flat Pitch Quicks:** Fast bowlers capable of reverse swinging the ball or being deadly accurate on flat pitches [00:13:06].
3.  **Batting Depth:** A strong lower order to withstand India's potent bowling attack [00:12:54].
4.  **All-rounders:** Needed to provide a fifth bowling option, matching India's five-bowler strategy [00:12:42].
5.  **Few Left-handers:** To minimize the impact of Ravichandran Ashwin, who has a strong record against left-handed batters [00:11:59, 00:13:36]. More than two left-handers would likely lead to a series loss [00:13:37].
