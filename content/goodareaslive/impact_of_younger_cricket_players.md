## Strategic Implications for India

The emergence of Nitesh Kumar Ready and Washington Sundar as a potential number six and seven combination provides India with an interesting strategic advantage <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="12:57:00">[12:57:00]</a>. This partnership means India could have permanent five bowling options and use their all-rounders when conditions favor them, rather than being forced to use them constantly <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="13:10:00">[13:10:00]</a>.

India has struggled with consistent first-innings totals recently <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="14:15:00">[14:15:00]</a>, placing pressure on bowlers like Ashwin, Jadeja, and Bumrah to compensate <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="14:51:00">[14:51:00]</a>. The performances of these younger players, particularly in the recent match, have saved the innings and kept the Test match and potentially the series alive <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="15:14:00">[15:14:00]</a>.

## [[challenges_and_expectations_for_young_cricket_talents | Challenges and Investment in Young Talent]]

The journey of all-rounders like Sundar, Ready, and even historical figures like Daniel Vettori, Garfield Sobers, Imran Khan, and Mitch Marsh, highlights a common pattern: they often develop their skills over time rather than arriving "fully formed" in Test cricket <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="22:06:00">[22:06:00]</a>. The challenge for teams is the modern tendency to expect instant success from [[emerging_cricket_talents_and_their_impact | young players]], leading to a reluctance to invest long-term in their development <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="24:22:00">[24:22:00]</a>.

Some players, like Ashton Agar, initially showed promise with the bat but didn't develop their bowling sufficiently, leading to a "conundrum" for the team <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="33:22:00">[33:22:00]</a>. Conversely, Nitesh Kumar Ready's success as a batter, even if he doesn't fully develop as a bowler, has "flipped" the problem for India, making him a batting asset rather than a positional issue <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="33:54:00">[33:54:00]</a>. This demonstrates the dynamic [[impact_of_star_players_in_cricket | impact of young players]] who adapt and excel, even if not in their initially intended role.
