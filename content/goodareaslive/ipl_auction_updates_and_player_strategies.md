## Key Auction Highlights

### Shreyas Iyer (Shi)
Shreyas Iyer, often referred to as "Shi," broke auction records with a final bid of 26.75 crores (CR), significantly higher than the initial expectation of 18-20 CR <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:04">[03:04]</a>. Delhi and Punjab were engaged in a bidding war for him <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:11">[03:11]</a>. While the price is considered an overpay, it reflects the demand for a captaincy option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:34">[06:34]</a>. Despite being 29 years old, a captain, and having batting flexibility, 26 CR is viewed as a huge overpay, especially considering uncertainty about his best batting position and whether he'd be among the top four batters in a side <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="47:00">[47:00]</a>.

### Rishabh Pant
Rishabh Pant's auction price reached 27 CR, with LSG being the acquiring team <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="29:02">[29:02]</a>. The speaker would prefer him at 27 CR over Shreyas Iyer at 26.75 CR <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="29:20">[29:20]</a>.
