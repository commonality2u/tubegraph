## Tournament Structure and Criteria
The tournament began with a list of 64 top IPL batters <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:31">[00:00:31]</a>. The initial list was compiled using specific criteria:
*   A minimum filter of 1,000 runs to include both older and newer batters <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:36">[00:00:36]</a>.
*   Removal of players with an "impact" less than zero <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>. Impact is defined as how much a player changes the predicted score, e.g., hitting a six that raises the predicted score by three runs results in a +3 impact on that ball <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>.
*   Removal of players whose "true average" and "true strike rate" were negative <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:59">[00:00:59]</a>.

The goal was to eliminate 63 players to crown a "GOAT" (Greatest Of All Time) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:23">[00:01:23]</a>.
