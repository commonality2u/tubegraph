## The Role of AI in Customer Engagement
Traditionally, customer engagement relied heavily on human interaction. Today, AI-powered systems are introducing new efficiencies and strategies. [[ai_voice_applications_and_technology | AI voice applications]] are a significant part of this shift, offering both inbound and outbound capabilities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="14:36:00">[14:36:00]</a>.

### Inbound vs. Outbound AI Voice
Initially, Trejan was drawn to AI for its potential in cold calling, aiming to automate a task he found undesirable <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="15:07:00">[15:07:00]</a>. While AI can still be used for business-to-business cold calling by clearly announcing the AI's identity and purpose, data has shown that this isn't always the most effective method for generating traffic <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="15:32:00">[15:32:00]</a>.

A more effective outbound strategy has proven to be [[ai_solutions_for_lead_management_and_customer_engagement | ringless voicemails]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="15:57:00">[15:57:00]</a>. By deploying thousands of voicemails and backing them up with text messages, AI can manage inbound replies, nurture conversations, and even schedule appointments, significantly boosting efficiency in [[application_of_ai_agents_in_lead_management_and_customer_success | lead management]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="16:31:00">[16:31:00]</a>.

The key to success in these endeavors is a relentless focus on data and providing undeniable value <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="16:53:00">[16:53:00]</a>.

### Legality and Best Practices
When using AI for outreach, especially with synthetic voice, it's crucial to understand the legal landscape. Trejan advises opting for opt-in methods, clearly announcing the AI's identity, and focusing on business-to-business propositions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="18:04:00">[18:04:00]</a>. He emphasizes that if a human can make a business proposition without it being illegal, an AI doing the same for a business is generally acceptable (though users should consult TCPA regulations for compliance) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="18:12:00">[18:12:00]</a>. Additionally, backing up AI outreach with high-volume organic content creation by sharing value across platforms is a robust strategy for long-term momentum and audience engagement <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="18:38:00">[18:38:00]</a>.

## Building Trust and Effective AI Interactions
A common concern is whether AI voices can sound too "slick" or artificial, breaking trust. Trejan's perspective is that people primarily care about value and efficiency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="34:28:00">[34:28:00]</a>. In customer support, for instance, users often prefer an intelligent AI communicator who can quickly provide information over waiting on hold for a human <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="34:41:00">[34:41:00]</a>. The objective is to efficiently and effectively provide the information needed, regardless of whether the voice is human or synthetic <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="35:25:00">[35:25:00]</a>.

Building trust with [[ai_in_customer_support_and_efficiency_gains | AI agents]] is akin to human relationships: announce yourself, ask valuable questions, and offer something of value <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="28:56:00">[28:56:00]</a>. This approach makes the interaction clear and beneficial, leading to engagement.

## Implementing AI Solutions in Business
The landscape of AI implementation is moving towards standardization and accessibility. Previously, integrating AI required navigating complex documentation and fragmented packages <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="26:00:00">[26:00:00]</a>. Now, companies are making it simpler for anyone to get an AI up and running on their website or platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="26:47:00">[26:47:00]</a>. Even if a clear step-by-step process isn't immediately available, tools like Claude can guide users through the process <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="26:55:00">[26:55:00]</a>.

For businesses looking to integrate AI, Trejan advises focusing on solving one specific bottleneck at a time to achieve a tangible goal <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="31:57:00">[31:57:00]</a>. This iterative approach allows businesses to collect data, assess the next biggest bottleneck, and continue scaling <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="32:02:00">[32:02:00]</a>. Rather than getting lost in a "grand grandiose vision," the focus should be on actionable steps and delivering real value <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="33:12:00">[33:12:00]</a>.

A core principle for success in the AI era is taking "massive imperfect action" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="53:36:00">[53:36:00]</a>. This means consistent, high-volume outreach and content creation, which leads to more feedback and opportunities for improvement. The ability to "crank volume" is a significant lever that [[automating_business_processes_with_ai_agents | AI empowers businesses of any size]] to pull <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="49:50:00">[49:50:00]</a>.

## The Future of AI in Customer Success
Looking ahead, Trejan envisions a future where AI becomes even more deeply integrated into daily life and business:

*   **AI-Powered Hardware:** Expect to see more AI-powered physical products for consumers, such as devices that record conversations, track habits, and augment reality, potentially leading to [[integrating_ai_tools_into_operational_processes | smart glasses]] that provide real-time translation and communication <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="39:42:00">[39:42:00]</a>. This could eliminate language barriers and foster more effective communication <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="40:25:00">[40:25:00]</a>.
*   **Native Voice AI Platforms:** In the next year, more platforms, including major CRM systems like Salesforce, will likely integrate native [[ai_voice_applications_and_technology | voice-enabled features]] for customer support <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="40:47:00">[40:47:00]</a>.
*   **Agent-to-Agent (A2A) Communication:** Within three years, AI agents will communicate directly with each other to handle everyday transactions. For example, a personal AI assistant could book flights and hotels by interacting with airline and hotel agents <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="42:12:00">[42:12:00]</a>. This means most transactions could be fully automated, with human oversight for confirmation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="42:50:00">[42:50:00]</a>.
*   **Augmented and Virtual Reality Workspaces:** Holographic overlays and digital workspaces within physical environments will become common <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="43:56:00">[43:56:00]</a>. Virtual reality will offer drag-and-drop workflow builders, allowing businesses to visualize and manage their operations in a virtual space <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="44:47:00">[44:47:00]</a>.
*   **Refined Communication:** As AI becomes more prevalent, the ability to clearly articulate expectations and communicate effectively—both with humans and AI—will be paramount <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="45:43:00">[45:45:00]</a>.
