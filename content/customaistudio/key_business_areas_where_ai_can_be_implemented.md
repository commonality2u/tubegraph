### Business Structure and AI Opportunities

Most businesses, beyond very small operations, typically exhibit a hierarchical structure <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:31">[00:03:31]</a>. This structure includes founders/managers/team leads overseeing various personnel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:57">[00:03:57]</a>. The organizational breakdown can be seen as:
*   **Job Function:** Overall role (e.g., Sales Development Representative - SDR) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:14">[00:05:14]</a>.
*   **Workflows:** Various processes within a job function (e.g., prospecting, outreach, engagement) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:16">[00:05:16]</a>.
*   **Microtasks:** Specific, repetitive actions within each workflow (e.g., navigating LinkedIn, data entry) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:22">[00:05:22]</a>.

Often, entry-level roles involve numerous repetitive tasks that are vital to the business's survival <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:27">[00:04:27]</a>. These microtasks within workflows are prime candidates for [[automating_business_processes_with_ai_agents | AI automation]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:09">[00:07:09]</a>.
