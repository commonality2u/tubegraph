*   **Founder/Manager/Team Lead:** At the top, managing various individuals <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:59">[00:03:59]</a>.
*   **Job Functions:** People are managed based on different or similar job functions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:04">[00:05:04]</a>.
*   **Workflows:** Within each job function are various workflows to complete the job <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:16">[00:05:16]</a>.
*   **Microtasks:** Each workflow consists of various microtasks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:20">[00:05:20]</a>.
