## Business Structure and AI Integration

Most businesses, beyond very small ones, typically follow a hierarchical pyramid structure <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:31">[00:03:31]</a>. This structure usually includes a founder or manager at the top, managing various team members, who in turn might manage others <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:01">[00:04:01]</a>. At the base of this pyramid are entry-level roles often responsible for repetitive, labor-intensive tasks crucial to the business's survival <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:16">[00:04:16]</a>.

A business function can be broken down into:
*   **Job Function:** The overarching role (e.g., SDR) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:14">[00:05:14]</a>.
*   **Workflows:** Various processes to complete that job (e.g., prospecting workflow for an SDR) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:18">[00:05:18]</a>.
*   **Microtasks:** Individual steps within each workflow (e.g., navigating to LinkedIn, searching for ICP, summarizing profiles) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:22">[00:05:22]</a>.

This granular breakdown reveals where [[Impact of Agentic AI on business automation | AI can fit in]] and automate tasks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:40">[00:10:40]</a>.

## AI Agent Design Philosophy

The [[designing_and_integrating_ai_agent_teams_to_automate_business_functions | blueprint for AI agent design]] is straightforward:
*   Each workflow can be assigned to one AI agent <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:04">[00:09:04]</a>.
*   Multiple agents working together can fulfill an entire job function <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:07">[00:09:07]</a>.

To achieve this, agents require:
*   **Tools:** Specific tools needed to accomplish their workflow <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:50">[00:10:50]</a>.
*   **Instructions:** Clear directives to ensure reliable workflow execution <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:58">[00:10:58]</a>.
*   **Data:** Contextual awareness and understanding for handling tasks effectively <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:03">[00:11:03]</a>.

This approach ensures [[integrating_ai_agents_with_business_tools | AI agents]] are built with a clear purpose and strategy, making them scalable and efficient <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:46">[00:12:46]</a>.
