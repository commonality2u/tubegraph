### Understanding Fat Metabolism

Fat metabolism is a two-step process:

1. **Fat Mobilization (Lipolysis):** Fat must be mobilized, which involves breaking down stored triglycerides into free fatty acids and glycerol. This step is essential to positioning fat for oxidation.
2. **Fat Oxidation:** Once mobilized, these fatty acids must then be oxidized in energy-producing cells, namely mitochondria, converting them into adenosine triphosphate (ATP) for energy use. 

The nervous system can profoundly affect each step in this process through distinct pathways and chemical releases.
