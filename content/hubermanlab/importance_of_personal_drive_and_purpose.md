## The Genesis of the Huberman Lab Podcast

Andrew Huberman attributes the birth of the Huberman Lab Podcast to Dr. Lex Fridman, who initially encouraged him to start his own. Fridman only gave Huberman two pieces of advice: first, to start a podcast, and second, to ensure it was not just him talking into a microphone without purpose. Huberman reflects on this pivotal moment, stating, “There would not be a Huberman Lab Podcast, were it not for Lex Fridman” <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:01">[00:09:01]</a>.
