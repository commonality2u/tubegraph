## Guys Farm: A Case Study in High APY Farming

Guys Farm launched on the Phantom network on October 6th <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:17">[00:01:17]</a>. It rapidly gained traction, growing its Total Value Locked (TVL) by $3 billion in a single day <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:24">[00:01:24]</a>. The platform functions similarly to a money market like Aave, allowing users to lend and borrow assets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:26">[00:01:26]</a>.

At its inception, Guys Farm offered an exceptionally [[understanding_supply_apy_and_stake_apr | high APY]] of approximately 10,000% for its liquidity pools <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:46">[00:04:46]</a>.

### Platform Mechanics

*   **Decentralized and Non-Custodial**: Guys Farm aims to differentiate itself by having no governance and no Venture Capital (VC) involvement, with all revenue directed back to token holders and equity providers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:20">[00:02:20]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:26">[00:02:26]</a>.
*   **Rewards Structure**: Users earn GUYS tokens as rewards for lending, borrowing, or providing liquidity to the GUYS-FTM liquidity pool <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:37">[00:02:37]</a>. Airdrops are also available for supplying on Curve <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:46">[00:02:46]</a>.
*   **Vesting and Penalties**: To prevent immediate selling and maintain ecosystem stability, a 50% penalty is applied if rewards are claimed before a 90-day holding period <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:02">[00:03:02]</a>.
*   **Locking Option**: Users can choose to lock their GUYS tokens for three months, which allows them to earn platform fees and supernative fees, but prevents early exit <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:26">[00:03:26]</a>.
*   **Security Measures**: Although the team is anonymous, they implemented a multisig (multi-signature) system for code changes. Three out of five high-profile signatories (including representatives from File Foundation, SpookySwap, and DeFi Llama) must sign off on any modifications <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:44">[00:03:44]</a>. A two-day time lock is also in place for transparency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:21">[00:04:21]</a>.

## Risks and Lessons Learned from High APY Farming

Despite initial high [[understanding_supply_apy_and_stake_apr | APY]]s, participating in new farms like Guys Farm carries significant risks, as illustrated by a personal experience of being down approximately 20% on an initial investment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:28">[00:05:28]</a>.

### Key Risks

1.  **Triple Hit of Volatility**:
    *   **Farming Token Price Depreciation**: The price of the farming token (GUYS) can drop significantly. For example, GUYS dropped from $8 to $1.95 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:42">[00:05:42]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:30">[00:08:30]</a>.
    *   **Decreasing APY**: As more participants join the farm or the reward emissions decrease, the [[understanding_supply_apy_and_stake_apr | APY]] naturally declines very quickly <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:14">[00:07:14]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:28">[00:07:28]</a>.
    *   **Liquidity Pool Value Depreciation**: If the liquidity pool consists of two volatile assets (e.g., GUYS and FTM), and both asset prices decline, the total value of the provided liquidity can drop substantially <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:47">[00:08:47]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:00">[00:09:00]</a>. This "triple hit" can lead to significant losses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:16">[00:10:16]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:21">[00:10:21]</a>.

2.  **Rapid Capital Flight**: Capital can move out of platforms much faster than expected. The speaker observed funds swiftly migrating from the Phantom network to Harmony due to new liquidity mining programs, such as those on [[evaluating_and_managing_yield_farming_risks | DeFi Kingdom]], Sushi, and Curve <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:35">[00:10:35]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:11">[00:11:11]</a>. This quick exodus drastically reduces a farm's TVL and profitability <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:35">[00:11:35]</a>.

### Strategic Mistakes and Considerations

*   **Jumping in Early**: While tempting, immediately investing in a new farm with extremely [[understanding_supply_apy_and_stake_apr | high APY]] can be risky, even if the team seems decent <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:28">[00:17:28]</a>. The rapid capital flow makes such early participation speculative.
*   **Buying Farm Tokens**: A common rule in yield farming, as advised by channels like "So Tacky," is to avoid buying the farm tokens directly, especially to provide liquidity. Breaking this rule (e.g., buying GUYS to pair with FTM) can expose one to greater risk when the farm token's price declines <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:41">[00:18:41]</a>.
*   **Degenerate Farming**: Participating in [[high_apy_and_staking_mechanics_in_meme_currencies | high APY]] pools is often considered "degen" (degenerate) farming, requiring constant monitoring and a high risk tolerance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:37">[00:13:37]</a>.

### Mitigation Strategies

*   **Stablecoin Farming**: Using stablecoins (like USDT, DAI, USDC) for farming can mitigate exposure to asset pricing volatility since their value remains pegged to $1 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:00">[00:13:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:06">[00:13:06]</a>. While the returns might be slower, this approach offers greater stability unless you have significant capital (e.g., five-figure investments) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:24">[00:13:24]</a>.
*   **Continuous Monitoring**: For non-stablecoin liquidity pools, constant monitoring of token prices, [[understanding_supply_apy_and_stake_apr | APY]], and overall platform TVL is crucial to react quickly to market changes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:35">[00:12:35]</a>.
*   **Long-Term Strategy**: If confident in a platform's long-term potential (e.g., strong TVL relative to its network), one might choose to vest rewards and lock them for longer periods to earn platform fees, even if it means short-term unrealized losses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:56">[00:15:56]</a>. This strategy acknowledges potential temporary downturns for future gains <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:36">[00:16:36]</a>.
