## [[differences_between_binance_sg_and_binance_global | Binance Singapore]] (Binance SG)

[[differences_between_binance_sg_and_binance_global | Binance Singapore]] is a subsidiary of Binance and is regulated by the Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>.

### Buying and Selling on Binance SG

*   **Currency**: All [[buying_and_selling_cryptocurrency_on_binance | transactions]] on the platform are conducted via Singapore Dollars (SGD) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:28">[00:00:28]</a>.
*   **Fees**: [[regulation_and_fees_on_binance_sg_vs_binance_global | Trading fees]] are set at 0.6% <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:32">[00:00:32]</a>.
*   **Cryptocurrencies**: Only eight cryptocurrencies are available for [[buying_and_selling_cryptocurrency_on_binance | trading]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:34">[00:00:34]</a>.
*   **[[deposits_and_withdrawals_on_binance_for_singapore_residents | Deposits]]**: To [[deposits_and_withdrawals_on_binance_for_singapore_residents | deposit money]] into [[differences_between_binance_sg_and_binance_global | Binance Singapore]], a third-party payment provider called Xfers is required <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[00:00:37]</a>.
*   **[[deposits_and_withdrawals_on_binance_for_singapore_residents | Withdrawal]] Fees**: There are no fees for [[deposits_and_ Ch]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:43">[00:00:43]</a>.

> [!warning] Important Note
> The wallets on [[differences_between_binance_sg_and_binance_global | Binance Singapore]] and [[differences_between_binance_sg_and_binance_global | Binance Global]] are not linked; they are two separate wallets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>. Therefore, you cannot [[buying_and_selling_cryptocurrency_on_binance | buy]] on [[differences_between_binance_sg_and_binance_global | Binance Singapore]] and [[buying_and_selling_cryptocurrency_on_binance | sell]] on [[differences_between_binance_sg_and_binance_global | Binance Global]] directly <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:32">[00:01:32]</a>.

## [[differences_between_binance_sg_and_binance_global | Binance Global]] (Binance.com)

[[differences_between_binance_sg_and_binance_global | Binance Global]] (binance.com) is not regulated by MAS <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>.

### Buying Crypto on Binance Global

*   **Currency**: All [[buying_and_selling_cryptocurrency_on_binance | transactions]] are primarily via USD <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:45">[00:00:45]</a>.
*   **Fees**: [[regulation_and_fees_on_binance_sg_vs_binance_global | Transaction fees]] are generally lower than on [[differences_between_binance_sg_and_binance_global | Binance Singapore]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:47">[00:00:47]</a>. An additional discount on [[regulation_and_fees_on_binance_sg_vs_binance_global | trading fees]] is available when [[buying_and_selling_cryptocurrency_on_binance | buying]] with Binance Coin (BNB) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>.
*   **Cryptocurrencies**: A much larger variety of cryptocurrencies is supported <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:53">[00:00:53]</a>.
*   **[[transaction_methods_for_buying_and_selling_on_binance_platforms | Buying Methods]]**: There are three main ways to [[buying_and_selling_cryptocurrency_on_binance | buy crypto]] on [[differences_between_binance_sg_and_binance_global | Binance Global]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:58">[00:00:58]</a>:
    1.  **Credit Card**: Direct purchase using a credit card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>.
    2.  **P2P (Peer-to-Peer)**: This method functions like a crowdsale, where you give SGD to another person in exchange for crypto <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:02">[00:01:02]</a>.
    3.  **Transferring BNB**: You can [[buying_and_selling_cryptocurrency_on_binance | buy]] Binance Coin (BNB) on [[differences_between_binance_sg_and_binance_global | Binance Singapore]], then transfer it to [[differences_between_binance_sg_and_binance_global | Binance Global]] to [[buying_and_selling_cryptocurrency_on_binance | purchase]] other desired cryptocurrencies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:11">[00:01:11]</a>.

### Selling Crypto on Binance Global

*   **[[transaction_methods_for_buying_and_selling_on_binance_platforms | Selling Methods]]**: The opposite process is true when [[buying_and_selling_cryptocurrency_on_binance | selling]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:17">[00:01:17]</a>:
    1.  **P2P (Peer-to-Peer)**: You can [[buying_and_selling_cryptocurrency_on_binance | sell]] your crypto to someone who gives you SGD in return <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:19">[00:01:19]</a>.
    2.  **Transferring BNB**: You can convert your crypto back into Binance Coin (BNB), send it back to [[differences_between_binance_sg_and_binance_global | Binance Singapore]], and then convert it into SGD <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:24">[00:01:24]</a>.
