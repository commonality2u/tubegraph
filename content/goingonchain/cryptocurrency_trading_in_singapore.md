To begin [[investing_in_cryptocurrency | investing in cryptocurrency]] in Singapore, one common approach involves using platforms like Binance SG and Binance Global (Binance.com) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:05">[00:00:05]</a>. These platforms are distinct entities, despite their shared branding <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>.

## [[differences_between_binance_sg_and_binance_global | Differences between Binance SG and Binance Global]]

### Binance SG

Binance Singapore (Binance SG) is a subsidiary of Binance and is regulated by the Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>.

Key features of Binance SG include:
*   **Transaction Currency:** All transactions on Binance SG are conducted via Singapore Dollars (SGD) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:28">[00:00:28]</a>.
*   **Trading Fees:** The trading fee is 0.6% <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:31">[00:00:31]</a>.
*   **Supported Cryptocurrencies:** Only eight cryptocurrencies are available for trading <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:34">[00:00:34]</a>.
*   **Deposits and Withdrawals:** To deposit funds into Binance SG, a third-party payment provider called Xfers is required <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[00:00:37]</a>. There are no fees for deposits or withdrawals <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>.

### Binance Global (Binance.com)

Binance Global (Binance.com) is not regulated by the MAS <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>.

Key features of Binance Global include:
*   **Transaction Currency:** All transactions are conducted via United States Dollars (USD) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:45">[00:00:45]</a>.
*   **Trading Fees:** Transaction fees are significantly lower than on Binance SG <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:47">[00:00:47]</a>. Additionally, buying with Binance Coin (BNB) can provide a discount on trading fees <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>.
*   **Supported Cryptocurrencies:** A much wider range of cryptocurrencies is supported on Binance Global <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:53">[00:00:53]</a>.

## [[methods_for_buying_and_selling_cryptocurrency | Methods for Buying and Selling Cryptocurrency]]

There are several [[methods_for_buying_and_selling_cryptocurrency | ways to buy and sell cryptocurrency]] on Binance Global:

### Buying Crypto on Binance Global
1.  **Credit Card:** Use a credit card to purchase cryptocurrency directly <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>.
2.  **Peer-to-Peer (P2P):** Engage in P2P transactions where you transfer SGD to another individual, who then transfers the cryptocurrency to you <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:02">[00:01:02]</a>.
3.  **Binance Coin (BNB) Transfer:** Purchase Binance Coin (BNB) on Binance SG, transfer it to Binance Global, and then use the BNB to buy other desired cryptocurrencies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>.

### Selling Crypto on Binance Global
1.  **Peer-to-Peer (P2P):** Sell your cryptocurrency via P2P, receiving SGD from another individual <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:17">[00:01:17]</a>.
2.  **Binance Coin (BNB) Transfer:** Convert your cryptocurrency back into Binance Coin (BNB), send the BNB back to Binance SG, and then convert it into Singapore Dollars <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:24">[00:01:24]</a>.

### Important Note
The wallets for Binance SG and Binance Global are not linked <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>. This means you cannot directly buy cryptocurrency on Binance SG and then sell it on Binance Global, as they operate as two separate wallets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:32">[00:01:32]</a>.
