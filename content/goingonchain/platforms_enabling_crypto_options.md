## Specific Crypto Options Platforms

Several projects are emerging to offer crypto options, attracting Total Value Locked (TVL) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:34">[00:00:34]</a>. Notable [[specific_crypto_option_platforms|specific crypto option platforms]] include:

*   **Ribbon Finance**: Offers options for Ethereum (ETH) and Avalanche (AVAX) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>. Ribbon Finance is currently a leader in the crypto options space, with a TVL close to $300 million <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:40">[00:00:40]</a>.
*   **Tatnuts**: Provides options for Fantom (FTM) and Polygon (MATIC) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:31">[00:00:31]</a>.
*   **Lyra**: Another platform in the [[crypto_options_market|crypto option]] sector showing growing TVL <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>.

While $300 million TVL is small compared to the overall Decentralized Finance (DeFi) sector, it highlights the significant [[growth_potential_of_crypto_options|growth potential of crypto options]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:45">[00:00:45]</a>. Investment in crypto often involves following narratives and money flow, and this area is demonstrating both <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:57">[00:00:57]</a>.

### Tatnuts: A Platform of Interest

Tatnuts is a platform of particular interest for several reasons:

*   It is considered a top [[specific_crypto_option_platforms|crypto option platform]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:06">[00:01:06]</a>.
*   It operates as a multi-chain play, deployed across various blockchain networks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:08">[00:01:08]</a>.
*   Significantly, it has not yet launched its own token, making it an interesting space to monitor for future developments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:12">[00:01:12]</a>.
