From: [[goingonchain]] <br/> 

[[clipto_platform_overview | Clipto]] is a Web3 service platform that allows users to request personalized videos from creators, similar to Cameo, but with [[introduction_to_cryptocurrencies | cryptocurrency]] payments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:00">[00:00:00]</a>. The platform operates on the Matic Chain, which helps keep transaction fees low <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:33:00">[00:33:00]</a>.

## [[methods_for_buying_and_selling_cryptocurrency | Cryptocurrency]] Payments
All payments on [[clipto_platform_overview | Clipto]] are made in [[introduction_to_cryptocurrencies | cryptocurrency]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:00:53">[01:00:53]</a>. This enables creators to receive [[earning_interest_on_crypto_assets | crypto payments]] directly, a feature often not permitted on Web2 platforms <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:54:99">[01:54:99]</a>.

## Creator Pricing
Creators on [[clipto_platform_overview | Clipto]] set their own prices for personalized videos <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:05:00">[01:05:00]</a>. For example, one creator charges "five dramatic for video" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:05:00">[01:05:00]</a>. Users can view a creator's portfolio and details on their profile before submitting a request <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:07:00">[01:07:00]</a>.

## Platform Fees
[[clipto_platform_overview | Clipto]] charges a 5% fee for each booking <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:37:00">[01:37:00]</a>.

## Refund Policy
To protect users, [[clipto_platform_overview | Clipto]] has a refund policy in place: if a creator fails to deliver the requested video, the client receives a refund <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:43:00">[01:43:00]</a>.

## User Monetization via NFTs
A unique feature of [[how_clipto_functions_on_the_blockchain | Clipto's system]] is that users receive their personalized videos in [[introduction_to_cryptocurrencies | NFT]] format <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:24:99">[01:24:99]</a>. This allows users the option to resell the video on platforms like OpenSea if they choose <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:28:00">[01:28:00]</a>.

## Future Token
Currently, [[clipto_platform_overview | Clipto]] does not have its own project token <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:31:99">[01:31:99]</a>. However, they are in the process of establishing a Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:33:99">[01:33:99]</a>.
