The speaker employs a dollar-cost averaging (DCA) strategy to accumulate strong projects, including Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Fantom (FTM), and Merit Circle <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:16">[00:00:16]</a>.

## Benefits and Application
*   The [[market_timing_vs_dollar_cost_averaging | DCA strategy]] is used to avoid "catching the falling knife" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:29">[00:00:29]</a>.
*   The optimal period for accumulating assets is during a bear market <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:24">[00:00:24]</a>. However, investors must be prepared to "stomach the pain" associated with market downturns <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>.
