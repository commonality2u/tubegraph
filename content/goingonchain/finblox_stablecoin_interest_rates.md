Finblox offers competitive annual percentage yields (APY) for stablecoins, making it a potential option for those looking to earn yield on their digital assets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:07">[00:00:07]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:09">[00:00:09]</a>.

## Offered Rates
*   **USDC and USDT:** 15% APY <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:11">[00:00:11]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:14">[00:00:14]</a>.
*   **SGD Coin (xSGD):** 6.5% APY <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:18">[00:00:18]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>.
    *   xSGD is a stablecoin by StraitsX (formerly known as Xfours) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:31">[00:00:31]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:33">[00:00:33]</a>. Users can mint SGD into xSGD through StraitsX <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:34">[00:00:34]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:36">[00:00:36]</a>.
    *   While Finblox supports cheaper chains for deposits, minting xSGD on StraitsX involves ERC20, which may incur higher fees <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:40">[00:01:40]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:43">[00:01:43]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:47">[00:01:47]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:48">[00:01:48]</a>.
