### What are Stablecoins?
[[types_of_stablecoins | Stablecoins]] are cryptocurrencies designed to maintain a stable value, often by being backed by fiat currencies like the U.S. dollar <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:55">[00:02:55]</a>. For example, [[types_of_stablecoins | USDC]] can be thought of as buying U.S. dollars in crypto form <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:12">[00:01:12]</a>. Other examples include [[types_of_stablecoins | USDT]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:54">[00:02:54]</a>.

### Benefits of Investing with Stablecoins

*   **Attractive Interest Rates**
    [[types_of_stablecoins | Stablecoins]] can offer attractive interest rates when lent out on crypto lending platforms <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:59">[00:02:59]</a>. For instance, lending 10,000 [[types_of_stablecoins | USDC]] could potentially yield an annual interest of 860, which is significantly higher than what traditional banks in Singapore offer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:21">[00:01:21]</a>. The rates discussed were correct as of June 20, 2021 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:04">[00:03:04]</a>.
