Clipto is a service platform described as the [[overview_of_clipto_and_its_relation_to_cameo | web3 version of Cameo]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:09">[00:00:09]</a>. It functions similarly to [[overview_of_clipto_and_its_relation_to_cameo | Cameo]], allowing users to pay a fee to have a favorite star or creator make a personalized video for them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:16">[00:00:16]</a>. For instance, a user could request the voice of Scooby-Doo to sing a birthday song for a friend <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>.

## Becoming a Creator

Clipto is built on the Magnetic Chain, which allows for low fees <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:33">[00:00:33]</a>. The process for joining Clipto as a creator is designed to be simple and permissionless <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:40">[00:00:40]</a>.

*   **Ease of Sign-Up**: To become a creator, users simply need to click on the "becoming a creator" option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:38">[00:00:38]</a>.
*   **Permissionless Access**: Anyone can join the platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:40">[00:00:40]</a>.
*   **Requirements**: To get started, a creator needs to connect a wallet and make a tweet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:43">[00:00:43]</a>.
*   **Platform Philosophy**: The project's creator, Greybro, initiated Clipto after his application to [[overview_of_clipto_and_its_relation_to_cameo | Cameo]] was rejected, driven by a belief that there should be no gatekeepers restricting creators <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:53">[00:00:53]</a>.

## Creator Participation and Features

Currently, the creators on Clipto are primarily [[introduction_to_cryptocurrency | crypto]] natives <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>.

*   **Setting Your Price**: Creators set their own fees for videos; for example, one creator charges five Dramatic for a video <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:02">[00:01:02]</a>.
*   **Profile Details**: On a creator's profile, potential clients can view their portfolio, key in their specific video requests, and access their Twitter profile <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>. Some creators might charge higher fees based on their specialized skills, such as animation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a>.
*   **[[nft_integration_in_clipto_for_video_content | NFT Integration]]**: A unique feature of Clipto is that clients receive their personalized video in an [[nft_integration_in_clipto_for_video_content | NFT]] format <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:24">[00:01:24]</a>. This allows the recipient the option to resell the video on platforms like OpenC <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:28">[00:01:28]</a>.

## [[payment_and_transaction_details_on_clipto | Payments]] and Fees

All [[payment_and_transaction_details_on_clipto | payments]] on Clipto are conducted in [[introduction_to_cryptocurrency | cryptocurrency]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>.

*   **Platform Fee**: Clipto charges a 5% fee for each booking <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>.
*   **User Protection**: Like other platforms, Clipto aims to protect its users by ensuring that if a creator fails to deliver the requested video, the client receives a refund <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:41">[00:01:41]</a>.

## Advantages for Creators

Clipto offers several benefits for creators, especially those in the [[introduction_to_cryptocurrency | crypto]] space:

*   **Fewer Restrictions**: Unlike Web2 platforms, Clipto has fewer restrictions, allowing creators to work on [[introduction_to_cryptocurrency | crypto]]-related requests and receive [[introduction_to_cryptocurrency | crypto]] [[payment_and_transaction_details_on_clipto | payments]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:49">[00:01:49]</a>.
*   **[[introduction_to_cryptocurrency | Crypto]]-Native Understanding**: Creators being [[introduction_to_cryptocurrency | crypto]]-native means they can more easily understand complex briefs from [[nft_integration_in_clipto_for_video_content | NFT]] and protocol owners <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:11">[00:02:11]</a>. This positions Clipto as a potential one-stop shop for such clients to find specialized creators <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:05">[00:02:05]</a>.

## Challenges for Clipto

From a platform perspective, one of the biggest [[challenges_and_opportunities_for_clipto_in_attracting_creators | challenges]] for Clipto is how to [[challenges_and_opportunities_for_clipto_in_attracting_creators | attract talent and creators]] to its platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:17">[00:02:17]</a>. Ensuring the delivery of quality work to clients is also a key concern <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:21">[00:02:21]</a>.
