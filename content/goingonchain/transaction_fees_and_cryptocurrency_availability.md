When considering [[investing_in_cryptocurrency | investing]] in Singapore, it is important to understand the differences between Binance SG and Binance.com (Binance Global), particularly regarding transaction fees and cryptocurrency availability <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:05">[00:00:05]</a>. While both platforms are subsidiaries of Binance, they operate under different regulations and offer distinct services <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:11">[00:00:11]</a>.

## Binance Singapore (Binance SG)

Binance SG is regulated by the Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>.

*   **Transaction Currency**
    *   All transactions on Binance SG are conducted via Singapore Dollars (SGD) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:28">[00:00:28]</a>.
*   **[[understanding_transaction_fees_and_withdrawals_on_binance | Transaction Fees]]**
    *   Trading fees are set at 0.6% <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:32">[00:00:32]</a>.
*   **Cryptocurrency Availability**
    *   Binance SG supports trading for only eight cryptocurrencies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:34">[00:00:34]</a>.
*   **Deposit and Withdrawal Fees**
    *   Deposits into Binance SG require a third-party payment provider called Xfers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[00:00:37]</a>. There are no fees for deposits or withdrawals on this platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:43">[00:00:43]</a>.

## Binance Global (Binance.com)

Binance Global, accessible via Binance.com, is not regulated by MAS <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>.

*   **Transaction Currency**
    *   All transactions on Binance Global are conducted via US Dollars (USD) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>.
*   **[[understanding_transaction_fees_and_withdrawals_on_binance | Transaction Fees]] and Discounts**
    *   Transaction fees on Binance Global are significantly lower than on Binance SG <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:47">[00:00:47]</a>.
    *   Users can receive a discount on trading fees if they buy with Binance Coin (BNB) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>.
*   **Cryptocurrency Availability**
    *   Binance Global supports a much wider range of cryptocurrencies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:53">[00:00:53]</a>, including Dogecoin <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>.
*   **[[methods_for_buying_and_selling_cryptocurrency | Methods for buying and selling cryptocurrency]]**
    *   There are three primary ways to buy crypto on Binance Global <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:58">[00:00:58]</a>:
        1.  Using a credit card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>.
        2.  Peer-to-peer (P2P) transactions, which allow direct exchanges between users (e.g., exchanging SGD for crypto) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:02">[00:01:02]</a>.
        3.  Buying Binance Coin (BNB) on Binance Singapore and transferring it to Binance Global to purchase other desired cryptocurrencies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:11">[00:01:11]</a>.
    *   Selling crypto can also be done via P2P (selling crypto for SGD) or by converting crypto back into Binance Coin and sending it to Binance SG to convert back to SGD <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:17">[00:01:17]</a>.

### Important Note: Wallet Separation

It is crucial to note that the wallets on Binance SG and Binance Global are separate and not linked <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>. This means it is not possible to buy cryptocurrency on Binance Singapore and directly sell it on Binance Global <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>.
