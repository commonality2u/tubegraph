From: [[goingonchain]] <br/> 

Clipto is a service platform described as the web3 version of [[comparison_of_clipto_with_cameo | Cameo]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:09">[00:00:09]</a>. It allows users to pay a fee to have their favorite "star" or creator make a personalized video for them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:16">[00:00:16]</a>. For example, a user could request the voice of Scooby-Doo to sing a birthday song <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:23">[00:00:23]</a>.

## How Clipto Functions

[[how_clipto_functions_on_the_blockchain | Clipto functions on the blockchain]] by operating on the Magnetic Chain, which ensures low transaction fees <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:33">[00:00:33]</a>. Payments on Clipto are made in [[monetization_and_payment_in_cryptocurrencies_on_clipto | crypto]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>. Currently, the project does not have its own token, but a DAO is in the process of being set up <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>.

A key feature of Clipto is that the personalized video received by the user is delivered in an NFT format <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:24">[00:01:24]</a>. This means users can resell the video on platforms like OpenSea if they choose <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:28">[00:01:28]</a>. The platform charges a 5% fee for bookings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>. Like other platforms, Clipto aims to protect users, ensuring refunds if creators fail to deliver <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:41">[00:01:41]</a>.

### Becoming a Creator

Becoming a creator on Clipto is a permissionless process, meaning anyone can join <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:40">[00:00:40]</a>. To sign up, creators simply need to connect a wallet and make a tweet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:43">[00:00:43]</a>. The creators currently on Clipto are primarily [[introduction_to_cryptocurrencies | crypto]] natives <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:32">[00:00:32]</a>.

The project's creator, Greybro, known for funny crypto-related videos on Twitter, started Clipto after his application to Cameo was rejected <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:47">[00:00:47]</a>. He believes there should be no gatekeepers restricting creators <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>.

### User Experience

When requesting a video, users can view a creator's profile, including their portfolio, and key in their request details <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>. Users can also access the creator's Twitter profile <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:13">[00:01:13]</a>. Pricing varies by creator; for instance, one creator charges five Dramas for a video <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:02">[00:01:02]</a>.

## Advantages and Challenges

### Advantages
*   **Accessibility:** Anyone can join as a creator, with fewer restrictions compared to Web2 platforms <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:47">[00:01:47]</a>.
*   **Crypto-focused content:** Creators can work on and receive payments for crypto-related requests, which some Web2 platforms might not allow <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:52">[00:01:52]</a>.
*   **Specialization for crypto users:** It serves as a one-stop shop for NFT and protocol owners to find creators who understand their crypto-native briefs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:02">[00:02:02]</a>.
*   **NFT video format:** Users receive videos as NFTs, allowing for potential resale <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:24">[00:01:24]</a>.

### Challenges
*   **Attracting Talent:** A significant challenge for the platform is how to attract talent and creators to join <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:17">[00:02:17]</a>.
*   **Quality Control:** Ensuring that creators deliver quality work to clients is another challenge <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:21">[00:02:21]</a>.
