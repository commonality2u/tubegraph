### [[cost_associated_with_using_argent_wallet | Activation Cost]]

When activating the Argent wallet, a one-time gas fee is required because it is a [[types_of_crypto_wallets | smart wallet]] built on a smart contract <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:27">[00:02:27]</a>.

### Key [[features_of_argent_smart_wallet | Features]]

Argent offers several unique [[features_of_argent_smart_wallet | features]], especially regarding [[security_measures_in_argent_wallet | security]]:

*   **No Seed Phrase Required** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:30">[00:02:30]</a>: Users do not need to remember a seed phrase. Instead, login is facilitated through email and phone <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:34">[00:02:34]</a>. The seed phrase is split and encrypted between Argent and iCloud <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:37">[00:02:37]</a>.
*   **Guardian Feature** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:42">[00:02:42]</a>: This [[security_features_of_crypto_wallets | security feature]] allows users to designate a trusted person, such as a close friend or spouse, as a guardian <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:44">[00:02:44]</a>. If a phone is lost, the guardian can remotely lock the wallet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:48">[00:02:48]</a>. The speaker chose to use Argent primarily due to its [[security_measures_in_argent_wallet | security setup]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:53">[00:02:53]</a>.
