## Drivers of Growth

A major factor contributing to the rise of options investing in traditional markets has been the accessibility provided by platforms like Robinhood, which enable easy access to these products <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:15">[00:00:15]</a>. This phenomenon is now being observed in the crypto market <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:24">[00:00:24]</a>.
