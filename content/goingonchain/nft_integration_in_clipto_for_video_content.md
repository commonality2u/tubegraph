## Personalized Video Content as NFTs

On [[overview_of_clipto_and_its_relation_to_cameo | Clipto]], when a client requests a personalized video from a [[signing_up_and_participating_as_a_creator_on_clipto | creator]], the delivered video is received in an NFT format <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:24:41">[01:24:41]</a>. This means the user gains ownership of the video as a digital asset.

## Reselling and Ownership

A significant feature of receiving the video as an NFT is the ability to resell it on platforms like OpenSea <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:28:44">[01:28:44]</a>. This provides a potential for [[NFT with Passive Income | passive income]] or trading for the NFT owner.

## Payments and Platform Fees

Payments on [[overview_of_clipto_and_its_relation_to_cameo | Clipto]] are made in crypto <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:00:99">[01:00:99]</a>.
