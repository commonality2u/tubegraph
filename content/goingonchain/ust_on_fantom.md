The introduction of UST on the Fantom (FTM) network is considered positive news for the crypto ecosystem <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:06">[00:00:06]</a>.

## Significance
This expansion is seen as beneficial because increased demand for UST typically leads to a better price for Luna <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:10">[00:00:10]</a>.

## Bridging Process
To facilitate the transfer of UST to Fantom, users need to follow specific steps for bridging <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:14">[00:00:14]</a>:
*   Use the bridge functionality, not the router <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:16">[00:00:16]</a>.
*   Set the bridge to "non-EVM" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:19">[00:00:19]</a>.
*   Search for UST <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>.
*   Connect both your Terra wallet and MetaMask <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:23">[00:00:23]</a>.
*   After connecting, you can bridge your UST <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:25">[00:00:25]</a>.
  *   For example, 5 UST was successfully bridged over to the [[Geist Finances phantom network features | phantom network]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>.
