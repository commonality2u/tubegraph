## Bridging Process using Satellite

The primary tool for bridging UST from Terra to [[tutorial_on_avalanche_usage | Avalanche]] is the Satellite software [00:00:26].

### Step-by-Step Bridge Configuration
1.  **Select Source Chain**: Choose "Terra" as the source chain [00:00:29].
2.  **Select Source Asset**: Choose "UST" as your source asset [00:00:32].
3.  **Select Destination Chain**: Choose "AVAX" (Avalanche) as your destination chain [00:00:37].
4.  **Enter Destination Address**: Input your [[tutorial_on_avalanche_usage | Avalanche]] address [00:00:39].

### Fees and Minimums
*   **Transfer Fee**: A fee of 0.1% applies to the transfer [00:00:41].
*   **Minimum Transfer**: The minimum amount for transfer is 15 UST [00:00:44].

### Initiating the Transfer
Once the details are set, click "Connect Wallet" and then "Transfer" [00:00:47]. This action will prompt a signature request on your MetaMask wallet [00:00:51]. After signing, a one-time deposit will be generated for the recipient [00:00:55].
