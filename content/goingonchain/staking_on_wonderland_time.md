Wonderland Time (TIME) is part of a "d-gen play" investment strategy <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:01">[00:00:01]</a>.

## Investment Overview
An investment in TIME has been held for approximately two weeks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:22">[00:00:22]</a>.

### Initial Investment
The initial capital put into Wonderland Time was about $4,000 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:24">[00:00:24]</a>.

### Current Holdings
The current balance is represented by 0.819 MEMO tokens <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>. MEMO acts as a receipt for the staked TIME <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:28">[00:00:28]</a>. The balance is calculated by multiplying MEMO by the current TIME price <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>.

### Current Value and Profit
The price of TIME is currently around $8,900 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:34">[00:00:34]</a>.
A calculation of 0.819 MEMO multiplied by $8,900 yields a current balance of approximately $7,289 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[00:00:37]</a>.
With an initial cost of $4,000, the current profit for this two-week period is about 80% <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:44">[00:00:44]</a>.
