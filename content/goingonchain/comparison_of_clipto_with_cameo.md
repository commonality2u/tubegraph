Clipto is described as the Web3 version of Cameo, operating as a service platform rather than a DeFi protocol <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:06">[00:00:06]</a>.

## Cameo

Cameo is a platform where users can pay a fee to have a personalized video created by a favorite star <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:16">[00:00:16]</a>. An example given is hiring the voice of Scooby-Doo to sing a birthday song <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:23">[00:00:23]</a>.

## Clipto

[[Clipto platform overview | Clipto]] is a platform where users can engage artists and creators <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>.

### Creator Onboarding and Features
The creators currently on [[Clipto platform overview | Clipto]] are primarily crypto natives <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:32">[00:00:32]</a>. The platform operates on the Magnetic Chain, which keeps fees low <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:35">[00:00:35]</a>.

Signing up as a creator is permissionless and easy: users connect a wallet, make a tweet, and they are ready to go <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:40">[00:00:40]</a>. The creator of [[Clipto platform overview | Clipto]] is Greybro, who started the project after his application to Cameo was rejected, believing there should be no gatekeepers against creators <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:58">[00:00:58]</a>.

### Monetization and Payment
[[Monetization and payment in cryptocurrencies on Clipto | Payments on Clipto]] are made in cryptocurrency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:01:00">[01:01:00]</a>. Creators set their own charges; for instance, one creator charges five MATIC for a video <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:05:05">[01:05:05]</a>. Users can view a creator's portfolio and Twitter profile on their detail page and key in their requests <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:07:07">[01:07:07]</a>.

### Unique Aspects of Clipto
Instead of just receiving a video, users receive the video in an NFT format, which allows for potential resale on platforms like OpenSea <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:24:01">[01:24:01]</a>.

The project does not currently have its own token but is in the process of setting up a DAO <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:33:04">[01:33:04]</a>.

### Fees and User Protection
[[How Clipto functions on the blockchain | Clipto]] charges a 5% fee for bookings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:37:05">[01:37:05]</a>. The platform protects users by offering refunds if creators fail to deliver the requested content <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:43:04">[01:43:04]</a>.

## Key Differences and Advantages of Clipto

*   **Web2 vs. Web3**: Clipto operates as a Web3 platform, contrasting with Web2 platforms like Cameo <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:49:01">[01:49:01]</a>.
*   **Permissionless Access**: Unlike Cameo, Clipto offers permissionless access for creators, meaning anyone can join <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:40:01">[01:40:01]</a>.
*   **Content and Payments**: Clipto allows for crypto-related requests and crypto payments, which some Web2 platforms may not permit <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:52:05">[01:52:05]</a>.
*   **Target Audience**: Clipto is particularly beneficial for NFT and protocol owners, serving as a "one-stop shop" to find creators <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:04:02">[02:04:02]</a>. Because the creators are crypto native, they can understand client briefs more easily <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:11:00">[02:11:00]</a>.
*   **Output Format**: Videos delivered through [[Clipto platform overview | Clipto]] are minted as NFTs, allowing for potential resale <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:26:01">[01:26:01]</a>.

## Challenges for Clipto

The biggest challenges for [[Clipto platform overview | Clipto]] as a platform include attracting talent and creators, and ensuring that quality work is consistently delivered to clients <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:17:01">[02:17:01]</a>.
