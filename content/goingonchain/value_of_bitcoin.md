[[bitcoin | Bitcoin]] is not backed by any physical assets or government body <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:06">[00:00:06]</a>. This characteristic makes it unlike assets such as US Treasury bonds or gold <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:11">[00:00:11]</a>.
