Clipto is a service platform described as the Web3 version of Cameo <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:09">[00:00:09]</a>. While not a DeFi protocol, it facilitates personalized video creation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:06">[00:00:06]</a>.

## Comparison to Cameo

Cameo is a platform where users can pay a fee to have their favorite star create a personalized video for them, such as a celebrity voice actor singing a birthday song <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:14">[00:00:14]</a>. Clipto offers a similar service but within the Web3 ecosystem <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:09">[00:00:09]</a>.

## Platform Overview

### Core Functionality
Clipto allows users to engage artists and creators to make personalized videos <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>. Users can key in their requests on a creator's profile page, which also displays their portfolio and links to their social media, such as Twitter <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>.

### Technology and Fees
The project operates on the Polygon chain, ensuring low transaction fees <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:33">[00:00:33]</a>. [[Payment and transaction details on Clipto | Payments]] for videos are made in cryptocurrency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>. Clipto charges a 5% fee for bookings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>. The platform also has a refund policy in place to protect users: if a creator fails to deliver the requested video, the client receives a refund <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:41">[00:01:41]</a>.

### [[NFT integration in Clipto for video content | NFT Integration]]
A unique feature of Clipto is that users receive their personalized videos in an NFT format <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:24">[00:01:24]</a>. This allows recipients the option to resell the video on secondary marketplaces like OpenSea <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:28">[00:01:28]</a>.

### Future Development
Currently, Clipto does not have its own token, but the team is in the process of setting up a Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>.

## Creator Participation

### Current Creators
The creators currently active on Clipto are predominantly crypto natives <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>.

### [[signing_up_and_participating_as_a_creator_on_clipto | Becoming a Creator]]
[[signing_up_and_participating_as_a_creator_on_clipto | Signing up to be a creator]] on Clipto is designed to be easy and permissionless <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:38">[00:00:38]</a>. Anyone can join by connecting their crypto wallet and making a tweet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:40">[00:00:40]</a>.

### Origin Story
The creator of Clipto is Greybro, known for making humorous crypto-related videos on Twitter <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:47">[00:00:47]</a>. Greybro started Clipto after his application to Cameo was rejected <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:53">[00:00:53]</a>. This experience fueled his belief that there should be no gatekeepers restricting creators <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>.

### Benefits for Creators
Clipto offers creators a platform with fewer restrictions compared to Web2 alternatives <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:49">[00:01:49]</a>. Creators can take on crypto-related requests and receive payments in cryptocurrency, which may not be permitted on some traditional Web2 platforms <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:52">[00:01:52]</a>.

## Client Perspective

From a client's perspective, Clipto is particularly beneficial for NFT and protocol owners <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:00">[00:02:00]</a>. It serves as a centralized platform to find creators for personalized videos <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:05">[00:02:05]</a>. Since Clipto's creators are largely crypto natives, they can more easily understand briefs related to the crypto space <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:11">[00:02:11]</a>.
