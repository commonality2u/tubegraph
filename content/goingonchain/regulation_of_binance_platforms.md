When looking to buy crypto in Singapore, it is important to understand the differences in regulation between [[differences_between_binance_sg_and_binance_global | Binance SG]] and [[differences_between_binance_sg_and_binance_global | Binance Global]] (Binance.com) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:07">[00:00:07]</a>. While they are related platforms, their regulatory statuses in Singapore differ significantly <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>.

## Binance SG

[[differences_between_binance_sg_and_binance_global | Binance Singapore]] is a subsidiary of Binance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:15">[00:00:15]</a>.
*   **Regulation**: It is regulated by the Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>.
*   **Transactions**: All transactions on the platform are conducted via SGD (Singapore Dollars) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:28">[00:00:28]</a>.
*   **Trading Fees**: Trading fees are set at 0.6% <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:31">[00:00:31]</a>.
*   **Supported Cryptocurrencies**: Only eight cryptocurrencies are available for trading <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:34">[00:00:34]</a>.
*   **Deposits & Withdrawals**: To [[buying_and_selling_cryptocurrency_on_binance | deposit money]] into [[differences_between_binance_sg_and_binance_global | Binance Singapore]], a third-party payment provider called Xfers is required <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[00:00:37]</a>. There are no [[understanding_transaction_fees_and_withdrawals_on_binance | fees for deposit and withdrawal]] on [[differences_between_binance_sg_and_binance_global | Binance Singapore]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>.
*   **Wallet Linkage**: Wallets on [[differences_between_binance_sg_and_binance_global | Binance Singapore]] are separate and not linked to [[differences_between_binance_sg_and_binance_global | Binance Global]] wallets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>.

## Binance Global (Binance.com)

[[differences_between_binance_sg_and_binance_global | Binance Global]] operates under the URL Binance.com <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:20">[00:00:20]</a>.
*   **Regulation**: It is not regulated by the MAS <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>.
*   **Transactions**: All transactions on [[differences_between_binance_sg_and_binance_global | Binance Global]] are conducted via USD (US Dollars) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:45">[00:00:45]</a>.
*   **Transaction Fees**: [[understanding_transaction_fees_and_withdrawals_on_binance | Transaction fees]] are notably lower than on [[differences_between_binance_sg_and_binance_global | Binance Singapore]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:47">[00:00:47]</a>. An additional discount on [[understanding_transaction_fees_and_withdrawals_on_binance | trading fees]] is available when buying with Binance Coin (BNB) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>.
*   **Supported Cryptocurrencies**: A much wider range of cryptocurrencies is supported for purchase <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:53">[00:00:53]</a>, including Dogecoin <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>.
*   **Ways to Buy Crypto**: There are three ways to [[buying_and_selling_cryptocurrency_on_binance | buy crypto]] on [[differences_between_binance_sg_and_binance_global | Binance Global]]:
    1.  Using a credit card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>.
    2.  Via P2P (peer-to-peer) transactions, which function like a crowdsale where SGD is exchanged for crypto directly with another person <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:02">[00:01:02]</a>.
    3.  By buying Binance Coin on [[differences_between_binance_sg_and_binance_global | Binance Singapore]], sending it to [[differences_between_binance_sg_and_binance_global | Binance Global]], and then using it to buy desired crypto <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:08">[00:01:08]</a>.
*   **Ways to Sell Crypto**: When [[buying_and_selling_cryptocurrency_on_binance | selling crypto]], the opposite methods can be used:
    1.  Using P2P to sell crypto for SGD <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:17">[00:01:17]</a>.
    2.  Converting crypto back into Binance Coin, sending it to [[differences_between_binance_sg_and_binance_global | Binance Singapore]], and then converting it back into Singapore dollars <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:23">[00:01:23]</a>.
*   **Wallet Linkage**: It is not possible to [[buying_and_selling_cryptocurrency_on_binance | buy crypto]] on [[differences_between_binance_sg_and_binance_global | Binance Singapore]] and [[buying_and_selling_cryptocurrency_on_binance | sell]] it on [[differences_between_binance_sg_and_binance_global | Binance Global]] because their wallets are separate <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>.
