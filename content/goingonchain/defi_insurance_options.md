## Why Consider DeFi Insurance?

[[decentralized_finance_options_and_liquid_staking | Decentralized finance]] protocols, while innovative, are susceptible to various vulnerabilities, including smart contract bugs and malicious attacks. Insurance in [[decentralized_finance_options_and_liquid_staking | DeFi]] aims to protect users' capital in the event of such incidents, allowing them to claim back funds if a covered protocol is hacked <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:44">[00:00:44]</a>.

## Available DeFi Insurance Providers

Several platforms offer insurance coverage for [[decentralized_finance_options_and_liquid_staking | DeFi]] protocols, though coverage can vary and might not be universally available for all projects.

### InsurAce

InsurAce is an insurance provider mentioned as an option for [[decentralized_finance_options_and_liquid_staking | DeFi]] users <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>.
*   **Coverage**: It covers various protocols within ecosystems, including the Fantom ecosystem <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:35">[00:00:35]</a>. For example, users with large positions in protocols like Abracadabra can buy insurance against hacks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:38">[00:00:38]</a>.
*   **Limitations**: It's important to note that InsurAce does not cover all protocols; for instance, it did not cover Grim Finance at a specific time <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:33">[00:00:33]</a>.
