To begin [[investing in cryptocurrency | investing]], individuals can utilize platforms such as Binance SG and Binance Global (Binance.com) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:05">[00:00:05]</a>. ## Binance Singapore (Binance SG)

Binance Singapore is a subsidiary of Binance and is regulated by the Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>.

Key features for [[cryptocurrency trading in Singapore | trading cryptocurrency in Singapore]] via Binance SG include:
*   **Transaction Currency** All transactions on this platform are conducted via [[using_sgd_to_buy_cryptocurrency_on_binance | SGD]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:28">[00:00:28]</a>.
*   **[[transaction_fees_and_cryptocurrency_availability | Trading Fees]]** The [[transaction_fees_and_cryptocurrency_availability | trading fee]] is 0.6% <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:31">[00:00:31]</a>.
*   **[[transaction_fees_and_cryptocurrency_availability | Cryptocurrency Availability]]** Only eight [[introduction_to_cryptocurrencies | cryptocurrencies]] are available for trade <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:33">[00:00:33]</a>.
*   **Deposits and Withdrawals** To [[buying_and_selling_cryptocurrency_on_binance | deposit money]] into Binance Singapore, a third-party payment provider called Xfers is required <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[00:00:37]</a>. There are no fees for deposits and [[buying_and_selling_cryptocurrency_on_binance | withdrawals]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>.

## Binance Global (Binance.com)

Binance.com, the global platform, is not regulated by MAS <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>.

Key features of Binance Global include:
*   **Transaction Currency** All transactions are conducted via USD <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:45">[00:00:45]</a>.
*   **[[transaction_fees_and_cryptocurrency_availability | Transaction Fees]]** [[transaction_fees_and_cryptocurrency_on_binance | Transaction fees]] are significantly lower <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:47">[00:00:47]</a>. A discount on [[transaction_fees_and_cryptocurrency_on_binance | trading fees]] is available when [[buying_and_selling_cryptocurrency_on_binance | buying]] with Binance Coin <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>.
*   **[[transaction_fees_and_cryptocurrency_availability | Cryptocurrency Availability]]** A much wider range of [[introduction_to_cryptocurrencies | cryptocurrencies]] is supported, including Dogecoin <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:53">[00:00:53]</a>.

### Methods for [[buying_and_selling_cryptocurrency_on_binance | Buying Crypto]] on Binance Global

There are three primary ways to [[buying_and_selling_cryptocurrency_on_binance | buy crypto]] on Binance Global:
1.  **Credit Card** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>
2.  **Peer-to-Peer (P2P)** This method involves exchanging [[using_sgd_to_buy_cryptocurrency_on_binance | SGD]] directly with another person for [[introduction_to_cryptocurrencies | crypto]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:02">[00:01:02]</a>.
3.  **Transferring Binance Coin** [[buying_and_selling_cryptocurrency_on_binance | Purchase]] Binance Coin on Binance Singapore and then send it to Binance Global to [[buying_and_selling_cryptocurrency_on_binance | buy]] desired [[introduction_to_cryptocurrencies | cryptocurrencies]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:08">[00:01:08]</a>.

### Methods for [[buying_and_selling_cryptocurrency_on_binance | Selling Crypto]] on Binance Global

To [[buying_and_selling_cryptocurrency_on_binance | sell crypto]] on Binance Global:
1.  **Peer-to-Peer (P2P)** [[buying_and_selling_cryptocurrency_on_binance | Sell]] your [[introduction_to_cryptocurrencies | crypto]] directly to someone in exchange for [[using_sgd_to_buy_cryptocurrency_on_binance | SGD]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:17">[00:01:17]</a>.
2.  **Transferring Binance Coin** Convert your [[introduction_to_cryptocurrencies | crypto]] back into Binance Coin, send it to Binance Singapore (BSG), and then convert it into Singapore dollars <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:23">[00:01:23]</a>.

> [!important] Wallet Separation
> It is crucial to note that wallets on Binance Singapore and Binance Global are separate and not linked <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>. Therefore, [[buying_and_selling_cryptocurrency_on_binance | cryptocurrency]] purchased on Binance Singapore cannot be directly [[buying_and_selling_cryptocurrency_on_binance | sold]] on Binance Global <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:32">[00:01:32]</a>.
