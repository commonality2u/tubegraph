When looking to [[buying_and_selling_cryptocurrency_on_binance | buy crypto in Singapore]], two primary Binance platforms are available: Binance Singapore (Binance SG) and Binance Global (Binance.com) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:05">[00:00:05]</a>. While sharing the same brand, these platforms have distinct differences, particularly regarding transaction methods and regulations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>.

## Binance Singapore (Binance SG)

Binance SG is a subsidiary of Binance and is regulated by the Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>.

### Transaction Details
*   **Currency**: All transactions on Binance SG are conducted via Singapore Dollars (SGD) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:28">[00:00:28]</a>.
*   **Trading Fees**: A trading fee of 0.6% applies to transactions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:31">[00:00:31]</a>.
*   **Supported Cryptocurrencies**: Only eight cryptocurrencies are available for trading on this platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:34">[00:00:34]</a>.
*   **Deposits**: To [[deposits_and_withdrawals_on_binance_for_singapore_residents | deposit money]] into Binance SG, a third-party payment provider called Xfers is required <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[00:00:37]</a>. There are no fees for deposits <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>.
*   **Withdrawals**: There are no fees for withdrawals on Binance SG <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:43">[00:00:43]</a>.

## Binance Global (Binance.com)

Binance Global, accessed via Binance.com, is not regulated by the MAS <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>.

### Transaction Details
*   **Currency**: All transactions on Binance Global are conducted via USD <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:45">[00:00:45]</a>.
*   **Trading Fees**: Transaction fees are significantly lower than on Binance SG <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:47">[00:00:47]</a>. An additional discount on trading fees is available if transactions are made using Binance Coin (BNB) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>.
*   **Supported Cryptocurrencies**: A much wider selection of cryptocurrencies is supported on Binance Global, including Dogecoin <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:53">[00:00:53]</a>.

### Buying Cryptocurrencies on Binance Global
There are three main methods to [[buying_and_selling_cryptocurrency_on_binance | buy crypto]] on Binance Global:
1.  **Credit Card**: Direct purchases can be made using a credit card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>.
2.  **Peer-to-Peer (P2P)**: This method involves direct transactions between users, where you can give SGD to another person in exchange for crypto <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:02">[00:01:02]</a>.
3.  **Transfer from Binance SG**: You can [[deposits_and_withdrawals_on_binance_for_singapore_residents | buy Binance Coin (BNB)]] on Binance SG and then transfer it to Binance Global to purchase other cryptocurrencies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:11">[00:01:11]</a>.

### Selling Cryptocurrencies on Binance Global
The opposite processes apply when selling crypto:
1.  **Peer-to-Peer (P2P)**: You can sell your crypto directly to another user who will give you SGD <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:17">[00:01:17]</a>.
2.  **Transfer to Binance SG**: Convert your crypto back into Binance Coin (BNB) on Binance Global, send it to Binance SG, and then convert it back into SGD <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:24">[00:01:24]</a>.

### Important Note on Wallets
It is crucial to understand that the wallets for Binance Singapore and Binance Global are not linked; they are two separate wallets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>. Therefore, you cannot buy crypto on Binance Singapore and sell it directly on Binance Global <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:32">[00:01:32]</a>.
