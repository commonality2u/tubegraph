From: [[goingonchain]] <br/> 

Clipto operates as a Web3 service platform, facilitating transactions for personalized video requests between clients and creators using cryptocurrency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:04">[00:00:04]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>.

## Payment Method and Pricing
Payments on Clipto are conducted directly in cryptocurrency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>. Creators set their own fees for videos, such as one creator charging five Matic per video <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:04">[00:01:04]</a>. When making a request, clients can input their specific requirements for the video <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:11">[00:01:11]</a>.

## Transaction Fees
Clipto charges a 5% fee for each booking made on the platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>. The project functions on the Polygon (Matic) chain, which helps keep transaction fees low <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:33">[00:00:33]</a>.

## Refund Policy
To protect users, Clipto has a refund policy in place. If a creator fails to deliver the requested video content, the client will receive a refund <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:42">[00:01:42]</a>.

## Video Delivery and Ownership
Once a request is fulfilled, the personalized video is delivered to the client in an [[nft_integration_in_clipto_for_video_content | NFT format]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:22">[00:01:22]</a>. This allows the client to potentially resell the video on secondary markets like OpenSea if they choose to do so <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:28">[00:01:28]</a>.
