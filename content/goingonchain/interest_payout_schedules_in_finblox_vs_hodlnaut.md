When considering where to park stablecoins or other cryptocurrencies, the frequency of interest payouts is a factor worth noting. Both [[comparison_between_finblox_and_hodlnaut | Finblox]] and Hodlnaut offer interest-earning opportunities, but their payout schedules differ <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:51">[00:00:51]</a>.

## Finblox
Interest on [[comparison_between_finblox_and_hodlnaut | Finblox]] is paid out daily <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:57">[00:00:57]</a>.

## Hodlnaut
In contrast, interest on Hodlnaut is paid weekly <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:51">[00:00:51]</a>.
