[[clipto_platform_overview | Clipto]] is described as the web3 version of [[comparison_of_clipto_with_cameo | Cameo]], offering a service platform for personalized videos from creators, but built with blockchain technology <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:09">[00:00:09]</a>.

## Blockchain Implementation

### Underlying Chain
[[clipto_platform_overview | Clipto]] operates on the Magnetic Chain, which is chosen for its low transaction fees <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:33">[00:00:33]</a>.

### Creator Onboarding and Access
Becoming a creator on [[clipto_platform_overview | Clipto]] is a permissionless process <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:40">[00:00:40]</a>. To join, prospective creators simply need to connect their crypto wallet and make a tweet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:43">[00:00:43]</a>. This open access reflects a core principle of web3, removing gatekeepers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:47">[00:01:47]</a>. The project's creator, Greybro, started [[clipto_platform_overview | Clipto]] after his application to [[comparison_of_clipto_with_cameo | Cameo]] was rejected, highlighting his belief in creator freedom <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:53">[00:00:53]</a>.

### [[monetization_and_payment_in_cryptocurrencies_on_clipto | Payment and Monetization]]
Payments on [[clipto_platform_overview | Clipto]] are exclusively made in [[monetization_and_payment_in_cryptocurrencies_on_clipto | cryptocurrencies]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>. This allows for transactions involving crypto-related requests and direct crypto payments, which may not be permitted on traditional web2 platforms <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:53">[00:01:53]</a>.

### Video Output as NFTs
A unique feature of [[clipto_platform_overview | Clipto]] is that the personalized videos delivered to clients are in an NFT (Non-Fungible Token) format <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:24">[00:01:24]</a>. This allows the recipient the option to resell the video on platforms like OpenSea <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:28">[00:01:28]</a>.

### Fees and User Protection
[[clipto_platform_overview | Clipto]] charges a 5% fee for each booking <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>. The platform also has mechanisms in place to protect users; if a creator fails to deliver the requested video, the client receives a refund <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:41">[00:01:41]</a>.

### Future Developments
While there is currently no native token for the [[clipto_platform_overview | Clipto]] project, plans are underway to establish a Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>.
