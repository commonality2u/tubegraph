## Introduction to Grow Protocol

Grow Protocol is a DeFi yield optimizer designed to balance risk and offer [[stablecoin_leveraged_yield_farming | leverage on stablecoin]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:08">[00:00:08]</a>. The protocol has been active on the mainnet with two key products:

*   **Powered**: This product functions similarly to a fixed deposit for stablecoins. It provides superior returns compared to traditional finance ("TradFi") and includes deposit protection <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>.
*   **Vault (Vote)**: The Vault product offers [[leverage_strategies_on_defi_platforms | leveraged yield]] on stablecoins <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:29">[00:00:29]</a>.

These two products operate in close conjunction. Increased deposits in the Powered saving pool contribute to higher returns for the Vault, as the Vault leverages assets from Powered <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:34">[00:00:34]</a>. Any protocol losses are primarily covered by the Vault <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:43">[00:00:43]</a>.

## Grow Protocol Labs on [[advantages_of_avalanche_platform | Avalanche]]

Grow Protocol has extended its operations to the [[advantages_of_avalanche_platform | Avalanche]] blockchain, introducing two active "Labs":
*   USDC leverage yield Lab <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:53">[00:00:53]</a>
*   USDT leverage yield Lab <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:55">[00:00:55]</a>

### How the Labs Operate
The Labs leverage farms from Alpha Homora v2, which is a [[leverage_strategies_on_defi_platforms | leveraged yield]] farming platform on [[advantages_of_avalanche_platform | Avalanche]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:58">[00:00:58]</a>. Funds from the Labs are utilized as collateral to borrow AVAX, and stablecoins are leveraged at 2x to establish new positions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>.

Strategies are executed automatically based on market conditions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a>. Each cycle of opening and closing a position is referred to as a "strategy cycle" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:20">[00:01:20]</a>. This process is fully automated, continuously rebalancing risks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:24">[00:01:24]</a>. While individual strategy cycles may sometimes result in negative returns, the average return is approximately 17% <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>.

The Lab UI transparently displays the Total Value Locked (TVL), the reserve (funds not currently deployed), and the AVAX exposure <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:39">[00:01:39]</a>.

### Comparison to Other Stablecoin Farms
When considering [[comparison_of_yield_strategies_on_avalanche | other stablecoin farms]], such as a USDT farm on Trader Joe that might offer a 17.8% total APR, it's important to note that this reward is typically split between pool APR and a native protocol token APR (e.g., JOE APR) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:46">[00:01:46]</a>. In contrast, the Grow Protocol USDT Lab yields around 17% APY in pure stablecoin, eliminating the need to sell volatile protocol tokens to convert back to stablecoin <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:00">[00:02:00]</a>.

### How to Use Grow Labs

To begin using Grow Labs:
1.  Connect your wallet and ensure you are on the [[advantages_of_avalanche_platform | Avalanche]] network <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:14">[00:02:14]</a>.
2.  Select "Lab" and choose either the USDC or USDT Lab <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:16">[00:02:16]</a>.
3.  You can deposit or withdraw stablecoins at any time <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:26">[00:02:26]</a>.
4.  The initial maximum deposit limit is $5,000 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:32">[00:02:32]</a>.
5.  To increase your deposit allowance beyond this limit, you must own at least 500 GROW tokens <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:37">[00:02:37]</a>. GROW is the governance token, currently available on the mainnet via Uniswap <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:41">[00:02:41]</a>.

## Case Study: Performance During AVAX Volatility

The Grow Protocol team conducted a test of their automated Lab strategy. On December 3rd, they deposited $5,000. Between December 3rd and December 6th, AVAX experienced a significant drop from $104 to $83, representing approximately a 20% decline <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:46">[00:02:46]</a>. Despite this substantial AVAX market volatility, the Lab strategy managed to yield a 0.5% gain during this period <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:01">[00:03:01]</a>. The Lab opened and closed positions 13 times within the same timeframe <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:07">[00:03:07]</a>.
