[[radiant_capital_overview_and_status | Radiant Capital]] quickly gained significant attention from yield farmers and investors, becoming a top 10 protocol on the Arbitrum Chain, ranking sixth with nearly $54 million in TVL in a short period <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:06">[00:00:06]</a>. Despite its rapid growth and high yields, certain concerns were raised about the protocol in its early stages <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.

## Initial Concerns

Concerns circulated via tweets regarding several aspects of [[radiant_capital_overview_and_status | Radiant Capital]]:
*   **Unaudited Contracts** The protocol's contracts were initially not audited <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:45">[00:00:45]</a>.
*   **Lack of KYC** There was no Know Your Customer (KYC) verification process <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:47">[00:00:47]</a>.
*   **No Time Lock** The absence of a time lock mechanism was noted <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:47">[00:00:47]</a>.
*   **Association with Sifu** The protocol was linked to "Sifu" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>.
*   **LP Snipers** There were reports of LP snipers at the launch <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:54">[00:00:54]</a>.

## Team's Response and Subsequent Updates

The [[radiant_capital_overview_and_status | Radiant Capital]] team released a statement to address these concerns and subsequently implemented updates to their contracts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:57">[00:00:57]</a>.

### Implemented Updates

*   **Oracle Ownership** Oracle ownership has been moved to a Tutor multi-sig <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:04">[00:01:04]</a>.
*   **Time Lock** A time lock has been added to the protocol <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>.
*   **Contract Verification and Audit** The contract is now verified and has been audited <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>.

### KYC Stance

Regarding KYC, [[radiant_capital_overview_and_status | Radiant Capital]] clarified their stance, stating they are still not KYC, in alignment with Offchain Labs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:14">[00:01:14]</a>.
