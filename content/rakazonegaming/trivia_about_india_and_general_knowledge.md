## "School Chale Hum" Series Overview

"School Chale Hum" is a series that invites favorite personalities to answer simple, school-related questions about subjects they might have studied [00:00:35]. The ultimate winner of the competition receives a prize of one lakh [00:00:49].

## Competition Format

The contest involves a series of questions, with participants asked five questions each, one by one [00:02:51]. A correct answer earns a plus one point, while a wrong answer yields zero points [00:02:54]. If a participant does not know the answer, the question can be passed to the other contestant, who will receive the point if they answer correctly [00:02:58].
