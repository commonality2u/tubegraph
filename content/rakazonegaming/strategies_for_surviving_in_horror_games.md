    *   **Hide in the Dark:** To avoid detection, a crucial [[gameplay_strategies | strategy]] is to put down light sources (like a lighter) and hide in dark corners. This makes the player character harder to find <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:24">[00:03:24]</a>.
    *   **Minimize Noise:** Running frequently can alert enemies, as "someone might hear you" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:26">[00:03:26]</a>. Moving quietly is essential to avoid detection.

*   **Thorough Exploration and Clue Gathering**
    *   **Check All Doors:** A common approach in horror games is to systematically check "all the doors" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:31">[00:05:31]</a> to uncover pathways and progress.
