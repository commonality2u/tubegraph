## Common Gaming Terminology

Players frequently use specific terms to describe various aspects of their gaming experience:

*   **Live Stream**
    Live streams are interactive broadcasts where players share their gameplay in real-time <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:11">[00:07:11]</a>.
*   **Chat**
    The in-game or stream chat allows players and viewers to communicate through text <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:09">[00:08:09]</a>.
*   **RP (Role Play)**
    RP refers to a style of gameplay where players adopt and portray specific characters within a game world <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:46">[00:14:46]</a>. This can involve structured "Role Play" servers, such as those mentioned for "Galaxy Town" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:04">[00:15:04]</a> or "Modern Town" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:14">[00:15:14]</a>.
*   **Server**
    A server is a central computer that hosts the game world and facilitates multiplayer interactions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:48">[00:14:48]</a>. Players often discuss joining or leaving servers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:51">[00:14:51]</a>.
*   **Ban**
    A "ban" is a penalty that prevents a player from accessing a game or server, often due to rule violations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:36">[00:15:36]</a>. A player mentioned being banned from "FM" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:19">[00:19:19]</a>.

    Games frequently receive updates to add new content, fix bugs, or improve performance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:11:09">[02:11:09]</a>. Daily updates for games like "5M" can be a point of discussion <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:11:09">[02:11:09]</a>.
*   **Lagging**
    "Lagging" refers to delays or unresponsiveness in game performance, often due to network issues <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:22:22">[02:22:22]</a>.
