## Series Structure and Gameplay

The format of "School chale hum" is structured around a quiz challenging guests on their fundamental knowledge from school.

### Participants
The series invites "favourites" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:35">[00:00:35]</a>, with episodes featuring guests such as India's first Radiant streamer, Wings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:07">[00:00:07]</a>, and Dhvani Bhatt <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:23">[00:00:23]</a>. Before the main quiz, guests often share a little about their [[educational_backgrounds_of_mili_and_sakshi | educational background]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:58">[00:00:58]</a>.

### Questions
The hosts ask simple and [[quiz_questions_related_to_school_subjects | school related questions]] covering subjects that participants might have studied during their schooling <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>. In one instance, five questions were asked to each participant, one by one <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:48">[00:02:48]</a>.

### Scoring System
The scoring rules are straightforward:
*   For every correct answer, a participant receives one point (+1) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:51">[00:02:51]</a>.
*   For a wrong answer, there is no penalty, resulting in zero points <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:54">[00:02:54]</a>.
*   If a participant doesn't know the answer, the question can be passed on to the other participant. If the second participant answers correctly, they receive the point <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:58">[00:02:58]</a>.

### Prize
The ultimate winner across the series will receive a grand prize of one lakh rupees <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>.
