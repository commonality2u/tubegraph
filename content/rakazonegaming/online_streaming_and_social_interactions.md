## Community Engagement
Streamers frequently greet their viewers by name, acknowledging their presence and fostering a sense of [[live_streaming_and_community_interactions | community]]. For example, a streamer might welcome "Hello everyone" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:37">[00:03:37]</a> and directly address individuals like "hello arpit chirag hello tak tyagi redan" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:11">[00:04:11]</a>, "Kunal whats up Sushant" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:01">[00:05:01]</a>, or "hello alex whats up hello Bewitched" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:08">[00:06:08]</a>. This direct address helps create a personal connection, promoting [[interpersonal_interactions_during_live_streaming_sessions | interpersonal interactions during live streaming sessions]].

The audience is often encouraged to participate through various means:
*   **Celebrating events** Streamers extend wishes for festive seasons, such as "Heartiest wishes for Dhanteras" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:44">[00:03:44]</a> and "Happy business day to all of you" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:48">[00:03:48]</a>, sharing personal well-wishes with the viewers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:52">[00:03:52]</a>.
*   **Encouraging participation** Viewers are asked questions like "how are you all" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:04">[00:04:04]</a> and prompted to share their excitement about new features, with statements like "Are you feeling excited about the new feature? Check it out now" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:49">[00:04:49]</a>.
*   **Polls and Likes** Streamers often ask viewers to engage by liking the stream and participating in polls <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:10:57">[01:10:57]</a>.

## Real-time Interaction
The nature of the [[live_streaming_and_interactions | live stream]] means that interaction happens in real-time. This includes:
*   Directly responding to viewer questions or comments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:06:05">[01:06:05]</a>.
*   Integrating viewer input into in-game actions or decisions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:08:29">[01:08:29]</a>.
*   Acknowledging viewer support through "Super Chat" messages <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:00:09">[02:00:09]</a>.

This constant back-and-forth between the streamer and the audience is a core element of [[streaming_community_and_viewer_interaction | streaming community and viewer interaction]].
