!> **Impact of Online Disputes**
When personal disputes between long-time friends and content creators are aired publicly on the internet, it can negatively impact both parties <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:44">[00:20:44]</a>. Such public fighting among "smart people" can even cause brands to distance themselves <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:29:11">[00:29:11]</a>. It is suggested that creators resolve personal matters privately, rather than streaming them for public consumption <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:29:33">[00:29:33]</a>. When disputes become public, viewers might question the authenticity of relationships, even if individuals have known each other for years or created content together <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:06">[00:22:06]</a>. This public airing can lead to a loss of viewership for both parties involved <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:12">[00:23:12]</a>.
