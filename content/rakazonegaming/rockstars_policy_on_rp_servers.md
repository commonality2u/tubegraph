> "We Regret to inform that the server is down for Next 14 days during this period Server activity will be suspended" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:40">[00:09:40]</a>.

While an explicit reason for the ban was not immediately provided in the official announcement <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:52">[00:09:52]</a>, a prevailing theory suggests it was due to the unauthorized use of real-world vehicle names and logos <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:04">[00:10:04]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:09">[00:10:09]</a>.

## Understanding Rockstar/5M Policy

The discussion highlighted several key aspects of the policies governing [[gta_rp_servers_and_bans | GTA RP servers]], particularly regarding branding and content.

### Reasons for Bans and Policy Violations
The primary reason for server bans often relates to the violation of platform license agreements, specifically regarding real-world intellectual property <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:12:11">[01:12:11]</a>.

*   **Real-World Branding**: Using actual brand names, logos, or designs of vehicles (e.g., Lamborghini) without explicit permission from the brand is prohibited <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:31:13">[01:31:13]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:52:49">[02:52:49]</a>. This includes custom branding or T-shirts within the game if they incorporate real-world intellectual property <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:03:54">[01:03:54]</a>.
*   **Fictional World Principle**: Rockstar aims to maintain a fictional world within its games to avoid legal complications. Associating real-world brands with the game's violent or criminal elements could lead to lawsuits <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:38:27">[01:38:27]</a>. For example, if a real drink company's logo appears, and it's linked to an incident, that company might sue Rockstar <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:38:44">[01:38:44]</a>. This policy ensures Rockstar can claim its content is purely for entertainment and not related to real-world events <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:38:39">[01:38:39]</a>.
*   **Acquisition of 5M**: When Rockstar acquired 5M, previous lenient rules changed. The platform now operates under stricter [[GTA 5 RP gameplay and roleplaying scenarios | GTA]] policies, requiring real-world logos and elements to be removed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:35:08">[01:35:08]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:35:18">[01:35:18]</a>.

### Revenue Models and Brand Sponsorships
[[challenges_in_running_rp_servers | RP servers]] can generate revenue through selling in-game assets, such as custom clothing or guns, or priority slots <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:30:39">[01:30:39]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:22:22">[02:22:22]</a>. However, direct brand sponsorships in RP servers are complicated:
*   **Approval and Revenue Share**: If a brand (e.g., PlayStation) wants to sponsor an in-game event (like a race), it needs explicit permission from Rockstar/5M <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:28:17">[01:28:17]</a>. Moreover, Rockstar/5M expects a percentage of any revenue generated through such branding <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:31:38">[01:31:38]</a>. This implies a need for formal communication and authorization <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:28:20">[01:28:20]</a>.
*   **Dependence on Brands**: The server owner's revenue model can be significantly impacted if it relies heavily on player base rather than brand partnerships. If player numbers drop due to internal conflicts or bans, revenue takes a hit <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:37:31">[01:37:31]</a>.

### Map Customization
While servers can customize interiors and certain aspects of the game, there are strict rules for map changes:
*   **Official Landmarks**: Major landmarks, like Vinewood, or standard GTA assets (like Burger Shot) cannot be altered or renamed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:24:48">[01:24:48]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:39:34">[01:39:34]</a>.
*   **Developer-Made vs. Purchased Assets**: Server owners can develop custom elements from scratch, which require approval from 5M <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:24:31">[01:24:31]</a>. However, many servers purchase assets online, leading to potential issues if those assets violate policies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:24:00">[01:24:00]</a>.

### Enforcement and Warnings
[[GTA RP servers and bans | Server bans]] are often preceded by warnings. Servers receive notices about policy violations, and they are expected to fix these issues. Failure to comply can lead to more severe penalties, including temporary or permanent shutdowns <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:56:19">[02:56:19]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:57:05">[02:57:05]</a>.

> "A team gets a notice once, a warning comes. There are some people inside that particular warning. Some people fix everything for themselves... If yes, then they must have received a notice first" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:56:22">[02:56:22]</a>.
