## Roleplay Scenarios and Dynamics
The goal of the server is to create an environment where players can engage in various [[gaming_and_role_play_dynamics | roleplay scenarios]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:03">[00:17:03]</a>. One notable example discussed involved a "mafia RP" where the objective was to take a character named Bablu hostage and solicit help from others <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:59:09">[01:59:09]</a>.
