## Impact on Live Streaming

These [[challenges_faced_in_live_streaming | challenges faced in live streaming]] can significantly impact the overall experience:

*   **Disruption of Content**: Technical glitches interrupt the flow of the game or conversation, leading to viewer frustration.
*   **Stress on Streamer**: The constant need to troubleshoot and manage technical issues during a live broadcast adds considerable stress to the streamer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:57:21">[03:57:21]</a>.
*   **Loss of Immersion**: When technical problems arise, especially in role-playing (RP) contexts, it breaks the immersion for both the streamer and the audience.
