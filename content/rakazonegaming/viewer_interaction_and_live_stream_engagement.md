* **Content Control**: Navigating requests from viewers, such as revealing "secret endings" in a game <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:51:26">[03:51:26]</a> or engaging in content they are not interested in <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:47:29">[03:47:29]</a>, while maintaining the stream's direction.
