## Testing Format

In the "School Let's Go" series, participants are asked "turn key questions" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:11">[00:04:11]</a>.
*   **Scoring:** A correct answer earns one point (+1), while a wrong answer yields zero points (0) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:13">[00:04:13]</a>.
*   **Passing:** Contestants have the option to pass on a question <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:16">[00:04:16]</a>.
*   **Hints:** Hints are provided to assist the participants <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:18">[00:04:18]</a>.
*   **Rapid Fire:** Future sessions will feature a "five rapid fire questions" segment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:08">[00:10:08]</a>. During this segment, participants are required to state their name before answering <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:13">[00:10:13]</a>.
