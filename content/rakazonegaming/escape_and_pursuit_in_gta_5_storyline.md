During intense scenarios, a character might express that there is "no use of you running away" or that they would "die here with you" if robbed, indicating the high stakes of avoiding capture <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:05">[00:00:05]</a>. ## Police Involvement and Court Proceedings

Police actions often escalate rapidly, such as putting a "truck stop alarm" on a vehicle to halt movement <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:12">[00:02:12]</a>.

These proceedings might occur if a character is caught by the police for mistakenly being part of a "rogues gang" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:45">[00:02:45]</a>. The police submit reports to the court <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:01">[00:03:01]</a>, and characters are tried by police, sometimes seeking exemptions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:14">[00:03:14]</a>.
