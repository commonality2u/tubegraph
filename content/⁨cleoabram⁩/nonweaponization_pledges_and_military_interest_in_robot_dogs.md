## Military Interest in Robot Dogs

Although early models were too loud for this purpose <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:40">[00:08:40]</a>, as the technology for four-legged robots advanced, military interest intensified <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:42">[00:08:42]</a>.

Today, companies like Ghost Robotics are actively developing this technology for military applications <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:47">[00:08:47]</a>. For instance, in 2020, the Air Force collaborated with Ghost Robotics to deploy robot dogs for inspection duties at Tyndall Air Force Base <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:51">[00:08:51]</a>. Notably, these robots were not equipped with weapons <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:56">[00:08:56]</a>.
