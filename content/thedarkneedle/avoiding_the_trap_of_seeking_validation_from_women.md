However, as his feelings deepened, so did his insecurity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:08">[00:02:08]</a>. Feeling confused, he prematurely confessed his feelings, leading her to politely state she only saw him as a friend <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:25">[00:02:25]</a>. Despite her clear rejections, he continued to pursue her, causing her to distance herself and eventually cease communication <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:49">[00:02:49]</a>.

This young man's experience highlights several traits of a "low value male" that ultimately sabotaged his chances <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:21">[00:03:21]</a>.

### 2. Don't Agree with Her Too Much

*   **The Solution:** Women desire a challenge <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:18">[00:05:18]</a>. You won't be a challenge if you agree with them on everything <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:19">[00:05:19]</a>.
