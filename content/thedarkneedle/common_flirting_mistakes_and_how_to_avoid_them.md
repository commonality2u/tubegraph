## Why Flirting Matters

Without flirting, conversations remain platonic, lacking spark and attraction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:06:00">[01:06:00]</a>. A conversation without flirtation, like two platonic individuals chatting, will likely lead to the man going home alone or, worse, into the friend zone <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:15:00">[01:15:00]</a>. Flirting makes it clear you have romantic intentions and helps her see you as a man she could be intimate with <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:31:00">[01:31:00]</a>.

## Common Flirting Mistakes

Many men make common errors when flirting, often leading to rejection <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:00">[00:18:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:47:00">[01:47:00]</a>.

### Being Too Obvious and Direct
Being overly obvious kills mystery and excitement <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:56:00">[01:56:00]</a>. Directness, especially sexually, makes a man seem desperate <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:09:00">[05:09:00]</a>.
*   **Example of awkward obvious flirting**:
    *   Girl: "I love sports bras."
    *   Guy: "I bet you'd look amazing in a sports bra." <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:06:00">[02:06:00]</a>

### Complimenting Physical Looks
Complimenting a woman directly on her physical appearance can be unattractive <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:30:00">[02:30:00]</a>. It can make you seem inexperienced with attractive women and eliminates any mystery about your intentions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:36:00">[02:36:00]</a>.

### Being Too Sexually Direct (Lack of Plausible Deniability)
Women are socialized to feel shame or fear about being perceived as "slutty" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:52:00">[02:52:00]</a>. When a man is too direct about sexual intent, especially with someone recently met, women often make excuses or disappear <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:02:00">[03:02:00]</a>. This is her "anti-slut defense" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:09:00">[03:09:00]</a>.

### Using Non-Sexual Banter
Many men use the same non-sexual banter with women that they use with male friends <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:51:00">[03:51:00]</a>. This leads to interactions that lack sexual tension <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:09:00">[04:09:00]</a>. ### [[Common misconceptions about attracting women | Playing It Too Safe]] or Being Too Easy
When a woman feels no challenge or effort is required, she may lose interest, similar to how a man might lose interest in a woman who "throws herself at his feet" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:42:00">[08:42:00]</a>. ## How to Avoid These Mistakes and Become an Irresistible Flirt

### Be Subtle and Indirect
Subtle flirting works through implication, creating mystery and intrigue, leaving women wanting more <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:15:00">[02:15:00]</a>.
*   **Example of subtle flirting**:
    *   Girl: "I love sports bras."
    *   Guy: "Really? Well, believe it or not, I love girls who love sports bras." <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:39:00">[04:39:00]</a>
This implies attraction without direct bluntness, showing you're not desperate <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:53:00">[04:53:00]</a>.

### [[Handling tests from women during flirting | Pass Her Tests]]
Women will test your confidence with snide remarks, tricky questions, or unreasonable requests <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:32:00">[05:32:00]</a>.
*   **Method: Agree and Amplify**: Agree with what she says and take it to an exaggerated, absurd level <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:03:00">[06:03:00]</a>.
    *   Girl: "I bet you use that line on all the girls."
    *   Guy: "Yeah, I wake up every morning surrounded by women. Is that good?" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:11:00">[06:11:00]</a>
    *   Girl: "How many women have you slept with?"
    *   Guy: "Today? Not that many." <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:17:00">[06:17:00]</a>
    *   Girl: "Buy me a drink."
    *   Guy: "Sure, would you like my ATM pin too?" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:22:00">[06:22:00]</a>

### Act Like You're the Prize
Flip the "boys chase girls" dynamic by playfully implying she is chasing you <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:37:00">[06:37:00]</a>. Frame the conversation as if she's the pursuer trying to seduce you <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:47:00">[06:47:00]</a>.
*   Girl: "I love sports bras."
*   Guy: "Don't try to make me think of you in a sports bra." <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:03:00">[07:03:00]</a>
*   Girl: "Want to grab another drink?"

*   Guy: "I live just around the corner, we can go and grab a drink at my place, but only if you promise not to try anything." <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:17:00">[07:17:00]</a>

### Push-Pull Flirting
This technique involves a playful mix of pushing her away and pulling her back in, verbally and physically <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:53:00">[07:53:00]</a>. This contradiction creates excitement and keeps her engaged <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:08:00">[08:08:00]</a>.
*   Guy: "You're too cute for me to handle." <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:00:00">[08:00:00]</a> (Pulls her in by finding her cute, pushes away by saying she's "too much.")
*   Guy: "You're awesome, you're like my little sister." <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:13:00">[08:13:00]</a> (Pulls her in with a compliment, pushes away with a non-romantic comparison.)
This dynamic creates intrigue and demonstrates you are a high-value guy <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:25:00">[08:25:00]</a>.

### Pretend It's Her Fault
Playfully accuse her of trying to seduce you <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:16:00">[09:16:00]</a>.
*   If she talks about her house, ask: "Are you already trying to bring me to your place? I'm not surprised." <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:28:00">[09:28:00]</a>
Women enjoy this teasing <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:34:00">[09:34:00]</a>.

### Use Sexual Allusions
Instead of directly stating sexual prowess, use indirect allusions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:51:00">[09:51:00]</a>.
*   If you've made her a cocktail and she says, "Wow, you're good at that," make eye contact and say: "I'm good at many things." <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:57:00">[09:57:00]</a>

### Encourage Her to Use You
Reverse the common accusation of men using women for sex <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:05:00">[10:05:00]</a>.
