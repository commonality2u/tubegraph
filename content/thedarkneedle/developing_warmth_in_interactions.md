Warmth is a key element of charisma, alongside presence and power <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:07:00">[01:07:00]</a>. It encompasses kindness, gentleness, affection, and love <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:53:00">[04:53:00]</a>.

## True Kindness vs. Hypocrisy
Being kind and compassionate does not mean constantly praising others or doing everything they ask <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:00:00">[05:00:00]</a>. If kindness is shown merely to gain charisma or be liked, it is considered hypocrisy and will not be well-received because it's not natural <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:05:00">[05:05:00]</a>.

## Communication Beyond Words
Human communication relies heavily on non-verbal cues: only 7% depends on words, while 93% depends on body language, feelings, and emotions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:19:00">[05:19:00]</a>.
Your inner feelings and emotions convey an "energy" that others unconsciously sense, affecting whether they want to be around you <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:45:00">[05:45:00]</a>. For example, if you dislike doctors and speak to one, even without showing your dislike, your feelings will be sensed and prevent a genuine [[building_emotional_connections_in_interactions | connection]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:39:00">[05:39:00]</a>. This emotional transmission is crucial for [[triggering_emotional_responses_during_conversations | triggering emotional responses]] and [[creating_positive_emotional_connections_through_dialogue | creating positive emotional connections]] leading to [[creating_emotional_attachment_through_conversation | emotional attachment]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:55:00">[05:55:00]</a>.

To cultivate warmth:
*   **Assume positive intent** When talking to someone, especially for the first time, assume they are a good person and look for their good qualities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:01:00">[06:01:00]</a>. Feel admiration for them, be enthusiastic, and genuinely want to listen <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:16:00">[06:16:00]</a>. This inner feeling will naturally project and "touch their heart" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:22:00">[06:22:00]</a>. As humans, we share feelings; if you're comfortable or want to be with someone, they will reciprocate <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:31:00">[06:31:00]</a>.
*   **Embrace the power of giving** Add value to a person's life by offering advice or information, but never impose it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:51:00">[06:51:00]</a>.
    *   If a friend mentions wanting to rent a house and you know a relevant website, share it without prompting <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:59:00">[06:59:00]</a>.
    *   Do not follow up or insist they use your advice <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:15:00">[07:15:00]</a>. The act of giving information is sufficient <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:27:00">[07:27:00]</a>. Sharing knowledge is a beautiful way to offer value <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:39:00">[07:39:00]</a>.
*   **Practice acceptance, understanding, and honesty (positively)**
    *   **Be honest:** Do not pretend to like something you don't just to be agreeable; people will eventually realize you're being dishonest, and constant agreement can make you seem boring <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:56:00">[07:56:00]</a>.
    *   **Accept:** While being honest about your preferences (e.g., "No, I don't like football"), accept that the other person likes it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:07:00">[08:07:00]</a>. Do not criticize their interests or tell them their hobbies are a "waste of time" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:20:00">[08:20:00]</a>. Accept people as they are <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:27:00">[08:27:00]</a>.
    *   **Be sincere:** When offering praise, ensure it is sincere <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:47:00">[08:47:00]</a>. If you offer criticism (to someone you have a close relationship with), do so briefly and without insistence <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:52:00">[08:52:00]</a>.

In summary, true kindness involves being honest while accepting and understanding others, sharing valuable information without imposing, sympathizing, and genuinely caring <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:09:00">[09:09:00]</a>. These are effective [[techniques_for_emotional_engagement | techniques for emotional engagement]].
