Men with standards rigorously filter everything in their lives—every person, activity, and environment—by asking: "Does this add value to my life?" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:53">[00:03:53]</a>. If the answer is no, they eliminate it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:01">[00:04:01]</a>. This represents a fundamental shift from judging to appraising, akin to an investor who understands their worth and refuses to undervalue it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:03">[00:04:03]</a>.

By practicing this daily, one's inner circle becomes more refined, thoughts become clearer, and women who previously overlooked you will begin to take notice <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:10">[00:04:10]</a>. This transformation begins with personal evolution, with everything else catching up later <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:19">[00:04:19]</a>.
