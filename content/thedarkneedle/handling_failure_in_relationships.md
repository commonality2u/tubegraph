## Demonstrating the Ability to Handle Failure

To demonstrate this trait:
*   **Express your failures without discouragement** If you apply for a job and fail an interview, share this information without showing discouragement or lost hope <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:53">[00:08:53]</a>. Immediately apply to other opportunities and continue your search <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:08">[00:09:08]</a>.
*   **Face relationship mistakes directly** If you make a mistake with a woman, whether in dating or a [[managing_expectations_in_relationships | relationship]], and it is your fault, do not back down <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:12">[00:09:12]</a>. Instead, face the issue, communicate, and work to resolve it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:20">[00:09:20]</a>. Women are naturally attracted to men who face challenges and solve problems <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:33">[00:09:33]</a>. The process of failing and then succeeding is more attractive than simply being successful <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:37">[00:09:37]</a>.

## What to Avoid

It is crucial to never remain stuck in failure <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:46">[00:09:46]</a>. This means:
*   **Do not give up** If you get injured while working out and stop training, or fail an exam and cease studying, it indicates giving up at the first sign of failure <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:51">[00:09:51]</a>.
*   **Do not complain or cry** When you fail, avoid letting discouragement take over or complaining <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:00">[00:10:00]</a>. Instead, pick yourself up and rise again <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:05">[00:10:05]</a>.
*   **Do not be ashamed of failure** Failure is a natural part of life and not a flaw; there's no need to be embarrassed by it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:07">[00:10:07]</a>.

Demonstrating the ability to handle failure assures a woman that if problems arise in the relationship, you will be able to overcome them, fostering a sense of safety and leading to increased attraction and attachment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:13">[00:10:13]</a>.
