Being like a rock means not reacting, regardless of external stimuli <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:24">[00:00:24]</a>. Just as a rock doesn't react to yelling or hitting, a non-reactive person remains steady and unaffected <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>.

## Dealing with Rejection in Life in General

The principle of non-reactivity is not just for dating; it's essential for [[handling_failure_in_relationships | dealing with rejection in life]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:48">[00:09:48]</a>.

*   **Rejection as a Test of Social Value:** Every person will react differently to you <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:57">[00:09:57]</a>. If someone can easily get you into a highly emotional state through their actions or words, it indicates high emotional reactivity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:09">[00:10:09]</a>.
*   **External Self-Worth Kills Attraction:** [[Strategies for maintaining confidence and selfworth in dating | If your self-worth comes from an external source]], like a girl's response, it directly kills attraction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:19">[00:10:19]</a>. If her positive response makes you feel good and a negative one makes you feel bad, you've given external factors control over your emotional state <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:27">[00:10:27]</a>.
*   **Rejection Reflects Your Reactive State:** Taking rejection personally and dwelling on it for days signifies a highly reactive state <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:50">[00:10:50]</a>. Often, the reason for rejection is that the other person senses your high reactivity and that your inner well-being depends entirely on them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:59">[00:10:59]</a>. Being reactive shows you view them as more valuable and need their approval <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:06">[00:11:06]</a>.
*   **Solution:** The key is not to try to change the other person's reaction, but to learn how to deal with it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:13">[00:11:13]</a>. Understand that one rejection doesn't mean all will follow <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:21">[00:11:21]</a>. Don't let it affect you at all <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:24">[00:11:24]</a>.
