This is because it eliminates the element of mystery and the uncertainty that initially drove attraction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:26">[00:09:26]</a>. Therefore, it is advised to never reveal true feelings too early, keeping her in a state of mystery and a guessing game <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:42">[00:09:42]</a>.

A balanced mix of emotions, where no single emotion dominates, creates lasting attraction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:37">[00:15:37]</a>. Unpredictability in behavior and conversation, maintaining a slight sense of mystery, keeps the woman guessing and engaged <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:51">[00:15:51]</a>.
