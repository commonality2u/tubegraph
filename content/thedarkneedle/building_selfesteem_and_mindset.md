## Avoiding Self-Doubt and Devaluation

A critical aspect of [[developing_a_confident_mindset | developing a confident mindset]] is preventing self-doubt when facing rejection or disinterest <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:22">[00:10:22]</a>.

### Recognize the Source of the Problem
It's important to avoid internalizing a girl's loss of interest as a personal flaw <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:25">[00:10:25]</a>. Do not question your attractiveness, personality strength, financial status, or shyness <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:27">[00:10:27]</a>. Such thoughts can lead to self-devaluation and lower [[building_selfconfidence_and_strong_body_language | self-esteem]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:35">[00:10:35]</a>.

### Focus on Behavior, Not Personal Identity
A girl's attraction or lack thereof is not an indictment of you as a person or your inherent personality <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:43">[00:10:43]</a>. Instead, it is entirely related to your actions and behavior <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:47">[00:10:47]</a>. If she doesn't like you, it signifies a dislike of your behavior, which is something that can be changed at any time <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:57">[00:10:57]</a>. This perspective promotes [[selfleadership_and_personal_responsibility | personal responsibility]] and growth rather than self-blame.

## Maintaining Composure and Strategic Distance

When a girl loses interest and begins to distance herself, a key strategy for both attraction and maintaining your own [[strategies_for_maintaining_confidence_and_selfworth_in_dating | self-worth]] is to give her space <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:08">[00:11:08]</a>.

### The Importance of Space and Calmness
Staying calm and composed and providing distance by not talking to her and staying away demonstrates that you are not desperate or easily affected <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:12">[00:11:12]</a>. When she observes this behavior, it is highly likely she will re-engage and reach out <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:17">[00:11:17]</a>.

### Avoiding Immediate Re-engagement
When she does return, do not immediately invite her on a date <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:31">[00:11:31]</a>. Doing so could lead her to believe that you were simply waiting for her and that your previous composure was a facade <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:33">[00:11:33]</a>. Instead, engage in casual conversation and gradually reignite the necessary emotions, as discussed in previous videos on [[the_mindset_of_attracting_women | attracting women]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:47">[00:11:47]</a>. Only after a period of time should you consider asking her on a date <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:52">[00:11:52]</a>.
