## Factors Determining the Number of Acquaintances

Two primary factors dictate how many acquaintances a person has:

### 1. Exposure
Exposure involves actively engaging with the world, leaving the house, and visiting diverse places <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:51">[00:01:51]</a>. It means having a social life instead of staying isolated <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:57">[00:01:57]</a>. More places visited lead to meeting more people, creating opportunities for connection <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:01">[00:02:01]</a>.

### 2. Ability to Speak (Your Tongue)
This factor allows individuals to capitalize on the opportunities created by exposure <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:22">[00:02:22]</a>. It refers to one's [[improving_social_skills_as_a_practice | ability to speak]] and engage effectively <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:28">[00:02:28]</a>. Two individuals with identical routines and exposure can yield vastly different social outcomes based on their [[social_skills_comparison | level of social skills]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:08">[00:02:08]</a>.
