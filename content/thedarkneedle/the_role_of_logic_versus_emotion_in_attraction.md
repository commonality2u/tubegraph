## Rules of Attraction: Navigating Emotional Dynamics

Her brain releases dopamine from anticipation and emotional highs and lows, which requires uncertainty <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:36">[00:04:36]</a>. Predictable men do not stay in her mind <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:46">[00:04:46]</a>.

This keeps her in an emotional fog, causing her to chase <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:19">[00:05:19]</a>.

*   **Inconsistent communication rhythm:** Vary your response times to texts. Reply instantly sometimes, then go cold for hours or disappear after a deep conversation. If she asks why, state, "I don't live on my phone. When I'm with someone, they get all of me. When I'm not, they get nothing" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:40">[00:05:40]</a>.
*   **Pull back after intensity:** After a deep moment (e.g., sex, a vulnerable story), pull back. Don't immediately text "I miss you."
