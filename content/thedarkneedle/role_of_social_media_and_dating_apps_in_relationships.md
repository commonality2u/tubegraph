## Increased Competition for Attention

Social media and dating applications have dramatically altered the landscape of romantic interest and attention. Where previously dozens of men might vie for a woman's attention, these platforms have expanded that number into hundreds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:06">[00:00:06]</a>. This shift intensifies the competition for a woman's favor, which can lead to women developing a heightened sense of self-importance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:10">[00:00:10]</a>. Consequently, if a man does not possess certain physical attributes or financial stability, a woman may perceive herself as superior to him and look down on him <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:12">[00:00:12]</a>.
