When women are asked what they seek in a man, "passionate" is a common answer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:26">[00:05:26]</a>. This is fundamentally true: enthusiasm breeds enthusiasm <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:33">[00:05:33]</a>. Showing what genuinely excites you can transform even a mundane subject into an engrossing [[creating_engaging_conversations_with_women | engaging conversation]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:41">[00:05:41]</a>.

Instead of hiding your interests, let your excitement about them shine through <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:58">[00:05:58]</a>. This means explaining what truly drives you, not just demonstrating your knowledge <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:02">[00:06:02]</a>. Women are drawn to men with drive who know what they want and pursue it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:07">[00:06:07]</a>.

## Connecting Passion to Emotions

Men often miss opportunities to demonstrate their passionate side due to nervousness and anxiety <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:57">[00:09:57]</a>. A key difference in communication styles is that women love to discuss the meaning behind actions and the emotions they evoke <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:13">[00:10:13]</a>. The emotions that arise during a conversation can subconsciously anchor the woman to you <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:18">[00:10:18]</a>.

It's vital to draw her into a positive emotional state <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:45">[00:10:45]</a>. If she discusses negative feelings associated with a past relationship, she may begin to associate those feelings with you <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:27">[00:10:27]</a>. Conversely, if you evoke positive emotions, she will [[triggering_emotional_responses_during_conversations | anchor positive feelings]] to you <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:43">[00:10:43]</a>.

### Technique: Prompting Emotional Responses
When a woman mentions a hobby or interest, shift the conversation from factual details (e.g., specific locations or equipment) to the feelings she experiences <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:51">[00:10:51]</a>.

For example, if she mentions snowboarding:
*   Instead of asking about *where* she snowboards, ask about *what excites her most* about it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:00">[00:11:00]</a>.
*   Prompt her to describe the feeling of adventure, the rush, or the sense of being truly alive while doing it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:17">[00:11:17]</a>.

By focusing on the meaning and emotions evoked by a topic, you put the woman in a positive mood, subconsciously anchoring feelings like adventure, excitement, and truly living to you <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:31">[00:11:31]</a>. This approach makes you an intriguing and exciting presence in her life <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:00">[00:05:00]</a>.
