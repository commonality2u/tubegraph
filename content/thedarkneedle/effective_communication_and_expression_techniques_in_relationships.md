As a man, feeding this inherent instinct can lead to a woman becoming more attracted to you <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:40">[00:00:40]</a>.

It's important to remember that relationships are primarily governed by emotion, not logic <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:29">[00:02:29]</a>.

## Cultivating the Right Mindset

A crucial aspect of [[communicating_effectively_to_build_connections | effective communication]] in romantic contexts is to shift your mindset.

### Do Not Try to Impress Her
One of the most attractive traits a man can possess is not being overly concerned with gaining a woman's approval <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:45">[00:02:45]</a>. The modern world often places a high value on women, leading men to subconsciously believe they need to earn a woman's approval <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:03">[00:03:03]</a>. This can cause men to forget this principle and automatically try to impress when talking to a woman <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:57">[00:02:57]</a>.

### Overcoming Internal Pressure
When you speak with an attractive woman, you might feel an internal pressure to impress her <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:31">[00:03:31]</a>. To counteract this subconscious programming:
1.  **Acknowledge the pressure:** Be aware of this feeling of wanting to impress <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:39">[00:03:39]</a>. Awareness allows you to control how you direct it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:43">[00:03:43]</a>.
2.  **Redirect the pressure:** Instead of acting on the urge to impress, redirect it. Mentally tell yourself, "If I want to impress her, I must not try to impress her" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:19">[00:04:19]</a>. This mindset shift allows you to overcome the pressure and gives her the space to want to gain *your* approval <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:43">[00:04:43]</a>. With practice, this process becomes automatic <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:40">[00:04:40]</a>.

## The "Recipe" for Inspiring Her Approval

The core [[effective_communication_strategies | strategy]] to make a woman seek your approval involves two main components <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:53">[00:04:53]</a>:
1.  **Not giving her validation immediately:** Women inherently crave admiration and validation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:09">[00:05:09]</a>. This means not liking every social media post, not commenting on everything, and not appearing obsessed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:26">[00:05:26]</a>.
2.  **Telling her what you like in a girl:** Express your preferences regarding a girl's personality and appearance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:40">[00:05:40]</a>.

When these two elements are combined, her internal instincts activate. She will wonder why she isn't receiving your validation and recognize that she needs to make you admire her <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:47">[00:05:47]</a>. This leads her to consider what you like and start doing those things, thereby actively trying to gain your admiration <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:55">[00:05:55]</a>.

### Conditions for Applying the "Recipe"

For this "recipe" to be effective, certain [[effective_conversation_techniques_with_women | communication techniques]] and rules must be followed:

#### 1. Partial Alignment
When expressing your preferences, a small portion (around 40%) of what you like should already be present in her, but the majority (around 60%) should not <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:26">[00:06:26]</a>. This avoids appearing insincere or intolerable. If you like girls who wear dresses, and she typically wears pants, your preference stands out because most other men would validate her current attire <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:44">[00:06:44]</a>.

#### 2. General Expression of Opinion
Speak generally and casually when stating your likes, reflecting only your opinion <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:56">[00:07:56]</a>. For example, say "I like girls with short hair" rather than "You should cut your hair" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:01">[00:08:01]</a>. Do not give the impression that you are telling her what to do to earn your acceptance or become your girlfriend <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:13">[00:08:13]</a>. The ability to express yourself effectively is a key skill for [[impact_of_communication_skills_on_building_relationships | building connections]] and a component of charisma <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:33">[00:08:33]</a>. She will automatically apply your general preferences to herself <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:50">[00:08:50]</a>.

#### 3. Do Not React to Everything She Does
Avoid reacting to something a girl says or does merely because she is beautiful or because she said it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:46">[00:09:46]</a>. If she says something dull or silly, do not laugh <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:07">[00:10:07]</a>. While this might cause her some embarrassment, it is part of the process to make her attracted to you <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:15">[00:10:15]</a>. Only react genuinely if something is truly good or remarkable <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:28">[00:10:28]</a>. This communicates that your admiration is not easily earned and requires genuine quality in her personality or behavior <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:33">[00:10:33]</a>.

#### 4. Approach with Curiosity, Not Admiration
When asking questions to get to know a girl, your demeanor should reflect curiosity, as if you haven't decided whether you admire her yet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:05">[00:11:05]</a>. Your body language and tone should convey this <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:20">[00:11:20]</a>. Avoid overly eager smiles or enthusiastic tones <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:23">[00:11:23]</a>. Internally, focus on the intention of pure curiosity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:39">[00:11:39]</a>.

#### 5. Avoid Being Interrogated
Never put yourself in a position where a girl interrogates you, especially early in a conversation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:54">[00:11:54]</a>. If she asks multiple questions and you continuously explain yourself, she gains control of the conversation and judges you, which goes against natural dynamics <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:09">[00:12:09]</a>. As the man, you are the one who chooses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:16">[00:12:16]</a>.
*   **Tactics to avoid interrogation:** Use phrases like "It's a long story," "Take a guess," or give a short answer and then shift the question back to her <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:21">[00:12:21]</a>.

When a girl begins to win your approval, she might mention qualities or achievements to impress you, such as practicing Taekwondo, loving kids, or reading books <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:44">[00:12:44]</a>. *   **Correct Response:** Instead of revealing a commonality or trying to match her achievement, either comment casually or, ideally, make it seem like you are not good at the activity she excels in <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:21">[00:13:21]</a>.
    *   *Example:* If she says, "I practice Taekwondo," you could respond, "I tried it once and almost broke my leg" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:30">[00:13:30]</a>.
