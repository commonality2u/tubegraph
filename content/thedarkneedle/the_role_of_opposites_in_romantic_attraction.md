## Sexual Electricity and Polarity
Sexual electricity, akin to a battery with a positive and negative side, is generated by the polarity between opposing forces <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:28:00">[00:28:00]</a>. If two individuals possess similar "charges" (e.g., two negatives or two positives), no electricity or attraction is generated <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:35:00">[00:35:00]</a>. A common misconception is to interpret "opposites attract" superficially, such as attraction based merely on physical traits like a woman having breasts or a man being tall <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:49:00">[00:49:00]</a>. In reality, this principle is the deep foundation for [[psychological_dynamics_of_attraction | psychological dynamics of attraction]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:05:00">[01:05:00]</a>.

### Friendship vs. Attraction
A golden rule to remember is:
*   Friendship happens when two similar people meet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:14:00">[01:14:00]</a>.
*   [[contrasting_personalities_in_relationships | Attraction happens when two opposite individuals meet]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:16:00">[01:16:00]</a>.

Historically, this principle was more naturally understood, as men and women embraced distinct roles <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:20:00">[01:20:00]</a>. However, modern societal factors like equality, feminism, and social media have caused men to lose their natural masculine essence, leading them to believe they need to be logical and find common ground to attract women <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:28:00">[01:28:00]</a>. Despite these changes, women's natural essence has remained constant; they are [[the_role_of_logic_versus_emotion_in_attraction | emotional beings]] who do not control actions through logic <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:46:00">[01:46:00]</a>. For a man, the key is to return to his natural state and instinctively become the opposite of the woman he's interacting with <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:55:00">[01:55:00]</a>.
