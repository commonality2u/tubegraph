These logical explanations are subconscious justifications rather than the true reasons for the purchase <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:44">[00:00:44]</a>.

Women are identified as the biggest consumers globally, buying more than men, because they are more emotional beings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>.
