## Differences in Attraction: Visual vs. Emotional

A core distinction in [[basic_principles_of_attraction | attraction]] dynamics between men and women is the primary mode of attraction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:18">[00:01:18]</a>:

*   **Men are attracted visually** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:20">[00:01:20]</a>. A straight man will instinctually be attracted to a woman with a beautiful body and a pretty face, regardless of his own physical traits or specific preferences <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>. This is an instinctual response that men cannot control <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:14">[00:01:14]</a>.
*   **Women are attracted emotionally** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:22">[00:01:22]</a>. If a man can trigger the right emotions in a woman, she will be attracted to him, irrespective of his physical appearance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:23">[00:01:23]</a>. This explains why a man surrounded by many women often appears more attractive to other women, as it suggests he possesses qualities that elicit a universal desired emotional response <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>.

## Behaviors that Fuel Attraction
