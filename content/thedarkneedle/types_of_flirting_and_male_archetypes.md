### Key Flirting Techniques

#### 1. [[the_role_of_subtlety_in_effective_flirting | Be Subtle and Indirect]]
Rather than blunt, clumsy, and overly direct flirting, great flirting works through implication <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:15:00">[02:15:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:28:00">[04:28:00]</a>. This also projects you as a "high value guy" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:15:00">[05:15:00]</a>.

#### 2. [[handling_tests_from_women_during_flirting | Handle Tests from Women]]
Attractive women will often "test" a man to gauge his confidence <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:28:00">[05:28:00]</a>. These tests can come as snide remarks, tricky questions, or unreasonable requests <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:51:00">[05:51:00]</a>.

*   **How to Pass**: The easiest method is to "agree and amplify." This means agreeing with what she says and exaggerating it to an absurd level, showing you are unfazed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:03:00">[06:03:00]</a>.

#### 3. [[role_of_boldness_and_cheekiness_in_attraction | Act Like the Prize]]
Most attractive women are accustomed to being chased. This technique involves flipping that dynamic by subtly insinuating that she is the one pursuing you <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:30:00">[06:30:00]</a>. By playfully twisting her words and actions, you frame the conversation as if she's trying to seduce you <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:52:00">[06:52:00]</a>.

#### 4. [[psychology_of_pushpull_dynamics_in_flirting | Push-Pull Flirting]]
This technique involves a playful mix of pushing a woman away and pulling her back in, both physically and verbally <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:53:00">[07:53:00]</a>. This contradiction creates excitement, keeps her engaged, and builds playful tension <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:08:00">[08:08:00]</a>. This dynamic also demonstrates that you are a "high value guy" because women tend to lose interest in men who make themselves too available, valuing what they have to work for <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:32:00">[08:32:00]</a>.

A good starting point is to playfully accuse her of trying to seduce you <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:16:00">[09:16:00]</a>. This allows her to understand the implication and become curious <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:01:00">[10:01:00]</a>.
