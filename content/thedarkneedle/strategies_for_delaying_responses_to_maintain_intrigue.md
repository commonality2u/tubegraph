*   **Example 1: Taco Outing**

This shows care and attention without appearing weak or overly eager <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:08">[00:10:08]</a>.
*   **Example 2: Beach Trip**

*   **Show Strength**: Do not give in to whining or immediate requests like most men <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:51">[00:10:51]</a>.
*   **Set Boundaries**: Gently but firmly make it clear that whining is not tolerated <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:55">[00:10:55]</a>.
