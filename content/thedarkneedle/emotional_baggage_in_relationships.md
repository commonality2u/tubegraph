## Identifying Emotional Baggage

Emotional baggage often manifests in specific behaviors and attitudes that indicate unresolved past issues <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:36:12">[07:36:12]</a>.

### Attachment to an Ex-Partner
One of the most significant indicators of emotional baggage is when a person is still attached to their ex-partner <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:29:13">[07:29:13]</a>. If a girl frequently mentions her ex or admits she still thinks about him often and hasn't gotten over him, it's a clear [[red_flags_in_relationships | red flag]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:29:13">[07:29:13]</a>. This suggests she is carrying heavy emotional baggage from her past that she hasn't dealt with <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:36:12">[07:36:12]</a>.

In a specific scenario, if a girl tells you about a past breakup, how much she loved her ex, and how she's trying to get him back, she is attempting to dump her emotions on you <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:49:10">[10:49:10]</a>. This behavior is a classic sign of being put into the friend zone and indicates she is genuinely interested in the other person <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="11:04:20">[11:04:20]</a>.
