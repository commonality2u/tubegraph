## Account Verification and Security

Before making any deposits or withdrawals on your [[introduction_to_advcash_platform | Advcash account]], it is crucial to verify it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:47">[00:06:47]</a>. Without verification, the account will be inoperative for these functions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:53">[00:06:53]</a>. Verification involves scanning your ID or passport and performing a mobile phone verification <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:37">[00:06:37]</a>. The [[account_creation_and_verification_on_advcash | account creation and verification on Advcash]] process also includes setting up two-factor authentication for increased security <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:44">[00:02:44]</a>.

## Depositing Funds

Advcash offers various methods for depositing money into your account.

### Fiat Currency Deposits

You can deposit national currencies, also known as fiat money, using several methods:
*   **Visa or Mastercard**: Deposits via these cards typically incur commissions of up to 3.5% <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:30">[00:04:30]</a>.
*   **SEPA Transfers**: Depositing via SEPA transfers incurs a commission of 0% plus one euro <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:47">[00:09:47]</a>. For example, a 100 euro deposit via SEPA would only cost one euro in commission <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:00">[00:10:00]</a>.
    *   **Limits**: The minimum deposit amount via SEPA is 100 euros, and the maximum is 10,000 euros <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:25">[00:10:25]</a>.
    *   **Processing Time**: SEPA transfers take approximately three business days to be reflected in your Advcash account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:11">[00:10:11]</a>.
    *   **Requirements**: You must use a bank account with the same name as your Advcash account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:56">[00:10:56]</a>.
*   **Other Exchanges/Payment Methods**: Advcash supports deposits from numerous external platforms such as PayPal, Sberbank, Skrill, Solana, and many others <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:20">[00:14:20]</a>. These methods may have specific instructions and associated commissions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:42">[00:14:42]</a>.

### Cryptocurrency Deposits

Depositing cryptocurrencies to your Advcash account typically does not incur any commission from Advcash itself <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:17">[00:12:17]</a>, as fees are usually charged by the exchange from which you are withdrawing <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:20">[00:12:20]</a>.
*   **Confirmation**: A deposit requires a certain number of block confirmations, such as six confirmations for some cryptocurrencies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:31">[00:12:31]</a>.
*   **Process**: For cryptocurrency deposits, you will receive a QR code and a wallet address <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:08">[00:13:08]</a>. The QR code is valid for up to 15 minutes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:12">[00:13:12]</a>. It is crucial to accurately transfer cryptocurrencies as errors can lead to permanent loss of funds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:28">[00:13:28]</a>.
*   [[currency_exchange_and_cryptocurrencies_on_advcash | Advcash]] accepts a wide range of cryptocurrencies for deposit, including Dai, USDC, Ripple, Bitcoin, and many others <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:00">[00:14:00]</a>.

## Withdrawing Funds

Advcash offers various methods for withdrawing money, each with its own commissions and conditions.

### Transfers to Other Advcash Users

*   Sending money to another Advcash user is instant and incurs no commission <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:28">[00:17:28]</a>.
*   Funds can be sent via email, even if the recipient does not yet have an Advcash account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:22">[00:18:22]</a>. A protection code can be added for security <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:29">[00:18:29]</a>.

### Withdrawals to External Cards

*   Funds can be sent to [[adding_and_using_debit_credit_cards_for_withdrawals | Visa or Mastercard]] cards in various currencies like USD or EUR <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:10">[00:03:10]</a>.
*   For a 100 euro deposit on a card, the commission is five euros <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:30">[00:19:30]</a>. The minimum commission is five euros, and then 2.5% applies when the amount exceeds five euros <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:50">[00:19:50]</a>.
*   Withdrawal limits are a minimum of one euro and a maximum of five thousand euros <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:22">[00:19:22]</a>.

### Withdrawals to Bank Accounts

*   **SEPA Transfers**: For SEPA transfers to accounts in the Eurozone, the commission is five euros plus 0.50% of the amount <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:00">[00:20:00]</a>.
*   **Local Bank Transfers**: Advcash supports local bank transfers in many countries, including Argentina, Australia, Canada, Colombia, Dominican Republic, Egypt, India, Japan, Nepal, and Pakistan <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:17">[00:20:17]</a>.
    *   Commissions are typically 0.5% of the amount <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:10">[00:20:10]</a>.
    *   A list of supported local banks is available, such as Citibank <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:21:01">[00:21:01]</a>.

### Cash Withdrawal

Advcash facilitates cash withdrawals in various countries where it has collaborations, such as Russia and Ukraine <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:21:40">[00:21:40]</a>.
*   **Processing Time**: From 15 minutes to 24 hours <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:21:47">[00:21:47]</a>.
*   **Exchange Rate**: Manual <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:21:51">[00:21:51]</a>.
*   **Minimum Amount**: In Russia, the minimum for cash withdrawal is 3,000 euros <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:21:54">[00:21:54]</a>.

### Withdrawals to Other Exchanges/Payment Methods

Similar to deposits, funds can be sent to other platforms like PayPal <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:22">[00:22:22]</a>. This process involves specific parameters for your external account and incurs specific commissions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:26">[00:22:26]</a>.

## Advcash Cards

Advcash offers virtual and plastic cards that can be used for payments worldwide <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:58">[00:02:58]</a>.
*   **Creation Cost**: A virtual card costs 9.95 euros to create <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:27:17">[00:27:17]</a>.
*   **Validity**: Cards are valid for three years <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:27:21">[00:27:21]</a>.
*   **Maintenance**: Maintenance is free <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:27:13">[00:27:13]</a>.
*   **Commissions**:
    *   Buying in the card's currency: No commission <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:27:29">[00:27:29]</a>.
    *   Currency exchange (Scrabble Shift Chains): 2.95% <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:27:32">[00:27:32]</a>.
*   **Important Note**: Card balances are separate from wallet balances, so you must charge your card before use <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:26:59">[00:26:59]</a>.

While depositing capital via SEPA transfers on Advcash is relatively cheap, withdrawing money through the same methods tends to be more expensive <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:57">[00:22:57]</a>. Key purposes for using Advcash include:
*   **International Transfers**: Sending money to local banks in various countries like Russia, Kazakhstan, Argentina, and the Dominican Republic <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:10">[00:23:10]</a>. Many users utilize Advcash to send funds to family members in these countries <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:18">[00:23:18]</a>.
*   [[currency_exchange_and_cryptocurrencies_on_advcash | Buying and Selling Cryptocurrencies]]: The platform allows buying and selling cryptocurrencies in collaboration with the Binance platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:33">[00:23:33]</a>.
*   **Generating Interest**: Users can maintain crypto savings to earn annualized interest, in collaboration with the Nexo platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:23">[00:25:23]</a>.
