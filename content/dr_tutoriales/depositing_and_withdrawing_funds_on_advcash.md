Advcash is an electronic or digital wallet that allows users to [[advcash_platform_features | manage capital online]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>. While similar to platforms like PayPal, Advcash distinguishes itself by enabling trading with various [[using_advcash_for_cryptocurrency_transactions | cryptocurrencies]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:01">[00:01:01]</a>. It supports both national fiat currencies and [[using_advcash_for_cryptocurrency_transactions | crypto-currencies]] within the same ecosystem <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:04">[00:02:04]</a>.

## Account Setup and Verification

To effectively manage funds on Advcash, it's essential to first [[creating_and_managing_an_advcash_account | create and verify an account]].

> Users can create a totally free account without maintenance costs, even in cases of inactivity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:34">[00:02:34]</a>. A referral link offering a 20% discount on transactions is available for new users <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:20">[00:00:20]</a>.

### Verification Process
Account verification is crucial for enabling deposits and withdrawals <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:47">[00:06:47]</a>. Without verification, the account remains inoperative <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:53">[00:06:53]</a>. The process involves:
*   Scanning an ID or passport <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:37">[00:06:37]</a>.
*   Performing a mobile phone verification <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:41">[00:06:41]</a>.

Double authentication verification methods are also available for enhanced [[advcash_security_and_verification_processes | security]], including Google Authenticator, email, or mobile phone <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:11">[00:07:11]</a>.

## Depositing Funds

Advcash offers various methods for depositing both fiat and [[using_advcash_for_cryptocurrency_transactions | cryptocurrency]] funds.

### Depositing Fiat Currencies

Users can deposit national currencies like USD or EUR using several methods:
*   **Visa / Mastercard:** Commissions of up to 3.5% apply <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:30">[00:04:30]</a>.
*   **SEPA Transfers:** A commission of €1 is charged <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:25">[00:04:25]</a>.
    *   **Limits:** The minimum deposit amount is €100, and the maximum is €10,000 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:25">[00:10:25]</a>.
    *   **Processing Time:** Funds typically take three business days to appear in the Advcash account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:11">[00:10:11]</a>.
    *   **Important Note:** Users must use a bank account registered under the same name as their Advcash account for transfers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:56">[00:10:56]</a>.
    *   Daily limit for SEPA is €10,000, monthly €30,000, and yearly €30,000 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:04">[00:11:04]</a>.

### Depositing Cryptocurrencies

Advcash does not charge a commission for depositing cryptocurrencies to the platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:16">[00:12:16]</a>. Commissions are typically applied by the external exchange from which the money is withdrawn <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:21">[00:12:21]</a>.
*   **Process:** Select the cryptocurrency (e.g., Litecoin), click deposit, and then use the provided QR code or wallet address <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:49">[00:12:49]</a>.
*   **Confirmations:** Deposits require a certain number of block confirmations (e.g., six for Litecoin) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:31">[00:12:31]</a>.
*   **Supported Cryptocurrencies:** The platform supports various cryptocurrencies including Dai, USD Coin, XRP, and Bitcoin <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:00">[00:14:00]</a>.
*   **Caution:** Users must ensure accuracy during [[understanding_cryptocurrency_withdrawal_processes | cryptocurrency transfers]] to avoid permanent capital loss <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:31">[00:13:31]</a>.

### Depositing via Other Services
Advcash integrates with numerous third-party payment methods for deposits, such as PayPal, Sberbank, Skrill, and Alipay <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:20">[00:14:20]</a>. Users need to follow specific instructions for each service, which may involve opening a new window for the exchange website <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:44">[00:14:44]</a>.

## Exchanging Currencies within Advcash

Advcash allows users to exchange currencies, for example, from EUR to USD <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:41">[00:15:41]</a>.
*   Users should independently verify exchange rates using external tools (e.g., Google) to identify any hidden commissions, as Advcash does not explicitly display them during the swap <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:38">[00:16:38]</a>.
*   Users can add different currency wallets, such as British Pound, to their account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:52">[00:16:52]</a>.

## Withdrawing Funds

While depositing funds, especially via SEPA, can be inexpensive, withdrawing funds from Advcash using the same methods can be more expensive <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:00">[00:23:00]</a>.

### Sending Funds to Other Advcash Users
Sending money to another Advcash user is instant and incurs no commission <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:47">[00:17:47]</a>. Funds can be sent directly to an email address, even if the recipient does not yet have an Advcash account. A protection code can be set for additional [[advcash_security_and_verification_processes | security]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:17">[00:18:17]</a>.

### Sending Funds to External Cards
Users can send funds to Visa or Mastercard, though not directly to their own Advcash card initially if they haven't ordered one <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:47">[00:18:47]</a>.
*   **Commissions:** A minimum commission of €5 plus 2.5% applies when the amount exceeds €5 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:50">[00:19:50]</a>.
*   **Availability:** This option is available for cards in countries like Russia, Kazakhstan, Ukraine, or general Mastercard in euros <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:15">[00:19:15]</a>.

### Sending Funds to Banks ([[withdrawing_cryptocurrency_funds_to_a_bank_account | Bank Withdrawal]])
Advcash supports [[withdrawing_cryptocurrency_funds_to_a_bank_account | withdrawing funds]] to banks through various methods:
*   **SEPA Transfers (Eurozone):** A commission of €5 plus 0.50% applies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:00">[00:20:00]</a>.
*   **Local Banks:** Available for transfers to many countries outside the Eurozone, such as Argentina, Australia, India, Japan, Nepal, Pakistan, Canada, Colombia, Dominican Republic, and Egypt <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:17">[00:20:17]</a>.
    *   Commissions are generally low, around 0.5% depending on the amount <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:10">[00:20:10]</a>.
    *   Recipient's full details (name, account type) are required <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:21:13">[00:21:13]</a>.

### Withdrawing Cash
Advcash offers cash withdrawal options in various countries where it has collaborations with exchange services, such as Russia and Ukraine <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:21:36">[00:21:36]</a>.
*   **Processing Time:** 15 minutes to 24 hours <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:21:47">[00:21:47]</a>.
*   **Exchange Rate:** Manual <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:21:52">[00:21:52]</a>.
*   **Minimum Amount:** For Russia, the minimum withdrawal is €3,000 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:21:54">[00:21:54]</a>.

### Sending Funds to Other Payment Platforms
Advcash allows sending money to external accounts like PayPal and other listed services <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:19">[00:22:19]</a>. This process involves specific commissions and parameters for each service <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:26">[00:22:26]</a>.

## Advcash Cards

Advcash offers virtual and plastic cards that can be used for payments worldwide <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:58">[00:02:58]</a>.
*   **Creation Cost:** A virtual card costs €9.95 to create <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:27:17">[00:27:17]</a>.
*   **Validity:** Valid for three years <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:27:22">[00:27:22]</a>.
*   **Maintenance:** Free of charge <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:27:22">[00:27:22]</a>.
*   **Usage:** No commission for buying in the card's currency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:27:29">[00:27:29]</a>.
*   **Currency Exchange:** A 2.95% commission applies for currency exchange transactions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:27:32">[00:27:32]</a>.
*   Cards and wallets are separate; users must remember to load their card before use <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:27:01">[00:27:01]</a>.
