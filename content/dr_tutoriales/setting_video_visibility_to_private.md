Setting a video's visibility to private on YouTube allows only selected individuals to view the content <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:10">[00:00:10]</a>. This means the video will not appear on your channel or in YouTube search results <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>.

## How to [[creating_private_videos_on_youtube | create private videos on YouTube]]

To make an uploaded video private:
1.  Access the YouTube platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:25">[00:00:25]</a>.
2.  Navigate to YouTube Channel Studio <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>.
3.  Go to the "Video" section of your channel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.
4.  Choose the video you wish to make private <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>.
5.  In the "Visibility" setting, select "Private" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>.

> [!info] Private Video Access
> Only the individuals you choose will be able to see a private video <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:03">[00:01:03]</a>.

## [[sharing_private_youtube_videos | Sharing Private YouTube Videos]]

Once a video has been set to private, you can share it with specific people:
1.  After setting the video to private, go to its "Details" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:08">[00:01:08]</a>. This is typically accessed via a pen icon next to the video, allowing you to [[editing_video_details_after_uploading_to_youtube | edit video details after uploading to YouTube]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:13">[00:01:13]</a>.
2.  Within the video's details, look for three dots in the top right corner <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:21">[00:01:21]</a>.
3.  Click on these dots and select "Share privately" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:24">[00:01:24]</a>.
4.  A new tab will open, allowing you to "add mailing list" or input email addresses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:26">[00:01:26]</a>.
5.  Enter the email addresses to which you want to send the video, and then click "Send" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:30">[00:01:30]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:56">[00:01:56]</a>.

### Recipient Experience

When you [[emailing_private_video_links | email private video links]], recipients will receive an email from YouTube containing a link to the video <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:38">[00:01:38]</a>. The email will state that the channel invites them to watch a video <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:11">[00:02:11]</a>. By simply clicking the provided link, a new tab will open, allowing them to view the video <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:18">[00:02:18]</a>.
