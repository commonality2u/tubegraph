This article outlines how to [[creating_private_videos_on_youtube | create private videos on YouTube]] and then [[sharing_youtube_videos_privately | share them privately]] with specific individuals via email, ensuring that only desired viewers can access the content <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:06">[00:00:06]</a>. A private video will not appear on the channel or in YouTube search results <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>.

## Setting Video Visibility to Private

To share a video privately, its [[visibility_and_copyright_considerations_on_youtube | visibility]] must first be set to private:
1.  Access the YouTube platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>.
2.  Navigate to [[youtube_studio_video_management | YouTube Studio]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:31">[00:00:31]</a>.
3.  Go to the "video" section of the channel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.
4.  Choose the desired video <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>.
5.  In the "visibility" section, set the video to "private" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>. This ensures only chosen people can see it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:01">[00:01:01]</a>.

## Sharing the Private Video via Email

Once the video is set to private, follow these steps to share it with specific recipients via email:
1.  Go to the video's details page by clicking the "pen" icon next to the video <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:11">[00:01:11]</a>.
2.  On the details page, locate and click the three dots in the top right corner <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:21">[00:01:21]</a>.
3.  Select the "share privately" option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:24">[00:01:24]</a>.
4.  A new tab will open, allowing you to add email addresses to a mailing list <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:26">[00:01:26]</a>. Enter the emails of the people you want to share the video with <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:30">[00:01:30]</a>.
5.  Click "send" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:56">[00:01:56]</a>.

### Recipient Experience

Recipients will receive an email from YouTube containing a link to the private video <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:38">[00:01:38]</a>. The email will state that the channel invites them to watch a video <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:11">[00:02:11]</a>. By clicking the link in the email, they can view the video <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:18">[00:02:18]</a>.
