## How to Insert Online Videos

To add an online video to a PowerPoint slide:
1.  Open your PowerPoint presentation and select the slide where you want to add the video <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17"></a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30"></a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:32"></a>.
2.  Navigate to the 'Insert' tab in the PowerPoint ribbon <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:39"></a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42"></a>.
3.  In the 'Video' section, choose the 'Online Video' option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:44"></a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:47"></a>.
4.  You will need the URL of the online video you wish to insert <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:06"></a>. For example, you can use a YouTube video link <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56"></a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:58"></a>.
5.  Paste the URL into the designated field and click 'Insert' <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:11"></a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:14"></a>.

The video will then be embedded and ready for direct viewing within your presentation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:20"></a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:22"></a>.

## Important Considerations for Online Videos

> [!warning] Internet Connection Required
> For an online video (e.g., a YouTube video) to be viewed during your presentation, an active internet connection is essential <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:25"></a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:27"></a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:29"></a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31"></a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:33"></a>. If there is no Wi-Fi or internet access, the video will not play <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:19"></a>.

This differs from [[adding_videos_from_a_computer_to_powerpoint | a video from a computer]], which can be viewed at any time without needing an internet connection <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:39"></a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:40"></a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:13"></a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:14"></a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:17"></a>.
