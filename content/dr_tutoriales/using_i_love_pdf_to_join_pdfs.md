This guide explains how to [[merging_pdf_files | merge PDF]] documents using the free online platform, [[online_pdf_editing_platforms_like_i_love_pdf | I Love PDF]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>. This process does not require any external programs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>.

## Step-by-Step Guide

### 1. Open Google Chrome
Begin by opening Google Chrome on your computer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:14">[00:00:14]</a>.

### 2. Access [[online_pdf_editing_platforms_like_i_love_pdf | I Love PDF]]
Navigate to the [[online_pdf_editing_platforms_like_i_love_pdf | I Love PDF]] website <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>. Once there, look for and select the "[[merging_pdf_files | Merge PDF]]" or "Join PDF" option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>.

### 3. [[selecting_and_uploading_pdfs_for_merging | Select Your PDFs]]
Click to [[selecting_and_uploading_pdfs_for_merging | select the PDFs]] you wish to merge <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:14">[00:01:14]</a>.
*   You will need to go to the location where your PDFs are stored <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:22">[00:01:22]</a>.
*   Choose both documents you intend to combine, such as a "Safari" ticket and a "Multicinema" ticket <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:22">[00:01:22]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:29">[00:01:29]</a>.
*   Click "Open" to upload them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:29">[00:01:29]</a>. The platform will indicate once the PDFs have loaded <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:38">[00:01:38]</a>.

### 4. Initiate the [[merging_pdf_files | Merge]]
With your PDFs uploaded, click the "[[merging_pdf_files | Merge PDF]]" or "Join PDF" button <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:38">[00:01:38]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:43">[00:01:43]</a>.

### 5. [[downloading_and_renaming_merged_pdf | Download and Rename]]
Once the process is complete, you can [[downloading_and_renaming_merged_pdf | download the merged PDF]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:43">[00:01:43]</a>. The merged document will appear as a single PDF <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:50">[00:01:50]</a>.
*   The downloaded file will typically be found in your "Downloads" section and might be named something like "Multicinema Safari Merge" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:55">[00:01:55]</a>.
*   You have the option to [[downloading_and_renaming_merged_pdf | edit the name]] of the merged file as desired <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:58">[00:01:58]</a>.
*   Upon opening the merged PDF, both original documents, such as the two invoices, will be combined within it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:00">[00:02:00]</a>.
