If you decide you no longer want the TikTok application on your computer, you can easily uninstall it through the Control Panel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>.

## Steps to Uninstall

To remove TikTok from your PC, follow these steps:
1.  Navigate to the Control Panel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:38">[00:01:38]</a>. You can do this by searching for "Control" in your Windows search bar <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:34">[00:01:34]</a>.
2.  Within the Control Panel, locate the option to "Uninstall a program" or "Programs and Features" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:41">[00:01:41]</a>.
3.  In the list of installed programs, find "TikTok" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:44">[00:01:44]</a>. The list is typically in alphabetical order, so you can find it under 'T' <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:44">[00:01:44]</a>.
4.  Once located, select TikTok and proceed with the uninstallation process <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:47">[00:01:47]</a>.
