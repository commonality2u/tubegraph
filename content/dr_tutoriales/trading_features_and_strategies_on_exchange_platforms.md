## Trading Commissions

[[exploring_trading_markets_for_cryptocurrency_conversion | Trading]] commissions are crucial for active users.

### [[using_binance_exchange_for_transactions | Binance]]
[[using_binance_exchange_for_transactions | Binance]] charges a base commission of 0.1% <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:41:00">[06:41:00]</a>. This fee can be significantly reduced by holding [[binance_trading_tips | Binance Coin (BNB)]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:47:00">[06:47:00]</a>. Users can reduce fees by paying them in [[binance_trading_tips | BNB]], leading to a 25% discount, bringing the rate down to approximately 0.075% <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:28:00">[07:28:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:31:00">[07:31:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:43:00">[07:43:00]</a>. For high-volume traders (e.g., over 50 [[binance_trading_tips | BNB]] held), [[exchange_platform_fees_and_commissions | commissions]] can drop to as low as 0.02% <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:52:00">[06:52:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:58:00">[06:58:00]</a>.

**Maker/Taker Fees:** [[using_binance_exchange_for_transactions | Binance]] differentiates between Maker and Taker orders <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:01:00">[08:01:00]</a>.
*   **Taker Orders**: These are [[market_orders_in_trading | market orders]] that execute immediately at the current market price <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:09:00">[08:09:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:11:00">[08:11:00]</a>.
*   **Maker Orders**: These are limit orders that are not executed immediately but await a specific price, adding liquidity to the order book <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:13:00">[08:13:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:15:00">[08:15:00]</a>.

### Coinbase
Coinbase has fixed commissions for trading, ranging from a maximum of 3 euros to a minimum of 0.99 euros. These fees also depend on the amount of Bitcoin or capital held on the platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:34:00">[08:34:00]</a>.

Coinbase Pro's [[exchange_platform_fees_and_commissions | trading commissions]] range from 0.50% down to 0.04% <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:46:00">[08:46:00]</a>.
