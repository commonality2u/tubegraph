[[looping_videos_and_music_on_youtube | Looping videos]] on the YouTube platform allows them to automatically restart once they finish, eliminating the need to manually hit replay <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:07">[00:00:07]</a>. This feature enables users to continuously listen to music or watch videos without any manual intervention <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>.

## How to Loop YouTube Videos

The process of [[how_to_put_youtube_videos_on_loop | putting YouTube videos on loop]] can be done using a browser like [[using_google_chrome_to_loop_youtube_videos | Google Chrome]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:36">[00:00:36]</a>.

### Activating Loop Mode

To [[steps_to_activate_and_deactivate_loop_mode_on_youtube | activate loop mode]]:
1.  Right-click on the video frame while the video is playing <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>.
2.  A dropdown menu will appear <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>.
3.  Click on the "Loop" option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:13">[00:01:13]</a>.
    *   Once activated, the video will automatically start again when it reaches its end <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a>.

### Deactivating Loop Mode

To [[steps_to_activate_and_deactivate_loop_mode_on_youtube | deactivate loop mode]]:
1.  Right-click on the video screen again <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:45">[00:01:45]</a>.
2.  The dropdown menu will reappear <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:47">[00:01:47]</a>.
3.  Click on "Loop" once more <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:49">[00:01:49]</a>.
    *   With loop mode deactivated, the video will end and pause playback once it finishes, rather than restarting <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:57">[00:01:57]</a>.
