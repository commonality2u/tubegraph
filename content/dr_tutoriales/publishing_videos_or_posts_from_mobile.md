This tutorial explains how to create posts on YouTube using a mobile device, applicable for both Android and iPhone users <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:05">[00:00:05]</a>.

## Prerequisites

To begin, ensure you have the YouTube platform downloaded on your mobile device <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:29">[00:00:29]</a>. This can be done via the App Store for iOS devices or Google Play for Android devices <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:34">[00:00:34]</a>.

## [[creating_youtube_posts_on_mobile | Steps to Create YouTube Posts on Mobile]]

Follow these [[steps_to_create_simple_youtube_posts | steps to create a simple YouTube post]]:

1.  **Access the YouTube App**
    *   Open the YouTube application on your mobile device <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:45">[00:00:45]</a>.
2.  **Log In to Your Account**
    *   Register or log in with your official YouTube account from which you wish to publish <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>.
3.  **Initiate a New Post**
    *   Navigate to the video camera icon located at the top right of the screen <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:05">[00:01:05]</a>.
    *   From the options presented (make a video, a live, or a publication), select "publication" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>.
4.  **Compose Your Post**
    *   Input your desired text for the post, for example, "How are you? Have a nice day" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:28">[00:01:28]</a>.
    *   You can also [[adding_photos_and_surveys_to_youtube_posts | add photos or surveys]] from your mobile phone using the options provided at the bottom of the screen <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:40">[00:01:40]</a>.
5.  **Publish the Post**
    *   Once your post is ready, tap "publish" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>.

## Post Visibility

After publishing, the post will be created <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:54">[00:01:54]</a> and will be visible to your followers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:02">[00:02:02]</a>.
