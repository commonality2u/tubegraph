## Deposit Methods on Binance

To deposit funds into [[how_to_transfer_cryptocurrencies_on_binance | Binance]], navigate to your wallet, then "Overview," and select "Deposit" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:51">[00:01:51]</a>. There are two primary options:

1.  [[transferring_cryptocurrency_to_fiat_currency | Deposit Fiat]] currency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:03">[00:02:03]</a>
2.  [[how_to_transfer_cryptocurrencies_on_binance | Deposit Cryptocurrency]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:03">[00:02:03]</a>

### Direct Fiat Deposits

[[transferring_cryptocurrency_to_fiat_currency | Fiat deposits]] involve national currencies such as the Euro, Dollar, Pound Sterling, Rubles, or Czech Crowns <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:14">[00:02:14]</a>. The available methods and associated commissions can vary depending on the chosen currency and the current market situation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:26">[00:02:26]</a>:

*   **Euro**:
    *   Visa/MasterCard bank card: 1.8% commission <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:45">[00:02:45]</a>.
    *   P2P bank transfer: 0% commission <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:51">[00:02:51]</a>.
    *   Wallet Make Cash: 0% commission, though additional fees may apply <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:54">[00:02:54]</a>.
*   **Pound Sterling**:
    *   Bank card: 1.8% commission <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:04">[00:03:04]</a>.
    *   Quick Payments: Currently suspended <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:07">[00:03:07]</a>.
    *   Suspensions of SEPA transfers and rapid payments have occurred in some European countries due to ongoing analyses of transfers and legality <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:09">[00:03:09]</a>. Banks and platforms have temporarily banned transfers to [[how_to_transfer_cryptocurrencies_on_binance | Binance]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:25">[00:03:25]</a>.
*   **Dollar**:
    *   Bank transfer SWIFT: 0% commission (this option was recently enabled) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:51">[00:03:51]</a>.

Direct [[transferring_cryptocurrency_to_fiat_currency | fiat deposits]] can often incur high commission costs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:23">[00:04:23]</a>.

## Transferring Cryptocurrencies to Save on Fees

An alternative to direct [[transferring_cryptocurrency_to_fiat_currency | fiat deposits]] is to [[transferring_cryptocurrency_between_platforms | deposit cryptocurrencies]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:27">[00:04:27]</a>. This method involves depositing fiat currency into a different platform, [[cryptocurrency_conversion_for_lower_fees | converting it to a cryptocurrency with low transfer fees]], and then sending that cryptocurrency to [[how_to_transfer_cryptocurrencies_on_binance | Binance]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:28">[00:05:28]</a>.

### Process for Economical Transfers

1.  **Choose an External Platform**: Select an external platform where [[transferring_cryptocurrency_to_fiat_currency | fiat deposits]] are cheaper or have 0% commission <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:39">[00:07:39]</a>.
    *   **[[using_different_platforms_like_kraken_and_crypto_dotcom_for_better_exchange_rates | Kraken]]**: Allows SEPA deposits for Euros, with funds typically available in one or two business days <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:17">[00:06:17]</a>. [[using_different_platforms_like_kraken_and_crypto_dotcom_for_better_exchange_rates | Kraken]] is considered a very good option for this type of transfer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:59">[00:11:59]</a>.
    *   **[[trading_and_transferring_cryptocurrencies_on_kucoin | KuCoin]]**: Another viable platform for deposits and transfers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:06">[00:12:06]</a>. Trading fees on [[trading_and_transferring_cryptocurrencies_on_kucoin | KuCoin]] can be as low as 0.1% <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:11">[00:11:11]</a>.
    *   **Crypto.com**: Considered one of the safest platforms, though it may have higher trading commission costs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:17">[00:12:17]</a>.
    *   **Coinbase**: Deposits via SEPA transfers can be very cheap, around 0.50-0.75 euros <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:26">[00:12:26]</a>.
2.  **[[cryptocurrency_conversion_for_lower_fees | Convert Fiat to Cryptocurrency]]**: Once fiat currency is deposited, [[cryptocurrency_conversion_for_lower_fees | convert it into a cryptocurrency with low transfer costs]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:51">[00:07:51]</a>.
    *   **Conversion Fees**: Converting Euros to a cryptocurrency can incur a commission of approximately 0.16% <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:22">[00:08:22]</a>. For a 2,000 Euro deposit, this would be about 3.2 Euros <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:32">[00:08:32]</a>.
    *   **Choosing Cryptocurrencies**: Look for cryptocurrencies with low withdrawal fees <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:04">[00:09:04]</a>.
        *   **Litecoin (LTC)**: A favorite for transfers due to low fees <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:18">[00:09:18]</a>. Withdrawal cost on [[using_different_platforms_like_kraken_and_crypto_dotcom_for_better_exchange_rates | Kraken]] is 0.02 LTC <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:29">[00:09:29]</a>.
        *   **Tron (TRX)**: Also has very low transfer fees <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:24">[00:09:24]</a>. Sending Tron to [[how_to_transfer_cryptocurrencies_on_binance | Binance]] from [[using_different_platforms_like_kraken_and_crypto_dotcom_for_better_exchange_rates | Kraken]] can cost as little as 10 cents USD <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:58">[00:09:58]</a>.
3.  **[[transferring_cryptocurrency_between_platforms | Transfer Cryptocurrency to Binance]]**: Send the chosen cryptocurrency from the external platform to [[how_to_transfer_cryptocurrencies_on_binance | Binance]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:07">[00:06:07]</a>. These [[transferring_cryptocurrency_between_platforms | crypto transfers]] have varying costs depending on the blockchain network used <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:15">[00:05:15]</a>.

By using this method, the total cost for a deposit, for example, from [[using_different_platforms_like_kraken_and_crypto_dotcom_for_better_exchange_rates | Kraken]] to [[how_to_transfer_cryptocurrencies_on_binance | Binance]], could be around 3.3 Euros (3.2 Euros for conversion + 0.10 Euros for transfer) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:12">[00:10:12]</a>. This contrasts with potentially higher direct fiat deposit fees on [[how_to_transfer_cryptocurrencies_on_binance | Binance]].

It is crucial to consider all commissions and make informed decisions to save money on transfers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:51">[00:11:51]</a>.
