This tutorial provides step-by-step instructions for getting YouTube Music on your computer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:00">[00:00:00]</a>. If you have any doubts, you can leave them in the comments for quick resolution <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:05">[00:00:05]</a>.

## Prerequisites

To begin, you will need a search engine such as Edge, Firefox, Opera, or Google Chrome <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>. The tutorial uses Google Chrome <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:16">[00:00:16]</a>.

## Installing YouTube Music

1.  Open your chosen search engine and a new tab <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:19">[00:00:19]</a>.
2.  Navigate to the YouTube Music website <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>. You can directly type "YouTube Music" into the search bar <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:24">[00:00:24]</a>. The correct link will typically appear first in the search results <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>. The official link will also be provided in the first comment of the tutorial video for easy access <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:31">[00:00:31]</a>.
3.  Once on the YouTube Music website, ensure your Google account is recognized and logged in <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:35">[00:00:35]</a>.
4.  Look for a small computer symbol with a downward arrow in the top right corner of the browser window <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>. This symbol indicates the option to [[installing_youtube_music_on_windows | install YouTube Music]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:47">[00:00:47]</a>.
5.  Click on this symbol and then select "Install" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>.
6.  The YouTube Music application will then be downloaded and installed directly onto your computer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>.

## Finding and Accessing the Installed App

After installation, the YouTube Music application will be available on your computer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>.

*   A [[creating_a_desktop_shortcut_for_youtube_music | shortcut]] will be automatically created on your desktop <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>.
*   You can find the app by searching for "music" in your computer's search bar, which will show "YouTube Music" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:11">[00:01:11]</a>.
*   You can then add it to your taskbar by right-clicking and selecting "Pin to taskbar" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:14">[00:01:14]</a>.

## Uninstalling YouTube Music

If you wish to [[uninstalling_youtube_music_from_windows | uninstall YouTube Music]] from your computer:

1.  Go to the Control Panel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:36">[00:01:36]</a>. You can search for "control panel" to access it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:39">[00:01:39]</a>.
2.  Within the Control Panel, find the "Uninstall a program" option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:42">[00:01:42]</a>.
3.  Locate "YouTube Music" in the list of programs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:45">[00:01:45]</a>.
4.  Right-click on "YouTube Music" and select "Uninstall" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:47">[00:01:47]</a>.
