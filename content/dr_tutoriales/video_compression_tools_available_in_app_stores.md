This article provides a [[step_by_step_video_compression_tutorial | tutorial]] on how to [[how_to_compress_videos_for_WhatsApp | compress videos for WhatsApp]] using mobile applications available in app stores <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:11">[00:00:11]</a>. WhatsApp has a capacity limit for sending videos, making [[video_compression | video compression]] necessary <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:19">[00:00:19]</a>.

### Finding a Video Compressor App
To begin, users need to download a [[video_compression | video compression]] application from their device's app store <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:47">[00:00:47]</a>.

1.  **Search the App Store**: Open the App Store and search for "video compressor" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:47">[00:00:47]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>.
2.  **Select an App**: While many options are available and generally useful, the tutorial recommends a specific app that offers less advertising and fewer payment requirements <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:04">[00:01:04]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>. Download the chosen application <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:18">[00:01:18]</a>.

### How to Compress a Video
Once the [[video_compression_tools_available_in_app_stores | video compressor app]] is installed, follow these steps to [[video_compression | compress a video]]:

1.  **Open the Application**: Launch the [[video_compression_tools_available_in_app_stores | video compressor application]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:27">[00:01:27]</a>.
2.  **Select Video for Compression**: Choose the "compress a video" option within the app <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>. Select a video from your device's gallery <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:36">[00:01:36]</a>. For example, a 3-minute video that is 34.6 megabytes can be selected <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:43">[00:01:43]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:45">[00:01:45]</a>.
3.  **Adjust Compression Settings**:
    *   The app will display compression indices, allowing you to determine the desired compression level <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:53">[00:01:53]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:57">[00:01:57]</a>.
    *   The percentage displayed indicates the compressed video's size relative to the original <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:02">[00:02:02]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:04">[00:02:04]</a>. A lower percentage indicates greater compression <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:08">[00:02:08]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:09">[00:02:09]</a>.
    *   For instance, compressing a 34.6 megabyte video at 85% would reduce it to 29.4 megabytes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:12">[00:02:12]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:16">[00:02:16]</a>.
    *   The goal is to reduce the video size while [[maintaining_video_quality_while_compressing | maintaining quality limits]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:44">[00:02:44]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:50">[00:02:50]</a>. A 70% compression setting is suggested, which can result in a 10-megabyte reduction for the example video, still maintaining good quality <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:52">[00:02:52]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:55">[00:02:55]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:57">[00:02:57]</a>.
    *   Some apps offer "Normal" (automatic rate reduction) and "Advanced" (manual control) modes for adjusting compression <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:31">[00:02:31]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:32">[00:02:32]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:36">[00:02:36]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:38">[00:02:38]</a>.
4.  **Initiate Compression**: Click the "compress" button <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:59">[00:02:59]</a>. The [[video_conversion_process | compression process]] typically takes about 20 to 30 seconds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:04">[00:03:04]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:07">[00:03:07]</a>.
5.  **Save Compressed Video**: Once compressed, save the video to your device's gallery <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:32">[00:03:32]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:36">[00:03:36]</a>. The compressed video will then appear in your gallery alongside the original <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:51">[00:03:51]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:55">[00:03:55]</a>.
