This tutorial outlines how to [[creating_private_videos_on_youtube | create private videos on YouTube]] and [[emailing_private_youtube_video_links | share them privately]] so that only specific individuals can view them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:06">[00:00:06]</a>. A private video will not appear on your channel or in YouTube search results <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:54">[00:00:54]</a>.

## Setting a Video to Private

### Accessing YouTube Studio

1.  Access the YouTube platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:25">[00:00:25]</a>.
2.  Navigate to [[youtube_studio_video_management | YouTube Channel Studio]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:31">[00:00:31]</a>.
3.  Go to the "Video" section of the channel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.

### Changing Video [[visibility_and_copyright_considerations_on_youtube | Visibility]]

1.  Choose the video you wish to make private from your video list <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>.
2.  In the [[visibility_and_copyright_considerations_on_youtube | visibility]] section, set the video's status to "private" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>.

## Sharing a Private Video

### Accessing Sharing Options

1.  Once the video is set to private, access its details <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:08">[00:01:08]</a>. This can typically be done by clicking the pen icon next to the video <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:12">[00:01:12]</a>.
2.  In the video's details, locate and click the three dots in the top right corner <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:21">[00:01:21]</a>.
3.  Select the "share privately" option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:24">[00:01:24]</a>.

### Adding Recipients

1.  A new tab will open, allowing you to add emails to a mailing list <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:26">[00:01:26]</a>.
2.  Enter the email addresses of the people you want to share the video with <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:30">[00:01:30]</a>.
3.  Once the emails are added, send the invitation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:56">[00:01:56]</a>.

### Recipient Experience

*   Recipients will receive an email from YouTube containing a link to the video <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:38">[00:01:38]</a>.
*   The email will indicate that your channel invites them to watch a video <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:11">[00:02:11]</a>.
*   Clicking the provided link will open a new tab, allowing them to view the private video <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:20">[00:02:20]</a>.
