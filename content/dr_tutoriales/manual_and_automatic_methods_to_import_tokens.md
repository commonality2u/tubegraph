This article outlines the process of adding the USDT token to your [[how_to_add_usdt_token_to_metamask | MetaMask]] wallet, detailing both manual and automatic import methods <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:04">[00:00:04]</a>. Having the token available in your wallet allows you to use it, [[methods_to_convert_cryptocurrencies | convert cryptocurrencies]] to USDT, and manage it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:10">[00:00:10]</a>.

## Prerequisites

Before importing a token, ensure you are logged into your [[how_to_add_usdt_token_to_metamask | MetaMask]] wallet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>. The process for adding tokens is the same whether you are using [[how_to_add_usdt_token_to_metamask | MetaMask]] on a computer or a mobile phone <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:40">[00:00:40]</a>.

To begin, you will need to access a web page that lists cryptocurrencies and their associated contracts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:15">[00:01:15]</a>. This link is usually provided in the video's first comment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:12">[00:01:12]</a>. On this platform, search for the "USDT" token <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:22">[00:01:22]</a>.

## Methods to Import USDT Token

There are two primary ways to add the USDT token to your [[how_to_add_usdt_token_to_metamask | MetaMask]] account:

### 1. Manual Method

The manual method involves directly inputting the token's contract address into your [[how_to_add_usdt_token_to_metamask | MetaMask]] wallet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:36">[00:01:36]</a>.

1.  Open your [[how_to_add_usdt_token_to_metamask | MetaMask]] extension or application <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:39">[00:01:39]</a>.
2.  Navigate to the "Import tokens" or "Add custom token" section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:49">[00:01:49]</a>.
3.  On the external platform where you searched for USDT, locate the contract address for USDT. Click to copy this address <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:57">[00:01:57]</a>.
4.  Paste the copied contract address into the designated "Token Contract Address" field in [[how_to_add_usdt_token_to_metamask | MetaMask]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:51">[00:01:51]</a>.
5.  [[how_to_add_usdt_token_to_metamask | MetaMask]] should automatically populate the "Token Symbol" (e.g., USDT) and "Token Decimal" fields <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:03">[00:02:03]</a>.
6.  Click "Add Custom Token" and then "Import Tokens" to finalize the process <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:08">[00:02:08]</a>. The USDT token will now appear in your wallet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:08">[00:02:08]</a>.

### 2. Automatic Method (Using the MetaMask Fox Icon)

An even simpler method is to use the integrated [[how_to_add_usdt_token_to_metamask | MetaMask]] icon found next to the token contract on the external platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:29">[00:01:29]</a>.

1.  On the external platform displaying the USDT contract, look for a small [[how_to_add_usdt_token_to_metamask | MetaMask]] fox icon <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:29">[00:01:29]</a>.
2.  Click on this "little fox" icon <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:12">[00:02:12]</a>.
3.  A [[how_to_add_usdt_token_to_metamask | MetaMask]] notification tab will open, prompting you to confirm adding the token <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:15">[00:02:15]</a>.
4.  Once the confirmation loads, you can add the token to your [[how_to_add_usdt_token_to_metamask | MetaMask]] platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:19">[00:02:19]</a>.
5.  Refresh your [[how_to_add_usdt_token_to_metamask | MetaMask]] page, and the USDT token will appear in your list <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:24">[00:02:24]</a>.

Once the USDT token is listed, you can effectively use it, [[methods_to_convert_cryptocurrencies | convert other cryptocurrencies]] into it, or withdraw it as needed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:28">[00:02:28]</a>.
