When withdrawing profits from cryptocurrency holdings to a bank account, it is essential to declare these earnings. A benefit statement must be prepared, and taxes are required to be declared in the country where you reside <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>.

> [!INFO] Tax Declaration
> When you withdraw money to your bank account, you will need to prepare a benefit statement and declare taxes in your country of residence <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:33">[00:00:33]</a>.
