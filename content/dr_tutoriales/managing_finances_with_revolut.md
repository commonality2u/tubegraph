The app functions like a conventional bank for sending money <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:51">[00:05:51]</a>.
*   **Sending Money**: Users can send money to contacts or new recipients <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:27">[00:05:27]</a>.
*   **Payment Links**: The app allows the creation of payment links for others to use by simply entering an amount <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:37">[00:05:37]</a>.
*   **Bank Consignees**: Users can add bank consignees by providing account numbers, regardless of the country (e.g., English, Spanish) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:42">[00:05:42]</a>.
*   **Instant Transfers**: Transfers can be made immediately, unlike traditional banks that might take longer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:58">[00:05:58]</a>.
*   **Transaction History**: A detailed history of transfers and transactions is available <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:06">[00:06:06]</a>.
