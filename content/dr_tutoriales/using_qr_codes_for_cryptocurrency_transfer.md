This article outlines the process of [[transferring cryptocurrencies between platforms | transferring cryptocurrencies]], specifically Bitcoin, from a Coinbase account to a Binance account, leveraging both [[mobile_and_computerbased_cryptocurrency_transactions | mobile and computer-based methods]] including the use of QR codes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:06">[00:00:06]</a>. ## Prerequisites

To follow this guide, you should have:
*   An account with Coinbase <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>.
*   An account with Binance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:18">[00:01:18]</a>.

## Obtaining the Binance Deposit Address

The first step in [[how_to_transfer_cryptocurrencies_on_binance | transferring cryptocurrencies on Binance]] is to get the deposit address for the cryptocurrency you wish to receive <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:42">[00:01:42]</a>.

1.  **Navigate to Wallet Overview**: On Binance, go to "Wallet" and then "Overview" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:51">[00:01:51]</a>.
2.  **Select Deposit**: Once the page loads, choose the "Deposit" option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:55">[00:01:55]</a>.
3.  **Choose Crypto Deposit**: Click on "Deposit Crypto" rather than "Deposit Fiat" (local currency like euros) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:01">[00:02:01]</a>.
4.  **Select Cryptocurrency**: Choose the specific cryptocurrency you intend to deposit. For instance, if you have Bitcoin on Coinbase, select Bitcoin here <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:26">[00:02:26]</a>. The QR code and address displayed will change based on the selected cryptocurrency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:37">[00:02:37]</a>.
5.  **Locate QR Code and Address**: You will see a QR code and a corresponding wallet address. Pay close attention to both, as they are crucial for making the deposit <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:44">[00:02:44]</a>.

## [[sending_receiving_and_swapping_cryptocurrencies | Sending Cryptocurrency]] from Coinbase

There are two primary methods to [[sending_receiving_and_swapping_cryptocurrencies | send cryptocurrencies]] from Coinbase to the Binance deposit address: via mobile phone using a QR code, or via computer by copying the address.

### Via Mobile Phone (Using QR Code)

1.  **Access Coinbase App**: Open the Coinbase application on your mobile phone <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:08">[00:03:08]</a>.
2.  **Select Cryptocurrency**: Navigate to the wallet of the specific cryptocurrency you wish to transfer (e.g., Bitcoin wallet) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:14">[00:03:14]</a>.
3.  **Initiate Send**: Look for a "paper plane" symbol or an arrow, which typically signifies the send option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:10">[00:04:10]</a>.
4.  **Scan QR Code**: Tap the QR code symbol, usually located on the top right <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:42">[00:03:42]</a>. This will activate your camera, allowing you to scan the Binance deposit QR code displayed on your computer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:44">[00:03:44]</a>.
5.  **Enter Amount**: Input the amount of Bitcoin (or other cryptocurrency) you wish to send <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:15">[00:04:15]</a>.
6.  **Confirm**: Proceed with the transfer.

### Via Computer (Copying Address)

1.  **Copy Binance Address**: On your computer, copy the Binance deposit address that appeared next to the QR code <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:38">[00:03:38]</a>.
2.  **Go to Coinbase**: Open your Coinbase account in your browser <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:08">[00:01:08]</a>.
3.  **Initiate Send/Receive**: Click on "Send and Receive" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:28">[00:01:28]</a>.
4.  **Choose Amount**: Select the amount of Bitcoin you want to transfer from Coinbase <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:45">[00:04:45]</a>.
5.  **Paste Address**: Paste the copied Binance wallet address into the recipient field <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:53">[00:04:53]</a>.
6.  **Add Note (Optional)**: You can add a personal note to the transaction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:59">[00:04:59]</a>.
7.  **Review and Confirm**: The system will display the details, including any commission fees (e.g., approximately a euro for a ~â‚¬110 transfer) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:16">[00:05:16]</a>. Confirm the transfer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:05">[00:05:05]</a>.
8.  **Verification Code**: Enter the verification code received on your mobile phone to complete the transaction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:30">[00:05:30]</a>.

## Transaction Confirmation and Management

After confirming the transfer from Coinbase, your balance will disappear from your Coinbase wallet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:38">[00:05:38]</a>. The transaction is expected to take approximately 10 minutes to process <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:45">[00:05:45]</a>.

To confirm the receipt of funds in Binance:
1.  **Check Binance History**: Go to the transaction history within your Binance account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:11">[00:06:11]</a>.
2.  **Refresh**: Refresh the page to see the latest transactions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:14">[00:06:14]</a>. A deposit from the Coinbase address should be listed, showing the amount of Bitcoin transferred <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:18">[00:06:18]</a>.
3.  **Fiat and Spot Wallet**: The transferred funds will appear in your Fiat and Spot wallet on Binance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:25">[00:06:25]</a>.
4.  **Email Confirmation**: You will also receive an email confirmation of the completed transaction from Coinbase to Binance, which can serve as collateral in case of any issues <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:47">[00:06:47]</a>.

Once the funds are in Binance, you can [[transferring_and_managing_cryptocurrency_assets | manage and use them]] within that platform as desired <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:39">[00:06:39]</a>.
