## Revolut Features Overview

Revolut offers various features designed to help users [[managing_finances_with_revolut | manage their money]] and make transactions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:11">[00:01:11]</a>.

### Financial Management & Analysis
Revolut allows users to manage their money through expense analysis, categorizing spending by types such as transportation, restaurants, or shopping <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:23">[00:02:23]</a>. This feature helps users understand their spending habits and control expenses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:33">[00:02:33]</a>. The platform also offers various discounts, which may vary depending on the chosen plan <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:43">[00:02:43]</a>.

### International Money Transfers
Revolut is well-known for its [[international_money_transfers_with_revolut | international money transfers]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>. Users can send money worldwide instantly, with what Revolut describes as "no hidden commissions" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:00">[00:03:00]</a>.

*   **Commission Structure**: The first 1,000 euros (or equivalent in another currency) sent in another currency to another bank account in a different country typically incurs no commission with Revolut <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:27">[00:03:27]</a>.
*   **Thresholds**: If the amount exceeds 1,000 euros, a commission may be charged for [[revolut_card_features | Standard accounts]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:39">[00:03:39]</a>. However, [[revolut_card_features | Premium]] and Metal account holders are generally not charged for transfers exceeding this amount <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:41">[00:03:41]</a>.
*   **Payments Abroad**: Paying like a local and getting the real exchange rate for spending abroad in over 150 currencies without additional charges is a key benefit <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:38">[00:06:38]</a>.

### Saving and Investing
Revolut offers features to help users achieve financial goals through "personal vaults" or "trunks" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:01">[00:05:01]</a>. This allows users to set specific savings goals (e.g., for travel) and contribute money regularly until the goal is met, promoting controlled saving and efficient money management <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:08">[00:05:08]</a>.

The platform also provides the option to [[investing_and_trading_with_revolut | buy cryptocurrencies and gold]] within seconds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:39">[00:05:39]</a>.

### Security
Revolut is recognized for its strong security measures, being among the top banks for preventing fraud <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:21">[00:06:21]</a>. Its anti-fraud systems are award-winning, keeping fraudulent transactions below 0.01 percent <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:26">[00:06:26]</a>.

## Account Plans
Revolut offers several plans, each with different features and benefits <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:49">[00:02:49]</a>:
*   **Standard**: Free <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:53">[00:06:53]</a>. Includes one free international transfer per month, with subsequent transfers costing 0.50 pounds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:51">[00:13:51]</a>.
*   **Plus**: Three euros <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:54">[00:06:54]</a>. Includes one free international transfer per month, after which transfers cost 0.50 pounds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:42">[00:13:42]</a>. Offers interbank changes with interest on savings, potentially up to 0.40% annual interest <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:21">[00:14:21]</a>.
*   **Premium**: Eight euros <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:55">[00:06:55]</a>.
*   **Metal**: Fourteen euros <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:56">[00:06:56]</a>. Offers daily cashback (0.01% within Europe, 1% outside Europe), unlimited international transfers without commissions, and travel protection/insurance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:35">[00:12:35]</a>. Investment interest can be up to 0.65% annual interest, paid daily <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:31">[00:14:31]</a>.

It is recommended to start with a [[revolut_card_features | Standard account]] and then consider upgrading based on financial objectives and situation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:07">[00:07:07]</a>.

## Creating a Revolut Account Step-by-Step

### Initial Registration and Benefits
To [[creating_a_revolut_account | create a Revolut account]], it is recommended to use a referral link provided in the first comment of the video <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>. Using this link provides a benefit, such as a 10-euro reward upon creating the account and making the first payment with the Revolut card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:41">[00:07:41]</a>.

1.  **Access the Link**: Click on the provided referral link <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:10">[00:08:10]</a>.
2.  **Enter Phone Number**: Input your mobile phone number on the Revolut website <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:22">[00:08:22]</a>. Revolut is largely managed through its mobile application <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:27">[00:08:27]</a>.
3.  **Register**: Click "Register" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:41">[00:08:41]</a>. Revolut will send an SMS with a link to download the application <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:50">[00:08:50]</a>.

### Mobile Application Setup
The primary way to manage a Revolut account is through the mobile application <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:54">[00:08:54]</a>.

1.  **Download/Update App**: Open the SMS link to download or update the Revolut app <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:16">[00:09:16]</a>.
2.  **Open App**: Once downloaded/updated, open the Revolut application <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:35">[00:09:35]</a>.
3.  **Register in App**: Select "Register" within the app <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:44">[00:09:44]</a>.
4.  **Choose Country**: Select your country of residence (e.g., United Kingdom, Spain) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:51">[00:09:51]</a>.
5.  **Enter Phone Number**: Input your phone number again <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:57">[00:09:57]</a>.
6.  **Verification Code**: Enter the code sent to your phone number <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:05">[00:10:05]</a>.
7.  **Postal Code**: Enter the postal code of your house <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:18">[00:10:18]</a>.
8.  **Personal Details**: Provide your legal first name, legal last name, and date of birth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:30">[00:10:30]</a>.
9.  **Email**: Enter your email address <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:47">[00:10:47]</a>.
10. **Selfie Verification**: Take a clear selfie for identity verification, ensuring good lighting and no obstructions on your face (like glasses or hats) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:51">[00:10:51]</a>.
11. **Contacts Access**: Allow Revolut access to your contacts to facilitate sending money <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:06">[00:11:06]</a>.
12. **Main Reason for Revolut**: Indicate your primary reason for using Revolut (e.g., sending money) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:17">[00:11:17]</a>.
13. **Access Code**: Set a Revolut access code for security <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:27">[00:11:27]</a>. You may also be prompted to enable face ID for future access <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:37">[00:11:37]</a>.
14. **Notifications**: Allow notifications from the app <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:42">[00:11:42]</a>.
15. **Activity Tracking**: Decide whether to allow activity tracking to personalize your experience and monitor logins from other devices <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:47">[00:11:47]</a>.
16. **Nationality and Document Scan**: Confirm your nationality and scan your national identity document <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:05">[00:12:05]</a>. Revolut will then verify your identity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:21">[00:12:21]</a>.

### Account Activation and Card
Once identity verification is complete, loading restrictions are lifted <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:42">[00:12:42]</a>.

1.  **Choose Plan**: Select your desired account plan. The [[revolut_card_features | Standard plan]] is recommended for initial setup <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:58">[00:13:58]</a>.
2.  **Virtual Debit Card**: Your virtual debit card will be ready for use, allowing you to start making secure payments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:44">[00:14:44]</a>. You can add it to your phone's payment app <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:51">[00:14:51]</a>.
3.  **Physical Card (Optional)**: You have the option to order a physical [[revolut_card_features | Revolut card]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:26">[00:15:26]</a>.
4.  **Add Money**: You can then add money to your new Revolut account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:30">[00:15:30]</a>.

Once these steps are completed, your Revolut account is verified and ready for use <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:37">[00:15:37]</a>. Further tutorials will delve into using the [[introduction_to_revolut_app | Revolut application]] and its features more efficiently <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:50">[00:15:50]</a>.
