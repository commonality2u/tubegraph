This article outlines how to use the screen recorder feature on an iPhone to capture videos, photos, or create video tutorials, including how to enable audio recording <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:04">[00:00:04]</a>.

## Activating iPhone Screen Recording

To begin [[using_iphone_screen_recorder | using the recorder screen of your iPhone]], you first need to enable the feature within your device's [[iphone_settings_for_airpods | settings]]:

1.  Enter the [[iphone_settings_for_airpods | settings]] application on your iPhone <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:16">[00:00:16]</a>.
2.  Navigate to the "Control Center" section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:19">[00:00:19]</a>. This is typically represented by a gray icon <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:22">[00:00:22]</a>.
3.  Within the Control Center, select "[[customizing_iphone_controls | Customize Controls]]" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>.
4.  Among the available options, find and add "Screen Recording" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:39">[00:00:39]</a>.
5.  You can also [[customizing_iphone_controls | customize]] the order of the controls as per your preference <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>.

Once activated, the screen recorder option will be accessible at all times by sliding down from the top of the screen to open the [[accessing_control_center_on_iphone | Control Center]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:59">[00:00:59]</a>.

## Enabling Microphone Audio for Screen Recordings

The iPhone screen recorder also allows you to record sound, capturing both internal audio from your device and external audio via the microphone:

*   To [[activating_microphone_for_screen_recording | activate the microphone]] for sound recording, press and hold the screen recording icon in the [[accessing_control_center_on_iphone | Control Center]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:11">[00:01:11]</a>.
*   Holding the icon down will enable the [[activating_microphone_for_screen_recording | microphone]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:13">[00:01:13]</a>.
*   This feature allows you to record videos that include both internal audio from your device and external audio captured by the microphone, such as your voice <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:18">[00:01:18]</a>.
