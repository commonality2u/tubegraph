To begin posting on YouTube from a mobile device (Android or iPhone), users must first ensure they have the YouTube application and are logged into their account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:05">[00:00:05]</a>.

## Prerequisites

Before proceeding, it is necessary to have the YouTube platform downloaded as an app <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:29">[00:00:29]</a>.

### Downloading the YouTube App

The YouTube app can be downloaded from:
*   The App Store for iPhone devices <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:34">[00:00:34]</a>.
*   Google Play for Android devices <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[00:00:37]</a>.

## Logging In

After downloading the app, the next step is to open it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:45">[00:00:45]</a>. Once inside the application, users must register or log in with their official YouTube account from which they intend to publish content <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:48">[00:00:48]</a>. This step is crucial for enabling the publishing features within the app <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:55">[00:00:55]</a>.
