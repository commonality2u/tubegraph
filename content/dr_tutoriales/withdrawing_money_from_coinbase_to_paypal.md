This tutorial explains how to withdraw funds from your [[withdrawing_money_from_coinbase_to_bank_account | Coinbase]] or [[how_to_withdraw_money_from_kucoin_to_a_bank_account | Coinbase Pro]] account to your [[linking_paypal_account_to_coinbase | PayPal]] account in a simple and intuitive way <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:05">[00:00:05]</a>.

## Prerequisites and Key Concepts

Before beginning the withdrawal process, it's essential to understand a few key concepts:

*   **Platform Specificity** Only the standard [[withdrawing_money_from_coinbase_to_bank_account | Coinbase]] platform allows direct transfers to [[linking_paypal_account_to_coinbase | PayPal]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:33">[00:01:33]</a>. If your money is in [[how_to_withdraw_money_from_kucoin_to_a_bank_account | Coinbase Pro]], you will first need to transfer it to [[withdrawing_money_from_coinbase_to_bank_account | Coinbase]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:40">[00:01:40]</a>. This means a two-step process if starting from [[how_to_withdraw_money_from_kucoin_to_a_bank_account | Coinbase Pro]], and a one-step process if starting from [[withdrawing_money_from_coinbase_to_bank_account | Coinbase]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:49">[00:01:49]</a>.
*   **Name Verification** The name on your [[linking_paypal_account_to_coinbase | PayPal]] account must match the name on your [[withdrawing_money_from_coinbase_to_bank_account | Coinbase]] account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:08">[00:02:08]</a>. This name should also be identical to the one on your official identity document (e.g., DNI in Spain) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:14">[00:02:14]</a>.

## Linking Your PayPal Account to Coinbase

To withdraw funds, you must first link your [[linking_paypal_account_to_coinbase | PayPal]] account to [[withdrawing_money_from_coinbase_to_bank_account | Coinbase]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:59">[00:01:59]</a>:

1.  **Access Settings** On the [[withdrawing_money_from_coinbase_to_bank_account | Coinbase]] platform, go to your profile in the top right corner and select "Configuration" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:28">[00:02:28]</a>.
2.  **Navigate to Payment Methods** Within Configuration, find "Financial Services" or "Payment Methods" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:34">[00:02:34]</a>.
3.  **Add PayPal** Click "Add a payment method" and choose [[linking_paypal_account_to_coinbase | PayPal]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:51">[00:02:51]</a>.
4.  **Confirm Linking** [[linking_paypal_account_to_coinbase | Coinbase]] will ask for permission to access part of your [[linking_paypal_account_to_coinbase | PayPal]] profile information <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:14">[00:03:14]</a>. Ensure the [[linking_paypal_account_to_coinbase | PayPal]] email is the same as your [[withdrawing_money_from_coinbase_to_bank_account | Coinbase]] account email <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:24">[00:03:24]</a>. Accept to link the accounts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:31">[00:03:31]</a>.

Once completed, your [[linking_paypal_account_to_coinbase | PayPal]] account will appear as a linked payment method <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:37">[00:03:37]</a>. You can unlink it at any time <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:29">[00:03:29]</a>.

## Transferring Funds from Coinbase Pro to Coinbase

If your funds are in [[how_to_withdraw_money_from_kucoin_to_a_bank_account | Coinbase Pro]], you cannot directly withdraw them to [[linking_paypal_account_to_coinbase | PayPal]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:39">[00:04:39]</a>. You must first transfer them to your standard [[withdrawing_money_from_coinbase_to_bank_account | Coinbase]] account:

1.  **Convert Cryptocurrencies** On [[how_to_withdraw_money_from_kucoin_to_a_bank_account | Coinbase Pro]], it is recommended to convert your cryptocurrencies into the fiat currency you wish to withdraw (e.g., euros) *before* transferring them to [[withdrawing_money_from_coinbase_to_bank_account | Coinbase]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:18">[00:05:18]</a>. This helps in [[avoiding_high_transaction_fees_when_withdrawing_from_coinbase | avoiding higher transaction fees]] that may occur if you perform the conversion within [[withdrawing_money_from_coinbase_to_bank_account | Coinbase]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:28">[00:05:28]</a>.
2.  **Initiate Withdrawal** Go to your portfolio on [[how_to_withdraw_money_from_kucoin_to_a_bank_account | Coinbase Pro]] and select "Withdraw" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:43">[00:05:43]</a>.
3.  **Choose Coinbase** Select the currency you wish to withdraw (e.g., EUR) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:46">[00:05:46]</a>. Among the options, choose "Transfer to [[withdrawing_money_from_coinbase_to_bank_account | Coinbase]]" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:53">[00:05:53]</a>.
4.  **Specify Amount** Enter the desired amount to transfer. The transfer is instant and costs zero fees <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:05">[00:06:05]</a>.
5.  **Confirm** Click "Withdraw" to complete the transfer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:08">[00:06:08]</a>.

Your funds will now be available in your standard [[withdrawing_money_from_coinbase_to_bank_account | Coinbase]] account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:12">[00:06:12]</a>.

## Withdrawing Funds from Coinbase to PayPal

Once your funds are in your standard [[withdrawing_money_from_coinbase_to_bank_account | Coinbase]] account:

1.  **Access Portfolio** Go to the initial page of [[withdrawing_money_from_coinbase_to_bank_account | Coinbase]] or navigate to your "Portfolio" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:27">[00:06:27]</a>.
2.  **Select Currency** Click on the fiat currency (e.g., euros) you wish to withdraw <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:42">[00:06:42]</a>.
3.  **Initiate Withdrawal** Click "Withdraw" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:45">[00:06:45]</a>.
4.  **Enter Amount** Specify the amount you want to withdraw. You can withdraw the entire amount or a partial amount <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:47">[00:06:47]</a>.
5.  **Choose PayPal** On the "Where do you want to remove it?" screen, select "Instant withdrawal" which is free, and choose [[linking_paypal_account_to_coinbase | PayPal]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:03">[00:07:03]</a>.
    *   *Note*: [[withdrawing_money_from_coinbase_to_bank_account | Coinbase]] also offers the option to withdraw to a [[withdrawing_money_from_coinbase_to_bank_account | bank account]] with a small commission (e.g., 0.15 euros in the European Commission) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:06">[00:07:06]</a>.
6.  **Confirm Withdrawal** Review the amount and ensure the withdrawal fee is zero euros for [[linking_paypal_account_to_coinbase | PayPal]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:24">[00:07:24]</a>. Click "Withdraw now" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:28">[00:07:28]</a>.

[[withdrawing_funds_from_coinbase_wallet_to_bank_account | Coinbase]] will send a notification once the request is filled <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:37">[00:07:37]</a>. The funds should appear almost instantly in your [[linking_paypal_account_to_coinbase | PayPal]] balance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:49">[00:07:49]</a>.

> [!NOTE]
> When transferring funds from [[how_to_withdraw_money_from_kucoin_to_a_bank_account | Coinbase Pro]] to [[withdrawing_money_from_coinbase_to_bank_account | Coinbase]], convert your cryptocurrencies to fiat currency on [[how_to_withdraw_money_from_kucoin_to_a_bank_account | Coinbase Pro]] first to [[avoiding_high_transaction_fees_when_withdrawing_from_coinbase | avoid higher commissions]] that may apply if conversion is done on [[withdrawing_money_from_coinbase_to_bank_account | Coinbase]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:28">[00:05:28]</a>.
