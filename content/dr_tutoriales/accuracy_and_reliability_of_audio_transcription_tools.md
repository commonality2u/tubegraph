Online tools for [[how_to_transcribe_audio_to_text | transcribing audio to text]] are available, many of which can be accessed through [[using_google_chrome_for_online_transcription | Google Chrome]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:18">[00:00:18]</a>. ## Free vs. Paid Transcription Tools

Many [[free_and_paid_transcription_tools | transcription tools]] are available, but their capabilities and accuracy often differ from paid services <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:43">[00:00:43]</a>.

- **Free Tools**
    - Two free tools are highlighted that can be used for basic audio transcription <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.
    - These tools are generally reliable for shorter audio clips, achieving approximately 80-90% successful word transcription <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:30">[00:03:30]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:35">[00:03:35]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:38">[00:03:38]</a>.
    - They are not designed for transcribing very long audios, such as entire movies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:51">[00:03:51]</a>.

- **Paid Tools**
    - Most advanced transcription services require payment, often after a [[comparison_of_transcription_service_pricing | free trial period]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:47">[00:00:47]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:54">[00:00:54]</a>.
    - The need for computational power to process audio often justifies the cost of these services <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:57">[00:00:57]</a>.
    - Examples of paid services include:
        -   **Happy Scribe:** Pricing is based on the number of minutes or words transcribed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:09">[00:01:09]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:14">[00:01:14]</a>.
        -   **Speech-to-Text Artificial Intelligence tools:** One example offers 180 minutes of transcription for ten dollars per month <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:24">[00:01:24]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:29">[00:01:29]</a>.
        -   **OpenAI and Bizz:** Also mentioned as services that require payment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:36">[00:01:36]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:41">[00:01:41]</a>.
    - Some free versions of paid tools may have limitations, such as a 30-minute cap for subtitle generation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:44">[00:01:44]</a>.
    - When a payment is made on a platform, the quality of the transcription is significantly higher <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:08">[00:04:08]</a>.

## Factors Influencing Transcription Accuracy

-   **Audio Speed:** Setting a normal playback speed for audio is recommended during transcription <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:54">[00:02:54]</a>. If the audio speed is too fast, the transcription process is likely to make errors <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:54">[00:02:54]</a>.

-   **Input Method:** The process involves uploading an audio file (e.g., from a mobile phone) directly into the online transcription tool <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:32">[00:02:32]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:37">[00:02:37]</a>.
