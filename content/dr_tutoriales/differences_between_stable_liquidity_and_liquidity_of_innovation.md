Within the [[how_to_use_liquid_swap_to_invest_in_cryptocurrencies | Liquid Swap]] financial product, users can choose between different types of liquidity provision <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:30:42">[01:30:42]</a>. These options are categorized into "stable liquidity" and "liquidity of innovation" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:52:43">[02:52:43]</a>.

## Stable Liquidity

Stable liquidity refers to products whose profitability "does not vary much" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:56:06">[02:56:06]</a>.

## Liquidity of Innovation

Liquidity of innovation involves products whose profitability "vary a little more" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:00:26">[03:00:26]</a>.

## Key Difference

The fundamental distinction between stable liquidity and liquidity of innovation is the degree of **profitability variation** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:56:06">[02:56:06]</a>. Stable liquidity offers more consistent returns, while liquidity of innovation presents returns that fluctuate more significantly.
