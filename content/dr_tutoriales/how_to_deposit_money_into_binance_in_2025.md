## Direct Deposits on Binance

[[depositing_fiat_and_cryptocurrencies_on_binance | Depositing funds]] directly into [[depositing_fiat_and_cryptocurrencies_on_binance | Binance]] can be done in two primary ways: fiat currency deposits or cryptocurrency deposits <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:49:00">[01:49:00]</a>.

### Fiat Currency Deposits

Fiat currencies include national currencies like Euro, Dollar, Pound Sterling, Rubles, or Czech Crowns, provided they are accepted by the platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:11:00">[02:11:00]</a>. The available deposit methods and associated commissions can vary depending on the currency chosen and the current market situation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:24:00">[02:24:00]</a>.

Common methods and their typical commission structures observed:
*   **Visa/MasterCard Bank Card:** Can incur commissions such as 1.8% for Euro or Pound Sterling <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:45:00">[02:45:00]</a>. This is generally considered high <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:37:00">[03:37:00]</a>.
*   **P2P Bank Transfer:** Often has 0% commission <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:49:00">[02:49:00]</a>.
*   **Wallet Make Cash:** Can have 0% commission, though additional fees may apply <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:52:00">[02:52:00]</a>.
*   **SEPA Transfer:** (e.g., for Euro) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:31:00">[02:31:00]</a>.
*   **Wire Transfer/Swift:** (e.g., for Dollar) Can be 0% commission, though availability may fluctuate <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:51:00">[03:51:00]</a>.

> **Note:** Some transfer methods, like SEPA transfers and rapid payments, may be temporarily suspended in certain countries, particularly within the European community, due to ongoing regulatory analysis <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:07:00">[03:07:00]</a>.

### Using Kraken as an Intermediary

Kraken is an example of a platform where you can deposit fiat currencies (like Euros) potentially with 0% commission via methods like SEPA or Swift <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:01:00">[06:01:00]</a>.
1.  **Deposit Fiat:** Deposit Euros into Kraken via SEPA <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:11:00">[07:11:00]</a>. This usually takes one to two business days <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:23:00">[07:23:00]</a>.
2.  **Convert Fiat to Crypto:** Convert the deposited Euros into a cryptocurrency. The conversion commission can be around 0.16% (e.g., for Bitcoin) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:22:00">[08:22:00]</a>. For a 2,000 Euro deposit, this would be approximately 3.2 Euros <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:23:00">[10:23:00]</a>.
3.  **Choose Low-Fee Crypto for Transfer:** Select cryptocurrencies with low withdrawal fees for transfer to [[depositing_fiat_and_cryptocurrencies_on_binance | Binance]]. Popular choices include:
    *   **Litecoin (LTC):** Known for low shipping costs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:18:00">[09:18:00]</a>.
    *   **Tron (TRX):** Also has very low transfer fees. For example, sending Tron from Kraken to [[depositing_fiat_and_cryptocurrencies_on_binance | Binance]] could cost as little as 10 cents USD <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:22:00">[09:22:00]</a>.
4.  **Transfer Crypto to Binance:** Transfer the chosen cryptocurrency from Kraken to [[depositing_fiat_and_cryptocurrencies_on_binance | Binance]]. This step usually has a very reduced price <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:07:00">[06:07:00]</a>.

> **Example Cost Analysis (Kraken to Binance for 2000 Euros via Tron):**
> *   Deposit Fiat (Euros) to Kraken: 0 Euros <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:14:00">[10:14:00]</a>
> *   Convert Euros to Tron on Kraken: ~3.2 Euros (0.16% of 2000 Euros) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:23:00">[10:23:00]</a>
