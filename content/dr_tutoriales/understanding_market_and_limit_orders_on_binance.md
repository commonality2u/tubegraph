## Buying BNB on PC

To buy BNB on the [[navigating_and_understanding_binance_app_features | Binance]] website:

1.  **Navigate to Markets**: Go to the "markets" section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:32">[00:01:32]</a>.
2.  **Search for BNB**: In the "zones" filter, select "everything" and search for "BNB" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:41">[00:01:41]</a>. BNB Coin is listed as the fourth cryptocurrency by capitalization <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:47">[00:01:47]</a>.
3.  **Select Trading Pair**: Click on BNB and then "operate" to open a new trading tab <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:01">[00:02:01]</a>. The graph defaults to BNB/USD (USDT), which is a stablecoin imitating the dollar's value <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:05">[00:02:05]</a>. You can choose other trading pairs, such as BNB/Euro, by clicking the dropdown arrow and searching for the desired pair <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:36">[00:02:36]</a>.
4.  **Place Order**: Locate the "place order" section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:06">[00:03:06]</a>. Here you can buy or sell, and choose different [[types_of_orders_on_kucoin | order types]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:11">[00:03:11]</a>.

### Using a Market Order

A market order allows you to buy or sell a cryptocurrency at the current market price in real-time <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:36">[00:03:36]</a>.

1.  **Select Market Order**: Choose the "Market" option from the order types <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:47">[00:03:47]</a>.
2.  **Specify Amount**:
    *   **By Total Amount**: Enter the total amount of fiat currency (e.g., Euros) you wish to spend to buy BNB <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:58">[00:03:58]</a>. For example, to buy 1,000 Euros worth of BNB <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:04">[00:04:04]</a>.
    *   **By BNB Quantity**: Enter the specific quantity of BNB you want to buy <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:14">[00:04:14]</a>. This will show the equivalent cost in your chosen fiat currency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:16">[00:04:16]</a>.
3.  **Execute Order**: Click "Buy BNB". The BNB will then appear in your spot wallet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:07">[00:04:07]</a>.

## Buying BNB on Mobile

To buy BNB using the [[navigating_and_understanding_binance_app_features | Binance]] mobile app:

1.  **Download and Open App**: Download the [[navigating_and_understanding_binance_app_features | Binance]] app and open it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:31">[00:04:31]</a>.
2.  **Navigate to Markets**: Tap on "Markets" in the bottom submenu <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:46">[00:04:46]</a>.
3.  **Search for BNB**: Use the magnifying glass icon to search for "BNB" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:53">[00:04:53]</a>.
4.  **Select Trading Pair**: Choose an exchange pair, such as BNB/Euro <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:07">[00:05:07]</a>. This will open the BNB graph with the selected pair <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:13">[00:05:13]</a>.
5.  **Place Order**: Tap "Buy" at the bottom of the screen <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:28">[00:05:28]</a>.
6.  **Select Order Type**: Similar to the desktop version, you can choose between different [[types_of_orders_on_kucoin | order types]] (Market, Limit, Stop-Limit, OCO) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:40">[00:05:40]</a>.
7.  **Specify Amount**: Enter the total amount of currency you wish to spend (e.g., 1,000 Euros) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:54">[00:05:54]</a>.
8.  **Execute Order**: Tap "Buy BNB". If sufficient funds are available, the BNB will be purchased and appear in your portfolio <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:01">[00:06:01]</a>.

## Viewing Purchased BNB

After buying BNB, it will appear in your **Spot Wallet**:

1.  **Go to Wallet**: Navigate to the "Wallet" section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:17">[00:06:17]</a>.
2.  **Spot Wallet**: Your newly purchased BNB will be visible in the "Spot Wallet" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:24">[00:06:24]</a>.
