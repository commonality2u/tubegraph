This guide provides a detailed, updated, and simple method for transferring funds from your cryptocurrency platforms, specifically Coinbase and Coinbase Pro, to your PayPal account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:05">[00:00:05]</a>.

## Important Considerations Before Transferring

*   **Platform Specificity**: Of the two platforms, only Coinbase allows direct transfers to PayPal <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:33">[00:01:33]</a>.
*   **Coinbase Pro Transfers**: If your funds are in Coinbase Pro, you must first transfer them to Coinbase before moving them to PayPal <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:40">[00:01:40]</a>. This requires two steps <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:49">[00:01:49]</a>.
*   **Coinbase Transfers**: If your money is already on the Coinbase platform, it's a single step to transfer to PayPal <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:52">[00:01:52]</a>.
*   **Name Verification**: The name on your PayPal account and your Coinbase account must match the name on your official identity document (e.g., DNI in Spain) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:08">[00:02:08]</a>.

## Linking Your PayPal Account to Coinbase

This is the first essential step to enable withdrawals to PayPal.

1.  **Access Settings**: Navigate to your profile settings by clicking on your profile icon at the top right of the Coinbase interface <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:28">[00:02:28]</a>. Then, select "Configuration" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:31">[00:02:31]</a>.
2.  **Payment Methods**: In the configuration section, go to "Financial Services and Payment Methods" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:34">[00:02:34]</a> or "Payment Methods" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:40">[00:02:40]</a>.
3.  **Add PayPal**: Select "Add a payment method" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:51">[00:02:51]</a> and choose "PayPal" from the options <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:53">[00:02:53]</a>.
4.  **Link Account**: Confirm linking your PayPal account to allow immediate fund withdrawals <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:57">[00:02:57]</a>.
    *   *Note*: Ensure your PayPal account is made with the same email as your Coinbase account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:24">[00:03:24]</a>.
5.  **Accept & Confirm**: Accept the linking request. Your PayPal account will now be linked to your Coinbase account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:37">[00:03:37]</a>. You can unlink accounts in the future if needed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:29">[00:03:29]</a>.

## Transferring Funds from Coinbase Pro to Coinbase

This step is necessary if your funds are currently in Coinbase Pro, as direct PayPal withdrawals are not possible from this platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:09">[00:04:09]</a>.

1.  **Access Coinbase Pro**: Go to the Coinbase Pro platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:03">[00:04:03]</a>.
2.  **Convert Cryptocurrencies**:
    *   It is recommended to transform your cryptocurrencies into the desired withdrawal currency (e.g., Euros) while still on Coinbase Pro <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:18">[00:05:18]</a>. This helps avoid higher commission fees that might apply if converting within Coinbase <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:28">[00:05:28]</a>.
3.  **Initiate Withdrawal**:
    *   Go to "Portfolios" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:44">[00:04:44]</a>.
    *   Select the cryptocurrency or fiat currency you wish to transfer (e.g., Euros) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:46">[00:05:46]</a>.
    *   Click "Withdraw" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:43">[00:05:43]</a>.
4.  **Choose Coinbase Transfer**:
    *   Select the option to "Transfer to Coinbase" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:53">[00:05:53]</a>.
    *   The daily limit for transfers to Coinbase is 250,000 Euros <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:56">[00:05:56]</a>.
5.  **Confirm Transfer**:
    *   Enter the amount to transfer. The commission for this transfer is zero euros <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:05">[00:06:05]</a>.
    *   The process is instant <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:07">[00:06:07]</a>. Click "Withdraw" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:08">[00:06:08]</a>.
6.  **Verify Transfer**: Your funds will now be in your Coinbase account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:12">[00:06:12]</a>. You can confirm this on the Coinbase initial page or within the "Portfolio" section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:27">[00:06:27]</a>.

## Transferring Funds from Coinbase to PayPal

Once your funds are in Coinbase, you can proceed with the withdrawal to PayPal.

1.  **Access Portfolio**: On the Coinbase platform, go to the "Portfolio" section to see your funds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:30">[00:06:30]</a>.
2.  **Select Funds for Withdrawal**: Click on the specific currency you wish to withdraw (e.g., Euros) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:42">[00:06:42]</a>.
3.  **Initiate Withdrawal**: Click the "Withdraw" button <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:45">[00:06:45]</a>.
4.  **Specify Amount**:
    *   Choose to withdraw the entire amount or specify a lesser quantity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:50">[00:06:50]</a>.
    *   Click "Continue" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:57">[00:06:57]</a>.
5.  **Choose Withdrawal Method**:
    *   Select "Instant withdrawal" to PayPal, which is free <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:03">[00:07:03]</a>.
    *   *Note*: There is also an option to [[transfer_money_from_bank_to_paypal | withdraw to your bank account]], which may incur a small commission (e.g., 0.15 euros in the European commission) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:06">[00:07:06]</a>.
6.  **Confirm Withdrawal**: Review the amount to withdraw, ensuring the withdrawal fee is zero euros for PayPal transfers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:24">[00:07:24]</a>.
7.  **Finalize Transfer**: Click "Withdraw now" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:28">[00:07:28]</a>.
8.  **Verification**: Coinbase will send a notification once the withdrawal request has been fulfilled <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:37">[00:07:37]</a>.

## Checking PayPal Account

1.  **Update PayPal Page**: Refresh your PayPal account page <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:46">[00:07:46]</a>.
2.  **Confirm Balance**: Your PayPal account balance should now reflect the transferred funds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:51">[00:07:51]</a>.
