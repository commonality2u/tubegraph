This article provides a tutorial on [[using_the_binance_app_on_a_mobile_device | using the Binance app]] on a mobile phone to buy different cryptocurrencies and assets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:05">[00:00:05]</a>. It specifically focuses on the mobile application, assuming the user has already created an account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:34">[00:00:34]</a>.

## Getting Started with the Binance App

The tutorial is applicable for both iPhone and Android devices, as the app offers the same functionalities across platforms <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>.

To begin:
1.  Go to Google Play or the App Store <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a>.
2.  Search for and download the [[using_the_binance_app_on_a_mobile_device | Binance app]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:24">[00:01:24]</a>.
3.  Once downloaded, tap the "Open" button <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:38">[00:01:38]</a>.
4.  Allow notifications when prompted <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:49">[00:01:49]</a>.
5.  Log in to your existing account using your email and password <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:55">[00:01:55]</a>.
6.  Complete the security verification (e.g., puzzle slide) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:03">[00:02:03]</a>.
7.  Enter the security code sent to your mobile phone <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:09">[00:02:09]</a>.

## App Interface Overview

Upon logging in, the main screen presents a sub-menu at the bottom with options for Home, Markets, Trade, Futures, and Wallet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:30">[00:02:30]</a>. At the top of the screen, you'll find icons for your profile, alerts (bell icon), and a QR scanner <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:48">[00:02:48]</a>.

### Top Menu Features

*   **Profile**: Access your profile by tapping the icon in the top left <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:06">[00:03:06]</a>.
    *   **Account Verification**: Shows if your account is verified <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:08">[00:03:08]</a>.
    *   **Binance Lite**: Offers an option to switch to a simplified format of the app with fewer menus, though it is not recommended for this tutorial <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:15">[00:03:15]</a>.
    *   **Discounts**: Displays available discounts, such as a 25% discount when paying with BNB <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:39">[00:03:39]</a>.
    *   **Rewards**: Check your rewards <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:49">[00:03:49]</a>.
    *   **Referral Programs**: View and manage [[setting_up_and_managing_binance_referral_programs | Binance's referral programs]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:50">[00:03:50]</a>.
    *   **Security and Settings**: Adjust [[security_features_and_account_settings_on_binance | security features on your account]], change passwords, send messages to support, or interact with other users for trading <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:55">[00:03:55]</a>.
*   **Notifications (Bell Icon)**: Located in the top right, this section displays all notifications <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:20">[00:04:20]</a>. You can mark them as read, clear them, or delete them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:32">[00:04:32]</a>.
*   **QR Scanner**: The symbol next to the bell icon allows access to the camera to scan QR codes and verify your mobile phone <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:54">[00:02:54]</a>.

### Bottom Menu Features

#### Home
The home screen displays various cryptocurrencies and their options <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:39">[00:02:39]</a>. It also provides options for:
*   **Transfer**: Allows transferring different assets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:59">[00:04:59]</a>.
*   **Convert**: Convert assets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:59">[00:04:59]</a>.
*   **Buying Cryptocurrencies**: Offers payment methods like bank transfer, Visa card, and other options <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:06">[00:05:06]</a>. For example, to buy [[how_to_buy_bnb_on_binance_using_a_mobile_device | BNB (Binance Coin)]], simply click on it, and it will direct you to the purchase menu <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:20">[00:05:20]</a>.
*   **Cryptocurrency Pair Classification**: Shows rankings of cryptocurrency pairs offered by the platform, allowing you to change the market view <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:39">[00:05:39]</a>.

#### Markets
This section provides a more detailed view of all markets compared to the Home screen <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:53">[00:05:53]</a>.
*   **Categories**: Includes menus for favorites, spot, and futures <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:57">[00:05:57]</a>.
*   **All Markets**: Lists all cryptocurrencies available on the platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:01">[00:06:01]</a>.
*   **Charts and Indicators**: You can view charts for any cryptocurrency (e.g., Bitcoin) and customize them by timeframes (days, hours) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:13">[00:06:13]</a>. You can also insert indicators like MACD <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:28">[00:06:28]</a>.
*   **Buy/Sell Options**: Directly buy or sell from the market charts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:32">[00:06:32]</a>.
*   **Favorites**: Add cryptocurrencies to your favorites list by tapping the star icon <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:41">[00:06:41]</a>.

#### Trade
This section allows for direct and fast trading of different assets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:01">[00:07:01]</a>.
*   **Asset Selection**: By default, it might show Bitcoin against another currency (e.g., USDT) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:19">[00:07:19]</a>. You can easily change the active asset by clicking on the current one <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:47">[00:07:47]</a>.
*   **Order Types**: Place limited orders, market orders, stop orders, or OCO (One Cancels the Other) orders <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:25">[00:07:25]</a>.
*   **Amount Input**: Specify the amount for your operation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:33">[00:07:33]</a>. The cost in USD will be displayed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:37">[00:07:37]</a>. This function enables [[using_binance_for_cryptocurrency_investments | super simple and easy cryptocurrency operations]] from your mobile device at any moment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:01">[00:08:01]</a>.

#### Futures
This section allows operations on cryptocurrency futures, similar to the main trading section but specifically for futures contracts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:10">[00:08:10]</a>. For more detailed information, refer to [[using_binance_futures_app_on_mobile | using the Binance Futures app on mobile]].

#### Wallet
The wallet section displays the assets you hold <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:22">[00:08:22]</a>.
*   **Portfolio Overview**: Shows your overall portfolio (censored for privacy in the tutorial) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:26">[00:08:26]</a>.
*   **Actions**: Options to deposit, withdraw, and transfer Bitcoins, cryptocurrencies, or funds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:31">[00:08:31]</a>.
*   **Detailed Views**: The wallet has sub-menus for:
    *   **Summaries**: General overview <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:41">[00:08:41]</a>.
    *   **Spot**: Shows profitability, assets, and balances based on your purchases <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:44">[00:08:44]</a>. For example, if you hold Bitcoin, it will show the amount and daily profitability, which can be configured <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:53">[00:08:53]</a>.
    *   **Margin**: For trading with leverage, it displays features related to risk and operational status <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:13">[00:09:13]</a>.
    *   **Futures**: Similar to the futures section, but from the wallet perspective <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:22">[00:09:22]</a>.
    *   **P2P (Peer-to-Peer)**: Manage P2P transactions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:22">[00:09:22]</a>.
