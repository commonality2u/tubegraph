MediaFire is a platform that allows users to [[how_to_upload_files_to_mediafire | upload files]] to the cloud and share them for free <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:11">[00:00:11]</a>. Before [[how_to_upload_files_to_mediafire | uploading files]], users need to [[creating_a_mediafire_account | create an account]] and select a plan <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>.

## Basic (Free) Account

A basic, free MediaFire account comes with specific features and limitations:
*   **Storage Limit**: The maximum cloud storage capacity is 10 gigabytes (GB) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:13">[00:01:13]</a>.
*   **Expanding Storage**: It is possible to increase basic MediaFire storage up to 50 GB <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:24">[00:01:24]</a>.
*   **Feature Limitations**: Users on a basic account cannot upload websites <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:48">[00:03:48]</a>.

## Premium Account

Premium accounts offer additional features, such as the ability to upload entire websites, a feature not available to basic users <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:41">[00:03:41]</a>.

Once an account is created, users can proceed to [[how_to_upload_files_to_mediafire | upload files]] and [[sharing_mediafire_file_links | share them]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:46">[00:01:46]</a>.
