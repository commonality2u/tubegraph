To create a MEGA account, you need to:
1.  Enter an email account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:45">[00:00:45]</a>.
2.  Receive a confirmation email <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>.
3.  Confirm the account through the email <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:53">[00:00:53]</a>.
