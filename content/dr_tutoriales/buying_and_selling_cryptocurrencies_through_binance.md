This article outlines how to use the Binance mobile application to buy and sell various cryptocurrencies and assets. The guide focuses on using the app from a mobile phone after an account has already been created and verified <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[00:00:37]</a>.

## Getting Started with the Binance Mobile App

The Binance app offers the same utilities for both iPhone and Android devices <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>.

### Downloading and Logging In
1.  **Download the app**: Access your device's App Store (for iPhone) or Google Play (for Android) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a>.
2.  **Search and install**: Search for "Binance" and download the official app <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:24">[00:01:24]</a>.
3.  **Open the app**: Once downloaded, tap "Open" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:38">[00:01:38]</a>.
4.  **Allow notifications**: Grant permission to send notifications when prompted <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:49">[00:01:49]</a>.
5.  **Log in**: Tap "Log in" at the top right <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:55">[00:01:55]</a>.
6.  **Enter credentials**: Input your email and password, then tap "Enter" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:00">[00:02:00]</a>.
7.  **Complete security verification**: Perform the security verification (e.g., reCAPTCHA puzzle) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:03">[00:02:03]</a>.
8.  **Enter phone security code**: A security code will be sent to your mobile phone; enter it to complete the login <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:09">[00:02:09]</a>.

## Buying Cryptocurrencies

From the Home screen, you can find options to buy cryptocurrencies:
*   **Directly select an asset**: Tap on a specific cryptocurrency, like BNV, to be taken directly to its buy/sell menu <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:20">[00:05:20]</a>.
*   **Use the "Transfer" or "Convert" options**: These options are found alongside "Buy" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:59">[00:04:59]</a>.
*   **Payment methods**: Binance offers various payment methods, including bank transfer and Visa card purchases <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:06">[00:05:06]</a>.

### Trade Section for Buying and Selling
The "Trade" section allows for direct and fast operations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:01">[00:07:01]</a>.
1.  **Select an asset pair**: For example, Bitcoin against USDT <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:19">[00:07:19]</a>.
2.  **Choose order type**: You can place different types of orders <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:25">[00:07:25]</a>:
    *   Limited order
    *   Market order
    *   Stop order
    *   OCO (One Cancels the Other)
3.  **Specify amount**: Enter the amount for your operation, either in the target cryptocurrency (e.g., Bitcoins) or in USD <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:33">[00:07:33]</a>.
4.  **Execute**: Tap "Buy Bitcoin" after setting your desired order type and amount <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:41">[00:07:41]</a>.
5.  **Change assets**: Easily switch the active asset pair by clicking on the current one (e.g., "Bitcoin") <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:47">[00:07:47]</a>.
