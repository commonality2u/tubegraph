To access and [[customizing_iphone_controls | customize controls]] within the Control Center on your iPhone, follow these steps <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:16">[00:00:16]</a>. This process allows you to add features like [[using_iphone_screen_recorder | screen recording]] for videos and tutorials <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:04">[00:00:04]</a>.

## Navigating to Control Center Settings

1.  **Enter Settings**: Begin by opening the "Settings" application on your iPhone <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:16">[00:00:16]</a>.
2.  **Locate Control Center**: Scroll down and select the "Control Center" section, identifiable by a gray icon <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:19">[00:00:19]</a>. Note that you do not access this feature directly from other apps <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:25">[00:00:25]</a>.
3.  **Customize Controls**: Within the Control Center settings, tap on "Customize Controls" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>.

## Adding Screen Recording

Once in "Customize Controls", you will see a list of available options <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:32">[00:00:32]</a>.
1.  **Select Screen Recording**: Identify "Screen Recording" among the options <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[00:00:37]</a>.
2.  **Activate**: Add it to your included controls. You can also reorder the controls to your preference <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>.

After activation, sliding down from the top of your screen will reveal the [[using_iphone_screen_recorder | screen recorder]] option in the Control Center at all times <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>.

## Recording with Sound

The screen recorder also allows you to capture audio <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:05">[00:01:05]</a>.
*   **Activate Microphone**: To enable sound recording, press and hold the [[using_iphone_screen_recorder | screen recording]] icon within the Control Center <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:11">[00:01:11]</a>. This action will allow you to activate the microphone, enabling the [[recording_internal_and_external_audio_on_iphone | recording of videos with sound]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:13">[00:01:13]</a>.
