This guide explains how to link a bank account to your PayPal account to deposit funds. The process is identical whether using a computer or a mobile phone <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:39">[00:00:39]</a>.

## Initial Steps & Considerations

Before depositing funds, it is crucial to [[linking_a_credit_or_debit_card_to_paypal | link your bank account to PayPal]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:44:00">[01:44:00]</a>. Transfers will be automatically rejected if the bank account is not linked <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:49:00">[01:49:00]</a>.

## Linking a Bank Account

To link a bank account:
1.  PayPal will send a small amount (e.g., a penny or "one centimile") to your bank account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:11:00">[02:11:00]</a>.
2.  You will need to confirm the exact amount of this transfer within PayPal <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:14:00">[02:14:00]</a>.
3.  Once confirmed, your account will be linked to the PayPal platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:22:00">[02:22:00]</a>.

Linking typically takes one to two days <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:08:00">[02:08:00]</a>.

## How to Deposit Funds into PayPal

1.  Log in to your PayPal account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:43:00">[00:00:43]</a>.
2.  Navigate to the "PayPal balance section" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:48:00">[00:48:00]</a>.
    *   Alternatively, go to "my wallet" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:03:00">[01:03:00]</a>.
3.  Click on "Transfer money" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:51:00">[00:51:00]</a>.
4.  You will be presented with options to transfer money between your PayPal account and your bank <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:18:00">[01:18:00]</a>. Select "Add money to your balance PayPal" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:21:00">[01:21:00]</a>.
5.  PayPal will provide details for a bank transfer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:33:00">[01:33:00]</a>:
    *   Use a bank account linked to PayPal <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:28:00">[01:28:00]</a>.
    *   Log in to your bank's online services or visit a physical bank <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:31:00">[01:31:00]</a>.
    *   Perform a transfer to the provided PayPal banking details, including the IBAN and beneficiary bank <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:31:00">[02:31:00]</a>.
    *   It is important to enter the beneficiary's name <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:40:00">[02:40:00]</a>.
    *   Do not put anything in the "concept" or description field of the transfer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:37:00">[02:37:00]</a>.

### Transfer Timeframe
After initiating the transfer, you will receive an email confirmation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:42:00">[02:42:00]</a>. Bank transfers can take one to two days to process <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:44:00">[02:44:00]</a>. This timeframe refers to working days only <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:47:00">[02:47:00]</a>.
