## Installing TikTok

To install TikTok on your computer, follow these steps:

1.  **Open a Search Engine**: Begin by opening any search engine, such as Google Chrome, Firefox, Opera, or Microsoft Edge <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:14">[00:00:14]</a>.
2.  **Search for TikTok**: In the search engine, type "TikTok" and press enter <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>.
3.  **Access Official Website**: Navigate to the main, official TikTok website <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>. It's crucial to ensure you are on the official site <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:35">[00:00:35]</a>.
4.  **Install the App**: Once on the TikTok homepage, look to the right side of the screen <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>. You will find an option depicted by a computer icon with a downward arrow <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>. This option is for [[how_to_download_tiktok_for_pc_2025|installing TikTok]] onto your computer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:59">[00:00:59]</a>. Click this option, then click "Install" to begin the installation of the [[how_to_download_tiktok_for_pc_2025|TikTok app]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:02">[00:01:02]</a>.

## [[accessing_tiktok_desktop_app|Accessing TikTok]]

After installation, the TikTok application will be accessible:

*   **Desktop Shortcut**: A direct shortcut to TikTok will appear on your desktop <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a>.
*   **Taskbar Search**: If you cannot find it on your desktop, go to your taskbar, type "TikTok", and the application will appear <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:18">[00:01:18]</a>.
*   **Pin to Taskbar**: You can pin the application to your taskbar for quick access <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:23">[00:01:23]</a>.

## [[uninstalling_tiktok_from_pc|Uninstalling TikTok]]

If you wish to [[uninstalling_tiktok_from_pc|remove TikTok]] from your computer:

1.  **Open Control Panel**: Go to the search bar and type "Control" to find and open the Control Panel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:34">[00:01:34]</a>.
2.  **Uninstall Programs**: Within the Control Panel, navigate to "Programs" or "Uninstall a program" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:41">[00:01:41]</a>.
3.  **Locate and Uninstall**: Find "TikTok" in the list of programs, which is arranged alphabetically, and then proceed to [[uninstalling_tiktok_from_pc|uninstall]] it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:44">[00:01:44]</a>.
