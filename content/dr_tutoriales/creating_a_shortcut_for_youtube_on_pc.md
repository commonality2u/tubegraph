This guide explains how to install YouTube as a desktop application on your PC, create a shortcut, and manage its presence, including [[pinning_youtube_to_taskbar | pinning it to the taskbar]] and [[uninstalling_youtube_from_pc | uninstalling it]].

## Downloading and Installing YouTube as a PC App

To begin the [[downloading_youtube_on_pc | downloading YouTube]] process:
1.  Open a web browser on your PC <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:05">[00:00:05]</a>. While [[using_google_chrome_for_youtube | Google Chrome]] is demonstrated, other browsers like Edge, Opera, or Firefox can also be used <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:08">[00:00:08]</a>.
2.  Navigate directly to the YouTube website <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>.
3.  Look for an option to "Install YouTube" within the browser interface, often indicated by a download symbol <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:29">[00:00:29]</a>.
4.  Click "Install" to download and install the YouTube app <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:34">[00:00:34]</a>. The application will begin downloading immediately <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:39">[00:00:39]</a>.

## Locating the Installed YouTube Shortcut

Once the app is installed, a shortcut is typically created on your desktop <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:48">[00:00:48]</a>.
Alternatively, you can find the YouTube application by typing "YouTube" into your PC's search engine <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:53">[00:00:53]</a>.

## [[pinning_youtube_to_taskbar | Pinning YouTube to the Taskbar]]

To [[pinning_youtube_to_taskbar | pin the YouTube app to your taskbar]] for quick access:
1.  Locate the YouTube shortcut (either on the desktop or via search).
2.  Right-click on the YouTube icon <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:58">[00:00:58]</a>.
3.  Select the option to "Anchor to the taskbar" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>. The icon will then appear on your taskbar <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:02">[00:01:02]</a>.

## [[uninstalling_youtube_from_pc | Uninstalling YouTube from PC]]

If you need to [[uninstalling_youtube_from_pc | uninstall YouTube from your PC]]:
1.  Access the Control Panel on your computer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:42">[00:01:42]</a>.
2.  Within the Control Panel, select "Uninstall a program" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:50">[00:01:50]</a>.
3.  Locate "YouTube" in the list of programs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:52">[00:01:52]</a>.
4.  Right-click on "YouTube" and click "Uninstall" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:57">[00:01:57]</a>.
