This article details how to [[creating_posts_on_youtube_using_mobile | create posts on YouTube]] directly from a mobile device, applicable to both Android and iPhone <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:03">[00:00:03]</a>.

## Prerequisites

Before creating a post, ensure the following:
*   The YouTube app is downloaded and installed on your device <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:29">[00:00:29]</a>. Users can download it from the App Store for iPhone or Google Play for Android <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:34">[00:00:34]</a>.
*   Log in to the YouTube app with the official account you wish to use for publishing <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>.

## How to Create a Post

Follow these steps to publish a post on YouTube from your mobile device:
1.  Open the YouTube app and navigate to the video camera icon located at the top right of the screen <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:03">[00:01:03]</a>.
2.  Upon tapping the icon, a menu will appear providing options to [[uploading_videos_and_live_broadcasting_on_youtube | make a video]], start a [[uploading_videos_and_live_broadcasting_on_youtube | live broadcast]], or create a "publication" (post) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>.
3.  Select the "publish" option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:15">[00:01:15]</a>.
4.  Write the content of your post in the provided text field <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:17">[00:01:17]</a>.
5.  Optionally, you can [[adding_photos_and_surveys_to_youtube_posts | add photos]] or [[adding_photos_and_surveys_to_youtube_posts | include surveys]] directly from your mobile device <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:39">[00:01:39]</a>.
6.  Once your post is ready, tap the "publish" button <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>.

## Post Publication

After following these steps, the post will be successfully created <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:54">[00:01:54]</a> and will be visible to your followers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:02">[00:02:02]</a>.
