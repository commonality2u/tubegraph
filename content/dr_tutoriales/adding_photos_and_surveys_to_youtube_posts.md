This guide outlines how to [[creating_posts_on_youtube_using_mobile | create posts on YouTube]] from a mobile device, including the option to add photos and surveys directly from your phone <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:00">[00:05:00]</a>. This process is applicable for both Android and iPhone devices <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:00">[00:08:00]</a>.

## Prerequisites
To begin, ensure you have the YouTube application downloaded on your device <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:29:00">[00:29:00]</a>. If not, you can download it from the App Store (for iOS) or Google Play (for Android) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:34:00">[00:34:00]</a>.

## Steps to Create a Post with Media
1.  **Open the YouTube App**: Once the app is downloaded, open it on your mobile device <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:45:00">[00:45:00]</a>.
2.  **Log In**: Ensure you are registered and logged in with your official YouTube account from which you wish to publish <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:50:00">[00:50:00]</a>.
3.  **Initiate a New Post**: Navigate to the video camera icon located at the top right of the screen <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:05:00">[01:05:00]</a>. This will present options to create a video, start a live broadcast, or make a publication <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:10:00">[01:10:00]</a>.
4.  **Select "Publication"**: Choose the "publication" option to begin creating your post <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:15:00">[01:15:00]</a>.
5.  **Compose Your Post**: Type the desired text for your publication <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:26:00">[01:26:00]</a>.
6.  **Add Media (Photos or Surveys)**: While composing your post, options will appear below the text input area on your mobile phone, allowing you to add photos or surveys <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:41:00">[01:41:00]</a>.
7.  **Publish Your Post**: After composing your text and adding any desired media, tap "publish" to finalize and share your post <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:37:00">[01:37:00]</a>.

Once published, the post will be visible to your followers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:57:00">[01:57:00]</a>.
