This guide outlines the process of transferring Bitcoin (BTC) from a Coinbase account to a Binance account. The transfer is recommended to leverage Binance's capabilities, as it offers more options for reinvestment compared to Coinbase <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:14">[00:00:14]</a>.

## Why Transfer from Coinbase to Binance?

Binance is considered a more effective broker for cryptocurrencies as it allows users to reinvest in various ways <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:14">[00:00:14]</a>. Coinbase, on the other hand, does not offer the same reinvestment opportunities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:23">[00:00:23]</a>. Transferring cryptocurrencies from Coinbase to Binance is advised to gain profitability <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>. New users can create a Binance account using a provided code to receive a 10% discount on commissions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>.

## Steps to Transfer Bitcoin

The transfer process involves obtaining a deposit address from Binance and then sending the Bitcoin from Coinbase to that address.

### 1. Obtaining the Binance Deposit Address

To receive Bitcoin in your Binance account, you need to generate a deposit address:
*   Navigate to your Binance account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a>.
*   Go to the "Wallet" section and select "Overview" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:50">[00:01:50]</a>.
*   Click on the "Deposit" option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:55">[00:01:55]</a>.
*   Choose "Deposit Crypto" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:01">[00:02:01]</a>.
*   Select "Bitcoin" (BTC) as the cryptocurrency to deposit <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:27">[00:02:27]</a>.
*   The system will generate a unique QR code and a corresponding wallet address. This address will change depending on the cryptocurrency chosen <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:37">[00:02:37]</a>.
*   Carefully copy this address, as it will be used in Coinbase <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:40">[00:03:40]</a>.

### 2. Sending Bitcoin from Coinbase

Once you have the Binance deposit address, you can initiate the transfer from Coinbase. The process can be done via mobile or computer.

#### On Mobile Device (Coinbase App)
*   Access the Coinbase application <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:08">[00:03:08]</a>.
*   Go to the Bitcoin wallet section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:20">[00:03:20]</a>.
*   To send, locate the "send" icon (often represented by a paper plane or arrow) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:10">[00:04:10]</a>.
*   Enter the amount of Bitcoin you wish to send <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:15">[00:04:15]</a>.
*   If using the QR code, use the camera icon within the Coinbase app to scan the Binance deposit QR code <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:42">[00:03:42]</a>.

#### On Computer (Coinbase Website)
*   Access your Coinbase account on the website <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:08">[00:01:08]</a>.
*   Select the "Send & Receive" option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:28">[00:01:28]</a>.
*   Choose the amount of Bitcoin you want to transfer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:45">[00:04:45]</a>. You can choose to send all available Bitcoin <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:50">[00:04:50]</a>.
*   Paste the copied Binance wallet address into the recipient field <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:53">[00:04:53]</a>.
*   Add an optional note for the transaction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:59">[00:04:59]</a>.
*   Click "Continue" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:05">[00:05:05]</a>.
*   Review the transfer details, including the commission fee <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:16">[00:05:16]</a>. A commission of approximately 1 euro might be charged for the transfer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:24">[00:05:24]</a>.
*   Confirm the transfer by entering a verification code sent to your mobile phone <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:30">[00:05:30]</a>.
*   After confirmation, the balance will disappear from your Coinbase wallet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:38">[00:05:38]</a>.

### 3. Confirmation on Binance

*   The transaction typically takes about 10 minutes to process <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:45">[00:05:45]</a>.
*   You will receive a confirmation message on your mobile phone <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:07">[00:06:07]</a>.
*   On Binance, check the transaction history. The deposited amount will appear in your fiat and spot wallet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:12">[00:06:12]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:25">[00:06:25]</a>.
*   You can also check your email for a confirmation of the transaction from Coinbase to Binance, which can serve as collateral in case of issues <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:47">[00:06:47]</a>.

Once the Bitcoin is in your Binance account, it can be used within the platform as desired <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:39">[00:06:39]</a>.
