## Buying BNB on a Computer

To buy BNB on a computer:
1.  **Navigate to Markets**: From the Binance homepage, go to the "markets" section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:32">[00:01:32]</a>.
2.  **Search for BNB**: In the markets section, search for the cryptocurrency "BNB" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:44">[00:01:44]</a>. BNB Coin is listed as the fourth in capitalization <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:51">[00:01:51]</a>.
3.  **Select a Trading Pair**: Choose a trading pair for BNB, such as BNB/USD (which uses a cryptocurrency imitating the dollar's value) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:08">[00:02:08]</a> or BNB/EUR <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:54">[00:02:54]</a>.
4.  **Place an Order**:
    *   Focus on the "place order" section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:06">[00:03:06]</a>.
    *   Binance offers various order types, including limit, market, and OCO (One Cancels the Other) orders <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:16">[00:03:16]</a>. For a real-time purchase, select a **market order** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:47">[00:03:47]</a>.
    *   Enter the desired amount of BNB to buy, or the total amount in euros/USD you wish to spend <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:55">[00:03:55]</a>.
    *   Confirm the purchase by clicking "buy BNB" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:09">[00:04:09]</a>.

### Order Types
The video explains the market order but notes that other order types (limit, OCO) are detailed in a separate tutorial <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:22">[00:03:22]</a>. It is recommended to familiarize oneself with these different order types <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:30">[00:03:30]</a>.

## Buying BNB on a Mobile Phone

To buy BNB using the [[using_binance_mobile_app_for_trading | Binance mobile app]]:
1.  **Download and Open App**: Ensure the [[using_binance_mobile_app_for_trading | Binance app]] is downloaded and open it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:34">[00:04:34]</a>.
2.  **Navigate to Markets**: From the homepage, tap on the "markets" section in the submenu at the bottom <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:43">[00:04:43]</a>.
3.  **Search for BNB**: Use the magnifying glass icon to search for "BNB" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:53">[00:04:53]</a>.
4.  **Select a Trading Pair**: Choose an exchange pair, such as BNB with the Euro <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:07">[00:05:07]</a>.
5.  **Place an Order**:
    *   Tap the "buying" part <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:29">[00:05:29]</a>.
    *   Select the desired order type (market order for immediate purchase).
    *   Enter the total amount in euros you wish to spend (e.g., 1000 euros) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:57">[00:05:57]</a>.
    *   Click "buy BNB" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:04">[00:06:04]</a>. The transaction will only be successful if sufficient funds are available <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:07">[00:06:07]</a>.

## Managing Purchased BNB

After purchasing BNB, it will appear in your **Spot Wallet** within the "wallet" section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:19">[00:06:19]</a>.
