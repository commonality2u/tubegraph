## Account Types

Revolut primarily offers two main types of accounts:
*   **Personal Account**: This is the focus of the tutorial and is intended for individuals <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:12">[00:01:12]</a>.
*   **Business Account**: This type is specifically dedicated to companies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:15">[00:01:15]</a>.

The tutorial specifically focuses on [[types_of_payoneer_accounts_and_their_differences | personal accounts]] for natural persons <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:21">[00:01:21]</a>.

## Individual Account Plans

Within personal accounts, Revolut offers [[different_plans_and_pricing_of_revolut | different plans]] with varying [[Features and benefits of Revolut | features and benefits]] and pricing <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:49">[00:02:49]</a>, including:
*   Standard
*   Plus
*   Premium
*   Metal

These plans determine the extent of [[overview_of_revolut_app_features | features and benefits]] users can access <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:51">[00:02:51]</a>.

### Plan Pricing

As of the video's recording, the monthly costs for the plans are:
*   **Standard**: Free <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:53">[00:06:53]</a>
*   **Plus**: 3 euros <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:54">[00:06:54]</a>
*   **Premium**: 8 euros <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:55">[00:06:55]</a>
*   **Metal**: 14 euros (the most expensive) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:57">[00:06:57]</a>

### Features by Plan

Different plans offer varied benefits, especially concerning [[International transfers with Revolut | international transfers]] and other services:

*   **International Transfers**:
    *   For the first 1,000 euros (or equivalent in pounds) converted to another currency and sent to another bank account in another country, there is no commission charged by Revolut for any plan <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:29">[00:03:29]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:32">[00:03:32]</a>.
    *   For amounts exceeding 1,000 euros:
        *   **Standard Account**: Commissions apply <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:41">[00:03:41]</a>. The standard plan includes 1,000 pounds in free conversions per month <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:16">[00:04:16]</a>. After this limit, a charge of 0.50 pounds applies per transfer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:54">[00:13:54]</a>.
        *   **Premium and Metal Accounts**: No commissions are charged for exceeding the 1,000-euro limit <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:43">[00:03:43]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:46">[00:03:46]</a>. Metal accounts offer unlimited international transfers without commissions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:50">[00:12:50]</a>.
        *   **Plus Account**: Allows one free international transfer per month, then 0.50 pounds per transfer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:43">[00:13:43]</a>.

*   **ATM Withdrawals**:
    *   **Metal Account**: Double withdrawals at ATMs compared to other plans <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:29">[00:13:29]</a>. Historically, this included approximately 800 pounds per month in free withdrawals <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:32">[00:13:32]</a>.
    *   **Premium Account**: Allows up to 400 pounds in ATM withdrawals without fees <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:35">[00:13:35]</a>.
    *   **Standard Account**: Allows up to 30 euros in ATM withdrawals without commission <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:45">[00:12:45]</a>.

*   **Cashback**:
    *   **Metal Account**: Offers 0.01% cashback on local transactions within Europe and 1% outside Europe <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:35">[00:12:35]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:38">[00:12:38]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:39">[00:12:39]</a>.

*   **Travel Protection/Insurance**:
    *   **Metal Account**: Provides significant coverage and insurance for travel, deemed highly useful for travel purposes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:06">[00:13:06]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:11">[00:13:11]</a>.
    *   **Plus and Premium Accounts**: Offer travel insurance, which is useful <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:15">[00:14:15]</a>. The Plus plan does not offer travel insurance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:19">[00:14:19]</a>.

*   **Interest on Savings**:
    *   **Plus Account**: Users can earn interest on savings, potentially up to 0.40% annual interest <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:21">[00:14:21]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:25">[00:14:25]</a>.
    *   **Metal Account**: Offers potentially higher annual interest rates, up to 0.65% paid daily on investments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:28">[00:14:28]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:32">[00:14:32]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:35">[00:14:35]</a>.

*   **Investment Features**:
    *   All plans, including Standard, allow the option to buy cryptocurrencies and gold <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:39">[00:05:39]</a>.
    *   **Metal Account**: Mentions investments in the stock market without commissions, though this is stated to be relative <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:01">[00:13:01]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:03">[00:13:03]</a>.
