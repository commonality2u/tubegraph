This tutorial outlines the process for [[creating_youtube_posts_on_mobile | creating posts on YouTube]] using a mobile device, applicable for both Android and iPhone users <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:08">[00:00:08]</a>.

## Prerequisites

Before creating a post, ensure you have the YouTube app downloaded on your device <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:29">[00:00:29]</a>. If not, download it from the App Store (for iPhone) or Google Play (for Android) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:34">[00:00:34]</a>.

## Creating a Post

1.  **Open the YouTube App**: Once downloaded, open the YouTube application <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:45">[00:00:45]</a>.
2.  **Log In**: Register or log in with the official YouTube account from which you wish to publish <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>.
3.  **Access Post Creation**: Navigate to the video camera icon located at the top right of the screen <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:05">[00:01:05]</a>. This icon presents options to make a video, a live stream, or a publication (post) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>. Select the "publication" option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:15">[00:01:15]</a>.
4.  **Type Your Post**: Enter the text for your post <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:26">[00:01:26]</a>. For example, "hello how are you" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:29">[00:01:29]</a> or "Have a nice day" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>.
5.  **Publish**: After typing your message, select the "publish" button <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>. The post will then be created and visible to your followers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:54">[00:01:54]</a>.

## Additional Options

While creating a post, you also have the ability to [[adding_photos_and_surveys_to_youtube_posts | add photos or surveys]] from your mobile phone <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:41">[00:01:41]</a>. These options are typically available at the bottom of the screen <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:43">[00:01:43]</a>.
