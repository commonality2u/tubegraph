To begin trading on Binance Futures, you first need to transfer funds from your Spot Wallet to your Futures account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:52:13">[02:52:13]</a>. The primary asset used for trading within Futures is USD (or USDT) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:28:13">[03:28:13]</a>, as it is always exchanged with other cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin and Ethereum <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:34:00">[03:34:00]</a>.

## Step-by-Step Guide

1.  **Navigate to the Wallet Section**: On your mobile device, open the Binance application and go to the "Wallet" section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:56:47">[02:56:47]</a>.
2.  **Access Futures Wallet**: Within the Wallet, select the "Futures" tab <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:59:15">[02:59:15]</a>.
3.  **Initiate Transfer**: Look for the "Transfer" option, which is recommended over "Loan" or "Repayment" due to associated interest <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:19:35">[03:19:35]</a>.
4.  **Specify Transfer Details**:
    *   Ensure you are [[how_to_transfer_cryptocurrencies_on_binance | transferring]] from your "Spot Wallet" to your "Futures Account" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:21:46">[03:21:46]</a>.
    *   The currency must be USD <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:28:13">[03:28:13]</a>.
    *   Enter the amount you wish to transfer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:55:00">[03:55:00]</a>.
5.  **Confirm Transfer**: Confirm the transfer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:59:00">[03:59:00]</a>. Once complete, your USD balance in the Futures wallet will increase <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:02:00">[04:02:00]</a>. Having the required amount in your Futures wallet is essential to [[operating_within_binance_futures_account | operate]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:09:59">[04:09:59]</a>.
