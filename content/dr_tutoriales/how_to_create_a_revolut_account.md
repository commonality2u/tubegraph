## [[overview_of_revolut_app_features | Revolut Features]] Overview
Before [[creating_an_account_on_revolut | an account]], it's helpful to understand the platform's [[overview_of_revolut_app_features | features]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:50:35">[00:50:35]</a>.

### Financial Management
*   **Expense Analysis**: [[managing_finances_with_revolut | Revolut]] allows users to manage their money through expense analysis, categorizing spending by areas like transportation, restaurants, or shopping <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:22:15">[02:22:15]</a>. This feature helps users understand their spending habits and control expenses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:32:00">[02:32:00]</a>.
*   **Discounts**: [[features_and_benefits_of_Revolut | Revolut]] offers various discounts, which may vary depending on the chosen [[different_plans_and_pricing_of_Revolut | plan]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:43:08">[02:43:08]</a>.
*   **Financial Objectives (Vaults)**: The platform allows users to create specific "vaults" for financial goals, such as saving for a trip. Users can set a target amount and regularly deposit money until the goal is reached, promoting controlled saving and efficient money management <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:00:27">[05:00:27]</a>.

### Transfers and Exchange
*   **[[international_transfers_with_revolut | International Transfers]]**: [[features_and_benefits_of_Revolut | Revolut]] is known for facilitating [[international_transfers_with_revolut | international transfers]] with low commissions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:37:37">[01:37:37]</a>. While it advertises "no hidden commissions," it does not mean there are no commissions at all <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:00:30">[03:00:30]</a>.
    *   The first 1,000 euros transferred in another currency to another bank account in another country are free of commission for [[Revolut account types and plans | Standard account]] holders <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:27:06">[03:27:06]</a>.
    *   [[Revolut account types and plans | Premium]] and [[Revolut account types and plans | Metal account]] holders are not charged commissions on international transfers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:43:40">[03:43:40]</a>.
    *   For transfers exceeding 1,000 euros with a [[Revolut account types and plans | Standard account]], commissions apply <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:40:48">[03:40:48]</a>.
*   **Real Exchange Rate**: Users can obtain the real exchange rate when abroad in over 150 currencies without hidden commissions, enabling payments in foreign countries without additional charges <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:38:15">[06:38:15]</a>.

### Other Features
*   **[[investing_in_stocks_and_cryptocurrencies_using_revolut | Investments]]**: [[features_and_benefits_of_Revolut | Revolut]] offers the option to buy cryptocurrencies and gold <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:39:07">[05:39:07]</a>.
