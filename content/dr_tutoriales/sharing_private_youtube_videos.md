This article explains how to [[creating_private_videos_on_youtube | create and share private videos on YouTube]] so that only specific individuals can view them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:03">[00:00:03]</a>.

### Setting a Video's Visibility to Private

To begin, access the YouTube platform and navigate to [[using_youtube_studio_for_video_management | YouTube Channel Studio]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:24">[00:00:24]</a>. Within YouTube Studio, go to the "Video" section of your channel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:39">[00:00:39]</a>.

1.  **Select the Video**: Choose the video you wish to make private <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>.
2.  **Set Visibility**: In the video's visibility options, select "Private" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>.
    *   A [[setting_video_visibility_to_private | private video]] will not appear on your channel or in YouTube search results <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>. Only individuals you specifically choose will be able to view it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:01">[00:01:01]</a>.

### Sharing a Private Video

Once the video's visibility is set to [[setting_video_visibility_to_private | private]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:08">[00:01:08]</a>:

1.  **Access Video Details**: Go to the details of the video (often indicated by a pen icon) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:11">[00:01:11]</a>.
2.  **Initiate Private Sharing**: Locate the three dots in the top right corner and select "Share privately" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:19">[00:01:19]</a>.
3.  **Add Email Addresses**: A new tab will open, allowing you to add email addresses to a mailing list <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:26">[00:01:26]</a>. These are the individuals to whom you can [[emailing_private_video_links | email the video]] so they can view it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:30">[00:01:30]</a>.
4.  **Send Email**: Recipients will receive an email from YouTube containing a direct link to the private video <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:38">[00:01:38]</a>.
5.  **Recipient Experience**: The email will display the channel's name and an invitation to watch the video, along with the link <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:05">[00:02:05]</a>. Clicking this link will open the video in a new tab <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:18">[00:02:18]</a>.
