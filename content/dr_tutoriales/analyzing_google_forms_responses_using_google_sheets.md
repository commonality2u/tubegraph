## Accessing and Managing Responses

Once a [[creating_a_form_with_google_forms | Google Form]] has been created and responses have been submitted, you can access them within the form interface itself <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:35">[00:00:35]</a>.

To [[how_to_view_responses_in_google_forms | view responses]] and manage settings:
*   Navigate to the "Responses" tab within your Google Form <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:35">[00:00:35]</a>.
*   Click on the three dots menu to reveal options such as:
    *   Receiving email notifications for new responses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:40">[00:00:40]</a>.
    *   Selecting where responses are sent <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>.
    *   Unlinking the form <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:45">[00:00:45]</a>.
    *   [[exporting_google_forms_responses_to_csv_or_excel | Downloading responses in CSV format]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:45">[00:00:45]</a>.
    *   Opening responses directly in Google Sheets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:47">[00:00:47]</a>.

## [[visualizing_individual_and_summary_responses_in_google_forms | Visualizing Responses Within Google Forms]]

Google Forms offers different ways to [[how_to_view_responses_in_google_forms | view responses]] directly:

*   **Summary View:** This view provides an aggregated analysis of all responses, displaying charts and summaries for each question, making it easy to quickly grasp overall trends for [[using_different_question_types_in_google_forms | different question types]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:51">[00:00:51]</a>. This view is often preferred for seeing all responses at a glance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:22">[00:01:22]</a>.
*   **Question View:** You can analyze responses question by question, seeing how everyone answered a specific query <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>.
*   **Individual View:** This allows you to review each person's complete submission individually, useful for detailed examination of specific responses or survey completions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:13">[00:01:13]</a>.

## Exporting Responses to Google Sheets

For more in-depth analysis, you can export your [[managing_survey_responses_in_google_forms | Google Forms responses]] to a Google Sheet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:35">[00:01:35]</a>.

To do this:
1.  Go to the "Responses" tab in your form <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:35">[00:01:35]</a>.
2.  Click the green Google Sheets icon or select "View in Sheets" from the three dots menu <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:39">[00:01:39]</a>.

Once opened in Google Sheets, the responses are organized with:
*   A timestamp for each submission, indicating the date, hour, and minute of response <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:40">[00:01:40]</a>.
*   Columns for each question from your form, displaying the corresponding answers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:43">[00:01:43]</a>.

This integration allows for advanced data manipulation, filtering, and custom visualizations beyond what's available directly in Google Forms <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:46">[00:01:46]</a>. You can also [[exporting_google_forms_responses_to_csv_or_excel | link this data to Excel]] if needed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:52">[00:01:52]</a>.
