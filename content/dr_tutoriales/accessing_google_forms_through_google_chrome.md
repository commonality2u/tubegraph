## Steps to Access Google Forms

The process to access [[creating_surveys_in_google_forms | Google Forms]] is described as very simple <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:25">[00:00:25]</a>:

1.  Navigate to the right side of your [[using_google_chrome_for_youtube | Google Chrome]] browser window <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>.
2.  Locate the part with circles, typically representing Google apps <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:29">[00:00:29]</a>.
3.  Find the "Forms" icon, which is purple <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:32">[00:00:32]</a>.
4.  Clicking on the "Forms" icon will lead you directly to the [[creating_surveys_in_google_forms | Google Forms]] interface <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:35">[00:00:35]</a>.

> "You go to the right here. In the part the Circles these from here, you go to Find the part directly of forms that are purple, Ok? You go to forms and you leads directly to what We're looking at it below." <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>
