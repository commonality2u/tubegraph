This tutorial outlines the process for depositing capital into the [[wise_platform_tutorial | Wise platform]] to manage funds, change currencies, and send money internationally with low [[commissions_and_fees_on_wise | commissions]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>. This guide covers various methods to [[how_to_deposit_money_into_wise | deposit money into Wise]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:05">[00:00:05]</a>. The process is identical whether using a PC or a mobile device <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:09">[00:01:09]</a>.

## Prerequisites

Before depositing, it is essential to have an account already created and verified on the [[wise_platform_tutorial | Wise platform]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:21">[00:01:21]</a>.

## Initiating a Deposit

1.  **Access Account**: Once logged in, your balance will be displayed in various currencies such as euros or British pounds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:29">[00:01:29]</a>.
2.  **Select Currency**: Choose the currency you wish to deposit, for example, euros, by clicking on it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:39">[00:01:39]</a>.
3.  **Add Funds**: Click the "Add" button to initiate the deposit <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:46">[00:01:46]</a>.
4.  **Enter Amount**: Specify the amount of money you intend to deposit, such as 200 euros <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:58">[00:01:58]</a>. The option to receive salary or pension via a bank account is also available <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:10">[00:02:10]</a>.
5.  **Continue**: Proceed to the payment section by pressing "Continue" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:18">[00:02:18]</a>.

## Available Deposit Methods

Wise offers several methods for depositing funds, each with different [[commissions_and_fees_on_wise | commissions]] and processing times <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:24">[00:02:24]</a>:

*   **[[using_bank_transfer_paypal_and_cards_with_wise | Debit Card / Credit Card]]**:
    *   **Commission**: Involves an associated commission (e.g., 0.82 euros for a 200 euro deposit) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:31">[00:02:31]</a>.
    *   **Speed**: Transactions are typically processed in a matter of seconds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:49">[00:03:49]</a>.

*   **[[using_bank_transfer_paypal_and_cards_with_wise | PayPal]]**:
    *   **Commission**: Usually has a similar commission to card transfers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:44">[00:02:44]</a>.
    *   **Process**: Requires entering your [[using_bank_transfer_paypal_and_cards_with_wise | PayPal]] account details and ensuring sufficient funds are available <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:27">[00:03:27]</a>.

*   **[[using_bank_transfer_paypal_and_cards_with_wise | Bank Transfer]]**:
    *   **Commission**: This method is free <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:50">[00:02:50]</a>.
    *   **Process**: You will be provided with the bank details (e.g., IBAN data) and a specific reference to include with your transfer. Wise does not directly access your bank account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:00">[00:04:00]</a>. You must confirm you are the account holder and perform the transfer via online banking <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:14">[00:04:14]</a>.
    *   **Speed**: Generally slower than card or [[using_bank_transfer_paypal_and_cards_with_wise | PayPal]] deposits <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:05">[00:03:05]</a>.

*   **Other Methods**:
    *   Options like "Behind Lee" (for electronic accounts) and "iDEAL" may also be available <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:54">[00:02:54]</a>.

### Choosing a Method

The choice of method depends on the urgency of the transfer and the cost preference <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:02">[00:03:02]</a>. While [[using_bank_transfer_paypal_and_cards_with_wise | bank transfers]] are free, [[using_bank_transfer_paypal_and_cards_with_wise | card]] or [[using_bank_transfer_paypal_and_cards_with_wise | PayPal]] deposits incur a small fee but are faster <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:07">[00:03:07]</a>.

## Completing the Deposit

After selecting a method, you will enter the necessary payment information. For [[using_bank_transfer_paypal_and_cards_with_wise | card]] payments, this involves entering the card number directly on the Wise platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:33">[00:04:33]</a>. Once completed, your money will be immediately available in your Wise balance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:40">[00:04:40]</a>.
