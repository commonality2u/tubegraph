This guide outlines the process of transferring Ethereum from your Coinbase Wallet to your Kraken account, with the ultimate goal of [[transferring_cryptocurrency_to_a_bank_account | withdrawing money to your bank account]]. The tutorial is performed on a mobile device but the steps are identical for Android, iOS, or desktop computers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:23">[00:00:23]</a>.

## Why Use Kraken for Withdrawals?

[[Using Kraken for cryptocurrency transactions | Kraken]] is preferred over Coinbase for bank withdrawals because Coinbase charges higher fees for this type of transaction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:01">[00:01:01]</a>. A referral link for Kraken is available in the first comment of the original video, offering a 20% discount on commissions for transactions, withdrawals, or capital deposits <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:05">[00:01:05]</a>.

## Process Overview

The transfer involves first depositing Ethereum into your Kraken account, then from Kraken, you can proceed to [[transferring_cryptocurrency_to_a_bank_account | withdraw funds to your bank account]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>.

### Step 1: Obtain Ethereum Deposit Address from Kraken

1.  Open the Kraken application <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:15">[00:01:15]</a>.
2.  Navigate to the "portfolio" section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:20">[00:01:20]</a>.
3.  Select "Ethereum" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:21">[00:01:21]</a>.
4.  Tap on the three dots at the top of the screen <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:27">[00:01:27]</a>.
5.  Choose "Deposit" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:29">[00:01:29]</a>.
6.  Confirm that the deposit will be done via the Ethereum blockchain, which is the network hosting the cryptocurrency in your Coinbase Wallet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:30">[00:01:30]</a>.
7.  The estimated deposit time is approximately 14 minutes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:39">[00:01:39]</a>.
8.  Copy the generated Ethereum deposit address from Kraken.

### Step 2: Send Ethereum from Coinbase Wallet

1.  Open your Coinbase Wallet.
2.  Locate your Ethereum balance (e.g., $4.16 in Ethereum was shown) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:48">[00:00:48]</a>.
3.  Initiate a "Send" transaction for your Ethereum <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:10">[00:02:10]</a>.
4.  Select to send "all" of your Ethereum <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:13">[00:02:13]</a>.
5.  Click "Next" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:15">[00:02:15]</a>.
6.  Paste the Ethereum deposit address copied from Kraken into the recipient field <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:16">[00:02:16]</a>.
7.  Review the transaction details, including the suggested network and commission costs, which were approximately $7.55-$7.59 USD to $6.52 USD <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:25">[00:02:25]</a>.
8.  Confirm the transaction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:37">[00:02:37]</a>.
9.  The transaction will then be executed, and the Ethereum will be sent <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:38">[00:02:38]</a>.

### Step 3: Verify Deposit on Kraken

1.  After the transaction is confirmed from Coinbase, your Ethereum balance in Coinbase Wallet will disappear <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:46">[00:02:46]</a>.
2.  Return to your Kraken account.
3.  Monitor your Kraken portfolio, where the specified quantity of Ethereum should appear once the transaction is complete <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:54">[00:02:54]</a>.
4.  Once the Ethereum is visible in your Kraken account, the transfer is successful <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:07">[00:03:07]</a>.
