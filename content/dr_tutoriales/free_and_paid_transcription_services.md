## Free Transcription Services

The video demonstrates the use of two completely free online tools for [[transcribing_audio_to_text_using_online_tools | audio transcription]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>. These tools typically allow users to upload audio files, such as recordings from mobile phones via WhatsApp <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:32">[00:02:32]</a>, and have them transcribed free of charge <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:45">[00:03:45]</a>.

### How Free Tools Work
To use a free online transcription tool:
1.  Navigate to a voice-to-text or online transcription website <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:27">[00:02:27]</a>.
2.  Import an audio file, for example, a WhatsApp audio recording <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:37">[00:02:37]</a>.
3.  Set the playback speed to normal, as very fast speeds can lead to inaccurate transcription <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:54">[00:02:54]</a>.
4.  Play the audio and observe the transcription as it appears on the screen <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:00">[00:03:00]</a>.

### Quality and Limitations of Free Services
Free tools are generally reliable, often achieving around 80-90% successful words in their [[comparison_of_transcription_service_quality | transcriptions]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:35">[00:03:35]</a>. However, they are not designed for large-scale tasks, such as transcribing an entire movie <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:51">[00:03:51]</a>.

## Paid Transcription Services

Many online transcription services offer free trials, but require payment for continued or more extensive use <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:47">[00:00:47]</a>. Payment is often necessary because these services require significant computational power to function <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:57">[00:00:57]</a>.

### Pricing Models and Examples
Pricing for paid services can vary:
*   **Happy Scribe**: Allows users to start for free, but pricing is typically based on the number of minutes or words transcribed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:12">[00:01:12]</a>.
*   **Speech Text Artificial Intelligence**: Charges approximately $10 per month for 180 minutes of transcription <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:26">[00:01:26]</a>.
*   **Bizz**: Also has limitations, such as offering 30 minutes of subtitles for free, then requiring payment for more <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:41">[00:01:41]</a>.

### Advantages of Paid Services
While free tools are convenient for basic tasks, moving to paid platforms for transcription offers a significantly higher [[comparison_of_transcription_service_quality | quality of transcription]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:08">[00:04:08]</a>. This makes paid services suitable for professional or more demanding transcription needs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:01">[00:04:01]</a>.
