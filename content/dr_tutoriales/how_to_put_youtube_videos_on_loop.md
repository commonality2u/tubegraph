This tutorial explains how to put videos on [[looping_videos_and_music_on_youtube | loop]] on the YouTube platform, allowing them to restart automatically once they finish playing <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:05">[00:00:05]</a>. This feature is useful for continuous [[benefits_of_looping_youtube_videos | listening to music or watching videos]] without manual intervention <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>.

## How to Activate Loop Mode

To [[steps_to_activate_and_deactivate_loop_mode_on_youtube | activate loop mode]] for a YouTube video:

1.  Access any YouTube video, preferably using a reliable browser like [[using_google_chrome_to_loop_youtube_videos | Google Chrome]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:36">[00:00:36]</a>.
2.  Once on the video page, right-click anywhere on the video frame <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>.
3.  A dropdown menu will appear <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>.
4.  Click on the "Loop" option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:13">[00:01:13]</a>.

With loop mode activated, the video will automatically restart once it reaches its end <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a>.

## How to Deactivate Loop Mode

To [[steps_to_activate_and_deactivate_loop_mode_on_youtube | deactivate loop mode]] and stop a video from looping:

1.  While the video is playing, right-click on the screen again <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:45">[00:01:45]</a>.
2.  The dropdown menu will reappear <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:47">[00:01:47]</a>.
3.  Click on the "Loop" option once more <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:49">[00:01:49]</a>.

After deactivating loop mode, the video will end and pause playback when it reaches its conclusion <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:57">[00:01:57]</a>.
