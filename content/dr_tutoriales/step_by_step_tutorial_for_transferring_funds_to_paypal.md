This tutorial explains how to withdraw cryptocurrency funds from a platform (like Coinbase, as shown in the video) to a [[using_paypal_for_receiving_payments | PayPal account]]. ## Overview
The process involves converting your cryptocurrency to fiat money (like Euros) and then initiating a withdrawal to your linked [[using_paypal_for_receiving_payments | PayPal account]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:55">[00:03:55]</a>. The video explicitly mentions a separate tutorial detailing the [[stepbystep_guide_for_money_transfer_process | withdrawal process]] to [[using_paypal_for_receiving_payments | PayPal]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>.

### Why Convert to Fiat Money?
Cryptocurrencies like Polygon (MATIC) cannot be directly stored or withdrawn to conventional banks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:40">[00:01:40]</a>. Therefore, the first step is to convert your crypto assets into a national currency (fiat money) such as Euros, which can then be withdrawn <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:57">[00:01:57]</a>.

## Withdrawal Process Highlights

While this specific video refers to another tutorial for the full step-by-step guide on [[withdraw_money_from_coinbase_to_paypal | withdrawing money from Coinbase to PayPal]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>, it provides key information regarding the [[how_to_deposit_funds_into_a_paypal_account | PayPal withdrawal]] option:

*   **Conversion to Fiat**: Before withdrawing, ensure your cryptocurrency is converted into a supported fiat currency (e.g., Euros) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:57">[00:01:57]</a>.
*   **Withdrawal Option**: Within your account (e.g., Coinbase), navigate to the euro section and select the "withdraw funds" option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:10">[00:03:10]</a>.
*   **Selecting PayPal**: Choose [[using_paypal_for_receiving_payments | PayPal]] as your withdrawal method <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:55">[00:03:55]</a>.

### Costs and Timeframe
*   **Commissions**: Withdrawing funds to [[using_paypal_for_receiving_payments | PayPal]] is described as "totally free" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:55">[00:03:55]</a>.
*   **Transfer Time**: A [[transfer_money_from_bank_to_paypal | PayPal transfer]] typically takes about one day to complete <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:15">[00:04:15]</a>.
*   **Confirmation**: You will receive an email confirmation to the email address associated with your account once the withdrawal is processed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:17">[00:04:17]</a>.
