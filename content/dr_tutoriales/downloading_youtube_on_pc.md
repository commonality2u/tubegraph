To download and install the YouTube application directly onto your PC, follow these steps <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:00">[00:00:00]</a>. This process creates a desktop application for easy access.

## Installing YouTube on PC

1.  **Open a Web Browser**
    You can use any search engine or browser such as [[using_google_chrome_for_youtube | Google Chrome]], Edge, Opera, or Firefox <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:07">[00:00:07]</a>. In this example, [[using_google_chrome_for_youtube | Google Chrome]] is used <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:07">[00:00:07]</a>. Open a new tab <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>.
2.  **Access YouTube**
    Navigate directly to the YouTube website <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>.
3.  **Initiate Installation**
    Look for an option, usually at the top of the browser window, that displays a download symbol and says "Install YouTube" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:29">[00:00:29]</a>. Click this option to install the application <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:33">[00:00:33]</a>. The application will begin downloading and will be ready quickly <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:39">[00:00:39]</a>.

### Accessing the Installed Application

Once downloaded, YouTube creates a shortcut on your desktop <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:48">[00:00:48]</a>. You can also find it by typing "YouTube" into your computer's search bar <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:53">[00:00:53]</a>.

To keep it readily accessible, you can pin it to your taskbar:
1.  Right-click on the YouTube icon <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:58">[00:00:58]</a>.
2.  Select "anchor to the taskbar" (or "Pin to taskbar") <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>.
The icon will then appear on your taskbar <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:02">[00:01:02]</a>. When you open the application, it will automatically register with your Google account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>.

## Uninstalling YouTube from PC

If you need to [[uninstalling_youtube_from_pc | uninstall YouTube]] from your PC:
1.  Close the YouTube application <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:41">[00:01:41]</a>.
2.  Open the Control Panel on your computer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:41">[00:01:41]</a>. You can search for "Control Panel" in your system's search bar <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:43">[00:01:43]</a>.
3.  Within the Control Panel, click on "Uninstall a program" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:50">[00:01:50]</a>.
4.  Locate "YouTube" in the list of programs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:52">[00:01:52]</a>.
5.  Right-click on "YouTube" and select "Uninstall" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:57">[00:01:57]</a>.
