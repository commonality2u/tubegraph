## How to Install TikTok on PC

To [[stepbystep_guide_to_installing_tiktok_on_a_computer | install TikTok on your computer]]:

1.  **Open a Search Engine**
    Begin by opening any search engine such as Google Chrome, Firefox, Opera, or Microsoft Edge <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:14">[00:00:14]</a>.
2.  **Search for TikTok**
    In the search engine, directly type "TikTok" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>.
3.  **Access the Official Website**
    Navigate to the main and official TikTok website <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>. It's crucial to ensure you are on the official site <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:35">[00:00:35]</a>.
4.  **Initiate Installation**
    Once on the TikTok website, look for an option on the right side of the page, typically represented by a computer icon with a downward arrow <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>. This option allows you to [[navigating_tiktoks_official_website_and_app_options | install TikTok]] on your computer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:02">[00:01:02]</a>. Click this option and then select "Install" to begin installing the TikTok app <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:05">[00:01:05]</a>.

## Accessing TikTok After Installation

After installation, TikTok will typically create a direct shortcut on your desktop <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a>.

If you can't find it on your desktop:
*   Go to your taskbar <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:19">[00:01:19]</a>.
*   Type "TikTok" directly into the search bar <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:20">[00:01:20]</a>. The application will appear <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:22">[00:01:22]</a>.

You can also pin the TikTok application to your taskbar for easy access <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:23">[00:01:23]</a>.

## How to Uninstall TikTok from a Computer

If you wish to [[how_to_uninstall_tiktok_from_a_computer | uninstall TikTok]] from your computer:

1.  **Open Control Panel**
    Go to your computer's search bar and type "Control" to find and open the Control Panel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:34">[00:01:34]</a>.
2.  **Navigate to Programs**
    Within the Control Panel, locate and select "Programs" or "Uninstall a program" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:41">[00:01:41]</a>.
3.  **Uninstall TikTok**
    Find "TikTok" in the alphabetical list of programs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:44">[00:01:44]</a>. Once found, you can proceed to uninstall it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:47">[00:01:47]</a>.
