Advcash (also referred to as "Advcash" or "Azkar" in the transcript) is an electronic or digital wallet platform designed for managing capital online <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>. It functions similarly to platforms like PayPal, but distinguishes itself by offering extensive cryptocurrency trading capabilities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:59">[00:00:59]</a>.

## Core Functionalities

Advcash provides users with a comprehensive suite of features for managing digital and fiat currencies:
*   **Capital Management** The platform allows users to manage their capital online <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>.
*   **Currency Support** It combines national fiat currencies within the same ecosystem <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:04">[00:02:04]</a>, supporting currencies such as US dollars, euros, rubles, and coins from Kazakhstan, among others <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:49">[00:03:49]</a>. Funds can be transferred to over 200 countries worldwide <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:55">[00:03:55]</a>.
*   **Cryptocurrency Operations** Users can receive, process, and send various cryptocurrencies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:31">[00:02:31]</a>, as well as buy and sell them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:52">[00:01:52]</a>. It supports several blockchain networks for transfers, including TRC 20 and ERC 20 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:11">[00:05:11]</a>.
*   **Account Types** Both personal and business accounts can be created for free, with no maintenance costs, even during inactivity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:34">[00:02:34]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:06">[00:04:06]</a>.
*   **Virtual and Plastic Cards** Users can request virtual or plastic cards from Advcash to make payments in various businesses worldwide <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:58">[00:03:03]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:36">[00:03:40]</a>. These cards can be recharged and used for withdrawals in any accepted currency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:36">[00:05:43]</a>.
*   **Payment Methods** The platform integrates a wide array of payment methods, including Visa, Mastercard, SEPA transfers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:10">[00:03:12]</a>, PayPal <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:22">[00:14:22]</a>, Sberbank, Skrill, Solana, and others <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:20">[00:15:24]</a>.

### Fees and Commissions
*   **Account Opening/Maintenance**: Free for both personal and business accounts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:06">[00:04:11]</a>.
*   **Transfers between Advcash users**: No commission <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:19">[00:04:23]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:47">[00:17:52]</a>.
*   **SEPA transfers**: A €1 fee for deposits <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:25">[00:04:27]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:48">[00:10:02]</a>.
*   **Visa/Mastercard deposits**: Up to 3.5% commission <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:28">[00:04:36]</a>.
*   **Cryptocurrency deposits**: Generally, no commission applied by Advcash itself, as fees are typically charged by the withdrawing exchange <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:14">[00:12:28]</a>.
*   **Withdrawals to Visa/Mastercard**: A minimum commission of €5 plus 2.5% for amounts exceeding €5 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:50">[00:19:55]</a>.
*   **Withdrawals to local banks**: Commissions can be as low as 0.5% depending on the amount and country <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:09">[00:20:15]</a>.
*   **Currency Exchange (Swap)**: While the platform does not explicitly show commissions for swaps, users are advised to verify the exchange rate independently to avoid hidden fees <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:38">[00:16:48]</a>.

## Security Features
Advcash allows users to enable double authentication verification (2FA) via Google Authenticator, email, or mobile phone to enhance account security <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:42">[00:02:46]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:11">[00:17:19]</a>.

## Customer Support
The platform offers a direct chat function to connect with support agents, although communication is primarily in English <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:13">[00:02:22]</a>.

## Partnerships

Advcash has established key collaborations to enhance its services:
*   **Binance** Advcash is known for its collaborations with the Binance platform, enabling users to buy and sell cryptocurrencies with competitive rates and low commissions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:05">[00:01:05]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:57">[00:05:59]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:57">[00:24:02]</a>. Users can connect their Advcash accounts to Binance to facilitate cryptocurrency purchases <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:37">[00:23:42]</a>.
*   **Nexo** Advcash collaborates with the Nexo platform to offer "crypto savings," allowing users to invest their cryptocurrencies and earn up to 8% annualized interest <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:01">[00:09:03]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:25">[00:25:32]</a>. Users can connect their Advcash accounts to Nexo to deposit cryptocurrencies and start earning interest <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:26:00">[00:26:06]</a>.
*   **Local Exchange Services** Advcash partners with major exchange services in countries like Russia and Ukraine to facilitate cash withdrawals <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:03">[00:22:07]</a>.

These partnerships enable Advcash to offer a wide range of services, particularly for those looking to send money internationally to local banks in various countries such as Argentina, Canada, Colombia, Dominican Republic, Egypt, India, and Japan, often with low commissions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:45">[00:20:57]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:21:01">[00:21:09]</a>.# Advcash: Platform Overview, Features, and Partnerships
