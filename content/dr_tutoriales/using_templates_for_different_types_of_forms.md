Google Forms provides various templates to help users quickly create different types of forms <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:53">[00:00:53]</a>. These templates are readily available when creating a new form <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:53">[00:00:53]</a>.

## Accessing Templates

To access Google Forms, navigate to the purple "forms" icon found within the circles section on the right side of the screen <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>. From there, you will see different templates <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:53">[00:00:53]</a>.

## Available Template Examples

Some of the templates offered include:
*   **Confirmation of attendance at an event**: Such as for a wedding <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:57">[00:00:57]</a>.
*   **Scheduling an appointment** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>.
*   **Contact details** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:02">[00:01:02]</a>.
*   **Customer feedback** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:15">[00:01:15]</a>.
*   **Comments on an event** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:15">[00:01:15]</a>.
*   **Order form** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a>.

While templates are useful, a specific template for a general "survey" might not always appear <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:11">[00:01:11]</a>. In such cases, or when a pre-designed template doesn't perfectly fit the need, users can [[how_to_create_a_google_form | create a new form]] from a blank template <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:22">[00:01:22]</a>. This allows for step-by-step [[customizing_google_form_questions | customization]] to build the desired form <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:27">[00:01:27]</a>.
