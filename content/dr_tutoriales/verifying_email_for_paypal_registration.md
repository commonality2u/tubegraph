Email verification is a crucial step in setting up a PayPal account correctly <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:03:00">[01:03:00]</a>. This process ensures that the account is fully functional and secured <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:16:00">[04:16:00]</a>.

## Why Email Verification is Important

When you [[creating_a_paypal_account_without_a_credit_card | create a PayPal account]], you register with an email address <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:00:00">[01:00:00]</a>. This email needs to be verified to confirm your identity and complete the account setup <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:16:00">[04:16:00]</a>. Without verification, you might see notifications in your PayPal account dashboard prompting you to finish setting up your account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:14:00">[04:14:00]</a>.

## How to Verify Your Email

The process involves a few simple steps:

1.  **Receive the Verification Email**: PayPal will send a verification email to the address you provided during registration <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:22:00">[04:22:00]</a>.
2.  **Access Your Email**: Go to your email inbox and locate the email from PayPal <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:33:00">[04:33:00]</a>. It will typically have a subject like "Confirm your email" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:36:00">[04:36:00]</a>.
3.  **Click the Confirmation Link**: Inside the email, you will find a link to confirm your email address <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:39:00">[04:39:00]</a>. Click on this link <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:43:00">[04:43:00]</a>.
4.  **Enter Your Password**: You may be prompted to enter your PayPal account password to confirm that it's you <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:46:00">[04:46:00]</a>.
5.  **Confirmation**: Once you enter your password, your email will be confirmed, and PayPal will check your details <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:51:00">[04:51:00]</a>.

>[!INFO]
>After confirming your email, your PayPal account will be fully set up, even if you haven't [[linking_a_credit_or_debit_card_to_paypal | linked a credit card]] or [[linking_a_credit_or_debit_card_to_paypal | debit card]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:12:00">[05:12:00]</a>.
