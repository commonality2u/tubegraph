PayPal is a widely used platform for managing online transactions, offering utility for both sending and receiving payments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:20">[00:00:20]</a>. It allows users to receive payments from various platforms, store money, and then use those funds to make payments to others, businesses, or in exchanges for sales <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:28">[00:00:28]</a>. Some users may prefer to [[creating_a_paypal_account_without_a_credit_card | create a PayPal account without a credit card]] or debit card, solely for the purpose of receiving payments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:15">[00:00:15]</a>.

## Creating a PayPal Account for Receiving Payments

To set up a PayPal account primarily for receiving payments, follow these steps:

### Accessing the PayPal Page
Access the official PayPal website, often via a [[paypal_account_setup_with_referral_links | referral link]] provided in the first comment of related video tutorials <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:48">[00:00:48]</a>. Upon entering the page, it is necessary to accept cookies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:18">[00:01:18]</a>.

### Account Type Selection
When offered the option to create a free account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:20">[00:01:20]</a>, you will choose between a personal account or a business account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>:
*   **Personal Account**: Recommended for essential functionalities such as buying, receiving money, or returning money without sharing payment information <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:44:00">[01:44:00]</a>. This is sufficient if the main goal is to receive payments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:04:00">[02:04:00]</a>.
*   **Business Account**: Suitable for buying and selling mass products, preparing invoices, and selling to anyone anywhere <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:53:00">[01:53:00]</a>.

### Account Setup Steps
1.  **Mobile Phone Number**: Enter a mobile phone number for verification purposes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:15:00">[02:15:00]</a>. A verification code will be sent to this number <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:20:00">[02:20:00]</a>.
2.  **Profile Configuration**: Fill in your email address, first name, last name, and create a password for your PayPal account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:27:00">[02:27:00]</a>. This email and password will be used for future logins <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:36:00">[02:36:00]</a>.
3.  **Billing Address**: Provide your billing address, including date of birth, city, province, and country of residence <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:47:00">[02:47:00]</a>.
4.  **Terms and Conditions**: Confirm that you have read the conditions of use and privacy policy <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:53:00">[02:53:00]</a>.
5.  **Account Creation**: Click "Accept and create account" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:09:00">[03:09:00]</a>.

### Email Verification
After creating the account, it is crucial to [[verifying_email_for_paypal_registration | confirm the email address]] used during registration <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:00:00">[01:00:00]</a>. PayPal will send a confirmation email containing a link <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:21:00">[04:21:00]</a>. Clicking this link will open a new tab where you will be prompted to enter your PayPal password to complete the verification <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:43:00">[04:43:00]</a>. This step fully confirms your PayPal account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:14:00">[05:14:00]</a>.

### No Debit or Credit Card Required for Receiving Payments
It is possible to complete the PayPal account setup and effectively use it for receiving payments without the immediate need to [[linking_a_credit_or_debit_card_to_paypal | link a debit or credit card]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:06">[00:00:06]</a>. While the option to link a card is presented during and after setup, it is not obligatory if your primary purpose is only to receive payments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:22:00">[03:22:00]</a>. After account creation and email verification, your PayPal account will be ready to receive funds even with a zero balance and no linked card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:03:00">[04:03:00]</a>.
