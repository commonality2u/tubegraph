This guide details how to create and [[sharing_youtube_videos_privately | share private videos]] on YouTube, ensuring only selected individuals can view them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:06">[00:00:06]</a>.

## Setting a Video to Private

To begin, access the YouTube platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>.

1.  Navigate to [[youtube_studio_video_management | YouTube Studio]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:31">[00:00:31]</a>. This is where you can manage videos you have already [[using_youtube_studio_for_video_uploads | uploaded to the channel]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:33">[00:00:33]</a>.
2.  Go to the "video section" of your channel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.
3.  Choose the specific video you wish to make private <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>.
4.  In the "visibility" setting, change it to "private" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>.
    *   A private video will not appear on your channel or in YouTube search results <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:54">[00:00:54]</a>.
    *   Only individuals you select will be able to see it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:03">[00:01:03]</a>.

## Sharing a Private Video

Once your video is set to private, you can share it with specific people:

1.  Go to the video's details by clicking the "pen" icon next to the video <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:11">[00:01:11]</a>.
2.  Within the video details, locate the three dots in the top right corner <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:21">[00:01:21]</a>.
3.  Select "share privately" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:24">[00:01:24]</a>.
4.  A new tab will open, allowing you to "add mailing list" or input email addresses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:26">[00:01:26]</a>.
5.  Enter the emails of the people you want to grant access to the video <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:30">[00:01:30]</a>.
6.  Click "send" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:56">[00:01:56]</a>.

### Recipient Experience

Recipients will receive an [[emailing_private_youtube_video_links | email from YouTube]] with a link to watch the video <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:38">[00:01:38]</a>. The email will state that the channel invites them to watch a video <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:14">[00:02:14]</a>. By clicking the link, a new tab will open, and they can view the private video <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:18">[00:02:18]</a>.
