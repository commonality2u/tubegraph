This tutorial demonstrates how to enable or disable the loop function for videos on the YouTube platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:03">00:00:03</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:09">00:00:09</a>. This feature allows a video to automatically restart once it finishes, eliminating the need to manually click replay <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:12">00:00:12</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">00:00:21</a>. It is particularly useful for continuous playback of music or videos <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">00:00:17</a>.

## Requirements

The tutorial primarily uses [[Using Google Chrome for YouTube | Google Chrome]] as the preferred browser due to its reliable performance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:36">00:00:36</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:38">00:00:38</a>.

## Steps to Loop a Video

1.  **Access a YouTube video**
    Navigate to any video on YouTube <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:44">00:00:44</a>. For example, a search for "[[creating_youtube_playlists | how to create playlists]] in 2021" can be used to find a relevant video <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:47">00:00:47</a>.
2.  **Right-click on the video frame**
    While the video is playing, right-click anywhere within the video frame <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">00:01:07</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:09">00:01:09</a>.
3.  **Select "Loop"**
    A dropdown menu will appear <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">00:01:10</a>. Click on the "Loop" option in this menu <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:13">00:01:13</a>.

Once "Loop" is selected, the video will automatically restart from the beginning when it reaches its end <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">00:01:16</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:34">00:01:34</a>.

## Deactivating Loop Mode

To turn off the loop feature:

1.  **Right-click on the video screen**
    Right-click on the video screen again <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:45">00:01:45</a>.
2.  **Click "Loop" again**
    The dropdown menu will reappear <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:47">00:01:47</a>. Click on "Loop" once more to deactivate it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:48">00:01:48</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:51">00:01:51</a>.

With loop mode deactivated, the video will pause playback at its conclusion instead of restarting <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:57">00:01:57</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:58">00:01:58</a>.
