## App Installation and Initial Login

The [[using_the_binance_app_on_a_mobile_phone | Binance app]] interface is similar for both iPhone and Android devices <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>.

To begin:
1.  Go to Google Play (for Android) or the App Store (for iPhone) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a>.
2.  Search for and download the [[using_the_binance_app_on_a_mobile_phone | Binance app]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:24">[00:01:24]</a>.
3.  Once downloaded, tap "Open" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:38">[00:01:38]</a>.
4.  Allow notifications when prompted <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:49">[00:01:49]</a>.
5.  Tap "Log in" at the top right <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:58">[00:01:58]</a>.
6.  Enter your email and complete the security verification (captcha) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:00">[00:02:00]</a>.
7.  A security code will be sent to your mobile phone for verification <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:09">[00:02:09]</a>.

## Initial App Interface Overview

Upon logging in, the app displays a screen with a submenu at the bottom <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:33">[00:02:33]</a>. This submenu includes:
*   **Home** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:39">[00:02:39]</a>
*   **Markets** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:39">[00:02:39]</a>
*   **Futures** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:39">[00:02:39]</a>
*   **Wallet** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:39">[00:02:39]</a>

At the top of the screen, you will find:
*   Your profile icon <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:48">[00:02:48]</a>
*   A bell icon for alerts/notifications <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:51">[00:02:51]</a>
*   A QR code scanner icon, which allows access to the camera to scan QR codes and verify your mobile phone <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:54">[00:02:54]</a>

*   **Payment** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:52">[00:03:52]</a>

### Notifications

The bell icon at the top right leads to the notifications section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:20">[00:04:20]</a>.
*   **View All Notifications**: See all alerts and messages received <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:26">[00:04:26]</a>.
*   **Management Options**: At the top right, you can erase, mark as read, or clean notifications <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:30">[00:04:30]</a>.

## Bottom Navigation Menus

### Home Menu

The Home menu displays different cryptocurrencies and various options <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:40">[00:02:40]</a>.
*   **Asset Management**: Includes options to transfer, convert, and other functions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:53">[00:04:53]</a>.
*   **Transfers**: Allows transfer of different assets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:03">[00:05:03]</a>.
*   **Buying Assets**: You can buy assets using various payment methods, including bank transfer and Visa card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:05">[00:05:05]</a>.
*   **Cryptocurrency Selection**: Tapping on a cryptocurrency like BNB immediately takes you to its buy/sell menu <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:20">[00:05:20]</a>.
*   **Cryptocurrency Pair Classification**: Find rankings of cryptocurrency pairs offered by the platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:39">[00:05:39]</a>. You can also change the market view <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:45">[00:05:45]</a>.

### Markets Menu

The Markets menu provides a more detailed view of the market than the Home screen <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:53">[00:05:53]</a>.
*   **Top Sub-Menus**: Includes Favorites, Spot, and Futures <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:57">[00:05:57]</a>.
*   **All Markets**: Shows all markets and cryptocurrencies available on the platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:01">[00:06:01]</a>.
*   **Graphs**: You can view charts for any cryptocurrency (e.g., Bitcoin) and adjust the timeframe (days, hours) or insert indicators like MacDe <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:13">[00:06:13]</a>.
*   **Buy/Sell**: Directly buy or sell cryptocurrencies from the market view <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:32">[00:06:32]</a>.
*   **Favorites**: Save specific assets as favorites using the star icon <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:41">[00:06:41]</a>.

### Trade Menu

The Trade section allows for direct and fast [[using_binance_mobile_app_for_trading | trading]] of different assets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:01">[00:07:01]</a>.
*   **Asset Pairs**: Example shown is Bitcoin against USDT <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:19">[00:07:19]</a>.
*   **Order Types**: Place limited orders, market orders, stop orders, and OCO (One-Cancels-the-Other) orders <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:25">[00:07:25]</a>.
*   **Amount Specification**: Input the desired amount for the operation (e.g., amount of Bitcoins) and see the cost in USD <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:33">[00:07:33]</a>.
*   **Change Asset**: Easily change the active asset for [[using_binance_mobile_app_for_trading | trading]] by clicking on the asset name <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:47">[00:07:47]</a>.

### Futures Menu
