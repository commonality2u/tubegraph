PayPal offers security features and buyer protection to ensure transactions are safe, particularly when purchasing goods or services <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:21">[00:02:21]</a>. Understanding these features can help users manage their transactions effectively and mitigate risks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:05">[00:03:05]</a>.

## Key Security Measures

PayPal prioritizes the security of its transactions:
*   **24/7 Transaction Monitoring** All transactions are monitored around the clock to prevent fraud <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:56">[00:02:56]</a>.
*   **Data Encryption** PayPal encrypts data to protect user information <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:53">[00:02:53]</a>.
*   **Fraud Prevention** The platform employs various measures, including electronic mail notifications, to prevent fraudulent activities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:00">[00:03:00]</a>.

## Understanding Buyer Protection

Buyer protection is a significant feature, especially when making purchases:
*   **Applicability** This protection is primarily for payments made for "articles or services" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:21">[00:02:21]</a>. When sending money for a purchase, the buyer is covered by PayPal's protection <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:34">[00:02:34]</a>.
*   **Seller Fees** For "article or service" payments, the seller typically pays a fee <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:31">[00:02:31]</a>.
*   **Claiming Money** Users can learn how to claim money back if needed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>.
*   **Reviewing Conditions** It is recommended to review PayPal's conditions, especially when buying or selling items, to avoid being scammed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:08">[00:03:08]</a>.

> [!INFO] Payment Types
> It's important to distinguish between sending money to a friend and making a payment for an item or service <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:37">[00:02:37]</a>. Sending money to a friend, particularly within the European Union, is generally free of charge <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:15">[00:02:15]</a>.

## [[managing_paypal_transaction_history | Managing PayPal Transaction History]]

Users can view their transaction history to monitor payments:
*   **Viewing History** After sending money, the transaction history will update, showing the reduced balance and the payment status <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:40">[00:03:40]</a>.
*   **Transaction Status** A transaction may initially appear as "unclaimed" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:54">[00:03:54]</a>.
*   **Receipts** Users have the option to print a receipt for any executed transaction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:04">[00:04:04]</a>.

## Receiving Payments

When receiving money, the funds typically appear in the PayPal balance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:22">[00:04:22]</a>. The transaction details, including the sender and the amount, are visible in the activity section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:30">[00:04:30]</a>. Transfers usually complete within approximately five minutes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:39">[00:04:39]</a>.
