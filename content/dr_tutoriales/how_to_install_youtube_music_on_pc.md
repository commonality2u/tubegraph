This tutorial provides step-by-step instructions for installing YouTube Music on your computer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:05">[00:00:05]</a>. If you have any questions, you can leave them in the comments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:07">[00:00:07]</a>.

## Requirements

To begin, you will need a search engine installed on your computer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>. Compatible options include Edge, Firefox, Opera, or [[using_google_chrome_for_youtube | Google Chrome]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:16">[00:00:16]</a>.

## Installation Process

1.  **Open your search engine** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:19">[00:00:19]</a>.
2.  **Navigate to the YouTube Music website** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:24">[00:00:24]</a>. You can do this by typing "YouTube Music" into the search bar <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>. The first result will be the correct page <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:31">[00:00:31]</a>.
3.  **Locate the install symbol** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>. Once on the YouTube Music website, pay attention to the top right corner of your screen <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>. You will see a small computer symbol with a downward arrow, indicating "Install YouTube Music" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:44">[00:00:44]</a>.
4.  **Click "Install"** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>. Clicking this button will directly install the application onto your computer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>.
5.  **Confirm Installation** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:55">[00:00:55]</a>. The app will be downloaded and installed, and you will typically be logged in with your Google account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:58">[00:00:58]</a>.

## Accessing YouTube Music on your PC

After installation, the app will create a [[creating_a_shortcut_for_youtube_on_pc | shortcut]] on your desktop <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>.

You can also find YouTube Music by typing "music" into your computer's search bar <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:11">[00:01:11]</a>. From there, you can [[managing_youtube_music_on_taskbar | add it to your taskbar]] by right-clicking and selecting "Pin to taskbar" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:14">[00:01:14]</a>.

> [!NOTE]
> For information on watching videos on loop within YouTube or looping a specific song, refer to tutorials available on the platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:22">[00:01:22]</a>.

## Uninstalling YouTube Music

If you wish to [[uninstalling_youtube_music_from_pc | uninstall YouTube Music]] from your computer:

1.  **Open the Control Panel** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:36">[00:01:36]</a>. You can search for "control panel" in your computer's search bar <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:39">[00:01:39]</a>.
2.  **Select "Uninstall a program"** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:42">[00:01:42]</a>.
3.  **Locate and uninstall YouTube Music** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:45">[00:01:45]</a>. Find YouTube Music in the list, right-click on it, and select "Uninstall" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:47">[00:01:47]</a>.
