This article outlines how to create posts on YouTube using a mobile device, applicable for both Android and iPhone users <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:08">[00:00:08]</a>. The process is simple and can be completed in a few steps <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:15">[00:00:15]</a>.

## Prerequisites

Before creating a post, ensure you have the YouTube application downloaded on your mobile device <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:29">[00:00:29]</a>.
*   For iPhone users, download from the App Store <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:34">[00:00:34]</a>.
*   For Android users, download from Google Play <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[00:00:37]</a>.

Once downloaded, open the app and log in with your official YouTube account from which you wish to publish <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>.

## [[steps_to_create_simple_youtube_posts | Steps to Create Simple YouTube Posts]]

1.  **Access the Creation Menu:** Navigate to the video camera icon, typically located at the top right of the screen <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:05">[00:01:05]</a>.
2.  **Select "Publication":** From the options that appear (make a video, a live stream, or a publication), choose "publication" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>.
3.  **Compose Your Post:** Type the desired text for your post <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:26">[00:01:26]</a>. For example, you can type "Have a nice day" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>.
4.  **Publish:** After composing your post, tap the "publish" button <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>.

## Additional Features

Beyond simple text posts, you can [[adding_photos_and_surveys_to_youtube_posts | add photos]] and [[adding_photos_and_surveys_to_youtube_posts | surveys]] to your YouTube posts directly from your mobile phone <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:41">[00:01:41]</a>. The option to do so is available within the publishing interface <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:43">[00:01:43]</a>.

Once published, the post will be visible to your followers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:57">[00:01:57]</a>. Users are encouraged to activate the notification bell to stay updated on new publications, videos, and tutorials <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:04">[00:02:04]</a>.
