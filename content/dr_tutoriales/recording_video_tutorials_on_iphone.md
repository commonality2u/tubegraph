The iPhone screen recorder can be used to record videos, photos, or to create [[video_tutorial_creation | video tutorials]] for colleagues and friends <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:08">[00:00:08]</a>.

## Enabling Screen Recording on iPhone

To utilize the iPhone's screen recording feature, you first need to enable it through the device's settings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:16">[00:00:16]</a>.

### Accessing Control Center Settings

1.  Enter the "Settings" application <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:16">[00:00:16]</a>.
2.  Navigate to the "Control Center" section, which is represented by a gray icon <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:19">[00:00:19]</a>.
3.  Within "Control Center," select "Customize Controls" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>.

### Adding Screen Recording to Controls

Once in "Customize Controls," you will see various options <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:32">[00:00:32]</a>.
1.  Locate "Screen Recording" among the available options <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:39">[00:00:39]</a>.
2.  Ensure that "Screen Recording" is active <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:54">[00:00:54]</a>.
3.  You can also adjust the order in which the controls appear, based on your preference <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:48">[00:00:48]</a>.

## Using the Screen Recorder

After activating the feature, you can easily access the [[how_to_use_iphone_screen_recorder | recorder screen of our iPhone]] by sliding your finger from the top to the bottom of the screen <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:59">[00:00:59]</a>.

### Activating the Microphone for Recording

The screen recorder also allows you to record sound <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:05">[00:01:05]</a>. To do this:
1.  Keep the screen recording button pressed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:11">[00:01:11]</a>.
2.  This action will activate the [[activating_microphone_for_screen_recording_on_iphone | microphone]], enabling you to record videos with internal mobile sound <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:13">[00:01:13]</a>.
