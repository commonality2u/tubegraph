## Using Social Media Profiles for Application

When [[creating_an_amazon_affiliates_account | signing up]] for the [[Amazon Affiliate Program Basics | Amazon Affiliate Program]], applicants are required to provide a list of web pages or apps where they intend to promote products <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>. While having a traditional website is beneficial, it is not strictly necessary <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:21">[00:02:21]</a>.

Social media profiles, such as those on Facebook, can be used as the required "website" during the application process <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:33">[00:02:33]</a>. The speaker in the tutorial, for instance, used a Facebook page created in a previous video tutorial as their designated website <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:50">[00:02:50]</a>.

> [!INFO]
> Even if you don't own a traditional business website, you can use your social media profile to register for the [[Amazon Affiliate Program Basics | Amazon Affiliate Program]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:27">[00:02:27]</a>.

## Generating Traffic and Promoting Products

Social networks are identified as a viable method for generating traffic to your affiliate links and promoted products <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:35">[00:05:35]</a>. A Facebook page can be utilized to promote specific products or even a YouTube channel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:41">[00:02:41]</a>.

The process of generating [[creating_amazon_affiliate_links | affiliate links]] through platforms like blogs and social media allows you to recommend products <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:10">[00:06:10]</a>. For example, if a friend or family member plans to purchase a product on Amazon, you can share your affiliate link, ensuring that you both benefit: they buy the product, and you earn a commission at no extra cost to them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:09">[00:04:09]</a>. The price of the product remains the same for the buyer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:23">[00:04:23]</a>.
