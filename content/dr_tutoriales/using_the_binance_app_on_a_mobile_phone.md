## Getting Started

The [[using_binance_mobile_app_for_trading | Binance app]] operates similarly on both iPhone and Android devices <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>.

### Downloading and Installing the App
1.  Open the App Store (for iOS) or Google Play (for Android) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a>.
2.  Search for "Binance" and download the application <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:24">[00:01:24]</a>.
3.  Once downloaded, tap the "Open" button <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:38">[00:01:38]</a>.

### Logging In
1.  Upon opening the app, you may be prompted to allow notifications; it's recommended to allow them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:49">[00:01:49]</a>.
2.  Tap "Log in" at the top right <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:55">[00:01:55]</a>.
3.  Enter your email and password <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:00">[00:02:00]</a>.
4.  Complete the security verification (e.g., puzzle slide) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:03">[00:02:03]</a>.
5.  A security code will be sent to your registered mobile phone for confirmation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:09">[00:02:09]</a>.
