## Free Transcription Services

Two specific transcription tools are highlighted as being "absolutely free of charge" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:43">[00:00:43]</a>. These free options are accessible online and do not require payment to use <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>.

*   **Usage:** Users can upload audio files, and the program will transcribe them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:45">[00:03:45]</a>.
*   **Limitations:** While effective for shorter audios, these free tools are not suitable for very long content like movies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:51">[00:03:51]</a>.
*   **[[accuracy_and_reliability_of_audio_transcription_tools | Accuracy and reliability of audio transcription tools | Accuracy]]**: Free tools can offer a "reliable" version, achieving an [[accuracy_and_reliability_of_audio_transcription_tools | accuracy]] of around 80-90% of words successfully transcribed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:32">[00:03:32]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:35">[00:03:35]</a>.

## Paid Transcription Services

Many online transcription services operate on a paid model, often requiring payment due to the computational power needed to provide their services <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:03">[00:01:03]</a>.

*   **Free Trials:** Some paid platforms offer free trials, but continuous use or new accounts will eventually require payment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>.
*   **Pricing Models:**
    *   **Happy Scribe:** Allows users to "get started for free" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:09">[00:01:09]</a>, but pricing is typically based on the number of minutes transcribed or the total word count <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:14">[00:01:14]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:18">[00:01:18]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:21">[00:01:21]</a>.
    *   **Speech Text Artificial Intelligence:** An example pricing is 180 minutes of transcription for $10 per month <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:24">[00:01:24]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:29">[00:01:29]</a>. Plans can vary <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:33">[00:01:33]</a>.
    *   **Open AI:** This service requires payment for access <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:36">[00:01:36]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:39">[00:01:39]</a>.
    *   **Biz There:** Offers a limitation of 30 minutes for free subtitles, after which payment becomes necessary <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:41">[00:01:41]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:47">[00:01:47]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:50">[00:01:50]</a>.

Generally, paying for a transcription platform results in significantly higher quality transcription <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:08">[00:04:08]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:12">[00:04:12]</a>.
