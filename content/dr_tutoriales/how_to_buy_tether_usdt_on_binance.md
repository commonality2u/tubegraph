<a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:12">[00:02:12]</a> To find different USDT exchange pairs:

*   <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:54">[00:02:54]</a> If USDT appears *first* (e.g., USDT/DAI), and you select "Buy," you would be [[buying_and_selling_cryptocurrencies_on_binance | buying USDT]] with DAI.
