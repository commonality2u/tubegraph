To embed your electronic ID card and its associated digital certificate onto a mobile device, certain prerequisites and steps must be followed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:00">[00:00:00]</a>.

## Prerequisites

Before attempting to embed the digital certificate on your mobile phone, it is indispensable to have already installed the [[verification_and_installation_process_for_digital_certificates | digital certificate]] within a computer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>. This is a crucial preliminary step <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:33">[00:00:33]</a>. A separate video tutorial explains this process, and it is advised to complete those steps first, as without them, the mobile embedding will not be possible <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.

## General Process Overview

While the specific apps on Android and iOS may not be exactly the same, the overall process for embedding digital certificates is similar <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:13">[00:01:13]</a>.

### Device-Specific Considerations

*   **Android:** You will need to activate the [[connecting_mobile_device_via_bluetooth | NFC antenna]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:20">[00:01:20]</a>.
*   **iPhone:** The [[connecting_mobile_device_via_bluetooth | NFC antenna]] is already activated <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:22">[00:01:22]</a>. This antenna is what enables features like contactless payments with your mobile phone <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:23">[00:01:23]</a>. No additional management is required for iPhone users regarding the NFC functionality <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:34">[00:01:34]</a>.

## Application Download and Verification

1.  **Download the App:** You will need to download an application called "Digital certificate" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:46">[00:01:46]</a>. If you cannot find it directly, search for "TNF" in the App Store <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:52">[00:01:52]</a>.
2.  **Verify Creator:** It is crucial to ensure that the creator of the application is "Digital certificate FNT" to safeguard your data <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:03">[00:02:03]</a>.

## Obtaining/Managing the Certificate via the App

1.  **Open the App:** Once downloaded, open the app <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:13">[00:02:13]</a>.
2.  **Request Certificate:** The app allows you to "Request the certificate digital" or "manage Digital Certificates Installed" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:21">[00:02:21]</a>.
3.  **Choose Method:** Select the option to [[verification_and_installation_process_for_digital_certificates | read the chip of your ID E]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:40">[00:02:40]</a>.
4.  **Accept Terms:** Proceed by accepting the terms and conditions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:46">[00:02:46]</a>.
5.  **Enter Email:** Provide your email address <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:54">[00:02:54]</a>.
6.  **Email Verification:** Complete the email verification process <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:57">[00:02:57]</a>.
7.  **Enter CAM Code:** Input the CAM code found on your physical ID card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:12">[00:03:12]</a>.
8.  **Enter PIN:** Enter the PIN associated with your DNI (ID card) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:20">[00:03:20]</a>. This PIN is obtained when you renew your DNI or can be set at a police station, as explained in the prerequisite tutorial <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:23">[00:03:23]</a>.
9.  **Scan ID Card:** Place your ID card on a smooth surface and position your phone, without a case, on top of it until the chip is detected <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:33">[00:03:33]</a>. The chip is usually detected near the top of the mobile phone, where the antenna is located <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:41">[00:03:41]</a>.
10. **Confirm Request:** Confirm the request for the Citizen's Certificate issued by the National Factory <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:51">[00:03:51]</a>.
11. **Await Notification:** You will receive a notification once your application is validated and the certificate is available <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:01">[00:04:01]</a>.

## [[installing_digital_certificates_on_iphone | Installing the Certificate]] on the Mobile Device

1.  **Access Pending Requests:** In the "Requests earrings" section of the app, locate your certificate <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:11">[00:04:11]</a>.
2.  **Initiate Installation:** Click on "Install on mobile" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:14">[00:04:14]</a>.
3.  **Accept Terms:** Accept the terms and conditions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:20">[00:04:20]</a>.
4.  **Create Password:** Set a password for your certificate <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:28">[00:04:28]</a>.
5.  **Download Profile:** Allow the website to download a profile, which contains your digital certificate <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:31">[00:04:31]</a>.
6.  **View Installed Certificate:** Click on "View Certificate installed" to access the profile downloaded <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:52">[00:04:52]</a>.
7.  **Final Installation:** In the VPN & Asset Management section, select "install," enter your device code, and then the password you created for the certificate <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:05">[00:05:05]</a>.
8.  **Verify Installation:** Return to the FNT app to confirm that your certificate is listed under "my installed certificates" and is active as a citizen certificate within your mobile device <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:16">[00:05:16]</a>.
