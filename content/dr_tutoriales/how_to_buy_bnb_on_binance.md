## Buying BNB on PC

1.  **Navigate to Markets**: From the Binance dashboard, go to the "markets" section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:32">[00:01:32]</a>.
2.  **Search for BNB**: In the markets section, go to "zones," then "everything," and search for "BNB" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:41">[00:01:41]</a>. BNB Coin is noted as the fourth in capitalization <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:47">[00:01:47]</a>.
3.  **Select Trading Pair**: Click on "operate" next to BNB <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:00">[00:02:00]</a>. A new tab will open showing a graph of BNB compared with USD (Tether) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:05">[00:02:05]</a>. USDT is a cryptocurrency that imitates the value of the dollar in the market <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:11">[00:02:11]</a>.
    *   Many exchanges are available for BNB <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:27">[00:02:27]</a>. You can click the downward arrow to see all available exchanges <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:36">[00:02:36]</a>, such as BNB/USD, BNB/Bitcoin, or BNB/Euro <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:42">[00:02:42]</a>. For example, selecting BNB/Euro will open its specific chart <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:54">[00:02:54]</a>.
4.  **Place Order**: Pay special attention to the "place order" section, where you can buy or sell <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:06">[00:03:06]</a>.
    *   **Order Types**: Binance offers different order types, including limit order, market order, stop-limit order, and OCO order <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:14">[00:03:14]</a>. These are explained in another tutorial <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:22">[00:03:22]</a>.
    *   **Market Order**: To buy immediately at the current real-time price, choose the "market" order <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:36">[00:03:36]</a>.
5.  **Specify Amount**:
    *   **By Fiat**: Enter the total amount in Euros (or other fiat currency) you wish to spend <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:58">[00:03:58]</a>. For example, to buy 1,000 Euros worth of BNB, enter "1000" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:04">[00:04:04]</a>.
    *   **By BNB Quantity**: Alternatively, specify the exact amount of BNB you wish to buy <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:14">[00:04:14]</a>. The equivalent price in fiat will be displayed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:18">[00:04:18]</a>.
6.  **Confirm Purchase**: Click "buy" to complete the transaction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:08">[00:04:08]</a>. The purchased BNB will appear in your portfolio <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:11">[00:04:11]</a>.

## Buying BNB on Mobile Phone

1.  **Download Binance App**: Ensure the Binance application is downloaded on your mobile phone <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:34">[00:04:34]</a>.
2.  **Open App**: Click the Binance app icon to open the homepage <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:39">[00:04:39]</a>.
3.  **Navigate to Markets**: From the homepage, go to the "markets" section, located in the submenu at the bottom <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:43">[00:04:43]</a>. This will display all trading pairs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:53">[00:04:53]</a>.
4.  **Search for BNB**: Use the magnifying glass icon at the top of the screen to search for "BNB" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:53">[00:04:53]</a>. This will show all available exchanges for BNB <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:00">[00:05:00]</a>.
5.  **Select Trading Pair**: Choose a desired trading pair, such as BNB/Euro <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:07">[00:05:07]</a>. The BNB graph with the selected currency will open <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:13">[00:05:13]</a>.
6.  **Place Order**:
    *   Locate the "buying part" at the bottom of the screen and click it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:28">[00:05:28]</a>.
    *   Similar to the PC interface, you can choose to buy or sell and select from different order types <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:37">[00:05:37]</a>.
7.  **Specify Amount**: Enter the total amount you wish to buy in fiat (e.g., 1,000 Euros) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:54">[00:05:54]</a>.
8.  **Confirm Purchase**: Click "buy BNB" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:03">[00:06:03]</a>. If you have sufficient funds, the BNB will be purchased and appear in your portfolio <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:09">[00:06:09]</a>.

## Locating Purchased BNB

After purchasing BNB, it will appear in your "Spot Wallet" within the "wallet" section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:19">[00:06:19]</a>.

### Investing BNB

It is also possible to invest your BNB to generate profitability <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:42">[00:06:42]</a>. Once invested, your BNB will be found in the "earn" section of your wallet, where it will show the invested amount and the generated profitability <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:57">[00:06:57]</a>.
