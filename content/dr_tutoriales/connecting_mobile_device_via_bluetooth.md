This guide explains how to activate Bluetooth on a Windows 10 computer and then connect a mobile device to it via Bluetooth. The process is straightforward and ensures perfect functionality <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:06">[00:00:06]</a>.

## Activating Bluetooth on Windows 10

To begin, ensure your computer's Bluetooth service is properly configured and running. This process involves accessing system services and adjusting settings.

### Accessing Services

1.  Navigate to the Windows Home bar <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:45">[00:00:45]</a>.
2.  In the search bar, type "Services" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:51">[00:00:51]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:54">[00:00:54]</a>.
3.  Open the "Services" window that appears <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>.

### Configuring Bluetooth Compatibility Service

Within the Services window, you'll need to locate and configure the [[setting_bluetooth_service_to_automatic | Bluetooth compatibility service]]:

1.  Scroll through the list of services to find "Bluetooth compatibility service" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:05">[00:01:05]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:08">[00:01:08]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:18">[00:01:18]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:38">[00:01:38]</a>. It is listed alphabetically under 'B' <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:34">[00:01:34]</a>.
2.  Right-click on "Bluetooth compatibility service" and select "Properties" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:40">[00:01:40]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:43">[00:01:43]</a>.
3.  In the Properties window, set the "Startup type" to "Automatic" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:47">[00:01:47]</a>.
4.  Click "Apply" and then "OK" to save the changes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:52">[00:01:52]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:56">[00:01:56]</a>.
5.  Right-click on the "Bluetooth compatibility service" again and choose "Restart" to apply the newly implemented changes to your computer's Bluetooth system <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:00">[00:02:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:04">[00:02:04]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:07">[00:02:07]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:10">[00:02:10]</a>. Wait for the service to restart <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:16">[00:02:16]</a>.

### Verifying Bluetooth Icon Visibility

After configuring the service, ensure the Bluetooth icon is visible in your notification area:

1.  Close the Services window <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:24">[00:02:24]</a>.
2.  Go back to the taskbar and search for "Bluetooth" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:29">[00:02:29]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:33">[00:02:33]</a>.
3.  Select "Bluetooth settings and other devices" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:37">[00:02:37]</a>.
4.  Once the "Bluetooth & other devices" window is open <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:42">[00:02:42]</a>, click on "More Bluetooth options" on the right <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:46">[00:02:46]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:49">[00:02:49]</a>.
5.  Ensure the option "Show the Bluetooth icon in the notification area" is selected <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:53">[00:02:53]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:56">[00:02:56]</a>. If it's not, select it and click "OK" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:01">[00:03:01]</a>.
6.  You should now see the Bluetooth symbol, indicating that Bluetooth is enabled on your computer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:12">[00:03:12]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:16">[00:03:16]</a>.

## Connecting a Mobile Device

Once Bluetooth is activated on your computer, you can proceed to connect your mobile device.

1.  Return to the "Bluetooth & other devices" settings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:27">[00:03:27]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:30">[00:03:30]</a>.
2.  Click on "Add Bluetooth or other device" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:34">[00:03:34]</a>.
3.  Select "Bluetooth" as the device type.
4.  Your computer will begin searching for nearby Bluetooth devices <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:38">[00:03:38]</a>.
5.  On your mobile device (e.g., iPhone), ensure its Bluetooth is turned on and it's discoverable <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:41">[00:03:41]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:44">[00:03:44]</a>.
6.  Once your mobile device appears in the list on your computer (e.g., "my iPhone"), select it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:47">[00:03:47]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:48">[00:03:48]</a>.
7.  The computer will attempt to connect <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:48">[00:03:48]</a>. You will then need to accept the connection request on your mobile device <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:51">[00:03:51]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:55">[00:03:55]</a>.
8.  Once linked and connected, the device is ready to communicate and transmit data between your phone and computer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:01">[00:04:01]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:04">[00:04:04]</a>.
