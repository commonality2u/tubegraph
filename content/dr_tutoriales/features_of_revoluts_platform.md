## Core Features

### Financial Management and Analysis
Revolut allows users to [[managing_finances_with_revolut | manage money]] effectively through several tools:
*   **Budget and Transfer Analysis** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:32">[00:01:32]</a>.
*   **Expense Analysis**: Users can classify expenses by categories like transportation, restaurants, or shopping, which helps in understanding spending habits and controlling expenditures <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:22">[00:02:22]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:32">[00:02:32]</a>.
*   **Financial Objectives (Vaults)**: This feature allows users to set specific saving goals, such as for travel, by creating "trunks" (vaults) and allocating money towards them until the goal is reached, facilitating controlled saving <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:01">[00:05:01]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:22">[00:05:22]</a>. This provides a level of financial control <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:36">[00:05:36]</a>.

### International Transfers
Revolut is renowned for its international transfer capabilities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>.
*   **Low Commissions**: It aims to charge low commissions for international transfers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:43">[00:01:43]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:00">[00:03:00]</a>. While it states "no hidden commissions," it doesn't mean there are no commissions at all <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:04">[00:03:04]</a>.
*   **Free Transfers**: The first 1,000 euros (or equivalent in another currency) sent to another bank account in a different currency are generally free of commission for standard accounts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:29">[00:03:29]</a>. Transfers exceeding this amount will incur charges for standard account holders <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:40">[00:03:40]</a>. However, Premium and Metal account holders do not pay commissions for these transfers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:43">[00:03:43]</a>.
*   **Real Exchange Rate**: Users can get the real exchange rate for transactions abroad in over 150 currencies without additional charges, allowing them to pay like a local <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:38">[00:06:38]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:41">[00:06:41]</a>.

### Investment and Trading
Revolut offers features for [[investing_and_trading_with_revolut | investing and trading]]:
*   **Cryptocurrencies and Gold**: Users can buy cryptocurrencies and gold within the app in a matter of seconds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:39">[00:05:39]</a>.

### Security and Protection
Revolut is recognized for its robust security measures:
*   **Fraud Prevention**: It is considered one of the top banks for preventing fraud, with anti-fraud systems that keep fraudulent transactions below 0.01% <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:21">[00:06:21]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:26">[00:06:26]</a>.
*   **Activity Tracking**: Users can allow activity tracking, which helps in monitoring account access from different devices <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:54">[00:11:54]</a>.
