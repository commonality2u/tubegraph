## The Importance of the [[using_imei_number_for_iphone_verification | IMEI Number]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>

The core of this verification process is the [[using_imei_number_for_iphone_verification | IMEI number]], which stands for the International Mobile Equipment Identity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>.
*   Every Apple device has an [[using_imei_number_for_iphone_verification | IMEI number]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:38">[00:00:38]</a>.
*   If an iPhone is lost or stolen, its [[using_imei_number_for_iphone_verification | IMEI number]] is registered with Apple as a lost, disappeared, or stolen device <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:44">[00:00:44]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:47">[00:00:47]</a>.
*   Checking the [[using_imei_number_for_iphone_verification | IMEI number]] is highly recommended before purchasing any second-hand device to ensure it's not reported as missing or stolen <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:09">[00:01:09]</a>.
