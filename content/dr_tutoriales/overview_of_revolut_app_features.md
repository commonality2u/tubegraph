## Getting Started with the App

Upon opening the [[Revolut account types and plans | Revolut]] app on a mobile device, users are presented with a personalized main menu <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:17">[00:01:17]</a>.

### Profile and Account Settings
The top-left corner displays account information, including a profile picture and options to upgrade a plan, invite friends, and view personal data <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:29">[00:01:29]</a>. Users can see their current pricing plan (e.g., Standard account) and access account boundaries, documents, and attached bank account details <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>.

The app's appearance can be customized, including the theme (e.g., dark mode) and the application icon, though icon customization may depend on the user's [[revolut_account_types_and_plans | Revolut account types and plans]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:44">[00:01:44]</a>.

Security settings allow users to manage their access key, privacy, block betting, and enable login with Face ID <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:02">[00:02:02]</a>. The app also provides information about [[Revolut]] on platforms like the App Store, Facebook, and Twitter <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:07">[00:02:07]</a>. Options to close the account and log out are also available <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:13">[00:02:13]</a>.

### Financial Statistics and Budgeting
On the right side of the main screen, users can view financial statistics, including monthly expenses, categorized by type (e.g., transfers, supermarkets) and even by country where money was spent <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:26">[00:02:26]</a>. This feature helps in analyzing spending habits <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:40">[00:02:40]</a>.

Users can set monthly budget goals and track their progress <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:57">[00:02:57]</a>. Expenditure data can be displayed in various chart formats, such as bar or line graphs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:16">[00:03:16]</a>.

### Currency Exchange and Favorites
The favorites section, marked by a star, displays real-time exchange rates for various currencies and assets, such as Euro to Pound, Dollar to Pound, Bitcoin to Pound, and Gold <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:33">[00:03:33]</a>. A converter tool allows users to see the equivalent value of a specific amount (e.g., 100 Pounds) in other assets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:43">[00:03:43]</a>.

### Alerts and Notifications
Users can set price alerts, for example, to be notified when Bitcoin reaches a specific price point, which can be useful for buying <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:57">[00:03:57]</a>. The app also features a notifications bell for general alerts and updates <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:36">[00:04:36]</a>.

## Core Account Management
The main dashboard provides quick access to core features like accounts, cards, stocks, and crypto <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:46">[00:04:46]</a>.

### Managing Accounts
The "Accounts" section displays current accounts, such as a Pounds Sterling account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:49">[00:04:49]</a>. Users can easily add new accounts in different currencies, including Euro and Dollar, facilitating currency exchange <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:03">[00:05:03]</a>. Options within an account include adding money, sending money, making currency exchanges, extracting bank statements, and viewing account details <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:15">[00:05:15]</a>.

### Sending Money
The app simplifies [[international_transfers_with_revolut | sending money]] to contacts or other entities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:27">[00:05:27]</a>. Users can send money to contacts, create payment links for others to use, or add new bank recipients by entering account numbers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:32">[00:05:32]</a>. The app also allows inviting friends <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:47">[00:05:47]</a>. [[international_transfers_with_revolut | Transfers]] within [[Revolut]] are immediate, unlike conventional banks that might take longer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:59">[00:05:59]</a>.

### Transaction History
Users can view a detailed history of all transactions and transfers, including dates and amounts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:06">[00:06:06]</a>.

### App Suggestions and News
The app provides suggestions and news about new versions and updates, keeping users informed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:17">[00:06:17]</a>.

## Advanced Financial Features

### Net Worth and Savings
The "Analysis" section, also accessible from the main dashboard, displays the net worth of the user's account in their primary currency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:30">[00:06:30]</a>. The "Savings" part shows total savings in various currencies (e.g., Pounds, USD) and potential annual rewards <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:44">[00:06:44]</a>. Higher reward rates are often tied to premium [[revolut_account_types_and_plans | Revolut account types and plans]], such as Metal <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:02">[00:07:02]</a>.

### Stocks Investment
[[managing_finances_with_revolut | Revolut]] allows users to invest in over 800 stocks, starting from as little as one dollar <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:02">[00:09:02]</a>. The platform provides market graphs, financial data (profit margins, annual/quarterly reports), and news related to specific companies (e.g., Tesla, Apple, Amazon, Coinbase) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:17">[00:09:17]</a>. To buy or sell stocks, users must register their social security number or national ID for tax quantification and government control <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:16">[00:10:16]</a>.

### Cryptocurrency Investment
Users can buy various cryptocurrencies such as Bitcoin, Cardano, Aptos, Ethereum, and Litecoin directly from the app <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:08">[00:11:08]</a>. Standard accounts incur a 2.5% commission on cryptocurrency transactions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:21">[00:11:21]</a>. Upgrading to a different [[revolut_account_types_and_plans | plan]], such as the Metal plan, can significantly reduce or eliminate these commissions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:32">[00:11:32]</a>.

### Rewards and Discounts
The "Rewards" section offers various discounts on products and services from partners like Protein, Apple, and Udemy <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:27">[00:07:27]</a>. Users with Premium or Plus accounts typically receive more discounts at a wider range of stores <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:42">[00:07:42]</a>.

Users can utilize disposable virtual cards for secure online payments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:07">[00:08:07]</a>. Physical cards can be requested through the app <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:59">[00:07:59]</a>.

### Adding and Customizing Cards
New debit or virtual debit cards can be added <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:19">[00:08:19]</a>. Cards can be customized with different designs, such as national flags <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:30">[00:08:30]</a>. To obtain special card materials like metal, users need to upgrade their [[revolut_account_types_and_plans | Revolut account types and plans]] to a Metal plan <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:35">[00:08:35]</a>.
