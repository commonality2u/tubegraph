Revolut offers various account plans designed to meet different user needs, from basic money management to extensive financial services and travel benefits <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:49:00">[02:49:00]</a>. Understanding the [[features_of_revoluts_platform | features of Revolut's platform]] and the distinctions between these plans is crucial for users to determine which option best suits their financial situation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:56:00">[02:56:00]</a>.

## Account Types Overview

Revolut offers four main personal account plans:
*   Standard <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:52:00">[02:52:00]</a>
*   Plus <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:54:00">[06:54:00]</a>
*   Premium <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:54:00">[02:54:00]</a>
*   Metal <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:54:00">[02:54:00]</a>

The cost and benefits vary significantly across these plans <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:49:00">[02:49:00]</a>. While the platform offers business accounts for companies, this article focuses solely on [[creating_a_personal_account_on_revolut | personal accounts]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:12:00">[01:12:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:17:00">[01:17:00]</a>.

## Standard Account

The Standard account is Revolut's free offering <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:07:00">[07:07:00]</a>. It provides essential services for [[managing_finances_with_revolut | managing finances]] and making international transfers.

*   **Cost**: Free <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:07:00">[07:07:00]</a>.
*   **International Transfers**:
    *   The first 1,000 euros (or equivalent in other currencies) sent in another currency to a different bank account in another country are commission-free <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:27:00">[03:27:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:37:00">[03:37:00]</a>.
    *   For amounts exceeding 1,000 euros, Standard account holders will incur commissions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:40:00">[03:40:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:41:00">[03:41:00]</a>.
    *   A free international transfer per month is included, with subsequent transfers costing 0.50 pounds each <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="13:51:00">[13:51:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="14:09:00">[14:09:00]</a>.
*   **Benefits**: Allows users to understand their spending habits through expense analysis by categorizing transactions (e.g., transportation, restaurants, shopping) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:23:00">[02:23:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:32:00">[02:32:00]</a>.

## Plus Account

The Plus account offers an upgrade from the Standard plan with additional benefits.

*   **Cost**: 3 euros per month <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:54:00">[06:54:00]</a>.
*   **International Transfers**: Includes one free international transfer per month, with subsequent transfers costing 0.50 pounds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="13:42:00">[13:42:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="13:47:00">[13:47:00]</a>.
*   **Savings**: Users can earn interest on their savings, potentially up to 0.40% annual interest <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="14:21:00">[14:21:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="14:26:00">[14:26:00]</a>.

## Premium Account

The Premium account is designed for users who make more frequent or larger international transactions and desire enhanced features.

*   **Cost**: 8 euros per month <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:55:00">[06:55:00]</a>.
*   **International Transfers**: For transfers exceeding 1,000 euros, Premium accounts are not charged additional commissions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:43:00">[03:43:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:47:00">[03:47:00]</a>.
*   **ATM Withdrawals**: Allows for higher commission-free ATM withdrawals compared to the Standard plan <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="13:29:00">[13:29:00]</a>, with around 400 pounds per month <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="13:35:00">[13:35:00]</a>.
*   **Travel Protection**: Includes various travel benefits and insurance coverage <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="14:15:00">[14:15:00]</a>, although it does not provide travel insurance specifically <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="14:19:00">[14:19:00]</a>.

## Metal Account

The Metal account is Revolut's most comprehensive and expensive plan, offering a range of premium benefits.

*   **Cost**: 14 euros per month <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:57:00">[06:57:00]</a>.
*   **International Transfers**: Similar to Premium, Metal accounts are not charged commissions for transfers exceeding 1,000 euros <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:43:00">[03:43:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:47:00">[03:47:00]</a>.
*   **Cashback**: Offers cashback on transactions: 0.01% within Europe and 1% outside of Europe <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="13:33:00">[13:33:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="13:35:00">[13:35:00]</a>.
*   **ATM Withdrawals**: Provides double the ATM withdrawals compared to Premium, with approximately 800 pounds per month in commission-free withdrawals <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="13:32:00">[13:32:00]</a>.
*   **Travel Protection**: Features substantial travel coverage and insurance, making it highly useful for frequent travelers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="13:06:00">[13:06:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="13:11:00">[13:11:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="13:15:00">[13:15:00]</a>.
*   **Investments**: Allows for [[saving_and_investing_features_on_revolut | investments]] in the stock market, though "commission-free" is relative <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="13:01:00">[13:01:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="13:04:00">[13:04:00]</a>. It offers up to 0.65% annual interest on investments, paid daily <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="14:31:00">[14:31:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="14:36:00">[14:36:00]</a>.
*   **[[revolut_card_features | Card Features]]**: Includes a physical metal card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="12:29:00">[12:29:00]</a>.

## Choosing a Plan

The optimal plan depends on individual financial objectives and economic situations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:24:00">[07:24:00]</a>. For those making significant international payments or transfers, a Premium or Metal account may be more cost-effective due to reduced commissions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:36:00">[04:36:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:45:00">[04:45:00]</a>. New users are often encouraged to start with the Standard plan and then upgrade if their needs change <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:18:00">[07:18:00]</a>.

When [[creating_a_revolut_account | creating a Revolut account]] through a referral link, users might receive a benefit, such as a 10-euro bonus after making their first payment with the Revolut card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:41:00">[07:41:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:43:00">[07:43:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:52:00">[07:52:00]</a>. This [[referral_commissions_and_benefits | referral commission]] makes it beneficial to use a provided link when initiating the account creation process <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:03:00">[01:03:00]</a>. The entire process, from [[introduction_to_revolut_app | introduction to the Revolut app]] to verifying identity, is primarily done via the mobile phone <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:25:00">[08:25:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:00:00">[09:00:00]</a>.
