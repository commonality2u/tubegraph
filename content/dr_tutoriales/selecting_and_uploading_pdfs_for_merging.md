To [[merging_pdf_files | merge PDF]] documents, you can utilize an online tool, eliminating the need for external programs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>. This process is demonstrated using Google Chrome <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:14">[00:00:14]</a>.

### Accessing the PDF Merging Tool

The recommended tool is [[using_i_love_pdf_to_join_pdfs | I Love PDF]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>. Once on the website, navigate to the "Merge PDF" or "Join PDF" option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>.

### Selecting Your PDF Files

1.  **Initiate Selection:** Click on the option to select PDFs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:14">[00:01:14]</a>.
2.  **Locate Files:** Browse to the directory where your PDF files are stored <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:22">[00:01:22]</a>. For example, if you have a "Safari" ticket PDF and a "Multicinema" ticket PDF <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:24">[00:00:24]</a>, you would select both <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:22">[00:01:22]</a>.
3.  **Confirm Selection:** Ensure both desired files are chosen <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:29">[00:01:29]</a> and click "Open" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:29">[00:01:29]</a>.

### Uploading and Loading

After selecting your files, the PDFs will load into the online tool <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:38">[00:01:38]</a>. Once loaded, you can proceed with the merging process by clicking "Merge PDF" or "Join PDF" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:38">[00:01:38]</a>.
