## Installing YouTube Music on PC

To get YouTube Music on your computer, follow these steps:

1.  **Open a Search Engine**
    You will need a search engine such as Edge, Firefox, Opera, or [[using_google_chrome_for_youtube | Google Chrome]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>. The tutorial uses [[using_google_chrome_for_youtube | Google Chrome]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:16">[00:00:16]</a>.
2.  **Navigate to YouTube Music Website**
    Enter "YouTube Music" into the search bar, or access it directly via a provided link (often found in the first comment of related tutorials) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:24">[00:00:24]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:31">[00:00:31]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:33">[00:00:33]</a>.
3.  **Install the Application**
    Once on the YouTube Music website, look for a small computer symbol with a down arrow in the top right corner of the window. This symbol indicates "Install YouTube Music" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:44">[00:00:44]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:47">[00:00:47]</a>. Click this symbol, then confirm by clicking "Install" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>.
    *   The app will download and install, requiring you to be logged in with a Google account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:55">[00:00:55]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:57">[00:00:57]</a>. [[how_to_install_youtube_music_on_pc | This process effectively installs YouTube Music on PC]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:55">[00:00:55]</a>.

## Accessing and Pinning YouTube Music

After installation, the application creates a shortcut on your desktop <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:09">[00:01:09]</a>. This is a form of [[creating_a_shortcut_for_youtube_on_pc | creating a shortcut for YouTube on PC]].

To easily access YouTube Music from your taskbar:

1.  **Search for YouTube Music**
    Close the application and search for "music" in your computer's search bar <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:11">[00:01:11]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:13">[00:01:13]</a>.
2.  **[[pinning_youtube_to_taskbar | Pin to Taskbar]]**
    Locate "YouTube Music" in the search results. Right-click on it and select the option to "pin to taskbar" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:14">[00:01:14]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:17">[00:01:17]</a>. The application will now be readily available on your taskbar <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:17">[00:01:17]</a>.

## [[uninstalling_youtube_music_from_pc | Uninstalling YouTube Music]]

If you wish to remove YouTube Music from your computer:

1.  **Access Control Panel**
    Go to your computer's Control Panel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:36">[00:01:36]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:39">[00:01:39]</a>.
2.  **Uninstall a Program**
    Within the Control Panel, find and select the "Uninstall a program" option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:42">[00:01:42]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:45">[00:01:45]</a>.
3.  **Locate and Uninstall**
    Scroll through the list of programs to find "YouTube Music" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:45">[00:01:45]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:47">[00:01:47]</a>. Right-click on it and choose "Uninstall" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:47">[00:01:47]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:50">[00:01:50]</a>.
