This guide outlines the process of [[installing_youtube_music_on_windows|installing YouTube Music]] on your computer, which automatically generates a desktop shortcut for easy access <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:00">[00:00:00]</a>.

## Requirements

To begin, you will need a web browser such as Edge, Firefox, Opera, or Google Chrome <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>. The tutorial uses Google Chrome <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>.

## Installation Steps

1.  **Access YouTube Music Website**: Open your preferred browser and navigate to the YouTube Music website <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>. You can do this by typing "YouTube Music" into the search bar <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>.
2.  **Identify Installation Option**: Once on the YouTube Music page, look for a small computer symbol with a downward arrow located in the top right corner of your browser window <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>. This icon indicates the option to [[installing_youtube_music_on_windows|install YouTube Music]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:47">[00:00:47]</a>.
3.  **[[downloading_youtube_music_for_pc|Install YouTube Music]]**: Click on this symbol, and then select "Install" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>. The application will be downloaded and installed directly onto your computer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>.
4.  **Login**: YouTube Music will automatically log in using your Google account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:57">[00:00:57]</a>.

## Accessing the Desktop Shortcut

After the installation, a shortcut for YouTube Music will automatically appear on your desktop <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>.

### Pinning to Taskbar

For even quicker access, you can [[pinning_youtube_to_taskbar|add YouTube Music to your taskbar]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:14">[00:01:14]</a>:

1.  Search for "YouTube Music" in your computer's search bar <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:11">[00:01:11]</a>.
2.  Right-click on the YouTube Music application.
3.  Select the option to "Pin to taskbar" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:14">[00:01:14]</a>.

## Uninstalling YouTube Music

If you wish to [[uninstalling_youtube_music_from_windows|remove YouTube Music]] from your computer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:32">[00:01:32]</a>:

1.  Go to the Control Panel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:36">[00:01:36]</a>.
2.  Select the "Uninstall a program" option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:42">[00:01:42]</a>.
3.  Locate YouTube Music in the list of programs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:45">[00:01:45]</a>.
4.  Right-click on YouTube Music and choose "Uninstall" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:47">[00:01:47]</a>.
