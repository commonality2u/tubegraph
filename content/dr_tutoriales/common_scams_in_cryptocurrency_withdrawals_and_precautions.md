## Identifying Withdrawal Scams

A common scam involves perpetrators asking for a significant amount of money from individuals before they can withdraw their cryptocurrency funds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:44">[00:01:44]</a>. If confronted with such a demand, it is advised to take no action <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:47">[00:01:47]</a>.

## Avoiding Scams During Withdrawal

Many individuals are scammed during the cryptocurrency withdrawal process because they are unfamiliar with the correct procedures <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:01">[00:02:01]</a>. This lack of knowledge is often exploited by scammers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:04">[00:02:04]</a>.
