[[creating_private_videos_on_youtube | Creating private videos on YouTube]] and sharing them privately can be managed through [[youtube_studio_interface | YouTube Studio interface]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:06">[00:00:06]</a>. This allows only selected individuals to view specific content <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:10">[00:00:10]</a>.

## Accessing YouTube Studio

To manage videos, begin by accessing the YouTube platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>. From there, navigate to [[youtube_studio_navigation | YouTube Studio navigation]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:31">[00:00:31]</a>. Once in [[youtube_studio_interface | YouTube Studio interface]], go to the "Video" section of the channel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.

## Setting a Video to Private

Once in the video section of your channel, follow these steps:
1.  Choose the desired video <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>.
2.  In the "Visibility" section, set the video to "Private" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>.

> [!INFO] Private Video Characteristics
> A private video will not appear on your channel or in YouTube search results <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>. It can only be seen by people you explicitly choose <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:01">[00:01:01]</a>.

## Sharing Private Videos

After setting a video's visibility to private, you can share it with specific individuals:
1.  Go to the video's details by clicking the "pen" icon next to the video <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:11">[00:01:11]</a>.
2.  In the video details, locate the three dots in the top right corner <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:21">[00:01:21]</a>.
3.  Click "Share Privately" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:24">[00:01:24]</a>.
4.  A new tab will open, allowing you to add email addresses to a mailing list <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:29">[00:01:29]</a>.
5.  Enter the email addresses of the people you wish to share the video with <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:30">[00:01:30]</a>.
6.  Click "Send" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:56">[00:01:56]</a>.

### Recipient Experience
Recipients will receive an email from YouTube containing a link to the private video <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:38">[00:01:38]</a>. The email will indicate that the channel invites them to watch a video <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:11">[00:02:11]</a>. Clicking this link will open the video in a new tab, allowing them to view it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:18">[00:02:18]</a>.
