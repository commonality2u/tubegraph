## Finding the Official TikTok Website

### 1. Open a Search Engine
Start by opening any preferred search engine <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:14">[00:00:14]</a>. Options include:
*   Google Chrome <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:15">[00:00:15]</a>
*   Firefox <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:15">[00:00:15]</a>
*   Opera <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:16">[00:00:16]</a>
*   Microsoft Edge <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>

The example uses a new Google Chrome tab <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:20">[00:00:20]</a>.

### 2. Search for "TikTok"
In the search bar, type the word "TikTok" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>.

### 3. Identify the Official Page
Access the main page of TikTok, which is typically the first result that appears <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>. It is crucial to ensure you are on the official TikTok website <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:35">[00:00:35]</a>.

## Installing the TikTok Application

### 1. Locate the Install Option
Once on the official TikTok website and logged into your account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>, look for an option on the right side of the page <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>. This option is typically represented by a computer icon with a downward arrow <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:58">[00:00:58]</a>, indicating "install TikTok" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>.

### 2. Initiate Installation
Click on this option to begin installing the TikTok app on your computer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:02">[00:01:02]</a>. Then, click "Install" when prompted <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:05">[00:01:05]</a>.

## Accessing the Installed TikTok App

After installation, the TikTok application will be accessible on your PC <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:11">[00:01:11]</a>.

### 1. Desktop Shortcut
A direct access shortcut for TikTok may appear on your desktop <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a>.

### 2. Using the Taskbar Search
If you cannot find it on your desktop, go to your taskbar and search for "TikTok" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:19">[00:01:19]</a>. The application will appear in the search results <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:22">[00:01:22]</a>.

### 3. Pinning to Taskbar
You can pin the TikTok application to your taskbar for easy access <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:23">[00:01:23]</a>. You can also unpin it if desired <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:29">[00:01:29]</a>.

## [[how_to_uninstall_tiktok_from_a_computer | Uninstalling TikTok from Your Computer]]

If you wish to [[how_to_uninstall_tiktok_from_a_computer | remove TikTok from your computer]]:
1.  Go to the Control Panel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:36">[00:01:36]</a>.
2.  Navigate to "Programs" or "Uninstall a program" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:43">[00:01:43]</a>.
3.  Locate "TikTok" in the alphabetical list <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:44">[00:01:44]</a>.
4.  Select it and choose to uninstall <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:48">[00:01:48]</a>.
