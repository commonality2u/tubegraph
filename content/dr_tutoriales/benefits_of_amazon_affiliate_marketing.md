The [[amazon_affiliate_program_basics | Amazon affiliate program]] allows individuals to earn commissions by recommending products sold on Amazon. When someone purchases a product through a unique link provided by an affiliate, the affiliate receives a commission <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:10">[00:00:10]</a>.

## Key Benefits

*   **Commission-Based Earnings**
    By recommending products, affiliates can earn a commission from purchases made through their unique links <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:10">[00:00:10]</a>. This allows for potential profits from all purchases made by people referred through these links <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:20">[00:00:20]</a>.
*   **No Additional Cost to Buyer**
    When a customer buys a product through an affiliate link, the price they pay for the product remains exactly the same <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:23">[00:04:23]</a>. The commission comes from Amazon, not added to the customer's cost.
*   **Free to Join**
    The [[amazon_affiliate_program_basics | Amazon affiliate program]] is "totally free" to join <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:30">[00:04:30]</a>. This makes it an accessible way for individuals to potentially gain "some real benefit" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:32">[00:04:32]</a>.
*   **Website [[monetization_strategies_with_amazon_affiliates | Monetization]]**
    A primary reason to join the [[amazon_affiliate_program_basics | Amazon affiliate program]] is to [[monetization_strategies_with_amazon_affiliates | monetize]] a website or online presence <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:18">[00:06:18]</a>. Platforms like [[how_to_use_social_media_for_amazon_affiliates | social networks]] can be used to generate traffic for affiliate links <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:33">[00:02:33]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:35">[00:05:35]</a>.
*   **Ease of Participation**
    Even without a traditional business website, individuals can [[creating_an_amazon_affiliates_account | create an Amazon affiliate account]] and use their social media profiles as valid "website" links <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:21">[00:02:21]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:33">[00:02:33]</a>.
