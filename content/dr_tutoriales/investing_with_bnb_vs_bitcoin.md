[[BNB Vault in Binance | BNB Vault]] is a financial product on Binance designed to help users obtain returns by investing their Binance Coin ([[BNB Vault in Binance | BNB]]) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:11">[00:00:11]</a>. This product allows users to generate profitability exclusively from the Binance cryptocurrency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>.

## Exclusivity to BNB
A key characteristic of [[BNB Vault in Binance | BNB Vault]] is its exclusivity to [[BNB Vault in Binance | BNB]]. Users cannot invest other cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin, Ethereum, or Polkadot within this specific product; only the Binance platform's native currency is accepted <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:28">[00:00:28]</a>.

## Accessing BNB Vault
To access [[BNB Vault in Binance | BNB Vault]], users should navigate to the "Finance" section on the Binance platform, located at the top menu <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:28">[00:01:28]</a>. Within the "Finance" section, users will find the "BNB Vault" option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:43">[00:01:43]</a>.

## How to Invest in BNB Vault
The process of investing [[BNB Vault in Binance | BNB]] in the vault is straightforward:
1.  Users "stake" their [[BNB Vault in Binance | BNB]] within the vault <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:11">[00:02:11]</a>.
2.  Select the desired amount of [[BNB Vault in Binance | BNB]] to invest <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:45">[00:02:45]</a>.
3.  Accept the service agreement <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:49">[00:02:49]</a>.

### Automatic Transfer Option
[[BNB Vault in Binance | BNB Vault]] offers an automatic transfer feature. If activated, all available [[BNB Vault in Binance | BNB]] balances in a user's spot account will be used to buy flexible savings and transfer to the [[BNB Vault in Binance | BNB Vault]] daily at 1 AM <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:21">[00:02:21]</a>. This option can be enabled or disabled by the user <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:37">[00:02:37]</a>.

## Benefits and Profitability
Investing [[BNB Vault in Binance | BNB]] in the vault offers multiple benefits:
*   **Returns from Launchpool**: [[BNB Vault in Binance | BNB Vault]] provides rewards based on the projects available in the Binance Launchpool <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:09">[00:03:09]</a>. The Launchpool is a tool that allows users to generate cryptocurrencies using other cryptocurrencies as reference <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:15">[00:03:15]</a>.
*   **Additional Savings Rewards**: Beyond Launchpool rewards, [[BNB Vault in Binance | BNB Vault]] also provides additional rewards from savings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:05">[00:04:05]</a>. This means users earn [[crypto_currency_investment_strategies | crypto coins]] in two ways: from the Launchpool and as a reward for savings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:20">[00:04:20]</a>.
*   **Random Rewards**: The platform periodically releases programs that can yield random rewards and other cryptocurrencies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:14">[00:05:14]</a>.
*   **Flexible Savings**: The savings are flexible, allowing users to withdraw their [[BNB Vault in Binance | BNB]] whenever they wish, as it is not subject to locked staking <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:26">[00:05:26]</a>. This makes it a secure and adaptable investment option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:36">[00:05:36]</a>.

### BNB Vault vs. Launchpool
While both [[BNB Vault in Binance | BNB Vault]] and Launchpool allow users to generate cryptocurrencies, [[BNB Vault in Binance | BNB Vault]] offers greater benefits. If [[BNB Vault in Binance | BNB]] is invested directly in Launchpool, users only obtain the cryptocurrencies offered by the Launchpool system <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:30">[00:03:30]</a>. However, by investing [[BNB Vault in Binance | BNB]] in the vault, users receive rewards from both the Launchpool and additional savings rewards, leading to more benefits and more [[crypto_currency_investment_strategies | crypto coins]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:41">[00:04:41]</a>.
