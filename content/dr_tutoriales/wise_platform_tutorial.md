This tutorial guides you on [[how_to_deposit_money_into_wise | how to deposit your capital]] within the Wise platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:05">[00:00:05]</a>. Wise provides bank accounts that allow users to manage money in various ways, including currency exchange and sending money to other countries with [[commissions_and_fees_on_wise | cheap commissions]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:12">[00:00:12]</a>. This specific [[video_tutorial_creation | video tutorial]] focuses solely on depositing capital <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:23">[00:00:23]</a>.

The steps demonstrated are performed on a PC, but the process is identical for mobile devices <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:09">[00:01:09]</a>.

## Prerequisites
Before beginning, it is assumed that you have already created and verified an account on the Wise platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:23">[00:01:23]</a>.

## Steps to Deposit Money

1.  **Select Currency**: From your Wise dashboard, click on the currency you wish to deposit (e.g., Euros) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:42">[00:01:42]</a>.
2.  **Initiate Deposit**: Click the "Add" button <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:46">[00:01:46]</a>. Other options like "convert," "send," or "request" will not be active if no funds have been added yet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:48">[00:01:48]</a>.
3.  **Enter Amount**: Specify the amount of money you want to add (e.g., 2000 euros) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:03">[00:02:03]</a>. You will be paying with the selected currency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:06">[00:02:06]</a>.
4.  **Review and Continue**: Press "Continue" to proceed to the payment section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:18">[00:02:18]</a>.

## [[methods_to_deposit_money_on_wise | Methods to Deposit Money]]

Upon reaching the payment section, you will see different [[methods_to_deposit_money_on_wise | payment methods]] available <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:24">[00:02:24]</a>. The choice of method may depend on urgency and associated [[commissions_and_fees_on_wise | fees]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:02">[00:03:02]</a>.

*   **Debit/Credit Card**:
    *   Debit and credit card payments typically incur a [[commissions_and_fees_on_wise | commission]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:29">[00:02:29]</a>. For example, depositing 200 euros via card might result in a total charge of 200.82 euros, indicating a 0.82 euro [[commissions_and_fees_on_wise | commission]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:37">[00:02:37]</a>.
    *   Payments with Visa or Mastercard are usually processed in a matter of seconds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:47">[00:03:47]</a>.

*   **PayPal**:
    *   Depositing via [[using_bank_transfer_paypal_and_cards_with_wise | PayPal]] also carries a similar [[commissions_and_fees_on_wise | commission]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:43">[00:02:43]</a>.
    *   You will need to input your [[using_bank_transfer_paypal_and_cards_with_wise | PayPal]] account details, ensuring it has the necessary funds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:27">[00:03:27]</a>.

*   **Bank Transfer**:
    *   A [[using_bank_transfer_paypal_and_cards_with_wise | bank transfer]] is free <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:50">[00:02:50]</a>.
    *   If you choose this method, Wise will provide the necessary bank details (e.g., IBAN) and you'll need to include a specific reference associated with your shipment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:56">[00:03:56]</a>.

*   **Other Methods**:
    *   Other options like "Ali" (for electronic accounts) and "Ideal" may also be available, though the presenter noted they had not used Ideal before <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:54">[00:02:54]</a>.

### Completing the Deposit
The tutorial demonstrates completing the deposit using a card for quick processing <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:41">[00:03:41]</a>. After entering the card number, the payment is processed immediately <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:35">[00:04:35]</a>. Once complete, your money in euros will appear in your Wise balance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:42">[00:04:42]</a>.
