To record the microphone audio while [[how_to_use_iphone_screen_recorder | screen recording]] on an iPhone, you must first ensure the screen recording feature is added to your Control Center.

## Adding Screen Recording to Control Center

1.  Enter `settings` <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:16">[00:00:16]</a>.
2.  Navigate to the `control center` section, which is represented by a gray icon <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:22">[00:00:22]</a>.
3.  Select `customize controls` <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>.
4.  Among the available options, find and add `screen recording` <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:39">[00:00:39]</a>. You can also arrange its order within the Control Center to your preference <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:48">[00:00:48]</a>.

Once activated, sliding down from the top of the screen will display the screen recorder option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:59">[00:00:59]</a>.

## Activating the Microphone for Recording

The screen recording feature also allows you to record sound <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:09">[00:01:09]</a>. To activate the microphone:
*   Press and hold the screen recording icon in the Control Center <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:01:11">[01:01:11]</a>. This action will activate the microphone, enabling the recording of videos with internal mobile sound <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:01:18">[01:01:18]</a>. This is useful for creating [[recording_video_tutorials_on_iphone | video tutorials]] or capturing audio along with screen activity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:11">[00:00:11]</a>.
