It is recommended to [[converting_cryptocurrencies_within_binance | convert Bitcoin]] to another cryptocurrency rather than directly to national currency, as national currencies tend to devalue over time compared to cryptocurrencies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:35">[00:01:35]</a>. ## Converting Bitcoin to Fiat Currency (e.g., Euro)

If you need to withdraw capital from the platform, you will need to [[converting_cryptocurrency_to_fiat_currency_on_binance | convert your cryptocurrency to your national currency]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:58">[00:01:58]</a>.

To [[converting_cryptocurrency_to_fiat_currency_on_binance | convert Bitcoin to Euros]] on [[how_to_exchange_cryptocurrencies_on_binance | Binance]]:
1.  Access the main page of [[how_to_exchange_cryptocurrencies_on_binance | Binance]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:15">[00:01:15]</a>.
2.  Navigate to a market, for example, search for "BTC Euro" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:07">[00:02:07]</a>. This will open a chart displaying Bitcoin's value against the Euro <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:12">[00:02:12]</a>.
3.  On this page, you will see options to "Buy Bitcoin" or "Sell Bitcoin" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:35">[00:02:35]</a>. To [[buying_and_selling_cryptocurrencies_on_binance | transform Bitcoin to Euros]], select the "Sell Bitcoin" part <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:49">[00:02:49]</a>.
4.  Enter the desired amount of Bitcoin you wish to sell <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:54">[00:02:54]</a>. You can manually enter a number or choose to sell the total amount <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:56">[00:02:56]</a>.
5.  Click "Sell Bitcoin" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:05">[00:03:05]</a>. The equivalent amount in Euros will then appear in your wallet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:07">[00:03:07]</a>.

### Converting Other Cryptocurrencies to Fiat

Some cryptocurrencies (e.g., Volkswat) cannot be directly [[converting_cryptocurrency_to_fiat_currency_on_binance | converted to Euros]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:14">[00:03:14]</a>. In such cases, you must first [[converting_cryptocurrencies_within_binance | convert the cryptocurrency to Bitcoin]] (or Dollar or other supported cryptocurrencies) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:22">[00:03:22]</a>, and then from Bitcoin, [[converting_cryptocurrency_to_fiat_currency_on_binance | convert it to your national currency]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:34">[00:03:34]</a>. This two-step process ensures you can always [[how_to_transfer_cryptocurrencies_on_binance | transfer to your desired currency]] using Bitcoin as an intermediary <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:40">[00:03:40]</a>.

### Order Types

*   **Limit Order**: This allows you to set a specific price at which you want to sell your Bitcoin. A sales order created this way is a limited order <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:08">[00:04:08]</a>.
*   **Market Order**: This allows you to sell your Bitcoin immediately at the current market price <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:11">[00:04:11]</a>. You choose the amount you want to sell <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:18">[00:04:18]</a>.

**Important Note**: There is a minimum order quantity; for example, a sale order might need to be at least 10 Euros <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:57">[00:03:57]</a>.

## Withdrawing Fiat to a Bank Account

Once your Bitcoin has been [[converting_cryptocurrency_to_fiat_currency_on_binance | converted to your national currency]] (e.g., Euros), you can [[how_to_transfer_cryptocurrencies_on_binance | withdraw it to your bank account]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:35">[00:04:35]</a>.

1.  Go to the "Wallet" section on [[how_to_exchange_cryptocurrencies_on_binance | Binance]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:58">[00:04:58]</a>.
2.  Select "Spot Wallet" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:11">[00:05:11]</a>.
3.  Choose the "Withdraw" option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:05">[00:05:05]</a>.
4.  Select your currency (e.g., Euro) and the payment method <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:48">[00:05:48]</a>.

There are generally two methods for withdrawal:
*   **Bank Transfer**: This method typically has lower commissions (e.g., 0.8 Euros) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:58">[00:05:58]</a>.
    *   Minimum withdrawal amount is 3 Euros <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:16">[00:06:16]</a>.
    *   Maximum withdrawal amount can be up to 50,000 Euros <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:13">[00:07:13]</a>.
    *   Funds are typically deposited into your account in one or two business days <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:31">[00:06:31]</a>.
    *   It is generally recommended to use bank transfer to save on commissions, especially for larger amounts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:07">[00:06:07]</a>.
*   **Bank Card**: This method offers faster transfers, sometimes up to 30 minutes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:49">[00:06:49]</a>.
    *   It charges higher commissions than bank transfers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:03">[00:06:03]</a>.
    *   Minimum withdrawal amount is 10 Euros, and maximum is 5,000 Euros <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:57">[00:06:57]</a>.

After selecting the amount and payment method, you will need to input your bank account details (IBAN) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:24">[00:06:24]</a>.
