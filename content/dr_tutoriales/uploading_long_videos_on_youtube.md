By default, new YouTube accounts do not permit [[requirements_for_uploading_longer_videos_on_youtube | uploading videos]] longer than 15 minutes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:19">[00:17:19]</a>. To [[how_to_upload_videos_longer_than_15_minutes_on_youtube | enable the upload of videos]] exceeding 15 minutes, as well as features like custom thumbnails and [[uploading_videos_and_live_broadcasting_on_youtube | live broadcasts]], an account must undergo [[verification_process_for_youtube_account_to_upload_longer_videos | phone verification]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:14:15">[01:14:15]</a>.

## Enabling Longer Video Uploads

To activate the ability to [[how_to_upload_videos_longer_than_15_minutes_on_youtube | upload videos longer than 15 minutes]], follow these steps:

1.  **Access YouTube Studio**: From your YouTube channel, directly access [[using_youtube_studio_for_video_uploads | YouTube Studio]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:46:47">[00:46:47]</a>.
2.  **Navigate to Settings**: Within [[youtube_studio_features_for_enabling_long_video_uploads | YouTube Studio]], go to the "Settings" section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:56:56">[00:56:56]</a>.
3.  **Select Channel Settings**: In "Settings," choose the "Channel" sub-tab <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:00:58">[01:00:58]</a>.
4.  **Go to Feature Eligibility**: Select "Feature eligibility" within the "Channel" settings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:04:49">[01:04:49]</a>.
5.  **Identify Verification Requirement**: Under "Feature eligibility," you will find "Features that require phone verification," which includes "videos of more than 15 minutes," "custom thumbnails," and "live broadcasts" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:14:15">[01:14:15]</a>.
6.  **Verify Phone Number**: Click on the option to [[verification_process_for_youtube_account_to_upload_longer_videos | verify the phone number]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:30:19">[01:30:19]</a>.
    *   You will need to select your country and how you prefer to receive the verification code (text message or call) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:50:52">[01:50:52]</a>.
    *   Enter your phone number <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:57:40">[01:57:40]</a>.
    *   A verification code will be sent to your phone <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:39:27">[01:39:27]</a>.
    *   Enter this code to complete the [[verification_process_for_youtube_account_to_upload_longer_videos | verification]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:07:34">[02:07:34]</a>.

Once the phone number has been verified, these advanced features, including the ability to [[how_to_upload_videos_longer_than_15_minutes_on_youtube | upload videos longer than 15 minutes]], will be enabled for your account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:11:58">[02:11:58]</a>.
