## Getting Started with the Binance App

The Binance app offers the same utilities across both iPhone and Android devices <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>.

### Installation and Login
To begin, download the Binance app from Google Play or the App Store <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a>. Once downloaded, open the app <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:38">[00:01:38]</a>. If prompted, allow notifications <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:49">[00:01:49]</a>. Then, select "Log in" in the top right corner <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:55">[00:01:55]</a>. Enter your email and other data, complete the security verification (to confirm you are not a robot) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:00">[00:02:00]</a>, and input the security code sent to your mobile phone <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:09">[00:02:09]</a>.

## Navigating the Binance Mobile App

Upon logging in, the app presents a main screen with a submenu at the bottom <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:30">[00:02:30]</a>, featuring:
*   **Home**: Displays various cryptocurrencies and options <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:39">[00:02:39]</a>.
*   **Markets**: Shows all available markets in more detail <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:51">[00:05:51]</a>.
*   **Futures**: For trading cryptocurrency futures <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:10">[00:08:10]</a>.
*   **Wallet**: Allows [[managing_and_storing_cryptocurrency_on_binance | managing and storing cryptocurrency]] assets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:39">[00:02:39]</a>.

### Top Menu Features
At the top of the screen, you'll find:
*   **Profile**: Access your account settings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:48">[00:02:48]</a>. Here you can see if your account is verified <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:06">[00:03:06]</a>, access discounts (e.g., 25% off when paying with BNB) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:39">[00:03:39]</a>, rewards, referral programs, messages, payment settings, security settings (to change passwords), and general settings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:49">[00:03:49]</a>. You can also contact Binance support <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:58">[00:03:58]</a>.
*   **Alerts Bell**: Displays all notifications received by the app <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:51">[00:02:51]</a>.
*   **QR Code Scanner**: Activates the camera to scan QR codes and verify your mobile phone <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:54">[00:02:54]</a>.

### Binance Lite
Binance Lite is an alternative format within the application that offers fewer dropdowns and menus, making the app simpler to use <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:15">[00:03:15]</a>. This tutorial focuses on the full version.

## [[how_to_exchange_cryptocurrencies_on_binance | Buying and Selling Cryptocurrencies]]

From the Home screen, you'll find options to [[how_to_transfer_cryptocurrencies_on_binance | Transfer]], [[converting_cryptocurrencies_within_binance | Convert]], and other functions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:56">[00:04:56]</a>.

### Purchasing Assets
To buy cryptocurrencies, you can use various payment methods offered by the platform, including bank transfer or Visa card purchase <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:06">[00:05:06]</a>.
If you wish to buy a specific currency, like BNB, you can click on it directly from the Home screen, which takes you to its dedicated purchase menu <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:20">[00:05:20]</a>.

### Accessing Trade Functionality
The "Trade" section allows you to [[how_to_exchange_cryptocurrencies_on_binance | trade different assets]] directly and quickly <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:01">[00:07:01]</a>. Here, you can:
*   **Select Assets**: By default, it might show Bitcoin against another currency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:19">[00:07:19]</a>. You can easily change the active asset by clicking on it to view other available options <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:47">[00:07:47]</a>.
*   **Place Orders**: Choose from different order types for buying and selling:
    *   **Limited order** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:25">[00:07:25]</a>
    *   **Market order** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:25">[00:07:25]</a>
    *   **Stop** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:25">[00:07:25]</a>
    *   **OCO (One Cancels the Other)** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:29">[00:07:29]</a>
*   **Specify Amount**: Input the amount of the asset you wish to trade, and the cost in USD will be displayed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:33">[00:07:33]</a>.
