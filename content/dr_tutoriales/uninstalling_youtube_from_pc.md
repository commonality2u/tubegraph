## Steps to Uninstall YouTube

1.  Close the YouTube application if it is open <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:41">[01:41]</a>.
2.  Navigate to the Control Panel on your computer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:43">[01:43]</a>. You can search for "control panel" in the Windows search bar <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:45">[01:45]</a>.
3.  Once in the Control Panel, select "Uninstall a program" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:50">[01:50]</a>.
4.  Locate "YouTube" in the list of installed programs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:52">[01:52]</a>.
5.  Right-click on "YouTube" and then click "Uninstall" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:57">[01:57]</a>.
