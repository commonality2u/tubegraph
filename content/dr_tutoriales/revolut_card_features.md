## Types of Cards

Revolut provides users with several types of cards:
*   **Virtual Cards** These are accessible within the [[introduction_to_revolut_app | Revolut]] app <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:56">[00:07:56]</a>.
*   **Physical Cards** Users can request a physical card, which will be delivered <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:58">[00:07:58]</a>. Ordering a card is a requirement to win 10 euros when [[creating_a_revolut_account | creating an account]] through a referral link <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:48">[00:00:48]</a>. A purchase must be made with the ordered card to qualify for the 10 euro reward <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:51">[00:00:51]</a>.
*   **Disposable Virtual Cards** These cards offer enhanced security for payments as they are single-use <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:08">[00:08:08]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:14">[00:08:14]</a>.
*   **Metal Cards** These are a premium card option that requires users to upgrade their [[features_of_revoluts_platform | Revolut plan]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:35">[00:08:35]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:41">[00:08:41]</a>.
