From: [[dr_tutoriales]] <br/> 
This article explains how to transfer cryptocurrency from [[buying_sending_and_swapping_cryptocurrencies_using_trust_wallet | Trust Wallet]] to [[options_for_bank_transfer_from_binance | Binance]], and subsequently how to convert it to fiat currency (e.g., Euro) for withdrawal to a bank account.

## Overview <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:04">[00:00:04]</a>

This tutorial demonstrates the process of transferring cryptocurrency from your [[buying_sending_and_swapping_cryptocurrencies_using_trust_wallet | Trust Wallet]] to an exchange like [[options_for_bank_transfer_from_binance | Binance]], which then allows for withdrawal to a bank account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:29">[00:01:29]</a>. The demonstration is conducted using a mobile phone (iPhone, though the process is identical for Android devices) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:44">[00:00:44]</a>.

## Transferring Funds from Trust Wallet to Binance

### Step 1: Open Trust Wallet and Select Cryptocurrency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:58">[00:00:58]</a>
*   Open your [[buying_sending_and_swapping_cryptocurrencies_using_trust_wallet | Trust Wallet]] application <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:58">[00:00:58]</a>.
*   Identify the specific cryptocurrency you wish to withdraw <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:04">[00:01:04]</a>. For example, BNB (Binance Coin) is accepted on [[options_for_bank_transfer_from_binance | Binance]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>.
*   Select the cryptocurrency and tap "Send" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:55">[00:01:55]</a>.

### Step 2: Open Binance and Generate Deposit Address <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:03">[00:02:03]</a>
*   Open your [[options_for_bank_transfer_from_binance | Binance]] application <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:03">[00:02:03]</a>.
*   Go to the "Deposit" section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:07">[00:02:07]</a>.
*   Choose the exact same cryptocurrency you intend to transfer (e.g., BNB) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:10">[00:02:10]</a>.
*   Select the correct blockchain network (e.g., Beacon Chain for BNB) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:15">[00:02:15]</a>.
*   Copy the generated wallet address and any required memo/tag <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:20">[00:02:20]</a>.

### Step 3: Complete Transaction in Trust Wallet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:21">[00:02:21]</a>
*   Return to [[buying_sending_and_swapping_cryptocurrencies_using_trust_wallet | Trust Wallet]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:21">[00:02:21]</a>.
*   Paste the copied wallet address into the recipient field <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:22">[00:02:22]</a>.
*   Paste the memo/tag (if required) into the corresponding field <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:24">[00:02:24]</a>.
*   Enter the amount you wish to send (e.g., 56 dollars worth of BNB) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:58">[00:01:58]</a>.
*   Review the transaction details and note any associated commissions (e.g., two cents on the dollar for this transaction) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:37">[00:02:37]</a>.
*   Confirm the transaction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:42">[00:02:42]</a>.

### Step 4: Funds Arrive in Binance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:45">[00:02:45]</a>
*   Your cryptocurrency will be removed from [[buying_sending_and_swapping_cryptocurrencies_using_trust_wallet | Trust Wallet]] and will appear in your [[options_for_bank_transfer_from_binance | Binance]] Spot Wallet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:45">[00:02:45]</a>.

## Withdrawing Funds from Binance to a Bank Account

Once your funds are in [[options_for_bank_transfer_from_binance | Binance]], you can convert them to fiat currency and withdraw to your bank account. This is part of the broader process of [[withdrawing_cryptocurrency_funds_to_a_bank_account | withdrawing cryptocurrency funds to a bank account]].

### Step 1: Convert Cryptocurrency to Fiat (e.g., Euro) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:01">[00:03:01]</a>
*   In [[options_for_bank_transfer_from_binance | Binance]], navigate to the "Trade" section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:01">[00:03:01]</a>.
*   Select "Convert" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:08">[00:03:08]</a>.
*   Choose the cryptocurrency you deposited (e.g., BNB) and convert it to your desired fiat currency, such as EUR (Euro) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:13">[00:03:13]</a>.

### Step 2: Initiate Fiat Withdrawal <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:35">[00:03:35]</a>
*   Once the cryptocurrency is converted to fiat (e.g., Euro), go to your Spot Wallet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:32">[00:03:32]</a>.
*   Select "Withdraw" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:35">[00:03:35]</a>.
*   Choose "Fiat" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:38">[00:03:38]</a>.
*   Select the currency you wish to withdraw (e.g., Euro) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:39">[00:03:39]</a>.

### Step 3: Choose Withdrawal Method <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:45">[00:03:45]</a>
*   Specify the amount you want to withdraw <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:45">[00:03:45]</a>.
*   Select your preferred withdrawal method <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:47">[00:03:47]</a>:
    *   **SEPA bank transfer** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:48">[00:03:48]</a>
    *   **Visa banking card** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:52">[00:03:52]</a>
*   Follow the prompts to complete the withdrawal. Tutorials are available for methods that charge no commission for deposits <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:56">[00:03:56]</a>.
