Revolut offers various account plans, each with distinct [[features_and_benefits_of_Revolut | features and benefits]], catering to different user needs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:23">[00:00:23]</a>. Users can view their current plan and explore options for upgrading directly within the Revolut app <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>.

## Standard Account

The Standard account is a base-tier plan that provides essential [[overview_of_revolut_app_features | functionalities]] for managing finances <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>.
*   The speaker currently uses the Standard account because it is free <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:50">[00:01:50]</a>.
*   Savings features, such as earning annual rewards on money stored, are not available with the Standard plan <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:14">[00:07:14]</a>.
*   Transactions involving cryptocurrencies incur a 2.5% commission with the Standard plan <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:27">[00:11:27]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:20">[00:12:20]</a>.

## Upgraded Plans (Plus, Premium, Metal)

Revolut offers upgraded plans, including Plus, Premium, and Metal, which provide additional benefits and reduced fees <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:50">[00:01:50]</a>. These plans are designed for users seeking more advanced features.

### Key Benefits of Upgraded Plans

*   **Enhanced Savings Rates**:
    *   The "NOS" plan (possibly Plus) offers a 0.20% annual reward on pounds held in a savings "trunk" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:05">[00:07:05]</a>.
    *   The Metal plan offers a higher 0.40% annual reward on both pounds and USD held in savings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:51">[00:06:51]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:08">[00:07:08]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:43">[00:12:43]</a>.
*   **Reduced Cryptocurrency Commissions**: Upgraded plans offer lower or even commission-free exchanges for cryptocurrency investments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:32">[00:11:32]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:40">[00:11:40]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:26">[00:12:26]</a>. The Metal plan offers "commission-free exchanges" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:43">[00:11:43]</a>.
*   **Exclusive Discounts**: Premium and Plus accounts provide access to a wider range of discounts at various stores <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:42">[00:07:42]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:45">[00:07:45]</a>.
*   **Customizable Cards**: Users with upgraded plans can customize the appearance of their physical Revolut cards, including options like metal cards <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:30">[00:08:30]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:35">[00:08:35]</a>. Obtaining a metal card requires upgrading to the Metal plan <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:39">[00:08:39]</a>.

The choice of plan depends on individual needs and desired features <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:11">[00:07:11]</a>. Upgrading a plan can provide more advanced functionalities, such as access to VIP lounges during travel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:47">[00:13:47]</a>.
