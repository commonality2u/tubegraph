Advcash (also referred to as "Aftas" or "Azkar" in the transcript) is an electronic or digital wallet that allows users to manage their capital online, including trading with various cryptocurrencies available in the market <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:48">[00:00:48]</a>. The platform differentiates itself by its focus on cryptocurrency trading <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:01">[00:01:01]</a>. Advcash has collaborations with other platforms like [[using_kraken_for_cryptocurrency_conversion | Binance]] ("pods" / "balance" platform in transcript) for buying and selling cryptocurrencies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:03">[00:01:03]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:56">[00:08:56]</a>, and [[nexo_platform | Nexo]] for earning interest on crypto savings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:01">[00:09:01]</a>.

## Key Features for Cryptocurrency Transactions

Advcash enables users to receive, process, and send cryptocurrencies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:31">[00:02:31]</a>. It allows for the buying and selling of crypto assets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:08">[00:03:08]</a>.

### Depositing Cryptocurrencies
Users can deposit various cryptocurrencies, such as Litecoin, into their Advcash account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:08">[00:12:08]</a>. There is no commission applied by Advcash for depositing cryptocurrencies, as fees are typically charged by the exchange from which the funds are withdrawn <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:14">[00:12:14]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:26">[00:12:26]</a>.

When depositing cryptocurrencies:
*   Six confirmations from the blockchain are required for the transaction to complete <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:31">[00:12:31]</a>.
*   Transaction limits apply, for example, from 1 to 99,999 USD <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:37">[00:12:37]</a>.
*   The platform provides a [[how_to_use_qr_codes_for_cryptocurrency_transactions | QR code]] and a wallet address for transfers, which is valid for up to 15 minutes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:08">[00:13:08]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:14">[00:13:14]</a>.
*   This address can be copied and pasted into other platforms like [[using_kraken_for_cryptocurrency_conversion | Binance]] or other exchanges such as KuCoin or Crypto.com <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:17">[00:13:17]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:47">[00:13:47]</a>.
*   Users must be cautious and precise when transferring cryptocurrencies, as any mistake can lead to the permanent loss of capital <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:28">[00:13:28]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:39">[00:13:39]</a>.

### Buying Cryptocurrencies
Advcash allows users to buy various cryptocurrencies, including DAI, USD Coin, Ripple, Bitcoin, and many others <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:00">[00:14:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:45">[00:23:45]</a>.
The primary method recommended for buying cryptocurrencies on Advcash is through its partnership with the [[using_kraken_for_cryptocurrency_conversion | Binance]] exchange, which offers competitive rates <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:37">[00:23:37]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:57">[00:23:57]</a>. Users can also buy crypto directly via Mastercard or Visa, though commissions are generally higher <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:43">[00:24:43]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:49">[00:24:49]</a>.

### Selling Cryptocurrencies
Users can sell the cryptocurrencies they hold on Advcash <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:08">[00:25:08]</a>. The capital obtained from selling cryptocurrencies and converting them into fiat currencies (like dollars or euros) will be stored within the Advcash platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:10">[00:25:10]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:16">[00:25:16]</a>.

### Saving Cryptocurrencies and Earning Interest
Advcash collaborates with the [[nexo_platform | Nexo platform]] to offer a crypto savings feature <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:25">[00:25:25]</a>. By keeping cryptocurrencies on the platform, users can earn up to 8% annualized interest <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:28">[00:25:28]</a>. Users can connect their Advcash account to [[nexo_platform | Nexo]] to deposit cryptocurrencies and start earning interest <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:26:00">[00:26:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:26:03">[00:26:03]</a>. It's advisable to compare the interest rates directly on the [[nexo_platform | Nexo platform]], as Advcash may retain a portion of the commission <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:26:21">[00:26:21]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:26:25">[00:26:25]</a>.

### Cryptocurrency Withdrawal Processes
Advcash applies commissions for withdrawing cryptocurrencies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:47">[00:04:47]</a>. For example, withdrawing to another Advcash user incurs no commission <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:52">[00:04:52]</a>. The platform supports various blockchains for transactions, including TRC20, which is noted for its low cost <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:11">[00:05:11]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:22">[00:05:22]</a>.
