## Getting Started with the Binance App

The Binance app functions identically on both iPhone and Android devices <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>.

### Downloading and Logging In
To begin, download the Binance app from Google Play or the App Store <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a>. Once downloaded, open the app <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:38">[00:01:38]</a>. Upon opening, you may be prompted to allow notifications <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:49">[00:01:49]</a>. Then, proceed to log in using your registered email and password <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:55">[00:01:55]</a>. A security verification step, often involving a puzzle, is required to confirm you are not a robot <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:03">[00:02:03]</a>, followed by a security code sent to your mobile phone <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:09">[00:02:09]</a>.

## Navigating the Binance App Interface

Upon logging in, you'll encounter the app's main screen, which includes a bottom submenu and a top menu <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:33">[00:02:33]</a>.

### Top Menu Features
The top menu provides access to:
*   **Profile**: This section displays your account status, including verification <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:06">[00:03:06]</a>. It also offers the option to switch to Binance Lite, a simplified interface with fewer menus, but the tutorial recommends staying in the standard view for full functionality <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:15">[00:03:15]</a>. Within your profile, you can access:
    *   Discounts (e.g., 25% discount when paying with BNB) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:39">[00:03:39]</a>
    *   Rewards <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:49">[00:03:49]</a>
    *   [[using_binance_referrals_via_mobile_app | Referral programs]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:50">[00:03:50]</a>
    *   Gifts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:51">[00:03:51]</a>
    *   Messages (to support or other users for trading) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:52">[00:03:52]</a>
    *   Security settings (e.g., changing passwords) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:56">[00:03:56]</a>
    *   General settings, including switching between light and dark modes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:54">[00:03:54]</a>
*   **Notifications**: Represented by a bell icon, this is where you can view, mark as read, or clear all notifications <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:20">[00:04:20]</a>.
*   **QR Code Scanner**: This symbol allows access to your camera to scan QR codes for verification <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:51">[00:02:51]</a>.

## Detailed App Sections

### Home
The Home screen displays various cryptocurrencies and options <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:41">[00:02:41]</a>. Key functionalities include:
*   **Transfer & Convert**: Options for managing different assets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:59">[00:04:59]</a>. You can transfer assets or [[how_to_deposit_money_into_binance | deposit money into Binance]] using various payment methods like bank transfer or Visa card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:03">[00:05:03]</a>.
*   **Buying Cryptocurrencies**: You can [[how_to_buy_bnb_on_binance_using_a_mobile_device | buy BNB on Binance using a mobile device]] or other cryptocurrencies by simply clicking on the desired asset, which then takes you to a purchase/sell menu <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:20">[00:05:20]</a>.
*   **Cryptocurrency Pairs**: A classification of cryptocurrency pairs is available, allowing you to change and view different market classifications <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:39">[00:05:39]</a>.

### Markets
This section provides a more detailed view of all available markets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:53">[00:05:53]</a>. You can view:
*   **Favorites**: Save specific cryptocurrency pairs for quick access <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:57">[00:05:57]</a>.
*   **Spot & Futures**: Categories for different types of trading <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:58">[00:05:58]</a>.
*   **All Markets**: A comprehensive list of all cryptocurrencies available on the platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:01">[00:06:01]</a>.
*   **Charts**: Click on any cryptocurrency to view its chart, adjust timeframes (days, hours), and insert indicators like MACD <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:13">[00:06:13]</a>. You can also buy and sell directly from the chart view <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:32">[00:06:32]</a>.

### Trade
The Trade section allows for direct and fast buying and selling of various assets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:01">[00:07:01]</a>.
*   **Asset Selection**: By default, it might show Bitcoin against another currency, but you can easily change the active asset by clicking on it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:19">[00:07:19]</a>.
*   **Order Types**: Supports various order types including limited order, market order, stop, and OCO (One Cancels the Other) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:25">[00:07:25]</a>.
*   **Transaction Details**: You can specify the amount for the operation, the quantity of cryptocurrency (e.g., Bitcoins), and view the cost in USD <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:33">[00:07:33]</a>.

### Futures
This section is dedicated to [[using_binance_futures_app_on_mobile | Binance Futures app on mobile]], allowing you to trade cryptocurrency futures <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:10">[00:08:10]</a>.

### Wallet
The Wallet section is where you manage your assets and funds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:22">[00:08:22]</a>.
*   **Portfolio Overview**: Shows your overall portfolio, though some details may be censored for privacy <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:26">[00:08:26]</a>.
*   **Fund Management**: Options to [[how_to_deposit_money_into_binance | deposit money into Binance]], withdraw, and [[how_to_transfer_cryptocurrencies_on_binance | transfer cryptocurrencies on Binance]] or other funds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:31">[00:08:31]</a>.
*   **Sub-accounts**: The wallet section also includes sub-menus for Summary, Spot, Margin, and Futures, allowing analysis of different asset types and balances <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:41">[00:08:41]</a>.
    *   **Spot**: Displays profitability and balances for assets purchased directly <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:47">[00:08:47]</a>. You can see the amount bought and daily profitability <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:59">[00:08:59]</a>.
    *   **Margin**: If trading with leverage, this section shows relevant features and risk assessment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:13">[00:09:13]</a>.
    *   **P2P**: Refers to Peer-to-Peer trading <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:22">[00:09:22]</a>.

The Binance app provides a comprehensive platform for [[using_binance_for_cryptocurrency_investments | cryptocurrency investments]] and management directly from your mobile device <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:26">[00:09:26]</a>.
