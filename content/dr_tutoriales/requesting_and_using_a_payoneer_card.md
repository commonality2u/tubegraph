## Requirements for Requesting a Payoneer Card

To apply for a Payoneer card, users must have received payments totaling at least one hundred dollars ($100) within the last six months <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:29">[00:07:29]</a>. This is a minimum requirement that needs to be met <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:33">[00:07:33]</a>.
