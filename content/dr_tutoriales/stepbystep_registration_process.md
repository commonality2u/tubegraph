This tutorial outlines the process of creating an Amazon affiliate channel, which allows users to earn commissions by recommending Amazon products <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:07">[00:00:07]</a>. This method can generate profits from purchases made through recommended links <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:22">[00:00:22]</a>. The process is entirely free and offers a real benefit <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:30">[00:04:30]</a>.

## Accessing Amazon Associates

To begin, navigate to the Amazon homepage <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:44">[00:00:44]</a>. On the main Amazon page, scroll down to the bottom left and select the "affiliate program" option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>.

## Joining the Program

Once on the affiliate program page, click the "join for free" button located at the top right <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:13">[00:01:13]</a>.

## Login

You will then be prompted to log in to your Amazon account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:23">[00:01:23]</a>.

## Registration Stages

The registration process consists of four main stages <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>:

### 1. Account Information

The first stage involves filling in your basic account information <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:48">[00:01:48]</a>.
*   **Address**: Your address may be pre-defined if you already have an Amazon account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:51">[00:01:51]</a>.
*   **Main Account Owner**: Indicate if you are the primary beneficiary or if another person owns the account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:58">[00:01:58]</a>.
*   **USA Tax Effects**: Answer the question regarding USA tax implications <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:09">[00:02:09]</a>.

### 2. Website or App List

This stage requires providing a list of your web pages or apps <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:16">[00:02:16]</a>.
*   While having a website is important, social media profiles also count as a web page <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:21">[00:02:21]</a>.
*   A video tutorial for [[steps_to_solve_facebook_account_issues | creating a Facebook page]] that can be used for promoting products or a YouTube channel is available <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:41">[00:02:41]</a>.
*   Provide the link to your chosen website or social media profile <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:55">[00:02:55]</a>.

### 3. Profile Information

The third stage is to complete your profile details <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:27">[00:03:27]</a>:
*   **Amazon Store ID**: Enter your desired Amazon Store ID (e.g., "tutorial for everyone") <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:33">[00:03:33]</a>.
*   **Website Content**: Describe what your web pages are about, for example, "computer utility" or "to put products" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:48">[00:03:48]</a>. This could involve recommending products to friends or family to earn commission <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:05">[00:04:05]</a>.
*   **Related Categories**: Select the categories that best relate to your website, such as "computer technology" and "software technology" for primary and "games" for secondary <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:36">[00:04:36]</a>.
*   **Amazon Products to Publish**: Choose the types of Amazon products you wish to publish on your website, like toys, movies, music, clothes, books, electronics, computers, and office electronics <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:58">[00:04:58]</a>.
*   **Website Type**: Indicate the type of your website, for instance, a "blog portal" or a "content web page" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:13">[00:05:13]</a>.
*   **Traffic Generation**: Explain how you will generate traffic to your website, such as through forums, social networks, and advertisements <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:29">[00:05:29]</a>.
*   **Monetization Strategy**: Describe how you will use your website to generate income, typically through affiliate ads <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:45">[00:05:45]</a>.
*   **Link Generation**: Specify how you usually generate links, for example, using blog editors <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:06">[00:06:06]</a>.
*   **Monthly Unique Visits**: Provide an estimate of your monthly unique visits (e.g., between 500 and 5,000) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:11">[00:06:11]</a>.
*   **Main Reason for Joining**: State the primary reason for adding the Amazon program, which is typically to monetize your site <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:16">[00:06:16]</a>.
*   **How You Learned About Us**: Indicate how you found out about the Amazon Associates program <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:21">[00:06:21]</a>.

### Captcha and Terms

Finally, enter the characters from the captcha and accept the terms and conditions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:36">[00:06:36]</a>. Ensure you read the terms in advance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:41">[00:06:41]</a>.

## Post-Registration

After submitting your application, Amazon will review your websites once you have generated three assigned sales <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:51">[00:06:51]</a>. Although you can enter your bank details immediately, it is recommended to do so after your application has been accepted and you have made the three required sales <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:06">[00:07:06]</a>. Your sites must be corroborated and confirmed to be of quality <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:21">[00:07:21]</a>.

Once the process is complete, you will be redirected to the Amazon page and are ready to start recommending products <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:38">[00:07:38]</a>.
