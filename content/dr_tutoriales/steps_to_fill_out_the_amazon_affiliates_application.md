From: [[dr_tutoriales]] <br/> 

The Amazon Affiliates program allows users to [[generating_commissions_from_amazon_affiliates | earn commissions]] by recommending Amazon products. When people purchase products through a user's recommendation, a commission is generated for the recommender <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:07">[00:00:07]</a>. This program is free to join and can provide real benefits <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:29">[00:04:29]</a>.

## Accessing the Amazon Affiliates Platform

To begin [[creating_an_amazon_affiliates_account | creating an Amazon Affiliates account]], navigate to the Amazon homepage <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:44">[00:00:44]</a>. Search for "Amazon Affiliates" or "affiliate program" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>. Once on the Amazon Affiliates page, click on "Join for free" located at the top right <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:13">[00:01:13]</a>. You will then need to log in to your Amazon account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:22">[00:01:22]</a>.

## Application Stages

The [[creating_an_amazon_affiliates_account | Amazon Affiliates application]] process involves four main stages <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>:
1.  Filling in account information <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:33">[00:01:33]</a>.
2.  Providing a list of websites or apps <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:35">[00:01:35]</a>.
3.  Modifying your profile <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:42">[00:01:42]</a>.
4.  Starting to [[using_amazon_affiliate_account | use Affiliate Central]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:45">[00:01:45]</a>.

### Stage 1: Account Information

The first step is to fill out the basic information for your account, starting with your address <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:48">[00:01:48]</a>. If you already have an Amazon account, much of this information may be pre-filled <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:51">[00:01:51]</a>. Identify the main owner and beneficiary of the account. You will also be asked about your tax status for U.S. tax purposes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:00">[00:02:00]</a>. After completing this section, click "Next" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:13">[00:02:13]</a>.

### Stage 2: Website or App List

It is important to have a website for the program <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:18">[00:02:18]</a>. However, individuals without a traditional website can use their social media profiles (e.g., Facebook page, YouTube channel) as their web presence <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:33">[00:02:33]</a>. You will need to copy and paste the link to your chosen website or social media profile into the application <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:50">[00:02:50]</a>. After adding the link, click "Next" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:22">[00:03:22]</a>.

### Stage 3: Profile Information

This stage involves providing details about your profile and website's purpose <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:27">[00:03:27]</a>:
*   **Website Description**: Describe what your website or social media page is about (e.g., "tutorial for everyone", "products") <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:30">[00:03:30]</a>.
*   **Categories**: Select the categories that best relate to your website, such as "computer technology" and "software technology", or "games" as a secondary subject <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:33">[00:04:33]</a>.
*   **Amazon Products**: Indicate what kind of Amazon products you intend to publish or recommend on your website (e.g., toys, movies, music, clothes, books, electronics, computers, office supplies) <a class="yt="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:58">[00:04:58]</a>.
*   **Website Type**: Specify the type of your website (e.g., blog, portal, content web page) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:12">[00:05:12]</a>.
*   **Traffic Generation**: Explain how you will generate traffic to your website (e.g., forums, social networks, advertisements) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:29">[00:05:29]</a>.
*   **Monetization**: Describe how you will generate income from your website, such as through affiliate ads <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:44">[00:05:44]</a>.
*   **Link Creation**: How you will normally generate links (e.g., blog editors) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:04">[00:06:04]</a>.
*   **Monthly Visits**: Estimate your website's unique monthly visits (e.g., between 500 and 5000) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:11">[00:06:11]</a>.
*   **Main Reason to Join**: The primary reason for joining the Amazon program is to monetize the site <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:16">[00:06:16]</a>.
*   **How You Heard About Us**: How you learned about the program (e.g., through search) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:21">[00:06:21]</a>.

Finally, enter the displayed characters for verification and accept the terms and conditions (after reading them) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:36">[00:06:36]</a>.

## Post-Application and [[navigating_the_amazon_affiliates_platform | Affiliate Central]]

After submitting your application, you will receive a confirmation message <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:49">[00:06:49]</a>. Your application will be reviewed once you have generated three assigned sales <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:54">[00:06:54]</a>. Amazon will also review your websites to confirm their quality <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:57">[00:06:57]</a>.

You can enter your bank details immediately or wait until your application has been accepted <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:06">[00:07:06]</a>. Once accepted, you will be redirected to the Amazon Affiliates page, ready to start [[creating_amazon_affiliate_links | recommending products]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:38">[00:07:38]</a>.
