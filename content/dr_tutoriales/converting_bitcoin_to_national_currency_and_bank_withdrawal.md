This tutorial outlines how to sell Bitcoin, transform it into other cryptocurrencies or national currencies, and then withdraw those funds to a bank account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:05">[00:00:05]</a>. It emphasizes the importance of declaring taxes on any profits in your country of residence <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:33">[00:00:33]</a>.

## Converting Bitcoin

The process involves first having Bitcoin in your account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:21">[00:01:21]</a>.

### Converting Bitcoin to Other Cryptocurrencies

It is generally recommended to [[selling_bitcoin_and_converting_to_other_cryptocurrencies | convert Bitcoin to another cryptocurrency]] instead of directly to national currency to potentially maximize profits <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:39">[00:01:39]</a>. This is because national currencies like the dollar tend to devalue over time compared to cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:51">[00:01:51]</a>.

### Converting Bitcoin to National Currency (Fiat)

To [[converting_cryptocurrency_to_fiat_currency | convert Bitcoin to a national currency]] (e.g., euros or dollars), you must convert it if you wish to [[transferring_cryptocurrency_to_a_bank_account | withdraw your capital from the platform]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:04">[00:02:04]</a>.

1.  **Select Market:** Navigate to the specific market for your desired currency pair, such as "BTC Euro" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:12">[00:02:12]</a>.
2.  **Sell Bitcoin:** On the Bitcoin-to-fiat currency chart page, select the "sell Bitcoin" option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:42">[00:02:42]</a>. This action transforms your Bitcoin into euros <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:51">[00:02:51]</a>.
3.  **Specify Amount:** Choose the amount of Bitcoin you wish to sell <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:56">[00:02:56]</a>. A minimum quantity of 10 euros is required for sales <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:57">[00:03:57]</a>.
4.  **Execute Sale:** After defining the amount, confirm by selecting "sell Bitcoin" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:05">[00:03:05]</a>. The equivalent amount in your chosen national currency will appear in your wallet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:08">[00:03:08]</a>. Sales can be made as a market order, directly transforming the cryptocurrency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:18">[00:04:18]</a>.

### Converting Other Cryptocurrencies to National Currency

Some cryptocurrencies cannot be directly [[converting_crypto_to_fiat_currency | converted to euros]] (e.g., Volkswat) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:22">[00:03:22]</a>. In such cases, a two-step process is required:
1.  **First Conversion:** [[methods_to_convert_cryptocurrencies | Convert the cryptocurrency]] (e.g., Volkswat) to Bitcoin or another compatible currency like the dollar <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:28">[00:03:28]</a>.
2.  **Second Conversion:** From Bitcoin, then [[converting_cryptocurrency_to_fiat_currency_on_binance | transfer it to your national currency]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:37">[00:03:37]</a>.

## Withdrawing National Currency to Bank Account

Once your Bitcoin or other cryptocurrency has been converted into national currency, you can [[best_practices_for_withdrawing_cryptocurrencies_to_bank_accounts | withdraw it to your bank account]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:41">[00:04:41]</a>.

1.  **Access Wallet:** Go to the "wallet" section within the platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:58">[00:04:58]</a>.
2.  **Select Withdrawal:** Choose the "withdraw" option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:08">[00:05:08]</a>.
3.  **Choose Currency and Method:** Select your national currency (e.g., Euro) and your preferred payment method <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:52">[00:05:52]</a>.
    *   **Bank Transfer:** This method is generally recommended due to lower commissions (e.g., 0.8%) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:00">[00:06:00]</a>.
    *   **Bank Card:** While faster (up to 30 minutes for transfer), this method incurs higher commissions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:54">[00:06:54]</a>.
4.  **Enter Amount:** Input the amount you wish to [[withdrawing_money_from_coinbase_to_bank_account | withdraw]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:21">[00:06:21]</a>.
    *   **Minimum Withdrawal:** A minimum amount (e.g., 3 euros for bank transfer, 10 euros for bank card) is required <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:16">[00:06:16]</a><a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:59">[00:06:59]</a>.
    *   **Maximum Withdrawal:** Maximums apply (e.g., 5,000 euros for bank card, 50,000 euros for bank transfer) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:07">[00:07:07]</a><a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:15">[00:07:15]</a>.
5.  **Provide Bank Details:** Enter your bank account information (IBAN) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:28">[00:06:28]</a>.
6.  **Confirm:** Select "continue" to finalize the withdrawal request <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:31">[00:06:31]</a>.
7.  **Processing Time:** Funds typically appear in your bank account within one or two business days for bank transfers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:34">[00:06:34]</a>.

It is always advisable to [[withdrawing_funds_from_coinbase_wallet_to_bank_account | save on commissions]] where possible <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:09">[00:06:09]</a>.
