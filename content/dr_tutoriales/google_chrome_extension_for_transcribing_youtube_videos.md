## The Glass Extension

The extension used for this purpose within [[using_google_chrome_for_online_transcription | Google Chrome]] is called "Glass" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:14">[00:00:14]</a>. This extension leverages ChatGPT to convert [[steps_to_transcribe_youtube_videos_to_text | YouTube videos to text]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>.

### Installation and Compatibility

The Glass extension can be installed directly from the Chrome website <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:34">[00:00:34]</a>. While the primary focus is on [[using_google_chrome_for_online_transcription | Google Chrome]], the speaker also mentions the possibility of installing "Google Chrome in Safari" and using it with [[integration_with_python_for_transcribing_videos | Python]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:23">[00:00:23]</a>.

## How to Transcribe a YouTube Video

Once the Glass extension is installed in [[using_google_chrome_for_online_transcription | Google Chrome]], the process to transcribe a video is straightforward:

1.  Open a new tab in [[using_google_chrome_for_online_transcription | Google Chrome]] and navigate to the [[using_google_chrome_for_youtube | YouTube]] platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:40">[00:00:40]</a>.
2.  Select and enter any [[using_google_chrome_for_youtube | YouTube]] video <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>. For example, a video discussing anime or manga <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:48">[00:00:48]</a>.
3.  After the extension is installed, a small window labeled "transcript and summary" will appear on the video page <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:57">[00:00:57]</a>.
4.  Click on this window to reveal the full transcript of the video <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:02">[00:01:02]</a>. The transcript breaks down the dialogue into paragraphs corresponding to specific seconds or sequences within the video <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>. The entire video's dialogue will be available, from start to finish <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:15">[00:01:15]</a>.

### Copying the Transcript

The transcribed text can be easily copied for other uses:

*   **Manual Selection:** Users can select the text directly from the transcript window and copy it to a word processor like Word <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:27">[00:01:27]</a>.
*   **Direct Copy:** There is also a "Copy as text" option available, allowing for direct copying of the entire transcript <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:34">[00:01:34]</a>. Once copied, the text can be pasted wherever desired <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:40">[00:01:40]</a>.
