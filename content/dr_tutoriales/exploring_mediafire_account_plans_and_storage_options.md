Mediafire is a free platform for uploading files to the cloud and sharing them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:11">[00:00:11]</a>. When [[creating_a_mediafire_account | creating a Mediafire account]], users are presented with different plans <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>.

## Basic (Free) Account
The most common option is the basic, free account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:12">[00:01:12]</a>.
*   **Storage Limit:** This free account has a maximum storage capacity of 10 gigabytes (GB) for files in the cloud <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:13">[00:01:13]</a>.
*   **Potential for More Storage:** It is possible to get up to 50 GB of storage on a basic Mediafire account, with a guide typically provided for this purpose <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:24">[00:01:24]</a>.

## Premium Accounts
Mediafire also offers premium user accounts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:46">[00:03:46]</a>. A notable feature unique to premium users is the ability to upload entire websites <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:41">[00:03:41]</a>. This option is not available to users with a basic account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:48">[00:03:48]</a>.
