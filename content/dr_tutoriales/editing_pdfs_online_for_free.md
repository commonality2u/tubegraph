## Why Edit PDFs Online?

Using an online PDF editor offers significant advantages:
*   **No software installation required**: You can edit PDFs directly in your web browser <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:09">[00:00:09]</a>.
*   **Completely free**: The recommended editor does not impose watermarks, require account registration, or demand payments for downloading edited files <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>.
