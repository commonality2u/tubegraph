The N26 Bank app is designed to provide an easy, efficient, and useful way to manage banking operations from a mobile device, helping to optimize financial transactions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:07">[00:00:07]</a>. N26 is characterized as a bank that does not charge commissions for domestic or international transfers, addressing a common issue with many traditional banks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:29">[00:00:29]</a>. The app's interface is consistent across both iPhone and Android operating systems <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:19">[00:01:19]</a>.

## App Interface Overview

The N26 app features two main menus:
*   **Lower Menu:** Includes "start," "spaces," "exploring," and "cards" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:45">[00:01:45]</a>.
*   **Upper Menu:** Offers options to view profile, access help/questions, see statistics, upcoming payments, statements, and search for transactions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:15">[00:02:15]</a>.

## Profile Management

Under the profile section, users can find:
*   **Account Information:** Displays the account's IBAN <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:39">[00:02:39]</a>.
*   **QR Code:** Allows for immediate transaction reception <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:42">[00:02:42]</a>.
*   **Visibility Settings:** Can be activated so other N26 users can send money instantly, add to contacts via QR code, phone, or email <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:50">[00:02:50]</a>. This feature is similar to common mobile-to-mobile transfers associated with bank accounts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:06">[00:03:06]</a>.
*   **Invitation Program:** Users can invite friends to open an N26 account, potentially earning five euros for both parties <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:36">[00:03:36]</a>.
*   **Configuration:** Access personal information, application settings (language, proximity sensor mode, promotional codes, marketing communications, licenses), and security options like changing PIN or password <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:48">[00:03:48]</a>. Users can also log out and open a new session for a different account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:08">[00:04:08]</a>.
*   **Bank Change Option:** The app provides an option to manage a bank change from an existing account, transferring addresses and payments to N26 accounts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:23">[00:04:23]</a>.

## Help and Support

The "questions" section allows users to access help and support resources <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:05">[00:05:05]</a>. This includes:
*   Accessing different sections depending on queries <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:08">[00:05:08]</a>.
*   Live chat with a robot that can escalate to a physical person <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:12">[00:05:12]</a>.
*   Information on payments, transfers, account types, and subscriptions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:20">[00:05:20]</a>.
*   Verification is required to access private information during a chat session <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:29">[00:05:29]</a>.

## Transactions and Balance

The app provides robust features for managing funds:
*   **Current Balance:** Can be toggled between visible and invisible <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:05">[00:06:05]</a>.
*   **Transaction History:** Users can view all past transactions and filter them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:55">[00:05:55]</a>.
*   **Receiving Money:** Options include bank transfer, debit/credit card, or QR code <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:23">[00:06:23]</a>.
*   **Sending Money:** Various methods available:
    *   Moneymbim (instant transfer to other N26 users) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:44">[00:06:44]</a>.
    *   Bank transfer (commission-free) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:51">[00:06:51]</a>.
    *   Foreign currency transfer (commission-free) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:54">[00:06:54]</a>.
    *   Scan QR code <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:57">[00:06:57]</a>.
    *   Option to donate to Women for Women International <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:00">[00:07:00]</a>.
*   **Adding Money:** Funds can be transferred to the N26 account via various payment methods <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:16">[00:07:16]</a>. The first transfer by credit or debit card is free <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:37">[00:07:37]</a>.

## [[card_and_space_management_in_n26 | Card Management]]

The app facilitates full control over N26 cards:
*   **Physical Card Requirement:** A minimum of ten euros in the account is required to issue a physical card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:08">[00:07:08]</a>.
*   **Ordering Physical Card:** Users can choose their card type based on their account service (e.g., transparent for the free account) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:48">[00:08:48]</a>. Shipping options include normal delivery (two weeks, ten euros) or express (three days) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:07">[00:09:07]</a>.
*   **Virtual Card and Mobile Payments:** An active virtual card is provided, which can be added to [[card_and_space_management_in_n26 | Apple Wallet]] or Android Wallet for mobile payments even before receiving the physical card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:36">[00:10:36]</a>.
*   **Card Settings:** Options to view card information, lock the card, and configure settings such as enabling/disabling payments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:43">[00:11:43]</a>.
*   **Withdrawals:** The standard (basic) account offers free withdrawals in extra currency, though it's a limited quantity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:07">[00:12:07]</a>. Premium accounts offer unlimited withdrawals <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:14">[00:12:14]</a>.

## Spaces (Subaccounts)

The "Spaces" feature allows users to organize their money:
*   **Purpose:** Create up to two space subaccounts to organize money, set savings goals, and diversify portfolios (e.g., a space to save, another to spend) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:50">[00:12:50]</a>.
*   **Functionality:** Users can create, delete, and move money between spaces <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:53">[00:12:53]</a>.
*   **Premium Feature:** "Spaces" are generally a premium feature, though a trial period (e.g., 59 or 60 days) may be offered for standard accounts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:57">[00:12:57]</a>.
*   **Customization:** Edit account spaces, receive/send money within them, rename, change images, and view excerpts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:17">[00:13:17]</a>.

## Explore Section

The "Explore" section of the app displays:
*   **Offers:** Various exclusive offers that update over time (e.g., discounts on Reebok, data plans) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:46">[00:14:46]</a>. More offers are accessible with a Premium Plan <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:01">[00:15:01]</a>.
*   **Account Service:** Details of the user's contracted service, such as N26 Standard <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:03">[00:14:03]</a>.
*   **Other Account Types:** Information on different N26 bank cards and services available, including N26 Metal (more expensive, seventeen euros/month) and N26 You (around ninety euros/month), which offer benefits like more free withdrawals, physical cards, and additional subaccounts for managing capital <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:16">[00:14:16]</a>.

The N26 app aims to allow users to optimize their finances with minimal expense while accessing a range of services <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:18">[00:15:18]</a>.
