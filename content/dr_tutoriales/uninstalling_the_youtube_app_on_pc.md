This guide explains how to uninstall the YouTube application from your PC.

## How to Uninstall YouTube

If you've previously [[how_to_download_youtube_on_pc | downloaded YouTube]] to your computer and now wish to remove it, follow these steps:

1.  Close the YouTube application if it is currently open <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:41:00">[01:41:00]</a>.
2.  Open your PC's search function <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:43:00">[01:43:00]</a>.
3.  Type "control panel" into the search bar and access it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:45:00">[01:45:00]</a>.
4.  Within the Control Panel, select "Uninstall a program" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:50:00">[01:50:00]</a>.
5.  Locate "YouTube" in the list of programs. It may take a moment for the list to finish loading <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:52:00">[01:52:00]</a>.
6.  Right-click on "YouTube" once it appears <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:57:00">[01:57:00]</a>.
7.  Click "Uninstall" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:58:00">[01:58:00]</a>.

> [!NOTE]
> The YouTube application, once installed, creates a [[creating_a_youtube_shortcut_on_pc | shortcut]] on your desktop <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:48:00">[00:48:00]</a>. You can also find it by searching "YouTube" in your PC's search engine <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:53:00">[00:53:00]</a>. For quick access, the app can be [[pinning_youtube_to_taskbar | pinned to the taskbar]] by right-clicking its icon and selecting "anchor to the taskbar" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:56:00">[00:56:00]</a>.
