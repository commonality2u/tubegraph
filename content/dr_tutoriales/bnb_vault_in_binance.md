The BNB Vault (also known as BNB Bolt or BNB Trunk) is a financial product offered by Binance that allows users to earn returns by investing the BNB cryptocurrency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:05">[00:00:05]</a>. This feature is designed to provide good profitability by yielding other cryptocurrencies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:18">[00:00:18]</a>.

## Key Features

*   **BNB-Exclusive Investment**: The BNB Vault only allows investment of the BNB cryptocurrency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:06">[00:02:06]</a>. Other cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin, Ethereum, or Polkadot cannot be invested in this product <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:28">[00:00:28]</a>.
*   **Automatic Transfer Option**: Users can set up an automatic daily transfer at 1 AM (UTC) to move available BNB balances from their spot accounts directly into the BNB Vault <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:21">[00:02:21]</a>. This option can be activated or deactivated <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:38">[00:02:38]</a>.
*   **Flexible Savings**: BNB Vault offers a flexible savings model, meaning users can withdraw their invested BNB whenever they wish <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:26">[00:05:26]</a>. It is not subject to [[blocked_savings_on_binance | locked staking]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:30">[00:05:30]</a>.

## Accessing BNB Vault

To access the BNB Vault, navigate to the "Binance" menu at the top of the platform, then select the "Finance" section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:23">[00:01:23]</a>. Within "Finance," you will find various products offered by Binance, including the BNB Vault <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:33">[00:01:33]</a>.

## How to Invest

To invest BNB in the BNB Vault:
1.  Go to the BNB Vault section.
2.  Select the "Stake" option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:45">[00:02:45]</a>.
3.  Choose the amount of BNB you wish to invest <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:47">[00:02:47]</a>.
4.  Accept the service agreement <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:49">[00:02:49]</a>.

## Profitability and Rewards

The primary purpose of the BNB Vault is to generate other cryptocurrencies as rewards <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:17">[00:01:17]</a>.

### BNB Vault vs. Launchpool

A key differential benefit of the BNB Vault lies in its ability to provide rewards from multiple sources, unlike investing directly in [[Launchpool]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:56">[00:03:56]</a>:

*   **Launchpool Integration**: The BNB Vault gives rewards based on what is available in the [[Launchpool]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:09">[00:03:09]</a>. [[Launchpool]] is a tool that allows users to generate cryptocurrencies by using others as a reference <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:15">[00:03:15]</a>. For example, by staking BNB, users can obtain cryptocurrencies like LIT and AEVO, earning annual returns of approximately 5% for LIT and 3.76% for AEVO <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:32">[00:03:32]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:36">[00:03:36]</a>.
*   **Dual Rewards**: When investing BNB in the BNB Vault, users receive rewards from two sources <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:13">[00:04:13]</a>:
    1.  From the [[Launchpool]] projects <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:20">[00:04:20]</a>.
    2.  From the savings reward (interest on the BNB itself) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:24">[00:04:24]</a>.
*   **Single Reward (Launchpool Direct)**: If BNB is invested directly into the [[Launchpool]], users would only receive the cryptocurrencies offered by the [[Launchpool]] system <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:30">[00:04:30]</a>.
*   **Conclusion**: Investing in the BNB Vault yields more cryptocurrencies and benefits compared to direct investment in [[Launchpool]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:38">[00:04:38]</a>.

### Additional Rewards

Beyond [[Launchpool]] and savings rewards, the BNB Vault can also provide other random rewards based on periodic programs released by Binance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:48">[00:04:48]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:11">[00:05:11]</a>.
