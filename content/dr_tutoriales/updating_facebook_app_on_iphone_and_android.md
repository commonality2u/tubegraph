## Checking for Updates

To update or download the [[facebook_application_setup_on_mobile_phones | Facebook application]]:
1.  Go to your device's application store (e.g., App Store for iPhone, Play Store for Android) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>.
2.  Search for "Facebook" in the search bar <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:06">[00:01:06]</a>.
3.  Select the [[facebook_application_setup_on_mobile_phones | Facebook application]] from the search results <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:06">[00:01:06]</a>.
4.  If an update is available, tap "Update" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>.
5.  If the app is already at its maximum updated version, tap "Open" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:13">[00:01:13]</a>.
