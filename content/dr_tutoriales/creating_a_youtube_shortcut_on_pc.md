## Installing YouTube on PC

1.  **Open a Web Browser**: You can use any search engine like Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, Opera, or Firefox <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:07">[00:00:07]</a>. The example uses Chrome <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>.
2.  **Navigate to YouTube**: Open a new tab and go directly to the YouTube website <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>.
3.  **Install the App**: Once on the YouTube page, look for the "Install YouTube" option, often represented by a computer icon with a download symbol, usually located near the address bar <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:29">[00:00:29]</a>. Click this to [[how_to_download_youtube_on_pc | install the app]] within YouTube <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[00:00:37]</a>. The application will then begin [[how_to_download_youtube_on_pc | downloading]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:39">[00:00:39]</a>.

## Finding and Accessing the YouTube Shortcut

After the [[how_to_download_youtube_on_pc | download]] is complete <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>:
*   A desktop [[creating_a_desktop_shortcut_for_youtube_music | shortcut]] will be [[creating_a_desktop_shortcut_for_youtube_music | created]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>.
*   You can also find it by typing "YouTube" into your PC's search bar <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:53">[00:00:53]</a>.

### Pinning YouTube to the Taskbar

To easily access YouTube, you can [[pinning_youtube_to_taskbar | pin it to the taskbar]]:
1.  Locate the YouTube shortcut (either on the desktop or via search).
2.  Right-click on the YouTube icon <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:58">[00:00:58]</a>.
3.  Select the option to [[pinning_youtube_to_taskbar | anchor to the taskbar]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>. The icon will then appear on your taskbar for quick access <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:02">[00:01:02]</a>.

## Uninstalling the YouTube App on PC

If you need to [[uninstalling_the_youtube_app_on_pc | uninstall the YouTube app]], follow these steps:

1.  **Access Control Panel**: Go to your PC's search bar and type "Control Panel" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:41">[00:01:41]</a>.
2.  **Go to Programs**: Within the Control Panel, click on "Uninstall program" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:50">[00:01:50]</a>.
3.  **Find and Uninstall YouTube**: Locate "YouTube" in the list of programs. Right-click on it and select "Uninstall" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:52">[00:01:52]</a>.
