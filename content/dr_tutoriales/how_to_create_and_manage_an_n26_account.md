This article provides a detailed guide on how to [[navigating_the_n26_app_for_basic_operations | use the N26 application]] from a mobile device, optimizing its operation for efficiency and usefulness <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:07">[00:00:07]</a>. ## Creating an [[introduction_to_n26_bank_and_its_features | N26 Account]]

A prior video tutorial explains the step-by-step process of [[introduction_to_n26_bank_and_its_features | creating an N26 account]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:24">[00:00:24]</a>. [[introduction_to_n26_bank_and_its_features | N26]] is known for not charging commissions on domestic or [[the_benefits_of_n26_for_international_transactions | international transfers]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>. Using a referral code can provide a five-euro bonus upon account creation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:55">[00:00:55]</a>.

## App Navigation and Menus

The [[navigating_the_n26_app_for_basic_operations | N26 application]] features two main menus:
*   **Bottom Menu**: Includes "start," "spaces," and "cards" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:09">[00:02:09]</a>.
*   **Top Menu**: Offers options to view your profile, access questions/help, see statistics/upcoming payments/statements, and search transactions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:14">[00:02:14]</a>.

The standard, basic account is free and does not incur monthly payments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:54">[00:01:54]</a>.

## Profile Management

Under the profile section in the top menu, you can find:
*   **Account Details**: Your IBAN and [[using_qr_codes_and_instant_transfers_with_n26 | QR code]] for instant transactions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:39">[00:02:39]</a>.
    *   To receive money instantly from other [[introduction_to_n26_bank_and_its_features | N26]] users via [[using_qr_codes_and_instant_transfers_with_n26 | QR code]], phone, or email, your visibility must be activated <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:50">[00:02:50]</a>.
*   **Invite Friends**: Option to invite friends and grant them five euros if they open an account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:36">[00:03:36]</a>.
*   **Configuration**: Personal information, app settings, and security options like changing your PIN or password <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:48">[00:03:48]</a>. You can also select the language and activate the proximity sensor mode <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:58">[00:03:58]</a>.
*   **Change Bank**: Provides an option to transfer addresses and payments from another bank to your [[introduction_to_n26_bank_and_its_features | N26 account]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:27">[00:04:27]</a>. However, some users might prefer to keep their national bank and use [[introduction_to_n26_bank_and_its_features | N26]] as an intermediary for [[the_benefits_of_n26_for_international_transactions | free international transactions]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:52">[00:04:52]</a>.

## Adding Funds to Your Account

To activate card issuance, you need at least ten euros in your account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:08">[00:07:08]</a>.
*   The first deposit by credit or debit card into [[introduction_to_n26_bank_and_its_features | N26]] is free <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:37">[00:07:37]</a>.
*   You can specify the amount to add (e.g., 50 euros) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:57">[00:07:57]</a>.
*   The app will prompt for card details and authentication for security <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:11">[00:08:11]</a>. Funds are immediately available upon successful authentication <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:23">[00:08:23]</a>.

## Card Management

*   **Requesting a Physical Card**: After depositing funds, you can request a physical card for ATM withdrawals and in-store payments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:37">[00:08:37]</a>.
    *   For the free account, the transparent card is selected <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:01">[00:09:01]</a>.
    *   Normal delivery costs ten euros and takes about two weeks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:19">[00:09:19]</a>. Express delivery (three business days) is also an option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:10">[00:09:10]</a>.
*   **Virtual Card and Mobile Payments**:
    *   A virtual card is immediately active <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:36">[00:10:36]</a>.
    *   You can add this virtual card to Apple Wallet or Android Pay for mobile payments even before receiving the physical card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:24">[00:10:24]</a>. The process involves confirming terms and conditions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:55">[00:10:55]</a>.
*   **Card Settings**: The app allows you to view card information, lock the card, and configure payment settings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:43">[00:11:43]</a>.
*   **Withdrawals**: The basic account offers free withdrawals in extra currency up to a certain quantity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:07">[00:12:07]</a>.

## Receiving and Sending Money

The home screen provides options for managing funds:
*   **Receive Money**: Options include bank transfer, debit/credit card to PLP, or [[using_qr_codes_and_instant_transfers_with_n26 | requesting money with a QR code]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:23">[00:06:23]</a>.
*   **Send Money**: Various methods are available:
    *   **MoneyBeam**: Instant transfers to other [[introduction_to_n26_bank_and_its_features | N26]] account holders <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:44">[00:06:44]</a>.
    *   Bank Transfer: Free bank transfers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:50">[00:06:50]</a>.
    *   Foreign Currency Transfer: Also totally free <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:54">[00:06:54]</a>.
    *   [[using_qr_codes_and_instant_transfers_with_n26 | Scan QR code]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:57">[00:06:57]</a>.
    *   Option to donate to Women for Women International is also present <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:00">[00:07:00]</a>.

## Account Features

### Spaces
*   **Concept**: "Spaces" allow you to create sub-accounts to organize money, set savings goals, and diversify your finances <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:50">[00:12:50]</a>.
*   **Availability**: Standard accounts typically get a 59-day trial <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:44">[00:12:44]</a>, after which this feature is only available for premium users <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:06">[00:13:06]</a>.
*   **Management**: Within a space, you can receive money, send money, modify settings, view transactions, and personalize its name or image <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:19">[00:13:19]</a>.

### Explore
*   **Offers**: This section displays exclusive offers (e.g., discounts at Reebok) that are regularly updated <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:46">[00:14:46]</a>. Premium plans grant access to more offers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:01">[00:15:01]</a>.
*   **Account Types**: You can view details of your current service (e.g., [[introduction_to_n26_bank_and_its_features | N26 Standard]]) and explore other [[introduction_to_n26_bank_and_its_features | N26]] account types like [[introduction_to_n26_bank_and_its_features | N26 Metal]] (more expensive, around 17 euros/month) and [[introduction_to_n26_bank_and_its_features | N26 You]] (around 90 euros/month) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:16">[00:14:16]</a>. These premium accounts often include unlimited withdrawals, physical cards, and more "spaces" sub-accounts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:31">[00:14:31]</a>.

## Support and Help

The "questions" section provides access to help, allowing users to chat with a robot that can escalate to a physical person, and find information on payments, transfers, account types, and subscriptions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:05">[00:05:05]</a>. Verification using a paired device is required for accessing private information during a chat <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:29">[00:05:29]</a>.
