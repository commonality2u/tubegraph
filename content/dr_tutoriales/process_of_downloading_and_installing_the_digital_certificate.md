This article details the process of downloading and [[installing_digital_certificates_on_iphone | installing a digital certificate]] from an electronic ID card (DNIe) onto a mobile device, specifically an iPhone.

## Prerequisites <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>

Before attempting to install the electronic ID card's digital certificate on a mobile device, an essential [[requirements_for_embedding_a_digital_certificate | requirement]] is to first embed it within a computer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>. This means the digital certificate must be previously installed on the computer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>, a step that precedes mobile installation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:36">[00:00:36]</a>. Without completing this initial step, the mobile installation process will not be successful <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>.

### NFC Activation

On Android devices, the NFC (Near Field Communication) antenna needs to be activated <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:20">[00:01:20]</a>. For iPhone devices, the NFC antenna is already activated by default <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:22">[00:01:22]</a>. This antenna facilitates interactions like mobile payments in establishments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:24">[00:01:24]</a>.

## Application Download and Installation

### Steps for Installation

1.  **Download the App**: Navigate to the App Store on your iPhone <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:52">[00:01:52]</a>. Search for "Digital Certificate" or "DNIe" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:48">[00:01:48]</a>. The application you are looking for is typically called "Digital Certificate" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:48">[00:01:48]</a>.
2.  **Verify Creator**: Ensure the application's creator is "certified digital FNT" to guarantee its authenticity and security <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:03">[00:02:03]</a>.

### Using the Application

1.  **Open the App**: Once downloaded, open the "Digital Certificate" app <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:13">[00:02:13]</a>.
2.  **Select Option**: Choose to "Request the digital certificate" or "Manage Digital Certificates Installed" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:21">[00:02:21]</a>. The process described focuses on reading the chip of the DNIe <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:40">[00:02:40]</a>.
3.  **Accept Terms**: Accept the terms and conditions presented by the application <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:46">[00:02:46]</a>.
4.  **Email Verification**: Enter your email address for verification <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:54">[00:02:54]</a>. A verification code will be sent to this email, which you will then enter into the app <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:00">[00:03:00]</a>.
5.  **DNIe Codes**:
    *   Enter the CAN code found on your physical DNIe <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:12">[00:03:12]</a>.
    *   Enter the PIN associated with your DNIe <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:20">[00:03:20]</a>. If you do not have or remember your PIN, you may need to visit a police station or refer to other tutorials for DNIe PIN management <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:23">[00:03:23]</a>.
6.  **Scan DNIe Chip**: Place your DNIe on a smooth surface without its case <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:34">[00:03:34]</a>. Place your phone near the top of the DNIe, where the chip is located, until it detects the chip <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:37">[00:03:37]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:41">[00:03:41]</a>.
7.  **Confirm Certificate Request**: Once detected, the app will display your data and prompt you to confirm the expedition of the Citizen's Certificate issued by the National Factory <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:51">[00:03:51]</a>.

## Certificate Installation on Mobile Device

### Final Steps

1.  **Notification**: After requesting, you will receive a notification when your certificate is available for installation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:04">[00:04:04]</a>. You can also check "Pending Requests" within the app <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:11">[00:04:11]</a>.
2.  **Install on Mobile**: Click on "Install on mobile device" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:14">[00:04:14]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:20">[00:04:20]</a>.
3.  **Password and Download**: Create a password for the certificate <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:28">[00:04:28]</a>. The app will then proceed to download your digital certificate <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:31">[00:04:31]</a>.
4.  **Allow Profile Download**: A website will attempt to download a profile <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:39">[00:04:39]</a>. Allow this action.
5.  **Install Profile**:
    *   Go to the "View Certificate Installed" section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:54">[00:04:54]</a>.
    *   Locate the downloaded profile, typically under "VPN & Device Management" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:57">[00:04:57]</a>.
    *   Tap "Install," enter your device's passcode, and then the password you created for the certificate <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:05">[00:05:05]</a>.

Upon successful completion, your digital certificate will be installed and active on your mobile device <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:12">[00:05:12]</a>, allowing you to use it as a citizen within the mobile environment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:27">[00:05:27]</a>. This process streamlines [[managing_digital_certificates_on_mobile_devices | managing digital certificates on mobile devices]].
