To record videos with sound using your iPhone's screen recorder, you first need to ensure the screen recording feature is enabled and then activate the microphone during the recording process <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:04">[00:00:04]</a>. This capability also allows for creating [[using_iphone_screen_recorder | video tutorials]] for others <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:11">[00:00:11]</a>.

## Enabling the Screen Recorder Feature

Before activating the microphone, ensure the screen recorder is accessible in your Control Center:
1.  Enter **Settings** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:16">[00:00:16]</a>.
2.  Navigate to the **Control Center** section, identified by a gray icon <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:19">[00:00:19]</a>.
3.  Select **Customize Controls** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>.
4.  Locate and add **Screen Recording** to your included controls <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[00:00:37]</a>. You can also reorder it as desired <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>.

Once activated, sliding down from the top of your screen will reveal the screen recorder option at all times <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>.

## Activating the Microphone for Recording

The screen recorder also allows you to [[recording_internal_and_external_audio_on_iphone | record sound]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:05">[00:01:05]</a>:
*   Once the screen recorder icon is visible in your Control Center, **press and hold** it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:11">[00:01:11]</a>.
*   This action will activate the microphone, allowing you to [[recording_internal_and_external_audio_on_iphone | record videos with internal mobile sound]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:13">[00:01:13]</a>.
