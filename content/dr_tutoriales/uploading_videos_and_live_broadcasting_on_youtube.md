To gain access to various features on YouTube, including the ability to [[uploading_long_videos_on_youtube | upload longer videos]] and conduct live broadcasts, it is necessary to verify your channel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:00">[00:00:00]</a>.

## Types of Channel Verification

It's important to distinguish between two types of channel verification:

1.  **"Ticket" Verification (100,000 Subscribers)**
    This verification, indicated by a ticket icon, requires a channel to have 100,000 subscribers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:25">[00:00:25]</a>.

2.  **Phone Number Verification**
    This verification is crucial for unlocking key features related to [[publishing_videos_lives_and_posts_on_youtube | publishing videos]] and [[publishing_videos_lives_and_posts_on_youtube | live streams]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:20">[00:00:20]</a>.

## How to Verify Your Channel for Publishing Features

To check and manage verification status, navigate to YouTube Studio:

1.  Go to [[using_youtube_studio_for_video_uploads | YouTube Studio]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:40">[00:00:40]</a>.
2.  Select "Configuration" (or Settings), located on the left-hand side <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:43">[00:00:43]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:45">[00:00:45]</a>.
3.  Click on "Permissions" or "Channel Permissions" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:48">[00:00:48]</a>.
4.  Within the channel settings, you can manage features <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:54">[00:00:54]</a>.
