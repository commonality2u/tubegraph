This guide will teach you how to [[downloading_youtube_music_for_pc | download]] and install YouTube Music directly onto your Windows computer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:05">[00:00:05]</a>.

## Prerequisites

To begin, you will need a search engine installed on your computer, such as Microsoft Edge, Firefox, Opera, or Google Chrome <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>. This tutorial uses Google Chrome <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:16">[00:00:16]</a>.

## Steps to Install YouTube Music

1.  **Access the YouTube Music Website**: Open your preferred search engine and navigate to the YouTube Music website <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>. You can do this by directly typing "YouTube Music" into the search bar <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>.
2.  **Locate the Install Option**: Once on the YouTube Music website, pay close attention to the top right corner of your browser <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>. You should see a small computer symbol with a downward arrow <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:44">[00:00:44]</a>. This symbol indicates the option to "Install YouTube Music" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:47">[00:00:47]</a>.
3.  **Initiate Installation**: Click on this symbol, then click "Install" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>. The application will be directly installed onto your computer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:51">[00:00:51]</a>.
4.  **Automatic Login**: The app will automatically log in with your Google account, which is typically linked to your YouTube channel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:57">[00:00:57]</a>.

Once installed, the YouTube Music application is ready to use <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:54">[00:00:54]</a>.

## Finding and Pinning the Application

After installation, a shortcut for YouTube Music will be created on your desktop <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>. To easily access it in the future:

1.  **Search for YouTube Music**: Type "music" into your Windows search bar <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:11">[00:01:11]</a>.
2.  **Pin to Taskbar**: Locate "YouTube Music" in the search results <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:13">[00:01:13]</a>. Right-click on it and select "Pin to taskbar" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a>. This will add the app to your taskbar for quick access <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:17">[00:01:17]</a>.

## Uninstalling YouTube Music

If you wish to [[uninstalling_youtube_music_from_windows | uninstall YouTube Music]] from your computer:

1.  **Open Control Panel**: Go to the Windows search bar and type "control panel" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:36">[00:01:36]</a>.
2.  **Access Programs**: Once in the Control Panel, click on the "Uninstall a program" option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:42">[00:01:42]</a>.
3.  **Uninstall**: Find "YouTube Music" in the list of programs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:45">[00:01:45]</a>, right-click on it, and select "Uninstall" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:47">[00:01:47]</a>.
