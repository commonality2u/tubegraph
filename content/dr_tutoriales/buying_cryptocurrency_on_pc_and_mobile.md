## Buying BNB on a Computer

To buy the BNB coin on a [[managing_cryptocurrency_transactions_via_mobile_and_computer | computer]]:
1.  Navigate to the "markets" section on the [[buying_and_selling_cryptocurrencies_on_binance | Binance]] website <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:32">[00:01:32]</a>.
2.  In the "zones" section, select "everything" and search for "BNB" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:41">[00:01:41]</a>. BNB Coin is listed as the fourth in capitalization <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:47">[00:01:47]</a>.
3.  Click on "operate" next to BNB <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:01">[00:02:01]</a>. A new tab will open showing the BNB chart compared with USD (USDT), a cryptocurrency that mimics the value of the dollar <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:05">[00:02:05]</a>.
4.  You can also select other trading pairs, such as BNB/Euro, by clicking the downward arrow next to the current pair <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:36">[00:02:36]</a>.
5.  Focus on the "place order" section, where you can buy or sell <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:06">[00:03:06]</a>.
6.  Different order types are available: limit, market, and OCO (One-Cancels-the-Other) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:14">[00:03:14]</a>. For an immediate purchase, select the "market order" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:36">[00:03:36]</a>. This executes the order in real time at the current market price <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:44">[00:03:44]</a>.
7.  Enter the desired amount of BNB to buy, or the total amount in Euros (or other currency) you wish to spend <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:55">[00:03:55]</a>.
    *   For example, to buy 1,000 Euros worth of BNB, enter "1000" and click "buy" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:06">[00:04:06]</a>.
8.  Once purchased, the BNB will appear in your portfolio <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:10">[00:04:10]</a>.

## Buying BNB on a Mobile Phone

To buy BNB using the [[using_the_binance_app_on_a_mobile_phone | Binance app]] on your [[managing_cryptocurrency_transactions_via_mobile_and_computer | mobile phone]]:
1.  Download and open the [[using_the_binance_app_on_a_mobile_phone | Binance app]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:34">[00:04:34]</a>.
2.  From the homepage, go to the "markets" section in the submenu at the bottom <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:42">[00:04:42]</a>.
3.  Use the magnifying glass icon at the top to search for "BNB" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:53">[00:04:53]</a>.
4.  Choose a trading pair, such as BNB/Euro <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:07">[00:05:07]</a>. The BNB chart with the Euro will open <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:13">[00:05:13]</a>.
5.  Locate the "buying part" at the bottom of the screen and click on it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:28">[00:05:28]</a>.
6.  This page is similar to the [[managing_cryptocurrency_transactions_via_mobile_and_computer | computer]] interface, offering options to buy or sell and choose between different order types (limit, market, OCO) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:34">[00:05:34]</a>.
7.  Select the desired amount you wish to buy (e.g., 1,000 Euros) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:54">[00:05:54]</a>.
8.  Click "buy BNB" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:03">[00:06:03]</a>. If sufficient funds are available, the BNB will be purchased and appear in your portfolio <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:07">[00:06:07]</a>.

## Checking Your BNB in the Wallet

After purchasing BNB, it will appear in your "spot wallet" within your [[buying_and_selling_cryptocurrencies_on_binance | Binance]] account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:19">[00:06:19]</a>. From there, you can choose to invest your BNB to generate profitability, such as through staking <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:47">[00:06:47]</a>. Invested BNB would typically be found in the section for "products" or "staking" within the [[buying_and_selling_cryptocurrencies_on_binance | Binance]] platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:54">[00:06:54]</a>.
