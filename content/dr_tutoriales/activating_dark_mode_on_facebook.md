This tutorial explains how to set the [[activating_dark_mode_on_facebook | dark mode]] in your [[activating_dark_mode_on_facebook | Facebook application]] on a mobile phone <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:04">[00:00:04]</a>. The process works for both [[setting_facebook_to_dark_mode_for_iphone | iPhone]] and [[setting_facebook_to_dark_mode_for_android | Android]] devices <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>.

## Prerequisites

Before beginning, ensure your [[activating_dark_mode_on_facebook | Facebook application]] is downloaded and [[updating_facebook_app_for_new_features | updated]] to the latest version <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:57">[00:00:57]</a>. You can check for updates in your device's App Store <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>.

## Steps to Activate Dark Mode

1.  **Open Facebook:** Launch the [[activating_dark_mode_on_facebook | Facebook application]] on your mobile device <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:13">[00:01:13]</a>.
2.  **Access Menu:** Tap the three horizontal lines located on the right side of the bottom menu <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a>.
3.  **Navigate to Settings:** Go to the "configuration part and privacy" section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:19">[00:01:19]</a>.
4.  **Find Dark Mode:** Locate and click on the "Dark Mode" option, which is typically represented by a moon-shaped icon <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:22">[00:01:22]</a>.

## Dark Mode Options

Upon selecting Dark Mode, you will find three options to choose from <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>:

*   **System:** This is the default selected option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:34">[00:01:34]</a>. When set to [[system_settings_integration_with_facebook_dark_mode | System]], Facebook will automatically adjust its appearance to match your mobile device's system settings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:36">[00:01:36]</a>. For example, if your phone is set to dark mode, [[activating_dark_mode_on_facebook | Facebook]] will also switch to dark mode <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:43">[00:01:43]</a>. Conversely, if your phone is in bright mode (white mode), [[activating_dark_mode_on_facebook | Facebook]] will display in white mode <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:47">[00:01:47]</a>.
*   **Deactivated:** Selecting this option will disable [[activating_dark_mode_on_facebook | dark mode]] for [[activating_dark_mode_on_facebook | Facebook]], regardless of your phone's system settings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:53">[00:01:53]</a>. [[activating_dark_mode_on_facebook | Facebook]] will always appear in white <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:57">[00:01:57]</a>.
*   **Activated:** Choosing this option will force [[activating_dark_mode_on_facebook | Facebook]] into [[activating_dark_mode_on_facebook | dark mode]], even if your mobile device's system settings have dark mode disabled <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:00">[00:02:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:04">[00:02:04]</a>.

### Appearance Note

It is important to note that the [[activating_dark_mode_on_facebook | dark mode]] in [[activating_dark_mode_on_facebook | Facebook]] is not a pure black <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:20">[00:02:20]</a>. Instead, it features a shade of gray, slightly lighter than true black <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:23">[00:02:23]</a>.
