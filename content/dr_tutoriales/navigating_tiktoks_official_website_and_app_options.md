This guide provides a step-by-step process for accessing the official TikTok website and managing the TikTok application on your computer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:02">[00:00:02]</a>.

## Accessing the Official TikTok Website

To begin, open any web search engine, such as Google Chrome, Firefox, Opera, or Microsoft Edge <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:14">[00:00:14]</a>.

1.  In the search bar, directly type "TikTok" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>.
2.  Access the main page of TikTok <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>. It is crucial to ensure you are on the official website <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:35">[00:00:35]</a>.

## Installing the TikTok App on a Computer

Once on the official TikTok website, you can [[stepbystep_guide_to_installing_tiktok_on_a_computer | install the TikTok app]] on your computer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:59">[00:00:59]</a>.

1.  Look to the right side of the page, where an option appears with a computer icon and a downward arrow <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>. This option is for [[how_to_download_tiktok_on_a_pc_using_windows | installing TikTok]] onto your computer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:59">[00:00:59]</a>.
2.  Click on this option to begin the installation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:02">[00:01:02]</a>.
3.  Follow the prompts to "Instol" (install) the TikTok app <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:05">[00:01:05]</a>.
4.  The [[accessing_and_using_tiktok_from_a_pc | TikTok app]] will then be installed on your computer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>.

## Accessing the TikTok App

After installation, a direct shortcut to the TikTok app will typically appear on your desktop <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a>.

*   If the shortcut is not on your desktop, you can easily find it by going to the taskbar and typing "TikTok" directly into the search bar <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:18">[00:01:18]</a>.
*   You have the option to pin the application to your taskbar for quick access <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:23">[00:01:23]</a>.
*   You can also unpin or remove it from the taskbar if you wish <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:29">[00:01:29]</a>.

## Uninstalling the TikTok App

If you decide you no longer want the TikTok app on your computer, you can [[how_to_uninstall_tiktok_from_a_computer | uninstall it]] by following these steps <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>:

1.  Go to the Control Panel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:38">[00:01:38]</a>.
2.  Navigate to the "Program" or "Uninstall Programs" section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:41">[00:01:41]</a>.
3.  Locate "TikTok" in the list (it will be in alphabetical order) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:44">[00:01:44]</a>.
4.  Select TikTok and choose to uninstall it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:47">[00:01:47]</a>.
