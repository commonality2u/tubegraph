[[How to deposit money into Wise | Depositing capital into Wise]] can be done using various methods, including debit and credit cards <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:24">[00:00:24]</a>. The process for adding funds to your [[Wise]] account is consistent across both PC and mobile devices <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:09">[00:01:09]</a>. This guide assumes you have an existing and verified [[Wise]] account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:23">[00:01:23]</a>.

## Process of Adding Funds via Card

To add money to your [[Wise]] account using a debit or credit card:
1.  Navigate to your account balance on the [[Wise]] platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:17">[00:01:17]</a>.
2.  Select the specific currency balance you wish to top up (e.g., Euros) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:39">[00:01:39]</a>.
3.  Click the "Add" button <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:46">[00:01:46]</a>.
4.  Enter the desired amount to deposit, for instance, 200 Euros <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:58">[00:01:58]</a>.
5.  Proceed to the payment section where various methods are displayed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:21">[00:02:21]</a>.
6.  Choose the "Debit card" or "Credit card" option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:29">[00:02:29]</a>.
7.  Click "Continue with transfer" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:33">[00:03:33]</a>.
8.  Enter your card details (card number, expiry date, CVV) to complete the payment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:35">[00:03:35]</a>.
9.  Once the payment is processed, the money will instantly appear in your [[Wise]] balance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:40">[00:04:40]</a>.

## Fees and Transaction Speed

When using a debit or credit card (Visa or Mastercard) to deposit funds on [[Wise]]:
*   **Commission**: A commission is associated with this method <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:31">[00:02:31]</a>. For example, depositing 200 Euros incurs a 0.82 Euro fee, resulting in a total payment of 200.82 Euros <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:37">[00:02:37]</a>.
*   **Speed**: Transactions made with Visa or Mastercard are typically completed in a matter of seconds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:49">[00:03:49]</a>.

### Comparison with Other Methods
While debit and credit card payments incur a fee, they offer speed. Other [[different_methods_of_depositing_on_wise | methods of depositing on Wise]] include:
*   **PayPal**: Also incurs a commission <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:44">[00:02:44]</a>.
*   **Bank Transfer**: This method is generally free <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:50">[00:02:50]</a> and is recommended if urgency is not a concern <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:06">[00:03:06]</a>.
*   **Other Electronic Methods**: Options like "iDeal" and "Behind Li" (an electronic account) are also available <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:54">[00:02:54]</a>.

> [!NOTE]
> The choice of payment method depends on the urgency of the transfer, as some methods are faster but incur fees, while others are free but may take longer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:02">[00:03:02]</a>.
