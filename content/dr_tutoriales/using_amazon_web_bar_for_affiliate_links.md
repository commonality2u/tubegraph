## Accessing the Amazon Web Bar

To use this feature, navigate to the official Amazon website after logging into your [[amazon_affiliate_program_basics | Amazon Affiliates]] account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:58:00">[02:58:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:00:00">[03:00:00]</a>. Once on any Amazon product page, you will find a "superior taskbar" at the top, labeled "Amazon Web Bar Affiliates" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:08:00">[03:08:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:10:00">[03:10:00]</a>. This bar is exclusive to accounts registered with [[amazon_affiliate_program_basics | Amazon Affiliates]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:15:00">[03:15:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:16:00">[03:16:00]</a>.

## Obtaining Affiliate Links

The Amazon Web Bar allows you to generate [[creating_amazon_affiliate_links | affiliate links]] for any [[amazon_affiliate_program_basics | Amazon products]] you are viewing <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:20:00">[03:20:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:23:00">[03:23:00]</a>.

To get a link:
1.  Browse for any product on the Amazon website <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:28:00">[03:28:00]</a>.
2.  Once on the product page, look for the "Amazon Web Bar Affiliates" at the top left <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:42:00">[03:42:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:44:00">[03:44:00]</a>.
3.  The bar offers options to get the link in "text format" or "text plus image" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:49:00">[03:49:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:51:00">[03:51:00]</a>.

### Link Formats

*   **Text Format**: Clicking on "text format" allows you to choose your desired affiliate ID for tracking <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:57:00">[03:57:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:00:00">[04:00:00]</a>. You can also select between a [[shortening_amazon_links_for_better_usage | short link]] or a full (long) link <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:20:00">[04:20:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:25:00">[04:25:00]</a>.
*   **Text Plus Image**: This option provides a different type of link <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:28:00">[04:28:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:29:00">[04:29:00]</a>. When clicked, it is designed to open the product page in a new tab <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:32:00">[04:32:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:34:00">[04:34:00]</a>.

> [!TIP]
> The "text plus image" option is super useful because it prevents users from leaving your website, maintaining tracking of your page while opening the Amazon product in a new tab <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:36:00">[04:36:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:40:00">[04:40:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:44:00">[04:44:00]</a>.

Once you have generated the desired link, simply copy and paste it onto your website or other platforms where you are promoting [[amazon_affiliate_program_basics | Amazon products]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:45:00">[04:45:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:47:00">[04:47:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:50:00">[04:50:00]</a>.
