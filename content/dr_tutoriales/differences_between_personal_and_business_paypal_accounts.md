When [[paypal_account_setup_for_sending_money | creating a PayPal account]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:27:06">[01:27:06]</a>, users are given the option to choose between a personal account and a business account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:31:07">[01:31:07]</a>. It is important to understand the characteristics of each to select the most appropriate one <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:37:78">[01:37:78]</a>.

## Personal Account

A personal account is designed for everyday use and covers most essential functionalities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:42:97">[01:42:97]</a>. With a personal account, users can:
*   [[benefits_of_using_paypal_for_payments | Buy]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:44:97">[01:44:97]</a>.
*   [[benefits_of_using_paypal_for_payments | Receive money]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:45:67">[01:45:67]</a>.
*   Return money <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:46:47">[01:46:47]</a>.
*   Perform these actions without sharing payment information with anyone <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:47:87">[01:47:87]</a>.

For general use, such as [[benefits_of_using_paypal_for_payments | receiving payments within PayPal]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:69">[00:19:69]</a>, [[using_paypal_for_online_transactions | buying online]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:15:37">[03:15:37]</a>, or [[understanding_paypal_money_transfer_for_friends_vs_businesses | sending money]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:19:57">[03:19:57]</a>, a personal account is often sufficient <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:03:07">[02:03:07]</a>.

## Business Account

A business account is tailored for commercial activities, particularly for buying and selling mass products <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:53:77">[01:53:77]</a>. Key features of a business account include the ability to:
*   Prepare invoices <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:59:07">[01:59:07]</a>.
*   Sell anywhere to anyone <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:00:87">[02:00:87]</a>.

This type of account is more suitable for users who require advanced functionalities related to business operations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:51:37">[01:51:37]</a>.

## Account Creation Process

Regardless of the account type chosen, the initial setup process for [[paypal_account_setup_for_sending_money | creating a PayPal account]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:04:88">[01:04:88]</a> involves:
1.  Accessing the official PayPal page <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:48:84">[00:48:84]</a>.
2.  Accepting cookies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:19:07">[01:19:07]</a>.
3.  Providing a mobile phone number for verification <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:14:87">[02:14:87]</a>. A verification code will be sent to the phone <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:21:07">[02:21:07]</a>.
4.  Configuring the profile with an email address, first name, surname, and a password <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:26:97">[02:26:97]</a>. This email will later need to be verified <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:02:87">[01:02:87]</a>.
5.  Entering the billing address, date of birth, city, and province of residence <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:47:07">[02:47:07]</a>.
6.  Confirming agreement to the conditions of use and privacy policy <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:52:47">[02:52:47]</a>.
7.  Verifying the email address by clicking a confirmation link sent to the registered email <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:17:47">[04:17:47]</a>. This step secures the account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:49:57">[04:49:57]</a>.

However, the option to [[linking_a_bank_account_to_paypal | link a card]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:30:47">[03:30:47]</a> is available and can be done at any time <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:24:97">[05:24:97]</a>.
