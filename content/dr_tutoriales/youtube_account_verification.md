When a new [[creating_and_managing_youtube_channel | YouTube channel]] account is created, the platform does not initially allow the upload of videos longer than 15 minutes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:19">[00:00:19]</a>. To enable this and other features, a phone verification process is required <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:21">[00:01:21]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:23">[00:01:23]</a>.

## Features Enabled by Verification

Verifying your YouTube account by phone unlocks several key features, including:
*   Uploading videos longer than 15 minutes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:15">[00:01:15]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:17">[00:01:17]</a>
*   Using custom thumbnails for videos <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:18">[00:01:18]</a>
*   Conducting live broadcasts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:19">[00:01:19]</a>

## Verification Process

To verify your YouTube account:

1.  **Access YouTube Studio**: Navigate to [[youtube_studio_navigation | YouTube Studio]] from your YouTube channel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:47">[00:00:47]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>.
2.  **Go to Settings**: On the left-hand menu, click on "Settings" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:58">[00:00:58]</a>.
3.  **Select Channel Settings**: In the settings pop-up, choose the "Channel" sub-tab <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:01">[00:01:01]</a>.
4.  **Feature Eligibility**: Click on "Feature eligibility" (or "Access to functions" as it may appear) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:05">[00:01:05]</a>.
5.  **Verify Phone Number**: Under the section for "Intermediate features" (or "functions that require verification by phone"), you will see that these features are "Available" but require phone verification <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:14">[00:01:14]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:21">[00:01:21]</a>. Click on the option to "Verify phone number" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:30">[00:01:30]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:32">[00:01:32]</a>.
6.  **Enter Phone Details**:
    *   Select your country <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:51">[00:01:51]</a>.
    *   Choose how you prefer to receive the verification code (e.g., text message) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:52">[00:01:52]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:54">[00:01:54]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:55">[00:01:55]</a>.
    *   Enter your phone number <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:57">[00:01:57]</a>.
7.  **Submit for Code**: Click "Submit" to send a verification code to your phone <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:01">[00:02:01]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:02">[00:02:02]</a>.
8.  **Enter Verification Code**: Once you receive the code via text or call <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:39">[00:01:39]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:40">[00:01:40]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:42">[00:01:42]</a>, enter it into the provided field.
9.  **Confirm Verification**: Click "Submit" (or "send") to finalize the verification <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:07">[00:02:07]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:09">[00:02:09]</a>.

After completing these steps, your account will be verified, enabling you to upload videos longer than 15 minutes and access other advanced features <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:12">[00:02:12]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:14">[00:02:14]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:21">[00:02:21]</a>.
