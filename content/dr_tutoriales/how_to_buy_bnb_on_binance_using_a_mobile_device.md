This guide outlines the process of purchasing the BNB cryptocurrency using the Binance app on a mobile phone.

## Overview <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:20">[00:00:20]</a>

The process involves downloading the [[using_the_binance_app_on_a_mobile_device | Binance application]], navigating to the markets section, searching for BNB, and executing a purchase order.

## Steps to Buy BNB on Mobile

1.  **Download and Open the Binance App**
    To begin, you must have the [[using_the_binance_app_on_a_mobile_device | Binance application]] downloaded on your mobile phone <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:31:00">[04:31:00]</a>. After downloading, open the app, which will lead you to the homepage <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:40:00">[04:40:00]</a>.

2.  **Navigate to Markets**
    From the homepage, direct your attention to the "Markets" section, located in the submenu at the bottom of the screen <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:42:00">[04:42:00]</a>. Tap on it to proceed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:52:00">[04:52:00]</a>.

3.  **Search for BNB**
    Once in the markets section, locate the magnifying glass icon at the top of the screen to initiate a search <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:54:00">[04:54:00]</a>. Type "BNB" into the search bar, which will display all available trading pairs for BNB on the platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:56:00">[04:56:00]</a>.

4.  **Select a Trading Pair**
    Choose a desired trading pair for BNB. For instance, you might select BNB/EUR, similar to the desktop trading process <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:04:00">[05:04:00]</a>. Tapping on the selected pair will open the BNB chart with the chosen currency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:12:00">[05:12:00]</a>. The interface will resemble the desktop version but in a more compressed format <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:20:00">[05:20:00]</a>.

5.  **Place a Buy Order**
    At the bottom of the screen, you will find the "Buy" option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:27:00">[05:27:00]</a>. Tapping this will open the order placement page, which is similar to the [[how_to_buy_bnb_on_binance_using_a_computer | computer interface]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:31:00">[05:31:00]</a>.

    *   **Choose Order Type:** The platform offers various order types (limit, market, etc.) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:40:00">[05:40:00]</a>.
        *   To purchase immediately at the current market price, select "Market Order" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:47:00">[03:47:00]</a>.
    *   **Enter Amount:** Specify the amount of BNB you wish to buy or the total amount of currency (e.g., euros) you intend to spend <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:54:00">[05:54:00]</a>. For example, if you want to buy 1,000 euros worth of BNB, enter that amount <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:58:00">[05:58:00]</a>.

6.  **Confirm Purchase**
    After inputting your desired amount, tap the "Buy BNB" button <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:04:00">[06:04:00]</a>. If you have sufficient funds, the BNB will be purchased and will appear in your Spot Wallet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:07:00">[06:07:00]</a>.

### Post-Purchase

After purchasing BNB, it will be visible in your Spot Wallet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:19:00">[06:19:00]</a>. From there, you can choose to invest it in products like the [[how_to_use_bnb_vault_on_binance | BNB Vault]] to earn profitability <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:42:00">[06:42:00]</a>. If invested, your BNB will then appear in the "Earn" section of your wallet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:57:00">[06:57:00]</a>.
