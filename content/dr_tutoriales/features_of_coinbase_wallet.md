[[how_coinbase_wallet_works | Coinbase Wallet]] is a secure application designed for saving crypto assets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:03">[00:01:03]</a>. It allows users to store cryptocurrencies, tokens, and NFTs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:08">[00:01:08]</a>.

## Availability
[[how_coinbase_wallet_works | Coinbase Wallet]] is available on various platforms <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:11">[00:01:11]</a>:
*   Google Play
*   iPhone App Store
*   As a Google Chrome extension

The mobile application for both Android and iOS devices offers a similar user experience <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:21">[00:01:21]</a>.

## Security and Storage
[[security_and_storage_options_in_coinbase_wallet | Coinbase Wallet]] provides secure storage for digital assets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:48">[00:01:48]</a>. Funds are protected with secure authentication, such as Face ID for iPhone users or a password for other mobile devices <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:27">[00:03:27]</a>. It also offers a cloud backup option for recovery <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:52">[00:01:52]</a>. Users must back up their recovery phrase, as losing it means losing access to the account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:44">[00:04:44]</a>.

## Account Setup and Privacy
When setting up an account, users can choose their privacy type <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:07">[00:03:07]</a>:
*   **Public**: Allows other [[how_coinbase_wallet_works | Coinbase Wallet]] users to search for a user's username and send them cryptocurrencies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:10">[00:03:10]</a>. This option is generally recommended <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:21">[00:03:21]</a>.
*   **Private**: Prevents other wallets from searching for the user's profile <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:16">[00:03:16]</a>.

## Managing Cryptocurrencies
The wallet's main menu provides options for various cryptocurrency transactions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:48">[00:05:48]</a>.

### Sending, Receiving, and Trading
[[how_to_send_receive_and_trade_cryptocurrencies_using_coinbase_wallet | Coinbase Wallet]] allows users to send, receive, and trade cryptocurrencies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:51">[00:05:51]</a>:
*   **Receiving**: Users can receive specific cryptocurrencies by selecting the coin and displaying their wallet address or QR code <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:58">[00:05:58]</a>. They can also share their username to accept payments from other [[how_coinbase_wallet_works | Coinbase Wallet]] users <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:30">[00:06:30]</a>.
*   **Sending**: Cryptocurrencies can be sent from the [[using_coinbase_and_coinbase_pro_for_transactions | Coinbase]] account or another wallet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:50">[00:06:50]</a>. Sending requires scanning a QR code or manually entering the recipient's wallet address <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:54">[00:08:54]</a>.
*   **Converting**: Users can convert one cryptocurrency to another or convert crypto to USD <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:37">[00:09:37]</a>.

### Connecting to the Coinbase Platform
[[how_coinbase_wallet_works | Coinbase Wallet]] can be connected to a user's main [[using_coinbase_and_coinbase_pro_for_transactions | Coinbase]] account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:06">[00:07:06]</a>. This connection allows for seamless transfers of funds between the wallet and the [[using_coinbase_and_coinbase_pro_for_transactions | Coinbase]] platform, including both cryptocurrencies and cash balances <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:26">[00:08:26]</a>.

### Buying Cryptocurrencies
Users can directly buy cryptocurrencies within the [[how_coinbase_wallet_works | Coinbase Wallet]] by connecting it to their [[using_coinbase_and_coinbase_pro_for_transactions | Coinbase]] account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:40">[00:07:40]</a>. Payment methods include credit/debit cards or cash held in the [[using_coinbase_and_coinbase_pro_for_transactions | Coinbase]] account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:09">[00:08:09]</a>. Purchases made through [[how_coinbase_wallet_works | Coinbase Wallet]] incur [[using_coinbase_and_coinbase_pro_for_transactions | Coinbase]]'s associated commissions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:10">[00:09:10]</a>.

## Integrated Browser
The wallet includes an in-app browser that allows users to access online platforms and decentralized applications (DApps) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:05">[00:10:05]</a>. This enables interaction with networks like PancakeSwap <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:20">[00:10:20]</a>.

## Key Distinction: Wallet vs. Exchange
It is important to understand that [[how_coinbase_wallet_works | Coinbase Wallet]] is an independent wallet separate from the [[using_coinbase_and_coinbase_pro_for_transactions | Coinbase]] exchange platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:29">[00:10:29]</a>. Transfers between [[how_coinbase_wallet_works | Coinbase Wallet]] and the [[using_coinbase_and_coinbase_pro_for_transactions | Coinbase]] platform, or other platforms, incur commissions associated with the specific blockchain being used <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:33">[00:10:33]</a>. It is often recommended to use blockchains with lower transaction fees for transfers and then convert cryptocurrencies within the wallet as needed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:43">[00:10:43]</a>.
