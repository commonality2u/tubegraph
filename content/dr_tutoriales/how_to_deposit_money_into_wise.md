Wise (formerly TransferWise) is a platform that offers bank accounts for managing money, performing currency exchanges, and sending money to other countries with low commissions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:10">[00:00:10]</a>. This guide outlines the steps to deposit capital into your Wise account.

## Prerequisites
To deposit funds into Wise, you need an existing and verified Wise account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:21">[00:01:21]</a>.

## Depositing Funds: Step-by-Step
The process for depositing funds is similar whether you are using a PC or a mobile device <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:09">[00:01:09]</a>.

1.  **Access Your Account**: Log in to your Wise account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:17">[00:01:17]</a>. Your current balance across different currencies (e.g., euros, British pounds) will be displayed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:29">[00:01:29]</a>.
2.  **Select Currency**: Choose the currency you wish to deposit, for example, euros <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:39">[00:01:39]</a>. Click on the selected currency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:42">[00:01:42]</a>.
3.  **Add Funds**: Click the "Add" button <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:46">[00:01:46]</a>. The "Convert," "Send," and "Request" buttons will typically be inactive until funds are added <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:48">[00:01:48]</a>.
4.  **Enter Amount**: Specify the amount of money you want to add (e.g., 200 euros) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:58">[00:01:58]</a>.
5.  **Proceed to Payment**: Press "Continue" to move to the payment section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:18">[00:02:18]</a>.

## [[different_methods_of_depositing_on_wise | Different methods of depositing on Wise]]
Wise offers several payment methods, each with varying commissions and processing times <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:27">[00:02:27]</a>:

*   **[[using_debit_or_credit_cards_on_wise | Debit Card]] / [[using_debit_or_credit_cards_on_wise | Credit Card]]**:
    *   **Commission**: An associated commission applies (e.g., 0.82 euros for a 200 euro deposit) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:31">[00:02:31]</a>.
    *   **Speed**: Transfers are typically completed in a matter of seconds with Visa or Mastercard <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:49">[00:03:49]</a>.
    *   **Process**: Enter your card number and other required details to make the payment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:35">[00:03:35]</a>.
*   **[[bank_transfers_and_paypal_transactions_on_wise | PayPal]]**:
    *   **Commission**: Similar commission to card payments may apply <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:45">[00:02:45]</a>.
    *   **Process**: You will need to introduce your PayPal account details, and the PayPal account must hold the specified amount of money <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:27">[00:03:27]</a>.
*   **[[bank_transfers_and_paypal_transactions_on_wise | Bank Transfer]]**:
    *   **Commission**: This method is typically free <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:50">[00:02:50]</a>.
    *   **Speed**: Generally slower than card or PayPal payments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:02">[00:03:02]</a>.
    *   **Process**: Wise will provide you with bank details (e.g., IBAN) and a reference code for the transfer. You must confirm you are the account holder and are making the transfer via online banking <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:56">[00:03:56]</a>.
*   **Other Electronic Methods**: Methods like "Behind Lee" (for electronic accounts) and "Ideal" may also be available <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:54">[00:02:54]</a>. The choice of method depends on the urgency of the transfer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:02">[00:03:02]</a>.

### Example: Depositing via Card
If you choose to deposit via a debit or credit card for speed, you will enter your card details on the payment screen <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:41">[00:03:41]</a>. After successful payment, the funds will be immediately available in your Wise balance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:39">[00:04:39]</a>.

## Confirmation
Once the deposit is complete, the money will appear in your Wise balance for the chosen currency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:42">[00:04:42]</a>. You can verify this by returning to the main screen of your Wise account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:54">[00:04:54]</a>.
