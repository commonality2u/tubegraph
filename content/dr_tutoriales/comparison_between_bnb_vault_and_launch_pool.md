Binance offers various financial products to help users generate returns from their cryptocurrency holdings. Among these, [[how_to_use_bnb_vault_on_binance | BNB Vault]] and Launch Pool are two mechanisms that allow users to [[generating_cryptocurrencies_with_bnb_vault | generate cryptocurrencies]] using the BNB currency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:18">[00:00:18]</a>. While both provide profitability, they differ in the scope of their rewards.

## BNB Vault
[[how_to_use_bnb_vault_on_binance | BNB Vault]] is a financial product on Binance (referred to as "Pads" in the transcript) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:22">[00:00:22]</a> that allows users to obtain returns by investing the BNB currency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>. It is exclusively for BNB; other cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin, Ethereum, or Polkadot cannot be invested here <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:28">[00:00:28]</a>.

To access [[how_to_use_bnb_vault_on_binance | BNB Vault]], users navigate to the "Finance" section on the Binance platform and select "Binance Earn," where they will find the BNB Vault option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:29">[00:01:29]</a>.

The core concept of [[how_to_use_bnb_vault_on_binance | BNB Vault]] is to stake BNB to produce other crypto coins, which can then be collected <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:14">[00:02:14]</a>. Users have the option to set up an automatic transfer, which moves all available BNB balances from their spot accounts to [[how_to_use_bnb_vault_on_binance | BNB Vault]] daily at 1 AM <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:21">[00:02:21]</a>.

A key advantage of [[how_to_use_bnb_vault_on_binance | BNB Vault]] is its flexibility. It is classified as a [[comparing_flexible_and_locked_savings | flexible savings]] product <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:26">[00:05:26]</a>, allowing users to withdraw their invested BNB whenever they wish, unlike [[locked_savings_on_binance | locked staking]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:28">[00:05:28]</a>. It is also considered quite secure <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:37">[00:05:37]</a>.

## Launch Pool
Launch Pool is a tool within Binance that enables users to generate new cryptocurrencies by using existing ones (like BNB) as a reference <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:15">[00:03:15]</a>. For instance, by investing BNB in Launch Pool, users can obtain new cryptocurrencies such as "everything" (with a 5% annual return) and "lit entry" (with a 3.76% annual return) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:36">[00:03:36]</a>.

## Comparison: BNB Vault vs. Launch Pool
The primary difference and key benefit of [[how_to_use_bnb_vault_on_binance | BNB Vault]] lies in its multi-faceted reward system compared to Launch Pool:

*   **Diverse Rewards:** [[how_to_use_bnb_vault_on_binance | BNB Vault]] provides rewards based on what is available in the Launch Pool <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:09">[00:03:09]</a>. However, it also offers additional rewards from savings, meaning users earn crypto coins in two ways: from Launch Pool activities and from the inherent savings rewards of BNB <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:20">[00:04:20]</a>.
*   **Additional Benefits:** Beyond Launch Pool rewards, [[how_to_use_bnb_vault_on_binance | BNB Vault]] can also provide random rewards from periodic programs released by Binance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:48">[00:04:48]</a>.
*   **Singular Rewards (Launch Pool):** In contrast, if BNB is invested directly into Launch Pool, users only obtain the specific crypto coins offered by that particular Launch Pool system <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:30">[00:04:30]</a>.

In conclusion, [[benefits_of_investing_in_bnb_vault | investing BNB in BNB Vault]] is generally recommended over direct investment in Launch Pool, as it leads to the acquisition of more crypto coins and greater overall benefits <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:41">[00:04:41]</a>.
