Advcash is an electronic or digital wallet platform designed for online capital management <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:48">[00:00:48]</a>. It functions similarly to platforms like PayPal or AlertPay <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:54">[00:00:54]</a>, but it uniquely allows trading with various cryptocurrencies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:01">[00:01:01]</a>. Advcash is also known for collaborations with platforms like Binance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:06">[00:01:06]</a>, enabling cryptocurrency purchases with minimal commissions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:14">[00:01:14]</a>.

The platform combines national fiat currencies within the same ecosystem <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:04">[00:02:04]</a>, has 20 years of experience, and offers fast support via a direct chat with agents, primarily in English <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:09">[00:02:09]</a>.

## Account Management

[[Creating and managing an Advcash account | Creating an Advcash account]] is free, with no maintenance cost, even for inactivity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:34">[00:02:34]</a>.

### Verification and Security
The platform features [[Advcash security and verification processes | double authentication verification]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:42">[00:02:42]</a>, which can be done via Google Authenticator, email, or mobile phone to enhance security <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:11">[00:07:11]</a>. To verify an account, users must scan their ID or passport and perform a mobile phone verification <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:37">[00:06:37]</a>. Without verification, an account is inoperative, preventing deposits or withdrawals <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:47">[00:06:47]</a>.

### Account Deletion
Users can delete their Advcash account by navigating to the verification section and selecting the profile option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:51">[00:07:51]</a>. It is crucial to withdraw all funds before deleting an account to avoid losing money permanently <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:09">[00:08:09]</a>.

## Financial Operations

### [[Depositing and withdrawing funds on Advcash | Depositing Funds]]
Advcash supports deposits of both fiat currencies and cryptocurrencies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:01">[00:01:01]</a>.

*   **Fiat Currency**: Funds can be deposited via Visa, Mastercard, or SEPA transfers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:31">[00:09:31]</a>.
    *   **Commissions**: SEPA transfers incur a flat fee of one euro, regardless of the amount <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:47">[00:09:47]</a>. Visa or Mastercard deposits have commissions of up to 3.5% <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:30">[00:04:30]</a>.
    *   **Limits**: The minimum deposit amount is 100 euros, and the maximum is 10,000 euros for SEPA transfers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:25">[00:10:25]</a>. Daily limits are 10,000 euros, monthly 30,000 euros, and yearly 30,000 euros <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:04">[00:11:04]</a>. These limits can be increased by contacting support and providing additional documents <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:11">[00:11:11]</a>.
    *   **Processing Time**: SEPA transfers take approximately three business days to process <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:11">[00:10:11]</a>.
*   **Cryptocurrency**: There are no commissions applied by Advcash for depositing cryptocurrencies; fees are typically charged by the originating exchange <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:16">[00:12:16]</a>. Deposits require a certain number of blockchain confirmations (e.g., six confirmations for Litecoin) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:33">[00:13:33]</a>.
    *   **Supported Cryptocurrencies**: Advcash supports a wide range, including Dai, USD Coin, Ripple, and Bitcoin <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:00">[00:14:00]</a>.
    *   **Safety**: Users are warned to be precise when transferring cryptocurrencies, as mistakes can lead to permanent loss of capital <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:29">[00:13:29]</a>.

### Currency Exchange
Advcash allows exchanging various currencies, including USD, EUR, RUB, and GBP <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:43">[00:15:43]</a>. While the platform does not explicitly show exchange commissions, users are advised to verify the real exchange rate through external tools like Google to identify any hidden fees <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:32">[00:16:32]</a>.

### Sending Funds
Funds can be sent instantly to other Advcash users with no commission <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:28">[00:17:28]</a>.

*   **To other Advcash users**: Transfers are instant and free <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:28">[00:17:28]</a>. Funds can be sent to an email address even if the recipient doesn't have an Advcash account yet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:19">[00:18:19]</a>. A protection code can be added for extra security, requiring the recipient to know the code to access the funds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:29">[00:18:29]</a>.
*   **To external cards (Visa/Mastercard)**: Commissions apply (e.g., 5 euros plus 2.5% for amounts exceeding 5 euros) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:30">[00:19:30]</a>.*   **To local banks**: Advcash offers the option to send money to local banks in numerous countries like Argentina, Australia, India, Japan, Nepal, Pakistan, Canada, Colombia, the Dominican Republic, and Egypt <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:17">[00:20:17]</a>. Commissions for local bank transfers can be as low as 0.5% depending on the amount <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:10">[00:20:10]</a>. For Eurozone transfers, a fee of five euros plus 0.5% for amounts exceeding that applies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:27">[00:20:27]</a>.
*   **Cash Withdrawal**: Users can withdraw cash in various countries where Advcash has collaborations, including Russia and Ukraine <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:21:40">[00:21:40]</a>. Processing times range from 15 minutes to 24 hours, and minimum withdrawal amounts (e.g., 3,000 euros in Russia) apply <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:21:47">[00:21:47]</a>.
*   **To other payment platforms**: Funds can be sent to other platforms like PayPal, Skrill, Sberbank, etc. <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:21">[00:22:21]</a>.

## Cryptocurrency Services

Advcash collaborates with Binance to facilitate cryptocurrency purchases <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:37">[00:23:37]</a>. Users can buy various cryptocurrencies directly through the platform's integration with Binance's exchange <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:57">[00:23:57]</a>. While buying crypto is possible through other methods like direct card payments (Visa/Mastercard) or other exchanges, it is generally recommended to use Binance for lower commissions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:45">[00:24:45]</a>.

### [[Saving and investing features on Revolut | Crypto Savings]]
Advcash partners with the Nexo platform to offer crypto savings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:25">[00:25:25]</a>. By holding cryptocurrencies on Advcash, users can earn up to 8% annualized interest <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:28">[00:25:28]</a>. The platform allows users to calculate potential earnings based on time, amount, and currency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:36">[00:25:36]</a>. Users can directly connect their Advcash account to Nexo to deposit cryptocurrencies and start earning interest <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:26:00">[00:26:00]</a>.

Advcash's main purpose is to provide a cost-effective way to deposit capital, especially through SEPA transfers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:54">[00:22:54]</a>. While withdrawing funds via the same methods can be more expensive <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:01">[00:23:01]</a>, Advcash is particularly useful for:
*   Withdrawing money to local banks in countries like Russia, Kazakhstan, Argentina, and the Dominican Republic <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:10">[00:23:10]</a>. Many users utilize it to send money to family in these countries <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:18">[00:23:18]</a>.
*   Buying and selling cryptocurrencies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:24">[00:23:24]</a>.
*   Maintaining savings and generating interest on cryptocurrencies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:29">[00:23:29]</a>.
