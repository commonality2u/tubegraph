This guide explains how to withdraw cryptocurrency funds from Coinbase and Coinbase Pro platforms to a PayPal account in a straightforward and updated manner <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:05">[00:00:05]</a>.

## Important Concepts and Prerequisites

Before initiating any withdrawals, it's essential to understand a few key concepts:

### Platform Differences for PayPal Transfers
Out of the two platforms, Coinbase and Coinbase Pro, only the **Coinbase** platform allows direct transfers to [[withdrawing_money_from_coinbase_to_paypal | PayPal]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:30">[00:01:30]</a>.
*   If funds are held on Coinbase Pro, they must first be transferred to Coinbase, then from Coinbase to PayPal. This process involves two steps <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:40">[00:01:40]</a>.
*   If funds are already on Coinbase, only a single step is required to transfer them to PayPal <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:52">[00:01:52]</a>.

### Name Verification
The name on your PayPal account and Coinbase account must match the name on your official identity document <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:08">[00:02:08]</a>.

### Commission Considerations
*   Transfers from Coinbase Pro to Coinbase are free <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:05">[00:06:05]</a>.
*   [[withdrawing_money_from_coinbase_to_paypal | Withdrawing funds]] from Coinbase to PayPal is free <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:03">[00:07:03]</a>.
*   It is recommended to transform cryptocurrencies into the desired withdrawal currency (e.g., euros) on Coinbase Pro *before* transferring to Coinbase, as commissions for currency changes within Coinbase itself are significantly higher <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:13">[00:05:13]</a>.

### Additional Benefits
*   Referral links for new Coinbase accounts can offer a bonus, such as $10, by signing up through the provided link <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>.
*   Coinbase Earn is a service offered by Coinbase that allows users to earn free cryptocurrencies by watching tutorials and answering questions related to them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>.

## Step-by-Step Process: Linking PayPal and Withdrawing Funds

### 1. [[linking_paypal_account_to_coinbase | Linking PayPal Account to Coinbase]]
To link your PayPal account:
1.  Log in to your Coinbase account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:27">[00:02:27]</a>.
2.  Navigate to your profile settings, usually found in the top-right corner, and select "Configuration" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:28">[00:02:28]</a>.
3.  Go to "Financial Services" or "Payment Methods" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:34">[00:02:34]</a>.
4.  Select "Add a payment method" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:51">[00:02:51]</a>.
5.  Choose PayPal from the options <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:53">[00:02:53]</a>.
6.  You will be prompted to confirm the linking of your PayPal account, which should use the same email as your Coinbase account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:11">[00:03:11]</a>.
7.  Accept the terms to complete the linking process <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:31">[00:03:31]</a>. Your PayPal account will now be linked to your Coinbase account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:37">[00:03:37]</a>.

### 2. Transferring Funds from Coinbase Pro to Coinbase (If Applicable)
If your funds are on Coinbase Pro:
1.  Go to the Coinbase Pro platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:03">[00:04:03]</a>.
2.  Access your portfolio (e.g., "by folios") to see your cryptocurrencies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:44">[00:04:44]</a>.
3.  **Important:** Transform your cryptocurrency into the desired withdrawal currency (e.g., euros) on Coinbase Pro to avoid higher fees on Coinbase <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:18">[00:05:18]</a>.
4.  Select "Withdraw" (or "Wid Drow") <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:50">[00:04:50]</a>.
5.  Choose the cryptocurrency or currency you wish to withdraw (e.g., euro) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:46">[00:05:46]</a>.
6.  Select the option to transfer to "Coinbay" (Coinbase) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:53">[00:05:53]</a>.
7.  Enter the desired amount (e.g., maximum) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:01">[00:06:01]</a>. The commission for this transfer is zero euros <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:05">[00:06:05]</a>.
8.  Confirm the withdrawal. The process is instant <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:08">[00:06:08]</a>.

### 3. [[withdrawing_money_from_coinbase_to_paypal | Withdrawing Funds from Coinbase to PayPal]]
Once your funds are on Coinbase:
1.  Go to the main Coinbase platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:22">[00:06:22]</a>.
2.  Navigate to your "Portfolio" to view your funds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:30">[00:06:30]</a>.
3.  Click on the currency you wish to withdraw (e.g., euros) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:42">[00:06:42]</a>.
4.  Select "Withdraw" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:44">[00:06:44]</a>.
5.  Enter the amount you wish to withdraw, or select to withdraw "everything" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:47">[00:06:47]</a>.
6.  Choose "Instant" withdrawal to PayPal, which is free <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:03">[00:07:03]</a>. (Note: A withdrawal to a bank account may incur a small fee in some regions, such as 0.15 euros in the European commission <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:11">[00:07:11]</a>).
7.  Confirm the transaction details, including the zero withdrawal fee <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:24">[00:07:24]</a>.
8.  Click "Withdraw now" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:26">[00:07:26]</a>.
9.  A notification will be sent once the withdrawal is completed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:37">[00:07:37]</a>.
10. Check your PayPal account; the balance should reflect the transferred funds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:46">[00:07:46]</a>.
