This guide explains how to install and manage YouTube Music on your computer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:00">[00:00:00]</a>.

## Prerequisites

To begin, you will need a web browser installed on your computer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>. Compatible browsers include:
*   Edge <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>
*   Firefox <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:14">[00:00:14]</a>
*   Opera <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:15">[00:00:15]</a>
*   [[using_google_chrome_for_youtube | Google Chrome]] (used in this example) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:16">[00:00:16]</a>

## [[how_to_install_youtube_music_on_pc | Installing YouTube Music on PC]]

1.  Open your preferred web browser <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:19">[00:00:19]</a>.
2.  Navigate to the YouTube Music website <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>. You can do this by typing "YouTube Music" into your search engine <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>, or by using a direct link often found in the first comment of related tutorials <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:31">[00:00:31]</a>.
3.  Once on the YouTube Music website, ensure your Google account is recognized and logged in <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:35">[00:00:35]</a>.
4.  Look for a small computer symbol with a downward arrow located in the top-right corner of the page <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>. This symbol indicates the option to "Install YouTube Music" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:47">[00:00:47]</a>.
5.  Click the "Install" button <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>. The application will then be downloaded and installed directly onto your computer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>, logged in with your Google account (e.g., your [[activating_youtube_channel_features | YouTube channel]] account) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:58">[00:00:58]</a>.

## Accessing YouTube Music After Installation

Upon successful installation, a shortcut for YouTube Music will be placed on your desktop <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>.

To find the application:
1.  Use the Windows search bar <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:11">[00:01:11]</a>.
2.  Type "music" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:11">[00:01:11]</a>.
3.  YouTube Music will appear in the search results <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:13">[00:01:13]</a>.

## [[managing_youtube_music_on_taskbar | Managing YouTube Music on the Taskbar]]

You can easily add YouTube Music to your taskbar for quick access:
1.  Find YouTube Music in the Windows search results <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:11">[00:01:11]</a>.
2.  Right-click on the YouTube Music icon.
3.  Select the option to "Pin to taskbar" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:14">[00:01:14]</a>.
Once pinned, it will always be available on your taskbar <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:17">[00:01:17]</a>.

## [[uninstalling_youtube_music_from_pc | Uninstalling YouTube Music from PC]]

If you wish to remove YouTube Music from your computer:
1.  Open the Control Panel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:36">[00:01:36]</a>. You can search for "control panel" in the Windows search bar <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:39">[00:01:39]</a>.
2.  Within the Control Panel, locate and click on "Uninstall a program" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:42">[00:01:42]</a>.
3.  Find YouTube Music in the list of installed programs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:45">[00:01:45]</a>.
4.  Right-click on YouTube Music and select "Uninstall" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:47">[00:01:47]</a>.
