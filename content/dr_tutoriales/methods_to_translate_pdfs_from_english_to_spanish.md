This article outlines three different methods for translating PDF documents, specifically from English to Spanish, assuming the PDF text is recognized as words and not a scanned image <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:43">[00:00:43]</a>. Scanned pages, being in image format, cannot be translated directly using these methods <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:54">[00:00:54]</a>. The PDF must be in text format for the methods to be effective <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:02">[00:01:02]</a>.

## Method 1: [[using_microsoft_word_for_pdf_translation | Using Microsoft Word for PDF translation]]

This method leverages [[using_microsoft_word_for_pdf_translation | Microsoft Word]]'s built-in translation features.
1.  **Open PDF in Word**: Right-click the PDF file and choose "Open with" [[using_microsoft_word_for_pdf_translation | Microsoft Word]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:18">[00:01:18]</a>. This will open the PDF in an editable text format <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:34">[00:01:34]</a>.
2.  **Select Text**: Select the specific text or paragraphs you wish to translate <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:49">[00:01:49]</a>. You can also select the entire document <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:25">[00:02:25]</a>.
3.  **Translate**: Right-click the selected text and choose the "Translate" option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:53">[00:01:53]</a>.
4.  **Choose Language**: On the right side of the screen, select the target language (e.g., Spanish) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:04">[00:02:04]</a>.
5.  **Insert Translation**: After the text is translated, click "Insert" to replace the original text with the Spanish translation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:17">[00:02:17]</a>.

## Method 2: [[online_tools_for_translating_pdfs | Using Online Tools for Translating PDFs]]

This method utilizes [[online_tools_for_translating_pdfs | online translators]] accessible via a web browser like Google Chrome <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:51">[00:02:51]</a>. The primary tools discussed are [[comparison_of_google_translate_and_deepl_for_document_translation | Google Translate]] and [[comparison_of_google_translate_and_deepl_for_document_translation | Deepl Translator]].

### [[comparison_of_google_translate_and_deepl_for_document_translation | Google Translate]]
1.  **Access Documents Section**: Open [[comparison_of_google_translate_and_deepl_for_document_translation | Google Translate]] in your browser and navigate to the "documents" section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:17">[00:03:17]</a>.
2.  **Upload Document**: Click "Explore your computer" to upload your PDF (or Word/PowerPoint document) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:22">[00:03:22]</a>.
3.  **Translate and Download**: Select the document, click "Translate," and once translated, download the translated version <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:37">[00:03:37]</a>.
    *   **Note**: [[comparison_of_google_translate_and_deepl_for_document_translation | Google Translate]] offers this as a completely free service, without asking for Pro subscriptions or character limits <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:07">[00:05:07]</a>.

### [[comparison_of_google_translate_and_deepl_for_document_translation | Deepl Translator]]
1.  **Access Deepl Translator**: Open [[comparison_of_google_translate_and_deepl_for_document_translation | Deepl Translator]] in your browser.
2.  **Upload Document**: Select a document (PDF, DOC, or PPTX) from your computer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:51">[00:03:51]</a>.
3.  **Choose Language**: Select the desired translation language, such as Spanish <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:36">[00:03:36]</a>.
4.  **Initiate Translation**: Click "Translate now" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:39">[00:03:39]</a>.
    *   **Limitation**: [[comparison_of_google_translate_and_deepl_for_document_translation | Deepl Translator]]'s free version has character limits for documents. For longer documents, it may prompt you to try [[comparison_of_google_translate_and_deepl_for_document_translation | Deepl Pro]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:52">[00:04:52]</a>. This platform allows translation of shorter PDF documents more readily than longer ones <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:05">[00:05:05]</a>.
