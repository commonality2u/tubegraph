If you have [[how_to_install_youtube_music_on_pc | YouTube Music]] installed on your computer and wish to remove it, the process involves accessing your system's Control Panel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:31:97">[01:31:97]</a>.

## How to Uninstall YouTube Music

### 1. Access the Control Panel
Navigate to the Control Panel on your computer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:37:63">[01:37:63]</a>. You can search for "Control Panel" in your system's search bar <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:39:10">[01:39:10]</a>.

### 2. Find and Uninstall the Program
Once inside the Control Panel, locate the "Uninstall a program" option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:42:30">[01:42:30]</a>. In the list of installed programs, find [[how_to_install_youtube_music_on_pc | YouTube Music]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:45:15">[01:45:15]</a>. Right-click on [[how_to_install_youtube_music_on_pc | YouTube Music]] and select "Uninstall" to remove it from your computer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:48:00">[01:48:00]</a>.
