This guide outlines the process of [[converting_cryptocurrency_to_fiat_currency | converting cryptocurrency]] to fiat currency on Binance, primarily focusing on moving funds from a crypto wallet to a bank account.

## Overview of the Process
To withdraw capital from a platform to a bank account, the cryptocurrency must first be on an exchange that supports fiat withdrawals, such as Binance, KuCoin, Coinbase, or Kraken <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:32">[00:01:32]</a>. The general flow involves:
1.  Identifying the cryptocurrency to withdraw <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:04">[00:01:04]</a>.
2.  Transferring the cryptocurrency to the Binance platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:41">[00:01:41]</a>.
3.  [[how_to_exchange_cryptocurrencies_on_binance | Converting the cryptocurrency to a fiat currency]] like Euro within Binance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:20">[00:03:20]</a>.
4.  [[withdrawing_fiat_and_cryptocurrencies_from_binance | Withdrawing the fiat currency]] to a bank account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:35">[00:03:35]</a>.

## Transferring Cryptocurrency to Binance
If the cryptocurrency is not already on Binance, it needs to be transferred there. This process is demonstrated using BNB:

1.  **Open Binance Application**: Access the Binance application on your mobile device (the process is identical for Android and iPhone) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>.
2.  **Initiate Deposit on Binance**: In the Binance app, navigate to the deposit section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:07">[00:02:07]</a>.
3.  **Select Cryptocurrency**: Choose the specific cryptocurrency you wish to deposit (e.g., BNB) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:10">[00:02:10]</a>.
4.  **Choose Blockchain Network**: Select the appropriate blockchain network for the cryptocurrency (e.g., Beacon Chain for BNB) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:15">[00:02:15]</a>.
5.  **Copy Wallet Address and Memo**: Copy the provided Binance wallet address and, if applicable, the memo/tag <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:21">[00:02:21]</a>.
6.  **Send from External Wallet**: Go to your external wallet (e.g., Trust Wallet) and initiate a send transaction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:55">[00:01:55]</a>.
7.  **Paste Details**: Paste the copied Binance wallet address and memo into the respective fields in your external wallet's send interface <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:30">[00:02:30]</a>.
8.  **Confirm Transaction**: Confirm the transaction, noting any commissions (e.g., two cents on the dollar for this transaction) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:37">[00:02:37]</a>.
    *   Once confirmed, the capital will be removed from your external wallet and will arrive in your Binance spot wallet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:45">[00:02:45]</a>.

## [[methods_to_convert_cryptocurrencies | Converting Cryptocurrency to Fiat within Binance]]
Once the cryptocurrency is in your Binance spot wallet, the next step is to [[converting_crypto_to_fiat_currency | convert it to your desired fiat currency]]:

1.  **Navigate to Trade Section**: In the Binance app, go to the "Trade" section, typically found in the bottom menu <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:05">[00:03:05]</a>.
2.  **Select Convert**: Choose the "Convert" option, usually located at the top left <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:09">[00:03:09]</a>.
3.  **Choose Cryptocurrencies**:
    *   Select the cryptocurrency you have (e.g., BNB) as the "From" currency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:13">[00:03:13]</a>.
    *   Choose the desired fiat currency (e.g., Euro) as the "To" currency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:20">[00:03:20]</a>. Alternatively, you might first convert to a stablecoin like [[converting_usdt_to_usd_on_binance | USDT]] and then to Euro <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:16">[00:03:16]</a>.
    *   The goal is to [[methods_to_convert_cryptocurrencies | convert the cryptocurrency to Euro]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:29">[00:03:29]</a>.

## [[withdrawing_fiat_and_cryptocurrencies_from_binance | Withdrawing Fiat Currency to Your Bank Account]]
After [[how_to_exchange_cryptocurrencies_on_binance | converting your cryptocurrency to fiat]], you can proceed with the withdrawal:

1.  **Access Spot Wallet**: Ensure you are in your Spot wallet section in Binance, where your Euro balance will appear <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:32">[00:03:32]</a>.
2.  **Initiate Withdrawal**: Select the "Withdraw" option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:35">[00:03:35]</a>.
3.  **Choose Fiat Withdrawal**: Select "Fiat" as the withdrawal type <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:38">[00:03:38]</a>.
4.  **Select Currency**: Choose the fiat currency you wish to withdraw (e.g., Euro) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:41">[00:03:41]</a>.
5.  **Enter Amount and Method**:
    *   Specify the amount you want to withdraw <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:45">[00:03:45]</a>.
    *   Choose your preferred transaction type, such as SEPA bank transfer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:48">[00:03:48]</a> or Visa banking card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:50">[00:03:50]</a>.
    *   Other methods may offer no commission for deposits or sends <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:56">[00:03:56]</a>.
