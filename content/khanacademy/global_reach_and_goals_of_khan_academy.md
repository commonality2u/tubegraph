## Current Global Footprint
[[khan_academy_and_its_educational_impact | Khan Academy]] has established a significant presence in [[online_learning_and_technology_in_education | online learning]]:
*   It boasts 120 million registered users <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:44">[00:00:44]</a>.
*   Between 20 and 30 million students actively use the platform each month <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:47">[00:00:47]</a>.
*   Collectively, users accumulate approximately 200 million hours of learning annually <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:51">[00:00:51]</a>.
*   The platform serves millions of students and teachers worldwide <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:38">[00:00:38]</a>.

## Educational Impact and Return on Investment
[[khan_academy_and_its_educational_impact | Khan Academy]] has demonstrated considerable [[khan_academy_and_its_educational_impact | educational impact]]:
*   Efficacy studies indicate its ability to significantly influence outcomes in classrooms <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:59">[00:00:59]</a>.
*   Its social return on investment ratio is estimated to be between 400 to 500 to 1, considerably higher than the typical good philanthropic return of 5 to 1 or 10 to 1 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:05">[00:01:05]</a>.
*   Every dollar donated helps to accelerate [[personalized_and_selfpaced_learning | learning]] across multiple dimensions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:03">[00:02:03]</a>.

## Ambitious Goals and Future Aspirations
[[khan_academy_and_its_educational_impact | Khan Academy]]'s long-term vision extends far beyond its current reach:
*   **Content Expansion** The organization aims to accelerate the creation of new content, covering all subjects from kindergarten through the early stages of college <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:22">[00:00:22]</a>. This includes focusing on science content and early [[personalized_and_selfpaced_learning | learning]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>.
*   **Engagement Enhancement** There is a goal to make its software and practice exercises even more engaging for students <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:33">[00:00:33]</a>.
*   **Global Service** Despite having the budget of a large high school, [[khan_academy_and_its_educational_impact | Khan Academy]] aspires to serve the entire globe <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:18">[00:02:18]</a>.
*   **Foundational Institution** The organization seeks to build a multi-generational institution that will enable future generations, including "future [[elon_musk_donation_to_khan_academy | Elon Musks]]," to access their [[confidence_in_learning_potential | potential]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>. This investment is viewed as foundational for uplifting civilization as a whole <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:43">[00:01:43]</a>.
*   **Educating Humanity** The ultimate mission is to educate all of humanity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:54">[00:02:54]</a>. This goal is framed as not too large, akin to solving climate issues, transportation challenges, or colonizing other planets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:14">[00:03:14]</a>. The vision even extends to educating inhabitants of other planets in the future <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:28">[00:02:28]</a>.

## [[financial_challenges_and_needs_of_khan_academy | Financial Needs]]
Despite its significant impact, [[khan_academy_and_its_educational_impact | Khan Academy]] faces [[financial_challenges_and_needs_of_khan_academy | financial challenges]]. For 2021, the organization was running at a deficit <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:57">[00:01:57]</a>. While the world collectively spends five trillion dollars annually on education, [[khan_academy_and_its_educational_impact | Khan Academy]]'s budget is only one one-hundred thousandth of that <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:11">[00:02:11]</a>. Contributions of all sizes are vital to supporting its mission <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:00">[00:02:00]</a>.
