From: [[khanacademy]] <br/> 

Subtracting a negative number is equivalent to [[adding_negative_numbers | adding]] a positive number <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:01:00">[03:01:00]</a>. This rule applies to all [[arithmetic_examples_with_negative_numbers | arithmetic examples with negative numbers]] and simplifies [[handling_equations_with_negative_numbers | handling equations with negative numbers]] involving negative numbers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:00">[00:00:00]</a>. While other videos delve into the explanation of why this concept makes sense, the fundamental rule is that "subtracting a negative" transforms into "adding a positive" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:56:00">[02:56:00]</a>.

> [!tip] The Rule
> When you [[subtracting_negative_numbers | subtract a negative]], it is the same thing as [[adding_negative_numbers | adding]] the positive equivalent <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:59:00">[02:59:00]</a>.

## Examples

### Positive Number Minus a Negative Number

Consider the expression: `2 minus negative 3` <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:47:00">[02:47:00]</a>.

Applying the rule, [[subtracting_negative_numbers | subtracting negative 3]] is identical to [[adding_negative_numbers | adding positive 3]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:04:00">[03:04:00]</a>.

So, `2 minus negative 3` becomes `2 plus 3` <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:09:00">[03:09:00]</a>.
The result is 5 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:18:00">[03:18:00]</a>.

### Negative Number Minus a Negative Number

Consider the expression: `negative 2 minus negative 3` <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:23:00">[03:23:00]</a>.

Even with multiple [[introduction_to_negative_numbers | negative numbers]], the rule remains consistent: [[subtracting_negative_numbers | subtracting a negative]] results in [[adding_negative_numbers | a positive]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:38:00">[03:38:00]</a>.

Therefore, `negative 2 minus negative 3` is the same as `negative 2 plus 3` <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:45:00">[03:45:00]</a>.

To visualize `negative 2 plus 3` on a number line:
1. Start at [[introduction_to_negative_numbers | negative 2]] (two units to the left of zero) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:55:00">[03:55:00]</a>.
2. Add 3 by moving three units to the right <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:59:00">[03:59:00]</a>.
3. This movement ends at positive 1 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:01:00">[04:01:00]</a>.
