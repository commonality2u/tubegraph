## Historical Significance
The [[elements_and_the_periodic_table|periodic table of elements]] is the result of thousands of years of human effort to comprehend the complexity of the world <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:02">[00:01:02]</a>. While often taken for granted today, it represents a significant achievement in understanding reality <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:55">[00:00:55]</a>.

## Fundamental Role
The [[elements_and_the_periodic_table|periodic table of elements]] helps us understand that the vast complexity observed in the universe is, at its essence, made of basic building blocks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:17">[00:02:17]</a>, similar to Legos that comprise everything <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:27">[00:02:27]</a>.

Key characteristics include:
*   **Finite Number**: There is a finite number of these fundamental building blocks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:31">[00:02:31]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:33">[00:02:33]</a>. New [[elements_and_the_periodic_table|elements]] are not discovered frequently anymore <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:37">[00:02:37]</a>.
*   **Commonality**: A select few [[elements_and_the_periodic_table|elements]] appear disproportionately in many seemingly different substances <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:41">[00:02:41]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:45">[00:02:45]</a>. For example, many substances are different compositions of [[elements_and_the_periodic_table|elements]] like carbon, oxygen, and hydrogen <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:48">[00:02:48]</a>.
*   **Sub-atomic Composition**: The [[elements_and_the_periodic_table|elements]] themselves are composed of [[fundamental_particles_of_atoms|protons]], [[fundamental_particles_of_atoms|electrons]], and [[fundamental_particles_of_atoms|neutrons]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:54">[00:02:54]</a>. These [[fundamental_particles_of_atoms|particles]] are rearranged in various ways to create [[elements_and_the_periodic_table|elements]] with distinct [[properties_of_substances|properties]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:00">[00:03:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:04">[00:03:04]</a>.
