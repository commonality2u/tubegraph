## Current Reach and Impact

Despite its relatively small budget compared to global education spending, [[Khan Academy and its educational impact | Khan Academy]] demonstrates a significant impact:
*   **User Base** The platform has 120 million registered users, with 20 to 30 million students actively using it monthly <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:44">[00:00:44]</a>.
*   **Learning Hours** Annually, users accumulate approximately 200 million hours of learning <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:51">[00:00:51]</a>.
*   **Social Return on Investment** Efficacy studies indicate a social return on investment ratio of 400 to 500 to 1, significantly higher than the typical philanthropic sector standard of 5 to 1 or 10 to 1 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:05">[00:01:05]</a>.
