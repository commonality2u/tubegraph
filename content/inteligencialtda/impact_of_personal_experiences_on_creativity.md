## Childhood Roots of Creativity

Even as a child, there was a natural inclination towards creative pursuits. This involved finding inventive ways to play with broken toys, wondering how actors from different countries communicated, and constantly asking "why" about the world around them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:32">[00:10:32]</a>. Growing up in a remote area without internet or readily available information, creativity became a primary tool for exploration and understanding <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:31">[00:11:31]</a>.

During a period spent with a grandmother in Santa Luz, Piauí, where there was no electricity or running water, exposure to the outside world was limited to a radio <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:16:11">[01:16:11]</a>. This environment fostered a unique imaginative process; for example, listening to the same few songs repeatedly led to creating an imagined rivalry between artists like Felipe Dylon, Michael Jackson, and Pepe Moreno, battling for global dominance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:54">[00:12:54]</a>. This self-generated narrative, born from a lack of external information, laid the groundwork for a storytelling style <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:37:45">[00:37:45]</a>.

Support from family, particularly a father who bought notebooks for drawing, further nurtured this creative impulse <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:03">[00:16:03]</a>. Learning drawing techniques from a neighbor and observing the world around him, even through limited media like the "Speed Racer" cartoon, contributed to an ability to weave elaborate stories in his head and visually depict them on walls <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:43">[00:15:43]</a>.

This foundation of observation and imaginative storytelling naturally transitioned into comedy. Early comedic experiences included school presentations and caricaturing teachers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:39:37">[00:39:37]</a>. The shift to a YouTube channel in 2013, even after an initial hack and deletion, demonstrated a resilience and a drive fueled by positive feedback and the realization of earning potential <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:43:36">[00:43:36]</a>. The early style of comedy was often about "storytelling," a term he later learned, rooted in recounting personal anecdotes and making observations from his life <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:42:19">[00:42:19]</a>.

The concept of an "artist" is viewed broadly as someone who can find a workaround for problems or create solutions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:54:51">[00:54:51]</a>. This perspective suggests that societal and educational systems, which often discourage unconventional thinking or label individuals based on academic performance ("bad at math"), can stifle innate [[Generative AI and Creativity | creativity]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:55:35">[00:55:35]</a>.

This contrasts with collaborative projects where individual progress might sometimes overshadow the collective good of the project <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:03:00">[01:03:00]</a>.
