[[Exorcism and spiritual battles in Catholic belief | Exorcism]] is a ministry within the Catholic Church aimed at casting out demons from individuals who are experiencing [[spiritual experiences and manifestations | demonic possession]] or severe [[spiritual experiences and manifestations | spiritual disturbances]]. It is considered a serious and delicate ministry requiring prudence and appointment by a bishop <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:21:05">[02:21:05]</a>.

## The Role of the Exorcist

A priest designated as an exorcist must be endowed with piety, knowledge, and prudence <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:21:27">[02:21:27]</a>. His primary role is to bring individuals back to a Christian life, as true liberation requires the person to return to God's grace <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:08:46">[04:08:46]</a>. The exorcist's main goal is not just expelling demons, but helping the person to change and fill their life with God <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:09:59">[04:09:59]</a>.

Father Duarte Lara, an exorcist from Portugal, spent 10 years studying in Rome and accompanied the renowned exorcist Father Gabriele Amorth for several years, providing support as an assistant <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:04">[05:04]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:06">[05:06]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:28:46">[03:28:46]</a>. He describes the experience of working with Father Gabriele, who would often pray with a "great spirit of humor" during [[exorcism_and_spiritual_battles_in_catholic_belief | exorcisms]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:05:05">[03:05:05]</a>. Father Gabriele's approach prioritized prayer over lengthy conversations with the demon <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:08:08">[03:08:08]</a>.

## Demonic Influence and Its Manifestations

Demonic activity is typically categorized into ordinary and extraordinary. Ordinary activity refers to [[understanding_and_interpretation_of_spiritual_warfare | temptation]], which aims to lead individuals into sin through suggestion <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:00:24">[01:00:24]</a>. Extraordinary activity involves more overt [[spiritual experiences and manifestations | disturbances]], such as hearing voices, seeing things, fainting, or incorporating other entities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:00:33">[01:00:33]</a>.

### Symptoms of Extraordinary Disturbances

Symptoms can be varied and ambiguous, making careful diagnosis crucial <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:19:07">[01:19:07]</a>. Common indicators include:
*   **Unexplained physiological problems:** Headaches, stomach or heart pain, back pain, fainting, or seizures that have no clinical explanation despite medical examinations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:19:33">[01:19:33]</a>.
*   **Atypical reactions to medication:** Individuals experiencing attacks may not be sedated even with high doses of tranquilizers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:22:42">[01:22:42]</a>.
*   **Aversion to sacred things:** Feeling sick, experiencing pain, vomiting, nausea, or headaches in church, at funerals, cemeteries, or during prayer meetings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:24:00">[01:24:00]</a>.
*   **Paranormal phenomena:** Objects moving, lights turning on/off inexplicably, computer malfunctions, or chairs dragging at night <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:13:58">[01:13:58]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:15:13">[01:15:13]</a>.
*   **Prophetic or "gifted" abilities:** Some individuals, with the help of the devil, may appear to have gifts like predicting the future or knowing hidden things, which is a form of diabolical inspiration <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:24:14">[01:24:14]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:25:01">[01:25:01]</a>. The devil knows the past and can gain trust by revealing hidden details, but he does not know the future <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:24:46">[01:24:46]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:27:32">[01:27:32]</a>.
*   **Suicidal thoughts or attempts:** The devil can influence individuals with suggestions to take their own lives <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:44:04">[01:44:04]</a>.
*   **Physical attacks:** In some cases, the demon may physically push people, causing falls and injuries, or even car accidents <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:45:00">[01:45:00]</a>.

### Causes of Extraordinary Disturbances

Extraordinary disturbances are typically linked to sins of [[influence_of_the_supernatural_in_everyday_life | idolatry]], [[influence_of_the_supernatural_in_everyday_life | divination]], and [[influence of the supernatural in everyday life | magic]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:00:46">[01:00:46]</a>. These sins involve placing trust in spiritual forces other than God, opening a door to demonic influence <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:00:52">[01:00:52]</a>.

Examples include:
*   **Witchcraft and curses:** Often stemming from envy, hatred, or revenge, witchcraft can be perpetrated through food, drink, or even gifts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:52:00">[03:52:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:06:37">[01:06:37]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:09:17">[01:09:17]</a>. These "harms" can affect specific aspects of a person's life, such as finances, relationships, or health <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:13:30">[01:13:30]</a>.
*   **Seeking fortune tellers or engaging in practices like astrology/horoscopes:** These are forms of divination that imply seeking spiritual knowledge outside of God <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:01:13">[01:01:13]</a>. Even if the practitioner is a charlatan, the individual's choice to seek such guidance is a "kind of adultery against God" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:05:18">[01:05:18]</a>.
*   **New Age practices:** Yoga, Reiki, and the Enneagram are considered forms of "white magic" or spiritual practices incompatible with Christian faith, as they derive from other religions and involve seeking union or healing from non-Christian sources <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:29:14">[01:29:14]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:59:00">[02:59:00]</a>.
*   **Spiritism:** Talking to the dead is forbidden in the Bible and is considered a dangerous form of divination, as the devil often imitates the deceased <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:28:40">[01:28:40]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:06:02">[02:06:02]</a>.
*   **Satanism and pacts with the devil:** Some individuals expressly ally themselves with the devil, seeking material gains or power <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:30:47">[01:30:47]</a>. These individuals may seem outwardly normal but ultimately lead empty and sad lives <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:58:50">[01:58:50]</a>.
*   **Concretions by parents or grandparents:** In some rare and tragic cases, children can be affected by consecrations or pacts made by family members with the devil, or even by a parent's rejection <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:49:52">[01:49:52]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:51:00">[01:51:00]</a>.

## Protection and Remedies

The most effective [[spiritual warfare and protective practices | protection against demonic influence]] and the path to liberation involves strengthening one's relationship with God.

### Spiritual Armor

Father Duarte recommends three key practices for building a spiritual armor:
1.  **Good Confession:** Regular confession (at least once a month) is described as "spiritual hygiene," cleansing the soul from sin and restoring God's grace <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:51:20">[01:51:20]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:53:15">[01:53:15]</a>.
2.  **Daily Mass and Communion:** Mass is considered a "spiritual Red Bull," providing immense grace and filling the soul with God's presence <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:52:16">[01:52:16]</a>. Receiving Communion worthily requires being in a state of grace (confessed) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:53:11">[01:53:11]</a>.
3.  **Praying the Rosary Daily:** Our Lady, in her apparitions at Fatima, repeatedly asked for the daily recitation of the Rosary, which Father Duarte states has "great power" against the devil <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:53:29">[01:53:29]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:49:55">[02:49:55]</a>.

### Other Protective Practices:
*   **Prayer of Deliverance:** For those experiencing disturbances, a specific prayer of deliverance (available on Father Duarte's website) can help destroy diabolical disorders <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:54:26">[01:54:26]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:47:47">[02:47:47]</a>. Even recorded prayers can be effective due to God's power and the priest's intention <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:14:57">[03:14:57]</a>.
*   **Blessing Objects and Food:** Blessing food before eating, or blessing gifts received, can neutralize any harm placed on them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:08:50">[01:08:50]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:09:17">[01:09:17]</a>. If suspicious objects are found, they should be destroyed outside the house, preferably after a prayer like the Our Father <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:12:24">[01:12:24]</a>.
*   **Forgiveness:** Holding onto resentment or anger can create "pet feelings" that prevent God's grace from entering one's heart <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:42:10">[01:42:10]</a>. Forgiveness is crucial for inner healing <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:43:06">[01:43:06]</a>.
*   **Avoidance of Occult Practices:** Any involvement with magic, divination, or New Age therapies opens doors to demonic influence, even if done unknowingly <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:01:00">[01:01:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:09:08">[01:09:08]</a>. Halloween, for example, is highlighted as the beginning of the satanic year and a promotion of the occult <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:52:30">[01:52:30]</a>.
