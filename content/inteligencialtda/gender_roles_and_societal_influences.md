## Evolution of Relationships

In the past, relationship options were limited to immediate social circles such as school, neighborhood, or workplace <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:07:08">[07:07:08]</a>. Communication involved direct contact, like calling a landline and speaking with family members before reaching the desired person, creating a social filter <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:33:04">[08:33:04]</a>.

Today, however, interconnectivity offers an almost infinite number of potential partners at the touch of a button, akin to food delivery services <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:14:00">[09:14:00]</a>. While this provides more options, it also leads to significant doubt and decision paralysis <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:29:00">[07:29:00]</a>. The human brain is naturally capable of maintaining 50 to 150 real affective connections <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:35:00">[10:35:00]</a>. Modern society, with its "multitudes" of interactions, overloads the brain, which is not programmed to digest so much information affectively <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="11:10:00">[11:10:00]</a>. This hyper-abundant environment creates a cognitive dissonance with a brain designed for scarcity, contributing to a rise in psychiatric illnesses like depression, anxiety, and bipolarity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="12:16:00">[12:16:00]</a>.

## Societal Ills and Solutions

The **absence of temperance**—the ability to deal with frustration when things don't go one's way—is identified as a major societal problem in the 21st century <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:05:00">[01:05:00]</a>. This leads to dramatic expectations and tantrums when reality doesn't align with desires <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:08:00">[01:08:00]</a>.

Social development and human flourishing occur when the individual is strengthened and given space for decision-making <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:04:00">[02:04:00]</a>. Conversely, collectivist ideologies are seen as "antichrist" because they suppress individual freedom and lead to suffering by prioritizing an abstract collective over personal development <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:11:00">[02:11:00]</a>. These ideologies exploit resentment and division, promising utopia but often leading to internal strife <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:18:00">[02:18:00]</a>.
