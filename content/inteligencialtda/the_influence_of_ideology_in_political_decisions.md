## Defining Ideology
An ideology is characterized as an "idiosyncrasy" where a series of positions are prioritized above common-sense analysis of concrete circumstances <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:48">[00:17:48]</a>. This means political positions can be placed above morality, which should ideally stem from the nature of things and real individuals <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:00">[00:18:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:02">[00:18:02]</a>.
