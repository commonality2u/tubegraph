Biblical prophecy plays a significant role in Christian theology, with approximately 40% of the Bible dedicated to prophetic aspects <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:30:36">[01:30:36]</a>. ## The Pre-existent Christ and Prophecy
The concept of Christ's pre-existence is fundamental to understanding biblical prophecy. Jesus, as God the Son, is eternal and was never created <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:00:54">[01:00:54]</a>. He is the "Word" or "Logos," the very essence and action of God through whom all things were created <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:03">[00:09:03]</a>. The Bible states that "all things were made through him" and "without him nothing that was made was made" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:09">[00:09:09]</a>.

The Trinity — Father, Son, and Holy Spirit — are one God, distinct persons but united in thought, feelings, and synergy <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:25:17">[01:25:17]</a>. They acted together in creation, with God the Father conceiving, the Word (Jesus) executing, and the Holy Spirit supervising <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:17">[00:09:17]</a>. The plural "Elohim" in Genesis 1:1 hints at this plurality within God <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:36">[00:09:36]</a>.

In the Old Testament, Christ often appeared as the "Angel of the Lord," a messenger who received worship, unlike created angels <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:11:16">[01:11:16]</a>. Examples include His appearances to Moses at the burning bush <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:11:59">[01:11:59]</a> and to Joshua before the battle of Jericho <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:22:23">[01:22:23]</a>.

## Prophecies of the First Coming

### The Incarnation: Foreseen from Eternity
Jesus' incarnation was not a response to man's sin but was planned in eternity, even before the creation of the cosmos <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:17:24">[01:17:24]</a>. Revelation 13:8 speaks of "the lamb that was slain since the foundation of the world," indicating that Jesus' death, though occurring at a specific time in history, was already decided in eternity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:19:15">[01:19:15]</a>. This demonstrates God's unconditional love, as He knew humanity would fall and already provided a redeemer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:20:03">[01:20:03]</a>.

### Types and Antitypes: Isaac as a Prefigurement
Many events and figures in the Old Testament serve as "types" that prefigure Christ, the "antitype" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:37:37">[02:37:37]</a>. The sacrifice of Isaac by Abraham is a prime example <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:43:40">[02:43:40]</a>. God asked Abraham to offer his only son, whom he loved, as a sacrifice on Mount Moriah <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:44:06">[02:44:06]</a>. This act was a test of Abraham's faith and mirrored God the Father's willingness to sacrifice His own Son <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:47:04">[02:47:04]</a>. The ram caught in the thicket, which served as a substitute for Isaac, represents Jesus, "the Lamb of God who takes away the sin of the world" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:54:39">[02:54:39]</a>.

### Fulfillment of Specific Prophecies
The entire Old Testament points to Jesus, with thousands of prophecies and "types" related to Him <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:54:39">[02:54:39]</a>.

#### Birthplace (Bethlehem)
Prophecy foretold that the Messiah would be born in Bethlehem Ephrathah <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:57:58">[03:57:58]</a>. Micah 5:2 states: "And you, Bethlehem Ephrathah, too small to be among the thousands of Judah, from you will come forth the one who will reign in Israel, and whose origins are from ancient times, from the days of eternity" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:58:02">[03:58:02]</a>. This prophecy was fulfilled through Caesar Augustus's decree for a census, which required Joseph and Mary to travel from Nazareth to Bethlehem, Mary's hometown, for Jesus' birth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:07:07">[04:07:07]</a>.

#### Manner of Death (Lamb of God)
Jesus' death was deeply prophetic and meticulously planned <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:25:25">[04:25:25]</a>.
*   **Paschal Lamb**: Jesus died at Easter, fulfilling the role of the Paschal lamb <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:51:30">[04:51:30]</a>. In the Passover festival, a one-year-old lamb was examined for four days before being sacrificed on the 14th day of the month <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:50:20">[04:50:20]</a>. Jesus entered Jerusalem on the 10th day and was examined by authorities until the 14th day <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:53:33">[04:53:33]</a>.
*   **Time of Sacrifice**: He was crucified at 9 AM, the time of the morning sacrifice, and died at 3 PM, the time of the afternoon sacrifice <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:59:56">[04:59:56]</a>.
*   **Martyrdom**: The Roman soldiers' mockery of Jesus as "King of the Jews" during his martyrdom mirrored the "Saturnalia" festival, where a condemned slave was temporarily made king and then sacrificed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:07:09">[05:07:09]</a>.

#### Purpose of Death (Atonement)
Jesus' death was not due to incompetence or weakness but a voluntary surrender <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:11:03">[05:11:03]</a>. His sacrifice was necessary for salvation because "without the shedding of blood there is no remission of sins" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:42:00">[05:42:00]</a>. The blood of animals in the Old Testament only "covered" sins (Kippur), but Jesus' blood "destroys" sins and makes us "whiter than snow" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:52:16">[05:52:16]</a>. His declaration "It is finished" on the cross translates to *tetelestai* in Greek, meaning "it is paid" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:48:27">[05:48:27]</a>.

## The Fullness of Time
Jesus came in the "fullness of time" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:50:00">[05:50:00]</a>, a period when specific conditions were met to allow for the widespread propagation of the Gospel.

### Language and Roads
*   **Universal Language**: Alexander the Great's Hellenization of the world resulted in Greek becoming a universal language, much like English today <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:08:08">[06:08:08]</a>. This enabled the New Testament to be written in Greek, making it accessible to a broader audience than if it had been written only in Hebrew <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:00:54">[06:00:54]</a>.
*   **Roman Roads and Peace**: The Roman Empire provided "Pax Romana," ensuring the safety of roads and routes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:17:17">[06:17:17]</a>. This facilitated the travel of early Christians and the preaching of the Gospel throughout the world <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:21:43">[06:21:43]</a>.

## Signs of the Second Coming ([[Biblical Prophecies and End Times | End Times Prophecies]])
Jesus explicitly spoke about His second coming, stating it would be as visible as lightning across the sky <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:37:41">[07:37:41]</a>. While no one knows the exact "day or hour" of His return <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:42:00">[07:42:00]</a>, numerous signs indicate the approaching "times and seasons" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:47:47">[07:47:47]</a>.

Today, many predicted signs are happening simultaneously, including:
*   **Natural Disasters**: Fires (Canada, Europe), earthquakes, and floods (Brazil) are occurring at an unprecedented rate <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:54:01">[07:54:01]</a>.
*   **Geopolitical Turmoil**: Wars and rumors of wars <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:58:08">[07:58:08]</a>.

### Prophecies concerning Israel
Israel plays a central role in [[biblical_prophecies_and_end_times | end-time prophecies]].
*   **Return to the Land**: Ezekiel 36 prophesied the return of the Jewish people from "all the nations of the earth" to their land, a fulfillment seen in the establishment of modern Israel in 1948 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:40:04">[08:40:04]</a>. Ezekiel 37's vision of the "valley of dry bones" represents the Holocaust and the subsequent rebirth of the nation of Israel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:52:03">[08:52:03]</a>.
*   **The Dead Sea**: Ezekiel also prophesied that the Dead Sea would become fresh and support life <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:02:02">[09:02:02]</a>. Today, the northern part of the Dead Sea is drying up, leading to the emergence of thousands of fresh water craters ("boles") and the appearance of fish and vegetation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:16:11">[09:16:11]</a>.
*   **Gog and Magog War**: Ezekiel 38 describes a future war where nations like Russia (Magog), Iran (Persia), and Turkey will invade Israel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:32:32">[09:32:32]</a>. This prophecy is significant as Persia (Iran), historically an ally of Israel, is now its greatest enemy <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:46:18">[09:46:18]</a>.

### The Antichrist and False Prophet
The Bible speaks of a "Satanic Trinity" that will emerge in the end times: Satan (Anti-God), the Antichrist, and the False Prophet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:50:50">[09:50:50]</a>. The Antichrist will be a political leader who will rule the world for seven years <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:54:00">[09:54:00]</a>. The False Prophet, a religious leader, will force people to worship the Antichrist and receive the "mark of the beast" (666) on their forehead or right hand, without which they cannot buy or sell <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:49:50">[10:49:50]</a>. Some interpret 666 as referring to "www" (vav vav vav in Hebrew) in relation to the internet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:52:12">[10:52:12]</a>.

Modern technology facilitates the potential for global control described in prophecies:
*   **Neuralink/Chips**: Companies like Elon Musk's Neuralink are developing chips to be implanted in human brains, connecting to concepts like storing memories and accessing the internet through thought <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:55:28">[10:55:28]</a>.
*   **Global Control**: Elon Musk's Starlink satellites control GPS, which in turn controls global banking, airplane routes, and weather, centralizing significant power <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="11:38:41">[11:38:41]</a>. His "super app" (X, formerly Twitter) aims to integrate all aspects of daily life, including payments, into one platform, potentially controlling half of the world's economy <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="11:42:24">[11:42:24]</a>.
*   **Global Agenda**: The UN's 2030 agenda, with its 17 goals, suggests a global push toward centralized control, clean energy, and a world where individuals may own nothing <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="11:58:14">[11:58:14]</a>.

## The Millennium
After Jesus' second coming, there will be a 1,000-year period known as the Millennium or "Golden Age" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="12:05:01">[12:05:01]</a>. During this time, Christ will be present, Satan will be imprisoned, and humanity will experience an era of peace, prosperity, and reduced sin <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="12:17:21">[12:17:21]</a>. There will be no injustice or corruption, as saints with glorified bodies will govern with Christ <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="12:18:49">[12:18:49]</a>. At the end of the Millennium, Satan will be released for a short time, and some will choose to side with him in a final rebellion before the ultimate judgment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="12:19:23">[12:19:23]</a>.

## Nature of Hell
The Bible describes different concepts related to the afterlife:
*   **Gehenna (Hell)**: This Greek term, used by Jesus, refers to the Valley of Hinnom outside Jerusalem, where ancient Canaanites sacrificed children to Molech and later, Jerusalem's garbage was burned continually <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="12:44:07">[12:44:07]</a>. Jesus used this place as a metaphor for eternal burning and "weeping and gnashing of teeth" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="12:43:33">[12:43:33]</a>. It is the definitive hell, also known as the "Lake of Fire," where the Antichrist, False Prophet, and eventually Satan and all the lost will be cast <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="12:56:58">[12:56:58]</a>.
*   **Hades/Sheol**: These are the Greek and Hebrew terms, respectively, for the "place of the Dead" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="12:59:58">[12:59:58]</a>. It is a waiting place for souls before the final judgment, not the definitive hell <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="12:56:58">[12:56:58]</a>.
