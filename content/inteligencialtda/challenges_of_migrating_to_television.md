He had previously gained experience in radio, starting at FM *People* in Ceará, where he eventually worked as a proofreader for a newspaper while doing radio tests <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:22:50">[02:22:50]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:30:00">[02:30:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:40:00">[02:40:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:40:00">[02:40:00]</a>. ## Overcoming Initial Obstacles

A significant turning point was his relentless pursuit of working with Chico Anysio, a prominent figure in Brazilian humor.

*   **Initial Rejection:** After recording a commercial for Chico's show, Tom was advised to take the tape to Chico, who was staying at a distant beach <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:10:00">[04:10:00]</a>. Chico's cousin, responsible for his rest, initially dismissed Tom, stating that Chico's time off should not be disturbed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:50:00">[04:50:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:57:00">[04:57:00]</a>.
*   **Persistent Effort:** Undeterred, Tom acquired Chico's address and traveled to his cousin's mansion, observing him from a distance before a security guard intervened <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:10:00">[04:10:00]</a>. Despite the initial hesitation, Chico allowed him to enter after asking what he wanted so far away <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:27:00">[04:27:00]</a>.
*   **The Audition:** Tom presented his recorded characters and voices, admitting he also wrote. Chico was impressed and offered him work, instructing him to meet his team in Rio de Janeiro in February <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:44:00">[04:44:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:51:00">[04:51:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:51:00">[04:51:00]</a>.
*   **Travel and Accommodation Challenges:** The journey to Rio involved getting a free ticket on a military instruction flight, facing turbulence, and unexpected delays due to a broken landing gear, causing concern for his host who had to pick him up multiple times <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:15:00">[05:15:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:27:00">[05:27:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:30:00">[05:30:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:30:00">[05:30:00]</a>. He then lived with his host for a month, making daily trips to Globo <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:03:00">[05:03:00]</a>.
*   **Navigating Globo's Gates:** Upon arrival at Globo, he was initially barred, as the staff he was supposed to meet were not in the room. This forced him to return to Niterói and try again the next day <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:50:00">[05:50:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:02:00">[06:02:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:02:00">[06:02:00]</a>. ## Transition to Acting and Character Development

Tom's television career began with presenting educational modules for TV Ceará and later on Globo's *Rural Northeast*, where his playful nature often caused disruptions during breaks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:39:00">[03:39:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:48:00">[03:48:00]</a>.

### *Escolinha do Professor Raimundo*

His big break came with the character João Canabrava on *Escolinha do Professor Raimundo*.

*   **Chico Anysio's Vision:** Chico saw potential in a "drunk" character for his classroom setting, predicting it would be revolutionary <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:10:00">[09:10:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:19:00">[09:19:00]</a>.
*   **Debut and Success:** Tom, ready with his acting honed from live performances, was spontaneously called by Chico during a recording. His improvised performance of João Canabrava was a massive hit, earning applause and launching him into national fame <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:51:00">[09:51:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:10:00">[10:10:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:42:00">[10:42:00]</a>. The show's popularity was so immense that it influenced air bridge flight schedules to accommodate viewers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="11:17:00">[11:17:00]</a>.

## Navigating Career Shifts and Public Perception

### Moving to *Sai de Baixo*

After four years with *Escolinha*, Tom received an invitation to join *Sai de Baixo*, a decision that caused "malaise" with Chico Anysio <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="13:12:00">[13:12:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="13:33:00">[13:33:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="13:40:00">[13:40:00]</a>. He eventually sought forgiveness, acknowledging the need for humility and open communication with his colleagues <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="14:44:00">[14:44:00]</a>.

*   **Creative Freedom:** The character Ribamar on *Sai de Baixo* was conceived as a bumbling doorman with significant creative freedom, allowing Tom to improvise and move freely across the set, which became a trademark <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:25:31">[01:25:31]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:26:41">[01:26:41]</a>.
*   **Impact on Audience:** *Sai de Baixo* became a form of therapy for its audience, with a survey even suggesting a decrease in suicide rates in São Paulo due to the show's uplifting humor on Sunday nights <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:40:17">[01:40:17]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:40:50">[01:40:50]</a>. Tom emphasizes that humor, despite appearing as a joke, can help people gain consciousness and reflect on societal behaviors <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:31:12">[01:31:12]</a>.

### Challenges of Working in the Entertainment Industry

Tom's career continued with significant shifts that presented [[challenges_of_working_in_the_entertainment_industry|challenges_of_working_in_the_entertainment_industry]]:

*   **Leaving Globo for Record (Mega Tom):** After *Sai de Baixo*, he received an "irrefutable" offer from Record, which included creative freedom and the opportunity to build his own team. This decision, however, was met with a veiled threat from Globo, implying he might not return <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:46:25">[01:46:25]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:51:01">[01:51:01]</a>. Tom's intuition, combined with the financial and creative appeal, led him to take the risk, bearing the responsibility of his new team's livelihood <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:52:24">[01:52:24]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:53:27">[01:53:27]</a>.
*   **Daily Show Demands:** *Mega Tom* was a daily program, requiring a different 45-minute show from Monday to Friday <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:57:44">[01:57:44]</a>. This intense routine led to significant physical and mental fatigue, causing Tom to decide to take a break and pay a hefty fine to leave his 7-year contract a year early <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:57:35">[01:57:35]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:01:20">[02:01:20]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:02:27">[02:02:27]</a>.
*   **Breaking Dogmas:** During his time at Record, Tom challenged conventions, such as showing religious items (like a crucifix) on Universal Church-owned television, and including segments like a hypnotist, which was controversial for the network <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:55:00">[01:55:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:56:00">[01:56:00]</a>.
