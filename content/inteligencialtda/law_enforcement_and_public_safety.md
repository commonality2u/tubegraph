## Perspectives on Criminal Justice Reform

The speaker, as a federal deputy, advocates for significant reforms in the [[The criminal justice system and its challenges | criminal justice system]].

### Stricter Punishments
He supports **life imprisonment** in Brazil and, personally, the **death penalty** for heinous crimes like those committed by serial rapists and murderers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:27:00">[02:27:00]</a>. He is working on a legislative decree to hold a plebiscite on life imprisonment, acknowledging the constitutional challenges of the death penalty <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:28:00">[02:28:00]</a>.

### Organ Donation from Criminals
A controversial proposal he supports is making **organ donation compulsory for criminals killed in confrontations** with police, without requiring family authorization. He argues this would allow criminals to contribute positively to society after death, helping those in transplant queues <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:53:00">[02:53:00]</a>.

### Addressing Organized Crime
To combat criminal factions like the PCC, he advocates for:
*   **Financial Suffocation**: Cutting off their financial power through blocking accounts and seizing assets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:34:00">[03:34:00]</a>.
*   **Increased Penalties**: Supporting projects that increase penalties for crimes that directly impact ordinary citizens, such as cell phone theft and robbery, due to the significant disruption they cause <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:17:00">[03:17:00]</a>.

### Gun Laws and Police Funding
He is a strong proponent of the **right of citizens to possess weapons for self-defense**, arguing that laws should be stricter for those who misuse weapons but allow law-abiding citizens to protect themselves and their families <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:31:00">[02:31:00]</a>.
