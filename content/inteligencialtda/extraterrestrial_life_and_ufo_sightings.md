## Evolution of Terminology
Historically, [[UFO Phenomena | unidentified aerial phenomena]] were often referred to as "unidentified flying objects" (UFOs) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:41:40">[01:41:40]</a>. More recently, terms like "non-human bodies" have been introduced <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:45:00">[01:45:00]</a>. NASA has shifted its terminology to "unidentified aerial phenomenon" (UAP), noting that not all phenomena are strictly "aerial" as they can originate from water <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:22:18">[02:22:18]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:22:21">[02:22:21]</a>. The current term "unidentified anomalous phenomenon" (UAP) is now used, acknowledging that these events could be unknown astronomical or meteorological phenomena, or even spy drones <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:42:29">[02:42:29]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:42:41">[02:42:41]</a>.

## Historical Cases and Theories

This incident occurred on June 24, 1947, a day celebrated as "world record day for flyers" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:14:45">[01:14:45]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:14:47">[01:14:47]</a>. Some speculate that these early sightings could have been secret U.S. weaponry <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:14:50">[01:14:50]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:14:53">[01:14:53]</a>.

The "ancient astronaut" theory suggests that [[ufology_and_extraterrestrial_life | extraterrestrials]] have influenced human history <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:19:15">[01:19:15]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:24:24">[01:24:24]</a>. Proponents point to ancient drawings or texts, like the Nazca Lines or biblical descriptions of flying chariots, as evidence <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:51:00">[01:51:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:51:13">[01:51:13]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:51:25">[01:51:25]</a>. However, much of what was once attributed to "astronaut gods" is now explained by classical archaeology, paleontology, and history, with no mysterious or [[ufology_and_extraterrestrial_life | extraterrestrial]] origins <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:53:06">[01:53:06]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:53:40">[01:53:40]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:53:47">[01:53:47]</a>. For example, Tutankhamun's dagger, once thought to be an alien gift, was made from meteorite material that fell in Egypt <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:57:04">[01:57:04]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:57:14">[01:57:14]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:57:42">[01:57:42]</a>.

## Credibility of Reports
The [[reports_and_credibility_of_ufo_sightings | credibility of UFO reports]] is a constant point of contention. While individuals often report what they genuinely believe they saw <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:29:50">[02:29:50]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:30:20">[02:30:20]</a>, human perception can be influenced by bias, lack of knowledge of atmospheric phenomena, or simple misinterpretation of common objects like satellites, space debris, or even clouds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:51:00">[02:51:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:59:41">[02:59:41]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:00:19">[03:00:19]</a>.

*   **Pilot and Military Reports**: Pilots and military personnel, though skilled in flying, may not possess extensive astronomical or meteorological knowledge, leading to misidentification <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:52:23">[03:52:23]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:09:56">[04:09:56]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:13:13">[04:13:13]</a>. Reports from the same incident can vary wildly, with pilots describing the same object with different shapes and colors <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:25:36">[04:25:36]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:39:27">[04:39:27]</a>.
*   **Physical Evidence**: [[Ufology]] today relies not only on stories but also on physical evidence, such as changes in soil where objects reportedly landed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:39:57">[03:39:57]</a>. This allows for scientific analysis in laboratories, which can provide greater credibility when combined with photos, videos, or radar detections <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:43:08">[03:43:08]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:53:36">[03:53:36]</a>.
*   **Stigma**: Military personnel may be reluctant to report [[ufo_sightings_and_abductions | UFO sightings]] due to a stigma of being deemed psychologically unstable or facing retaliation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:50:00">[05:50:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:55:00">[05:55:00]</a>.

### Operation Plate (1977-1978)
Operation Plate (Operação Prato) was a Brazilian Air Force investigation into [[UFO Phenomena | UAPs]] in the Amazon region, particularly in Pará and Maranhão, from 1977 to 1978 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:02:00">[01:02:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:03:10">[01:03:10]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:07:57">[01:07:57]</a>. *   **Observations**: Military teams conducted vigils, recorded videos and photos, and attempted interaction with the objects <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:05:05">[01:05:05]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:05:09">[01:05:09]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:05:21">[01:05:21]</a>. Witnesses described objects throwing spotlights that caused paralysis and other severe injuries, including burns and minor blood extraction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:56:54">[00:56:54]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:57:00">[00:57:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:57:03">[00:57:03]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:57:10">[00:57:10]</a>. *   **Documentation**: The operation generated hundreds of pages of documentation, photos, and videos, some of which are now publicly available in the National Archives <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:02:22">[01:02:22]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:02:28">[01:02:28]</a>. *   **U.S. Involvement**: U.S. interest in these cases became evident, with some Operation Plate documentation allegedly being transferred to the United States <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:01:48">[01:01:48]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:01:50">[01:01:50]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:01:52">[01:01:52]</a>.

### Ubatuba UFO Explosion (1957)
The Ubatuba case, occurring on September 7, 1957, involved the alleged explosion of an object over Ubatuba beach, with pieces falling onto the shore <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:29:41">[01:29:41]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:29:46">[01:29:46]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:29:55">[01:29:55]</a>.

While the possibility of a meteorite was considered, further analysis ruled it out based on morphology and chemical composition, as known meteorites have a maximum of 26% magnesium <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:33:38">[01:33:38]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:33:43">[01:33:43]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:34:02">[01:34:02]</a>.
