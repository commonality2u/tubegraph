## Impact of Media Representation

The discussion emphasizes that media can play a crucial role in promoting acceptance. Experiencing diverse shows can "move minds" and encourage acceptance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:40:17">[01:40:17]</a>. Being able to work and express one's art freely, regardless of identity, is considered a significant form of activism in itself <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:39:33">[01:39:33]</a>. This perspective highlights that simply by existing and performing, artists contribute to [[representation_and_diversity_in_media | representation and diversity]].
