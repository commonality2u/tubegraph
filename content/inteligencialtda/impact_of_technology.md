This constant stimulation may contribute to an inability to solve basic problems, increased dissatisfaction, and a heightened perception of illness among children <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:23:49">[01:23:49]</a>. Giving children tablets in restaurants to keep them quiet is cited as an example of this pervasive capture by technology <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:26:06">[02:26:06]</a>. ## [[Effect of technology in healthcare | Medical Technology and Neuromodulation]]
Technological advancements are also transforming healthcare, particularly in the realm of brain function and mental health.
*   **Neurofeedback** involves brain gymnastics via computer to stabilize brain waves, which can improve conditions like epilepsy and ADHD <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:09:10">[03:09:10]</a>.
*   **Magnetic Stimulation** utilizes magnetic fields to redistribute energy in the brain, aiding in conditions like Parkinson's disease and stroke recovery <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:10:39">[03:10:39]</a>.
*   **Neuromodulation** broadly refers to machine-based techniques that significantly improve cerebral activity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:11:19">[03:11:19]</a>.
*   **Neuralink** (brain-computer interface technology) is discussed as a potential means to "upgrade" human intelligence, improve the lives of people with dementia, or activate areas for conditions like Parkinson's <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:14:19">[03:14:19]</a>. An example of a microchip implant improving the quality of life for a Parkinson's patient is given <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:15:02">[03:15:02]</a>.

Despite these medical advancements, concerns remain about the limits of artificial intelligence, particularly in autonomous systems like self-driving cars. A notable incident involving a Tesla car demonstrates AI's inability to fully evaluate unexpected situations, as it failed to recognize a gymnastics athlete in a low position or children crossing the street <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:12:35">[03:12:35]</a>. This highlights that AI lacks the "surprise" element of human consciousness <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:12:12">[03:12:12]</a>.
