## Modern Societal Vices

The current culture is described as promoting two major vices that are detrimental to the world <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:51:34">[01:51:34]</a>.

### Selfishness and its Consequences
In the modern world, a pervasive selfishness makes it difficult to perceive the logic of true love <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:15">[00:07:15]</a>. This focus on what one can receive from others does not lead to happiness, contributing instead to increased rates of suicide, depression, and a sense of emptiness in life <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:26">[00:07:26]</a>. In contrast, in the past, despite having less material possessions, people lived with joy because they thought about others <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:33">[00:07:33]</a>.

### Promotion of Occult and Divination
A significant issue is the broad cultural interest in divination, magic, and the occult <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:51:45">[01:51:45]</a>. This interest is evident in various forms, from cartoons for children, which "catechize" them in occult vocabulary from a young age, to films from Hollywood <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:52:00">[01:52:00]</a>.

## Halloween and its Dangers
The Halloween celebration is unfortunately considered the beginning of the satanic year <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:52:51">[01:52:51]</a>. While many participate innocently, it is seen as "playing with fire" for Catholics <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:53:06">[01:53:06]</a>.
On this day, which starts the satanic year, there are more human sacrifices to demons <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:54:17">[01:54:17]</a>. Some people even conceive children to sacrifice them at 3 AM on Halloween <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:54:25">[01:54:25]</a>.
A recommended alternative for parents who want their children to participate in dress-up festivities is to dress them as good Christian examples or saints, creating a "Catholic Halloween" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:53:31">[01:53:31]</a>. The original term "All Hallows' Eve" was Catholic, marking the eve of All Saints' Day <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:53:51">[01:53:51]</a>.

Life on Earth is fundamentally a spiritual struggle, a reality many people are unaware of <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:50:34">[02:50:34]</a>. Recognizing this struggle is the first step to winning the war <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:50:57">[02:50:57]</a>.

## Modern Challenges to Faith
Societal changes are impacting how individuals approach their [[spirituality_and_personal_beliefs | faith]].
*   **Health and Lifestyle:** Modern society's aggressive nature, with unhealthy food consumption and excessive screen time, leads to panic attacks, anxiety, and a general lack of temperance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:01:43">[03:01:43]</a>. Maintaining physical health is seen as crucial for spiritual life, as poor health makes it harder to love God and others <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:02:25">[03:02:25]</a>.
*   **New Age Infiltrations:** Concepts from New Age, such as mantras, discussions about energies, and practices like Reiki and Yoga, are sometimes incorporated into seemingly Catholic movements or even public schools <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:58:38">[02:58:38]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:06:35">[02:06:35]</a>. These are considered incompatible with Christian faith as they seek salvation outside of God's word and sacraments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:42:52">[02:42:52]</a>.
*   **Digital Dictatorship:** There is a concern about a coming "digital prison" through a single digital currency and identity, implemented by controlling financial capital and governments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:23:55">[02:23:55]</a>. This system would coerce individuals and restrict freedoms, presenting a dilemma for Christians who may have to choose between adhering to the system and living their faith <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:26:26">[02:26:26]</a>. This imposition is seen as a "satanic principle" of "ordo ab chaos" (order from chaos), where chaos is generated to impose a desired solution <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:27:54">[02:27:54]</a>.

## Spiritual Solutions and Protection
To combat these societal influences and spiritual attacks, several practices are recommended:
*   **Regular Confession:** Making a good confession at least once a month is essential for "spiritual hygiene" and cleanses the soul <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:51:20">[00:51:20]</a>.
*   **Daily Mass and Communion:** Attending Mass daily and receiving communion, when properly prepared, provides a powerful spiritual boost <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:52:06">[00:52:06]</a>. It is considered the most potent form of prayer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:53:04">[00:53:04]</a>.
*   **Praying the Rosary:** Our Lady of Fatima explicitly asked for the daily recitation of the rosary <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:53:22">[00:53:22]</a>. It is a powerful tool against the devil <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:48:55">[02:48:55]</a>.
*   **Blessing Food and Gifts:** It is advisable to bless food, drinks, and gifts, as these can be channels for harm through witchcraft or malevolence <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:06:39">[01:06:39]</a>. The presence of God invoked through blessing destroys the presence of evil <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:09:03">[01:09:03]</a>.
*   **Inner Healing and Forgiveness:** Holding onto resentment and negative feelings can block God's grace. Prayers for inner healing to forgive those who have caused pain are important <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:42:15">[01:42:15]</a>.
*   **Spiritual Companionship:** Seeking support from priests and active prayer groups, such as those within the [[Exploration of personal and collective spirituality | Catholic Charismatic Renewal]], can provide guidance and help <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:46:57">[02:46:57]</a>.
