## Key Theological Concepts in End Times

### The Great Tribulation

*   **The 70th Week**: The final "week" (seven years) is yet to be fulfilled and is explicitly intended for "Daniel's people," meaning Israel, to bring about their conversion <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:56:49">[02:56:49]</a>. This period is also known as the [[tribulation_and_biblical_prophecy | Great Tribulation]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:24:28">[02:24:28]</a>.

The 7-year tribulation is divided into two halves:
*   **First Half (3.5 years)**: Characterized as a period of "false peace" established by a "firm alliance" made by a "prince who is to come" (the Antichrist) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:29:56">[02:29:56]</a>. This involves a peace agreement with Israel, leading to the establishment of the beast system and its economic control <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:59:58">[02:59:58]</a>.
*   **Second Half (3.5 years)**: Referred to as the "great great tribulation," this period begins when Satan falls to Earth, and the Antichrist breaks his covenant with Israel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:33:12">[02:33:12]</a>. He will enter the "holy of holies" in the rebuilt temple, committing the "Abomination of Desolation" as foretold by Daniel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:33:52">[02:33:52]</a>. This is a time of intense persecution <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:32:12">[02:32:12]</a>.
