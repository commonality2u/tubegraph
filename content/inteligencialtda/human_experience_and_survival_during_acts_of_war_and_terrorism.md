## The October 7th Hamas Attack

The attack by the terrorist group Hamas on Israel, occurring on the dawn of Saturday, October 7th (Friday night to Saturday) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:04:00">[03:04:00]</a>, was described as unprecedented and the largest attack in Israel's history <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:26:00">[08:26:00]</a>. It is being compared to the September 11th attacks in the U.S. and even Pearl Harbor due to its surprise nature, scale, and violence <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:40:00">[08:40:00]</a>. Reports include terrible accounts of abused women, kidnapped children, and elderly individuals, highlighting the cruelty of terrorism <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:09:00">[06:09:00]</a>.

The surprise element of the attack reminded some of the Yom Kippur War in the 1970s <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:56:00">[08:56:00]</a>. The number of dead, kidnapped, and the sheer volume of shocking images made the attack particularly frightening <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:10:00">[09:10:00]</a>. This event starkly illustrates [[the_impact_of_terrorism_as_a_political_force | how much terrorism is a threat]] and a form of political force <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:24:00">[09:24:00]</a>. Unlike a common criminal organization, a terrorist organization has a political objective, aiming to destroy or recreate a state, or achieve autonomy <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:51:00">[09:51:01]</a>.

Israeli society was caught off guard, indicating failures in intelligence, defense systems, monitoring, and the army <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:42:00">[10:42:00]</a>. These failures will be thoroughly investigated by [[the_nature_of_israeli_domestic_politics_and_societal_response_to_conflict | Israeli society]] once the immediate conflict subsides <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="11:01:00">[11:01:00]</a>. One contributing factor to this vulnerability was the internal political division within Israel, which led to a focus on internal matters rather than external threats <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="11:50:00">[11:50:00]</a>. Prime Minister Netanyahu's proposed judicial reform had caused significant controversy and divided Israeli society, with even army reservists threatening not to serve if the reform passed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="12:20:00">[12:20:00]</a>. However, the attack had the opposite effect, uniting Israel, with the government and opposition forming a single consensus government <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="12:59:00">[12:59:00]</a>.

The festival's location was sent via a link just two hours before, placing it extremely close to Gaza, about 15 minutes away by car <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="44:01:00">[44:01:00]</a>.

Rafael recounted the terrifying experience:
> "it all started at 6 in the morning more or less and I remember I was right in front of the DJ when he was happening the party was alone in moment and then my friends I was with hanani who is one of the missing and with Rafaela who is his girlfriend And then we ended up separating them if not me I was mistaken when I was in the bathroom alone and then several missiles started coming several missiles I looked at the sky and s missile had come missile without stopping and several anti-missiles thank God the aerodom the army is very effective in this if you managed to disarm these bombs in the air" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="43:59:00">[43:59:00]</a>

The party stopped with an urgent "red alert" message over the microphone, instructing people to hide <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="45:29:00">[45:29:00]</a>. Rafael, along with two friends, sought refuge in a tiny, doorless bunker at a bus stop, just two minutes from the party <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="46:31:00">[46:31:00]</a>. About 40-50 people crammed into the small space <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="46:45:00">[46:45:00]</a>.

Soon after, a hellish ground attack began with gunfire and grenades <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="46:57:00">[46:57:00]</a>. ## The Nature of Hamas: A Terrorist Organization

Hamas, described as a criminal and violent organization, has a political goal unlike common criminals <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:51:00">[09:51:00]</a>. They seek to destroy or recreate a state, or achieve autonomy <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:00:00">[10:00:00]</a>. Their actions are designed to create terror, which they perceive as a victory <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="38:52:00">[38:52:00]</a>. The graphic images and videos released by Hamas serve to show their strength and capability, gaining legitimacy among those Palestinians who believe armed resistance is the only solution against Israel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="39:19:00">[39:19:00]</a>.

Hamas seized power in Gaza by force, winning an election against Fatah and subsequently establishing a dictatorship, massacring all political rivals <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="25:20:00">[25:20:00]</a>. The claim that Gaza is the "biggest open-air prison in the world" is rebutted, with the argument that Hamas itself created this situation by declaring war on Israel, forcing border closures <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="21:18:00">[21:18:00]</a>. Egypt also keeps its border with Gaza closed due to Hamas being a threat to its security <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="22:41:00">[22:41:00]</a>.

Israel has historically used a paradoxical military strategy, choosing the unexpected, more difficult path to achieve surprise <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="26:28:00">[26:28:00]</a>. For years, Hamas relied on tunnels to infiltrate Israel, which became Israel's focus for counter-operations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="28:07:00">[28:07:00]</a>. However, Hamas used a similar paradoxical strategy in the October 7th attack: instead of using tunnels, they came "through the front door" by tearing down the border fence with tractors and paragliding <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="29:16:00">[29:16:00]</a>. This frontal assault was inconceivable to Israel, which had extensive surveillance at the border <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="29:40:00">[29:40:00]</a>. The electronic equipment along the border may have failed, possibly due to Iranian-supplied jamming technology <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="31:13:00">[31:13:00]</a>.
