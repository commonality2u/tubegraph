*   **70th Week (The Tribulation):** This final week, spanning seven years, has not yet been fulfilled <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:29:21">[01:29:21]</a>. It will begin with a peace agreement between the antichrist and "many" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:29:56">[01:29:56]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:30:04">[01:30:04]</a>.
    *   **First Half (3.5 years):** A period of relative peace and a false covenant <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:30:53">[01:30:53]</a>. The emergence of the antichrist and the "Mark of the Beast system" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:31:16">[01:31:16]</a>.
    *   **Second Half (3.5 years - Great Tribulation):** This period is much worse, marked by Satan's fall to Earth, causing great fury <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:33:12">[01:33:12]</a>. The antichrist will break the covenant with Israel and enter the Holy of Holies in the Temple, performing the "Abomination of Desolation" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:33:57">[01:33:57]</a>.

*   **Seals:** The opening of the first seal introduces the antichrist <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:32:39">[01:32:39]</a>. Subsequent seals bring war (red horse), hunger and plagues (black horse), and death (pale horse) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:32:43">[01:32:43]</a>.

*   **Bowls/Cups:** These represent the "chalice of wrath" or "wine of wrath," symbolizing God's judgment and people being "crushed like grapes" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:00:23">[03:00:23]</a>.
