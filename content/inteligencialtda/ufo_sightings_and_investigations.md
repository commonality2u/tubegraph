## Historical Context and Terminology
The term "flying saucer" originated from the 1947 sighting of nine objects by Kenneth Arnold in Washington, which were described as saucer-shaped <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:32:00">[01:32:00]</a>. This term was later Portuguese-ized to "disco voador" (flying saucer) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:39:00">[01:39:00]</a>. Prior to this modern era of "flying saucers," [[Alien sightings and UFOs | UFOs]] were observed, with a newspaper article from December 1939 in Minas Gerais, Brazil, reporting a mysterious light seen by over two thousand people <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:36:00">[01:36:00]</a>. This luminous sphere made "evolutions like a drone," a technology that did not exist at the time <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:33:00">[01:33:00]</a>.

The US military has since adopted the acronym "UAP" (Unidentified Aerial Phenomenon), which is a broader term than UFO, covering any unidentified atmospheric phenomenon <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:08:40">[01:08:40]</a>.

## Types of Encounters
Dr. J. Allen Hynek, who worked on Project Blue Book, established a nomenclature for classifying encounters <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:51:00">[03:51:00]</a>:
*   **First Degree Contact:** Sighting of lights or objects where identification is clear but distant <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:55:00">[03:55:00]</a>.
*   **Second Degree Contact:** Physical evidence left on the ground, electromagnetic interference, animal mutilations, or burns on human skin <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:59:00">[03:59:00]</a>.
*   **Third Degree Contact:** Sighting of the [[extraterrestrial encounters and abductions | occupant]] (humanoid or otherwise), regardless of whether a [[Alien sightings and UFOs | UFO]] is present <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:46:00">[03:46:00]</a>.
*   **Fourth Degree Contact:** [[extraterrestrial encounters and abductions | Abductions]] where individuals are taken aboard a craft <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:46:00">[03:46:00]</a>.
*   **Fifth Degree Contact:** Interaction involving communication, often telepathy, but sometimes in a localized language <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:56:00">[03:56:00]</a>.

## Types of Beings
The most common types of [[extraterrestrial encounters and abductions | alien]] beings reported are:
*   **Alphas (Greys):** Short, around 1.5 meters tall, with disproportionately large heads and large black eyes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:46:00">[03:46:00]</a>. They are commonly associated with [[extraterrestrial encounters and abductions | abductions]] and represent about 80% of reported cases <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:23:00">[03:23:00]</a>.
*   **Betas:** Appear very similar to humans, often described as having beautiful features, blonde hair, and blue eyes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:16:00">[04:16:00]</a>. They can have normal height or be disproportionately tall (up to 2.5 meters) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:21:00">[04:21:00]</a>. They account for about 15% of cases <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:14:00">[04:14:00]</a>.
*   **Gammas (Exotic or Disproportionate ETs):** Include beings with bodies covered in hair, robotic features, or unusual characteristics like those described in the [[Varginha UFO incident | Varginha UFO incident]] (e.g., no fingers or toes, lumps on head, red eyes) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:17:00">[04:17:00]</a>.
