> Imagine a group of people born into a cave, chained by their legs and necks, facing the back wall <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:31">[00:00:31]</a>. The only thing they can see are shadows cast by a fire behind them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.
