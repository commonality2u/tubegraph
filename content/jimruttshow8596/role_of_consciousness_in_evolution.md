## Evolution as Knowledge Creation
Evolution is a knowledge creation process through blind variation and natural selection <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:38:02">[00:38:02]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:38:07">[00:38:07]</a>. Organisms make copies of themselves, and genetic mutations introduce new designs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:38:14">[00:38:14]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:38:20">[00:38:20]</a>. Designs that effectively predict and interact with the environment persist, while dysfunctional ones are weeded out <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:38:32">[00:38:32]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:38:36">[00:38:36]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:38:40">[00:38:40]</a>. This process of natural selection acts as an information channel, allowing organisms to become more statistically correlated with their environment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:50:09">[00:50:09]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:50:11">[00:50:11]</a>.

This accumulated genetic information, or adaptive information, is considered "knowledge" because it reduces environmental uncertainty for the organism <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:39:12">[00:39:12]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:39:17">[00:39:17]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:39:23">[00:39:23]</a>. The biosphere itself is seen as a memory system, encoding adaptive solutions discovered by life to persist far from equilibrium <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:51:56">[00:51:56]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:52:00">[00:52:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:52:07">[00:52:07]</a>.

## Agency: A Precursor to Consciousness
[[sentience_and_consciousness | Agency]] is defined as goal-oriented, purposeful, or teleological movement in living systems <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:47:42">[00:47:42]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:47:45">[00:47:45]</a>. Unlike inanimate objects, which move only by external forces, organisms exhibit inherent goal-directed behavior, such as a bacterium swimming towards food or a plant growing towards sunlight <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:47:24">[00:47:24]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:47:39">[00:47:39]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:47:50">[00:47:50]</a>.

This agency is a product of adaptive information encoded in the system through evolutionary processes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:48:02">[00:48:02]</a>. It's a behavioral output of information processing systems programmed by evolution to evade disorder <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:52:21">[00:52:21]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:52:25">[00:52:25]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:52:29">[00:52:29]</a>. While agency is a form of intelligence, it doesn't necessarily imply [[concept_of_consciousness | consciousness]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:46:57">[00:46:57]</a>.

## The Role of Brains and Information Processing
The emergence of brains marks a significant shift in information processing capabilities, moving beyond phylogenetic (generational) learning to ontogenetic (real-time) learning <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:42:37">[00:42:37]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:42:41">[00:42:41]</a>. Brains enable an organism to encode the consequences of its actions and form memories, creating a "data variable" for the agent and allowing it to become aware of itself in a rudimentary sense <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:33:01">[01:33:01]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:33:51">[01:33:51]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:34:04">[01:34:04]</a>.

### The Bayesian Brain Hypothesis
The Bayesian brain hypothesis, particularly the active inference paradigm, proposes that for a system to persist, it must minimize its prediction error <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:59:10">[00:59:10]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:59:15">[00:59:15]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:00:08">[01:00:08]</a>. This process, seen in how a baby learns to reach for a bottle, involves correcting errors to achieve goals, leading to the minimization of the world model's prediction error <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:00:26">[01:00:26]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:01:28">[01:01:28]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:01:35">[01:01:35]</a>. Prediction error, in this context, refers to the difference between the brain's internal model of the world and the actual world <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:02:17">[01:02:17]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:02:21">[01:02:21]</a>.

This minimization of prediction error is essential for survival, ensuring that the brain's internal model of the environment is increasingly accurate and robust <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:01:40">[01:01:40]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:02:29">[01:02:29]</a>.

## Defining and Locating Consciousness
The [[concept_of_consciousness | concept of consciousness]] can be distinguished into two types:
*   **Phenomenal Consciousness:** Raw sensation and subjective experience, a unified, cohesive field of experience with a point of view <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:37:16">[01:37:16]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:37:21">[01:37:21]</a>. Proponents of [[theories_of_consciousness | Integrated Information Theory]] (IIT) suggest the substrate for this is in the posterior hot zone at the back of the brain <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:37:58">[01:37:58]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:38:00">[01:38:00]</a>.
*   **Access Consciousness:** The ability to consciously think about and access information, such as rehearsing a phone number or recalling a friend's image <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:37:30">[01:37:30]</a>. This is considered more sophisticated and is associated with the frontal lobe, according to [[consciousness_theories | Global Workspace Theory]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:38:10">[01:38:10]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:38:13">[01:38:13]</a>.

While some theories like [[theories_of_consciousness | Integrated Information Theory]] suggest even simple single-celled organisms could have some form of phenomenal [[concept_of_consciousness | consciousness]], this view is debated <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:34:51">[01:34:51]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:34:55">[01:34:55]</a>. Critics argue that systems can have high integrated information without being conscious, suggesting information is necessary but not sufficient for [[concept_of_consciousness | consciousness]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:41:48">[01:41:48]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:41:49">[01:41:49]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:41:51">[01:41:51]</a>.

From an [[evolutionary_purpose_and_cost_of_consciousness | evolutionary purpose and cost of consciousness]] perspective, [[concept_of_consciousness | consciousness]] is seen as a specific biological process that evolved because it is useful, despite being energetically expensive <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:30:40">[01:30:40]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:30:44">[01:30:44]</a>. It serves as a tool for information processing, enabling more efficient use of resources and aiding in reproduction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:31:15">[01:31:15]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:31:21">[01:31:21]</a>.
