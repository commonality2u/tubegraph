## Watches as a Key Accessory

Watches are particularly significant for several reasons:
*   **Conveying Status** Watches can signal to others that an individual is busy and values time <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:02">[00:06:02]</a>.
*   **Completing the Look** Similar to [[impact_of_shoes_on_overall_outfit | shoes]], watches are essential in completing an outfit <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:08">[00:06:08]</a>.
*   **Changing Perception** Different types of watches can drastically alter the feel of an entire look <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:13">[00:06:13]</a>.
