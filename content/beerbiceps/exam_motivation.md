## The Right Motivation: Your [[studying_for_personal_growth_and_future_self | Future Self]]

The purpose of studying should not be for external validation or rewards:
*   Don't study for your parents or to impress friends <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:09">[00:02:09]</a>.
*   Don't study for grades or a future pay package <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:11">[00:02:11]</a>.
