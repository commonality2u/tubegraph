The fundamental theme of the house is **calmness and peace**, aimed at counteracting the fast-paced and erratic nature of entrepreneurship and content creation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:57">[00:00:57]</a>. This calm environment is believed to foster greater creativity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:46">[00:01:46]</a>. The house also integrates elements for spiritual progress and meditation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:41">[00:02:41]</a>, and acts as a reminder of hard work and self-improvement <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:30">[00:04:30]</a>.

## Key Design Elements

### Macrame and Jute Decor
The entire house is decorated with macrame home decor, crafted by artists from Mumbai <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:47">[00:00:47]</a>. The owner has a fascination with cloth-based and jute-based home decor, finding these materials calming <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>. This theme extends to the balcony, where more jute and cloth-based decor keeps the space feeling humble <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:00">[00:06:00]</a>.

### Ocean Views and Inspiration
A significant factor in choosing the flat was the direct view of the ocean <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:16">[00:03:16]</a>. Waking up to the city, greenery, and the ocean is described as a blessing <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:37">[00:03:37]</a>. This view contributes to calmness from a neuroscience perspective <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:51">[00:03:51]</a> and inspires the owner to keep going, helping to alleviate self-doubt <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:56">[00:03:56]</a>. The ocean also reminds the owner of the importance of hard work <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:30">[00:04:30]</a>. The sunsets from this location are also fantastic <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:47">[00:04:47]</a>. The ocean is a recurring theme throughout the house, with abstract wall art in the podcast studio representing the seaside <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:31">[00:11:31]</a>.

### Blue Color Palette
Despite blue not being the favorite color, it is heavily featured, particularly in the living room, where a large blue element is designed to represent the ocean <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:27">[00:01:27]</a>. The color blue is considered spiritual and calming, aligning with the house's central theme <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:38">[00:01:38]</a>.

### Buddha Idols
Numerous small Buddha idols are placed throughout the house to serve as reminders for spiritual progress and meditation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:33">[00:02:33]</a>.

### Greenery and Plants
Plants, initially not a personal preference, were recommended by the architect and have proven beneficial, signifying a part of growing up for the owner <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:53">[00:02:53]</a>. The owner considers plants as "plant babies" and new pets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:27">[00:05:27]</a>, especially enjoying meditating under a Bougainvillea plant <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:06">[00:05:06]</a>.

### Lighting
The architect emphasized lighting, which dramatically changes the energy of the space at night, making it even more focused while maintaining themes of Buddha, greenery, calm, creativity, and teamwork <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:38">[00:06:38]</a>.

### Color Psychology
Color psychology plays a significant role in the decor <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:25">[00:09:25]</a>:
*   **Blue**: For calm <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:31">[00:09:31]</a>.
*   **Yellow**: For activity, positivity, and energy, used in the office/editing studio <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:37">[00:07:37]</a>.
*   **Gray**: For focus, also in the office/editing studio to ensure colors in videos appear beautifully <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:39">[00:07:39]</a>.
*   **Orange**: For creativity and optimism, seen in the private bedroom <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:33">[00:09:33]</a>. It was the owner's favorite color growing up <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:13">[00:15:13]</a>.
*   **Purple**: Representative of magic, the "pre-step to creativity," used in the team's ideation room <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:35">[00:09:35]</a>.
*   **Green**: The primary theme of the podcast studio, representing nature, peace, and knowledge <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:19">[00:13:19]</a>.
