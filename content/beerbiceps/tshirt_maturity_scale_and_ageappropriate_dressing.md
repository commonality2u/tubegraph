## The T-Shirt Maturity Scale <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:29">[00:00:29]</a>

The scale ranges from "Young" on one side to "Mature" on the other <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:32">[00:00:32]</a>. Ideally, an individual should [[youth_selfconfidence_and_societal_expectations|dress according to their age]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[00:00:37]</a>.

### Factors Influencing Maturity in T-Shirts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:51">[00:00:51]</a>

Two primary factors determine whether a T-shirt makes you look younger or older:
1.  **Pattern vs. Plain:** Whether the T-shirt has a pattern or is plain <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:54">[00:00:54]</a>.
2.  **Color:** Whether the T-shirt is bright and colorful, or dark/white <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:58">[00:00:58]</a>.

*   **Dark or white colors** tend to make you look older <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:04">[00:01:04]</a>.
*   **Bright and colorful T-shirts with many patterns** tend to make you look younger <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>.

### Combining Factors <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:11">[00:01:11]</a>

Combining these two factors can result in different perceived ages:
*   A **dark T-shirt with a pattern** doesn't significantly impact age perception (neither old nor young) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:17">[00:01:17]</a>.
*   A **plain T-shirt that is bright in color** also doesn't significantly impact age perception <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:23">[00:01:23]</a>.
*   A **brightly colored T-shirt with a lot of patterns** will make you look very young <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:29">[00:01:29]</a>.
*   A **dark, plain T-shirt** will make you look significantly more mature <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:34">[00:01:34]</a>.

### Age-Specific Guidelines <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:38">[00:01:38]</a>

*   **Above 22 years old:** It is advisable to dress higher on the scale, opting for a slightly older look <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:40">[00:01:40]</a>.
*   **Still in college:** You can afford to dress a bit younger, wearing bright colors and more [[graphics_and_pattern_rules_on_tshirts|graphics and patterns]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:44">[00:01:44]</a>.
