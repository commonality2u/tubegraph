## Bare Essentials

The foundation of a versatile t-shirt collection includes:

*   **Black V-Neck T-Shirt** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:13:16">[06:13:16]</a>: This is considered the most versatile piece of clothing, suitable for various occasions. It can be worn by itself with jeans, paired with jackets, or even for some formal events <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:17:19">[06:17:19]</a>.
*   **White V-Neck T-Shirt** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:28:30">[06:28:30]</a>: Similar to the black V-neck, it offers great versatility <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:30:31">[06:30:31]</a>.
*   **White Round Neck T-Shirt** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:33:34">[06:33:34]</a>: Distinct from the white V-neck, this classic piece can be paired with many [[tips_for_choosing_shirt_fits_and_layering | jackets]] or worn on its own with jeans <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:39:41">[06:39:41]</a>.
