## How Stress Affects Hair Health

When you experience stress or anxiety, your body's cortisol levels elevate <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:34:00">[07:34:00]</a>. Cortisol, known as a stress hormone, promotes the aging process <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:39:00">[07:39:00]</a>. This elevation negatively affects the quality of both your skin and your hair <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:43:00">[07:43:00]</a>. In the long term, chronic stress may even contribute to hair loss <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:47:00">[07:47:00]</a>.

## Stress Management for Hair Health

While it's easy to say "avoid stress and anxiety," the practical solution lies in effective management techniques <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:50:00">[07:50:00]</a>. The most significant solution for managing stress and anxiety is regular, consistent [[psychological_factors_and_stress_management_through_meditation_for_teens | meditation]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:58:00">[07:58:00]</a>.

By incorporating meditation into your routine, you can support the health of your hair, skin, and mind <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:07:00">[08:07:00]</a>.
