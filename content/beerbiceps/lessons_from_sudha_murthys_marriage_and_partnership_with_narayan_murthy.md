### Shared Vision and Purpose
Sudha Murthy notes that while she cannot perfectly articulate her husband's concept of life, after 43 years together, she understands it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:13">[00:05:13]</a>. For Narayan Murthy, life is a passion project where he works extremely hard, focused entirely on his aim <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:21">[00:05:21]</a>. He believes that the creation of wealth is vital for the nation, not for personal enjoyment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:38">[00:05:38]</a>. His purpose involves creating jobs and uplifting people from lower economic levels <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:51">[00:05:51]</a>. He holds that respect is earned by demonstrating quality and capability, as India did in the software industry <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:05">[00:06:05]</a>.

Sudha Murthy aligns with this view, stating that neither of them prioritizes enjoying wealth over creating it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:46">[00:05:46]</a>. She supports the idea that working passionately to uplift oneself is key to earning respect <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:53">[00:06:53]</a>.

### Key Learnings from Marriage
Sudha Murthy attributes some of her personal growth to her husband:
*   **Mindful Communication**: She learned from Narayan Murthy not to talk excessively, but to speak only if someone is listening <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:37">[00:20:37]</a>.
*   **Passion and Sacrifice**: Narayan Murthy taught her that passion, not money, drives achievement, and that passion requires personal sacrifice and the sacrifice of those supporting you <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:58">[00:20:58]</a>. ### Supporting Infosys's Genesis

She knew she had to support him due to his hard work and passion <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:10">[00:22:10]</a>. Despite these "hiccups," she remained positive, believing things would improve after a few years <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:17">[00:23:17]</a>.

When facing difficulties, Sudha Murthy would reassure her husband, saying she had a degree and could always work <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:48">[00:24:48]</a>. Their expectations were modest, without dreams of bungalows or luxury cars <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:50">[00:25:50]</a>.
