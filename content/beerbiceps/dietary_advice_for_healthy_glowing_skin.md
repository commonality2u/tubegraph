Achieving clear, glowing skin can be significantly influenced by focusing on your [[importance_of_diet_and_lifestyle_for_skin_health | diet]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:23">[04:23]</a>. The primary factors for this are proper hydration and sufficient intake of vegetables and fiber <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:24">[04:24]</a>.

## Hydration
Keeping yourself well-hydrated throughout the day is crucial for healthy skin <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:27">[04:27]</a>. A good guideline for hydration is to aim for clear urine for most of the day <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:30">[04:30]</a>. If your urine is slightly yellow, it indicates a need to drink more water <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:34">[04:34]</a>.

## Vegetable and Fiber Intake
The second key factor is focusing on your vegetable and fiber intake <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:36">[04:36]</a>. While fruits can be included, the primary focus should be on vegetables, as they are generally more nutritionally dense <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:40">[04:40]</a>. This means that for the same amount of food, you receive a greater quantity of vitamins and minerals <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:49">[04:49]</a>. A simple rule of thumb for maximizing nutritional benefits is to consume a variety of colorful vegetables <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:52">[04:52]</a>. This approach offers a straightforward path to maintaining healthy skin <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:57">[04:57]</a>.
