## 1. The Role of Shoes
Shoes are essential for completing any outfit, yet they are often overlooked by Indian men <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>. A common mistake is pairing sports shoes or bulky shoes with every outfit, which can detract from a manly appearance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:11">[00:01:11]</a>.

To achieve a classy look, opt for flat-soled shoes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>. Wearing flat-soled shoes suggests comfort with one's height and fashion choices <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:48">[00:01:48]</a>. For those new to fashion, incorporating loafers into their wardrobe is a good starting point <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:57">[00:01:57]</a>. Different shoes can dramatically alter an outfit's feel; for example, combining a black t-shirt and blue jeans with black Converse gives a casual, boyish look, while pairing them with manly boots enhances the "manliness factor" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:14">[00:02:14]</a>.

Learning effective combinations is a primary fashion rule for men <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:46">[00:02:46]</a>. Classic and dependable combinations include a black t-shirt with dark blue jeans, or a white shirt with blue jeans <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:50">[00:02:50]</a>.

Education is key; individuals should research classic men's dressing <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:27">[00:03:27]</a>, including how to pick [[formal_dressing_rules_for_men | a suit]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:33">[00:03:33]</a>. Continuous experimentation and research lead to improved dressing skills <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:56">[00:03:56]</a>.

## 3. Investing in Different Pants
Expanding beyond standard jeans can significantly change one's overall look <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:12">[00:04:12]</a>. Chinos are a recommended starting point for experimentation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:27">[00:04:27]</a>. For instance, pairing a black t-shirt with gray chinos instead of dark blue jeans lightens the outfit's mood, making it suitable for various occasions like clubbing or a lunch date <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:39">[00:04:39]</a>.

If investing in additional pants, chinos should be the priority <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:53">[00:04:53]</a>. Accessories, particularly watches, play a crucial role in classic dressing <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:46">[00:05:46]</a>. Watches can convey that one is a busy individual who values time <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:02">[00:06:02]</a>, and they complete an outfit's look <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:11">[00:06:11]</a>. Many men begin to dress better in or after college, and initial nervousness about wearing new clothes can prevent experimentation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:17">[00:07:17]</a>.
