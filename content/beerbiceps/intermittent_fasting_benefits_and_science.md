## How Intermittent Fasting Works

The body has natural fasting and feasting mechanisms <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:10">[00:02:10]</a>. After a good night's sleep, the body is in a detoxification and elimination mode, which is interrupted upon eating <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:52">[00:03:52]</a>. It is recommended to eat only when physical hunger cravings appear <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:00">[00:04:00]</a>.

For many benefits, a minimum of 12 hours of fasting is effective <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:11">[00:03:11]</a>. For example, finishing dinner at 8:00 PM and eating breakfast at 8:00 AM already constitutes a 12-hour fast <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:16">[00:03:16]</a>. If hunger isn't present, one can extend the fasting window to 9:00 AM, 10:00 AM, or 11:00 AM <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:23">[00:03:23]</a>.

### Individualization is Key

[[intermittent_fasting_benefits_and_science | Intermittent fasting]] should not be approached as a rigid fad like the 16:8 method <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:01">[00:03:01]</a>. The duration of fasting (e.g., 15, 16, or 20 hours) should be tailored to individual body needs and not compared to others <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:04">[00:03:04]</a>.

It is crucial to listen to one's body <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:31">[00:03:31]</a>. If fasting doesn't suit an individual, they shouldn't do it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:36">[00:03:36]</a>. Factors like metabolism differences, diabetes, or fluctuating sugar levels may make fasting unsuitable for some individuals <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:40">[00:03:40]</a>. Daily variations in mental work or stress can also affect hunger levels <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:54">[00:04:54]</a>.

*   **During the fast**, only water should be consumed to allow the digestive system to shut down <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:25">[00:05:25]</a>. *   **Initial challenges**: The first few days of fasting can be difficult, but the body adapts and changes its physiology <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:52">[00:05:52]</a>.
