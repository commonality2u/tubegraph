## General Shoe Care Principles

*   **Avoid wearing new sneakers outdoors:** To maintain their pristine condition, especially for white sneakers, it's advised to wear them only indoors initially, such as for parties, dates, or inside restaurants <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:16">[00:06:16]</a>.
*   **Clean after indoor use:** Even when worn indoors, shoes can accumulate dust. Upon returning home, promptly wipe the white portions of sneakers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:33">[00:06:33]</a>.
*   **Reserve new shoes for important occasions:** To preserve their quality and appearance, purchase new shoes for significant events and wear them only for those specific occasions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:19">[00:07:19]</a>.
*   **Know when to retire shoes:** Once a shoe becomes worn out or dirty, it should no longer be worn <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:15">[00:07:15]</a>.

## Specific Shoe Types and Their Care

### Sneakers
Sneakers are currently very fashionable <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:49">[00:05:49]</a>. The white trim on sneakers is often a highlight and should be kept clean to avoid a messy appearance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:02">[00:06:02]</a>.

### Loafers
Classy loafers are frequently made from suede material, which requires specific care <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:44">[00:06:44]</a>.
*   For black suede shoes, use a black suede spray <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:51">[00:06:51]</a>.
*   For suede shoes of any other color, use a plain suede spray <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:55">[00:06:55]</a>.
These sprays help maintain the shoe and increase its shelf life <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:57">[00:06:57]</a>.

### Casual Footwear
Loafers and sneakers are highly fashionable for casual wear <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:06">[00:07:06]</a>. For everyday use, boots and flip-flops are recommended <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:13">[00:07:13]</a>.
