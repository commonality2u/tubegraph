## Common Footwear Mistakes

A frequent error among Indian men is pairing sports shoes or bulky shoes with every outfit, including formal or party wear <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:11">[00:01:11]</a>. Such choices are considered unsuitable for parties and do not contribute to a "manly" appearance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:19">[00:01:19]</a>. This mistake is particularly common among school and college students who may mistakenly believe bulky shoes convey a "Rambo" look <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:27">[00:01:27]</a>.

## Choosing the Right Shoes

To achieve a classy look, it is recommended to opt for flat-soled shoes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>. Flat-soled shoes convey confidence in one's height, appearance, and fashion choices <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:47">[00:01:47]</a>.

For those new to men's fashion, loafers are an excellent starting point due to their versatility and affordability <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:57">[00:01:57]</a>.

Different types of shoes can dramatically alter an outfit's vibe <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:14">[00:02:14]</a>:
*   Pairing a black t-shirt and blue jeans with black Converse shoes creates a casual, boyish look <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:17">[00:02:17]</a>.
*   The same outfit, when combined with "manly" boots featuring a relatively flat sole and a slight heel, enhances the manliness factor <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:24">[00:02:24]</a>. These types of boots are suitable for a "badass" style <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:29">[00:02:29]</a>.

Ultimately, [[choosing_formal_footwear_and_accessories | experimenting]] with different shoe combinations is key to finding what works best for individual style and confidence <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:02">[00:07:02]</a>.
