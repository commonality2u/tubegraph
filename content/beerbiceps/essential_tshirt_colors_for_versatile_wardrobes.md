## The Bare Essentials

### Black and White T-shirts
Both black and white T-shirts are considered the most versatile pieces of clothing <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:19">[00:06:19]</a>.
*   **Black V-neck** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:16">[00:06:16]</a>: This T-shirt is a must-have for every man's wardrobe <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:17">[00:06:17]</a>. It can be dressed up for semi-formal events by pairing it with jackets, or worn casually with jeans <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:21">[00:06:21]</a>.
*   **White V-neck** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:28">[00:06:28]</a>: Similar to the black v-neck, a white v-neck offers immense versatility <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:30">[00:06:30]</a>.
*   **White Round Neck** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:34">[00:06:34]</a>: Distinct from the white v-neck, the white round neck pairs well with various jackets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:41">[00:06:41]</a>. It's another classic piece that can be worn on its own with jeans <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:43">[00:06:43]</a>.

## T-shirt Maturity Scale and Color Choice
The choice of T-shirt color can influence perceived age:
*   **Younger Appearance**: Bright and colorful T-shirts, especially those with many patterns, tend to make the wearer look younger <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>. If you are in college, you can comfortably wear brighter colors and more graphics <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:45">[00:01:45]</a>.
*   **Older/More Mature Appearance**: Dark or white T-shirts make you look older <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:04">[00:01:04]</a>. A dark, plain T-shirt will make you appear more mature <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:36">[00:01:36]</a>. If you are above 22 years old, it is advisable to dress slightly higher on the maturity scale <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:40">[00:01:40]</a>.

Combining factors also affects perception:
*   A dark T-shirt with a pattern will not make you look significantly older or younger <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:19">[00:01:19]</a>.
*   A plain T-shirt that is bright in color also doesn't significantly impact perceived age <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:23">[00:01:23]</a>.
