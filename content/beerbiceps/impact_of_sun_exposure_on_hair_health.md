While sunlight is beneficial for overall health, providing vitamin D, very harsh and frequent sun exposure is not good for hair <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:27:00">[02:27:00]</a>.

## How UV Rays Affect Hair
The sun contains UV rays that can damage hair and scalp <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:40:00">[02:40:00]</a>. These UV rays destroy epithelial cells on the scalp, which are crucial for hair growth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:45:00">[02:45:00]</a>.

## Recommendations
To protect hair health, it is advisable to avoid prolonged exposure to harsh sunlight <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:51:00">[02:51:00]</a>.
