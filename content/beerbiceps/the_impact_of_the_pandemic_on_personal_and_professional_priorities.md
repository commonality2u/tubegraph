### Impact on Personal Priorities and Authenticity

For individuals, the pandemic acted as a catalyst for profound self-discovery and a shift towards greater authenticity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:09">[00:09:09]</a>. Janhvi Kapoor, for instance, expresses a personal transformation post-COVID <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:11">[00:09:11]</a>:

> "I've been giving less and less of a [expletive] about what I'm putting out and I think it's throwing people off a little bit but um but I I I just I'm tired I think of um taking the pressure of catering a certain image and I just want to find out who I am for myself more than for others now and it feels good." <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:34">[00:08:34]</a>

This reflects a broader trend of individuals prioritizing personal growth and self-discovery over external pressures and perceived expectations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:53">[00:08:53]</a>. The shift includes:
*   **Embracing Authenticity**: A desire to be genuine rather than maintaining a curated image <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:53">[00:08:53]</a>.
*   **Realigning Priorities**: Understanding that [[balancing_career_success_and_personal_wellbeing | happiness]] and fulfillment are highly individual and diverse <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:26">[00:09:26]</a>.
*   **Reducing External Pressure**: A conscious decision to stop catering to a "certain image" and instead focus on self-identification <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:47">[00:08:47]</a>.

*   **Film Production and Financing**: The methods of making and financing films have evolved <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:57">[00:00:57]</a>.
*   **Audience Reception**: The way audiences receive films has changed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:01">[00:01:01]</a>.
