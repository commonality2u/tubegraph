## Content Volume and Reach

The Ranveer Show (TRS) has a substantial content library, consisting of over 3,000 videos and 450 podcasts, accumulated over eight years of content creation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:00">[00:00:00]</a>. It is recognized as "India's most curious podcast" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:06">[00:00:06]</a>.

## Philosophy and Growth

The podcast is continually [[growth_and_success_of_the_ranveer_show | expanding]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:08">[00:00:08]</a>, with its growth attributed to the team's enjoyment of the content creation journey <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:14">[00:00:14]</a>. A core aspect of the show's philosophy is the continuous learning process from each episode and the joy derived from sharing this new knowledge with the audience <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:19">[00:00:19]</a>.

## Key Milestones

A significant achievement for TRS is becoming the first brand partnership for a self-produced Indian podcast <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:32">[00:00:32]</a>. The show is now presented as "AIO presents TRS" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:38">[00:00:38]</a>.
