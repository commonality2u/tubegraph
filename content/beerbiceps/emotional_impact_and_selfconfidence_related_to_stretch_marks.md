## Normalizing Stretch Marks

Stretch marks are a natural phenomenon that happens to every single human being <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:19">[00:09:19]</a>. Many individuals feel embarrassed and question what others might think of them due to their stretch marks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:22">[00:09:22]</a>. However, it is important to remember that stretch marks typically appear on the torso and thighs, areas of the body rarely exposed to the world, minimizing any practical need for embarrassment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:28">[00:09:28]</a>.
