The discussion around [[Paranormal investigations and beliefs | paranormal phenomena]] often delves into its intersections with spirituality, folklore, and even witchcraft, highlighting diverse cultural perspectives on the existence and nature of entities beyond conventional understanding.

## Indian Folklore and Entities

Indian culture is rich with tales of supernatural beings, each with distinct origins and characteristics:

*   **Chudel and Diyan**
    These entities share a similar origin theory <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:00">[00:00:00]</a>. It is believed that if a pregnant woman or a woman who dies during childbirth is not cremated properly with all rituals, her latent desires lead her to become a Chudel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:06">[00:00:06]</a>. As a Chudel, she remains on Earth unsatisfied and unable to witness the happiness of other women <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:25">[00:00:25]</a>. Chudel is considered a type of witch, and Diyan is similar in nature <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:31:19">[01:31:19]</a>.

*   **Yakshin**
    Described as demigoddesses, Yakshin are believed to reside in a realm between Earth and Heaven, specifically in Alkapuri, governed by Kubera <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:11:37">[01:11:37]</a>. While they can appear on Earth, they often do so in evil forms <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[00:00:37]</a>. Initially beautiful and voluptuous, their true evil form emerges after seduction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:45">[00:00:45]</a>. Their victims are typically men <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>. Numerous folklore tales exist of people encountering Yakshin <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:29:56">[01:29:56]</a>.

*   **Pishach**
    In Hindu mythology, a Pishach is believed to be created when a man dies a very violent death in a surge of anger <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:03:33">[01:03:33]</a>. Unlike poltergeists, which are noisy and mischievous but rarely kill, Pishach are capable of causing death <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:11:00">[01:11:00]</a>.

*   **Betal**
    A Betal is a hanging ghost, distinct from a gargoyle, and usually formless <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:47:00">[01:47:00]</a>. It is a man who died with unfulfilled wishes and remains attached to a tree, a permanent fixture <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:47:00">[01:47:00]</a>. Betals are said to haunt passersby, travel with them, and jeopardize their lives by creating havoc <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:19:14">[01:19:14]</a>.

## The Nature of Ghosts and Suffering

The realm of ghosts is often depicted as one of suffering <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:32">[00:05:32]</a>. Spiritual and philosophical texts suggest that when an individual experiences an unnatural death before their appointed time, they spend the remainder of that time in this realm as a ghost <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:46">[00:05:46]</a>.

Not all ghosts are considered evil; the majority are believed to be good <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:53">[00:09:53]</a>. Evil ghosts are typically those with unfinished business or who experienced an unjust or violent death <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:01:01">[01:01:01]</a>.

## Science, Technology, and the Paranormal

*   **K2 Meters:** Devices like K2 meters detect electromagnetic fields (EMF) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:44:00">[02:44:00]</a>.
