## Balancing Public Persona and Private Life
Ajay Nagar is a very private person, preferring to stay within his home, a characteristic he has maintained since childhood <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:43">[00:04:43]</a>. His room serves as his world where he works, plays games, and relaxes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:02">[00:05:02]</a>. He intentionally keeps the character of CarryMina "very open," but considers "Ajay" to be personal, something he cannot and chooses not to share publicly <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:17">[00:01:17]</a>. This deliberate separation helps him maintain a sense of privacy <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:38">[00:01:38]</a>, which he values highly <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:23">[00:05:23]</a>.

This approach aligns with [[balancing_personal_life_and_public_persona | balancing personal life and public persona]], where the challenge lies in managing public expectations while safeguarding personal boundaries.

## Dealing with Public Attention and Family
While he enjoys meeting people, Ajay expresses a desire to avoid "unwanted things" that come with fame <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:25">[00:05:25]</a>. A specific concern arises when he is with his parents <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:35">[00:05:35]</a>. Fans sometimes address him using the same language or demeanor he employs in his videos, which may be inappropriate in front of his parents <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:40">[00:05:40]</a>. He emphasizes that "parents are parents, you can't speak anything in front of them" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:45">[00:05:45]</a>.

This highlights a key aspect of [[dealing_with_public_attention_and_celebrity_life | dealing with public attention and celebrity life]], where the spontaneous nature of fan interactions can sometimes clash with family values and traditional respect.

## Family Reactions to Fame
Ajay's parents are also experiencing his fame as something new for them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:02">[00:10:02]</a>. They have had colleagues call them, asking to be introduced to their son <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:05">[00:10:05]</a>. This indicates that both Ajay and his parents are "learning together how to deal with it" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:15">[00:10:15]</a>.

Regarding the use of strong language in his videos, Ajay's mother has advised him not to use certain abuses, particularly those involving family members <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:24">[00:10:24]</a>. He is mindful of this, especially during live streams, thinking "my mother must be watching" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:33">[00:10:33]</a>, although he admits to occasional slips when startled, for instance, during a horror game <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:40">[00:10:40]</a>.
