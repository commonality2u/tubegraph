## AIO Presents TRS

The podcast has partnered with "AIO," transforming the show's title to "AIO presents TRS" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:38">[00:00:38]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:09">[00:01:09]</a>. While the core elements of the podcast—its curiosity, magnanimous guests, and free-flowing conversations—remain unchanged <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>, this partnership introduces a "different one size style," making TRS "more fashionable than ever" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:47">[00:00:47]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:53">[00:00:53]</a>. The collaboration kicks off with 16 new episodes featuring "successful Minds" who have "achieved it in style" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>.
