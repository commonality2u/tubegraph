## Dietary Factors

To promote healthier skin, focus on dietary adjustments:
*   **Reduce Sugar and Junk Food** Avoid sugar, fast food, and heavily processed restaurant meals <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:45">[00:09:45]</a>.
*   **Eliminate Packaged Fried Snacks** These often contain unhealthy oils and trans fats, which can negatively impact skin health <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:51">[00:09:51]</a>.
*   **Increase Fruits and Vegetables** Incorporate more fresh fruits and vegetables into your daily [[role_of_diet_and_nutrition | diet]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:04">[00:10:04]</a>.
*   **Maintain Dietary Balance** Avoid overconsumption of any single food type, whether it's non-vegetarian options, eggs, or specific vegetarian protein sources like paneer or soya chunks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:08">[00:10:08]</a>.

## Lifestyle Factors

Beyond [[role_of_diet_and_nutrition | diet]], lifestyle choices play a crucial role in skin well-being:
*   **Stress Management** [[role_of_stress_and_hormonal_imbalances_in_skin_issues | Stress]] significantly impacts skin health <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:26">[00:10:26]</a>.
    *   **Cortisol Levels** High stress elevates cortisol, a hormone that negatively affects skin aging, overall skin quality, and can increase oil secretion from sebaceous glands <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:28">[00:10:28]</a>.
    *   **Reduction Strategies** To lower cortisol long-term, prioritize good sleep hygiene and practice meditation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:42">[00:10:42]</a>. Both can reduce stress levels and improve skin quality <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:50">[00:10:50]</a>.

These [[modern_lifestyle_challenges_and_their_effects_on_health | lifestyle factors]] are as vital as [[role_of_diet_and_nutrition | diet]] for achieving healthy skin <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:56">[00:10:56]</a>.
