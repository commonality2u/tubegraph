## Understanding Negative Entities and Their Influence

*   **Pretas (Angry Spirits)**: More dangerous than Bhutas, these are very angry spirits, possibly former human beings whose karmas led to a negative state <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:21:00">[04:21:00]</a>. They can arise from accidental or violent deaths at a young age, or suicide <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:56:00">[04:56:00]</a>. Pretas can cause diseases, obstructions, accidents, and unusual negative occurrences <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:27:00">[05:27:00]</a>.
*   **Pishachas**: Extremely dangerous entities, often not human in origin <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:48:00">[05:48:00]</a>. They are like "mad bulls" that attack <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:57:00">[05:57:00]</a>. Brahma Pishachas, however, are highly knowledgeable human beings who misused their spiritual knowledge <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:58:00">[05:58:00]</a>. Pishachas are confined to specific areas of extreme negative or filthy energy and are associated with foul odors <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:44:00">[05:44:00]</a>.
*   **Rakshasas and Asuras**: Highly powerful beings, often described as gods in the negative sense, possessing exceptional intelligence and knowledge of spiritual realms <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:12:00">[01:12:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:37:00">[01:37:00]</a>.
