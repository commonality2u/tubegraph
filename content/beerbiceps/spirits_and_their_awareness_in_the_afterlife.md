The concept of spirits and their awareness in the afterlife suggests a complex and varied existence beyond physical death. ## Types of Spiritual Awareness

Based on numerous investigations, spirits exhibit different levels of awareness regarding their post-mortem state:

*   **Unaware Spirits** Many spirits, approximately eight out of ten cases, are unaware that they are dead <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:09">[00:06:09]</a>. They remain trapped in a loop, continuing their day-to-day routines <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:16">[00:06:16]</a>. For instance, a German prisoner of war from the 1800s encountered in a Kandala graveyard had no idea it was the 21st century <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:00">[00:00:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:09">[00:00:09]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:49">[00:10:49]</a>. Similarly, a spirit encountered at Hulker Bridge in Pune, a purportedly haunted location in India, had been stuck there since the 11th century <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:18">[00:00:18]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:08">[00:11:08]</a>. These spirits may try to gain attention because they are confused about their situation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:55">[00:06:55]</a>.
*   **Aware and Voluntarily Present Spirits** Some spirits are aware of their death and capable of crossing over to their own world, but they choose to remain for various reasons <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:00">[00:07:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:12">[00:11:12]</a>. These reasons can range from seeking revenge to attempting to help family members <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:11">[00:07:11]</a>.
*   **Spirits Fearing Judgment** A significant reason spirits do not cross over is their fear of "Judgment Day," a concept taught in various religions that suggests consequences for sins committed in life <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:50:50">[00:50:50]</a>. This fear can lead them to remain trapped in places like prisons <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:05">[00:22:05]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:36">[00:22:36]</a>.
*   **Good Spirits** Not all spirits are malevolent. Many are considered "good spirits" that actively help the living without seeking attention <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:39">[00:00:39]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:56:44">[00:56:44]</a>. Even places of worship like temples and churches, often perceived as un-haunted, can be "haunted" by these beneficial entities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:31">[00:00:31]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:56:41">[00:56:41]</a>.

## Life as a Spirit

The experience of being a spirit, especially one that is stuck, can be likened to "hell" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:31">[00:07:31]</a>. Imagine being constantly around a loved one who cannot see, hear, or acknowledge you; this creates a profound sense of hurt and isolation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:11">[00:08:11]</a>. Spirits may also be confined to specific locations, experiencing a disorientation if they try to move beyond their perceived boundaries <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:34">[00:08:34]</a>.

Furthermore, some spirits exist without a concept of time, still believing they are in a past century despite current reality <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:39">[00:10:39]</a>.

## Possessions and Demonic Entities

In [[experiences_and_narratives_involving_ghosts_and_hauntings | experiences and narratives involving ghosts and hauntings]], instances reported as demonic possessions are often found to be either psychological issues or manifestations of powerful human spirits <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:31:51">[00:31:51]</a>. Demons, categorized as "inhuman spirits," are entities that have never walked the Earth in a physical form <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:30:43">[00:30:43]</a>. They are considered more powerful and capable of causing greater emotional and physical damage than ordinary spirits <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:32:09">[00:32:09]</a>.

Serial killers, for example, are sometimes believed to be influenced by demonic entities or to have become "demons" themselves through their heinous acts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:29:20">[00:29:20]</a>. However, it's crucial to distinguish between genuine [[interaction with spirits and entities | spiritual or demonic influence]] and psychological conditions such as schizophrenia <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:34:28">[00:34:28]</a> or excited delirium, where a person can exhibit extraordinary strength due to extreme focus or adrenaline <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:39:35">[00:39:35]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:40:20">[00:40:20]</a>.

The concept of demons originated in religious texts across various faiths to create a balance between light and darkness, encouraging faith in God through fear of darker entities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:33:01">[00:33:01]</a>. This falls under the broader topic of the [[role_of_spirituality_and_witchcraft_in_paranormal_phenomena | role of spirituality and witchcraft in paranormal phenomena]].

The scientific community often discredits paranormal studies due to historical instances of fake practitioners <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:25">[00:14:25]</a>.
