## Your Perception of Flaws

Many personal flaws, such as acne or body fat, are often perceived as much larger than they actually are <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:12">[00:01:12]</a>. The truth is that nobody is perfect <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:22">[00:01:22]</a>, and individuals tend to notice their own imperfections more than anyone else does <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:28">[00:01:28]</a>. In a world characterized by fakeness and insecurity, it is vital to [[promoting_selfconfidence_and_selfacceptance | learn to love yourself]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:33">[00:01:33]</a>.

## Things You Can and Cannot Change

When it comes to personal traits, there's a distinction between what can be changed and what cannot <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:40">[00:01:40]</a>.

### Accepting Unchangeable Traits

For aspects of yourself that cannot be altered, such as your hair, skin color, or nose, the key is to [[promoting_selfconfidence_and_selfacceptance | learn to love those things]] as they are fundamental to who you are <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:51">[00:01:51]</a>.

### Working on Changeable Traits

Conversely, for external factors that can be changed, such as clothing choices or other aspects of external appearance, it's beneficial to invest effort in learning how to improve them with correct knowledge <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:05">[00:02:05]</a>. This process is a natural part of transitioning from childhood to adulthood <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:15">[00:02:15]</a>.

## The Path to [[building_selfconfidence_and_happiness | Confidence and Happiness]]

Developing external confidence can lead to internal confidence, which in turn fosters internal happiness <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:20">[00:02:20]</a>.

Life is a continuous process of [[personal_growth_and_overcoming_failure | self-improvement]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:55">[00:00:55]</a>. Rather than dwelling on negative situations or perceptions, one can choose to grow in every aspect of life to become the best version of themselves <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:57">[00:00:57]</a>.
