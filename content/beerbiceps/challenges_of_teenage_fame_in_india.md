Ajay Nagar maintains a clear distinction between his personal self and his public persona, CarryMina <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>. While the CarryMina character is kept very open and transparent <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:21">[00:01:21]</a>, Ajay Nagar himself is a very private person <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:17">[00:01:17]</a>. This desire for privacy stems from his childhood, as he was always an introvert who preferred staying home rather than going out <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:50">[00:04:50]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:57">[00:04:57]</a>.

## Impact on Personal Life and Development

Being a famous teenager takes a significant toll on one's life <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:23">[00:00:23]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:37">[00:07:37]</a>. Nagar states that "everything is difficult" when you are a famous teenager <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:57">[00:06:57]</a>.

### Missed Experiences
One of the primary challenges is the disruption of a normal developmental cycle. Nagar explains that he missed out on basic life experiences due to lack of time:
*   He doesn't know how to drive because he never had the time to learn <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:29">[00:00:29]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:40">[00:07:40]</a>.
*   He never experienced college life <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:48">[00:07:48]</a>.
*   His "normal cycle" of walking, going to school, college, and then a job was broken <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:52">[00:07:52]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:00">[00:08:00]</a>.

He sometimes feels he missed out on these experiences <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:04">[00:08:04]</a> and expresses a desire to live in a hostel or flat-share to experience college life and create content based on it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:14">[00:08:14]</a>.
