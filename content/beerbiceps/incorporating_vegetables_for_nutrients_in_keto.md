Maintaining a balanced [[basics_of_the_ketogenic_diet | ketogenic diet]] requires careful consideration of all macronutrients, especially carbohydrates. While limiting carbs is central to [[basics_of_the_ketogenic_diet | keto]], the source of those limited carbohydrates is crucial for overall health and preventing deficiencies.

## Importance of Green Leafy Vegetables <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:09">[00:04:09]</a>

The [[basics_of_the_ketogenic_diet | keto diet]] mandates consuming only 20 grams of carbohydrates per day <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:04">[00:04:04]</a>. It is vital to choose wisely how these 20 grams are utilized <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:06">[00:04:06]</a>. Instead of filling your carb allowance with dairy products like cheese, it is ideal to prioritize green leafy vegetables <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:09">[00:04:09]</a>.

Examples of suitable green leafy vegetables include lettuce, cabbage, and spinach, which can be stir-fried with ingredients like garlic and curry leaves <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:17">[00:04:17]</a>.

### Carb Counting and Deficiencies

It's essential to meticulously count carbohydrates from all sources, including vegetables and even garlic pods, to ensure strict adherence to the 20-gram daily limit on the [[basics_of_the_ketogenic_diet | ketogenic diet]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:24">[00:04:24]</a>.

Neglecting to include sufficient vegetables in your [[meal_planning_for_ketogenic_diet | diet plan]] can lead to:
*   Nutritional deficiencies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:34">[00:04:34]</a>
*   Severe constipation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:37">[00:04:37]</a>

Therefore, incorporating this component of the [[basics_of_the_ketogenic_diet | ketogenic diet]] is extremely important <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:39">[00:04:39]</a>. While the taste might not always be preferred, it is a health necessity and should not be skipped <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:41">[00:04:41]</a>.
