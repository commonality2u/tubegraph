The Ranveer Show (TRS), described as India's most curious podcast, has experienced significant growth and success since its inception.

## Journey and Milestones

Over eight years of content creation, [[ranveer_show_history_and_statistics | TRS]] has produced more than 3,000 videos and 450 podcasts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:00">[00:00:00]</a>. The show's expansion is attributed to the enjoyment of the journey and the continuous learning involved in each episode <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:14">[00:00:14]</a>. A primary joy for the creators is sharing new knowledge with the audience <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>.

## Key Achievements

*   **First Brand Partnership** The efforts of the community and the podcast's inherent curiosity led to TRS securing the first-ever brand partnership for a self-produced Indian podcast <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:32">[00:00:32]</a>.
*   **AIO Partnership** As a result of this partnership, the show is now presented as "AIO presents TRS" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:34">[00:00:34]</a>.
*   **Consistency and Evolution** While now "more fashionable than ever" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:53">[00:00:53]</a>, [[ranveer_show_history_and_statistics | TRS]] maintains its core identity with the "same curiosity, same magnanimous guest, same free flowing conversation" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>.
*   **New Episodes** The show is set to release 16 new episodes featuring "16 of the most successful Minds" who have "achieved it in style" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>.
