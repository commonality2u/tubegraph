## Foundational Rule: Be Fit

Before delving into shirt specifics, a key principle for looking great in a t-shirt is to be physically fit <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:56">[00:03:56]</a>. This doesn't mean being a bodybuilder, but rather having above-average arms, chest, and back <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:59">[00:03:59]</a>.

## T-Shirt Length Guidelines

The length of your t-shirt is crucial for a flattering look:
*   **Avoid excessive length:** The t-shirt should not be so long that it covers your entire crotch area <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:17">[00:04:17]</a>.
*   **Avoid being too short:** When you lift your arms, the t-shirt should not expose any skin <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:21">[00:04:21]</a>.
*   **Ideal length:** The t-shirt should end in the middle of your groin region <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:30">[00:04:30]</a>.

## Sleeve Length and Fit

The sleeves contribute significantly to the overall aesthetic:
*   **Preference for short and tight sleeves:** Aim for sleeves that are both short and snug <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:35">[00:04:35]</a>.
*   **Tailoring:** If a t-shirt has slightly longer sleeves, consider getting it tailored to make them tighter and shorter <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:39">[00:04:39]</a>.
*   **Avoid extreme shortness:** Do not go so short that the sleeves appear to be seeking attention <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:44">[00:04:44]</a>.
*   **Optimal sleeve length:** The sleeve should just cover your entire shoulder muscle, allowing a good portion of your tricep and bicep to be exposed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:49">[00:04:49]</a>.

## Torso Fit

The fit around the torso dictates how well the t-shirt complements your physique:
*   **Upper Torso:** The t-shirt should be tight in the upper torso region, specifically everything above the line of your nipples <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:58">[00:04:58]</a>.
*   **Mid-Torso:** Everything below the line of your nipples should be slightly looser <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:03">[00:05:03]</a>.
*   **Important Note:** Never wear t-shirts so tight that your nipples are visible through the fabric <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:11">[00:05:11]</a>.
