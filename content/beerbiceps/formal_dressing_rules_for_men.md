## Types of Formal Dressing

There are two main types of formal dressing:

*   **[[traditional_formal_vs_modern_formal_dressing | Traditional Formal Dressing]]**: This type has very strict rules and is common in old-school offices where fashion liberties are not given <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:01">[00:01:01]</a>.
*   **[[traditional_formal_vs_modern_formal_dressing | Modern Formal]] / Business Casual**: This allows for more flexibility and options in styling <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:12">[00:01:12]</a>. Modern formal rules typically apply to new-age offices or formal parties <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:43">[00:02:43]</a>.

## Top 5 Rules for Formal Dressing

### 1. Shirts

*   **For [[traditional_formal_vs_modern_formal_dressing | Traditional Formal]] Offices**:
    *   **Colors**: Stick to white, shades of pink, or shades of blue <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:36">[00:01:36]</a>. Always opt for lighter shades, as offices are typically daytime situations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:42">[00:01:42]</a>. Darker colors are generally for nighttime events <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:49">[00:01:49]</a>.
    *   **Style**: Avoid half-sleeve shirts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:56">[00:01:56]</a>.
    *   **[[Pattern shirt rules | Patterns]]**: Do not wear anything with too loud a pattern <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:58">[00:01:58]</a>. Avoid colors that are super saturated or have very high intensity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:02">[00:02:02]</a>. [[traditional_formal_vs_modern_formal_dressing | Old-school formal dressing]] emphasizes subtle and sober aesthetics <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:07">[00:02:07]</a>.
*   **For [[traditional_formal_vs_modern_formal_dressing | Modern Formal]] / Business Casual**:
    *   **[[Pattern shirt rules | Patterns]]**: Subtle patterns are acceptable, but avoid anything too eye-catching <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:15">[00:02:15]</a>.
    *   **[[Basic shirt colors for mens wardrobe | Colors]]**: Prioritize lighter shades <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:26">[00:02:26]</a>. However, some darker shades like black, navy blue, brown, or shades of gray can be worn occasionally <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:29">[00:02:29]</a>.

### 2. Pants

*   **Colors**: Stick to black, charcoal gray, navy blue, and some shades of brown, such as super light tan (which can be paired with white or blue shirts) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:07">[00:03:07]</a>.
*   **Types**:
    *   **Traditional Formal Pants (Suit Pants/Trousers)**: These are highly formal and suitable for any office environment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:21">[00:03:21]</a>.
    *   **Chinos**: A popular [[traditional_formal_vs_modern_formal_dressing | modern formal]] option, chinos are a blend between jeans and suit pants. They are slightly casual cotton pants that are tight around the thigh <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:50">[00:03:50]</a>. While available in many colors, adhere to the recommended formal pant colors <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:02">[00:04:02]</a>.
*   **Tailoring**: Always get your pants tailored to be tapered towards the base for a sleeker, leaner, and sharper look <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:33">[00:03:33]</a>.
*   **[[Clothing and color combinations for men | Color Combination Rule]]**: The shade of your pants must be darker than that of your shirt <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:10">[00:04:10]</a>. Most attention should be drawn to your upper body, not your legs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:19">[00:04:19]</a>.

### 3. Belts

*   **Material**: Avoid cotton belts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:27">[00:04:27]</a>. Opt for a leather or faux leather belt that gives a leathery feel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:42">[00:04:42]</a>.
*   **[[Choosing formal accessories like belts shoes and watches | Color Matching]]**: Always match the color of your belt to your shoes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:30">[00:04:30]</a>. They don't have to be exact, but they should be similar (e.g., light brown shoes with a dark brown belt) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:33">[00:04:33]</a>.
*   **Versatile Colors**: Prioritize black and dark brown belts for versatility and style <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:51">[00:04:51]</a>.

### 4. Footwear

*   **[[Clothing and color combinations for men | Sock Color]]**: Match the color of your socks to that of your pants <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:03">[00:05:03]</a>.
*   **[[Clothing and color combinations for men | Shoe Color for Traditional Formal]]**: The color of your shoes should either be the same shade as your pants or one shade darker <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:09">[00:05:09]</a>.
*   **[[Choosing formal accessories like belts shoes and watches | Modern Formal Shoe Options]]**:
    *   **Brogues**: Formal shoes with slight patterns at the top <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:20">[00:05:20]</a>.
    *   **Monk Strap Shoes**: A slightly feminine-looking shoe with buckles, which can be very elegant when paired correctly <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:27">[00:05:27]</a>.
    *   **Dark-Colored Loafers**: Smart and suitable for both parties and office wear <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:38">[00:05:38]</a>.
*   **Quality**: Invest in high-quality formal footwear that will last <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:50">[00:05:50]</a>. Ensure they are well-polished at all times <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:58">[00:05:58]</a>.

### 5. Wristwatches

*   **Importance**: A wristwatch is an essential accessory for formal dressing and completes any formal outfit <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:05">[00:06:05]</a>.
*   **[[Choosing formal accessories like belts shoes and watches | Traditional Formal]]**: A good quality metallic watch is preferred, serving as a power symbol in meetings or super formal events <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:15">[00:06:15]</a>.
*   **[[Choosing formal accessories like belts shoes and watches | Modern Formal]]**: Leather-looking straps and thinner dials are acceptable <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:28">[00:06:28]</a>.
*   **What to Avoid**: Do not wear digital watches, very thick and big eye-catching dials, or watches with patterned straps <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:41">[00:06:41]</a>.

These rules cover most aspects of [[formal_clothing_tips_for_indian_men | formal dressing]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:52">[00:06:52]</a>. Aspects such as suits/blazers and ties are typically covered in separate discussions, as ties are best pulled off with a smart jacket or blazer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:01">[00:07:01]</a>.
