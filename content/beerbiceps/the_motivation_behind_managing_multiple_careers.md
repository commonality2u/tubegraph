Gorov Taneja is known for managing multiple demanding careers simultaneously, including being a YouTuber with two channels (Flying Beast and Fit Muscle TV), and a pilot <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:34">[00:00:34]</a>. He is also an alumnus of IIT <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:45">[00:00:45]</a>. ## Core Motivations

Gorov Taneja's primary motivation for pursuing and excelling in multiple careers is his profound passion for each endeavor <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>. He likens his work to a child's love for playing cricket, stating, "I love doing what I do so much that everything revolves around my work" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:05">[00:01:05]</a>. This intense passion means he never feels like he is "working" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:39">[00:02:39]</a>, even to the point where he sometimes pushes himself beyond physical limits <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:22">[00:01:22]</a>. The interviewer also observes that this [[the_role_of_personal_ambition_in_achieving_success | passion]] and love are visible in his videos <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:43">[00:06:43]</a>.

## Evolution of Career Paths

### From IIT to Pilot

Gorov initially aspired to be a pilot after 12th grade, but financial constraints prevented him from pursuing it at the time <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:43">[00:01:43]</a>. He then entered the competitive race for engineering and medical careers, excelling because of his love for physics and math <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:51">[00:01:51]</a>. His commitment to hard work drove him to aim for the best institute in the country, leading him to IIT <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:10">[00:02:10]</a>.

Later, his "old passion" for flying revived, prompting him to take the step to become a pilot despite his father's wish for him to pursue a master's degree in the US <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:23">[00:02:23]</a>. He convinced his father to trust his feeling, a decision that has resulted in him being very happy in his profession, not feeling like he's working when he goes to his job <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:31">[00:02:31]</a>.

### From Pilot to YouTuber

Gorov's journey into content creation stemmed from his involvement in fitness and competing <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:44">[00:02:44]</a>. He realized that one-on-one advice in a gym could only reach a limited number of people <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:51">[00:02:51]</a>. Seeing a gap in the market for professional fitness advice from a bodybuilder, he decided to start sharing his knowledge <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:02">[00:03:02]</a>. He initially started on Facebook, then transitioned to YouTube after realizing it was a more suitable platform for videos <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:17">[00:03:17]</a>. This led to the creation of his fitness channel, Fit Muscle TV <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:10">[00:03:10]</a>.

The idea for his vlogging channel, Flying Beast, emerged during a personal transformation journey. He wanted to document his progress but realized that solely filming a transformation would be repetitive <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:51">[00:03:51]</a>. To make the content more engaging and reach a broader audience beyond just fitness enthusiasts, he decided to incorporate vlogging <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:14">[00:04:14]</a>. This was a calculated risk, as his wife initially expressed concern about him starting another channel given his busy schedule <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:31">[00:04:31]</a>. However, he trusted his instinct that it was a "better move" and the channel quickly proved successful, allowing him to expand into a different domain <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:35">[00:04:35]</a>.

## Key Advice for Career Choices

Gorov's advice to young people, particularly those in engineering who are dissatisfied with their studies, centers on trust in one's inner conviction and the importance of [[influence_of_discipline_and_preparation_in_career_success | taking action]]:

*   **Trust Your Idea:** If you are very sure about your idea and feel you can do it, take the risk and go for it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:40">[00:05:40]</a>. Don't be influenced by others <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:52">[00:05:52]</a>.
*   **Take Action:** Planning is important, but without putting your foot down and taking action, efforts will not be fruitful <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:16">[00:06:16]</a>.
*   **Discover Hidden Talents:** Everyone has a different talent hidden within them. If your heart is truly in something, you will become good at it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:25">[00:06:25]</a>.
*   **Prioritize Wellbeing:** [[balancing_fitness_and_personal_life | Take time out]] of your busy schedule to spend on your fitness <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:51">[00:06:51]</a>. It's often difficult to start, but once you do, like stepping into a gym, you get a rush <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:00">[00:07:00]</a>.
*   **Connect with Family:** In an increasingly online world, it's crucial to give time to your family and connect with them offline <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:27">[00:07:27]</a>.
