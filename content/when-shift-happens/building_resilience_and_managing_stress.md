*   **Optimistic and Open Mindset** Maintaining an optimistic and open mindset is key to the entire entrepreneurial journey <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:10">[00:16:10]</a>.

*   **Never Give Up** A core principle is to never give up, persevering through setbacks and relentlessly pursuing objectives in a healthy manner <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:45:42">[00:45:42]</a>.

*   **Gratitude** Take a moment in the morning to express gratitude for your place in the world <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:26:04">[00:26:04]</a>.

### Red Flags for Mental Health
Entrepreneurs should pay attention to several alarm bells indicating potential mental health struggles:
*   Consistent tiredness <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:33:27">[00:33:27]</a>.
*   Snapping at people <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:33:33">[00:33:33]</a>.
*   A messy personal physical environment, which can reflect a chaotic state of mind <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:33:38">[00:33:38]</a>.
*   Prioritizing business over personal well-being, even when feeling "weird or really bad," is a significant red flag <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:30:35">[00:30:35]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:30:48">[00:30:48]</a>.
