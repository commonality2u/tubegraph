From: [[when-shift-happens]] <br/> 

Olly Yonchev, co-founder and CEO of Flight Story, discusses the intricate relationship between high ambition, personal identity, and [[mental_health_and_personal_development | mental health]]. He emphasizes the importance of balancing professional drive with personal well-being to maintain sanity and happiness <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:36">[00:01:36]</a>.

## Ambition and Identity
Olly acknowledges that his career and professional pursuits are a significant part of his identity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:03">[00:01:03]</a>. He finds it challenging to detach what he does from who he is, a common experience for entrepreneurs or independent individuals <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:41">[00:07:41]</a>. This deep connection means that his mind "doesn't stop," and he feels uncomfortable with too much downtime <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:04">[00:07:04]</a>. He views this relentless drive as a "bit of a curse" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:02">[00:07:02]</a>.

He eventually came to peace with this aspect of his personality, reframing it not as a problem but as "the price of entry" for his ambitious endeavors <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:29">[00:08:29]</a>.

## Maintaining Balance for Mental Health
Despite his intense ambition, Olly states that he has not experienced depression, though he has had low moments or weeks with less motivation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:36">[00:10:36]</a>. His primary coping mechanism for [[mental_health_challenges_in_highstakes_trading | managing mental health and burnout]] is to ensure [[balancing_professional_success_with_personal_life | balance]] and success in other areas of his life <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:15">[00:09:15]</a>.

Key elements that contribute to his [[mental_health_and_personal_development | mental health]] and happiness include:
*   **Strong Relationships** Having a great family and good relationships <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>.
*   **Physical Activity** Maintaining an active lifestyle with various sports like kickboxing and football has been a foundational anchor <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:25">[00:11:25]</a>.
*   **Goal Setting** Consistently setting goals, even if not monumental, provides a sense of fulfillment from working towards them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:41">[00:11:41]</a>.

He emphasizes that not being anchored to just one thing (like work) is a helpful coping mechanism, especially when professional challenges arise <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:47">[00:09:47]</a>. Investing energy into passions outside of work helps maintain happiness even through difficult times <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:17">[00:10:17]</a>.

## The Role of Personal Choice
Olly believes that many problems people face, including [[personal_hardships_and_mental_health | health concerns]], could be addressed by making different choices <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:34:35">[00:34:35]</a>. While acknowledging that life circumstances can be challenging, he suggests that people often know the "playbook for fulfillment" (e.g., healthy eating, exercise, good relationships) but find it hard to implement these actions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:35:10">[00:35:10]</a>.

> "I believe that 90 percent could solve a lot of their problems with different choices, but I'm not saying it's easy" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:35:03">[00:35:03]</a>.

## Advice for Aspiring Entrepreneurs
For young individuals with entrepreneurial ambitions, Olly's primary advice is to "start it" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:36:14">[00:36:14]</a>. He encourages them to take the leap early, as regret is a significant burden later in life <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:33:33">[00:33:33]</a>. He acknowledges his own regret for not pursuing entrepreneurship earlier in his career <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:30:37">[00:30:37]</a>.

Key takeaways for success in any endeavor, including personal branding and business:
*   **Discipline and Consistency** These are critical ingredients that separate those who succeed from those who don't <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:01">[00:16:01]</a>. Most people start but don't continue <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:40">[00:16:40]</a>.
*   **Just Do It** Don't hesitate, overthink, or pursue perfection. Overcome self-consciousness and simply take action <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:15">[00:16:15]</a>.
*   **Lean into Natural Strengths** For personal branding, leverage what comes naturally, whether it's writing, video, or conversation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:38">[00:15:38]</a>.
*   **Embrace Discomfort** Moving to a new environment and making oneself uncomfortable can be hugely eye-opening and valuable, especially for young people who have the asset of time <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:32:53">[00:32:53]</a>.
