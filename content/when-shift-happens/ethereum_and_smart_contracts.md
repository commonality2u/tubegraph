## The "Aha Moment" of Ethereum

For George Nezes, a programmer, Bitcoin was interesting but not revolutionary due to its focus solely on money <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:13:17">[01:13:17]</a>. However, [[the_evolving_narrative_and_importance_of_Ethereum | Ethereum]]'s introduction of a programming language, Solidity, and the ability to define custom rules for updating state via smart contracts, proved to be a "pretty cool" and exciting development <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:13:26">[01:13:26]</a>. This innovation allowed for the programmability of the blockchain, enabling much more than simple monetary transactions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:13:38">[01:13:38]</a>.

Before [[the_evolving_narrative_and_importance_of_Ethereum | Ethereum]], it was unclear if transaction processing could even be achieved in an open, permissionless setting <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:33:00">[01:33:00]</a>. Once [[the_evolving_narrative_and_importance_of_Ethereum | Ethereum]] established that smart contracts could be built to define custom transactions, the focus shifted to optimizing these systems for better throughput, lower latency, and overall performance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:32:47">[01:32:47]</a>.

## Addressing Limitations and Scaling

Despite its revolutionary introduction of programmability, [[Ethereums_performance_challenges_and_limitations | Ethereum]]'s early iterations faced practical limitations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:51:52">[01:51:52]</a>. Users experienced delays of up to 10 minutes for transactions to settle on Bitcoin, and observed high costs on [[the_evolving_narrative_and_importance_of_Ethereum | Ethereum]] itself <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:52:12">[01:52:12]</a>. For instance, breeding a CryptoKitty, a popular early use case, could cost nearly $100 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:52:32">[01:52:32]</a>. These firsthand experiences highlighted the need for cheaper and faster systems <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:53:09">[01:53:09]</a>.

This recognition of [[Ethereums_performance_challenges_and_limitations | Ethereum]]'s limitations spurred efforts to research and develop solutions for scaling these systems, leading to the creation of companies like Chainspace, which focused on increasing transaction throughput and lowering latency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:34:16">[01:34:16]</a>.

## CryptoKitties: Expanding Blockchain Imagination

CryptoKitties, a digital collectible game on [[the_evolving_narrative_and_importance_of_Ethereum | Ethereum]], is considered a revolutionary idea <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:53:35">[01:53:35]</a>. It demonstrated that blockchain transaction systems could be used for more than just money <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:53:49">[01:53:49]</a>, introducing the concept of virtual assets like digital pets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:53:54">[01:53:54]</a>. The social aspect of breeding digital cats and the ability to interact with others through these virtual items expanded the imagination of what these systems could achieve, beyond serious financial applications to include fun and social experiences <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:54:12">[01:54:12]</a>.

## Ethereum's Role in a Broader Vision

[[the_evolving_narrative_and_importance_of_Ethereum | Ethereum]], along with Bitcoin, provided the foundational understanding that transaction systems could be built in open, permissionless settings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:31:31">[01:31:31]</a>. It showcased that a shared state, like a ledger of ownership, could be consistently updated and agreed upon by all participants <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:31:37">[01:31:37]</a>. This capability is seen as a key aspect of coordination, which blockchains excel at <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:47:32">[01:47:32]</a>.

The development of [[the_evolution_and_future_of_ethereum_layer_2_solutions | Ethereum layer 2 solutions]] like Mentle, which enable borrowing and lending Bitcoin in DeFi and offer liquid ETH staking, further illustrates the platform's expanding infrastructure and utility <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:32">[02:32]</a>. The continued [[ethereum_development_and_challenges | development and challenges]] of [[the_evolving_narrative_and_importance_of_Ethereum | Ethereum]] and its ecosystem highlight its [[ethereums_role_and_infrastructure_in_financial_markets | growing role]] as a coordination layer and a foundation for decentralized applications, moving beyond initial limitations to enable a wider range of activities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:53:09">[01:53:09]</a>.
