### Identifying Crypto Opportunities
The "real game" in [[investing_in_crypto_and_identifying_market_opportunities | crypto]] involves:
*   **ICOs and IDOs (Initial Coin Offerings and Initial DEX Offerings):** Bryant identifies these as one of the best ways to make money in crypto <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:27:17">[00:27:17]</a>. Gaining allocation in these offerings can be life-changing, potentially yielding 100x or 200x returns <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:27:20">[00:27:20]</a>.
*   **Being Early to Trends:** Identifying emerging trends like AI or meme coins and investing early is crucial <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:28:26">[00:28:26]</a>. Even a solid project will fail if the investor is late <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:28:35">[00:28:35]</a>.
*   **Project Evaluation:** Look for projects with:
    *   A solid product <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:28:42">[00:28:42]</a>.
    *   A known team with a track record <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:28:44">[00:28:44]</a>.
    *   Paid marketing, especially in a bull market, where influencers can drive price increases <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:28:49">[00:28:49]</a>. However, paid marketing in a bear market is ineffective as influencers dump tokens due to low liquidity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:28:55">[00:28:55]</a>.

## Social Media as a Platform for Opportunity

Social media offers unique advantages for wealth creation:
*   **Zero Startup Costs:** It's one of the only businesses that can be started with no initial investment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:01">[00:00:01]</a>.
*   **No Downside:** If social media efforts fail, you remain in the same position as before; if they succeed, you can achieve financial freedom <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:32:10">[00:32:10]</a>.
*   **Monetization Potential:** Money has "never been easier to make" in 2023 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:59">[00:25:59]</a>. Examples include using AI to chop long-form content into viral clips for platforms like TikTok, where the Creator Beta program pays for views <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:26:04">[00:26:04]</a>.

### Achieving Virality and Monetization
To succeed in social media and crypto:
*   **Develop a Social Media Presence:** This enables access to ICO/IDO allocations by providing value to the table and bringing clients <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:27:40">[00:27:40]</a>.
*   **Strong Hooks:** The first 3 seconds of a video are crucial, requiring a strong hook to capture attention <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:34:15">[00:34:15]</a>.
*   **Emotional Engagement:** Play with people's emotions by telling them what they want to hear ("greatest opportunity") and what they don't ("you're fat as shit") <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:34:35">[00:34:35]</a>.
*   **Mass Produce Content:** Have multiple accounts and editors, posting across different regions with local SIM cards <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:35:13">[00:35:13]</a>.
*   **Relatability:** Being relatable helps people connect and believe they can achieve similar success <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:35:46">[00:35:46]</a>.
*   **Add Value:** The most important thing is to provide value to someone's life, which builds a "cult following" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:36:34">[00:36:34]</a>. Helping someone lose weight or make money will make them remember you <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:36:50">[00:36:50]</a>.
*   **Niche Down:** Focus on a specific niche, such as finance (stocks, crypto, Forex), as monetization is stronger in these areas <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:37:15">[00:37:15]</a>. A smart, attractive woman dropping knowledge in a male-dominated industry like crypto can experience skyrocketing views <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:37:24">[00:37:24]</a>.
*   **Approach Brands:** Create a loyal following, then leverage that to approach brands with engagement numbers to secure deals <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:37:57">[00:37:57]</a>. Brands depend on advertising and marketing to be relevant <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:38:14">[00:38:14]</a>.

## Ethical Considerations and Avoiding Scams
Bryant differentiates between legitimate course creators and outright scammers:
*   **Ethical Course Creation:** Courses are acceptable if they provide genuine value and are not overly promising <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:42:01">[00:42:01]</a>. The speaker highlights that if a course costs $500 but saves you tens of thousands by sharing learned scams, it's worthwhile <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:41">[00:08:41]</a>.
*   **Identifying Scams:** Be wary of young, inexperienced traders selling courses from their basement, as this is "natural selection" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:44">[00:00:44]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:50">[00:08:50]</a>.
