[[nexus_protocol_v1_vaults_launch | Nexus Protocol]] implements a governance model that allows its community to participate in decision-making processes regarding the platform's future development and operations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="11:19:00">[11:19:00]</a>.

## PSI Governance Token

The governance token of [[nexus_protocol_v1_vaults_launch | Nexus Protocol]] is called PSI <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:07:07">[03:07:07]</a>. Holders of PSI tokens have the ability to influence the platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="11:25:00">[11:25:00]</a>. While not yet live at the time of recording, staking for the governance token is expected to be available soon, with APRs to be published at a later date on the governance page <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="11:00:00">[11:00:00]</a>.

## Community Participation and Voting

Similar to other decentralized applications, the governance page on [[nexus_protocol_v1_vaults_launch | Nexus Protocol]] will feature polls <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="11:13:00">[11:13:00]</a>. As a governance token holder, users will be able to vote and interact with the platform to determine its future direction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="11:25:00">[11:25:00]</a>. This includes decisions on:
*   Changes to existing vaults <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="11:19:00">[11:19:00]</a>.
*   Addition of new [[using_liquidity_pools_and_yield_farms_on_nexus_protocol | liquidity pools]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="11:19:00">[11:19:00]</a>.
