## Luca Netz's Entrepreneurial Journey and Insights

Luca Netz began his entrepreneurial career at a young age, starting his first business at 18 and becoming a millionaire by 19 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:01">[00:00:01]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:03">[00:07:03]</a>. His initial venture involved selling fake gold chains and cubic zirconia diamonds online <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:29">[00:00:29]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:06">[00:07:06]</a>. He chose this niche due to hip-hop being the fastest-growing trend in America, aiming to help kids emulate their favorite rappers affordably <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:13">[00:07:13]</a>.

He quickly recognized the power of marketing and branding, considering himself among the pioneers of influencer marketing <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:01">[00:08:01]</a>. In the early days, he would pay fan pages of rappers, like "KendrickLamar.fanpage," around $100 for a story or post, which would generate thousands in return <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:23">[00:08:23]</a>. This strategy propelled his business from $20,000 to multi-million dollar months when he was 18-19 years old <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:59">[00:08:59]</a>.

Netz emphasizes that "it's all about marketing" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:11">[00:09:11]</a>, stating that even the best product is useless if no one knows about it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:12">[00:09:12]</a>. He later realized the importance of building Enterprise Value and long-term brand integrity over mere cash flow, a lesson learned after an initial period of easy money <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:41">[00:12:41]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:02">[00:16:02]</a>.

This realization led him to develop what he calls "The Playbook," a repeatable formula applied to businesses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:32">[00:18:32]</a>. He successfully applied this playbook to revive the Von Dutch brand, taking it from a defunct status to a $100 million company in 12 months by leveraging his influencer network and focusing on high-quality, high-integrity products <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:06">[00:18:06]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:24">[00:18:24]</a>.

## Entry into the Crypto and NFT Space

Netz's "aha moment" with crypto came from observing the "basic human right" pattern: the ability to transact freely without a middleman <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:22">[00:22:22]</a>. He believed crypto would be the next big wave, investing in Bitcoin and Litecoin as early as 2016-2017 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:21:21">[00:21:21]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:54">[00:22:54]</a>.

When NFTs emerged in 2021, they resonated with his long-standing passion for collecting watches, art, and other collectibles <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:34">[00:25:34]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:40">[00:25:40]</a>. He found NFTs to be a convenient way to fulfill his collecting needs without the logistical challenges of physical items <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:47">[00:25:47]</a>. His first Profile Picture (PFP) NFT was a [[pudgy_penguins_and_the_pengu_meme_coin_potential | Pudgy Penguin]], which he actively traded, turning $10,000 into $500,000 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:26:17">[00:26:17]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:26:40">[00:26:40]</a>.

## The Acquisition and Transformation of Pudgy Penguins

In 2021, an opportunity arose for Netz to acquire the [[pudgy_penguins_and_the_pengu_meme_coin_potential | Pudgy Penguins]] project <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:27:44">[00:27:44]</a>. Having previously been the CMO of Gel Blaster, North America's fastest-growing toy company, he saw [[pudgy_penguins_and_the_pengu_meme_coin_potential | Pudgy Penguins]] as perfectly positioned to become a major IP in the toy and character world, similar to what he observed at Disney and Warner Bros conferences <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:28:17">[00:28:17]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:28:40">[00:28:40]</a>. He noted the project's strong community and affluent holder base, which helped it survive even when it seemed to be a "rug pull" early on <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:28:48">[00:28:48]</a>.

Netz acquired [[pudgy_penguins_and_the_pengu_meme_coin_potential | Pudgy Penguins]] for 750 ETH, equivalent to $2.5 million at the time <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:29:11">[00:29:11]</a>. The original creators were young college students with no business structure, so Netz's initial focus was to establish a proper business foundation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:31:16">[00:31:16]</a>.

## Pudgy Penguins' Web3 Strategy

Netz differentiates a true web3 business from a web2 business selling digital products on the blockchain <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:29:44">[00:29:44]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:30:31">[00:30:31]</a>. For him, a web3 business is "built with the hive mind that is your community" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:29:50">[00:29:50]</a>. His role is to act as the "muscle" and "brain" to the community, taking their ideas and feedback to build the best product <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:30:09">[00:30:09]</a>.

Upon acquisition, Netz immediately engaged the community. He conducted a one-week "Sprint," spending several hours a day for seven days to gather their wants, needs, and vision for the project <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:30:48">[00:30:48]</a>. This collaborative approach shaped his vision, and after months of building trust and executing on promises, the community recognized his commitment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:31:30">[00:31:30]</a>.

*   The number one NFT project globally <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:32:21">[00:32:21]</a>.
*   The face of crypto, blockchain, NFTs, and web3 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:32:24">[00:32:24]</a>.
