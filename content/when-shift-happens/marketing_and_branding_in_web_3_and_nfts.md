Luca Netz, a serial entrepreneur and CEO of Pudgy Penguins, asserts that the Web3, NFT, and broader crypto industries are fundamentally about marketing and branding <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>. ## The Importance of Marketing and Branding in Crypto

Netz identifies marketing and branding as the "Achilles' heel" of the crypto and Web3 industry <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:37">[00:09:37]</a>. He observes that while the industry emphasizes technology and technical minds, which are crucial, there's a significant gap in [[the_importance_of_product_as_marketing_in_cryptocurrency | packaging and branding]] products for the consumer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:06">[00:10:06]</a>. He likens the current state of crypto to IBM and Microsoft in the early computer era, where the technology existed but lacked the consumer-friendly branding of Apple or Macintosh <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:38">[00:10:38]</a>.

He explains that the problem lies in how crypto is sold:
> "The problem with crypto is everyone tries to sell it like the front end, everyone thinks that's the hook that's the drying power but it's not it's what powers the experience it's the back end and I think that is the misconception in crypto today" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:05">[00:11:05]</a>.

## Early Entrepreneurial and Marketing Insights

Netz started his first business at 18 and became a millionaire by 19 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:01">[00:00:01]</a>. This venture involved selling fake gold chains and cubic zirconia diamonds online <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>. He chose this niche because hip-hop was a rapidly growing trend, and he sought to offer an affordable way for kids to emulate their favorite rappers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:15">[00:07:15]</a>.

His early success was built on effective marketing strategies:
*   **Drop Shipping**: Initially utilized Drop Shipping through platforms like Amazon and Alibaba, but quickly shifted to buying inventory and shipping from the US to address pain points like long shipping times <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:35">[00:07:35]</a>.
*   **Pioneering [[sponsorship_and_marketing_strategies_in_the_crypto_industry | Influencer Marketing]]**: Around 2016, Netz claims to have pioneered [[digital_marketing_strategies_in_the_era_of_social_media_and_crypto | influencer marketing]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:01">[00:08:01]</a>. Unable to afford famous rappers, he approached their fan pages on Instagram with large followings. He would pay fan pages, for example, $100 for a story or post, which would yield $1000 in return, a strategy he scaled for months before eventually paying the rappers themselves <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:13">[00:08:13]</a>.
*   **Focus on Marketing**: This experience solidified his belief that marketing is paramount <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:07">[00:09:07]</a>. He states, "You can have the best processes the best product ever if no one knows, what are you doing?" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:12">[00:09:12]</a>.

The "easy period" of this marketing strategy, where he could rapidly scale from $20,000 to multi-million dollar months, lasted about three years <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:01">[00:09:01]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:25">[00:15:25]</a>. When it became harder, he realized the importance of building a strong brand, lifetime value, and customer retention, rather than just acquiring new customers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:51">[00:15:51]</a>.

## Web3 and NFT Marketing Challenges and Opportunities

Netz notes that many NFT projects made significant money without building sustainable businesses, leading to complacency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:11">[00:14:11]</a>. He stepped into the NFT space to demonstrate how to build real value <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:15">[00:15:15]</a>.

### Pudgy Penguins: A Case Study in Web3 Branding

Netz acquired Pudgy Penguins, his first PFP NFT, in 2021 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:26:20">[00:26:20]</a>, investing 750 ETH (approximately $2.5 million at the time) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:29:11">[00:29:11]</a>. He saw Pudgy Penguins as perfectly positioned for toys and IP, noting its strong name, art, character, and resilient community that never "went to zero" despite early challenges <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:28:20">[00:28:20]</a>.

His approach to [[building_a_successful_crypto_community_and_marketing_strategy | building a successful crypto community and marketing strategy]] for Pudgy Penguins illustrates his Web3 philosophy:
*   **Community-Led Vision**: Netz believes a true Web3 business is built with the "hive mind" of its community, leveraging holders for ideas and decisions, rather than a top-down dictatorship <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:29:47">[00:29:47]</a>. He sees himself as the "muscle that is the brain to the community," responsible for extracting feedback and building the best product based on collective wants and needs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:30:09">[00:30:09]</a>.
*   **Building Trust**: Upon acquisition, he held a week-long sprint to gather community input and vision, aligning his plans with their desires. He spent months establishing business structure and building trust with the community <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:31:02">[00:31:02]</a>.
*   **Viral Content**: Pudgy Penguins focuses on creating highly shareable content that generates 50-60 million views daily across Instagram, Giphy, TikTok, and Facebook <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:32:51">[00:32:51]</a>.

Netz's end destination for Pudgy Penguins is to become the number one NFT project globally, the "face of crypto and blockchain and NFTs and Web3," and one of the world's most beloved characters and new IP brands, including significant physical content <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:32:21">[00:32:21]</a>.
