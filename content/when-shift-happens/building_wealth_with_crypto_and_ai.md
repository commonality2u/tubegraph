Building wealth in the evolving digital landscape involves understanding the intersection of cryptocurrency and artificial intelligence (AI), along with new forms of value like attention and influence. ## The Role of Attention as Currency
In the current digital economy, attention has become a fundamental currency with significant value, especially within the crypto space <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:40">[00:00:40]</a>. This is evident in platforms like X (formerly Twitter) which monetizes engagement, and in the rise of influencers and Key Opinion Leaders (KOLs) who are compensated for their reach <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:02:11">[01:02:11]</a>. People are heavily influenced by content and personalities, driving shopping decisions and lifestyle choices based on those they relate to <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:03:02">[01:03:02]</a>.

### The Attention Economy
The attention economy signifies that a project with zero attention, even if it has strong fundamentals, will likely not see investment or price appreciation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:04:49">[01:04:49]</a>. This means that attention directly drives liquidity and holds significant value <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:04:10">[01:04:10]</a>. As everything becomes financialized, limited capital gravitates towards what captures the most attention <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:04:26">[01:04:26]</a>.

## The Intersection of Crypto and AI

### AI in Financial Trading
Traditional financial firms like Citadel have been leveraging AI capabilities for a long time to gain an edge in trading, often years before these technologies become public <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:35:57">[00:35:57]</a>. Quantitative trading strategies, in particular, have heavily relied on AI long before the prevalence of large language models <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:38:14">[00:38:14]</a>. This has fundamentally changed the game, as machines can digest news and react to market changes faster than humans <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:39:17">[00:39:17]</a>.

### Token-Powered Businesses and AI
Kito AI, a "distribution center of crypto for information, attention, and capital powered by AI" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:24">[00:00:24]</a>, has shifted from a profitable equity business to a token-powered model to build a retail product and solve industry problems <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:05:00">[01:05:00]</a>. This transition is aimed at creating a fairer, more transparent, and efficient distribution system for information and capital <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:46:03">[00:46:03]</a>.

A key challenge in crypto is the "KOL (Key Opinion Leader) game," where projects overpay for often low-quality attention from influencers with fake followers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:31:39">[01:31:39]</a>. Kito AI aims to address this with its "yaps" system, which tokenizes attention and influence <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:47:51">[01:47:51]</a>. Yaps points are calculated by an algorithm that discourages farming activities and incentivizes real, original, and creative content <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:48:42">[01:48:42]</a>. This system allows for the fair distribution of rewards, promoting genuine community engagement and fostering evangelists for projects <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:42:50">[01:42:50]</a>.

The concept of "proof of attention" is being made verifiable on-chain, allowing builders to access Yaps data and integrate it into their systems without permission <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:07:05">[02:07:05]</a>. This open, composable data can be used for incentivized testnets, discounts, or for identifying real, influential people in the space <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:09:04">[02:09:04]</a>.
