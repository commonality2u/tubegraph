## [[introduction_to_astroport_and_its_features | Astroport]]'s Differentiating Features

[[introduction_to_astroport_and_its_features | Astroport]] is designed to offer capabilities that distinguish it from current AMMs, including [[Comparison of Ethereum with other blockchain platforms | Uniswap]], by incorporating:
*   **Multiple Pool Types**: Support for both constant product pool types and stable swap pools within a single protocol, leading to better pricing and trade efficiency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:47">[00:00:47]</a>.
*   **Advanced Analytics**: Enhanced charting and analytics for liquidity provider (LP) positions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:59">[00:00:59]</a>.
*   **Dual Token Rewards**: Liquidity providers and Astro stakers can share trading fees on the platform and potentially receive dual token rewards <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:01">[00:01:01]</a>.
*   **Tiered Governance**: The governance token can be kept liquid or locked to amplify voting rights <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:14">[00:01:14]</a>.

These features are in addition to standard DEX functionalities like slippage controls, multi-hop routing, price impact previews, and exchange rate/fee calculations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:27">[00:01:27]</a>.
