## Key Focus Areas

Ava Labs is concentrating on three primary areas for the growth of Avalanche:

### Enterprises
A significant focus is placed on attracting enterprises to the Avalanche ecosystem <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:41:30">[00:41:30]</a>. This strategy aims to onboard larger organizations and integrate [[blockchain_and_crypto_innovation | blockchain solutions]] into their operations.

### Ava Cloud
Ava Cloud is a fully managed blockchain service designed to simplify the deployment of [[blockchain_and_crypto_innovation | blockchain solutions]] for enterprises <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:41:41">[00:41:41]</a>.
*   **Ease of Use**: It allows companies to launch a blockchain solution in minutes without needing to hire a full engineering team <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:41:47">[00:41:47]</a>.
*   **Competitive Advantage**: Unlike services like Amazon, which primarily support Ethereum, Ava Cloud focuses on Avalanche's underlying performance stack <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:42:03">[00:42:03]</a>. A unique advantage is that the team building Ava Cloud is also responsible for developing the core Avalanche protocol, allowing for quicker reactions and integrated development compared to third-party managed services <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:42:30">[00:42:30]</a>. The product is expected to go live very soon <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:43:01">[00:43:01]</a>.

### Avasant
Avasant is an artist grants program initiated by Ava Labs. It aims to support up-and-coming artists who are interested in using [[blockchain_and_crypto_innovation | blockchain technology]] but lack the knowledge of how to begin <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:41">[00:17:41]</a>.
*   **Curriculum-Based Support**: The program offers music curriculum, guiding artists from a basic understanding of [[blockchain_and_crypto_innovation | blockchain]] to developing their own projects and leveraging NFTs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:11">[00:18:11]</a>.
*   **Collaborative Spirit**: This initiative fosters a collaborative environment, with established artists becoming evangelists for the program, helping new artists achieve their dreams through [[blockchain_and_crypto_innovation | blockchain]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:02">[00:19:02]</a>.
*   **Product Validation**: By working closely with artists, Ava Labs can validate and refine their product direction for music NFTs and other creative applications before public release, gathering crucial feedback on potential solutions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:05">[00:17:05]</a>.

## Lessons from the Crypto Market

Jay Kurahashi-Sof emphasizes key lessons for builders and investors in the [[the_future_of_the_crypto_market | crypto market]]:
*   **Critical Thinking**: It's crucial not to take things at face value <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:23">[00:24:23]</a>. Given the speculative nature of [[the_future_of_the_crypto_market | crypto]] where "money is just numbers on a screen" and prices can fluctuate wildly like a lottery <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:10">[00:11:10]</a>, thorough research and independent assessment are essential <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:35:37">[00:35:37]</a>.
*   **Avoiding "Aping In"**: He advises against impulsively jumping into projects (known as "aping in") based on hype or quick dopamine rushes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:35:48">[00:35:48]</a>. This behavior, often seen in bull markets, can lead to significant losses if the inherent value or long-term potential is not understood <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:36:10">[00:36:10]</a>.
*   **Building Inherent Value**: For artists considering NFTs, the focus should be on building inherent value for their community, not on "get-rich-quick schemes" that can burn their fanbase <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:19">[00:20:19]</a>. Validating product direction with potential users before launch is a crucial step <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:05">[00:17:05]</a>.
*   **Long-term Vision**: The industry is still akin to "high school" with manipulation and tribes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:40:00">[00:40:00]</a>. Success in [[the_future_of_the_crypto_market | crypto]] requires understanding the underlying "science" and applying consistent, hard work, rather than relying on luck <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:31:54">[00:31:54]</a>.
