## The Vision Behind Solana Mobile

Anatoly Yakovenko, co-founder of Solana, views [[Solana Mobile Development and Future | mobile]] as his "baby," stemming from his extensive background working at Qualcomm <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:12:21">[01:12:21]</a>. He believes there's a significant opportunity to create a better experience for both users and developers in the [[the_evolving_landscape_of_web3_and_blockchain_technologies | crypto]] space <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:45">[00:00:45]</a>.

Key motivations include:
*   **Enhanced User Experience** Providing a more secure and slicker signing experience for [[the_evolving_landscape_of_web3_and_blockchain_technologies | crypto]] transactions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:13:10">[01:13:10]</a>. Mobile chips already contain secure elements and trusted display technology capable of making cryptographic signatures as secure as a hardware wallet like Ledger <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:12:33">[01:12:33]</a>.
*   **Developer Opportunities** Offering a [[the_evolving_landscape_of_web3_and_blockchain_technologies | crypto]]-friendly app store that eliminates the traditional 30% fees charged by dominant app stores <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:13:18">[01:13:18]</a>, thereby reducing costs for developers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:19:15">[01:19:15]</a>.
*   **Onboarding New Users** While Apple and Google are expected to eventually integrate [[the_evolving_landscape_of_web3_and_blockchain_technologies | crypto]] support, Solana aims to capitalize on the current window of opportunity to onboard a large number of [[the_evolving_landscape_of_web3_and_blockchain_technologies | crypto]] users to a new platform before native support becomes widespread <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:15:26">[01:15:26]</a>.

## Solana Seeker: Features and Market Position

The Solana Seeker (Saga) is positioned as the "definitive [[the_evolving_landscape_of_web3_and_blockchain_technologies | Web3]] mobile device" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:14:13">[01:14:13]</a>.
*   **Target Audience**: The device is not necessarily aimed at convincing existing iPhone users to switch, especially those tied to corporate security systems that primarily support iPhones <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:14:18">[01:14:18]</a>. Instead, it encourages a "second device" approach, where users can have a dedicated [[the_evolving_landscape_of_web3_and_blockchain_technologies | NFT]]/[[the_evolving_landscape_of_web3_and_blockchain_technologies | crypto]] phone for financial activities, separating it from their daily personal phone for security <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:14:44">[01:14:44]</a>.
*   **Global Market**: A significant portion of the world (80% of mobile sales) is not as reliant on iPhone ecosystems as the US, making regions where Android is dominant more receptive to such a device <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:14:52">[01:14:52]</a>.
*   **Seed Vault**: A core feature of the Solana Seeker is the Seed Vault, powered by the Soulflare wallet company. This technology securely stores the user's seed phrase, allowing a single wallet application to be shared across various applications without users needing to re-enter their seed phrase or risk their entire [[the_evolving_landscape_of_web3_and_blockchain_technologies | crypto]] portfolio by sharing it across different in-app wallets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:17:09">[01:17:09]</a>. The goal is to make the signing experience as seamless as Apple Pay <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:18:24">[01:18:24]</a>.

## Impact and Future Outlook

The [[Solana Mobile Development and Future | Solana mobile]] initiative has faced initial skepticism, with some calling it a "bull market idea" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:11:47">[01:11:47]</a>. However, early successes, such as the Bonk airdrop which at one point covered the phone's cost, demonstrated its potential <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:11:17">[01:11:17]</a>.

The end goal for [[Solana Mobile Development and Future | Solana mobile]] is to disrupt the current duopoly of Apple and Google in the mobile market <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:18:55">[01:18:55]</a>. By introducing competition, Solana aims to incentivize these tech giants to reduce their fees and foster innovation that benefits developers and users <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:19:12">[01:19:12]</a>.

While the initial sales of 20,000 phones seemed small, the subsequent achievement of 150,000 pre-orders has established a sizable developer ecosystem, creating a path towards sustainable growth and potentially reaching 10 million units in the future <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:19:42">[01:19:42]</a>. This step-by-step approach reflects the "acceleration" mindset prevalent in the Solana community, emphasizing swift decision-making and action to drive progress in a rapidly evolving technological landscape <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:20:01">[01:20:01]</a>.
