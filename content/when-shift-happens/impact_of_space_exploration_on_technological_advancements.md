## Personal Fascination and Motivation

Adony Aboten, co-founder and CPO at Mist Labs, developed a lifelong fascination with space from a young age, growing up with "Star Trek" and being captivated by the idea of a Starship captain exploring unknown worlds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:22:10">[01:22:10]</a>. This passion led him to initially study astrophysics <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:22:29">[01:22:29]</a>, highlighting the intrinsic human desire to understand the universe and push boundaries <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:22:36">[01:22:36]</a>.

## Driving Force for Innovation

Space exploration is seen as a means to gain significant scientific advancement <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:27:16">[01:27:16]</a>. The technologies developed for spacecraft, such as propulsion systems, can be adapted to create faster planes for Earth-bound travel, potentially reducing long-haul flights from 15-17 hours to just one or two <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:28:05">[01:28:05]</a>. This demonstrates a direct technological impact that benefits daily life.

## Public Funding and Private Enterprise

While a significant portion of space exploration is funded by public tax money, which raises questions about public input on spending <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:27:02">[01:27:02]</a>, the returns on investment in scientific advancement are considered high <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:27:17">[01:27:17]</a>. There is a strong argument for transparency, providing the public with data and information about the return on investment from space exploration to foster informed opinions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:27:36">[01:27:36]</a>. This could make people more sympathetic if they see how space expenditures lead to tangible benefits, such as curing diseases <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:27:45">[01:27:45]</a>.

The success of private enterprises like [[the_exponential_age_thesis_and_its_impact_on_technology_and_investment | Elon Musk's]] SpaceX in comparison to government space agencies highlights the potential for profit-driven initiatives to make more efficient decisions in developing advanced technologies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:29:01">[01:29:01]</a>. If private companies identify a return on investment, they are motivated to fund and develop space technologies that can be applied to other sectors, like building faster aircraft <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:28:43">[01:28:43]</a>.
