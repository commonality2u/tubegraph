From: [[zeihanongeopolitics]] <br/> 

Israel, despite being considered the "gold standard for intelligence," failed to detect the recent Hamas assault from Gaza <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:40">[00:01:40]</a>. This oversight is described as the "greatest intelligence debacle in the world in the last 50 years" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:46">[00:07:46]</a>.

## Unprecedented Oversight
The intelligence failure is particularly baffling because Israel missed an operation involving hundreds of fighters, dozens of vehicles, and six different transport options <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:37">[00:04:37]</a>. This was unexpected, given that Israeli intelligence had historically been "obsessed" with Gaza <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:31">[00:04:31]</a>.

### Focus on Gaza
Israel's intelligence efforts primarily concentrated on the Palestinian territories, especially Gaza, due to other regional threats being more predictable or less pressing <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:54">[00:02:54]</a>:
*   **Lebanon**: While Hezbollah is a known issue, it operates as a political party with established communication lines that can be monitored <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:52">[00:01:52]</a>.
*   **Syria**: The Syrian border has been quiet for over a decade due to the civil war and Israel's control of the Golan Heights <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:21">[00:02:21]</a>.
*   **Jordan**: Jordan is a satellite state economically dependent on Israel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:33">[00:02:33]</a>.
*   **Egypt**: Egypt is functionally an ally, with most of its population far from the border <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:41">[00:02:41]</a>.
*   **West Bank**: The PLO in the West Bank maintains semi-functional relations with Israel, allowing for monitoring <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:01">[00:03:01]</a>.

Gaza, an "open-air prison" housing 3 million people in a highly dense, industrialized, but resource-starved area, became Hamas's stronghold <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:38">[00:03:38]</a>. The dire living conditions foster "less than savory ideologies" and a sense of hopelessness <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:17">[00:04:17]</a>.

## Political and Societal Factors
The intelligence failure will have significant [[political_dynamics_in_israel|political connotations]] in Israel, likely leading to the fall of the government <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:49">[00:04:49]</a>. While [[benjamin_netanyahus_political_strategy|Netanyahu]] formed a national war council, the civilian government is in trouble <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:07">[00:05:07]</a>.

A contributing factor is [[israels_domestic_challenges_and_demographics|Israel's demographics]] and political system <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:16">[00:05:16]</a>. Laws protecting those committed to religious study mean 10-30% of the population may not work or serve in the military but can still vote <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:19">[00:05:19]</a>. This group is a rising demographic, serving as "king maker" in governments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:01">[00:06:01]</a>. Israel's proportional representation system, lacking a floor for entry, allows a "whole rainbow of wack job rightwing parties" (religious fundamentalist parties) to gain power <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:33">[00:06:33]</a>. Many officials from these parties are described as "mind-numbingly incompetent" due to a lack of value placed on secular education <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:08">[00:07:08]</a>. These are the individuals now tasked with explaining the intelligence debacle <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:41">[00:07:41]</a>.

## Implications for US-Israel Relations
The [[usisrael_relations|United States]] did not foresee the attack, not due to intelligence failure on its part, but because its focus was on Ukraine, and it outsourced intelligence on Gaza to Israel, a capable country <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:00">[00:14:00]</a>. Gaza holds "zero interest" economically for the US <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:26">[00:14:26]</a>.

However, the presence of American citizens kidnapped and taken into Gaza changes the dynamic <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:52">[00:14:52]</a>. Due to Israel's intelligence failure, the US lacks information on their location <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:22">[00:15:22]</a>. This puts the US in an awkward position, potentially forcing an increased intelligence focus on Gaza and even diplomatic engagement with Iran <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:32">[00:15:32]</a>. Since [[irans_approach_to_the_israel_hamas_situation|Iran]] may have influenced the timing of the assault and could have access to the perpetrators <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:26">[00:16:26]</a>, Tehran might be the only source of information for the US to fulfill its "sacred social contract" of retrieving its citizens <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:11">[00:17:11]</a>.
