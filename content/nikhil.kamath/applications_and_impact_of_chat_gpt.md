## Defining Chat GPT

Chat GPT is an application built upon GPT (Generative Pre-trained Transformer) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:36:50">01:36:50</a>.
*   **GPT** is a trained Transformer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:16:46">01:16:46</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:36:41">01:36:41</a>. A Transformer is a type of computer primarily used for translation, but now also for next-word prediction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:41:40">01:41:40</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:44:03">01:44:03</a>.
*   The fundamental mechanism of GPT is to predict the most likely next word based on a given set of words, drawing from a "probability cluster" of words <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:12:26">01:12:26</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:13:08">01:13:08</a>.
*   GPT is "trained" by dumping vast amounts of data, primarily from the internet, including forums like Reddit, into the Transformer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:17:18">01:17:18</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:20:03">01:20:03</a>. This training allows it to learn the underlying patterns of language <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:17:40">01:17:40</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:18:15">01:18:15</a>.
*   **Chat GPT** specifically refers to GPT that has been trained to simulate a conversation between a human and an AI assistant <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:15:40">01:15:40</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:16:16">01:16:16</a>. It leverages its completion mode to fill in conversational blanks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:16:19">01:16:19</a>.
*   The "Eureka moment" for AI was the removal of the need for middlemen (coders/programmers) for computers to understand spoken language instructions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:12:07">01:12:07</a>. Previously, computers required very specific, verbose code, where even a missed word or syntax error would prevent understanding <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:23:31">01:23:31</a>.

## Current and Future [[applications_and_future_of_ai_in_various_industries | Applications of AI]]

### Current Applications
*   **Content Generation**: Chat GPT can generate content for creators, such as helping with espresso feeds on platforms <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:39:39">00:39:39</a>.

*   **Idea Generation**: It can act as a chat box for suggestions and ideas <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:10:41">01:10:41</a>.

*   **Coding**: It can be prompted with API documentation to write code <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:25:50">01:25:50</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:26:01">01:26:01</a>.

### Future Capabilities: Auto GPT and Plugins
*   **Auto GPT** is a more advanced concept that adds memory to Chat GPT and allows it to delegate tasks to other Chat GPT instances, creating a hierarchy similar to an organization chart <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:59:01">02:59:01</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:04:04">03:04:04</a>.
    *   It differentiates by having long-term memory, enabling it to weave through different conversational topics <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:53:54">02:53:54</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:54:44">02:54:44</a>.
    *   It has access to external tools, including the ability to search using Google and execute Python scripts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:11:16">03:11:16</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:18:03">03:18:03</a>. For example, it can find and download a YouTube to MP4 downloader script and execute it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:34:00">03:34:00</a>.
    *   While Chat GPT can output code, it cannot execute it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:47:00">03:47:00</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:51:24">03:51:24</a>. Auto GPT, however, can access a terminal (command prompt) and execute code <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:57:04">03:57:04</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:58:30">03:58:30</a>.
    *   Plugins for Chat GPT exist that allow code execution and internet access, though they are not fully released due to safety concerns and the risk of "all hell breaking loose" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:56:56">03:56:56</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:13:00">04:13:00</a>.
*   **Robotics Integration**: Chat GPT's cognition could be dumped into robots, allowing them to create code on the fly to control their movements and perform tasks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:32:18">01:32:18</a>.
*   **Human Augmentation**: Future technologies, such as neural links and glasses, could allow AI to amplify human senses (e.g., "Superman vision" or "Superman hearing") and provide "charisma on demand" by suggesting real-time responses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:42:01">01:42:01</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:43:02">01:43:02</a>.

## [[the_impact_of_artificial_intelligence_on_society | Impact of AI on Society]]

### Ethical and Societal Concerns
*   **Misinformation and Propaganda**: The ability of AI to generate real-looking content and manipulate information could lead to widespread misinformation and even start wars <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:11:09">04:11:09</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:22:00">04:22:00</a>. The problem is that with AI, persuasion towards incorrect information could increase significantly <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:16:08">04:16:08</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:36:05">04:36:05</a>.
*   **Data Scrutiny**: People may become more skeptical of online content due to the prevalence of AI-generated information <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:38:00">04:38:00</a>.
*   **Privacy Invasion**: AI could lead to increased privacy invasion, as devices like phones already throw ads based on conversations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:12:44">01:12:44</a>. Advanced tools like "Superman hearing" earbuds could amplify and tune into conversations around a user <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:44:00">01:44:00</a>.
*   **Addiction**: The increasing frequency of dopamine hits from technology, accelerated by AI-generated content, could lead to addiction and depression if not constantly engaged <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:03:00">02:03:00</a>.
*   **AI Alignment**: A major concern is ensuring AI does what humans intend without going "off on a tangent," as seen in the "paperclip maximizer theory" where an AI optimizing for a single goal could inadvertently destroy humanity to achieve it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:32:27">01:32:27</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:32:43">01:32:43</a>.

Large IT services companies like Infosys may hire fewer people but become more efficient <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:02:22">01:02:22</a>.
    *   **Data Entry Operators & Call Center Employees**: These roles are highly susceptible to automation, with AI capable of real-time customer interaction and support <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:02:33">01:02:33</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:02:45">01:02:45</a>.
    *   **Marketeers**: Especially those running ads, are likely to be affected <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:03:45">01:03:45</a>.
    *   **Paralegals**: Given the large outsourcing of legal work, this profession is also at risk <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:03:51">01:03:51</a>.
    *   **Designers**: AI can generate thumbnails and other designs from prompts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:03:53">01:03:53</a>.
    *   **Project Managers (PMs)**: Tools like "Baby AGI" can provide step-by-step guidance for building products, potentially replacing the need for human PMs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:54:00">01:54:00</a>.
    *   **SAS (Software as a Service) Front-ends**: The future of SAS may shift to voice-first interfaces, with AI directly interacting with back-end systems, making traditional front-end interfaces less necessary <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:18:22">01:18:22</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:18:50">01:18:50</a>.
*   **Emergence of New Roles**:
    *   **Prompt Engineers**: Individuals with deep knowledge and life experience to craft precise prompts for AI will be highly valuable <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:57:56">01:57:56</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:58:38">01:58:38</a>.
    *   **Authentic Content Creators/Influencers**: Those who have built trust and a personal brand will become even more powerful, as their distribution channel and authentic opinion will be highly valued <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:04:04">01:04:04</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:05:07">01:05:07</a>.
    *   **Offline Experience Roles**: Jobs involving complex, multi-faceted offline tasks, such as hotel hospitality or concierges for specific groups, will be difficult for AI to replicate <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:56:38">01:56:38</a>.
*   **The Problem of the "Disgruntled Elite"**: As white-collar jobs are displaced, highly educated individuals losing their status may become desperate and create extreme content or cause societal unrest <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:05:05">02:05:05</a>.

### [[impact_of_ai_on_social_and_economic_structures | Economic and Governance Impacts]]
*   **Capitalism's Evolution**: Capitalism, though currently seen as "broken" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:49:33">00:49:33</a>, is still considered better than alternatives and will need to evolve. It may shift towards a "compassionate capitalism" model, where those with disproportionate wealth contribute more through taxes like inheritance, property, and estate taxes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:22:00">02:22:00</a>.
*   **Universal Basic Income (UBI) / Universal Basic Resources (UBR)**: With widespread job displacement, UBI or UBR (providing resources instead of just money to avoid inflation) may become necessary to sustain populations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:24:21">02:24:21</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:51:00">02:51:00</a>.
*   **Information as Capital**: Information is the underlying asset of capitalism <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:52:28">00:52:28</a>. If AI can manipulate information at a high level, it could disrupt capital markets and economies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:53:09">00:53:09</a>.
*   **Regulation**: The ability to regulate AI, particularly the manufacturing and sale of powerful GPUs and access to computational resources, is seen as a way to control its exponential growth, similar to pharmaceutical regulation or nuclear proliferation treaties <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:49:37">01:49:37</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:51:07">01:51:07</a>. However, the open-source nature of many AI models makes stopping their spread difficult <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:49:19">01:49:19</a>.
*   **Economic Progress**: Despite potential challenges, there is an optimistic view that the world, including India, will continue to see economic progress and create sustainable employment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:15:54">02:15:54</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:18:02">02:18:02</a>.
