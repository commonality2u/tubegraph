[[Bill Gates on innovation and work | Bill Gates]] shares his views on innovation, offering advice for entrepreneurs on different approaches to building new ventures.

## Building on Existing Platforms
For young entrepreneurs, [[Bill Gates on innovation and work | Bill Gates]] recommends focusing on [[Tech Innovations and Future Trends | AI]] and leveraging existing large platforms, such as those from Google or Microsoft, to build new applications and services <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>. This suggests a strategy of creating value by innovating on top of established technological foundations.

## Encouraging Foundational and [[Bold approaches in business strategy | Bold Innovation]]
Despite his advice to build on existing platforms, [[Bill Gates on innovation and work | Bill Gates]] also expresses respect and encouragement for individuals who are "bold enough" to pursue foundational innovation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:34">[00:00:34]</a>. He acknowledges that he "could be wrong" in his own recommendations and praises those who choose to develop entirely new, foundational technologies, stating, "hey good for you" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:40">[00:00:40]</a>. This indicates his appreciation for ambitious and transformative [[Bold approaches in business strategy | approaches]] in the tech industry.
