This dedication can sometimes blur the line between passion and addiction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:25">[00:00:25]</a>. Individuals can be both "soft, tender, and still be extremely competitive" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a> in their fitness pursuits.
