## Advice for Young Entrepreneurs

For a young entrepreneur without significant capital, entering the [[energy_transition_solutions | energy transition]] sector is feasible.

### Finding Support and Capital
*   **Philanthropic Support:** Ideal starting point is to find a philanthropist <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:29">[00:15:29]</a>. Breakthrough Energy, for example, offers a fellows program that provides grants for good early-stage ideas, without requiring a business plan initially <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:32">[00:15:32]</a>.
*   **Venture Capital:** Following the early stage, specialized Venture Capital groups are actively looking to fund risky [[Climate Change Solutions | climate technologies]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:51">[00:15:51]</a>. There is a network of venture capitalists, including Breakthrough Energy, interested in discovering promising ideas in India <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:03">[00:16:03]</a>.
*   **Current Opportunity:** It is a favorable time for securing risk capital if an idea is attractive and poised to have a significant impact <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:23">[00:16:23]</a>.

### Leveraging AI in Business
For a 25-year-old in India with limited capital (e.g., $100,000), a viable path is to build an AI-based solution using existing platforms like Google or Microsoft <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:29">[00:24:29]</a>. This approach emphasizes deep customer understanding, continuous data improvement, and targeting demanding customers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:39">[00:24:39]</a>.

Specific AI applications that can benefit society and offer business opportunities include:
*   **Legal System:** Addressing backlogs in the legal system using AI to enhance productivity (e.g., making the legal system four times more productive) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:21:12">[00:21:12]</a>. This can fundamentally change justice and benefit all business activity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:21:17">[00:21:17]</a>.
*   **Health Advice:** Delivering medical advice and prescriptions via AI models presents a significant opportunity, especially in middle-income countries like India <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:17">[00:23:17]</a>. While quality standards are crucial, AI could eventually lead to "lifelong medical agents" that process sensor data, personal preferences, and the latest medical literature <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:51">[00:23:51]</a>. Regulation in some Western countries might be a barrier, potentially creating an [[business_opportunities_in_ev_and_renewable_energy_sectors | arbitrage opportunity]] for India <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:27">[00:22:27]</a>.
*   **Agriculture:** AI can be used to create farmer advisory systems <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:08">[00:10:08]</a>.
*   **Education:** Developing AI-powered educational tutors has shown great results, with pilot projects in India <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:12">[00:17:12]</a>.

Most AI opportunities for entrepreneurs lie in building applications on top of foundational models, rather than creating the base models from scratch, which requires billions in capital <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:52">[00:20:52]</a>. India's unique demographics and regulatory environment could provide advantages in areas like health and agriculture <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:11">[00:22:11]</a>.
