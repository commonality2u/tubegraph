[[Join The Dots Foundation overview and mission | Join The Dots Foundation]] actively invests in the holistic development of students in rural areas of Andhra Pradesh and Karnataka, which includes significant efforts in sports and cultural activities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:04">[00:01:04]</a>.

## Expanding Access to Sports

The foundation recognizes the importance of physical activity and sports in the lives of children.

### Park Inauguration
[[Join The Dots Foundation overview and mission | Join The Dots Foundation]], with support from Nikhil Kat and WTF, has brought recreational infrastructure typically found in metropolitan cities to rural schools <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:26">[00:02:26]</a>. An example of this is the inauguration of a children's park in Jari Pali school <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:09">[00:02:09]</a>. Rajendra from [[Join The Dots Foundation overview and mission | Join The Dots Foundation]] noted that this infrastructure would be greatly enjoyed by the children <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:33">[00:02:33]</a>.

### Fencing Program
Shila, the Sports Coordinator for [[Join The Dots Foundation overview and mission | Join The Dots Foundation]], highlights the foundation's investment and hard work in sports <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:32">[00:04:32]</a>. A specific and special sport they support is fencing, which is showcased through a demonstration <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:48">[00:04:48]</a>.

### Other Sports Activities
Beyond dedicated programs, the foundation also facilitates fun activities such as cricket matches <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:42">[00:09:42]</a>.

## Fostering Cultural Talent

In addition to sports, [[Join The Dots Foundation overview and mission | Join The Dots Foundation]] nurtures cultural talents among students.

### Carnatic Music and Flute Training
Vandana, who heads the cultural pillar of [[Join The Dots Foundation overview and mission | Join The Dots Foundation]], oversees programs that introduce students to traditional arts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:43">[00:08:43]</a>. For over a year, nearly 20 students have been learning Carnatic music <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:53">[00:08:53]</a>. Additionally, for several months, a few students have been learning to play the flute <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:59">[00:08:59]</a>.
