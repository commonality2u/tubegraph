The WTF Fund stands as a significant initiative focused on providing non-dilutive grants to young founders under the age of 25 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:16">[00:00:16]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:39">[00:00:39]</a>. This fund aims to support individuals with "great ideas" but "no money to execute" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:20">[00:00:20]</a>. Starting with 1,500 applications, the fund successfully shortlisted 160, eventually presenting grants to nine startups <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>.

## Bridging Gaps for Women with St. Pille

Among the funded startups, "St. Pille" directly addresses the mission of [[challenges_faced_by_women_entrepreneurs | empowering women through entrepreneurship]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:14">[00:01:14]</a>. The company's core aim is to "bridge the gap from just managing to solving the period concerns and the discomforts that a woman go through" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>. This focus on addressing specific women's health issues highlights a direct application of entrepreneurship for social impact and [[inspirational_entrepreneurial_journeys | inspirational entrepreneurial journeys]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:14">[00:01:14]</a>.

## Supporting Young Innovators

*   **Pixi** – A venture breathing life into toys, founded by an individual from Jaipur <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:19">[00:01:19]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:24">[00:01:24]</a>. The founder learned about the WTF Fund through a podcast, emphasizing accessibility <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:27">[00:01:27]</a>.
*   **Urban Animal** – India's first dog DNA test, aiming to help pet parents identify diseases early and improve dogs' lifespan <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:18">[00:02:18]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:21">[00:02:21]</a>.
*   **Bio Computer** – A project focused on using DNA for data storage to make data centers more efficient <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:35">[00:02:35]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:40">[00:02:40]</a>.
*   Medical students from Government Medical College in Chandigarh who received USFDA Breakthrough device designation for early gastric cancer technology <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:48">[00:02:48]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:53">[00:02:53]</a>.
*   A cloud computer specifically designed for creative professionals, offering a cloud workstation accessible from various devices <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:02">[00:03:02]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:08">[00:03:08]</a>.
*   A tool that assists sales professionals by researching the internet and structuring insights into a single page <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:14">[00:03:14]</a>.
*   A company focused on pet nutrition, with plans to create protein or food from unused carbon efficiently <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:31">[00:03:31]</a>.

This broad support for young entrepreneurs, including those addressing specific women's needs, contributes to a wider ecosystem of empowerment through innovation and business creation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:16">[00:00:16]</a>.
