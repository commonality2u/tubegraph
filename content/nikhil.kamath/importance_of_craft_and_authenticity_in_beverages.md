## The Role of Authenticity and Quality
Authenticity is paramount in the [[craft_beverages_industry | craft beverage industry]], with quality being a direct outcome of genuine processes and ingredients. For a 25-year-old, good coffee, good training, and even good food are becoming the "bare minimum" expectations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>. There is a noticeable shift in consumer preference towards specialized beverages like tonic waters and kombucha, moving away from more generic, mass-produced options <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:17">[00:01:17]</a>. The preference for these [[craft_beverages_industry | craft beverage]] options over brands like Swepps indicates a market where consumers seek out more authentic and differentiated products <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:20">[00:01:20]</a>.
