## Differing Outlooks Among Newer Generations

A notable observation regarding the [[generation_zs_consumption_habits_and_preferences | generation]] born after 1995 or 2000 is that they do not possess the same level of optimism as previous generations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:00:59">[01:00:59]</a>. This indicates a shift in [[generational_choices_and_conformity | generational attitudes]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:00:59">[01:00:59]</a>.

### Call to Action for Younger Generations

In response to this perceived lack of optimism, there is an encouragement for these younger individuals to "get involved" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:11:00">[01:11:00]</a>. The advice suggests moving beyond passive commentary or online engagement:

> "You can sit on the sidelines and you can comment and get behind your keyboard and sound off, but otherwise get into the arena and actually give it a go." <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:13:28">[01:13:28]</a>
