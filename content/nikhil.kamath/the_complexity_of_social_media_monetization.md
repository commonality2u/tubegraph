## User Engagement and Value Generation

A core aspect of [[the_business_models_and_monetization_of_social_media_platforms | social media monetization]] is the user's role in generating value. The act of users posting personal content, such as holiday pictures, is tied to a "vicious cycle of a dopamine hit" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:29">[00:00:29]</a>. This cycle provides a "dopamine hit" at "both ends," benefiting the platform side and the individual user <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:35">[00:00:35]</a>. This continuous engagement creates an "ecosystem of people" that platforms cultivate, akin to "market makers" in the stock market <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:57">[00:00:57]</a>. An example of this engagement is a mother posting personal grievances about her children on Facebook <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:02">[00:01:02]</a>.
