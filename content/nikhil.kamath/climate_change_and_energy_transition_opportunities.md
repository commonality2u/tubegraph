## [[Energy Transition as a Solution to Climate Change | Energy Transition]] Opportunities

    *   Improving solar cells <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="13:52:00">[13:52:00]</a>.

### Support for Young Entrepreneurs

For a young entrepreneur without significant capital, the ideal starting point is to seek support from philanthropists <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="15:29:00">[15:29:00]</a>. The Breakthrough Energy fund, for example, offers a fellows program that provides grants for promising early-stage ideas <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="15:32:00">[15:32:00]</a>. Beyond grants, Venture Capital groups specializing in [[Challenges in engineering and implementing climate solutions | climate technologies]] are willing to fund very risky ventures <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="15:51:00">[15:51:00]</a>. There is an active network looking for good ideas, including those from India <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="16:04:00">[16:04:00]</a>. This is considered a very good time for risk capital if an idea is attractive and could have a significant impact <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="16:23:00">[16:23:00]</a>.

### Opportunities for Indian Entrepreneurs in AI

For Indian entrepreneurs, building foundational AI models from the ground up requires substantial capital (billions of dollars) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="20:09:00">[20:09:00]</a>. It's suggested that only a few Indian companies might pursue this base level, potentially focusing on specific Indian applications <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="20:37:00">[20:37:00]</a>.

The greater opportunity lies in building applications on top of existing AI platforms <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="20:52:00">[20:52:00]</a>. Examples include:
*   Helping architects <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="20:57:00">[20:57:00]</a>.
*   Assisting farmers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="21:00:00">[21:00:00]</a>.
*   Improving the Indian legal system, which currently faces significant backlogs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="21:03:00">[21:03:00]</a>. Making the legal system more productive would enhance justice <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="21:14:00">[21:14:00]</a>.

India may have a unique advantage, or "arbitrage," particularly in health and agriculture, where regulations might be more conducive to rapid AI adoption than in countries like the US, which might over-regulate out of risk aversion <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="22:31:00">[22:31:00]</a>. The business of delivering medical advice and prescribing non-human models has significant potential in India <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="23:13:00">[23:13:00]</a>. This could start with specific areas like pregnant women or diabetes patients, eventually leading to a "lifelong medical agent" that processes sensor data and medical literature <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="23:39:00">[23:39:00]</a>.

If starting a business in India with $100,000, focusing on an AI application that leverages Google or Microsoft platforms and staying close to the customer to refine data and user interfaces is recommended <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="24:29:00">[24:29:00]</a>. Clearing legal backlogs is cited as a fascinating area, as it supports all business activity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="24:55:00">[24:55:00]</a>.
