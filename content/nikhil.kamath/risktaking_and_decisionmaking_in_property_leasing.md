## Passion vs. Profit

While passion for one's work is often lauded, a pragmatic view on decision-making in real estate suggests that financial gain can outweigh emotional attachment. Given two scenarios—passionately engaging in an endeavor that loses money, or dispassionately pursuing something that generates income—the preference is firmly for the latter <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:41">[00:01:41]</a>. The rationale is to make money first, and then pursue other passions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:53">[00:01:53]</a>.
