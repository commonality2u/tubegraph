## Challenges and Perspectives

A significant challenge in implementing solutions is often the "engineering mindset," which can lead to the belief that certain things "cannot be done" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:39">[00:01:39]</a>.

There are differing views on the immediate impact of specific technologies:
*   While nuclear fusion has been credited with saving billions of tons of CO2 in an existing industry <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:17">[00:01:17]</a>, there is a contention that "anything you can do on Fusion will help you in this decade" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:23">[00:01:23]</a>, suggesting that its impact might not be immediate enough for the current decade's urgent needs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:25">[00:01:25]</a>.
