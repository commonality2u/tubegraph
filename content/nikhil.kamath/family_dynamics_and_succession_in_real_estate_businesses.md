## [[family_management_in_real_estate_business | Managing Family in Real Estate Businesses]]

*   **Maturity and Consensus:** Family members need a "certain level of maturity" in handling each other. Decisions are ideally unanimous, based on conviction supported by numbers and facts, moving from an intuitive, person-dependent model to a process-driven one <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:07">[00:22:07]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:28">[00:24:28]</a>.
*   **Transparency:** For listed companies, transparency in value helps manage individual expectations and reduces potential conflicts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:48">[00:22:48]</a>.

## Generational Differences and Succession
Succession rates show that only 36% of businesses transition to the second generation, 11-15% to the third, and a mere 4% to the fourth, indicating the inherent difficulties <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:07">[00:18:07]</a>.

**Prior Generation's Approach:**
*   **Risk-Taking:** The prior generation (referred to as "OGs") is seen as having more "risk-taking ability" and intuition, making "bold calls" and building "very strong relationships" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:27">[00:25:27]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:26:01">[00:26:01]</a>. This might be due to having "lesser to lose" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:26:36">[00:26:36]</a>.
*   **Work Ethic:** They often worked "long hours, 7 days a week," with their work being their "passion," or rather, a necessity, as "passion is a luxury" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:26:41">[00:26:41]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:26:53">[00:26:53]</a>.

**Current Generation's Approach:**
*   **Analysis Paralysis:** The current generation tends to "think a little bit too much" before acting, leading to "analysis paralysis" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:46">[00:25:46]</a>.
*   **Building on Foundations:** While the prior generation's approach might be better for starting from scratch, the current generation is more suited to "take over a business and to build a business from a platform or a foundation that it is at today" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:26:14">[00:26:14]</a>.
*   **Process-Driven:** As organizations grow, they need to evolve from "person dependent" to "process driven" with logic, numbers, and financing guiding decisions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:41">[00:24:41]</a>.

The **passing on** of the business is "extremely different in every single family," influenced by factors like the number of siblings and their interest levels <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:51">[00:20:51]</a>.
