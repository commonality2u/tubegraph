## The "Dirty Place" Stereotype

For many, particularly those from backgrounds such as a South Indian middle-class family, a common understanding taught from childhood is that [[politics_and_global_leaders | politics]] is a "dirty place" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:45">[00:01:45]</a>. This notion becomes deeply ingrained in the collective psyche, making it very difficult to change <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:56">[00:01:56]</a>.

## Encouraging Positive Engagement

Despite the negative stereotypes, there is a call for "good people" to continuously enter [[politics_and_global_leaders | politics]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:53">[00:00:53]</a>.
