## Origin Story: Addressing a Rural Need

The concept for a long-lasting mobile phone originated from observations in rural areas lacking basic infrastructure <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:04">[00:00:04]</a>.

*   **Infrastructure Challenges**
    *   Many villages had no electricity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:09">[00:00:09]</a>.
    *   Cellular signal was often unavailable at homes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>.
*   **Ingenious Solutions**
    *   An AEL ISDPCO booth operator powered his setup with a large truck battery <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:11">[00:00:11]</a>.
    *   Signal was acquired using a 20 dB gain antenna mounted on a pole <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:16">[00:00:16]</a>.
    *   To charge devices, the operator traveled 11 km to a village with electricity, charging overnight and returning in the morning <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:23">[00:00:23]</a>. This service generated approximately 4,000 rupees a month, a significant income in such an environment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>.
*   **High Cost of Charging**
    *   Further travels in the interiors of Bihar revealed people paying 25 to 40 rupees to charge their phones <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:39">[00:00:39]</a>. This highlighted a significant [[market_need_for_extended_battery_life_phones | market need for extended battery life phones]].

## The Micromax Solution

These observations led to the development of a mobile phone designed for [[longevity | long battery life]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:47">[00:00:47]</a>.

*   The goal was a phone with a one-month [[battery_technology_and_management | battery]] backup <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:47">[00:00:47]</a>.
*   The first Micromax phone was launched with the tagline "mobile kebab" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:02">[00:00:02]</a>, indicating its 30-day standby time <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:54">[00:00:54]</a>.

## Market Impact and Differentiation

The extended [[innovative_solutions_for_power_and_signal | battery]] life proved to be a powerful [[differentiation_strategies_in_the_competitive_beverage_market | differentiator]] in the market <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:58">[00:00:58]</a>. Within 10 days of launch, the phones were sold out <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:57">[00:00:57]</a>, as there was nothing else like it available <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:59">[00:00:59]</a>. The battery was the key differentiating feature <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:01">[00:01:01]</a>.
