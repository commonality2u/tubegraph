## Personal Work Ethic and Views

[[bill_gates_perspective_on_personal_success_and_motivation | Bill Gates]] applies a rigorous standard to himself, believing that to work hard and avoid self-deception, one must be quite self-critical <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:10">[00:00:10]</a>. He emphasizes that he [[bill_gates_on_innovation_and_work | chooses to work]] rather than being compelled to, stating, "I don't have to work. I choose to work" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>.

He reflects on the philosophical question of [[bill_gates_and_wealth | wealth]], pondering how it makes sense to simultaneously want to earn money and give it away <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:11">[00:01:11]</a>.
