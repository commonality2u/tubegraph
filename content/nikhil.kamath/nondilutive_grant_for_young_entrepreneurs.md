The WTFund is a unique initiative designed to provide non-dilutive grants to [[entrepreneurial_journey_and_advice_for_young_aspiring_entrepreneurs | young]] founders <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:39">[00:00:39]</a>. It functions as a community-driven grant program, not a traditional fund <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:02">[00:00:02]</a>.

## Purpose and Target Audience
The WTFund aims to support [[entrepreneurship_for_beginners | young people]] under the age of 25 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:16">[00:00:16]</a> who possess great ideas but lack the financial means to execute them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:20">[00:00:20]</a>. The initiative seeks to bridge the gap for these aspiring [[entrepreneurship and startup success | entrepreneurs]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>.

## Application and Selection Process
The WTFund's inaugural round saw 1,500 applications <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>. This pool was narrowed down to 160 shortlists <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>, with the first nine startups ultimately receiving funding <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>.

## Funded Startups and Innovations
The initial cohort of nine [[innovative_startups_funded_by_wtfund | startups]] funded by the WTFund demonstrate a diverse range of innovative ideas:

*   **Women's Period Concerns** One startup focuses on solving period concerns and discomforts, aiming to empower women <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>.
*   **Pixi: Bringing Life to Toys** Based in Jaipur, Pixi is developing technology to "breathe life into toys" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:22">[00:01:22]</a>.
*   **Healthy Snacks** An initiative crafting healthier chips from nuts, stemming from a desire for "junk but healthier" food options <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:58">[00:01:58]</a>.
*   **Urban Animal: Dog DNA Testing** Urban Animal has launched India's first dog DNA test, with a mission to help pet parents identify diseases earlier and improve dogs' quality of life, aspiring to increase dog lifespans by up to three years over the next decade <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:17">[00:02:17]</a>.
*   **Bio-computer: DNA for Data Storage** A bio-computer project is exploring the use of DNA as a method for storing data to make data centers more efficient <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:38">[00:02:38]</a>.
*   **Early Gastric Cancer Technology** A team of third-year MBBS students at Government Medical College in Chandigarh has received USFDA Breakthrough Device Designation for their early gastric cancer technology <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:48">[00:02:48]</a>.
*   **Cloud Computer for Creative Professionals** This project is building a cloud workstation specifically for creative individuals, accessible from various devices like iPads or MacBooks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:02">[00:03:02]</a>.
*   **AI-Powered Sales Research** A solution designed to help sales professionals by researching the internet for them, structuring insights into a concise one-pager to aid in sales interactions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:14">[00:03:14]</a>.
*   **Sustainable Protein Production** Starting with pet nutrition, this company aims to produce protein or food on a larger scale in previously inaccessible regions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:31">[00:03:31]</a>, focusing on converting unused carbon in carbohydrate form into more efficiently produced protein <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:42">[00:03:42]</a>.
