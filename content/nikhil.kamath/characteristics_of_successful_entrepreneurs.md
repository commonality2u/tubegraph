Several key [[character_traits_of_successful_entrepreneurs | characteristics]] contribute to [[success_factors_for_entrepreneurs | entrepreneurial success]]:

## Core Attributes

*   **Contrarian Thinking**
    Being [[character_traits_of_successful_entrepreneurs | contrarian]] is considered a highly valuable trait for an [[entrepreneurship_and_business_advice | entrepreneur]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:18">[00:00:18]</a>.
*   **Risk-Taking Ability**
    [[success_factors_for_entrepreneurs | Entrepreneurial success]] often comes down to the ability to take risks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:35">[00:00:35]</a>.
*   **Profit Orientation**
    Profit is essential, as "money only comes home when the business makes profit" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:24">[00:00:24]</a>.

## Business Acumen

*   **Innovation and Differentiation**
    For a business, such as a clothing brand, it's crucial to "find a difference" to stand out <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:32">[00:00:32]</a>.
*   **Growth Mindset**
    The name "Rare Rabbit" for a brand was chosen because a rabbit "multiplies the fastest in the world," reflecting a desire for rapid multiplication and growth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>. The founder noted they had "multiplied Thrice" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>.

## Personal Drive

*   **Pivotal Life Events as Catalysts**
    Significant personal events, such as having a child, can become a catalyst for an [[inspirational_entrepreneurial_journeys | entrepreneur's]] work <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:03">[00:01:03]</a>.
