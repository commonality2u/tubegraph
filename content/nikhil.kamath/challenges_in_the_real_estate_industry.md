## Key Challenges

*   **High Risk and Bold Decision-Making**
    Success in real estate necessitates taking bold calls and a significant amount of risk <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>.
*   **Legal Complexities**
    A substantial proportion of legal disputes in India are land-related, with estimates suggesting that 70% to 80% of all court cases fall into this category <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:35">[00:00:35]</a>. This highlights the intricate legal landscape and potential for disputes within the industry.

## Required Mindset

Given the demanding nature of the business, individuals in real estate must cultivate a passion for what they do or "learn to love" the challenges involved <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:48">[00:00:48]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:09">[00:01:09]</a>.
