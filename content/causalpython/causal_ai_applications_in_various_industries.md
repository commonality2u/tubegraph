## Cross-Cutting Challenges and Opportunities

Several common [[Causality and AI Challenges and Opportunities|challenges]] emerged across different applications of [[causal_ai_and_machine_learning|causal AI]]:

*   **Communication is Key**: Effectively transmitting insights and asking proper questions are crucial for unlocking problems and ensuring project success <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:29">[00:03:29]</a>. This includes communicating with various stakeholders, both technical and non-technical, to ensure everyone is "on the same level" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:30">[00:02:30]</a>.
*   **Stakeholder Buy-in and Understanding**: It can be challenging to get everyone on board and understand [[causal_ai_and_machine_learning|causal methods]] properly <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:21">[00:05:21]</a>. It's important for business stakeholders to grasp the value of answering problems in a causal way <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:57">[00:05:57]</a>, often achieved through careful approaches and analogies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:07">[00:06:07]</a>.
*   **Problem Contextualization and Scope**: Each new problem comes with different factors and settings, making understanding the specific problem and its factors a constant challenge <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:17">[00:03:17]</a>. When designing a [[Causal AI and machine learning|causal analysis]], a key question is where to stop the diagram and what factors are relevant to the scope <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:52">[00:04:52]</a>.
*   **Methodological Nuances**: A key distinction to grasp is between identification and estimation when applying [[causal_ai_and_machine_learning|causal methods]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:30">[00:05:30]</a>. Users must be careful to understand if a method assumes randomized treatment assignment or not <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:44">[00:05:44]</a>.
*   **Dynamic Environments**: Nothing is stationary or static, posing the theoretical question of "what happens when the causal graph changes over time" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:58">[00:02:58]</a>. The constant change in industries, like data science and AI, makes it challenging to keep up with new models, understand their assumptions, and analyze them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:11">[00:08:11]</a>.
*   **Integrating Internal and External Vision**: Bringing external insights and visions into internal company processes and testing them can be difficult <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:13">[00:10:13]</a>.
*   **Convincing Business Leaders**: A significant challenge is convincing business people about the importance of advanced analytics <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:22">[00:11:22]</a>. This often involves resolving conflicts between different departmental goals, such as a CMO wanting to spend more on marketing and a CFO wanting to curtail budgets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:23">[00:17:23]</a>.
*   **Verifying Causal Models**: Ensuring that estimated treatment effects are accurate <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:06">[00:12:06]</a> and that "causal graphs are actually true" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:56">[00:11:56]</a> is a critical challenge.
*   **Resource Limitations**: Despite the breakthrough potential of [[causal_ai_applications_in_business_and_technology|causal AI]], limitations in tools and human resources can hinder the ability to answer all pressing questions quickly and effectively <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:08">[00:19:08]</a>.
*   **Bridging [[Causal AI and machine learning|Causal AI]] with Business Intuition**: Matching [[causal_ai_and_machine_learning|causal AI]] insights with business intuition and integrating business insights into causal models is crucial <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:59">[00:19:59]</a>.

## The Value Proposition of [[Causal AI and machine learning|Causal AI]]

[[Causal AI and machine learning|Causal AI]] offers significant advantages over classical machine learning models by addressing their critical limitations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:00">[00:14:00]</a>. It provides directional flows and feature importance, contributing significantly to explainable AI <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:09">[00:14:09]</a>. This explainability makes it easier to communicate with stakeholders, allowing them to understand "why they want to take certain directions or to go for certain options" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:34">[00:14:34]</a>.

Ultimately, [[Causal AI applications in business and technology|causal AI]] helps differentiate between correlation and actual causation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:50">[00:14:50]</a>, fulfilling a dream for many data scientists <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:56">[00:14:56]</a>.
