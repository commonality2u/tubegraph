His happiness is primarily derived from helping others, to the point where personal achievements, like making money, hold little joy unless shared with loved ones <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:06:00">[03:06:00]</a>.

This [[impact_of_childhood_and_trauma_on_mental_health | state of depersonalization]] is identified as a coping mechanism for trauma, where the mind "unplugs from the reality" of painful experiences <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:34:50">[01:34:50]</a>. It allows him to feel like the trauma happened "20 years back in the past" even when it was recent, as a way to "get used to the change" and survive <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:22:00">[05:22:00]</a>.

*   **Mother's Inconsistent Parenting:** His single mother, described as "always 16 at height," had a series of husbands, often from the same family as his biological father, who used her for visas or money <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:20:00">[02:20:00]</a>. Doc felt he had to "educate" and "care for" his own mother <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:25:00">[02:25:00]</a>. At one point, she "shoved him off" to his grandparents, indicating she wanted to live her "own life" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:47:00">[02:47:00]</a>. She only brought him back when threatened with losing child support payments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:50:00">[02:50:00]</a>.
*   **Grandparents as Father Figures:** While living with his grandparents (ages 6-12), his grandfather provided a "special place" in his heart, showering him with gifts, but did not truly "raise" him or understand him as a person <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:07:00">[02:07:00]</a>.
*   **Childhood Exclusion and Bullying:** Doc faced exclusion and bullying from neighborhood kids, who were told by their parents not to play with him due to rumors about his mother <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:40:00">[01:40:00]</a>. *   **Mother's Murder:** His most recent trauma involved his mother's murder by her last stepfather, who then committed suicide <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:09:00">[03:09:00]</a>. This stepfather was initially seen as a positive influence, bringing "common sense" and genuine love to the family <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:00:00">[04:00:00]</a>. However, his mother's extreme jealousy and unhealthy behavior led to a violent confrontation. Doc witnessed the murder, an event that made him feel he was going to die next <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:47:00">[04:47:00]</a>.
