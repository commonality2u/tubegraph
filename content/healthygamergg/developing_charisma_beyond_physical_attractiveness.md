## What is Charisma?

Contrary to popular belief, charisma is not simply [[the_impact_of_physical_attractiveness_on_relationships | physical attractiveness]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:44">[00:01:44]</a>. Modern research indicates that [[the_impact_of_physical_attractiveness_on_relationships | physical attractiveness]] is merely one of six dimensions of charisma, and it's not even among the most important <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:48">[00:01:48]</a>. There are three attributes that contribute more to charisma than [[the_impact_of_physical_attractiveness_on_relationships | physical attractiveness]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:57">[00:01:57]</a>.

Research from a paper titled "Generic Charisma: Conceptualization and Measurement" shows that [[the_impact_of_physical_attractiveness_on_relationships | attractiveness]] accounts for only 10.5% of the variance between charismatic and non-charismatic individuals <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:36">[00:02:36]</a>. The most significant aspects of charisma are:
*   [[importance_of_communication_skills_in_charisma | Communication skills]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:49">[00:02:49]</a>
*   The ability to attract admiration and inspire <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:55">[00:02:55]</a>
*   Being honest and reliable <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:58">[00:02:58]</a>

These three attributes collectively account for about half of the charisma an individual exudes, and they are entirely unrelated to [[the_impact_of_physical_attractiveness_on_relationships | physical attractiveness]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:10">[00:03:10]</a>.

### [[role_of_vision_and_vision_implementation_in_charisma | Vision and Vision Implementation]]

[[role_of_vision_and_vision_implementation_in_charisma | Vision]] is not about setting concrete targets or goals (e.g., selling $100,000 worth of flowers) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:20">[00:04:20]</a>. Instead, it's an abstract concept, an idea about the direction of one's life (e.g., "we sell beauty" instead of flowers) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:06">[00:05:06]</a>.

#### Why Vision is Difficult to Develop
*   **Societal Conditioning**: Society often actively conditions out the instinct for [[role_of_vision_and_vision_implementation_in_charisma | vision]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:37">[00:05:37]</a>. Educational systems, for example, function as "factories" designed to create standardized competencies rather than cultivating a [[role_of_vision_and_vision_implementation_in_charisma | vision]] for life <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:06">[00:06:06]</a>.
*   **Uncertainty of the Future**: In an unpredictable world with frequent "once-in-a-lifetime crises," it's psychologically challenging to invest in a long-term [[role_of_vision_and_vision_implementation_in_charisma | vision]] because it feels like setting oneself up for disappointment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:07">[00:07:07]</a>.

Despite these challenges, cultivating a [[role_of_vision_and_vision_implementation_in_charisma | vision]] is a key part of becoming more charismatic <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:11">[00:08:11]</a>. In a chaotic world, encountering someone with [[role_of_vision_and_vision_implementation_in_charisma | vision]] for their life is incredibly inspiring <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:28">[00:08:28]</a>.

#### How to Develop Vision
1.  **Discard Goals**: [[role_of_vision_and_vision_implementation_in_charisma | Vision]] is not about goals <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:44">[00:08:44]</a>. Instead of "lose 5 pounds," think "live a life of health" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:03">[00:09:03]</a>.
2.  **Focus on Ideals**: Think about the ideals you want to live towards, embracing "floofy and uncalculated" ideas that you might have previously dismissed as unachievable <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:35">[00:09:35]</a>.
3.  **Clarification**: Reflect on what you want your life to look like when you look back on it, how you want it to be perceived, and what would make you happy on your deathbed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:16">[00:10:16]</a>. This involves introspective work to clarify your [[role_of_vision_and_vision_implementation_in_charisma | vision]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:02">[00:11:02]</a>.
4.  **Structure (When, Where, How)**: For your ideal, don't focus on "what" the goal is. Instead, focus on the "where, when, and how" of the process <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:17">[00:12:17]</a>. This emphasizes the process over the outcome <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:30">[00:12:30]</a>.
5.  **Strategic Thinking**: When setbacks occur, think strategically about how to move past them and continue towards your [[role_of_vision_and_vision_implementation_in_charisma | vision]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:43">[00:14:43]</a>. This involves a relentless strategic approach to always moving forward, which others find attractive because it demonstrates perseverance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:53">[00:14:53]</a>.

*   **Temper with Compassion**: Draw inspiration from Eastern philosophies like Satya (truthfulness) being subject to Ahimsa (non-violence or compassion) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:43">[00:18:43]</a>.
