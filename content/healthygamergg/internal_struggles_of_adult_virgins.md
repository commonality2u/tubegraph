## The Virginity Trap
Life involves social milestones, such as dating in high school or college, and losing one's virginity by a certain age <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:40">[00:09:40]</a>. Missing these milestones can lead to two main problems:
1.  **Insecurity and Identity**: Each missed milestone can foster an identity of being "unlovable" or "unable to get laid" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:16">[00:10:16]</a>. This internal identity then sabotages future attempts at social connection by manifesting as a lack of confidence in social situations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:30">[00:10:30]</a>.
2.  **Lack of Social Skills (XP)**: Individuals who miss these milestones also miss opportunities to develop social skills and "level up" their social experience <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:59">[00:10:59]</a>. Even if they manage to overcome their [[struggles_with_selfworth_and_selfesteem | shame]], a lack of practical social skills (e.g., flirting) can still lead to failure and reinforce existing [[struggles_with_selfworth_and_selfesteem | insecurities]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:23">[00:11:23]</a>.

This creates a self-reinforcing cycle: missing milestones leads to [[struggles_with_selfworth_and_selfesteem | insecurity]], which makes it harder to hit future milestones, further increasing [[struggles_with_selfworth_and_selfesteem | insecurity]] and making it difficult to catch up <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:26">[00:25:26]</a>.
