## Psychological Concepts

The experience aligns with the psychological term **depersonalization** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:33:48">[01:33:48]</a>, which is a dissociative symptom where an individual feels detached from their own body, thoughts, feelings, or actions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:34:16">[01:34:16]</a>. This is distinct from **derealization**, where the world around the individual feels unreal <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:33:59">[01:33:59]</a>. Depersonalization is understood as a survival mechanism where the mind "unplugs from the reality" of overwhelming or traumatic situations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:35:20">[01:35:20]</a>.
