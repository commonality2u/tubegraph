## The [[role_of_parental_support_in_academic_success | Role of Parental Support]]

### Creating a Conducive Environment
Dr. NR was a day scholar, and his family, particularly his grandmother, played a vital role in his preparation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:43">[00:23:43]</a>. His grandmother managed daily needs like food, ensuring he was well taken care of <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:45">[00:23:45]</a>.

The family actively minimized distractions:
*   They cut down on watching TV, movies, and matches <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:30:06">[00:30:06]</a>.
*   Strict instructions were followed to reduce noise, such as managing cooking schedules around his study times <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:21">[00:24:21]</a>.

This dedicated family effort helped create a serene and focused study environment that would be challenging to replicate in a hostel setting <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:29">[00:24:29]</a>. A [[the_importance_of_support_systems_in_exam_success | positive environment]] significantly contributes to successful exam preparation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:38">[00:24:38]</a>.
