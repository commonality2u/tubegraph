While initially not using the question bank extensively, Dr. Dian found it to be a "game changer" in his final year <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:02:00">[06:02:00]</a>. The question bank provided:

## Grand Tests and Test Series: Simulating Exam Conditions

Grand tests were one of the most utilized tools by Dr. Dian <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:27:00">[08:27:00]</a>. His strategy evolved:
*   **Early acclimatization:** In his third and final year, his primary aim was to get accustomed to the format of grand tests, including answering 200 questions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:43:00">[08:43:00]</a>.
*   **Testing guessing abilities:** He used initial grand tests to test his intuitive and educated guessing abilities, even when not fully prepared <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:12:00">[09:12:00]</a>.
*   **Regular, live participation:** During his internship, he took grand tests regularly and always participated when they were live to get an accurate All India Rank, which helped him gauge his preparation effectively <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:27:00">[09:27:00]</a>. ## [[effective_revision_techniques_for_medical_exams|Effective Revision Techniques for Medical Exams]]: Note-taking and Bookmarks

[[revision_techniques_and_managing_study_notes|Having precise and concise notes]] is essential for quick revision <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:40:00">[10:40:00]</a>.
*   **Printed notes for efficiency:** Dr. Dian was not a "passionate note-taker" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:58:00">[10:58:00]</a>; instead, he used Marrow's printed notes and leveraged the active spaces provided to jot down essential points <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="11:10:00">[11:10:00]</a>. ## Staying Updated with Evolving Information

Given the continuous evolution of medical knowledge, staying updated is crucial, especially for competitive exams like INI CET <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="14:02:00">[14:02:00]</a>.
*   **Leveraging update sections:** Dr. Dian regularly used Marrow's "updates" section to keep abreast of newer developments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="13:52:00">[13:52:00]</a>.
*   **Targeted updates:** Instead of rewatching entire updated editions, he focused on areas with known updates, such as in Pharmacology or Surgery, sometimes checking with juniors or directly opening notes to add new information <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="14:14:00">[14:14:00]</a>.
*   **Sticking to a core source:** He emphasized sticking to his original preparation source and only adding information as needed, avoiding repeated viewing of videos to prevent confusion <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="14:50:00">[14:50:00]</a>. The core of the subject remains constant; it's the small, evolving changes that make a huge difference at top-tier competitive levels <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="15:20:00">[15:20:00]</a>.
