## Prevention of Malaria Relapse

Primaquine is specifically used for the prevention of malaria relapse, rather than for the symptomatic treatment of active uncomplicated malaria <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:32:00">[04:32:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:05:00">[05:05:00]</a>.
