Upon arrival on the island, the first task is to assess available resources and potential dangers. The group, aware of imminent darkness, prioritized [[survival_strategies_and_skills_in_closed_environments | building a shelter]] to have a safe place to rest at night. ## Addressing Environmental Hazards

It was crucial to recognize and address environmental hazards immediately. The island in question was home to the world’s most dangerous tree, which secretes sap capable of causing severe skin reactions [<a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>]. Identifying such hazards ensured that the group avoided setting up their camp near these trees.

With limited resources, meticulous management became essential, particularly concerning water and food reserves.
