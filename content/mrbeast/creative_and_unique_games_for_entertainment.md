## Skill-Based Challenges

    Contestants attempt to hit a bullseye with a dart to win $20,000 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>.

*   **Half-Court Basketball Shot**
    A classic basketball challenge, where a half-court shot can win $30,000 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>.
