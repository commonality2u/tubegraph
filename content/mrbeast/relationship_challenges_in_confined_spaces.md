A significant twist was introduced on day ten: a daily alarm would sound at a random time, requiring both Hugo and Rayne to place their hands on separate scanners within one minute to prevent their prize money from incinerating <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:22">[00:03:22]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:33">[00:03:33]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:41">[00:03:41]</a>.
