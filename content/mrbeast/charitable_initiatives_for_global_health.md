## The Problem and Solution
Half of all cases of blindness worldwide are due to conditions that can be remedied with a 10-minute surgical procedure <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>. The surgery addresses cloudiness in the eye's lens by using a tiny vacuum to remove the clouded lens and replace it with an artificial one <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:54">[00:00:54]</a>. This relatively simple procedure allows patients to regain their sight <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:58">[00:00:58]</a>.
