## $50,000 Vacation in Paris

This vacation in Paris offered unparalleled private access to famous landmarks and [[unique_cultural_experiences | unique cultural experiences]]:
*   **Private Transportation** The trip began with a completely private luxury ferry ride <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:11">[00:03:11]</a>.
*   **Eiffel Tower Exclusivity** The highlight was having the entire Eiffel Tower completely to themselves, a rare privilege as it is usually packed with people <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:52">[00:03:52]</a>.
*   **Exclusive Dining** Guests dined at a world-class restaurant on the first floor of the Eiffel Tower, also reserved exclusively for them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:26">[00:04:26]</a>.
*   **Top-Level Access** They were allowed to go to the very top of the tower, a point normally inaccessible to tourists <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:35">[00:05:35]</a>.
*   **Controlling the Lights** The vacation even included the ability to control the Eiffel Tower's iconic lights, turning them on for a "disco show" and then completely off, plunging the landmark into darkness <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:12">[00:06:12]</a>.
