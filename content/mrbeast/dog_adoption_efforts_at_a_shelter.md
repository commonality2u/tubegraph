## Marketing Campaigns to Promote Adoption

A significant [[impact_of_marketing_campaigns_for_pet_adoption | marketing campaign]] was initiated to increase awareness and traffic to the shelter <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:03">[00:00:03]</a>.
*   **Billboards:** An initial billboard was purchased to draw visitors to the shelter <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>. Later, 50 more billboards were purchased and put up to further boost visibility <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:43">[00:05:43]</a>.
*   **Flyers:** Flyers were created and distributed throughout the town <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:24">[00:00:24]</a>.
*   **Box Truck:** A large box truck was utilized to help the dog shelter <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:34">[00:00:34]</a>.
*   **Professional Photos:** Professional photos were taken of all the dogs to be used on the billboards and other marketing materials <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:12">[00:03:12]</a>.
*   **Radio Interview:** A radio station interview was conducted by Ethan, who was chosen for his anonymity, ensuring people adopted dogs out of genuine desire rather than celebrity influence <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:02">[00:06:02]</a>.
*   **Costumed Mascot:** An individual dressed in a dog costume ("Puddles") held a "Cute Doggos Inside" sign outside the shelter to attract attention <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:27">[00:06:27]</a>.

## Adoption Process and Incentives

The team monitored cameras to track the [[impact_of_marketing_campaigns_for_pet_adoption | impact of marketing campaigns]] and potential adopters <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:17">[00:01:17]</a>. Adopters were vetted through applications and other procedures to ensure the dogs went to suitable homes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:07">[00:09:07]</a>.

To encourage [[successful_adoption_stories_and_incentives | successful adoption stories]], various incentives were provided:
*   **Free Dog Food:** Adopters received 10 years' worth of free dog food <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:42">[00:01:42]</a>, and later, unlimited dog food was offered <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:54">[00:13:54]</a>.
*   **Monetary Gifts:** Some adopters received $5,000 as a thank you <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:37">[00:02:37]</a>.
*   **Gift Cards:** Large Petco gift cards, often $10,000, were given to new owners <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:06">[00:05:06]</a>.
*   **Waived Adoption Fees:** Adoption fees were waived, making the process free for new owners <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:57">[00:04:57]</a>.
*   **Pet Insurance:** Some adoptions included 10 years of pet insurance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:24">[00:08:24]</a>.
*   **Toys and Supplies:** Adopters received various dog toys and supplies as part of their "parting gift" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:13">[00:13:13]</a>.
