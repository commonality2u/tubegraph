## Conquering the Burj Khalifa
The ultimate experience in Dubai involves climbing the Burj Khalifa, the tallest building on Earth, standing at 2,700 feet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="15:35:00">[15:35:00]</a>.

### The Ascent
The journey to the peak begins with a series of elevators that ascend over 160 stories into the sky <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="15:43:00">[15:43:00]</a>. For the remaining 900 feet, the climb must be completed manually via ladders <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="15:51:00">[15:51:00]</a>. This part of the climb is a significant physical challenge, taking over an hour <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="16:06:00">[16:06:00]</a>.
