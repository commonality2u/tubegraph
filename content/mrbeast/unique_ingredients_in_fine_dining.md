Fine dining experiences often feature [[expensive_food_experiences | high cost luxury foods]] that utilize rare, premium, or exceptionally prepared ingredients to justify their exorbitant prices <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:12">[00:01:12]</a>. ## Precious Metals

[[edible_gold_in_gourmet_dishes | Edible gold]] is a recurring element in various [[gourmet_dishes_with_gold_and_truffles | gourmet dishes with gold and truffles]] that command high prices <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:34">[00:00:34]</a>:
*   **Golden Wings** Ainsworth's famous 24 karat gold wings are one such example <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>.
*   **Luxe Milkshake** This [[highend_ice_cream_creations | highend ice cream creation]] includes 23 karat [[edible_gold_in_gourmet_dishes | edible gold]] alongside Tahesian vanilla ice cream and donkey caramel sauce <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:04">[00:01:04]</a>.
*   **Grilled Cheese** A $250 grilled cheese sandwich features 23 karat [[edible_gold_in_gourmet_dishes | edible gold]] with a French homeman loaf of bread and Kaco Cavalo Poleico cheese <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:41">[00:01:41]</a>.
*   **Golden Opulence Sundae** This $1,000 sundae contains three scoops of Tahesian vanilla ice cream rolled in 23 karat [[edible_gold_in_gourmet_dishes | edible gold]] and a chocolate sauce made from the rarest chocolate in the world <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:22">[00:02:22]</a>.

## Rare Meats and Seafood

*   **Black Wagyu Short Ribs** These $6,000 short ribs are seared, marinated for two days, and slow-cooked for 8 to 12 hours <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:12">[00:08:12]</a>.

*   **Smoked Sea Salt** Used as a finishing touch on the $70,000 pizza <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:48">[00:10:48]</a>.
