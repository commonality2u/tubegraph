## Budget-Friendly Experience: Camel Riding
The cheapest experience found was a camel ride for $1, which lasted for two minutes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:31">[00:00:31]</a>. ## $10,000 Experience: Hot Air Balloon Dinner
For $10,000, participants had dinner 3,000 feet in the sky in a hot air balloon, where a Michelin-star chef used the balloon's flame to cook the gourmet meal <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:02">[00:01:02]</a>. The experience was deemed very beautiful and impressive <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:00">[00:02:00]</a>.
