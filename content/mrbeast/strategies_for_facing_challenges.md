## Embrace the Fear

Instead of denying his fears, Mack acknowledges them as he faces each challenge. This acknowledgment not only helps in preparing mentally but also in strategizing effectively. By recognizing his [[overcoming_fears | fears]], Mack can focus his energy on overcoming them rather than being surprised or overwhelmed by them. One of Mack's notable strategies is his ability to assess risks while maintaining focus on the prize. For instance, during the [[fear_of_heights_and_overcoming_challenges | fear of heights]] challenge, he chooses to freefall while trusting that his safety is assured, despite the unsettling sensation of being unattached to anything <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:24">[00:01:24]</a>. His decision reflects an understanding that [[problemsolving_strategies_in_highpressure_situations | calculated risks]] are necessary when aiming for a significant reward.

## Stay Calm Under Pressure

Throughout the challenges, maintaining calmness in high-pressure situations is key. Whether underwater salvaging money from a sinking car <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:07">[00:04:07]</a> or deciding when to ring a bell to be unearthed from a coffin <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:52">[00:13:52]</a>, Mack's calm demeanor allows him to think clearly and make rational decisions, exemplifying the importance of [[decision_making_under_pressure | composure]] in overcoming challenges.

## Motivational Leverage

Mack uses his past experiences as motivation to push forward. Having once lost $800,000 on a [[challenge_with_a_monetary_reward | similar challenge]], he views this as a second chance at redemption <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:05">[00:03:05]</a>.
