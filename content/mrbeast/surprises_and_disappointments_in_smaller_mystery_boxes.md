The world of mystery boxes often contains a mix of excitement, unexpected items, and sometimes, considerable disappointment. ## Lower-Priced Boxes ($100 - $700)

The initial mystery boxes explored started at the lower end of the spectrum, revealing diverse contents for relatively modest prices.

*   **$100 Mystery Box:** This box contained a candle that smelled like "Nature's nuts" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:29">[00:00:29]</a>. The reaction was one of amused confusion, clarifying it was not the presenter's "nuts" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>.
*   **$300 Mystery Box:** Inside was a Bob Ross Monopoly game <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:45">[00:00:45]</a>. This was considered "better" than the contents of the $400 box <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>.
*   **$400 Mystery Box:** This box held "a whole lot of magic stuff" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:54">[00:00:54]</a>, prompting the question of whether viewers would prefer Bob Ross memorabilia or magical items <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:55">[00:00:55]</a>.
*   **$500 Mystery Box:** Given to someone's mom, this box contained headphones <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:08">[00:01:08]</a>, eliciting a positive reaction and desire for the item <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:17">[00:01:17]</a>.
*   **$700 Mystery Box:** This box was filled with Chick-fil-A sauce, much of which was either expired or nearing its expiration date within two days <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:30">[00:01:30]</a>. The recipient expressed significant disappointment, stating there was "no way this is worth $700" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:33">[00:01:33]</a>. This experience led to a general recommendation against buying mystery boxes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:41">[00:01:41]</a>.

### Conclusion on Smaller Boxes

The unboxings demonstrated that the value and nature of items in smaller mystery boxes varied wildly, from minor novelties and custom collectibles to significant losses or surprisingly valuable finds. The contents often reflected the whimsical or sometimes disappointing nature of the "mystery" element.
