## What is Competitive Eating?

Competitive eating is a sport where participants compete against each other or the clock to consume large quantities of food within a specific timeframe. Unlike eating for sustenance, competitive eating requires strategy, mental fortitude, and physical capability to quickly consume mass amounts of food without inducing discomfort or illness.
