## Diverse Laser Maze Environments

The competition features multiple distinct laser obstacle courses, each with unique layouts and challenges:

*   **Level Four: Giant Death Beams** This level introduces "giant blue death lasers" that periodically fire, requiring contestants to time their movements carefully to avoid being "cremated" while also avoiding red lasers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:07">[00:07:07]</a>.

The lasers briefly disappear every 30 seconds, offering a risky "easy way out" by running instead of jumping <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:09">[00:13:09]</a>.
*   **Level Seven (Re-entry): The Laser Maze of Choice** Contestants face a maze with "a billion lasers" and two paths, one being a dead end <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:07">[00:16:07]</a>. *   **Level Eight: Friend-Designed Lasers** This level features only three lasers, each placed by one of the host's friends (Nolan's "up and down," Tareq's "side to side," and Karl's combination laser) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:54">[00:19:54]</a>.
