## The $500,000 Boat Car
The "$500,000 boat car" is a vehicle designed to operate both on land and in water <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:17">[00:04:17]</a>. On land, it appears as a normal car <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:22">[00:04:22]</a>. Its transformation into a boat involves simply driving it into the water <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:27">[00:04:27]</a>. Once in the water, a button is pressed to activate its aquatic mode, allowing it to be driven like a normal car on the water <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:45">[00:04:45]</a>.
