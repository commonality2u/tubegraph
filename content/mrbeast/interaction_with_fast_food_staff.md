From: [[mrbeast]] <br/> 

Throughout a challenge to go through a fast-food drive-through 1000 times, the participants had various interactions with the restaurant staff <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:01">[00:00:01]</a>. Initially, the staff were standard, but as the day progressed, their reactions and the nature of the interactions evolved.

## Initial Encounters

The challenge began with standard [[drivethrough_experience_and_challenges | drive-through]] orders, with staff welcoming them to Hardee's <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:12">[00:00:12]</a>. Participants ordered regular menu items like a number six <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>, apple turnover <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>, and the "hometown special" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:55">[00:00:55]</a>.

## Unusual Requests and Situations

As the challenge wore on, the interactions became more unconventional:
*   **Odd Transport** Chris offered to mow the grass for a biscuit, which the attendant accepted <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:47">[00:01:47]</a>. Mr. Beast ordered from a golf cart <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:12">[00:02:12]</a>, and later, the team used a limousine for laps <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:23">[00:06:23]</a>. Chris even attempted to go through on a girl's bike <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:18">[00:04:18]</a>.
*   **Unique Orders** Participants made unusual requests such as changing the clock to Pacific time to get onion rings before they were typically served <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:24">[00:03:24]</a>, ordering only a bun <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:59">[00:08:59]</a>, a patty <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:07">[00:09:07]</a>, 10 pickles <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:17">[00:09:17]</a>, or a single slice of cheese <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:23">[00:09:23]</a>. At one point, Mr. Beast ordered items alphabetically, including "upcharge" and "zero Coke" (Coke Zero) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:13">[00:11:13]</a>.
*   **Chandler's Stint** Chandler briefly "worked" the [[drivethrough_experience_and_challenges | drive-through]] window as an attendant <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:55">[00:02:55]</a>.

## Staff Reactions

Initially, staff appeared to find the constant returns amusing <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:03">[00:01:03]</a>. One attendant described the experience as "definitely different" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:46">[00:05:46]</a>. As the day progressed, the staff seemed to have accepted the "weirdness" of the situation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:04">[00:09:04]</a>.

## Donations and Support

To avoid wasting food from so many orders, the team began ordering only bottles of water and orange juice for some laps, which were later donated <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:05">[00:05:05]</a>. They also ordered a significant amount of food for the local police department <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:12">[00:05:12]</a> and the hospital <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:10">[00:10:10]</a>.

## Conclusion of the Challenge

Towards the end of the 1000 laps, the participants noted that the staff seemed tired and less engaged <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:26">[00:12:26]</a>. On the final lap, Mr. Beast thanked the attendant, Latonia, for "putting up with our crap" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:48">[00:14:48]</a> and shared that he would "never eating fast food until the day I die" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:33">[00:14:33]</a>. The staff applauded and cheered as the team completed their 1000th lap <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:52">[00:14:52]</a>, and Mr. Beast gave them [[giving_away_cash_and_prizes_to_restaurant_customers | money]] for their help <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:05">[00:15:05]</a>.
