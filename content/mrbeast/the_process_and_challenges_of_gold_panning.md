## Statistical Outlook on Gold Panning

Despite stories of early prospectors striking rich in gold rushes, the reality is that modern gold panning doesn't equate to significant financial gain for most individuals. The [[economic implications | earnings, while theoretically promising]], are mostly achieved by hobbyists with love for the craft rather than those seeking a serious income.
