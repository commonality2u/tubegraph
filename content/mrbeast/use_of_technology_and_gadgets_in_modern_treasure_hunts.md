## Tracking and Surveillance

A primary application of [[use_of_technology_in_tracking | technology]] in these challenges is real-time tracking and observation:

*   **Phone Tracking** A professional bounty hunter utilized a [[use_of_tracking_devices | tracker]] placed on the target's phone to pinpoint his exact location and facilitate pursuit <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>. This allowed the bounty hunter to know where the target was, even when he changed vehicles <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:15">[00:01:15]</a> or hid in buildings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:59">[00:01:59]</a>. However, the target sometimes exploited this by leaving the tracked phone behind as a decoy <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:17">[00:03:17]</a>, leading the pursuers astray <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:31">[00:03:31]</a>. The [[use_of_tracking_devices | tracker]]'s continued signal even when the target was on a boat helped the bounty hunters maintain pursuit <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:00">[00:11:00]</a>.
*   **Security Cameras** The target employed security cameras linked to his phone to monitor the bounty hunters' movements, particularly when resting at a rented mansion <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:39">[00:09:39]</a>. This surveillance allowed him to anticipate their arrival and plan escapes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:03">[00:10:03]</a>, such as fleeing to a boat <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:05">[00:10:05]</a>. He also used cameras to monitor their location within a movie theater, gaining a head start when they were on the roof <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:23">[00:05:23]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:07">[00:06:07]</a>. The bounty hunters also noticed a suspicious camera on the roof, suggesting they were aware of the target's surveillance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:36">[00:05:36]</a>.

## Communication

Mobile phones are essential for communication between team members and to interact with the target:

*   **Strategic Calls** The target made phone calls to taunt the bounty hunters and misdirect them, such as pretending to be trapped in a porta-potty lifted by a helicopter <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:35">[00:08:35]</a>.
*   **Coordination** Team members on both sides used phones to coordinate movements and share updates on the chase <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:34">[00:00:34]</a>.

## Transportation and Diversion Tactics

While not strictly "gadgets," various modes of transport are utilized strategically, sometimes as part of a technological diversion:

*   **Multiple Vehicles** The target frequently switched between cars, including buying new ones on the fly <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:45">[00:00:45]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:47">[00:01:47]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:47">[00:10:47]</a>. *   **Helicopter Diversion** In one elaborate trap, a helicopter was used to lift a porta-potty, creating a visual distraction to make the bounty hunters believe the target was inside, while he actually escaped in a hidden car <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:48">[00:07:48]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:27">[00:08:27]</a>. ## Infrastructure Manipulation

The target demonstrated clever manipulation of existing infrastructure to aid his escape:

*   **Vault Installation** A secure vault, similar to those found in banks, was installed in a remote warehouse <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:45">[00:01:45]</a>. This served as a temporary, seemingly impenetrable hiding spot with a secret escape tunnel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:31">[00:02:31]</a>.
*   **Fire Alarm Disconnection** A fire alarm in a movie theater was strategically disconnected beforehand by contacting the fire department <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:48">[00:05:48]</a>. Activating it later caused chaos and created another window for escape <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:51">[00:05:51]</a>.
