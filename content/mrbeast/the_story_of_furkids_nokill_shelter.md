## The Mission and Impact of Furkids

Furkids is dedicated to rescuing and providing safe haven to domesticated animals, ensuring they receive the care, love, and medical treatment they need. As a no-kill shelter, their commitment is to never euthanize a healthy animal they rescue. They operate multiple facilities, working tirelessly to rehome dogs and cats that come through their doors.

In their 19 years of operation, Furkids has built a reputation for genuinely caring about animal welfare and helping thousands of pets find forever homes. <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="15:20">[15:20]</a> The collaboration with MrBeast helped bring unprecedented attention to their cause, marking a milestone in their operational history.
