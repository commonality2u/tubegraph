## Types of Challenges and Decisions

### Cash vs. Mystery Prize
One common [[challenge_format_and_rules | challenge format and rules]] involves choosing between a guaranteed sum of money and a mystery prize, often represented by a key <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:06">[00:00:06]</a>.

*   **Javier's Choice**: Javier was given 10 seconds to decide between $10,000 cash or a key to an expensive item <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:06">[00:00:06]</a>. Despite not knowing what the key unlocked, he chose the key based on a "gut feeling" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:28">[00:00:28]</a>. This decision proved successful, as the key opened a brand new car valued at $26,000 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:55">[00:00:55]</a>, significantly more than the cash alternative. His advice: "Listen to your gut" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:13">[00:01:13]</a>.
