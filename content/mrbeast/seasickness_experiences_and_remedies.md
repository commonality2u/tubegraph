### Motion Sickness Management

Though the team experienced substantial discomfort and related incidents, these occurrences offer insight into potential seasickness remedies:
- **Seeking Calm Waters:** As demonstrated, relocating to mildly turbulent areas reduced nausea significantly.
- **Rest and Relaxation:** When possible, lying down or positioning oneself along the centerline of the vessel can reduce motion perception.
