## Initial Driving Experience

Driving a Lamborghini for the first time can be a challenging experience, especially for an inexperienced driver <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:33">[00:01:33]</a>. Under suboptimal conditions, such as pouring rain, the experience can be particularly daunting <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:38">[00:01:38]</a>. An initial attempt at driving resulted in a significant amount of smoke and loud noise from the back of the car, causing concern <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:50">[00:01:50]</a>. The car was later noted to be much smoother to drive after overcoming initial difficulties <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:00">[00:02:00]</a>. For ease of driving, a preference for an automatic transmission was expressed due to the car's high value, reducing the risk of error <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:03">[00:02:03]</a>.

## Social Perceptions and Reactions

Owning a luxury car like a Lamborghini can lead to varied public reactions:
*   **Indifference:** A McDonald's employee showed no particular reaction to the car <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:19">[00:02:19]</a>.
*   **Excitement:** Children were observed to be "freaking out" and complimenting the car <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:37">[00:02:37]</a>.
*   **Lack of Reaction in Luxury Settings:** When the driver presented as "homeless" while buying expensive items, retail staff did not react to the luxury car or the unusual scenario <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:17">[00:03:17]</a>.

The ownership of such an expensive vehicle can also be a source of stress <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:50">[00:13:50]</a>. Furthermore, concerns arose about friendships being based solely on the possession of the Lamborghini, leading to a desire to distance from such relationships <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:56">[00:13:56]</a>. This perspective highlights a sentiment of disinterest in owning super expensive cars <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:00">[00:14:00]</a>.

## "Living" and Camping in the Car

The idea of "living" in a Lamborghini was considered, including the question of what it feels like to sleep in it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:29">[00:00:29]</a>. Although the car itself isn't suitable for living, the team did camp out for seven hours in a parking garage with the Lamborghini nearby while waiting for a car they had accidentally hit <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:39">[00:13:39]</a>.
