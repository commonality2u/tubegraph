This article details a "last to leave" [[challenge_to_win_money | challenge to win money]] set within the world's largest cereal bowl.

## The Setup

The challenge was set in the "world's largest cereal bowl," which was built and filled with 2000 gallons of powdered milk and expired cereal <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:00">[00:00:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:09">[00:00:09]</a>. The host noted the smell of milk and described the situation as "innovation" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>. After the bowl was filled, additional cereal was added <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:46">[00:01:46]</a>, officially making it the "world's largest bowl of cereal" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:54">[00:01:54]</a>.

## The Challenge and Prize

This [[survival_challenge_for_money | survival challenge for money]] required participants to be the last one to leave the giant cereal bowl <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:36">[00:00:36]</a>. The last person remaining would win $10,000 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:40">[00:00:40]</a>. Additionally, the team member (recruiter) who picked the winner would receive $5,000 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:53">[00:00:53]</a>.
