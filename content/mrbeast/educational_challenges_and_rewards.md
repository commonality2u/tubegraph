## The Challenge of Academic Progression

Starting from first grade, participants had to pass a series of quizzes and tests tailored to the educational level they were visiting. Each grade presented unique challenges representative of typical curriculum milestones. For instance, basic reading and arithmetic were tested in the first grade, whereas more complex subjects like chemistry and economics were reserved for the higher grades.

3. **Adapting to Increasing Complexity** - As the participants progressed to higher grades, such as fifth grade, the expectation was to solve complex problems and engage in critical thinking through historical context and abstract concepts. 4. **Specialized Knowledge** - High school levels focused on specialized subjects, such as the challenge of understanding chemistry and economics, highlighting the need for specialized knowledge at advanced stages of education <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:45">[00:13:45]</a>.
