Challenges presented often feature escalating [[moneybased_competitions_and_incentives | money-based competitions]] with significant [[prize_incentives and participant decisions | prize incentives]] to motivate participants. The prize pool typically starts at $10,000 and increases by $10,000 each time a contestant fails to complete their task <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:07">[00:00:07]</a>. This steady increase aims to heighten the stakes and [[contestants_motivations_for_winning | contestant motivation]].
