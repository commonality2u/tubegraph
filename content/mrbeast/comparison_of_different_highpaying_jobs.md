## Lowest Paying Job: Gold Panning

Gold panning is presented as a job that can yield as little as $1 per year <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:19">[00:00:19]</a>. The process involves placing dirt in a mechanism and using shaking and water to filter gold to the bottom <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:25">[00:00:25]</a>. After five hours of searching, only a small nugget worth $0.02 was found <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:34">[00:00:34]</a>, demonstrating the low income potential of this activity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:43">[00:00:43]</a>.
