## The $1 House: Barely Functional Shelter

We begin our journey with a house valued at just $1. This nearly dilapidated shelter offers minimal comforts and structural stability. The residence is so precarious that it risks being swept away with the next storm. The interior is sparsely equipped, providing only a recliner, a mattress, and part of a floor. A significant leap from the $1 house, the $1 million residence offers a glimpse into the lifestyle of a millionaire. Spacious kitchens, multiple bedrooms, and luxurious bathrooms with extra-large bathtubs define this home. As we venture into the $15 million estate, the luxury significantly amplifies with astonishing views of Los Angeles and amenities that include a personal gym, guest bedrooms, entertainment areas, and more.
