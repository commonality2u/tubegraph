## The $70,000 Golden Pizza

This pizza, which costs a staggering $70,000, takes luxury dining to a new level [<a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:10">09:10</a>]. The creation is crafted by a private chef who typically serves billionaires, indicating the exclusivity of its ingredients and preparation [[goldinfused_foods | with ingredients like gold]].

### Ingredients and Preparation

The pizza boasts a lavish list of ingredients:
- **Gold Leaf Crust**: The pizza's crust is encrusted with a full ounce of gold leaf, enhancing its opulence [<a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:44">09:44</a>].
- **Parmesan Basham Sauce**: Aged for ten years, this sauce serves as a luxurious base. 
- **Japanese Beef**: This ingredient has been marinated overnight in a $10,000 bottle of grape juice, offering an unparalleled depth of flavor [<a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:56">09:56</a>].
- **Hudson Valley Foie Gras**: After being sautéed, it is flamed with a $6,000 bottle of apple juice for an exquisite taste [<a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:02">10:02</a>].
- **White Truffle and Albino Caviar**: Flown in from Italy, $4,000 worth of white truffles, and $16,000 in rare caviar adorn the pizza [<a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:18">10:18</a>].
- **Smoked Sea Salt**: Used to finish the pizza, adding a subtle yet vital touch.
