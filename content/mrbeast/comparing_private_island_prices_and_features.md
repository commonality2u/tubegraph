Private islands are often seen as the epitome of luxury and exclusivity. The video featuring Mr. Beast explores a variety of private islands, each with unique features and price points, ranging from a single dollar to a quarter of a billion dollars. Here is a detailed comparison of these islands.

## The One Dollar Island

The cheapest island in the video is a small, nondescript piece of land purchased for just one dollar. Its features include:

- A small pile of bricks
- An old toilet
- Some wooden poles or pylons

This island offers minimal amenities and is primarily noted more for its novelty value than practical usability <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:22">[00:00:22]</a>.

## Monkey Island: The $1 Million Wonder

Monkey Island, [[experiences_on_a_1_private_island | valued at $1 million]], offers visitors a more exotic experience:

- The island is home to hundreds of monkeys.
- There are luxury cabanas available, suspended above the water housing both humans and monkeys <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:30">[00:01:30]</a>.
- The island is not entirely solid ground but emerged from an underwater plant network <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:01">[00:02:01]</a>.

## The $5 Million Frozen Island

Moving up in price, this [[adventures_on_a_5_million_frozen_island | $5 million island]] offers a winter wonderland experience:

- Access to the island via hovercrafts during the frozen season <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:47">[00:02:47]</a>.
- Futuristic self-heating huts that float or sit on ice, depending on the season <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:06">[00:03:06]</a>.
- The opportunity to view the northern lights and enjoy activities like dog sledding <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:25">[00:03:25]</a>.

## The Luxurious $45 Million Island

A substantial leap in budget is the $45 million island that includes:

- A massive estate with beachside bar and private chef <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:37">[00:06:37]</a>.
- Five luxury beachside cabanas and a hot tub overlooking a cove <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:43">[00:06:43]</a>.
- Historic ruins giving the island historical intrigue <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:10">[00:07:10]</a>.

## The $150 Million Island

Next on the price scale is the $150 million island, notable for sustainability and luxury:

- Equipped with eight luxurious mega mansions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:24">[00:08:24]</a>.
- Fully powered by solar panels and wind turbines <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:28">[00:08:28]</a>.
- Home to exotic and endangered species, promoting conservation efforts by its owner <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:09">[00:11:09]</a>.

## The $250 Million Super Island

Finally, the quarter-billion dollar [[exploring_features_of_a_250_million_private_island | island offers unparalleled luxury]]:

- Features an amusement park with over a dozen waterslides, and other activities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:16">[00:13:16]</a>.
- Accommodates up to 13,000 guests with multiple pools and numerous lifeguards <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:16">[00:14:16]</a>.
- A massive waterslide (the tallest in North America) provides a unique entertainment experience <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:02">[00:16:02]</a>.
