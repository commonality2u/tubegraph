## Origin
The organization was founded in memory of [[yardley_loves_death | Yardley Love]], a 22-year-old sports scholar and lacrosse player at the University of Virginia <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:51">[01:51]</a>. A photograph of Yardley, aware of obstacles yet moving forward, became a symbol for this globally recognized organization <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:01">[02:01]</a>.
