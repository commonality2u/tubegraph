## Early Life and Family Background

Jennifer Pan was born and raised in Markham, Ontario, within a middle-class household alongside her older brother <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:32">[00:00:32]</a>. Her parents, Bich and Han, were originally from Vietnam and practiced a very strict, almost authoritarian, method of parenting <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[00:00:37]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:44">[00:00:44]</a>. Initially, Jennifer embraced the pressure, dedicating herself to figure skating. She displayed exceptional talent and was at one point expected to compete in the Winter Olympics <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:04">[00:01:04]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>. However, a serious knee injury at age 14 ended her competitive career <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:11">[00:01:11]</a>. This meant her parents' high expectations now had to be met through traditional education, an area where Jennifer was not as gifted <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:17">[00:01:17]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:21">[00:01:21]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:24">[00:01:24]</a>.

## The Double Life

Jennifer, averaging a C-minus when her parents demanded straight A's, chose to meet their expectations under false pretenses rather than communicate <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:28">[00:01:28]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:32">[00:01:32]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:34">[00:01:34]</a>. In her parents' perception, Jennifer was destined for a noble career in medical healthcare <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:50">[00:01:50]</a>. In reality, she was a high school dropout secretly living with her drug dealer boyfriend, whom she had been dating for nearly eight years <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:56">[00:01:56]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:58">[00:01:58]</a>.

### Discovery and Ultimatum

In 2010, Jennifer's double life was exposed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:04">[00:02:04]</a>. ## The Planned Home Invasion

Jennifer chose neither option, instead creating a third: she asked her boyfriend to arrange a mock home invasion <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:33">[00:02:33]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:37">[00:02:37]</a>. ## The Interrogation (November 22nd, 2010)

Detective Gates adopted a "how and why solution" strategy, gradually shifting blame away from Jennifer and onto the external circumstances that prompted the crime, such as her parents' overly controlling nature and the unfair limitations it imposed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:06:07">[05:06:07]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:10:07">[05:10:07]</a>.
