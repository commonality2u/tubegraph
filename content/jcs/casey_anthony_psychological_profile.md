During closing arguments, Baez urged the jury to set aside emotion and focus only on what was proven beyond a reasonable doubt <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:04:02">[01:04:02]</a>. He argued that the prosecution's strategy was to "paint [[casey_anthonys_behavior_and_public_perception | Casey Anthony]] as a party girl, as a girl who lies," to make the jury hate her and thus overlook the lack of concrete evidence <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:03:07">[01:03:07]</a>.
