### Steering Wheel Control Dynamics

With a steering wheel, the player needs to adapt the core movements and functions to the distinct physical mechanics of driving controls. Here is how the steering wheel functionalities were mapped:

- Turning the wheel right and left mimics the in-game horizontal look movement.
- The d-pad is used for looking up and down.
- Pedals (gas, clutch, and brake) are repurposed for moving forward, jumping, and crouching, respectively <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:06">[00:00:06]</a>.
