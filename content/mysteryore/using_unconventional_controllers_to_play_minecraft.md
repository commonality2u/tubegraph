## Steering Wheel and Pedals

The journey begins with the integration of a steering wheel and pedals, typically used for racing games. These controls are creatively configured to manipulate the Minecraft world:

- **Steering Wheel**: Turning the wheel right or left moves the player's view right and left.
- **D-Pad**: Up and down directions correspondingly adjust the player's vertical view.
- **Gas Pedal**: Pushing the gas pedal moves the player forward.
- **Clutch Pedal**: This pedal is used to jump.
- **Brake Pedal**: Engages crouch mode.
