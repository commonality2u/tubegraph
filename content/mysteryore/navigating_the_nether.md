Navigating the Nether realm in Minecraft requires careful preparation and execution, as discussed in the latest video review of [[mojangs_official_minecraft_guides | Mojang's official game guides]]. Here, we break down key steps and advice for ensuring a safe and successful journey through this perilous dimension.

## Entering the Nether

Before entering the Nether, it's crucial to gather certain supplies to increase survival odds. According to Mojang's guidance, your inventory should include a **sword**, **bow**, **pickaxe**, **water bucket**, **wood**, **food**, and **torches** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:48">[00:05:48]</a>. Furthermore, although not directly mentioned, it is common knowledge to have some **gold armor** to placate hostile piglins <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:20">[00:06:20]</a>.

## Constructing a Portal

Constructing a Nether portal requires obsidian. Surprisingly, the Mojang guide inaccurately states that you can create obsidian by having lava flow into a water source block—this results in cobblestone, not obsidian. The correct method is to pour water over stationary lava <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:52">[00:06:52]</a>.

## Armor and Gear

Mojang's guides provide conflicting advice on the ideal armor. While one guide suggests wearing full gold armor <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:44">[00:05:44]</a>, another mentions needing just a diamond helmet while naked, equipped with only diamond swords, or merely a golden chestplate <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:11">[00:06:11]</a>. Community consensus leans toward wearing any piece of gold armor so that piglins don't attack on sight, combined with stronger armor for protection from other threats.

## Exploring and Finding Resources

Once in the Nether, your primary goals are to locate a **Nether fortress** and find **blaze rods**. The guides humorously suggest using snowballs against blazes and even snow golems, a tactic known to be ineffective, indicating a possible error in the guides <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:41">[00:07:41]</a>.

For navigation, take advantage of the Nether's unique terrain. While the landscape can be disorienting, using **torches on the right hand side of tunnels** can help trace your path back to the portal <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:58">[00:05:58]</a>.

## Leveraging Piglins

Piglins can be bartered with to obtain valuable items such as ender pearls. Bringing gold ingots for trading can be a strategic move. However, be aware that interactions can be unpredictable and require caution <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:18">[00:08:18]</a>.

## Exiting the Nether

After obtaining the necessary resources, it's crucial to traverse back to your portal. Given the Nether's treacherous environment—filled with lava lakes and cliffs—ensure you have enough blocks or ladders to manage terrain height differences safely.

> [!info] Travel Tips
>
> As noted, always wear a piece of gold armor to avoid piglin hostility <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:22">[00:06:22]</a>. Staying alert, prepared, and armed with the correct knowledge will significantly increase your odds of a successful Nether expedition.

Navigating the Nether can be a challenging but rewarding experience in Minecraft. By taking heed of preparation advice and adjusting strategies based on these observations, players can efficiently gather the materials needed to progress their game's journey.
