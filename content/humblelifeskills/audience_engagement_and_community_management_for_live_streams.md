## Mobilizing Moderators for Global Reach

A key strategy for [[social_media_interaction_and_community_engagement | community management]] on a 24/7 global live stream channel involves finding and mobilizing moderators from around the world <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:48">[00:00:48]</a>. These moderators can be individuals interested in the specific content being streamed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:51">[00:00:51]</a>. For instance, they might be:
*   Other tutors <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>
*   People passionate about a certain style of music <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:54">[00:00:54]</a>
*   Enthusiasts of a particular fashion <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>
*   Those interested in a specific type of food <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:57">[00:00:57]</a>

By engaging a global team of moderators, content creators can manage and interact with their community around the clock <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:45">[00:01:45]</a>. This is part of what is seen as a "next generation" of live on-demand content, allowing individuals to effectively run their own "TV station" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:59">[00:00:59]</a>. While the course explores [[creating_a_247_youtube_live_stream_channel | creating a 24/7 YouTube live stream channel]], [[automation_tools_for_youtube_live_streaming | software like Nightbot]] can be used to help manage the stream <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:17">[00:01:17]</a>.
