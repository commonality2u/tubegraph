Automated voting tools on [[introduction_to_steem_and_tron_for_new_users | Steemit]] allow users to manage their upvotes, follow curation trails, schedule posts, and claim rewards efficiently. Two prominent services for this are [[differences_between_steemvoter_and_steemauto | Steemvoter]] and [[features_of_steemauto_such_as_curation_trails_and_scheduling_posts | Steemauto.com]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:00">[00:00:00]</a>.

## Steemvoter Overview

[[differences_between_steemvoter_and_steemauto | Steemvoter]] is a generally reliable system with a standard user interface <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>. It displays your Steem power and voting power, and allows you to add rules for automated voting <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>. Users can specify vote power percentages and delay times for votes on specific individuals <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:32">[00:00:32]</a>. For some users, [[differences_between_steemvoter_and_steemauto | Steemvoter]] serves a dual purpose beyond financial rewards, helping track who is voting on their posts and reminding them to visit other users' blogs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:09">[00:01:09]</a>.

The downtime of [[differences_between_steemvoter_and_steemauto | Steemvoter]] can impact payouts and remove the ability to see who has voted on posts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:57">[00:00:57]</a>. It's advisable to copy down your voting rules to a notepad for [[managing_voting_rules_and_redundancy | redundancy]] in case the service experiences issues again <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:48">[00:03:48]</a>.

## Steemauto.com Features

[[features_of_steemauto_such_as_curation_trails_and_scheduling_posts | Steemauto.com]] is a newer alternative that offers several advanced features <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:06">[00:00:06]</a>.

### Secure Login with Steam Connect

Initially, the login process for [[features_of_steemauto_such_as_curation_trails_and_scheduling_posts | Steemauto]] might seem uncertain as it doesn't immediately present a Steam Connect option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:07">[00:02:07]</a>. However, after initial registration, users are given the choice to log in using Steam Connect <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:48">[00:02:48]</a>. Steam Connect is a secure service, similar to Facebook Connect, used by [[introduction_to_steem_and_tron_for_new_users | Steemit]] to securely connect users to the blockchain APIs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:13">[00:02:13]</a>. This ensures that [[features_of_steemauto_such_as_curation_trails_and_scheduling_posts | Steemauto]] does not capture login details directly <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:03">[00:09:03]</a>.

### Fan Base Voting

Like [[differences_between_steemvoter_and_steemauto | Steemvoter]], [[features_of_steemauto_such_as_curation_trails_and_scheduling_posts | Steemauto]] includes a fan base feature where you can add specific people to upvote <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:27">[00:03:27]</a>. You can set the weight (0-100%) and delay time for your upvote <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:34">[00:03:34]</a>. The platform also lists top fans, showing their follower counts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:26">[00:04:26]</a>.

### Curation Trails

A notable feature of [[features_of_steemauto_such_as_curation_trails_and_scheduling_posts | Steemauto]] is the ability to follow curation trails <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:42">[00:04:42]</a>. This allows your account to automatically upvote posts that a chosen curation trail or account upvotes, with a specified weight limit <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:27">[00:05:27]</a>. This is useful for following projects or maintaining account activity when you are unable to actively blog <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:19">[00:05:19]</a>. Users can also create their own curation trails <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:51">[00:05:51]</a>.

### Scheduled Posts

[[features_of_steemauto_such_as_curation_trails_and_scheduling_posts | Steemauto]] offers a post-scheduling feature, which is not natively available on [[introduction_to_steem_and_tron_for_new_users | Steemit]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:15">[00:06:15]</a>. Users can set a specific number of hours from the current time for a post to be published <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:26">[00:06:26]</a>. It's recommended to test with a draft post first to ensure formatting and alignment are correct <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:35">[00:06:35]</a>.

### Upvoting Comments

This service also allows for automatically upvoting comments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:06">[00:07:06]</a>. You can set a delay and vote weight for comments. Each user will receive one upvote per post and a maximum of two upvotes per day, even if they comment more <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:10">[00:07:10]</a>. This feature can encourage regular engagement on your blog <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:31">[00:07:31]</a>.

### Claim Rewards

A convenient feature in [[features_of_steemauto_such_as_curation_trails_and_scheduling_posts | Steemauto]] is the automatic claiming of rewards <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:39">[00:07:39]</a>. By enabling this option, [[features_of_steemauto_such_as_curation_trails_and_scheduling_posts | Steemauto]] checks your account every 30 minutes to claim available benefits from the blockchain <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:53">[00:07:53]</a>. This moves funds to your Steem Dollar (SBD) line, with the rest automatically going into your Steem Power balance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:17">[00:08:17]</a>. This can reduce the need to manually click the "claim" button on your wallet page <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:59">[00:07:59]</a>.

## Managing Redundancy

To ensure continuous automated voting, it's beneficial to set up rules on both [[differences_between_steemvoter_and_steemauto | Steemvoter]] and [[features_of_steemauto_such_as_curation_trails_and_scheduling_posts | Steemauto]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:08">[00:04:08]</a>.
