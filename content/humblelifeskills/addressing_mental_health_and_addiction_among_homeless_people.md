## The Need for Tailored Support
When providing housing, it is crucial to recognize that not all homeless individuals are ready for independent living <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:14">[00:03:14]</a>. A significant number require additional support due to various factors:
*   **Mental Health Issues** Individuals with [[information_overload_and_mental_health | mental health]] conditions need specific support to navigate their challenges <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:35">[00:02:35]</a>.
*   **Addiction Problems** Those struggling with drug or alcohol addictions require structured assistance to overcome these issues <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:36">[00:02:36]</a><a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:45">[00:02:45]</a>.
*   **Past Trauma** Some individuals have endured horrendous types of abuse, and these past [[experiences_of_homeless_individuals | experiences]] may continue to affect them, even in older age, if not properly addressed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:02">[00:03:02]</a>.

Providing comprehensive support requires funding to connect individuals with various organizations that can offer specialized help <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:40">[00:02:40]</a><a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:42">[00:02:42]</a><a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:44">[00:02:44]</a>.
