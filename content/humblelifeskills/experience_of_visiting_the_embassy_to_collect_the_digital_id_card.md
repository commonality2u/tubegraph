## Embassy Visit Details

The embassy's opening hours varied: on Tuesdays, it opened at 1:00 PM, while on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays, it opened at 9:00 AM <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:29">[00:00:29]</a>. The speaker was in London specifically to pick up their [[features_and_uses_of_the_estonian_eresidency_digital_id_card | digital ID]] from Estonia, which is an electronic residency card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:44">[00:00:44]</a>. It's important to note that this card does not grant actual residency in Estonia, nor does it provide voting rights; it functions primarily as a [[features_and_uses_of_the_estonian_eresidency_digital_id_card | digital ID]] with very strong encryption <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:58">[00:00:58]</a>.

The location of the embassy was easy to find <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>.

## [[collection_process_for_the_eresidency_digital_id_card | Collection Process for the E-residency Digital ID Card]]

The [[collection_process_for_the_eresidency_digital_id_card | collection process]] at the embassy was described as "super straightforward," taking less than 5 to 10 minutes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:18">[00:01:18]</a>. There were only three people present at the time <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:22">[00:01:22]</a>. The steps involved included a fingerprint scan and presenting a passport <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:26">[00:01:26]</a>. The entire procedure was completed in about five minutes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:29">[00:01:29]</a>.

Overall, the visit was straightforward, with the total trip (including travel) taking about three or four hours <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:34">[00:01:34]</a>. After waiting a month for the card details, the speaker visited the embassy within 10-15 minutes of its opening <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:02">[00:04:02]</a>. Upon arrival at the reception area, they provided their passport and had both fingerprints scanned, completing the process in about ten minutes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:09">[00:04:09]</a>.

The cost of the card was €50, as it was obtained before February 1st, after which the price increased to €100 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:18">[00:02:18]</a>.

## Contents of the [[collection_process_for_the_eresidency_digital_id_card | Digital ID Pack]]

The card came in an interesting little pack <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:39">[00:01:39]</a>. The pack contained:
*   The [[features_and_uses_of_the_estonian_eresidency_digital_id_card | card]] itself, featuring RFID and a chip <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:40">[00:01:40]</a>.
*   Instructions for [[instructions_for_setting_up_and_using_the_digital_id_card | using the card]] with a reader, ensuring contact between the chip and the reader, and directing users to "e-estonia.ee/welcome" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:48">[00:01:48]</a>.
*   A "rippable code thing" (PIN code) presumed necessary for card authorization upon sign-up <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:02">[00:02:02]</a>.
*   A sheet outlining the conditions for use of certificates issued for identity cards, residence permit cards, and [[features_and_uses_of_the_estonian_eresidency_digital_id_card | digital ID cards]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:10">[00:02:10]</a>.
*   A small black [[instructions_for_setting_up_and_using_the_digital_id_card | USB card reader]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:27">[00:02:27]</a>. This reader, made by a company called Web Brick Works, is designed for the card to slide into it, touching internal points and a metal peg on the back, before connecting to a computer via USB <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:50">[00:02:50]</a>.

## Utility of the Digital ID Card

The [[features_and_uses_of_the_estonian_eresidency_digital_id_card | digital ID card]] provides 2048-bit digital encryption, ensuring that documents signed with it are uniquely from the cardholder and cannot be faked <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:28">[00:03:28]</a>. This capability opens access to various new [[features_and_uses_of_the_estonian_eresidency_digital_id_card | digital services]] from Estonia, including the ability to set up a business there <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:37">[00:03:37]</a>.

The overall [[process_of_obtaining_an_estonian_eresidency_digital_id_card | process of obtaining an Estonian e-residency digital ID card]] and collecting it was described as "super easy" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:59">[00:03:59]</a>.
