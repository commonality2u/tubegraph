## Features of the E-Residency Digital ID Card
The [[features_and_uses_of_the_estonian_eresidency_digital_id_card | e-residency card]] comes as part of a kit that includes:
*   The [[features_and_uses_of_the_estonian_eresidency_digital_id_card | e-residency card]] itself, which functions with RFID <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:40">[00:01:40]</a>.
*   A rippable code or PIN, likely needed for initial authentication <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:02">[00:02:02]</a>.
*   A sheet detailing conditions for use of certificates issued for identity cards and digital ID cards <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:10">[00:02:10]</a>.
*   A portable card reader that connects via USB to a computer, designed to ensure contact between the card's chip and the reader <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:27">[00:02:27]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:17">[00:03:17]</a>.

## Key Benefits and Use Cases

### Enhanced Digital Security and Authentication
The [[estonias_digital_id_system | Estonian digital ID system]] provides robust security features:
*   **Strong Encryption:** The card enables digital encryption, specifically 2048-bit encryption, for documents. This ensures that documents originate uniquely from the e-resident and cannot be faked <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:28">[00:03:28]</a>. This capability is central to [[estonian_eresidency_digital_signing_capabilities | digital signature using an Estonian digital ID card]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:28">[00:03:28]</a>.
*   **Secure Login:** The [[features_and_uses_of_the_estonian_eresidency_digital_id_card | digital ID card]] can be used as a secure login partner for various [[using_eresidency_as_a_login_partner_for_online_services | online services]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:43">[00:04:43]</a>.

### Access to Digital Services and Business Setup
[[what_is_eresidency | E-residency]] opens up a range of [[advantages_of_eresidency_for_digital_nomads | digital services]] offered by Estonia:
*   **Business Establishment:** A primary benefit is the ability to easily set up a business in Estonia <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:37">[00:03:37]</a>. This streamlines the process for remote entrepreneurs and is a significant implication of [[eresidency_and_its_implications_for_global_talent | e-residency for global talent]].
*   **Digital Service Access:** Beyond business, the card provides access to various other digital services offered by the Estonian government and private sector <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:37">[00:03:37]</a>.

### Streamlined Application Process
The [[process_of_obtaining_an_estonian_eresidency_digital_id_card | process of obtaining an Estonian e-residency digital ID card]] is noted for its simplicity and efficiency:
*   Waiting period for card details: Approximately one month <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:02">[00:04:02]</a>.
*   Embassy pickup: The actual pickup at the embassy is quick, typically taking less than 10-15 minutes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:18">[00:01:18]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:06">[00:04:06]</a>.
*   Requirements: Involves presenting a passport and undergoing fingerprint scans <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:26">[00:01:26]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:13">[00:04:13]</a>.
*   Cost: As of February 1st, the price for the e-residency card is 100 Euros <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:21">[00:02:21]</a>.

The overall process is described as "super easy" and "straightforward" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:29">[00:01:29]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:59">[00:03:59]</a>.
