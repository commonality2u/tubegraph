Users can position their iPad with notes (e.g., from Pages, supported by iCloud) directly up to the camera lens <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>. This setup is described as making things "a lot easier" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:59">[00:00:59]</a> and is considered a "perfect great birthday present" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:58">[00:00:58]</a>.

### [[pros_and_cons_of_the_iklip_stand | Pros and Cons]]

*   **Pro**: Allows for easy teleprompter use by positioning the iPad close to the lens <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:51">[00:00:51]</a>.
*   **Con**: It cannot easily transition into landscape orientation without unscrewing a bolt <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:01:01">[01:01:01]</a>.

Despite this minor drawback, the overall impression is positive, with the user stating, "it's good that is going to work nicely" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:01:03">[01:01:03]</a>.
