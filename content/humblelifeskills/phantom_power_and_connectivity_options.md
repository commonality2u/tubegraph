## Key Features of the My Port Pro

The My Port Pro includes several important features for audio recording:
*   **Adjustments** - It offers minimum and maximum settings for both microphone input and headphone output <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>.
*   **Headphone Connector** - A connector at the bottom allows for direct headphone monitoring <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.
*   **Phantom Power** - The device provides 48-volt phantom power, which is necessary to power certain microphones <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:44">[00:00:44]</a>.

## [[device_connectivity_options | Device Connectivity Options]]

The My Port Pro features [[device_connectivity_options | USB connectivity]] to connect directly to a computer for recording <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:51">[00:00:51]</a>.

## Microphone [[compatibility_with_xlr_microphones | Compatibility]]

The device is designed to fit directly into [[compatibility_with_xlr_microphones | XLR microphones]], such as the [[rode_ntg2_microphone_features | Rode NTG2]] or any other microphone with an XLR connector <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:13">[00:01:13]</a>. This creates a flush, all-in-one unit that can connect to a laptop for broadcast-quality sound recording <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:21">[00:01:21]</a>. The [[rode_ntg2_microphone_features | Rode NTG2 microphone]] is specifically mentioned as being used with this setup <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:32">[00:01:32]</a>.
