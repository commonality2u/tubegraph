## Why VPNs are Needed for Privacy

Recent legislative efforts, particularly in the US, have aimed to grant Internet Service Providers (ISPs) and the government the ability to access user log files at will <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:48:48">[00:48:48]</a>. This has been a contentious issue regarding [[privacy_concerns_and_surveillance_laws | privacy concerns and surveillance laws]] for many years <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:56:06">[00:56:06]</a>.

Key privacy issues that VPNs address include:
*   **Data Reselling and Targeting**: User data can be resold, leading to targeted advertising <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:17:15">[01:17:15]</a>. This explains why ads on platforms like Facebook often seem to know what you're looking for <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:22:00">[01:22:00]</a>.
*   **Government and ISP Surveillance**: The ability for ISPs and government to look into user log files at will raises significant privacy concerns <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:48:48">[00:48:48]</a>.

## VPNs and Security

Beyond privacy, VPNs also provide significant security benefits:
*   **Encrypted Tunnels**: Data passed through an encrypted tunnel helps mitigate potential attacks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:57:42">[01:57:42]</a>.
*   **Protection Against DoS/DDoS Attacks**: For activities like live streaming, a VPN can provide resilience against Denial of Service (DoS) or Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) attacks, which aim to knock users offline <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:36:00">[01:36:00]</a>. By routing data through an encrypted tunnel, the attack surface of the computer is reduced <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:57:42">[01:57:42]</a>.
*   **IP Address Protection**: When live streaming, it's advised to avoid chatting directly in the chat box, as some tools can uncover your host's IP address from chat interactions, allowing attackers to flood your connection and disrupt your stream <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:58">[00:01:58]</a>. Using moderators or a proxy to connect to chat is recommended to prevent your internet connection from being flooded <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:28:30">[01:28:30]</a>.

## VPN Services and Their Features

Different VPNs offer varying features, making it an "art form" to choose the right one for specific needs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="11:21:00">[11:21:00]</a>.

### Speedify
Speedify is a VPN client known for its unique ability to [[vpn_connection_bonding | bond connections together]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:56:06">[02:56:06]</a>. This allows users to combine multiple internet connections (e.g., 4G tethering, Ethernet, Wi-Fi) for increased speed and reliability <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:09:00">[03:09:00]</a>. It can use connected devices together for both reliability and a speed boost, or in redundant mode, sending all traffic across each connection for improved reliability <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:30:17">[03:30:17]</a>. Speedify supports bonding up to 10 devices <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:45:00">[03:45:00]</a>.
Speedify can also act as a traditional VPN, allowing connections to the closest country or a specific server like a P2P server <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:57:08">[04:57:08]</a>. It is described as very lightweight and easy to use <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:17:00">[05:17:00]</a>. For general daily use, such as browsing websites and YouTube, Speedify is considered consistent and reliable <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="13:02:44">[13:02:44]</a>. Its ability to bond multiple connections makes it a perfect tool for mission-critical video streaming, offering redundancy in case one network goes down <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="14:31:00">[14:31:00]</a>.

### IBVPN
IBVPN is another VPN provider that offers an "ultimate VPN pack" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:38:40">[06:38:40]</a>. It supports four [[different_vpn_protocols_and_their_uses | different VPN protocols and their uses]], each with distinct compression or speed benefits, requiring users to experiment to find the best fit <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:52:43">[06:52:43]</a>. It has a vast network of servers globally, including dedicated P2P (peer-to-peer) servers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:08:18">[07:08:18]</a>. IBVPN also offers a double VPN feature, bouncing the connection through two different locations for enhanced security <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:15:39">[07:15:39]</a>. While it can be used for general purposes, a specific "gaming Netherlands" server with OpenVPN protocol demonstrated exceptionally high download speeds due to being hosted on an incredibly fast backbone within a data center that also hosts gaming servers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:29:43">[07:29:43]</a>. This setup, however, is optimized for gaming traffic and may not perform well for other uses like live streaming <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="11:00:10">[11:00:10]</a>.

While [[vpn_for_gaming_and_streaming | VPN for gaming and streaming]] benefits from low ping times, the trade-off with high-speed gaming-optimized servers is that the latency (ping) might increase <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:24:24">[08:24:24]</a>. Different VPNs are often used for different purposes; for instance, Speedify for streaming and general browsing, and IBVPN for specific gaming scenarios <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="11:26:00">[11:26:00]</a>.
