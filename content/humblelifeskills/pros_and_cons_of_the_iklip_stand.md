The [[iklip_microphone_stand_overview | iKlip microphone stand]] adapter for [[ipad_adapter_compatibility | iPad one and two]] has arrived <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:20">[00:00:20]</a>.

## Advantages

*   **Teleprompter Functionality**: The iKlip stand can be positioned directly up to the lens, allowing it to be used as a [[using_iklip_stand_for_teleprompter | teleprompter]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>. This feature is useful for stepping through notes created in apps like Pages, which are supported by iCloud <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:44">[00:00:44]</a>.
*   **Ease of Use**: The device is noted to "make things a lot easier" for recording <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:59">[00:00:59]</a>.

## Disadvantages

*   **Landscape Orientation Limitation**: A minor inconvenience is that the iKlip stand cannot be adjusted to landscape orientation without unscrewing a bolt <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:01">[00:01:01]</a>.
