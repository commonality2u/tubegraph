## The International Phil Campbell Convention

An international event, known as the [[Phil Campbell convention | Phil Campbell convention]], is being organized to take place in [[Phil Campbell community | Phil Campbell, Alabama]] in June <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:10">[00:00:10]</a>. The primary organizer is Phil Campbell, a writer from New York <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:04">[00:00:04]</a>. The aim of the convention is to ensure that "no Phil Campbell is Left Behind" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:54">[00:00:54]</a>.

### Collaborative Planning
The organization involves a collaborative effort, with Phil Campbell from New York working alongside a Phil Campbell in Australia <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:19">[00:00:19]</a> and a Phil Campbell in Great Britain <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:24">[00:00:24]</a>.

### Outreach and Communication
A significant part of the organization involves finding as many individuals named Phil Campbell as possible. This includes all derivations of the name, such as Phyllis, Philipe, Philippa, and Phillips <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:32">[00:00:32]</a>.

*   **[[Phil Campbell online blog | Online Blog]]** – A Posterous Blog, `philcampbell.posterous.com`, has been set up. Emails sent to `philcampbell@posterous.com` with a title in the subject line can be posted directly to the blog <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:14">[00:01:14]</a>.

The event aims to gather "the Phil Campbell of the world" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:04">[00:01:04]</a> for what is anticipated to be a "great time in Alabama" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>.
