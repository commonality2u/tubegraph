Marks and Spencer, a UK-based company, appears to offer certain products exclusively under their own brand. One such item discussed is a USB missile launcher <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:12">[00:00:12]</a>.

## The USB Missile Launcher

The USB missile launcher is identified as a "Marks and Spencer's own brand" product <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:44">[00:00:44]</a>. The packaging explicitly features "Copyright Marks and Spencer PLC," suggesting they are the sole proprietors of this item <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:48">[00:00:48]</a>. The device plugs into a computer and, with included software, can aim and launch small missiles <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:18">[00:00:18]</a>. It also includes launch sound effects <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:32">[00:00:32]</a>.

### Availability
