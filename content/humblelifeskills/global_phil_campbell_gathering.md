The [[international_gathering_of_phil_campbells | Global Phil Campbell Gathering]] is an initiative rooted in an original plan to hold a [[phil_campbell_convention | convention]] in the town of [[phil_campbell_community | Phil Campbell, Alabama]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:19">[00:00:19]</a>. This [[phil_campbell_convention | convention]], planned about eight weeks prior to the video's creation, aimed to bring hundreds of individuals named Phil Campbell from around the world to [[phil_campbell_community | Phil Campbell, Alabama]] for a "hoedown and a convention" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:22">[00:00:22]</a>.

## Shift in Purpose After Tornado Devastation

Due to a "huge tornado series of tornadoes" that devastated [[phil_campbell_community | Phil Campbell]] and other parts of Alabama, the focus of the [[international_gathering_of_phil_campbells | gathering]] shifted <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>. The initiative transformed into a platform for [[charitable_work_through_social_media | charitable work through social media]] and global outreach.

The current "site" (referring to the online presence or initiative) is a collective contribution from Phil Campbells worldwide to assist and help the town <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:40">[00:00:40]</a>. Its primary objectives are:
*   Raising awareness <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:47">[00:00:47]</a>
*   Gaining sponsorship <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>
*   Collecting donations for the [[rebuilding_efforts_for_phil_campbell_alabama | rebuilding efforts for Phil Campbell]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>
