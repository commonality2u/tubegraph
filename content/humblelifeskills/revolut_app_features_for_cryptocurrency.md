## Benefits and Usability

The app aims to make buying and exchanging cryptocurrencies easier, especially for first-time buyers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:17">[00:02:17]</a>. Once funds are added to a user's balance, they can swap, change, and exchange money between different currencies, including crypto <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:22">[00:02:22]</a>. A significant benefit highlighted is the real-time exchange rate displayed within the app <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:37">[00:02:37]</a>. This contrasts with other methods that can be technical or offer less favorable exchange rates <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:32">[00:02:32]</a>.
