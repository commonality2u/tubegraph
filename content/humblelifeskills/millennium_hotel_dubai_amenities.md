The Millennium Hotel Dubai is described as a "really, really nice hotel" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26"></a> with a potentially large number of rooms and floors <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:01"></a>.

### Key Amenities

*   **Grand Ballroom** The hotel features a grand ballroom <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:10"></a>.
*   **Dining and Bars** Guests have access to a "20 restaurant bar" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:12"></a> and a "Jazbar" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:18"></a>. Breakfast service is also available <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:39"></a>.
*   **Health and Wellness** Amenities include the "Jasmine Spar Health Club" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:14"></a>.
*   **Non-Smoking Floors** The hotel offers non-smoking floors <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:23"></a>.

### Navigation and Access
The hotel's [[hotel_layout_and_navigation_challenges | layout]] can be confusing, described as a "myriad of corridors," leading to guests getting lost <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:32"></a>. Access to certain areas or rooms requires the use of a card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52"></a>.
