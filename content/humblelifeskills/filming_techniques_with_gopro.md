### Rig Features and Filming Applications

The design of this [[gopro_rig_for_hero3 | GoPro rig for Hero3]] allows for various filming techniques:
*   **Panning Shots** The rig includes a grip on the side, making it ideal for smooth panning shots <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:26">[00:01:26]</a>.
*   **Low-Angle Shots** A handle on top facilitates capturing low-down shots <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:28">[00:01:28]</a>.
*   **Mounting** The standard GoPro mount from the original box clips onto the bottom of the rig, allowing the camera to be screwed in securely <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a><a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:39">[00:01:39]</a>. For mounting the camera outside its case, a separate frame is necessary <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:43">[00:01:43]</a>.
*   **Portability** The rig features small hooks on each side for carrying and is designed to be easily dropped into a bag <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:00">[00:02:00]</a><a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:05">[00:02:05]</a>.
*   **Versatile Filming** It is suitable for capturing B-roll footage and, with the addition of a frame, could be used effectively for interviews <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:07">[00:02:07]</a><a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:13">[00:02:13]</a>. Overall, it is highly effective for achieving fixed level shots <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:03">[00:03:03]</a>.
