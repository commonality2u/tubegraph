The Revolut app includes a feature that tracks expenses, categorizing them into different areas like travel and groceries <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:10:18">[04:10:18]</a>. This functionality is appealing to users who do not specifically track their spending in various categories <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:16:18">[04:16:18]</a>.

A significant benefit of this feature is its compatibility with Apple Pay <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:22:20">[04:22:20]</a>. Users can load funds into the Revolut app from other cards that may not support Apple Pay and then use Revolut for payments at the till <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:32:00">[04:32:00]</a>. This consolidation of funds allows for a "one-stop shop" approach, centralizing transactions and simplifying the process of [[managing_spending_habits | managing spending habits]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:39:19">[04:39:19]</a>.
