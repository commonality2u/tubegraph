## The Distribution-First Strategy for Founders

For founders aiming to build a [[the_significance_of_media_and_distribution_in_modern_tech_companies | distribution]] advantage through content, Jason offers key advice:
1.  **Study Successful Marketing**: Like Tim Ferriss, founders should archive online videos, media, and Twitter posts that effectively grab attention and inspire them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:29">[00:17:29]</a>.
2.  **Experiment**: Slowly experiment with replicating or adapting successful content strategies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:02">[00:18:02]</a>.
3.  **Find Your Niche**: Identify what resonates with your audience and aligns with your strengths <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:10">[00:20:10]</a>. For B2C apps, focus on content that converts users; for founders with interesting stories, consider sharing weekly lessons or experiences in a series <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:16">[00:19:16]</a>. This "building in public" approach can lead to significant follower growth over time <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:54">[00:19:54]</a>.

### Case Study: The CrowdStrike Video
Jason broke down his viral CrowdStrike video, which garnered 6.6 million views on Instagram <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:21">[00:24:21]</a>.
*   **Trending Topic**: It capitalized on a highly relevant tech and mainstream issue (flight delays due to CrowdStrike) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:21:11">[00:21:11]</a>.
*   **Strong Hook**: The video started with an erratic, yelling hook to immediately capture attention <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:21:38">[00:21:38]</a>.
*   **Contextual Information**: Including a news article snippet clarified the skit's context and added an educational element, encouraging follows <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:09">[00:23:09]</a>.
*   **Rapid Shipping**: The key was posting the video super fast, within hours of the incident, rather than aiming for perfection <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:03">[00:24:03]</a>. This allowed him to dominate the trend <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:01">[00:24:01]</a>.
*   **Frequency over Quality (Platform Dependent)**: For platforms like Instagram, posting frequently (ideally daily) is crucial, even if it's not always perfect <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:10">[00:25:10]</a>.

## Audience Value over Size

Jason emphasizes the importance of a high-value audience over sheer follower count. He prefers to have an audience of "smart founders" and executives, who have disposable income and are potential customers or partners, rather than a large audience of "low IQ people" or high school students <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:30:57">[00:30:57]</a>. This strategy makes his content more valuable for sponsorships and future product sales <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:31:11">[00:31:11]</a>. This also highlights a shift where monetization is increasingly tied to the value of a niche audience rather than just audience size <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:30:04">[00:30:04]</a>.
