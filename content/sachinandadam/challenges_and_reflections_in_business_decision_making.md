### Decision-Making Mental Model
Reef employs a "mental model" for decision-making, especially when evaluating opportunities. It involves:
1.  **Ideas:** Brainstorming potential actions or initiatives <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:26:47">[00:26:47]</a>.
2.  **Dependencies:** Identifying what needs to happen or who needs to be involved for an idea to proceed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:26:57">[00:26:57]</a>.
3.  **Perceived Outcome:** Estimating the potential impact on a scale of 1 to 10 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:27:08">[00:27:08]</a>.
4.  **Perceived Cost (Time or Effort):** Assessing the resources required <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:27:32">[00:27:32]</a>.

This model helps prioritize efforts, favoring "low hanging fruit" with high perceived outcomes and low dependencies/effort <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:27:54">[00:27:54]</a>.

## Advice for Early-Stage Entrepreneurs
Reef offers several pieces of advice for those starting their entrepreneurial journey:
*   **Clearly define your "why":** Understand the deep, individual motivations for starting the business and what success truly means beyond monetary gain <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:55:54">[00:55:54]</a>.
*   **Craft a clear, concise vision:** Refine your company's description into a "15-second snapshot" or "two-minute version" that is easy for others to understand and articulate <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:04:11">[01:04:11]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:05:12">[01:05:12]</a>. This compounds over time as more people share your message effectively <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:05:35">[01:05:35]</a>.
*   **Set big, ambitious goals:** Embrace a "10x rule" mindset for internal goals, as thinking bigger increases the likelihood of achieving significant results <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:09:54">[01:09:54]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:10:28">[01:10:28]</a>.
*   **Reverse engineer your path:** Start with the top-line goal (e.g., $100 million revenue) and then break it down into actionable tranches (Year 1, Year 2, Year 3 goals) without getting bogged down in overly granular financial models initially <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:07:40">[01:07:40]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:08:14">[01:08:14]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:11:11">[01:11:11]</a>.
*   **Focus relentlessly on earning money:** In the early stages, the primary focus should be on generating revenue. "Revenue solves and profit solves a lot of problems" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:12:53">[01:12:53]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:13:17">[01:13:17]</a>.
