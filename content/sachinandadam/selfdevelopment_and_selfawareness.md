*   **Societal Standards vs. Personal Values**: A significant challenge in adulthood is defining one's own standards and [[the_role_of_values_and_self_reflection_in_personal_and_business_growth | values]] rather than getting "sucked up in other people's standards" or societal expectations regarding career, academic performance, or success <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:49">[00:24:49]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:01">[00:25:01]</a>.
