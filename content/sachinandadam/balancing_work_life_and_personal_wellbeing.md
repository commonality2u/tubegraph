## Strategies for Managing the Demands
To manage the intense demands, several strategies are employed:
*   **Time Blocking and Day Theming**: Structuring days around specific tasks or types of meetings to maximize productivity. This allows for focused work periods and consolidates similar activities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="21:14:00">[21:14:00]</a>.
*   **Leveraging AI**: Utilizing AI tools for content creation, investor reports, and other tasks to increase personal productivity. The founder notes having "30 workers" trained on their voice and tone, allowing them to "pump out so much work" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="22:05:00">[22:05:00]</a>.
*   **Involving Family in the Journey**: To foster connection and presence, the founder intentionally involves her daughters in the content creation process and the business journey itself. This allows the children to feel part of the story rather than sidelined <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="33:47:00">[33:47:00]</a>. This approach highlights a unique way of [[balancing_career_and_family_in_modern_society | balancing career and family in modern society]].
