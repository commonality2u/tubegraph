## Co-hosts' Passions Driving the Show

The direction and themes of the podcast are significantly shaped by the personal interests and passions of its co-hosts:

*   **Sachin**, a 21-year-old university student, expresses an obsession with [[social_venture_capital_and_impact_investing | social enterprise]], fitness, and the philosophy of seeking discomfort <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:43">[00:00:43]</a>.
*   **Adam**, also a 21-year-old university student, pursues a unique blend of economics, philosophy, and finance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:53">[00:00:53]</a>. His primary passion lies in exploring the [[the_intersection_of_art_and_science_in_venture_investing | intersection of business and social impact]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:01">[00:01:01]</a>.

The hosts acknowledge that they are on their own "journeys of self-discovery and finding [their] place in the world" and invite listeners to join them on this exploration <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>.
