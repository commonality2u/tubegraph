## Observing Emerging Trends

The ability to discern shifts and trends is a skill that can be developed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:30:48">[00:30:48]</a>. It's often described as a matter of taste and practice rather than a strict science <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:30:50">[00:30:50]</a>. The world changes rapidly, and what might seem absurd initially can quickly become a significant trend <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:30:59">[00:30:59]</a>.

### Founders as Emerging Trends

Identifying emerging founders is akin to seeing a candidate emerge in politics; you can't manufacture them, but you can spot them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:11">[00:00:11]</a>. Early signs of a serious founder often involve them saying something novel or "weird" that hasn't been widely heard before <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:19">[00:23:19]</a>. This indicates they have developed their own unique thesis on a subject <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:25">[00:23:25]</a>.

Storytelling methods are constantly evolving, often driven by new technologies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:31">[00:09:31]</a>.

This evolution highlights a medium-agnostic approach to storytelling <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:47">[00:09:48]</a>.

### Technology and New Mediums

Technology consistently creates new mediums for storytelling, such as podcasts and streaming television <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:42">[00:09:42]</a>. New forms of storytelling and technology should be taken seriously, as they are often on the ascent, while older methods may receive too much reverence <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:06">[00:10:06]</a>.

## Identifying "Seriousness" in Founders

"Seriousness" in a founder refers to their deep, intentional beliefs and maniacal focus on a purpose <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:51:57">[00:51:57]</a>.

### Counter-Intuitive Ideas

Serious individuals often articulate ideas that initially seem strange or are met with laughter <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:06">[00:24:06]</a>. For instance, Elon Musk's early endeavors with SpaceX were often met with skepticism and laughter, as the idea of becoming a multi-planetary species seemed absurd <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:48">[00:23:48]</a>. Yet, his unwavering focus led to a company of extraordinary value <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:17">[00:24:17]</a>. Similarly, Chris Pal of Hadrien was vocal about building a stronger industrial base and the threat of China before these ideas became widely accepted in the founder network <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:24">[00:22:24]</a>.

### The "Weirdness" Indicator

When meeting a founder, a strong reaction – positive or negative – can be an indicator that you are onto something significant <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:35:01">[00:35:01]</a>. Companies that elicit an immediate feeling of "weirdness" or skepticism can represent significant investment opportunities, as they often challenge existing norms <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:00">[00:25:00]</a>. A Catholic meditation app, Hallow, is cited as an example; it was initially met with laughter but has since become a market leader <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:33:01">[00:33:01]</a>.

## "American Dynamism" as a Movement

"American Dynamism" is a specific focus that involves investing in the core components underpinning the American Dream to make it more real <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:40:40">[00:40:40]</a>.

### Core Components

Key areas of investment include housing, transportation, education, public safety, logistics, defense, and aerospace <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:45:00">[00:45:00]</a>. The belief is that a strong America contributes to a safer, more civilized world, with technology serving as the backbone for maintaining this order <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:06">[00:01:06]</a>.

### Building for America and Allies

The focus extends to supporting the national interest of the U.S. and its allies, such as Australia and the UK, which are often the next markets for American defense and aerospace companies after establishing themselves in the U.S. <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:42:40">[00:42:40]</a>. Strong ties, like those seen with Australia, are crucial for information sharing and industrial partnership <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:43:35">[00:43:35]</a>.

## Cultivating Seriousness and Spotting Trends

Seriousness and the ability to spot [[predicting_future_trends_in_society_space_art_and_technology | future trends]] are nurtured through specific approaches to life and intellectual engagement.

### Family and Community as Foundations

Strong family ties and community are foundational for establishing good values and character traits <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:49">[00:19:49]</a>. While social media is sometimes seen as detrimental to community, it can also facilitate finding and building communities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:07">[00:20:07]</a>. Startups, by focusing on a strong mission and purpose, also build communities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:59">[00:21:03]</a>.

The ability to cultivate seriousness often stems from an environment that encourages curiosity, experimentation, and a lack of fear of failure, allowing individuals to try and build new things <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:27:08">[00:27:08]</a>.

### Openness and Humility

In venture capital, high openness to new ideas and a realization of how quickly the world changes are essential <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:36:06">[00:36:06]</a>. Investors must continuously educate themselves about current trends and remain humble, accepting that past knowledge may not fully apply to the future <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:36:34">[00:36:34]</a>. This often means making investments that go against conventional wisdom <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:37:00">[00:37:00]</a>.

### Protecting Intellectual Independence

To maintain independent thought and avoid being swayed by noise, it's beneficial to create a divide between one's physical and online worlds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:47:05">[00:47:05]</a>. While the online world might reveal cutting-edge future trends, staying connected to "normal people" in the physical world provides a crucial touchpoint with reality <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:47:40">[00:47:40]</a>. Being skeptical that everyone is selling a message, even seemingly benign ones, is also important <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:48:14">[00:48:14]</a>.
