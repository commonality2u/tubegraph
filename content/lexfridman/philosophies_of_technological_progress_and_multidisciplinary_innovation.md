**Effective Accelerationism (EAC)** promotes rapid technological progress, suggesting that advances in AI can serve as a social equalizer. EAC followers perceive themselves as opposed to the cautious view that AI needs strict regulation due to its unpredictability <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:06">[00:01:06]</a>.
