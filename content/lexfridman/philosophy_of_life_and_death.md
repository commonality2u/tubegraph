## Nietzsche's Eternal Recurrence

One pivotal concept discussed is Nietzsche's thought experiment of eternal recurrence. This asks individuals to imagine reliving their lives over and over. The experiment challenges one to consider whether each moment in life is worthy of endless repetition and whether the cumulative decisions and experiences are fulfilling or regrettable <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a>. Nietzsche uses this paradox to probe one's relationship with life and to encourage living in a manner that embraces every moment as if it were to be relived infinitely.

> [!quote] Friedrich Nietzsche
> "This life as you now live it and have lived it, you will have to live once more and innumerable times more."
