Ivanka Trump shares her journey and experiences as a mother and the significant role her family plays in her life, both in personal decisions and through the tumultuous journey of politics and public service.

## The Role of Family

Ivanka emphasizes the importance of family in her life, particularly in her decision to prioritize her young children and private life over political engagement. She reflects on her years in Washington and the decision to take a step back from politics to focus on her family:

> "First and foremost, it was a decision rooted in me being a parent, really thinking about what they need from me now" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:35:19">[02:35:19]</a>.

She describes politics as a "rough business" and emphasizes the need to be either fully committed or entirely disengaged, a decision influenced greatly by the impact political life might have on her family <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:35:38">[02:35:38]</a>.

## Lessons from Her Own Parents

Reflecting on her own upbringing, Ivanka speaks about the influence of her parents, especially her mother, who taught her to enjoy life and have courage. Her mother’s adventurous spirit and success as a businesswoman were significant influences, offering a model of how to balance career and family effectively <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:30:22">[00:30:22]</a>.

## Parenting Philosophy

Ivanka has cultivated significant learnings from motherhood, noting that being a parent requires developing heightened empathy and seeing each child for who they truly are. She acknowledges how different her children’s personalities are and how this can affect parenting strategies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:19:39">[02:19:39]</a>.

Being a parent has also rekindled her sense of play and joy:

> "My kids have taught me how to play again and that's beautiful" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:22:00">[02:22:00]</a>.

## Balancing Family with Career Ambitions

Ivanka speaks candidly about the challenge of balancing family life with professional and public responsibilities. She reflects on the struggle to find balance, especially considering her previous high-profile roles <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:14:51">[02:14:51]</a>.

Rather than striving for perfect balance, she emphasizes living in accordance with one's priorities, suggesting an alignment between personal and professional life that satisfies both without compromising on what’s important <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:14:13">[02:14:13]</a>.

## Conclusion

Ivanka Trump's insights reflect her deep commitment to her family and a thoughtful approach to motherhood. Her journey illustrates the challenges and decisions that come with balancing public life with family priorities, emphasizing the profound impact love and family have on personal decisions.
