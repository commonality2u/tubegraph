## The Great Nap

Weinstein introduces the concept of the "great nap," a period of relative peace and prosperity that has led to complacency in leadership and institutional structures <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:00">[00:10:00]</a>. This period, characterized by the absence of profound global disruptions, might have left institutions unprepared for the multiple challenges posed by the pandemic.
