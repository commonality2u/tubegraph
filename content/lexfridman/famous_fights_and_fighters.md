## The Evolution of UFC

The UFC has revolutionized the sport of mixed martial arts, providing a platform that showcases the best fighters and the most thrilling matches <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:35">[00:00:35]</a>. Its growth can be attributed to the passion and vision of Dana White, who has steered the organization through its challenging formative years.

### Key Moments in UFC History

- **The Founding and Growth**: The UFC was founded in 1993, initially featuring martial art versus martial art competitions. Over the years, it has evolved significantly, with its rapid growth revolutionizing combat sports faster than any time in history <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:14">[00:11:14]</a>.

- **Noteworthy Fights**: Iconic matchups such as Conor McGregor versus [[khabib_nurmagomedov | Khabib Nurmagomedov]] have captivated audiences globally, making them legendary in the annals of [[mixed_martial_arts_strategy_and_history | MMA history]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:44:55">[00:44:55]</a>.

- **The UFC 299 Post-Fight Conference**: In illustrating the making of legends, Dana White mentioned how fighters, like Dustin Poirier, have risen to legendary status by participating in evenly matched fights that pushed them to new heights <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:40:29">[00:40:29]</a>.

### Figures of Influence

- **Connor McGregor**: A fighter known not only for his prowess inside the Octagon but also for his business acumen and larger-than-life persona <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:50:57">[00:50:57]</a>.
  
- **Joe Rogan**: As a commentator, Rogan has played a crucial role in educating the audience on the intricacies of MMA, helping to make the sport accessible to a broader audience <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:35:04">[00:35:04]</a>.

- **Jon Jones**: Considered by many as possibly the greatest of all time, Jones's career remains unbeaten, showcasing sheer dominance across different weight classes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:47:49">[00:47:49]</a>.
