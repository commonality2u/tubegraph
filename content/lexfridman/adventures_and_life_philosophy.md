## Winston Churchill's Lifestyle and Celebration

Bert Kreischer embraces the lifestyle of Winston Churchill, celebrating his admiration by mimicking the statesman's daily routine on the anniversary of Churchill's death, January 24th. Kreischer described Churchill's elaborate routine, which included a substantial breakfast, drinks, cigars, and a relaxed lifestyle seemingly filled with indulgence and reflection. Kreischer uses this celebration as a personal tradition to honor Churchill, by relishing in the simplicity and luxury that marked the historical figure's life <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:00">[00:00:00]</a>.

## The Joy of Writing and Reflection

Kreischer shares his perspectives on writing, drawing inspiration from Ernest Hemingway's notion that writing is akin to bleeding out on a typewriter. Kreischer finds this particularly resonant with journaling, stating that it helps uncover the true essence of thoughts. He admires Hemingway for his simple yet profound writing style, which he found accessible due to his dyslexia <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:27">[00:01:27]</a>.
