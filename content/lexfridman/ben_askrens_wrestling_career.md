## Wrestling Style and Philosophy

Askren is acclaimed for his "funky" wrestling style, characterized by its creativity and adaptability. Despite lacking traditional athletic attributes such as explosive speed and strength, Askren's innovative technique allowed him to defeat opponents who might have been considered more physically gifted <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:55:02">[00:55:02]</a>.

## International and Olympic Experience

In 2008, Ben Askren represented the United States at the Summer Olympics in Beijing. While he did not achieve a podium finish, his participation in the international circuit marked the pinnacle of his amateur wrestling career. Askren admitted the Olympics was a significant learning experience and reflected that, at that stage, he simply wasn't good enough yet against seasoned international competitors <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:17:30">[01:17:30]</a>.
