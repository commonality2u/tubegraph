## The Giving Pledge: A Global Movement

The Giving Pledge, started by Warren Buffett and Bill Gates in 2010, encourages the wealthiest individuals and families to commit more than half of their wealth to charitable causes during their lifetimes or in their wills. This initiative fosters a community of engaged philanthropists who aim to solve real-world problems through strategic and impactful giving.

### Objectives and Impact
