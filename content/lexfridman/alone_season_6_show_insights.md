## The Concept of Alone

Alone pits ten participants against nature. Each contestant is allowed to select ten items from a prescribed list of basic survival tools. Participants are then dropped off in different locations within the wilderness, where they must use their chosen items to survive as long as possible, documenting their journey with a few cameras provided to them. Without a support crew, contestants deal with the raw elements entirely on their own <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:19">[00:01:19]</a><a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:59">[00:01:59]</a>.

## Key Survival Strategies

### Shelter and Resources

Survival hinges on the contestant's ability to build a suitable shelter and gather resources. Contestants strategically pick items such as an axe, saw, and a bow and arrow to help them acquire food and build shelter. They must take into account the availability of resources, such as firewood and water, when selecting a site for their shelter. It's imperative the location is protected from harsh weather yet access to food and resources remains viable <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:21">[00:02:21]</a><a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:31:01">[00:31:01]</a>.

### Food Sourcing and Preservation

Contesting in Alone requires the ability to adapt quickly to the environment. Participants need to leverage fishing, trapping, and hunting to secure food. Notably, one successful strategy was creating alarm systems with string and cans to detect game movement.
