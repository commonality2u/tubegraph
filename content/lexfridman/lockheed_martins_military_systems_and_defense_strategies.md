Lockheed Martin is a renowned defense and aerospace company known for its extensive contributions to military systems and defense strategies. Under the leadership of its CTO, Keoki Jackson, the company continues to innovate and develop systems critical to national defense and security.

## Historical Context

Lockheed Martin has a storied history spanning over a century. The merger of Lockheed and Martin, alongside numerous other companies, has positioned Lockheed Martin as a leader in the defense sector, tasked with solving hard mission problems for the U.S. military and its allies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="28:23">[28:23]</a>.

## Build of Critical Defense Systems

### Advancements in Fighter Jets

Lockheed Martin has been instrumental in the design and production of advanced aircraft, including [[technological_advancements_in_fighter_jets]], tactical fighters, and stealth aircraft. The F-35, for example, is one of the most advanced fighter jets, featuring supersonic capabilities, stealth, and a data fusion system <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="39:37">[39:37]</a>.

### Missile Defense Systems

Lockheed Martin is responsible for systems like the Aegis Combat System and THAAD (Terminal High Altitude Area Defense), which provide missile defense against ballistic threats to both military assets and civilian populations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="29:58">[29:58]</a>. These systems demonstrate the company's commitment to protecting vital regions from potential threats <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="31:01">[31:01]</a>.

## Autonomy and AI in Warfare

The integration of [[advancements_in_ai_and_autonomy_by_lockheed_martin]] and [[autonomous_weapon_systems_and_ai_in_warfare]] is seen across Lockheed Martin's portfolio. This includes developing autonomous wingman capabilities in fighter jets and piloted aircraft <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="44:46">[44:46]</a>. Moreover, Lockheed Martin is pushing the boundaries of AI, exploring applications that assist in both strategic and operational military decision-making <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="54:01">[54:01]</a>.

## Ethical Considerations and Military-Industrial Complex

Pentagon policy governs the use of autonomous systems to ensure human oversight in military applications, addressing concerns about the [[the_militaryindustrial_complex_and_war_profiteering]] and [[war_and_military_strategy]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="56:09">[56:09]</a>. The company is committed to developing systems ethically, ensuring privacy, reducing bias, and preventing the delegation of critical decisions to algorithms alone <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="57:12">[57:12]</a>.

## Nuclear Deterrence and Cyber Defense

Lockheed Martin contributes to nuclear deterrence with systems like the Trident submarine-launched ballistic missile, ensuring strategic stability by maintaining a deterrent against nuclear threats <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="59:30">[59:30]</a>. Additionally, cyber threats have become a strategic focus, as attacks on infrastructure could have devastating impacts, necessitating advanced defensive measures <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="59:10">[59:10]</a>.
