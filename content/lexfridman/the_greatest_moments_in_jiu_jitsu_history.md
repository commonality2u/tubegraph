Hodger Gracie's victory over Buchecha is a testament to the enduring legacy of traditional [[jiu_jitsu_training_and_techniques | Jiu Jitsu techniques]] and the strategic mind behind them. His approach to Jiu Jitsu showcases the combination of technical acuity, mental preparation, and the sheer will to dominate through submission rather than points<a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:48:03">[02:48:03]</a>.
