## Introduction
Thomas Tull, the founder of Legendary Entertainment, is a prominent figure in the film industry, renowned for producing some of the biggest blockbusters in recent history. His production credits include the Batman Dark Knight trilogy, The Hangover franchise, Godzilla, Inception, Jurassic World, and 300, among others. Despite his inexperience in films and television prior to Legendary, Tull's passion for storytelling and strategic partnerships propelled him to significant success in Hollywood <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:03">[00:00:03]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>.

## Building Legendary Entertainment

### Founding and Philosophy
In 2004, Tull founded Legendary Entertainment with a vision to produce films that would resonate globally while rooted in strong storytelling. His strategy was to provide institutional capital to the movie business, which at the time, lacked such financial backing despite being a $30 billion industry. His approach aimed at bringing long-term and disciplined investments into filmmaking <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:52">[00:03:52]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:10">[00:05:10]</a>.

### The Importance of Collaboration
Tull emphasizes the necessity of collaborating with great directors and using strong scripts to develop fresh yet respectful takes on iconic characters and stories <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:04">[00:02:04]</a>. His early collaboration with director Christopher Nolan is a testament to this approach, resulting in some of the most successful films of the time <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:56">[00:01:56]</a>.

### Major Productions and Success
Legendary Entertainment is synonymous with major cinematic success stories. Films like Jurassic World demanded massive commitments to visual effects and innovation, demonstrating the scale and ambition that Tull pushed for in Legendary's projects <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:49">[00:15:49]</a>. Moreover, Tull's admiration for figures like [[collaboration_with_james_cameron_and_quentin_tarantino | James Cameron]] reflects his interest in creative filmmaking and technology-driven film production <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:50">[00:19:50]</a>.
