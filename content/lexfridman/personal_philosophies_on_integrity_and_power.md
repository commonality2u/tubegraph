## The Role of Integrity in Investigative Journalism

Integrity is the backbone of journalism, especially within the realm of [investigative journalism](https://www.youtube.com/coffeezilla). For individuals like Coffeezilla, maintaining integrity is about resisting the temptation of financial incentives and instead focusing on uncovering truths. He emphasizes that real, impactful journalism isn't about maximizing personal gain but about holding individuals and institutions accountable for their actions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:52:10">[02:52:10]</a>.

This level of integrity involves a deep commitment to truthfulness and an aversion to manipulating facts for personal or financial gain. It's about being trustworthy, not just to one’s audience, but to oneself. Coffeezilla mentions that maintaining integrity brings a certain level of happiness and fulfillment that is invaluable <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:56:11">[02:56:11]</a>.

## Power and Accountability

The concept of power, as explored by Coffeezilla, is not just about the influence one wields but how that influence is used responsibly. Many of the subjects of his investigations, such as [[FTX]] and [[Sam Bankman-Fried]], exhibit [reckless use of power](https://www.youtube.com/coffeezilla), often prioritizing personal gain over ethical considerations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:00:00">[03:00:00]</a>.

It is crucial for those in positions of power to be held accountable. Coffeezilla stresses that one should never become complacent or overly trusting of those in power, as history has shown that unchecked power often leads to unethical behavior and systemic corruption. He believes that actively questioning and challenging those in power are vital functions of responsible journalism and societal progress <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:08:00">[03:08:00]</a>.

Coffeezilla’s journey is a testament to the power of choosing integrity over short-term gains. His focus on ethical journalism illustrates a commitment to truth that goes beyond financial reward, creating a lasting impact and earning public trust.
