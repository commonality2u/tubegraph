## Problems Identified

The development of HealthX addresses several key issues in the healthcare industry:
*   **Rising Costs** Healthcare spending is a significant and unsustainable financial burden <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>.
*   **Lack of [[healthcare_personalization | Personalization]]** There is a need for more tailored healthcare solutions that utilize data-driven decisions for individual health <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:28">[00:00:28]</a>.
*   **Shortage of Doctors and Facilities** Countries like India face a shortage of doctors and adequate medical facilities, especially in rural and remote areas <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:40">[00:00:40]</a>. This highlights the need for platforms that coordinate between doctors and patients <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>.
*   **Reactive Treatment Model** The current model often treats critical diseases (e.g., heart disease, lung cancer) reactively, rather than proactively <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:57">[00:00:57]</a>.
