## Business Model and Opportunities
The business model for [[ai_business_ideas | AI business ideas]] in companionship often mirrors "Drop Shipping" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:01">[00:02:01]</a>. Just as Drop Shipping uses an existing product supplier (like Alibaba) and focuses on marketing, AI companionship apps utilize existing powerful AI models (like OpenAI's API) to generate the "magic" interactions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:40">[00:02:40]</a>. The primary effort for entrepreneurs becomes the "marketing bit" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:49">[00:02:49]</a>, rather than developing the core AI technology.

This approach means that entrepreneurs who are skilled in "internet marketing" and understanding "how to make a funnel and how to market something" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:21">[00:20:21]</a> are well-positioned for success.
