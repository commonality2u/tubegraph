The business now operates on a strategy of "firing bullets and failing fast" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:00">[00:17:00]</a>. An "actionable insight" forms a "bullet," which is a "minimal viable investment into testing something" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:05:10">[01:05:10]</a>. If the bullet works, it becomes a "cannonball," prompting significant investment to "build the recipe around that" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:05:22">[01:05:22]</a>. This "fast fail" mindset, or "fast starts" in FMCG, focuses on rapid testing of minimal viable products to learn quickly what works and what doesn't <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:05:32">[01:05:32]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:05:50">[01:05:50]</a>.
