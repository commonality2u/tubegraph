## Optimizing the Artist's "Product"

The speaker views his entire career as a "product" that requires optimization. This includes:
*   **"Barbell Approach" to Content:** Creating independent content on platforms like Instagram and YouTube, while also leveraging larger platforms like Netflix for wider reach <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:29:18">[01:29:18]</a>.
*   **Audience Funnel:** Thinking about an audience funnel:
    *   **Top of Funnel:** Attracting new fans through mainstream exposure (e.g., morning shows) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:52:54">[01:52:54]</a>.
    *   **Middle of Funnel:** Converting them into first-time experiencers (e.g., attending a show) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:53:13">[01:53:13]</a>.
    *   **Bottom of Funnel:** Maximizing value and loyalty through deeper engagement and monetization opportunities like NFTs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:53:25">[01:53:25]</a>.
*   **End-to-End Experience:** Recognizing that the fan experience extends beyond the stage, encompassing everything from ticket purchasing to merchandise, which should be smooth and intentional <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:54:16">[01:54:16]</a>.

This comprehensive approach highlights the strategic blend of artistic passion and business acumen necessary for sustained success in show business, especially in a rapidly evolving digital landscape.
