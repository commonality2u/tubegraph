## Navigating the Entertainment Industry
The entertainment industry, like show business, is described as extremely "predatory," often relying on artists being desperate, uninformed, and not owning their intellectual property (IP) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:03:26">[01:03:26]</a>. To counter this, the speaker and his collaborators have started a company that independently writes, produces, and executive produces projects, maintaining full ownership and control <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:04:06">[01:04:06]</a>. This strategy aims to shift leverage back to the artists, allowing them to capture the value they create <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:21:53">[01:21:53]</a>.

He emphasizes the need for artists to become more financially literate to avoid common pitfalls, such as squandering lump sums or falling into debt <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:07:07">[01:07:07]</a>. This financial literacy allows artists to make informed decisions about their wealth, securing their future and creative freedom <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:09:01">[01:09:01]</a>. He seeks advice from trusted individuals who are experts in finance and have "skin in the game," ensuring their guidance is aligned with his best interests <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:18:54">[01:18:54]</a>.

## Cultivating a "No Waiting" Mindset
A key philosophy is the "no waiting" mindset: not deferring good feelings or desired actions until arbitrary future goals are met <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:39:55">[01:39:55]</a>. This means cultivating a feeling of confidence and security in the present, rather than waiting to feel it only after achieving a specific financial milestone <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:39:55">[01:39:55]</a>. By acting and carrying oneself as if success has already been achieved, it can lead to better performance and more opportunities, a concept akin to the "happiness effect" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:41:36">[01:41:36]</a>. This allows for a proactive and confident approach to career and life, rather than being driven by fear or scarcity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:40:05">[01:40:05]</a>.
