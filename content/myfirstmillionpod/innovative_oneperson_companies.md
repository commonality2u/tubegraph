## Characteristics of Companies of One

These businesses often demonstrate:
*   **Solo or Minimal Team:** Many are run entirely by one person, possibly with contractors, rather than a large staff <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:30:00">[03:30:00]</a>.
*   **High Revenue Potential:** Despite their small size, they can achieve millions in annual revenue <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:55:00">[03:55:00]</a>.
*   **Focus on Niche Problems:** They identify and solve specific, often overlooked, problems that still have a large user base <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:32:00">[01:32:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:53:00">[02:53:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:53:00">[03:53:00]</a>.
*   **Technical Proficiency & Creativity:** Founders often handle the entire stack (marketing, design, development), leading to innovative and unconstrained approaches <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:00:00">[02:00:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:03:00">[02:03:00]</a>.
*   **Bootstrapped Growth:** Many start without venture capital, often growing through strong user engagement or unique marketing <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:26:00">[02:26:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:44:00">[02:44:00]</a>.

## Other Examples of Lean and Innovative Businesses

*   **SurveyMonkey:** In 2009, SurveyMonkey reportedly generated $19 million in revenue with only 12 employees, demonstrating efficiency even if not a one-person company <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:57:00">[01:57:00]</a>.
*   **Craigslist:** Around 2016, Craigslist was making approximately $300 million in revenue with only about 14 employees working out of a small apartment office, highlighting extreme frugality and efficiency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:45:00">[01:45:00]</a>.
*   **Glitchin Deals:** Websites and Facebook groups dedicated to finding and sharing e-commerce pricing errors or "glitches" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:11:00">[02:11:00]</a>. The Glitchin Deals site gets 4 million page views a month, and an associated Facebook group has over 342,000 members with more than 250 posts daily <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:54:00">[02:54:00]</a>. This highlights a strong community around finding deals <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:27:00">[02:27:00]</a>.

*   **Nugget Comfort:**
    *   **Function:** A company that sells modular "pillow couches" for children, which can be rearranged into different shapes and forts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:04:00">[02:04:00]</a>.
    *   **Growth:** Started relatively recently (around a year old) and made over $4 million in sales in one month with zero ad spend <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:22:00">[02:22:00]</a>.
    *   **Team Size:** While not a one-person company, its rapid growth suggests a lean approach, evidenced by active hiring for various operational roles <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:45:00">[02:45:00]</a>. The product's success tapped into the "endless budget" parents often have for children's items <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:01:00">[03:01:00]</a>.

*   **Public Domain Review:**
    *   **Function:** A site that tracks intellectual property moving into the public domain, such as books and art <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:26:00">[03:26:00]</a>.
    *   **Monetization:** Sells prints of public domain art <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:47:00">[03:47:00]</a>.
    *   **Opportunity:** The entry of popular IPs like Winnie the Pooh into the public domain could create opportunities for new businesses in merchandising or adapted works <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:13:00">[03:13:00]</a>.

*   **Foodtimeline.org:**
    *   **Function:** A website that compiles the historical origins and first citations of various foods <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:44:00">[03:44:00]</a>.
    *   **Traffic:** Receives over 150,000 page views per month <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:20:00">[03:20:00]</a>.
    *   **Creator:** Lynn Oliver, a librarian and food historian, was the sole author, showcasing how individuals can dedicate themselves to niche, passion-driven projects <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:05:00">[03:05:00]</a>. The domain is set to expire in April 2022 due to her passing, presenting a potential opportunity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:56:00">[03:56:00]</a>.
