Artificial intelligence (AI) has the potential to significantly enhance business efficiency, intelligence, and customer service <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:27">[00:16:27]</a>, but it will not be implemented universally overnight <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:34">[00:16:34]</a>. ## The Consulting Opportunity

The core idea for AI-driven consulting is to combine conferences, content, and consulting services <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:42">[00:16:42]</a>. For individuals with expertise in strategy and implementation, such as those from traditional consulting firms like McKinsey, the path to building a successful AI consulting firm involves:
*   **Specialization** Identifying a specific type of customer, such as mid-market industrial companies, law firms, or dental practices <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:18">[00:17:18]</a>.
*   **AI Market Fit** Pinpointing a specific AI tool or process that directly benefits that chosen customer segment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:28">[00:17:28]</a>.
*   **Focused Consulting** Starting with targeted consulting services within that niche <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:34">[00:17:34]</a>.

This approach allows for the creation of a multi-million dollar service business that can expand over time <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:37">[00:17:37]</a>. It is predicted that billion-dollar AI-specific consulting firms will emerge, which major consulting companies (like Deloitte, PWC, and McKinsey) will eventually acquire <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:52">[00:17:52]</a>.

## Conferences and Content

Beyond direct consulting, building a comprehensive "conferences, content, and consulting" package involves strategic development of related events and educational materials <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:09">[00:18:09]</a>.

Instead of generic AI conferences, a more effective strategy is to create "AI for X" conferences <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:27">[00:18:27]</a>. These events would focus on how AI can be specifically applied within a particular industry or niche, such as:
*   AI for healthcare <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:31">[00:18:31]</a>
*   AI for farming <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:00">[00:19:00]</a>

Such targeted conferences would connect AI companies, startups, and experts with companies in specific verticals, demonstrating how AI can be used to build better businesses over the next three years <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:18:45">[00:18:45]</a>. This approach leverages the uneven distribution of technology by actively propagating AI knowledge and solutions to specific sectors <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:11">[00:16:11]</a>.
