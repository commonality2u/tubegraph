## Magic School: AI for Educators
Magic School is an [[AI in education and personalized tutoring | AI application]] specifically designed for educators <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:44:06">[00:44:06]</a>. It assists teachers in leveraging large language models (LLMs) and other foundation models to streamline their work, from creating assignments to providing interactive [[AI in education and personalized tutoring | AI]] experiences for students <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:44:09">[00:44:09]</a>. Essentially, it offers a comprehensive suite of [[AI in education and personalized tutoring | AI]] tools tailored for the education field <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:44:21">[00:44:21]</a>.

The creator of Magic School was a teacher who learned to code during the COVID-19 pandemic using the Replit platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:44:25">[00:44:25]</a>. Along with another individual, they built the initial version of Magic School entirely on Replit, taking an idea from conception to deployment within the same environment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:44:39">[00:44:39]</a>.

### Impact and Growth
Magic School experienced rapid, viral growth, with minimal need for traditional marketing <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:44:53">[00:44:53]</a>. Its revenue ramp has been described as one of the "craziest" ever seen in the education sector <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:44:59">[00:44:59]</a>. This is particularly notable given that selling into schools and reaching overworked, underpaid teachers has historically been considered one of the hardest markets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:45:02">[00:45:02]</a>.

The application addresses a significant pain point for teachers, who often spend several hours after school grading papers, creating lesson plans, quizzes, and multiple-choice tests <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:45:16">[00:45:16]</a>. Magic School allows educators to quickly generate these materials, such as lesson plans for fifth-grade biology or pop quizzes on mitosis <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:45:51">[00:45:51]</a>. This functionality can save teachers "four hours a night" on material creation, as [[AI in education and personalized tutoring | AI]] can perform these tasks for them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:46:04">[00:46:04]</a>.

As of its launch around July 2023 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:46:31">[00:46:31]</a>, Magic School boasts over four million educators and their students as users <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:46:15">[00:46:15]</a>, with millions of monthly unique visitors <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:46:22">[00:46:22]</a>. The company has also raised approximately $20 million in funding <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:46:25">[00:46:25]</a>, demonstrating its strong trajectory in the Software as a Service (SaaS) market <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:46:47">[00:46:47]</a>.
