The concept of "hosted buyer meetings on a plane" involves offering B2B decision-makers a free vacation in exchange for agreeing to a specified number of conversations with vendors during the flight <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:05">[00:00:05]</a>. This idea is likened to a timeshare offer, where a free flight is provided, but the plane is filled with vendors <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:09">[00:00:09]</a>. The visual is humorously compared to the movie "Snakes on a Plane," but instead of snakes, it's B2B software vendors ready to "barrage" passengers for hours on their way to destinations like Maui <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:19">[00:00:19]</a>.

## Business Potential
This concept is discussed as a potential [[ai_business_ideas|startup idea]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:54">[00:09:54]</a>. Despite the humorous "Snakes on a Plane" comparison, it's acknowledged that such a business could be built, especially if incentives like first-class travel are offered for signing up for a free trial <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:36">[00:10:36]</a>. This type of event could facilitate [[networking_and_meeting_influential_people|networking and meeting influential people]] by providing a captive audience for B2B interactions.

This business model leverages the idea of packaging value differently, shifting from brands paying for advertising to customers paying for insights or curated experiences <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:04">[05:04]</a>.
