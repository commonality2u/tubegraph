## The Vision of AI Tutors
Imagine a future where everyone has their own AI tutor <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:56">[00:24:56]</a>. This AI would be:
*   **Patient and Infinitely Intelligent** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:00">[00:25:00]</a>: Capable of assisting with any problem.
*   **Interactive and Flexible** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:12">[00:25:12]</a>: Users could take a picture of a problem and ask the AI to explain it, provide more examples, or adjust the pace of instruction (slower or faster) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:07">[00:25:07]</a>.
*   **Assessment-Capable** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:18">[00:25:18]</a>: The tutor could quiz users after explanations to ensure comprehension <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:18">[00:25:18]</a>.
*   **Visually Engaging** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:23">[00:25:23]</a>: Able to draw and animate diagrams faster and more effectively than a human tutor using a chalkboard <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:25">[00:25:25]</a>.
*   **Personalized Curriculum Development** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:33">[00:25:33]</a>: The AI could track a student's mastery and areas needing improvement, then customize a curriculum to fit their individual needs, moving beyond a standard textbook approach <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:33">[00:25:33]</a>.

This technology is expected to revolutionize teaching and learning, especially for those who are motivated to learn <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:45">[00:25:45]</a>.
