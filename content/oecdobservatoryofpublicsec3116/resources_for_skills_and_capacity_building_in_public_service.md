## The OPSI Toolkit Navigator: A Guiding Compass

To address this challenge, the OPSI Toolkit Navigator was developed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>. This resource connects a specific situation, problem, or challenge to the relevant toolkit that can assist in finding a solution <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>. Its primary function is to filter out clutter, allowing [[empowering public servants | public servants]] to see a clearer path forward <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>.

### Development of the Navigator

The OPSI Toolkit Navigator was created in collaboration with hundreds of [[empowering public servants | public servants]] and [[Leadership and collaboration in public service | leaders]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>. The development process involved several key steps:
1.  **Analysis and Organization** Existing toolkits were analyzed and organized based on the actions they inform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:04">[00:01:04]</a>.
2.  **Situation Exploration** The types of situations, problems, and challenges commonly faced by [[empowering public servants | public servants]] were thoroughly explored <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:11">[00:01:11]</a>.
3.  **Conceptualization of Needs** Researchers investigated how [[empowering public servants | public servants]] typically describe their desired actions or conceptualize their problems <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:18">[00:01:18]</a>.
4.  **Navigational Path Design** Navigational path options were designed to bridge the gap between situations and tools, enabling the OPSI Toolkit Navigator to suggest tools based on desired actions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:24">[00:01:24]</a>.
