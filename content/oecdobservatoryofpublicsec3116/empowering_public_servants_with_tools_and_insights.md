## Role of the OECD Observatory of Public Sector Innovation (OPSI)

The OECD Observatory of Public Sector Innovation (OPSI) is dedicated to positioning [[Innovation in public services | innovation]] at the core of government operations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:36">[00:00:36]</a>. OPSI aims to empower public servants by equipping them with new insights, tools, and connections, thereby enabling them to explore new possibilities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>. This support helps in the [[Developing Leadership Skills for Public Sector Innovation | development of leadership skills]] and fosters [[Innovation Skills for Public Sector | innovation skills]] among public sector employees, contributing to their [[Public Service Skills and Development | professional development]].

### Key Activities

OPSI's work involves several key activities:
*   Conducting cutting-edge research to uncover emerging trends <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:55">[00:00:55]</a>.
*   Collaborating with leading experts to provide trusted advice <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>.
*   Transforming novel ideas and approaches into standard practices <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:02">[00:01:02]</a>.

These efforts contribute to [[Impact of insights on public policy | impacting public policy]] by integrating new knowledge and innovative methods into governance.
