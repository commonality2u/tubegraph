## Key Technological Innovations and Programs

Portugal's Administrative Modernization Agency (AMA) actively contributes to public service innovation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:32:00">[00:32:00]</a>.
*   **Citizen Shops**: Introduced in the late 1990s as one-stop service centers, these facilities allow citizens to interact with multiple public services in one trip <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:22:00">[01:22:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:24:00">[01:24:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:19:00">[05:19:00]</a>. *   **Digital Identification**: The national identification card has evolved to include multiple functionalities in a single card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:24:00">[01:24:00]</a>.

*   **Simplex Program**: Portugal's flagship innovation and modernization program for the past 15 years <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:24:00">[07:24:00]</a>. This program enabled a rapid adaptive response to the 2020 pandemic crisis, releasing about 200 measures in weeks, based on broad interaction with citizens, companies, and civil society <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:33:00">[07:33:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:41:00">[07:41:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:50:00">[07:50:00]</a>.
*   **LabX**: The laboratory for experimentation in public administration, which brings civic participation to the forefront of public administration work <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:05:00">[07:05:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:11:00">[07:11:00]</a>.
*   **"On at 18" Project**: A project aimed at understanding how to best provide public services to young people, focusing on redesigning their [[role_of_digital_advancements_in_public_sector_innovation | digital experience]] with government, using plain language, providing support, and aiming for a single point of contact for relevant services <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:02:40">[01:02:40]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:03:00">[01:03:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:04:30">[01:04:30]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:05:10">[01:05:10]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:05:37">[01:05:37]</a>.

### Japan's Governance Innovation
Japan's Ministry of Economy, Trade and Industry (METI) promotes "Society 5.0," a human-centric society driven by cyber-physical systems <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:07:00">[03:07:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:09:00">[03:09:00]</a>. *   **Dynamic goal definition**: Goals of governance continuously change with technology and society <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:43:00">[04:43:00]</a>.
*   **System design**: Not just technological, but also organizational systems and applicable rules <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:51:00">[04:51:00]</a>.
*   **Continuous monitoring and disclosure**: Based on real-time data, with proper disclosure to stakeholders for accountability <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:04:00">[04:04:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:06:00">[04:06:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:08:00">[04:08:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:11:00">[04:11:00]</a>.

### United States' 10x Program
The 10x program by the General Services Administration (GSA) aims to improve the public's experience with the US government by taking a venture capital mindset and adapting it to a government context <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:13:25">[01:13:25]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:13:28">[01:13:28]</a>.
