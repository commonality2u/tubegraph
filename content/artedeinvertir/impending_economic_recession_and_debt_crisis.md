*   **Purest Form of Money** Gold is seen as the "purest form of money" because no government controls it, and unlike fiat currencies, it cannot be printed and constantly devalued <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:12">[00:12:12]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:23">[00:12:23]</a>.
