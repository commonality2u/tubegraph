### David Tepper's Investment Philosophy

David Tepper, founder of Appaloosa Management, has one of the best track records in money management over the last 30 years <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>. He has generated an impressive annual return of 25% for clients, surpassing even Warren Buffett during that period <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:28">[00:01:28]</a>. Tepper is known for being one of the first to buy stocks during times of market panic when other investors are hesitant <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:40">[00:01:40]</a>. His best years for returns have consistently followed these periods of market panic, as he is able to invest at the most attractive prices <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:06">[00:02:06]</a>.

In a recent interview, Tepper stated that while the market could fall another 10-15% if panic continues, the approval of a package of measures by the US Congress could signal the end of the falls <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:35">[00:02:35]</a>. These economic measures were approved two days later <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:09">[00:03:09]</a>.

Tepper emphasizes that investors should not buy just any stock, regardless of how much it has fallen <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:30">[00:03:30]</a>. Instead, it is crucial to [[investment_opportunities_in_traditional_companies | invest in good companies]] with solid competitive positions and low debt <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:37">[00:03:37]</a>. Weaker, indebted companies are less likely to navigate difficult economic periods <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:40">[00:03:40]</a>. He is confident that many companies will be worth significantly more in one to two years than their current trading prices <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:56">[00:03:56]</a>.

### United Healthcare (Healthcare Sector)

United Healthcare, listed in the US under the ticker UNH, was identified as a favored stock in the healthcare sector by David Tepper's investment vehicle, Appaloosa Management <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:47">[00:10:47]</a>.

*   **Business Overview**: United Healthcare is a leading provider of private healthcare plans in the US, operating within an oligopoly controlled by four companies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:26">[00:11:26]</a>. It is considered a very defensive business, as people rarely cancel their healthcare insurance even during crises <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:34">[00:11:34]</a>. The company increased both sales and profits during the 2008 and 2009 crisis <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:40">[00:11:40]</a>.
*   **Performance**: Over the last 30 years, United Healthcare shares have risen from $1 to over $240 per share <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:49">[00:11:49]</a>.
*   **Valuation**:
    *   The company maintains low debt <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:58">[00:11:58]</a>.
    *   It is a mature business expected to grow at 5-10% annually for the next few years <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:01">[00:12:01]</a>.
    *   Last year, it earned approximately $15 per share <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:06">[00:12:06]</a>.
    *   Expected earnings are projected to exceed $20 per share in three years <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:12">[00:12:12]</a>.
    *   Such high-quality, predictable businesses with low debt and strong history should not trade below 20 times earnings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:18">[00:12:18]</a>.
    *   Applying a 20x multiple to future expected earnings of $21 per share results in a target [[valuation_and_performance_of_specific_stocks_and_sectors | valuation]] of over $400 per share <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:28">[00:12:28]</a>. This represents a significant potential upside from its current trading price of $240 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:37">[00:12:37]</a>.
