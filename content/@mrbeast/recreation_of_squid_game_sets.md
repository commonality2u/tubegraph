Mr. Beast's recreation of the Squid Game sets was a colossal endeavor, meticulously designed to mirror the original sets seen in the series. From the oversized playground reminiscent of "Red Light, Green Light" to the intricate glass bridge, every set was crafted with attention to detail to ensure both aesthetic and functional authenticity.
