This is a "step function change" compared to the Falcon 9, which can lift 17 metric tons to orbit at a variable cost of around $15 million <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:07">[00:05:07]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:22">[00:05:22]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:29">[00:05:29]</a>. This dramatic cost reduction "massively changes the economics" for projects like [[starship_and_spacexs_progress | Starlink]] and sending anything into orbit <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:00">[00:06:00]</a>.
