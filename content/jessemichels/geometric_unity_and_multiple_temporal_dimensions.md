Geometric Unity postulates the existence of multiple temporal dimensions beyond the single time dimension we currently understand <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:27:09">[02:27:09]</a>. Two strong versions of Geometric Unity are discussed:
*   **Version 1** Six extra time dimensions, leading to a total of seven time dimensions, and four extra spatial dimensions, resulting in seven spatial dimensions. This is referred to as a "split signature metric seven and seven" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:26:31">[02:26:31]</a>.

*   **Multiple Futures** Instead of a single arrow of time, there would be more than one future <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:27:34">[02:27:34]</a>.
*   **Absence of a Single Arrow of Time** Time would become a "whirlpool of time" or could be described by a "right-hand rule of time," indicating a time orientation rather than a linear progression <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:27:34">[02:27:34]</a>.
*   **Non-Retracing Travel** It suggests a new possibility: the ability to return to an earlier event by moving through an extra time dimension without having to retrace time steps <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:27:59">[02:27:59]</a>.
*   **Ultra-Hyperbolic Equations** These concepts lead to complex "ultra-hyperbolic equations," which are not yet well-understood or widely studied in physics <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:28:19">[02:28:19]</a>.
