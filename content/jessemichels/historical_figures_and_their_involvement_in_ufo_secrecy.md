## Edward Condon
Edward Condon, a quantum physicist and close friend of J. It's speculated that Condon may have been blackmailed, as an FBI dossier was compiled on him during his investigation for communist sympathies by agent Guy Hoddle <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:03">[00:08:03]</a>. Hoddle is famous for his 1952 memo describing a crashed [[historical_accounts_of_ufos | UFO]] with an alien crew <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:16">[00:08:16]</a>.

This connection suggests that past public-facing [[historical_accounts_of_ufos | UFO]] debunkers might have been "Atomic Insiders" involved in [[historical_government_involvement_with_ufo_investigations | UFO reverse engineering programs]] all along <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:17">[00:06:17]</a>.
