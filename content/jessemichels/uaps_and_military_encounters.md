Senator Ted Stevens of Alaska, a World War II pilot, admitted to [[experiences_and_testimonials_of_military_personnel witnessing_ufo_activities | encountering "Foo Fighters"]] during the war <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:22:00">[02:22:00]</a>.
