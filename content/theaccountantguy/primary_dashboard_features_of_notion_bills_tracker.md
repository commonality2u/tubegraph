The primary dashboard of the [[creating_a_bills_tracker_in_notion | Notion bills tracker]] provides a comprehensive overview of financial obligations and expenditures <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:48:09">[05:48:09]</a>. ## Summary Section

The [[summary_section_of_notion_bills_tracker | summary section]] is divided into three key subsections <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:55">[00:00:55]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:53:50">[05:53:50]</a>:

*   **Upcoming Bills** This subsection displays bills that are pending payment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:57">[00:00:57]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:55:27">[05:55:27]</a>. It includes the amount due, the due date of the bill, the number of days remaining until payment is required, and the current status of payment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:01">[00:01:01]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:05">[00:01:05]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:57:46">[05:57:46]</a>.
*   **Past Due Bills** This section highlights bills that have exceeded their due date <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:57">[00:00:57]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:08:52">[06:08:52]</a>. It shows the expense amount, the original due date, the number of days past due, and the payment status <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:09:59">[06:09:59]</a>.
*   **Bills Paid** This part of the summary lists all bills that have been successfully paid on time <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:59">[00:00:59]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:20:25">[06:20:25]</a>. Details include the amount, the due date, and the bill's payment status <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:22:15">[06:22:15]</a>.

## Bills Distribution by Priority

This section categorizes bills by their priority level: low, medium, or high <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:20">[00:01:20]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:27:54">[06:27:54]</a>. For each priority, it displays the total amount incurred and the percentage of bills paid within that priority <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:25">[00:01:25]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:32:02">[06:32:02]</a>.

## Bills Distribution by Category

Bills are further classified into six distinct categories: entertainment, food and groceries, utility bills, travel and transportation, loans and debts, and others <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:33">[00:01:33]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:36:20">[06:36:20]</a>. Each category shows the total amount paid and the percentage of bills paid for that specific classification <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:42">[00:01:42]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:46:17">[06:46:17]</a>.
