This article details how to utilize a Notion template to track credit card expenses and manage payments effectively <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:00">[00:00:00]</a>. The tracker helps monitor all credit card payments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:05">[00:00:05]</a>.

## Tracker Overview Sections

The tracker is structured with several key sections to provide a comprehensive financial overview:

### Credit Cards Overview
This section displays all added credit cards, such as "Student Credit Card," "Cash Back Credit Card," "Travel Credit Card," and "Rewards Credit Card" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:15">[00:00:15]</a>. For each card, it shows:
*   The total amount due <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:23">[00:00:23]</a>.
*   The amount that has been repaid so far <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>.
*   The remaining balance amount due <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>.

### Monthly Overview
This section provides a summary of amounts due, repaid, and the balance amount due for each month <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:32">[00:00:32]</a>. This view can be customized to individual requirements <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[00:00:37]</a>.

### Yearly Overview
Similar to the monthly overview, this section shows the total amount due, amount repaid, and the balance amount due across different years <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.

### Expense Detail Section
This area lists all expenses that have been added in detail <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:51">[00:00:51]</a>. This is where individual transactions are recorded.

### Expenses Overview
This section offers a summary of all expenses categorized and booked <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>. It includes:
*   Total expenses added <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:05">[00:01:05]</a>.
*   Budgeted amount for each category <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:05">[00:01:05]</a>.
*   Comparison of expenses to the budgeted amount <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:09">[00:01:09]</a>.
*   Budget status (under budget or over budget) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:11">[00:01:11]</a>.

### Notification Section
This section notifies users about upcoming repayment dates and the number of days remaining until the next repayment for each credit card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a>.

## Using the Tracker: Booking Expenses

To begin [[using_notion_to_track_credit_card_expenses | tracking credit card expenses]], you start by booking expenses in the **Expense Details Overview section** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>.

### Expense Details Section Fields
When entering an expense, you will fill in the following columns:
*   **Date**: The date of the expense <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:42">[00:01:42]</a>.
*   **Expense Details**: A description of the expense <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:44">[00:01:44]</a>.
*   **Period**: The accounting period for the expense <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:46">[00:01:46]</a>.
*   **Year**: The year of the expense <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:49">[00:01:49]</a>.
*   **Credit Card**: The specific credit card used for the expense <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:51">[00:01:51]</a>.
*   **Category**: The assigned category for the expense <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:54">[00:01:54]</a>.
*   **Expense Amount**: The monetary value of the expense <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:57">[00:01:57]</a>.
*   **Repayment Section**: Where you record payments made to the credit card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:00">[00:02:00]</a>.

You can also enter an **opening balance due** for any balances carried forward from previous months <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:08">[00:02:08]</a>.

### Adding New Credit Cards and Categories
*   To add a new credit card, you can type its name in the "Credit Card" column, and the system will prompt you to create it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:33">[00:02:33]</a>.
*   Similarly, you can add new categories by entering details in the category section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:22">[00:05:22]</a>. Existing credit cards and categories can also be edited <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:16">[00:05:16]</a>.

### Adding Receipts
There is a "receipt column" where you can attach bills or receipts for your expenses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:34">[00:05:34]</a>.

## Tracking Expenses and Payments

Once expenses are booked, the tracker automatically updates the overview sections <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:07">[00:03:07]</a>.

### Expenses Overview
This section allows you to manage expense categories and budgets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:42">[00:03:42]</a>.
*   You can see the total expenses incurred for each category <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:52">[00:03:52]</a>.
*   **Setting Budgeted Amount**: Click on the "budget amount" column to enter or adjust the budgeted amount for each category <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:56">[00:03:56]</a>.
*   **Budget Status**: The tracker indicates if expenses are "under budget" or "over budget" compared to your set amount <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:19">[00:04:19]</a>. For example, if expenses are $5,300 against a $5,000 budget, it will show "over budget" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:14">[00:04:14]</a>.

### Notification Section
This section helps you stay on top of [[debt_payment_tracking_using_notion | debt repayment]]. You can set your own repayment dates for each credit card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:35">[00:04:35]</a>. It will then display how many days are pending until the next repayment is due <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:49">[00:04:49]</a>, helping you pay credit card debts on time <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:05">[00:05:05]</a>.

## Detailed Views and Customization

The tracker provides multiple ways to view and summarize your financial data, offering flexibility similar to [[setting_up_a_database_in_notion_for_financial_tracking | setting up a database in Notion for financial tracking]].

### Summarization Views
*   **Period View**: Clicking on a specific period (e.g., month) opens a new window showing transactions for that period, including dates, details, amounts, and tags (green for repayment, regular for expense) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:44">[00:05:44]</a>.
*   **Year View**: Clicking on a year provides similar detailed transaction information for that particular year <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:14">[00:06:14]</a>.
*   **Credit Card View**: Selecting a specific credit card displays all transactions related to it, including status and categories <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:24">[00:06:24]</a>. You can change the status and add categories for each credit card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:36">[00:06:36]</a>.
*   **Category View**: Clicking on any category will show all expenses associated with it, providing relevant details for each <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:48">[00:06:48]</a>.
