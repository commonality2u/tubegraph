Maintenance tracking for assets is a key component of an asset planner template, falling under the [[asset_operational_management | operational management]] section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:08">[00:00:08]</a>. This section specifically addresses maintenance details related to an asset <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:12">[00:00:12]</a>.

## Maintenance Section
The dedicated maintenance section within the template covers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>:
*   Maintenance ID <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>
*   Asset ID <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>
*   Everything else related to the maintenance details of an asset <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>

## Related Asset Management Sections
In addition to maintenance, the asset planner also includes other operational and regulatory management sections:
*   **Asset Details**: Covers information such as asset ID, name, and type <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>.
*   **[[financial_management_of_assets | Financial Management]]**: Displays current value, depreciation method, and depreciation rate <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:33">[00:00:33]</a>.
*   **[[asset_performance_analysis | Asset Performance]]**: Shows asset performance as it is created <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[00:00:37]</a>.
*   **[[compliance_and_documentation_management_for_assets | Regulatory Management]]**: Includes sections for compliance and documentation related to the asset <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>.
*   **Reviews**: Allows users to add reviews about the assets being used <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:53">[00:00:53]</a>.

The template is fully customizable to suit specific requirements <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:59">[00:00:59]</a>.
