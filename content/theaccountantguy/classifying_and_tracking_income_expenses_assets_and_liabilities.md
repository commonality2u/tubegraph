This article outlines how to use a Notion template for [[Classifying and managing income and expenses | small business bookkeeping]], enabling users to [[Tracking income expenses and cash flow movements | track income, expenses, cash flow movements]], assets, and liabilities efficiently <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>.

## Template Structure
The Notion template is divided into three main sections <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>:
1.  **Start Here**: This section is used to enter foundational business information <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:40">[00:00:40]</a>.
2.  **Summary**: Provides an overview of all entered transactions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>.
3.  **Transactions Window**: Where all business transaction details are entered <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:35">[00:00:35]</a>.

## Setting Up Accounts
The "Start Here" section features an account section for [[Classifying accounts for financial tracking | classifying accounts]] into categories such as <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>:
*   **Expenses**: Examples include purchase expenses, utility bills, subscriptions, and salaries and wages <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:58">[00:00:58]</a>.
*   **Liabilities**: Examples include accounts payable, taxes payable, accrued expenses, and credit card debt <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:04">[00:01:04]</a>.
*   **Income** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>
*   **Assets** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:54">[00:00:54]</a>

### Account Management
Users can customize these accounts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>:
*   **Editing**: Hover over an item, click the pencil icon, edit details, and click outside to save <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:12">[00:01:12]</a>.
*   **Deleting**: Click the three-dot menu next to an item and select "delete" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:22">[00:01:22]</a>.
*   **Adding**: Scroll to the relevant category and click "new page" to add a new account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:28">[00:01:28]</a>.

### Account Properties
Each account item includes several customizable properties <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:43">[00:01:43]</a>:
*   **Opening Balance**: Users can enter opening balances for liabilities and assets that are carried forward <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:46">[00:01:46]</a>.
*   **Classification**: Items can be classified as bills, debt, or savings, etc. <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:53">[00:01:53]</a>. For example, utility bills are classified as "bills," and subscriptions as "subscription" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:01">[00:02:01]</a>.
*   **Payment Frequency**: For recurring payments (e.g., monthly, quarterly, half-yearly) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:12">[00:02:12]</a>.
*   **Opening Units**: For inventory or assets at the start of the business <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:27">[00:02:27]</a>.
*   **Asset Class**: Can be set as either "inventory" or "investment" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:42">[00:02:42]</a>.

## Recording Transactions
The "Transactions" section allows for detailed entry of all business financial movements <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:04">[00:03:04]</a>.

### Adding Transactions
Users can add specific transaction types by clicking dedicated buttons <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:08">[00:03:08]</a>:
*   **Add Income** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:10">[00:03:10]</a>
*   **Add Expense** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:01">[00:06:01]</a>
*   **Add Asset** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:03">[00:06:03]</a>
*   **Add Liability** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:04">[00:06:04]</a>

### Transaction Details
Each transaction includes the following fields <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:19">[00:04:19]</a>:
*   **Date**: Automatically set to the current date, but can be changed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:13">[00:03:13]</a>.
*   **Period**: Users can select or add the specific month and year (e.g., "October 2024") <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:24">[00:03:24]</a>.
*   **Description**: A brief explanation of the transaction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:42">[00:03:42]</a>.
*   **Category**: Automatically mapped (e.g., "income" if "Add Income" was clicked) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:45">[00:03:45]</a>.
*   **Account**: Linked to the accounts set up in the "Start Here" section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:50">[00:03:50]</a>. New accounts added here will also appear in the "Start Here" section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:05">[00:04:05]</a>.
*   **Payment Mode**: How the payment was received or made (e.g., bank account, cash account) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:21">[00:04:21]</a>.
*   **Transaction Type**: Can be "inflow" (money coming in) or "outflow" (money going out) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:51">[00:04:51]</a>.
*   **Amount**: The monetary value of the transaction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:07">[00:05:07]</a>.
*   **Document**: Option to attach relevant documents <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:15">[00:05:15]</a>.
*   **Tax Rate and Tax Amount**: If a tax rate is entered, the tax amount is automatically calculated <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:21">[00:05:21]</a>. For non-taxable transactions, the rate can be set to 0% <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:27">[00:05:27]</a>.
*   **Multiplier and Currency**: For multi-currency transactions, a multiplier can be added to convert between currencies (e.g., USD to Euro) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:36">[00:05:36]</a>.

### Investment and Inventory Tracking
The transactions window also includes sections for tracking <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:07">[00:06:07]</a>:
*   **Investments**: Details like units, price, total value, and investment value <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:09">[00:06:09]</a>. It also tracks profit or loss from selling investments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:17">[00:06:17]</a>.
*   **Inventory**: Shows current inventory levels and profit or loss obtained <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:26">[00:06:26]</a>.

## Financial Summary
The "Summary" section automatically updates with information from entered transactions, providing a comprehensive overview of the business's financial health <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:49">[00:06:49]</a>.

### Finances Overview
This section offers an "entire glance of the business" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:54">[00:06:54]</a>, including:
*   **Assets**: Breakdown of accounts receivable, input taxes, other receivables, and cash value <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:05">[00:07:05]</a>.
*   **Income Sources** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:01">[00:07:01]</a>
*   **Liabilities** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:02">[00:07:02]</a>
*   **Expenses** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:03">[00:07:03]</a>

### Detailed Financial Tracking
The summary further breaks down financial data into specific categories:
*   **Debt**: [[Debt and liability tracking | Tracks debt payments]], including past payments and upcoming payments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:23">[00:07:23]</a>.
*   **Budgeting**: Shows budgeted expenses and income, and the percentage of expenses incurred against the budget, aiding [[Income and expense tracking with budget targets | budget targets]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:03">[00:08:03]</a>.
*   **Savings**: Displays accumulated savings for planned funds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:16">[00:08:16]</a>.
*   **Bills**: Similar to the debt section, it shows past payments for bills <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:22">[00:08:22]</a>.
*   **Subscriptions**: Provides a similar overview to the bills section for subscriptions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:31">[00:08:31]</a>.
*   **Tax**: Quarterly taxes payable, available tax credit, and total tax liability are displayed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:35">[00:08:35]</a>.
*   **Net Worth**: Compares a user-set net worth goal with the total calculated net worth, showing the difference, total growth, and percentage of the goal achieved <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:51">[00:08:51]</a>.
*   **Investments**: Summarizes the value and units of current investments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:36">[00:09:36]</a>.
*   **Inventory**: Displays the status of inventory <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:43">[00:09:43]</a>.
*   **Overall Summary**: Presents total income, total expenses, gross profit, taxes paid, and the business's net profit <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:47">[00:09:47]</a>.

By using this template, users can effectively [[managing_funds_and_balance_tracking | manage funds and track balances]], gaining a clear financial picture of their business <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:54">[00:09:54]</a>. The process involves setting up accounts, entering transactions, and reviewing the automatically updated summary sections <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:58">[00:09:58]</a>.
