The [[business_plan_template_in_Notion | business plan template]] in Notion includes an "events timeline" view, which is designed to help visualize planned business activities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:10">[03:10]</a>.

## Overview
The events timeline presents all previously discussed planning elements in a calendar and timeline format <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:10">[03:10]</a>. This view provides a quick overview of all planned activities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:35">[03:35]</a>.

## Information Displayed
The timeline shows:
*   Specific deadline dates for [[stages_of_business_planning | stages]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:17">[03:17]</a>. For example, a timeline might show December 3 to December 20 for specific stages <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:21">[03:21]</a>.
*   The [[stages_of_business_planning | stage]] name <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:28">[03:28]</a>.
*   The [[task_management_within_business_stages | tasks]] associated with each [[stages_of_business_planning | stage]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:30">[03:30]</a>.
*   The date for each [[task_management_within_business_stages | task]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:32">[03:32]</a>.
*   The status of the [[task_management_within_business_stages | task]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:35">[03:35]</a>.
*   Different dates and necessary properties specified for all [[stages_of_business_planning | stages]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:44">[03:44]</a>.

## Navigation
Users can see little arrows on the timeline that reflect specific dates <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:40">[03:40]</a>. Clicking on these arrows will navigate to the specific date, allowing users to view the details of all [[stages_of_business_planning | stages]] and their associated properties <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:50">[03:50]</a>.
