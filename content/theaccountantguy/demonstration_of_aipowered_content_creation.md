## Content Creation Options

Wizen provides two primary methods for [[ai_content_generation_for_notion | generating AI content]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:10">[00:00:10]</a>:
*   **Notion Page** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:11">[00:00:11]</a>: A simple blank page within Notion <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>.
*   **Notion Database** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:11">[00:00:11]</a>: Content structured in rows and columns <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>.

## [[creating_ai_generated_content_on_a_notion_page | Creating Content on Notion Pages]]

To [[creating_ai_generated_content_on_a_notion_page | generate AI content on a Notion page]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:22">[00:00:22]</a>, the process involves:
1.  **Setting up a connection** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:35">[00:00:35]</a>: This includes naming the connection (e.g., "notion page") and selecting "Notion page" as the content destination <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:36">[00:00:36]</a>.
2.  **Providing the Notion Page URL** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.
3.  **Entering a prompt** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:48">[00:00:48]</a>: For example, "write me a block of 100 words on notion" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>.

Once the prompt is entered, the AI immediately generates the content directly onto the specified Notion page <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:06">[00:01:06]</a>.

## [[generating_ai_content_on_a_notion_database | Generating Content in Notion Databases]]

For [[generating_ai_content_on_a_notion_database | generating AI content on a Notion database]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:19">[00:01:19]</a>:
1.  **Setting up a connection** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:21">[00:01:21]</a>: Similar to a page, this involves naming (e.g., "notion database") and selecting "Notion database" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:23">[00:01:23]</a>.
2.  **Providing the Notion Database URL** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:27">[00:01:27]</a>.
3.  **Entering a prompt based on database topics** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>: If the database contains multiple topics in different rows, a prompt like "write me a block of 100 words on the topic" can be used <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:48">[00:01:48]</a>.

The AI then populates the content for each individual topic within the database, generating distinct content for each row <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:01">[00:02:01]</a>. Wizen effectively utilizes this to [[generating_content_in_notion_databases | generate AI content for Notion databases]] as well as [[creating_content_on_notion_pages | Notion pages]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:22">[00:02:22]</a>.
