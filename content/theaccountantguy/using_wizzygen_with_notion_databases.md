[[Introduction to Wizzygen | Wizzygen]] is a platform that facilitates the creation of AI-generated content directly within a Notion workspace <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:04">[00:00:04]</a>. It offers two primary options for content generation: Notion pages and Notion databases <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:11">[00:00:11]</a>.

## Understanding Notion Databases

In Notion, a database is structured in rows and columns <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>. [[creating_a_database_in_notion | Wizzygen]] can generate AI content directly into these databases <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:22">[00:00:22]</a>.

## Generating Content in a Notion Database

To generate AI content within a Notion database using [[introduction_to_wizzygen | Wizzygen]], follow these steps:

1.  **Name the connection** Provide a name for the connection, for example, "notion database" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:21">[00:01:21]</a>.
2.  **Select "Notion database"** Choose the "Notion database" option from the available selections <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:25">[00:01:25]</a>.
3.  **Provide the Notion database URL** Navigate to your Notion database, copy its URL, and paste it into the designated URL section in [[introduction_to_wizzygen | Wizzygen]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:27">[00:01:27]</a>.
4.  **Define the content prompt** Specify the type of content you want the AI to generate for each topic within the database <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>. For example, you can request a 100-word blog for each topic present in the database <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:42">[00:01:42]</a>.
5.  **Initiate generation** Once the prompt is set, the AI will begin populating the database with the generated content <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:58">[00:01:58]</a>. The content will be generated and displayed within the respective rows of the database <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:07">[00:02:07]</a>.

This process allows [[introduction_to_wizzygen | Wizzygen]] to efficiently generate AI content for multiple entries in a Notion database, capturing every row <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:17">[00:02:17]</a>.
