The employee paycheck tracker is designed to help keep track of all employee payments in one central location <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:04">[00:00:04]</a>.

## Employee Records Overview
The "records" section of the template contains comprehensive [[employee_details_management_in_notion | employee details]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>. This includes:
*   Whether employees are permanent <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:16">[00:00:16]</a>.
*   Payments due to them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>.
*   Number of months of payment due <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:19">[00:00:19]</a>.
*   How employees are paid (monthly or hourly basis) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>.
*   The amount of payment being made <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>.
This section serves as a complete record of all employees <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:31">[00:00:31]</a>.

## Adding New Employees
To add a new employee to the system, simply click on the "New" option within the records section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:33">[00:00:33]</a>.

## Adding and Editing Categories/Departments
*   **Adding a New Category**: To add a new category, click on the "+" button <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[00:00:37]</a>.
*   **Editing Existing Departments**: Existing employee departments can be edited by clicking on them and modifying the details <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.

## Managing Employee Payment Details
Beneath the main summary, you can input specific payment details for each employee <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:04">[00:01:04]</a>.

### Payment Fields
*   **Date**: The date of the payment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>.
*   **Period**: Specify the period for which the payment is being made <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:09">[00:01:09]</a>.
*   **Description**: A description of the payment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:13">[00:01:13]</a>.
*   **Employee Details**: Select the specific employee from the list of those you have added <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a>.
*   **Pay Rate**:
    *   For hourly paid employees, specify the pay rate and the number of hours worked, and the system will automatically calculate the payment amount <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:20">[00:01:20]</a>.
    *   For monthly paid employees, specify the pay rate and leave the "hours" column blank to indicate a monthly payment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:28">[00:01:28]</a>.
*   **Payment Status**: A column indicates whether a payment has been made. Unticking this box will show the payment as unpaid <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:34">[00:01:34]</a>.

The system also divides payments into "permanent" and "contractual" sections based on the details entered <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:53">[00:01:53]</a>. This contributes to the overall [[managing_employee_payment_records | management of employee payment records]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:06">[00:00:06]</a>.
