## Upgrading Subscription Plans

Users operating under the free plan will observe a "made with [[introduction_to_pdfoutput_tool | PDFOutput]]" watermark on their generated PDF documents <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:18">[00:10:18]</a>. To remove this watermark and gain a higher limit on PDF generation, users can upgrade to any of the available paid plans <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:22">[00:10:22]</a>.

To upgrade:
1.  Navigate to the "Upgrade" option in the sidebar <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:18">[00:10:18]</a>.
2.  Click on "Upgrade subscription" to view the available plans <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:30">[00:10:30]</a>.
3.  After upgrading a plan, click on "Activate subscription" to apply the changes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:35">[00:10:35]</a>.

## [[using_pdfoutput_settings_and_subscription_options | Configuring PDF Settings and Properties]]

The "Settings" section allows users to define various PDF output properties <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:40">[00:10:40]</a>.

### Page Format
Users can define the page format for their PDF documents <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:41">[00:10:41]</a>. The default page format is A4 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:43">[00:10:43]</a>, but other formats can be selected from the available options <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:47">[00:10:47]</a>.

### Adding More Templates and Databases
After the initial setup, users can add more templates and databases by going to the "Settings" section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:49">[00:10:49]</a>. Click "Click here to add templates" to choose and integrate additional templates and databases <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:51">[00:10:51]</a>.

### Custom PDF Documents
For generating custom PDF documents not based on predefined templates and databases, users can click on the "Help" option in the sidebar <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:19">[00:11:19]</a>. This section explains the steps for generating documents in a custom PDF format <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:24">[00:11:24]</a>.
