This article outlines how to utilize a Notion credit card expenses tracker to manage and monitor credit card payments effectively <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:05">[00:00:05]</a>. The system provides a comprehensive overview of credit card activity, detailed expense tracking, and repayment notifications <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:25">[00:01:25]</a>.

## Tracker Overview

The tracker is structured into several key sections designed to provide a holistic view of credit card finances:

### Credit Cards Overview
This section displays all added credit cards, showing the total amount due, the amount repaid so far, and the remaining balance amount due for each card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:23">[00:00:23]</a>. For demonstration purposes, examples include a Student Credit Card, Cash Back Credit Card, Travel Credit Card, and Rewards Credit Card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:18">[00:00:18]</a>.

### Monthly Overview
A monthly breakdown shows the amount due, amount repaid, and balance amount due for each month, which can be customized <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:32">[00:00:32]</a>.

### Yearly Overview
Similar to the monthly overview, this section displays the total amount due, amount repaid, and balance amount due across different years <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.

### Expense Details Section
This area records all individual expenses in detail <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:51">[00:00:51]</a>.

### Expenses Overview
This section provides a summary of all booked expenses categorized by type <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>. It shows total expenses, budgeted amounts, and whether expenses are "under budget" or "over budget" compared to the set budget <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:05">[00:01:05]</a>.

### Notification Section
This crucial section indicates when the next repayment is due for each credit card and how many days are remaining until that date <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a>.

## Using the Tracker

To begin [[setting_up_credit_card_overview_and_tracking_payments_in_notion | tracking payments in Notion]], you start by booking expenses in the "Expense Details" overview section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>.

### Entering Expenses
In the "Expense Details" section, you input the following information for each expense:
*   **Date** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:42">[00:01:42]</a>
*   **Expense Details**: A description of the expense <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:44">[00:01:44]</a>
*   **Period**: The month for which the expense is booked <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:46">[00:01:46]</a>
*   **Year** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:49">[00:01:49]</a>
*   **Credit Card**: The associated credit card (you can add new ones as you type) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:51">[00:01:51]</a>
*   **Category**: Assign a category to each expense <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:54">[00:01:54]</a>
*   **Expense Amount**: The amount of the expense <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:57">[00:01:57]</a>
*   **Opening Balance Due**: For any balances carried forward from previous months <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:07">[00:02:07]</a>

### Recording Repayments
When repaying a credit card, you can enter "credit card repayment" in the details and input the repayment amount in the "repayment" column <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:58">[00:02:58]</a>. All entered details automatically update the overview sections at the top <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:07">[00:03:07]</a>.

### Managing Budgets
The "Expenses Overview" allows you to track [[budgeting_and_tracking_expenses_in_notion | budgeting and tracking expenses in Notion]] against set budgets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:52">[00:03:52]</a>.
*   **Set Budgeted Amount**: You can click on the "Budget Amount" field for each category and enter your desired budget <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:59">[00:03:59]</a>.
*   **Budget Status**: The tracker automatically updates to show if you are "under budget" (expenses within the budget) or "over budget" (expenses beyond the budget) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:26">[00:04:26]</a>.

### Setting Repayment Notifications
In the "Notification" section, you can set the next repayment date for each credit card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:35">[00:04:35]</a>. This feature automatically calculates and displays the number of days remaining until the next repayment, helping you pay credit card debts on time <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:49">[00:04:49]</a>. This is one of the key [[benefits of tracking expenses in Notion | benefits of tracking expenses in Notion]] for managing credit.

## Additional Features and Customization

### Receipts
A "Receipt" column in the expense details section allows you to attach bills or receipts for your expenses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:34">[00:05:34]</a>.

### Summarization Views
The tracker offers various summarization views for detailed insights:
*   **Period View**: Clicking on a specific period opens a new window showing transaction details such as date, period, description, amount, and a tag indicating if it's an expense or repayment (green for repayment) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:44">[00:05:44]</a>.
*   **Year View**: Similarly, clicking on a year provides all transaction information for that particular year <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:16">[00:06:16]</a>.
*   **Credit Card View**: Selecting a specific credit card displays all transactions related to that card, along with its status and category <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:26">[00:06:26]</a>.

### Editing and Adding
You can edit existing credit card information or add new credit cards and categories using the pencil icon or "new" button <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:16">[00:05:16]</a>. Categories can also be added directly by entering their details <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:24">[00:05:24]</a>.

This comprehensive Notion template allows for effective [[tracking_personal_finances_in_notion | tracking personal finances in Notion]] and specifically [[tracking_income_and_expenses_in_Notion | tracking income and expenses in Notion]] related to credit cards, providing a clear [[using_notion_for_expense_management | expense management]] system. The template is customizable to fit personal needs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:22">[00:07:22]</a>.
