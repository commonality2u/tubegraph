Wizzygen is a platform designed to facilitate the creation of AI-generated content directly within a [[overview_of_notion_tools | Notion]] workspace <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:04">[00:00:04]</a>. It offers two primary options for content generation: a [[overview_of_notion_tools | Notion database]] or a [[overview_of_notion_tools | Notion page]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:10">[00:00:10]</a>. A [[overview_of_notion_tools | Notion page]] is a blank page, while a [[overview_of_notion_tools | Notion database]] is structured with rows and columns <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>.

## Generating Content for a Notion Page

To generate AI content for a [[overview_of_notion_tools | Notion page]] using Wizzygen, follow these steps:

1.  **Name the Connection**: Provide a name for the connection, for example, "notion page" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:35">[00:00:35]</a>.
2.  **Select Content Type**: Choose "Notion page" as the content type <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:38">[00:00:38]</a>.
3.  **Enter Notion URL**: Copy the URL of the specific [[overview_of_notion_tools | Notion page]] where you want to generate content and paste it into the URL section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.
4.  **Proceed**: Click "next" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>.
5.  **Input Prompt**: Enter your desired prompt, such as "write me a block of 100 words on notion" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>.
6.  **Generate Content**: The AI will then generate and populate the content directly onto the specified [[overview_of_notion_tools | Notion page]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:06">[00:01:06]</a>.

## Generating Content for a Notion Database

To generate AI content across multiple entries within a [[how_to_create_a_database_in_notion | Notion database]]:

1.  **Name the Connection**: Assign a name to the connection, such as "notion database" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:23">[00:01:23]</a>.
2.  **Select Content Type**: Choose "Notion database" as the content type <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:25">[00:01:25]</a>.
3.  **Enter Notion URL**: Navigate to your [[setting_up_a_database_in_notion | Notion database]], copy its URL, and paste it into the designated URL section in Wizzygen <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:28">[00:01:28]</a>.
4.  **Proceed**: Click "next" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:36">[00:01:36]</a>.
5.  **Input Prompt**: Formulate a prompt that will apply to each topic or row in your database, for instance, "write me a block of 100 words on the topic" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:48">[00:01:48]</a>. This allows the AI to generate specific content for each entry in the database <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:40">[00:01:40]</a>.
6.  **Generate Content**: Wizzygen will then generate content for each row, populating the database with AI-created text <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:08">[00:02:08]</a>.

Wizzygen allows users to leverage AI for efficient content creation on both individual [[overview_of_notion_tools | Notion pages]] and structured [[overview_of_notion_tools | Notion databases]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:21">[00:02:21]</a>.
