*   **Customizable Categories**: Users can define and rename [[Adding and managing expenses | expense]] categories to align with their specific needs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:22">[00:00:22]</a>. Categories can be edited or deleted, and new ones can be [[Adding and managing expenses | added]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:48">[00:00:48]</a>.

*   **Budgeting and Tracking**: Each category includes a budgeted [[Understanding and tracking budgeted versus actual expenses | expense]] amount and an actual [[Understanding and tracking budgeted versus actual expenses | expense]] amount <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:53">[00:00:53]</a>. *   **Automated Updates**: When [[Adding and managing expenses | expense]] amounts are entered, all categories are automatically updated with the respective [[Tracking Expenses | expenses]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:03">[00:01:03]</a>.

*   **Summary Section**: A dedicated summary section provides an overview of the total budgeted [[Understanding and tracking budgeted versus actual expenses | expense]] amount and the actual [[Understanding and tracking budgeted versus actual expenses | expenses]] amount <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:27">[00:01:27]</a>.
