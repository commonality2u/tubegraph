## Generating [[process_of_generating_ai_content_with_wizzygen | AI Generated Content]] on a Notion Page

[[introduction_to_wizzygen | Wizzygen]] can be used to generate [[process_of_generating_ai_content_with_ai_content | AI content]] directly onto a Notion page <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>.

The process involves:
1.  **Naming the Connection**: Provide a name for the connection being built, for example, "notion page" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:33">[00:00:33]</a>.
2.  **Selecting the Page Type**: Choose the "Notion page" option from the available selections <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:38">[00:00:38]</a>.
3.  **Providing the URL**: Copy the URL of the specific Notion page where the content is to be generated and paste it into the URL section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.
4.  **Entering the Prompt**: Input the desired prompt for the [[process_of_generating_ai_content_with_wizzygen | AI content]] generation. For instance, a prompt could be "write me a block of 100 words on notion" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>.
5.  **Content Generation**: Upon submission, the [[process_of_generating_ai_content_with_wizzygen | AI content]] will be generated and appear on the specified Notion page <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:04">[00:01:04]</a>.

This demonstrates how [[introduction_to_wizzygen | Wizzygen]] can be utilized to populate Notion pages with [[process_of_generating_ai_content_with_wizzygen | AI generated content]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:22">[00:02:22]</a>.
