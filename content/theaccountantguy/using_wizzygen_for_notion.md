[[Introduction to Wizzygen and Notion integration | Wizzygen]] is a platform designed to help create [[ai_content_generation_for_notion | AI-generated content]] directly within a [[notion_application | Notion]] workspace <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:04">[00:00:04]</a>. It offers two main options for content generation: [[notion_application | Notion]] pages and [[generating_content_in_notion_databases | Notion databases]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:10">[00:00:10]</a>.

## Generating Content on Notion Pages

A [[notion_application | Notion]] page is a simple blank page within the [[notion_application | Notion]] environment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>. To generate AI content on a [[notion_application | Notion]] page using Wizzygen:

1.  **Name the Connection**: Provide a name for the connection, such as "notion page" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:35">[00:00:35]</a>.
2.  **Select Content Type**: Choose "notion page" as the content option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:11">[00:00:11]</a>.
3.  **Provide Page URL**: Copy and paste the URL of the target [[notion_application | Notion]] page into the URL section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:44">[00:00:44]</a>.
4.  **Enter Prompt**: Input the desired content prompt, for example, "write me a block of 100 words on Notion" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:01">[00:01:01]</a>.
5.  **Generate**: Once the prompt is entered, the [[ai_content_generation_for_notion | AI-generated content]] will appear directly on the specified [[notion_application | Notion]] page <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:06">[00:01:06]</a>.

## Generating Content on Notion Databases

A [[notion_application | Notion]] database is structured in the form of rows and columns <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>. [[Generating content in Notion databases | Wizzygen can generate content]] for each item (row) within a database <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:40">[00:01:40]</a>. To generate AI content on a [[generating_content_in_notion_databases | Notion database]]:

1.  **Name the Connection**: Assign a name to the connection, for instance, "notion database" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:23">[00:01:23]</a>.
2.  **Select Content Type**: Choose "notion database" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:25">[00:01:25]</a>.
3.  **Provide Database URL**: Copy and paste the URL of the target [[generating_content_in_notion_databases | Notion database]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:34">[00:01:34]</a>.
4.  **Enter Prompt**: Input the content generation prompt. For databases, this can be tailored to generate content based on existing topics in rows, for example, "write me a block of 100 words on the topic" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:54">[00:01:54]</a>.
5.  **Generate**: The AI will then populate the content in the respective rows of the [[generating_content_in_notion_databases | Notion database]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:08">[00:02:08]</a>. The content will start populating across multiple sections or rows automatically <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:13">[00:02:13]</a>.

Wizzygen allows users to efficiently generate [[ai_content_generation_for_notion | AI content]] for both individual [[notion_application | Notion]] pages and structured [[generating_content_in_notion_databases | Notion databases]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:22">[00:02:22]</a>.
