Notion can be used to set up a small business bookkeeping template to track all bookkeeping needs for a small business <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:03">[00:00:03]</a>. This template helps [[using_notion_for_financial_tracking | keep track]] of all financial data and provides a comprehensive overview of a business's financial health <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:20">[00:00:20]</a>.

## Template Sections

The small business bookkeeping template in Notion is divided into three main sections <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>:
1.  **Start Here Section**: Used for initial setup and entering business-related information <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>.
2.  **Summary Section**: Provides a summary of all entered transactions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>.
3.  **Transactions Window**: Where all transaction details related to the business are entered <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:35">[00:00:35]</a>.

## Start Here Section: Accounts Setup

This section allows you to define and manage various accounts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>. Accounts are categorized under major financial heads <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:55">[00:00:55]</a>:
*   Expenses (e.g., Purchase Expense, Utility Bills, Subscription, Salaries and Wages) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:58">[00:00:58]</a>
*   Liabilities (e.g., Accounts Payable, Taxes Payable, Accrued Expenses, Credit Card) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:03">[00:01:03]</a>
*   Income <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>
*   Assets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:54">[00:00:54]</a>

### Managing Accounts
*   **Editing**: Hover over an item and click the pencil icon to edit details <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>.
*   **Deleting**: Click the three-dot icon next to an item and select "Delete" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:22">[00:01:22]</a>.
*   **Adding**: Go to the respective category and click "New Page" to add a new account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:28">[00:01:28]</a>.

### Account Details
For each added item, you can specify additional details <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:43">[00:01:43]</a>:
*   **Opening Balance**: Enter balances carried forward for liabilities and assets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:44">[00:01:44]</a>.
*   **Classification**: Classify items as Bills, Debt, Savings, Subscriptions, or other relevant categories <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:53">[00:01:53]</a>.
*   **Payment Frequency**: For recurring payments (e.g., monthly, half-yearly, quarterly) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:12">[00:02:12]</a>.
*   **Opening Units**: For inventory or assets you begin the business with <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:27">[00:02:27]</a>.
*   **Asset Class**: Classify as Inventory or Investment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:42">[00:02:42]</a>.

## Transactions Window: [[Entering transactions in Notion | Entering Transactions]]

This section is for recording all financial transactions. It is categorized by transaction type <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:04">[00:03:04]</a>:
*   Income <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:06">[00:03:06]</a>
*   Expense <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:06">[00:03:06]</a>
*   Asset <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:06">[00:03:06]</a>
*   Liability <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:08">[00:03:08]</a>

### Steps to Enter a Transaction
1.  **Select Transaction Type**: Click buttons like "Add Income", "Add Expense", etc. <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:09">[00:03:09]</a>. This automatically adds an entry with the current date <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:16">[00:03:16]</a>.
2.  **Period**: Enter the relevant period (e.g., "October 2024") <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:24">[00:03:24]</a>.
3.  **Description**: Provide a description of the transaction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:42">[00:03:42]</a>.
4.  **Category**: This is automatically mapped based on the transaction type selected <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:45">[00:03:45]</a>.
5.  **Account**: Choose from the accounts set up in the "Start Here" section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:50">[00:03:50]</a>. New accounts added here will also reflect in the "Start Here" section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:05">[00:04:05]</a>.
6.  **Payment Mode**: Indicate how payment is received or made (e.g., Bank Account, Cash Account). More accounts can be added as needed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:21">[00:04:21]</a>.
7.  **Transaction Type (Inflow/Outflow)**: Specify if money is coming into (inflow) or going out of (outflow) your account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:51">[00:04:51]</a>.
8.  **Amount**: Enter the monetary value of the transaction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:07">[00:05:07]</a>.
9.  **Document Section**: Attach relevant documents <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:15">[00:05:15]</a>.
10. **Tax Rate and Tax Amount**: If applicable, enter the tax rate; the tax amount will be automatically calculated. For non-taxable transactions, enter 0% <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:21">[00:05:21]</a>.
11. **Multiplier and Currency**: For multi-currency transactions, enter a multiplier to convert values to the base currency (e.g., USD to Euro) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:36">[00:05:36]</a>.

### Investments & Inventory Tracking
The transactions window also includes sections for:
*   **Investments**: Track units, price, total value, and reflect profit or loss upon selling <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:07">[00:06:07]</a>.
*   **Inventory**: Monitor inventory levels and track profit or loss obtained from specific items <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:26">[00:06:26]</a>.

## Summary Section: Financial Overview

All transactions entered in the "Transactions Window" are automatically updated in the "Summary Section", providing a comprehensive financial glance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:49">[00:06:49]</a>.

### Sub-sections within Summary:
*   **Finances**: Overview of Assets, Income Sources, Liabilities, and Expenses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:54">[00:06:54]</a>. It shows account balances (e.g., Accounts Receivable, Input Taxes, Cash Value) and their percentage of total assets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:06">[00:07:06]</a>.
*   **Debt**: Shows current debt payments based on liabilities entered. Differentiates between past payments and upcoming payments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:23">[00:07:23]</a>.
*   [[Entering Transactions and Budgeting in Notion | Budgeting]]: Displays budgeted expenses and income, total percentage of expenses incurred compared to the budget, and similar metrics for income <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:03">[00:08:03]</a>.
*   **Savings**: Tracks savings made for particular funds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:16">[00:08:16]</a>.
*   **Bills**: Similar to the debt section but specifically for bills, showing past and upcoming payments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:22">[00:08:22]</a>.
*   **Subscriptions**: Functions similarly to the bills section for tracking subscriptions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:31">[00:08:31]</a>.
*   **Tax Section**: Provides quarterly tax information including payable taxes, tax credit available, and tax liability <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:36">[00:08:36]</a>. This is automatically populated from income and expense entries <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:44">[00:08:44]</a>.
*   **Net Worth**: Compares your set net worth goal to your calculated total net worth (derived from assets, liabilities, and transactions). It shows the difference, total growth from the start of the financial year, and the percentage of the net worth goal achieved <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:51">[00:08:51]</a>.
*   **Investment Summary**: Displays the value of investments and units held <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:36">[00:09:36]</a>.
*   **Inventory Summary**: Shows the status of inventory <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:43">[00:09:43]</a>.
*   **Overall Summary**: Presents total income, total expenses, gross profit, taxes paid, and net profit of the business <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:47">[00:09:47]</a>.
