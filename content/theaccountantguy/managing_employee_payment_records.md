From: [[theaccountantguy]] <br/> 

This article provides a quick walkthrough of an [[using_notion_for_employee_paycheck_tracking | employee paycheck tracker]] template designed to help keep track of all [[summary_and_reporting_of_employee_payments | employee payments]] in one place <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:04">[00:00:04]</a>.

## Template Sections

The template is organized into three main sections:
*   Records <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:11">[00:00:11]</a>
*   Payments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:12">[00:00:12]</a>
*   Summary <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>

### Records Section

The Records section contains comprehensive details about each employee <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>. This includes:
*   Whether they are permanent employees <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:16">[00:00:16]</a>
*   The total payment due to them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:18">[00:00:18]</a>
*   The number of months of payment due <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:20">[00:00:20]</a>
*   How they are paid (monthly or hourly basis) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:22">[00:00:22]</a>
*   The amount of payment being paid to them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>

To manage employees and categories:
*   Add a new employee by clicking "new" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:33">[00:00:33]</a>.
*   Add a new category by clicking the plus icon <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[00:00:37]</a>.
*   [[adding_and_editing_employee_categories_and_details | Edit existing departments]] where employees are placed by clicking and editing the details <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.

### Payments Section

The Payments section provides key financial figures related to paychecks:
*   Total paycheck due <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:48">[00:00:48]</a>
*   Amount that has been paid <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>
*   Balance paycheck still due <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>
*   Any advance amount paid <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:51">[00:00:51]</a>

### Summary Section

The Summary section gives an [[summary_and_reporting_of_employee_payments | overall summary]] of payments:
*   Total paycheck due <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>
*   Amount that has been paid <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:57">[00:00:57]</a>
*   Outstanding balance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:58">[00:00:58]</a>
*   Percentage of the total paycheck that is due <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>

## Entering Payment Details

Users can feed in all payment details at the bottom of the template <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:04">[00:01:04]</a>:
*   **Date:** Enter the date of the payment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>.
*   **Period:** Specify the period for which the payment is being made <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:08">[00:01:08]</a>.
*   **Description:** Add a description for the payment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:13">[00:01:13]</a>.
*   **Employee Details:** Select the employee from the list of added employees <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a>.
*   **Pay Rate:**
    *   For hourly employees, specify the pay rate and the number of hours worked to automatically calculate the payment amount <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:21">[00:01:21]</a>.
    *   For monthly employees, specify the pay rate and leave the hour column blank <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:28">[00:01:28]</a>.
*   **Payment Status:** A column indicates if the payment has been made. Untick this box if a payment is due but not yet paid, marking it as unpaid <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:34">[00:01:34]</a>.

## Quarterly Reporting

A dedicated quarterly section shows the total paycheck due, amount paid, and net balance for every quarter <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:46">[00:01:46]</a>. Payments are also categorized into permanent and contractual sections based on the entered details <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:53">[00:01:53]</a>.
