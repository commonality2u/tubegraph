## Model Comparison and Usage

The choice of GPT model impacts the accuracy and quality of the generated Notion formulas.

*   **GPT 3.5 Turbo**
    *   This is an older model <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:43">[00:05:43]</a>.
    *   It is slightly less accurate compared to GPT 4.0 106 Preview <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:44">[00:05:44]</a>.
    *   Users with a free OpenAI account typically receive a $5 free credit upon creation, which can be utilized for testing the chatbot with the GPT 3.5 Turbo model <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:06">[00:05:06]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:13">[00:05:13]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:16">[00:05:16]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:00">[00:06:00]</a>.
    *   The demonstrations provided in the video were performed using GPT 3.5 Turbo <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:07">[00:06:07]</a>.

*   **GPT 4.0 106 Preview**
    *   This model offers better and more accurate results for prompts and questions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:54">[00:05:54]</a>.
    *   It is recommended for users who are on a paid OpenAI plan <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:49">[00:05:49]</a>.
