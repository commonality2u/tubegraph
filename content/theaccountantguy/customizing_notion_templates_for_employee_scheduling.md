The Notion [[notion_employee_schedule_template_setup | employee schedule template]] is designed to help organizations keep track of employee schedules <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:06">[00:00:06]</a>. It offers a comprehensive solution for [[managing_employee_details_in_notion | managing employee details in Notion]], [[organizing_tasks_with_notion_task_schedule | organizing tasks with Notion task schedule]], and monitoring performance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:10">[00:00:10]</a>.

The template is organized into four main views:
*   **Employee Details** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:14">[00:00:14]</a>
*   **Task Schedule** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:19">[00:00:19]</a>
*   **Monthly View** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:24">[00:00:24]</a>
*   **Employee Performance** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:33">[00:00:33]</a>

## Employee Details

This section allows you to list and manage information for all employees <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:14">[00:00:14]</a>.

### Adding Employees and Categories
*   **Adding New Employees**: To add a new employee, click the "New" button <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:43">[00:00:43]</a>. You can also use the "Add Employee" quick action button at the top <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:33">[00:01:33]</a>.
*   **Employee Information**: When adding a new employee, you'll be prompted to enter their name, employee ID, and contact information <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:06">[00:01:06]</a>. Further details like department, position, and email ID can also be included <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:17">[00:01:17]</a>.
*   **Categories**: Employees can be categorized, for example, into "Operations," "Marketing," and "Finance" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>.
    *   **Add New Category**: Click the plus icon to add a new category <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:54">[00:00:54]</a>.
    *   **Edit Existing Category**: Click on an existing category to edit its details <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:57">[00:00:57]</a>.

## Task Schedule

After adding employee details, the task schedule section allows for the entry of all task-related information <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:25">[00:01:25]</a>.

### Managing Tasks and Shifts
*   **Shifts**: Tasks are divided into different shifts, such as first, second, and third shifts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:50">[00:01:50]</a>.
*   **Time and Details**:
    *   **Start/End Time**: Enter the desired date and time for task commencement and completion <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:59">[00:01:59]</a>. You can also indicate the period to which the time belongs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:06">[00:02:06]</a>.
    *   **Task Detail**: Add specific information about the task <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:10">[00:02:10]</a>.
*   **Employee Assignment**:
    *   **Assign Employee**: Click on the employee field to select an employee from those added earlier <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:15">[00:02:15]</a>. You can also add a new employee directly from this section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:21">[00:02:21]</a>.
    *   **Automatic Department Assignment**: Once an employee is selected, their respective department (set in employee details) will automatically appear <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:25">[00:02:25]</a>.
*   **Auto Computation of Hours**: The template automatically calculates the hours an employee has worked on a specific task based on the entered time information <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:33">[00:02:33]</a>.
*   **Attendance and Task Status**:
    *   **Attendance**: Employees can mark their attendance as "Present," "On Leave," or "Day Off" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:40">[00:02:40]</a>.
    *   **Task Status**: Employees can update the status of their tasks as "In Progress," "Completed," or "Not Started" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:49">[00:02:49]</a>. For days off or leave, the task status can be marked "Not Applicable" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:58">[00:02:58]</a>.

## Monthly View

The monthly view offers an overview of performance across different months <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:19">[00:03:19]</a>.

### Performance Overview
*   **Quarterly Division**: Months are divided into different quarters <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:25">[00:03:25]</a>.
*   **Hours Tracking**: This view displays the total hours worked for a specific month and the total hours an employee has been on leave <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:29">[00:03:29]</a>. It also provides a total sum of working and leave hours <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:33">[00:03:33]</a>. This provides a quick overview of monthly performance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:38">[00:03:38]</a>.

## Employee Performance

For a more granular look at individual performance, the employee performance section is available <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:45">[00:03:45]</a>.

### Individual Performance Tracking
*   **Employee-Centric View**: This view presents the same information as the monthly view but is organized by individual employees <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:50">[00:03:50]</a>.
*   **Detailed Hours**: For each employee, you can see the hours they worked, the hours they were on leave, and their total hours in totality <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:56">[00:03:56]</a>. This allows for detailed tracking of individual contributions and time off <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:03">[00:04:03]</a>.
