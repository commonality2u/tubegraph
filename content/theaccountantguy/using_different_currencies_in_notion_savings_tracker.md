## Changing Currency Settings

To [[changing_currency_settings_in_notion_subscription_tracker | change the currency]] display in the template:
1.  Click on any amount field that currently shows a dollar currency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:11:00">[04:11:00]</a>.
2.  Select "Edit property" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:15:00">[04:15:00]</a>.
3.  Within the property settings, locate the "Number format" option, which provides a list of all currencies supported by [[tracking_personal_finances_in_notion | Notion]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:16:00">[04:16:00]</a>.
