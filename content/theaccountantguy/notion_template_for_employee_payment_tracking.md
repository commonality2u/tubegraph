The Notion Employee Paycheck Tracker is a straightforward template designed to centralize and manage all employee payment information <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:04">[00:00:04]</a>.

## Template Sections

The template is divided into three primary sections: Records, Payments, and Summary <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:10">[00:00:10]</a>.

### Records

The Records section provides detailed information about each employee <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>. It includes:
*   Whether employees are permanent <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:16">[00:00:16]</a>.
*   Payments due to them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>.
*   Number of months payment is due <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>.
*   Payment frequency (monthly or hourly basis) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:22">[00:00:22]</a>.
*   The amount paid to them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>.

This section serves as a comprehensive record of all employees <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:29">[00:00:29]</a>.

### Payments

The Payments section displays key financial figures related to employee compensation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>:
*   Total paycheck due <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:48">[00:00:48]</a>.
*   Amount paid <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>.
*   Balance paycheck due <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:51">[00:00:51]</a>.
*   Any advance amount paid <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>.

### Summary

The Summary section offers an overview of the payment status <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:55">[00:00:55]</a>:
*   Total paycheck due <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>.
*   Amount paid <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:57">[00:00:57]</a>.
*   Outstanding balance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:59">[00:00:59]</a>.
*   Percentage of total paycheck due <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>.

## Managing Employees and Payments

### Adding and Editing Details

Users can easily add new employees by clicking the "new" button <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:33">[00:00:33]</a>. To add a new category, click the "plus" button <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[00:00:37]</a>. Existing employee departments can be edited by clicking on them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.

### Feeding Payment Information

At the bottom of the template, users can input specific payment details <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:04">[00:01:04]</a>:
*   **Date**: The date of payment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>.
*   **Period**: The period for which the payment is being made <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:09">[00:01:09]</a>.
*   **Description**: A description of the payment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:13">[00:01:13]</a>.
*   **Employee Details**: Selection of the specific employee from the added records <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a>.
*   **Pay Rate**:
    *   For hourly employees, specify the pay rate and the number of hours worked to automatically calculate the payment amount <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:21">[00:01:21]</a>.
    *   For monthly employees, specify the pay rate and leave the hours column empty <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:28">[00:01:28]</a>.
*   **Payment Status**: A column indicates whether the payment has been made. Payments can be marked as unpaid by unticking the box <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:34">[00:01:34]</a>.

## Reporting and Breakdown

The template includes a quarterly section that shows the total paycheck due, amount paid, and net balance for each quarter <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:46">[00:01:46]</a>. Payments are also categorized and divided into permanent and contractual sections based on the employee details entered <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:53">[00:01:53]</a>.
