## Business Expenses Summary

The top section of the template features a business expenses summary, which is divided into three key areas: Account, Expenses, and Summary <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:11">[00:00:11]</a>.

### Account Section
In the Account section, users can set up various heads of expenses under broader categories like Operational, Administration, or Marketing <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>.
*   **Adding New Categories**: A new expense category can be added by scrolling to the right and clicking a plus icon <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:28">[00:00:28]</a>.
*   **Editing Existing Categories**: Existing categories can be edited by clicking on them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:34">[00:00:34]</a>.
*   **Budgeted Amount**: After setting up expense categories, a budgeted amount can be specified for each <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>.

### Expenses Section
This section displays current expenses in comparison to the budgeted amounts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:04">[00:01:04]</a>.
*   **Visual Indicators**:
    *   Expenses exceeding the budgeted amount are indicated in red, showing a high percentage <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:08">[00:01:08]</a>.
    *   Expenses within the budgeted amount are indicated in green <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:14">[00:01:14]</a>.
*   **Automatic Updates**: All expense data updates automatically as transactions are entered into the transaction section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:53">[00:00:53]</a>.

### Summary Section
The Summary section provides a quick overview, reflecting:
*   The [[budget_summary_and_tracking | total budgeted expense]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:19">[00:01:19]</a>.
*   The [[budget_summary_and_tracking | total expenses]] incurred so far <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:22">[00:01:22]</a>.
*   The difference between the budgeted and actual expenses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:24">[00:01:24]</a>.
*   The percentage of budgeted expense that was set <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:25">[00:01:25]</a>.

## Transaction Section

The transaction section, located at the bottom of the template, is where users book expenses under their respective categories <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:33">[00:01:33]</a>. Expenses are itemized by month, with the most recent month appearing at the top <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:44">[00:01:44]</a>.

*   **Quick Expense Entry**: Buttons on the left side allow for quick addition of expenses, automatically setting the current date <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:51">[00:01:51]</a>.
*   **Adding Details**: Users can add a description, associate the expense with a relevant account, and enter the amount <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:02">[00:02:02]</a>.
*   **Date Modification**: The date of any expense can be easily changed as needed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:22">[00:02:22]</a>.

### Alternative Expense Entry Methods
*   **Quick Add Button**: A quick button at the top allows for adding a different expense source <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:40">[00:02:40]</a>.
*   **"New" Icon**: A "new" icon provides another way to quickly add an expense under different categories <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:50">[00:02:50]</a>. This also allows for adding a new account under a specific category and setting its budgeted amount <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:56">[00:02:56]</a>.
