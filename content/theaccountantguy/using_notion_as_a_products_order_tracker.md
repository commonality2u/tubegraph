## Overview

This template helps businesses track all product orders placed by customers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:07">[00:00:07]</a>. It is a simple tool designed for [[using_notion_for_business_tracking | business]] tracking of product orders <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:10">[00:00:10]</a>.

## Template Components

The Notion products order tracker is built around a database that captures various essential details related to each order.

### Product and Customer Data

The database includes specific fields to capture necessary information:
*   **Product Details**
    *   Product Name <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>
*   **Customer Information**
    *   Customer Name <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:15">[00:00:15]</a>
    *   Billing Address <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:18">[00:00:18]</a>
    *   Customer Phone Number <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:19">[00:00:19]</a>
    *   Customer Email <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:20">[00:00:20]</a>

### Payment and Order Details

Further fields provide insights into the transaction specifics:
*   **Payment Sources**: Specifies how the customer will pay <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:22">[00:00:22]</a>.
*   **Gift Item Indicator**: Notes whether the product is intended as a gift <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>.
*   **Order Amount**: The total amount for the placed order <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:31">[00:00:31]</a>.

## Order Summary and Analytics

At the top of the tracker, an order summary provides aggregated metrics for quick insights <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:34">[00:00:34]</a>:
*   **Total Order Value**: The cumulative value of all orders placed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:36">[00:00:36]</a>.
*   **Number of Orders**: Total count of orders <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:40">[00:00:40]</a>.
*   **Number of Customers**: Count of unique customers who have placed orders <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>.
*   **Gift Recommendation Percentage**: Indicates the percentage of customers looking to place products as gift items, which dynamically changes based on data entries (e.g., 67%) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>.
*   **Payment Sources Breakdown**: Shows how many customers are paying through different sources like debit card, PayPal, credit card, and other options, along with their respective percentages <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:02">[00:01:02]</a>.

## Customization and Expansion

This template is fully customizable to suit specific business needs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a>. Users can add fields such as "average order value" and other necessary KPIs (Key Performance Indicators) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:19">[00:01:19]</a>. The database can also be expanded to include more items as required <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:32">[00:01:32]</a>.
