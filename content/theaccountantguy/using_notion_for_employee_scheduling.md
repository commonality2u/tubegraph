The employee schedule template in Notion can be utilized to keep track of the schedules of all employees within an organization <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:05">[00:00:05]</a>. This template is fully customizable to suit specific needs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:13">[00:04:13]</a>.

## Template Views

The template is organized into four distinct views <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:12">[00:00:12]</a>:
*   **Employee Details**: Lists employee information <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:14">[00:00:14]</a>.
*   **Task Schedule**: Helps enter tasks for employees based on different dates <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:19">[00:00:19]</a>.
*   **Monthly View**: Shows monthly data divided into quarters, including hours worked and leave hours <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:25">[00:00:25]</a>.
*   **Employee Performance**: Displays detailed information for individual employees <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:32">[00:00:32]</a>.

## Employee Details

The "Employee Details" section is used to add and manage employee information <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:39">[00:00:39]</a>.
*   **Adding a New Employee**: Click "new" to add an employee under relevant categories <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>. Pre-defined categories include Operations, Marketing, and Finance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>.
*   **Managing Categories**: New categories can be added by clicking the plus icon <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:53">[00:00:53]</a>, and existing ones can be edited <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:57">[00:00:57]</a>.
*   **Employee Information**: When adding a new employee, details such as name, employee ID, contact information <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:06">[00:01:06]</a>, department, position, and email ID can be entered <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:17">[00:01:17]</a>.
*   **Quick Action Button**: A "quick action button" titled "add employee" on the top can automatically add a new employee <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>.

## Task Schedule

After adding employee details, the "Task Schedule" section is used to enter task-related information <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:25">[00:01:25]</a>.
*   **Shift Organization**: Tasks are divided into different shifts, such as First Shift, Second Shift, and Third Shift <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:50">[00:01:50]</a>.
*   **Task Entry**: Employees can enter details for their assigned shift <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:56">[00:01:56]</a>:
    *   **Start Time**: Enter the desired date and time <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:59">[00:01:59]</a>.
    *   **End Time**: Indicate the period to which the time belongs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:04">[00:02:04]</a>.
    *   **Task Detail**: Add specific task information <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:09">[00:02:09]</a>.
    *   **Employee Assignment**: Select the employee name from those previously added <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:15">[00:02:15]</a>. New employees can also be added directly from this section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:21">[00:02:21]</a>.
    *   **Department Auto-fill**: The respective department automatically appears based on the employee's details <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:25">[00:02:25]</a>.
    *   **Hours Worked**: Automatic computation of hours worked on a specific task occurs based on entered information <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:32">[00:02:32]</a>.
    *   **Attendance**: Employees can mark their attendance as present, on leave, or day off <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:40">[00:02:40]</a>.
    *   **Task Status**: Employees can update the task status (in progress, completed, not started, or not applicable for day off/leave) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:48">[00:02:48]</a>.

## Monthly View

The "Monthly View" provides an overview of performance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:16">[00:03:16]</a>.
*   **Quarterly Division**: Months are divided into different quarters <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:22">[00:03:22]</a>.
*   **Performance Metrics**: It displays the total hours worked for a specific month, total hours on leave, and the total hours (summation of working and leave hours) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:28">[00:03:28]</a>. This offers a quick overview of monthly performance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:38">[00:03:38]</a>.

## Employee Performance

The "Employee Performance" section allows for individual employee performance tracking <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:45">[00:03:45]</a>. It shows the same information as the monthly view but is organized by employee <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:50">[00:03:50]</a>. For each employee, users can see hours worked, hours on leave, and total hours <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:56">[00:03:56]</a>.
