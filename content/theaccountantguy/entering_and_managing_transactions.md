## Defining Expense Categories

Before [[entering_transactions_in_notion | entering transactions]], you can define the different types of expenses your business incurs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:10">[00:00:10]</a>.

*   **Predefined Categories** The tracker includes some predefined expense categories <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>.
*   **Modifying Categories** You can click on a category to modify or change it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:18">[00:00:18]</a>. After making changes, click "done" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:22">[00:00:22]</a>.
*   **Editing Subcategories** To edit a subcategory, click the pencil icon <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:23">[00:00:23]</a>.
*   **Adding New Categories** Use the "new" button to add a custom category <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:29">[00:00:29]</a>.
*   **Setting Budgets** You can set a budgeted amount for each expense category <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:34">[00:00:34]</a>. This budget will automatically be reflected in the tracker's overview <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[00:00:37]</a>.

## Expenses Overview

The "expenses overview" section provides a summary of your financial status related to expenses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.

*   **Total Expenses** Displays the total expenses incurred <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:43">[00:00:43]</a>.
*   **Budgeted Expenses** Shows the total budgeted expenses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:45">[00:00:45]</a>.
*   **Budget Performance** A percentage indicates whether you have exceeded or stayed within your set budget <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:47">[00:00:47]</a>.
*   **Automatic Updates** Any changes to budgeted amounts in the expense categories will automatically update the expenses overview <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>.

## Entering Transactions

The "transaction side" is where you [[entering_transactions_in_notion | enter your business expenses]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:05">[00:01:05]</a>.

To [[adding_and_managing_transactions | enter a transaction]]:
1.  **Select Date** Click to choose the date of the expense; it will automatically populate <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:08">[00:01:08]</a>.
2.  **Add Description** Provide a description for the expense <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:14">[00:01:14]</a>.
3.  **Select Account** Choose an account from your predefined list, or edit and add new accounts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a>.
4.  **Enter Amount** Input the amount of the expense <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:20">[00:01:20]</a>.
