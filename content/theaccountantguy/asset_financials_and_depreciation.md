The asset planner template provides various sections for managing assets, including specific details on financial aspects and performance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:05">[00:00:05]</a>.

## Financial Details
Within the [[operational_management_of_assets | operational management]] section of the asset planner, financial details related to the asset are covered <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:12">[00:00:12]</a>. This includes information such as the asset's current value <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:33">[00:00:33]</a>, the depreciation method used <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:34">[00:00:34]</a>, and the depreciation rate <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:35">[00:00:35]</a>.

## Asset Performance
The template also includes a performance section, which tracks the asset's performance from the point of its creation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[00:00:37]</a>.

## Other Related Sections
In addition to financial and performance tracking, the asset planner also encompasses:
*   **Asset Details**: Covers specifics like asset ID, asset name, and asset type <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>.
*   **Maintenance Details**: Includes information on [[asset_maintenance_details | maintenance]] ID and asset ID, covering all aspects related to asset maintenance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>.
*   **Regulatory Management**: Contains a compliance section for all compliance-related matters <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>.
*   **Documentation**: Allows for the addition of all relevant asset-related documents <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:48">[00:00:48]</a>.
*   **Reviews**: Provides a space for users to submit reviews about the assets in use <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:53">[00:00:53]</a>.
