This article details the utilization of a Notion-based [[tracking_credit_card_expenses_in_notion | credit card expenses tracker]] designed to monitor credit card payments and expenses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:05">[00:00:05]</a>. The tracker provides a comprehensive financial overview and helps manage [[debt_overview_and_categorization | credit card debts]] efficiently <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:28">[00:03:28]</a>.

## Key Sections of the Tracker

The tracker is structured with several key sections to provide a holistic view of credit card activity:

*   **Credit Cards Overview**
    This section displays all added credit cards <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:15">[00:00:15]</a>. For each card, it shows:
    *   The total amount due <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:24">[00:00:24]</a>
    *   The amount repaid so far <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>
    *   The remaining balance amount due <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:28">[00:00:28]</a>
    Examples include "Student Credit Card," "Cash Back Credit Card," "Travel Credit Card," and "Rewards Credit Card" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:18">[00:00:18]</a>.

*   **Monthly Overview**
    This section summarizes amounts due, repaid, and balance due for each month <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:32">[00:00:32]</a>. This can be customized to individual requirements <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:39">[00:00:39]</a>.

*   **Yearly Overview**
    Provides a summary of total amount due, amount repaid, and balance amount due across different years <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.

*   **Expense Detail Section**
    This is where all [[tracking_income_and_expenses | expenses]] are recorded in detail <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:51">[00:00:51]</a>.

*   **[[overview_and_analysis_of_expenses_in_notion | Expenses Overview]]**
    Offers a summarized view of all booked [[tracking_income_and_expenses | expenses]] categorized by different types <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>. It compares total [[tracking_income_and_expenses | expenses]] against a budgeted amount and indicates whether the budget is "under budget" or "over budget" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:05">[00:01:05]</a>.

*   **Notification Section**
    This section notifies users about upcoming repayment dates and the number of days remaining until the next repayment for each added credit card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a>.

## Booking Expenses

To begin using the tracker, [[adding_and_managing_expenses | expenses]] must be booked under the "Expense Details" section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>.

### Expense Details Fields

The "Expense Details" section includes fields for comprehensive data entry:
*   **Date** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:42">[00:01:42]</a>
*   **Expense Details**: A description of the expense <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:44">[00:01:44]</a>
*   **Period**: The period for which the expense is booked <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:46">[00:01:46]</a>
*   **Year** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:49">[00:01:49]</a>
*   **Credit Card**: The specific credit card used for the expense <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:51">[00:01:51]</a>
*   **Category**: Assignment of a category for each expense <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:54">[00:01:54]</a>
*   **Expense Amount**: The amount of the expense <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:57">[00:01:57]</a>
*   **Repayment Section**: Where repayment amounts for a particular credit card are entered <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:00">[00:02:00]</a>

An "opening balance due" field is available for balances carried forward from previous months <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:09">[00:02:09]</a>. New credit cards or categories can be added directly by typing them in or by using the 'new' button on the dashboard <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:37">[00:02:37]</a>.
Entering these details automatically updates the overview sections at the top of the tracker <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:07">[00:03:07]</a>.

## Managing Expenses and Notifications

### [[overview_and_analysis_of_expenses_in_notion | Expenses Overview]] and Budgeting

The [[overview_and_analysis_of_expenses_in_notion | Expenses Overview]] section helps monitor expenses per category <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:42">[00:03:42]</a>. It shows:
*   Total incurred expense amount <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:52">[00:03:52]</a>
*   Budgeted amount set for each expense category <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:56">[00:03:56]</a>

Users can set or modify the budgeted amount by clicking on the "budget amount" field <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:00">[00:04:00]</a>. The tracker indicates "under budget" if expenses are within the set budget, and "over budget" if expenses exceed it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:26">[00:04:26]</a>.

### Repayment Notifications

The notification section helps in paying credit card [[debt_overview_and_categorization | debts]] on time <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:28">[00:03:28]</a>. Users can set their own repayment dates for each credit card, and the tracker will display how many days are pending until the next repayment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:40">[00:04:40]</a>.

## Additional Features and Detailed Views

The tracker offers enhanced functionalities for better expense management:

*   **Receipt Column**: Users can attach bills or receipts for expenses in the receipt column <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:34">[00:05:34]</a>.

*   **[[using_a_summary_section_to_track_payment_totals | Summarization View]] by Period**: Clicking on a specific period opens a new window showing detailed [[tracking_transactions_and_expenses | transactions]], including date, period, transaction details, amount, and a tag (green for repayment, regular for expense) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:44">[00:05:44]</a>.

*   **Summarization View by Year**: Similar to the period view, but for a specific year <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:16">[00:06:16]</a>.

*   **Summarization View by Credit Card**: Clicking on a specific credit card displays all [[tracking_transactions_and_expenses | transactions]] related to that card, including its status (which can be changed) and category <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:26">[00:06:26]</a>.

*   **Summarization View by Category**: Provides details of all [[tracking_income_and_expenses | expenses]] grouped by a particular category <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:53">[00:06:53]</a>.

This tracker template is customizable to personal needs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:23">[00:07:23]</a>.
