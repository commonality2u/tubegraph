This Notion template is designed to help users track their emergency funds effectively <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:07">[00:00:07]</a>. It features a summary section at the top, which provides an overview of the fund setup, savings, and required contributions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:09">[00:00:09]</a>. The template's elements can be [[customizing_notion_templates_for_personal_finance_management|customized]] to fit individual requirements <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:16">[00:10:16]</a>.

## Fund Setup

The "Fund Setup" section is where you configure your emergency funds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>.

### Customizing Fund Categories
The template comes with three predefined categories: "Financial Safety," "Asset Protection," and "Health and Wellness" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:29">[00:00:29]</a>.
*   To [[customizing_categories_in_notion_expense_tracker|edit existing categories]], click on the category name and define your preferred name <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:35">[00:00:35]</a>.
*   To [[setting_up_expense_categories_in_notion|add a new category]], click the "add a group" button <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:40">[00:00:40]</a>.

### Adding and Detailing Emergency Funds
1.  **Add a new fund**: Click the "add fund" button <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:51">[00:00:51]</a>. A new fund will appear under the "no classification" tab <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:57">[00:00:57]</a>.
2.  **Assign to a category**: Click the three dots next to the new fund and move it to its respective category <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:02">[00:01:02]</a>.
3.  **Edit fund details**: Click the pencil icon for each fund to enter specific information <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:11">[00:01:11]</a>:
    *   **Fund Detail**: A descriptive name for the fund <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:14">[00:01:14]</a>.
    *   **Starting Balance**: The initial balance of the fund <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:17">[00:01:17]</a>.
    *   **Goal Amount**: The targeted savings amount for this specific emergency fund <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:23">[00:01:23]</a>. This helps in [[setting_net_worth_goals_in_notion|setting net worth goals]] and [[setting_and_tracking_budget_goals_with_notion|tracking budget goals]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:25">[00:01:25]</a>.
    *   **Start Date**: The date you begin contributing money to this fund <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:32">[00:01:32]</a>. This date automatically calculates savings made to date <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:42">[00:01:42]</a>.
    *   **Target End Date**: The desired date by which the total emergency fund should be available <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:48">[00:01:48]</a>.

## Contributions Section

This section details how to input money towards your emergency savings funds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:20">[00:02:20]</a>. Contributions are organized by month, with the latest month appearing at the top <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:35">[00:02:35]</a>.

Each contribution entry includes:
*   **Date**: The specific date of the contribution <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:45">[00:02:45]</a>.
*   **Description**: A brief description of the contribution <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:46">[00:02:46]</a>.
*   **Period**: The month and year the contribution pertains to (e.g., March 2024) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:47">[00:02:47]</a>.
*   **Fund**: Select the specific emergency fund from a dropdown list that contains all funds added in the setup section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:13">[00:03:13]</a>.
*   **Amount**: The amount of savings or contribution made for the month towards the selected fund <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:24">[00:03:24]</a>.
*   **Requirement**: This column indicates the required monthly contribution to meet the targeted end date and goal amount <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:35">[00:03:35]</a>. If the entered amount meets or exceeds the required amount, a tick mark will appear, indicating the target for that month has been met <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:00">[00:04:00]</a>.

To quickly add a new line item for the current month and date, use the "quick action button" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:31">[00:04:31]</a>.

## Quarterly Overview

The "Quarterly Overview" provides a snapshot of contributions across different quarters <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:09">[00:05:09]</a>. Months are grouped into quarters (e.g., January-March for Quarter 1) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:17">[00:05:17]</a>.
It displays:
*   Total savings made for each month <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:31">[00:05:31]</a>.
*   The required savings for that period <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:34">[00:05:34]</a>.
*   The percentage of the total target met for that month <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:14">[00:06:14]</a>.
*   Color-coding is used to provide a quick visual assessment of your financial progress <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:29">[00:06:29]</a>.
*   It also shows the total savings made for each quarter <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:41">[00:06:41]</a>.

## Savings Overview

The "Savings Overview" section categorizes savings funds similarly to the fund setup <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:58">[00:06:58]</a>. This [[setting_up_a_savings_funds_dashboard_in_notion|dashboard]] provides detailed information for each emergency fund <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:05">[00:07:05]</a>:
*   **Total Savings**: The cumulative savings made so far for that fund, including the starting balance and all contributions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:09">[00:07:09]</a>.
*   **Targeted Savings**: The goal amount set in the fund setup <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:35">[00:07:35]</a>.
*   **Achievement Percentage**: The percentage of the total savings requirement achieved <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:46">[00:07:46]</a>.
*   **Targeted Savings Date**: The end goal date set for the fund <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:50">[00:07:50]</a>.
*   **Months Left**: The remaining time until the targeted end date, calculated from the current date <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:06">[00:08:06]</a>.
*   **Contribution Required Per Month**: The monthly contribution needed to meet the fund's goal by the targeted end date <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:31">[00:08:31]</a>.

## Summary Section

The "Summary Section" provides a high-level overview of all your emergency funds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:17">[00:09:17]</a>.
*   **Total Savings**: The sum of the opening balances of all funds plus total contributions made <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:20">[00:09:20]</a>.
*   **Savings Required**: The summation of all targeted savings amounts set for each fund <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:33">[00:09:33]</a>.
*   **Difference**: The difference between the total savings and the total required savings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:42">[00:09:42]</a>.
*   **Percentage**: The percentage of total savings made compared to the total targeted savings amount <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:52">[00:09:52]</a>.

This [[customizing_notion_for_personal_budget_management|customizable Notion template]] helps in managing personal finances by providing a clear structure for tracking and achieving emergency fund goals <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:07">[00:00:07]</a>.
