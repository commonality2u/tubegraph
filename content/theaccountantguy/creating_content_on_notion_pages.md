Wizen is a platform designed to [[integrating_aigenerated_content_into_notion | create AI generated content]] directly onto a [[creating_ai_generated_content_on_notion_pages_and_databases | Notion]] workspace <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:04">[00:00:04]</a>. It offers two primary options for content generation: a [[creating_ai_generated_content_on_notion_pages_and_databases | Notion database]] and a [[creating_ai_generated_content_on_notion_pages_and_databases | Notion page]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:11">[00:00:11]</a>. A [[creating_ai_generated_content_on_notion_pages_and_databases | Notion page]] is a simple blank page, while a [[creating_ai_generated_content_on_notion_pages_and_databases | Notion database]] is structured in rows and columns <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>.

## Creating AI Generated Content on a Notion Page

To generate content on a [[creating_ai_generated_content_on_notion_pages_and_databases | Notion page]] using Wizen:
1.  Ensure the target [[creating_ai_generated_content_on_notion_pages_and_databases | Notion page]] is empty <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>.
2.  In Wizen, provide a connection name (e.g., "Notion page") <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:32">[00:00:32]</a>.
3.  Select "Notion page" as the content destination <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:38">[00:00:38]</a>.
4.  Copy the URL of the specific [[setting_up_and_sharing_a_notion_page_for_public_use | Notion page]] and paste it into the URL section in Wizen <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.
5.  Input the desired AI prompt, such as "write me a block of 100 words on Notion" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>.
6.  Upon execution, Wizen will generate the content directly onto the designated [[creating_ai_generated_content_on_notion_pages_and_databases | Notion page]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:04">[00:01:04]</a>.

## Creating AI Generated Content on a Notion Database

To generate content within a [[creating_ai_generated_content_on_notion_pages_and_databases | Notion database]] using Wizen, particularly for multiple topics or entries:
1.  In Wizen, provide a connection name (e.g., "Notion database") <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:21">[00:01:21]</a>.
2.  Select "Notion database" as the content destination <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:25">[00:01:25]</a>.
3.  Copy the URL of the target [[populating_notion_databases_with_aigenerated_content | Notion database]] and paste it into the URL section in Wizen <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:28">[00:01:28]</a>.
4.  Formulate an AI prompt that references the database's topics, for instance, "write me a block of 100 words on the topic" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:48">[00:01:48]</a>. This allows the AI to generate specific content for each row or topic within the database <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:38">[00:01:38]</a>.
5.  Wizen will then [[populating_notion_databases_with_aigenerated_content | populate Notion databases with AI generated content]] for each identified topic or row within the database <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:59">[00:01:59]</a>.

This process demonstrates how Wizen can be utilized to efficiently [[creating_ai_generated_content_on_notion_pages_and_databases | create AI generated content on Notion pages and databases]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:21">[00:02:21]</a>.
