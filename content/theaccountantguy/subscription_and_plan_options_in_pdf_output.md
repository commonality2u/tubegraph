[[introduction_to_pdfoutput_tool | PDF Output]] offers various subscription plans to enhance its features and remove limitations like watermarks. Users can manage their current plan and upgrade as needed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:05">[00:10:05]</a>.

## Managing Your Subscription <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:05">[00:10:05]</a>
Within the [[using_pdf_output_features_and_settings | settings tab]] of PDF Output, users can find the "upgrade option" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:05">[00:10:05]</a>. This section displays information about the user's current plan, including:
*   The active plan (e.g., Enterprise plan) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:07">[00:10:07]</a>
*   The renewal date <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:09">[00:10:09]</a>
*   The number of files downloaded <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:11">[00:10:11]</a>

## Upgrading Your Plan <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:13">[00:10:13]</a>
To upgrade a plan, users can click on the "upgrade subscription" option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:14">[00:10:14]</a>. After upgrading, the subscription needs to be activated by clicking "activate subscription" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:17">[00:10:17]</a>.

## Watermarks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:27">[00:10:27]</a>
For new users or those not on a Pro or other paid plan, a watermark will be visible in the generated PDF documents <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:22">[00:10:22]</a>. However, if a user is on any paid plan, the watermark will not be visible <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:31">[00:10:31]</a>.
