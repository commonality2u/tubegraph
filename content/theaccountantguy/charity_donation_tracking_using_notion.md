This article explains how to utilize a charity donation tracker built within [[tracking_savings_and_contributions_with_notion | Notion]] to manage all your donations towards charity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:06">[00:00:06]</a>. The tracker is organized into several key sections for comprehensive financial oversight <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:12">[00:00:12]</a>.

## Donation Funds Overview

The top section provides an overview of donation funds, divided into three main parts: Fund Setup, Donations Overview, and Summary <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>.

### Fund Setup

The Fund Setup section allows you to define and manage the different categories and specific funds you donate to <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:25">[00:00:25]</a>.

#### Categories
Donations are initially divided into three default categories: Health, Environment, and Education <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>.
*   **Editing Categories**: Existing categories can be edited by clicking on them and typing new names <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:33">[00:00:33]</a>.
*   **Adding New Categories**: To add a new category, slide the bottom slider to the right and click the plus icon to create a new group <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>.

#### Adding and Editing Funds
Underneath each category, specific funds are listed (e.g., American Red Cross, WHO, Care Funds for Health; Sierra Club, The Nature Conservancy, The Rainforest Foundation for Environment) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>.
*   **Adding New Funds**: Click the "Add Funds" button to create a new fund <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a>. New funds initially appear without classification, requiring you to move them to an appropriate category using the three-dots menu <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:24">[00:01:24]</a>.
*   **Editing Fund Details**: Click the pencil icon next to a fund to edit its details <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:42">[00:01:42]</a>.
*   **Deleting Funds**: Funds can be deleted by clicking the three-dots menu and selecting "Delete" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:14">[00:03:14]</a>.

#### Fund Details
When setting up or editing a fund, you input several key pieces of information <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:47">[00:01:47]</a>:
*   **Name of the Fund**: The specific name of the charity or fund <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:49">[00:01:49]</a>.
*   **Opening Balance**: The total donations made to this fund at the start of the financial year <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:00">[00:02:00]</a>. This is an amount you can input <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:07">[00:02:07]</a>.
*   **Targeted Donation**: The goal amount you aim to contribute to this specific fund <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:15">[00:02:15]</a>.
*   **Start Date**: The date since you began donating to this fund <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:25">[00:02:25]</a>.
*   **Target Date**: The target end date by which you aim to reach your donation goal for this fund <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:44">[00:02:44]</a>.

### Recent Donations

The "Recent Donations" section, located at the bottom of the tracker, details individual donations, organized month-wise with the latest month at the top <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:33">[00:03:33]</a>.

#### Fields for Each Donation
*   **Date**: The date the donation was made <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:48">[00:03:48]</a>.
*   **Description**: A brief description of the donation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:51">[00:03:51]</a>.
*   **Period**: The month and year of the donation (e.g., November 2023) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:53">[00:03:53]</a>. New periods can be created by typing the date and clicking the plus icon <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:06">[00:04:06]</a>.
*   **Fund**: The specific fund to which the donation was made, selected from the funds set up earlier <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:18">[00:04:18]</a>.
*   **Recommend**: This figure indicates the monthly contribution needed from the fund's start date to reach its targeted amount by the end date <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:27">[00:04:27]</a>.
*   **Amount**: The actual amount of donation made for the month <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:59">[00:04:59]</a>.
*   **Status Indicator**:
    *   A check mark appears if the donation amount is greater than or equal to the "Recommend" figure, signifying sufficient contribution <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:10">[00:05:10]</a>.
    *   A cross mark indicates that the donation is falling short of the monthly requirement <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:26">[00:05:26]</a>.

#### Adding and Deleting Donations
*   **Adding New Donations**: Click the "Add Donation" button to automatically add a new line item with the current date, allowing you to input details <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:38">[00:05:38]</a>.
*   **Deleting Donations**: You can quickly delete unwanted line items <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:54">[00:05:54]</a>.

### Quarterly Overview

The "Quarterly Overview" section categorizes all donations by month within their respective quarters (Q1: January-March, Q2: April-June, etc.) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:01">[00:06:01]</a>.

*   **Monthly Figures**: For each month, it displays the total donations made and the required amount <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:20">[00:06:20]</a>.
*   **Color-Coded Status**:
    *   **Red**: Indicates that the donation is falling short of the requirement, along with the percentage contributed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:29">[00:06:29]</a>.
    *   **Green**: Signifies that enough or more than enough donation has been made for the specific month <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:40">[00:06:40]</a>.
    *   **Blue**: Shows that the total donation is equal to the donation requirement <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:16">[00:07:16]</a>.
*   **Quarterly Totals**: A calculation at the top of each quarter displays the combined total donations for all months within that quarter <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:35">[00:07:35]</a>.
*   **Automatic Updates**: All figures in this section are automatically updated as you add more funds in the donations page <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:51">[00:06:51]</a>.

### Donations Overview (Fund-wise)

This view provides a specific, fund-wise breakdown of your donation progress <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:54">[00:07:54]</a>.

*   **Total Donation**: This includes the opening balance set in the fund setup and any subsequent donations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:08">[00:08:08]</a>.
*   **Target Amount**: The established goal for that specific fund <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:18">[00:08:18]</a>.
*   **Percentage Reached**: Shows how much of the targeted amount has been achieved <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:21">[00:08:21]</a>.
*   **Time Remaining**: Calculates the months left until the "Targeted Savings Date" (end date) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:25">[00:08:25]</a>. This updates automatically as time passes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:37">[00:08:37]</a>.
*   **Contribution Required Per Month**: The calculated monthly contribution needed from the start date to reach the targeted donation amount by the end date <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:49">[00:08:49]</a>.
*   **Category Totals**: At the top of each category, there is a total contribution amount for all funds within that specific category <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:11">[00:09:11]</a>.

### Summary Section

The "Summary" section provides a quick, overarching view of your total donation progress <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:47">[00:09:47]</a>.

*   **Total Donation Required**: The sum of all individual fund donation requirements <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:51">[00:09:51]</a>.
*   **Total Donation Made So Far**: The cumulative amount of all donations across all funds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:56">[00:09:56]</a>.
*   **Shortfall**: The remaining amount needed to reach the overall targeted donations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:00">[00:10:00]</a>.
*   **Percentage of Requirement Met**: The overall percentage of the total donation requirement that has been fulfilled <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:05">[00:10:05]</a>.
