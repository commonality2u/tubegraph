## Methods for AI Content Creation

Wizzygen supports [[generating_content_using_ai_prompts_for_notion_pages_and_databases | creating AI-generated content]] on both Notion pages and Notion databases <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:24">[00:00:24]</a>.

*   **Notion Page**: A simple, blank page within Notion <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:15">[00:00:15]</a>.
*   **Notion Database**: Content organized in rows and columns, similar to a spreadsheet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:19">[00:00:19]</a>.

## Generating Content on a Notion Page

To [[using_notion_pages_for_ai_content | generate AI content on a Notion page]], follow these steps:
1.  Name the connection (e.g., "notion page") <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:36">[00:00:36]</a>.
2.  Select "Notion page" as the content creation option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.
3.  Copy and paste the URL of the desired Notion page into the URL section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>.
4.  Input a specific prompt, such as "write me a block of 100 words on Notion" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:01">[00:01:01]</a>.
5.  Upon processing, the AI-generated content will appear directly on the specified Notion page <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:09">[00:01:09]</a>.

## Generating Content in a Notion Database

For [[utilizing_notion_databases_for_ai_content | generating content within a Notion database]]:
1.  Name the connection (e.g., "notion database") <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:23">[00:01:23]</a>.
2.  Select "Notion database" as the content creation option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:26">[00:01:26]</a>.
3.  Copy the URL of the Notion database and paste it into the URL section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:34">[00:01:34]</a>.
4.  Craft a prompt that specifies the content desired for each topic in the database, for example, "write me a block of 100 words on the topic" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:54">[00:01:54]</a>. This allows the AI to generate content for multiple topics automatically <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:43">[00:01:43]</a>.
5.  The AI will then populate the content for each row in the database based on the prompt and the specific topic within that row <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:19">[00:02:19]</a>.

This process demonstrates how [[using_wizzygen_to_create_aigenerated_content_in_notion | Wizzygen]] can be utilized for [[creating_aigenerated_content_in_notion | automating AI content generation]] for both Notion pages and databases <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:25">[00:02:25]</a>.
