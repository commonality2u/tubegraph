## How to Set Up Recurring Bill Payment Reminders <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:05">[00:00:05]</a>

To create a new [[setting_up_bill_payment_reminders_on_whatsapp | bill payment reminder]] that repeats automatically:

1.  **Create a New Template**:
    *   Click on the drop-down menu. <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>
    *   Select "New template." <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:20">[00:00:20]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>
2.  **Enter Bill Details**:
    *   Name the bill (e.g., "Mobile bill"). <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:23">[00:00:23]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>
    *   Specify the bill amount (e.g., $1500). <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:28">[00:00:28]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:31">[00:00:31]</a>
    *   Click "Back." <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:33">[00:00:33]</a>
3.  **Set Recurring Schedule**:
    *   Click the drop-down menu again. <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:35">[00:00:35]</a>
    *   Click on the three dots next to the bill. <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[00:00:37]</a>
    *   Select "Set the bill to repeat on the recurring basis." <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:39">[00:00:39]</a>
    *   Choose the frequency, such as "every month." <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:43">[00:00:43]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:44">[00:00:44]</a>
    *   Specify the exact time and day you wish to receive the notification (e.g., 11:55 AM on the 20th of every month). <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:47">[00:00:47]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>
    *   Click "Save." <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>

Once saved, this template will ensure you receive a notification every month on the specified date and time. <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:58">[00:00:58]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:03">[00:01:03]</a>
