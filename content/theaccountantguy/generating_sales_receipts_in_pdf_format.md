A summary section at the top provides key performance indicators:

## Generating PDF Sales Receipts with PDF Output.com

### Generating a Single PDF Sales Receipt

To generate a single PDF sales receipt:
1.  Navigate to PDF Output.com <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:08:08">[03:08:08]</a>.
2.  From the "Template Selection" dropdown, search for and select the "Sales Receipts" template <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:12:00">[03:12:00]</a>.
3.  Fill in all required information, including:
    *   Receipt number, receipt date, payment method <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:48:00">[03:48:00]</a>.
    *   Business name and address <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:53:00">[03:53:00]</a>.
    *   Item descriptions, quantities, and unit prices <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:55:00">[03:55:00]</a>.
    *   Tax rate <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:26:00">[04:26:00]</a>.
    *   Any notes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:35:00">[04:35:00]</a>.
4.  Select the desired currency (e.g., US Dollars, Euro) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:40:00">[04:40:00]</a>.
5.  Click "Download PDF" to generate and download the sales receipt document <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:53:00">[04:53:00]</a>.
