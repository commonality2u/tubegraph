The business cash flow template is a tool designed to help users track their business's cash flow <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:06">[00:00:06]</a>. It allows for detailed monitoring of cash movements, account balances, and business activities over various periods <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:08">[00:00:08]</a>. This template is part of a broader set of [[business_tracking_solutions | business tracking solutions]] and [[finance_templates_in_notion | finance templates]].

## Cash Flow Overview

The left side of the template features a summary section providing key cash flow figures <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:10">[00:00:10]</a>. These include:
*   **Opening Balance:** The starting cash balance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:11">[00:00:11]</a>.
*   **Total Inflow:** The total cash received from all transactions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:14">[00:00:14]</a>.
*   **Cash Outflow:** The total cash paid out for all transactions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:19">[00:00:19]</a>.
*   **Closing Balance:** The final cash balance, which updates automatically as transactions are entered <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>.

## Template Sections

On the right side of the template, there are three main sections <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:28">[00:00:28]</a>: Account, Quarterly Overview, and Business Activity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>.

### Account Section

This section allows users to set up and manage different financial accounts under various heads <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:34">[00:00:34]</a>.
*   **Default Accounts:** Pre-specified accounts include cash, bank, receivables, input taxes, and other receivables under assets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.
*   **Customization:** Users can add their own assets or edit existing ones using the pencil icon <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:47">[00:00:47]</a>. New items can be added by clicking "New" under any category <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:51">[00:02:51]</a>.
*   **Account Details:** For each item, users can specify:
    *   **Opening Balance:** The starting balance when the template begins being used <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:05">[00:01:05]</a>.
    *   **Classifications:** How an item is categorized (e.g., credit card as debt) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:12">[00:01:12]</a>. New classifications can be added or existing ones edited <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:30">[00:01:30]</a>.
    *   **Payment Frequency:** How often payments are received for a particular item, useful for income and expenses (e.g., quarterly for interest income) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:39">[00:01:39]</a>.
    *   **Opening Units:** Relevant for investments or inventory, specifying the starting number of units <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:04">[00:02:04]</a>.
    *   **Asset Class:** Applicable for inventory (e.g., put under "inventory" asset class) or investments (e.g., put under "investment" asset class) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:17">[00:02:17]</a>.
*   **Applicability:** This principle of adding and editing items applies to assets, liabilities, income, and expenses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:33">[00:02:33]</a>.

### Quarterly Overview

This section provides an overview of cash flow movements for each month, divided into different quarters <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:08">[00:03:08]</a>.
*   **Structure:** It displays four quarters, with specific inflow, outflow, and net balance amounts for each month within them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:16">[00:03:16]</a>.
*   **Data Points:**
    *   **Inflow:** Cash coming into the business <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:23">[00:03:23]</a>.
    *   **Outflow:** Cash going out of the business <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:27">[00:03:27]</a>.
    *   **Net Balance:** The difference between inflow and outflow <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:31">[00:03:31]</a>.
*   **Updates:** These figures automatically update as transactions are added <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:49">[00:03:49]</a>.

### Business Activity

This section categorizes cash flow movements based on business activities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:54">[00:03:54]</a>.
*   **Operating Activities:** Relate to the core operations of the business, such as salary payments or revenue receipts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:59">[00:03:59]</a>. This includes inflow, outflow, and net balance for these activities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:08">[00:04:08]</a>.
*   **Investing Activities:** Involve investments made by the business, such as stock or mutual fund investments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:12">[00:04:12]</a>.
*   **Financing Activities:** Pertain to raising or repaying loans or debt to run the business <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:33">[00:04:33]</a>.

## Entering Transactions

The bottom section of the template is dedicated to entering transactions, which is core to the cash flow template <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:52">[00:04:52]</a>.
*   **Transaction Views:** The template offers different views for transactions, including Income, Expense, Asset, Liability, Investments, and Inventory <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:56">[00:04:56]</a>.
*   **Adding Transactions:** Dedicated buttons like "Add Income," "Add Expense," "Add Asset," and "Add Liability" automatically create new transactions with the current date <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:34">[00:05:34]</a>.
*   **Transaction Details:** Users can specify the following for each transaction:
    *   **Date:** Can be changed using a calendar <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:53">[00:05:53]</a>.
    *   **Period:** Users can define and select the relevant period (e.g., "January 2024") <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:06">[00:06:06]</a>.
    *   **Description:** A brief explanation of the income or expense (e.g., "sales revenue") <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:32">[00:06:32]</a>.
    *   **Account:** Selection from previously added accounts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:42">[00:06:42]</a>.
    *   **Payment Mode:** How the payment was received or made (e.g., cash, bank) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:54">[00:06:54]</a>.
    *   **Amount:** The monetary value of the transaction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:09">[00:07:09]</a>.
    *   **Cash Flow Type:** Categorization into Operating, Investing, or Financing activities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:17">[00:07:17]</a>.
    *   **Transaction Type:** Indicates whether cash is flowing in (Inflow) or out (Outflow) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:54">[00:07:54]</a>. Income is always inflow, expenses are always outflow <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:02">[00:08:02]</a>.
    *   **Documents:** Option to attach relevant documents like sales receipts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:39">[00:08:39]</a>.
    *   **Tax Rate:** Allows for incorporating tax calculations into transactions; entering a percentage automatically calculates the tax amount <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:48">[00:08:48]</a>.

### Investments and Inventory

Within the transaction views, dedicated sections exist for Investments and Inventory <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:07">[00:10:07]</a>.
*   **Investments:** Users can specify units purchased, price, and investment value (for purchases) or sale value (for sales) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:20">[00:10:20]</a>. The template helps calculate profit or loss on investment transactions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:54">[00:10:54]</a>.
*   **Inventory:** Users can track total inventory value at purchase price and inventory sold at sale price, indicating units and price to automatically calculate profit or loss <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:58">[00:10:58]</a>.
