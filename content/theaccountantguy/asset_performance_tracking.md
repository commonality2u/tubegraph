It is designed to be entirely customizable to suit specific requirements <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:59">[00:00:59]</a>.

## Template Sections

The template is divided into several key sections to cover various aspects of asset management:

### 1. Operational Management

This primary section focuses on the day-to-day oversight of assets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:08">[00:00:08]</a>. It includes:

*   **Asset Details**
    Users can track essential information such as asset ID, asset name, and asset type <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>. Assets are categorized to provide a clear overview <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:24">[00:00:24]</a>.
*   **[[asset_maintenance_details | Maintenance Details]]**
    This part covers all aspects related to asset maintenance, including maintenance IDs and asset IDs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>.
*   **[[tracking_investments_and_inventory_values | Financial Details]]**
    It displays the current value of assets, the depreciation method used, and the depreciation rate <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:33">[00:00:33]</a>. This contributes to overall [[managing_funds_and_balance_tracking | financial management]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>.
*   **Performance**
    This sub-section specifically tracks the performance of an asset from its creation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[00:00:37]</a>.

### 2. Regulatory Management

This section handles compliance-related aspects for assets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>.

### 3. Documentation

Users can upload and manage all relevant documents pertaining to their assets in this section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:48">[00:00:48]</a>.

### 4. Reviews

This section allows users to add their reviews or feedback about the assets being used <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:53">[00:00:53]</a>.
