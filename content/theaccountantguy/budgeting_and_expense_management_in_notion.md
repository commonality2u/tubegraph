## Overview of the Notion Expense Tracker

The [[overview_of_notion_expense_tracker | Notion Expense Tracker]] provides several sections for comprehensive financial management:

*   **Credit Cards Overview** Shows all added credit cards, displaying the total amount due, amount repaid, and remaining balance due for each <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:10">[00:00:10]</a>. For demonstration purposes, examples include Student Credit Card, Cash Back Credit Card, Travel Credit Card, and Rewards Credit Card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:18">[00:00:18]</a>.
*   **Monthly Overview** Presents monthly summaries of amounts due, repaid, and balance due, which can be customized <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:32">[00:00:32]</a>.
*   **Yearly Overview** Provides an annual summary of total amount due, amount repaid, and balance due <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.
*   **Expense Detail Section** Lists all expenses entered in detail <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:51">[00:00:51]</a>.
*   **Expenses Overview** Gives a categorized breakdown of expenses, including total expenses, budgeted amount, and budget status (under or over budget) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>.
*   **Notification Section** Displays upcoming repayment dates and the number of days remaining until the next payment for each credit card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a>.

## Using Notion for Expense Tracking

To begin [[using_notion_for_expense_tracking | expense tracking]] and [[managing_finances_with_notion | managing finances]] with this Notion tracker, users must enter expenses under the "Expense Details" section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>.

### Entering Expense Details

For each expense, the following information can be entered:
*   **Date** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:42">[00:01:42]</a>
*   **Expense Details** (description of the expense) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:44">[00:01:44]</a>
*   **Period** (e.g., month) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:46">[00:01:46]</a>
*   **Year** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:49">[00:01:49]</a>
*   **Credit Card** (associated with the expense) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:51">[00:01:51]</a>
*   **Category** (e.g., "Home & Utilities", "Shopping") <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:54">[00:01:54]</a>
*   **Expense Amount** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:57">[00:01:57]</a>

Users can add new credit cards by typing their names, which prompts the creation of a new card entry <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:37">[00:02:37]</a>. An opening balance for amounts carried forward from previous months can also be entered <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:09">[00:02:09]</a>.

### Entering Repayments

When a repayment is made, it is entered in the "Repayment" column, typically labeled as "credit card repayment" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:00">[00:03:00]</a>. All overview sections update automatically as details are entered <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:07">[00:03:07]</a>.

### Attaching Receipts

A "receipt" column allows users to upload bills or receipts for each expense, providing a complete record <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:34">[00:05:34]</a>.

## Budgeting and Saving Strategically with Notion

The "Expenses Overview" section is crucial for [[budgeting_and_saving_strategically_with_notion | budgeting and saving strategically with Notion]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:42">[00:03:42]</a>. It compares actual expenses against predefined budgeted amounts for each category <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:56">[00:03:56]</a>.

### Setting and Adjusting Budgets

Users can [[setting_and_adjusting_budgets_for_specific_expenses_in_notion | set and adjust budgets for specific expenses in Notion]] by clicking on the "Budget Amount" column and entering the desired figure <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:59">[00:03:59]</a>. The tracker immediately updates to show how current expenses relate to the set budget <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:04">[00:04:04]</a>.

*   **Under Budget:** Indicates expenses are within the set budget <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:28">[00:04:28]</a>.
*   **Over Budget:** Signifies that expenses have exceeded the budgeted amount <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:26">[00:04:26]</a>. For example, if $5,300 is spent against a $5,000 budget, it shows as "over budget" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:14">[00:04:14]</a>.

## Credit Card Repayment Tracking

The notification section helps users stay on top of credit card debt by displaying upcoming repayment dates <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:44">[00:03:44]</a>. Users can set custom repayment dates for each credit card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:40">[00:04:40]</a>. The tracker then shows how many days are left until the next repayment is due <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:49">[00:04:49]</a>, ensuring timely debt payment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:05">[00:05:05]</a>.

## Customization and Detailed Views

The Notion template offers various customization and detailed viewing options:

*   **Editing Credit Cards and Categories:** Credit card information can be edited using a pencil icon, or new credit cards and categories can be added <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:16">[00:05:16]</a>. Categories can also be added directly by entering their details <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:24">[00:05:24]</a>.
*   **Summarization Views:**
    *   Clicking on a specific **period** opens a new window showing transaction details like date, period, description, amount, and a tag indicating if it's a repayment (green) or an expense <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:44">[00:05:44]</a>.
    *   Clicking on a **year** provides similar detailed information for that specific year <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:14">[00:06:14]</a>.
    *   Clicking on a **credit card** displays all transactions related to that card, including its status and category <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:26">[00:06:26]</a>.
    *   Clicking on a **category** shows all expenses associated with that particular category <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:51">[00:06:51]</a>.

This [[using_notion_for_expense_management | Notion-based expense management]] template is highly customizable to individual needs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:24">[00:07:24]</a>.
