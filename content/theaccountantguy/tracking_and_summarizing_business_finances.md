This article describes how to set up and utilize a small business bookkeeping template in Notion to manage all your financial tracking needs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>.

## Template Structure

The template is organized into three main sections <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>:
1.  **Start Here**: Located at the top, this section is for initial business information setup <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>.
2.  **Summary**: Provides an overview of all entered transactions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>.
3.  **Transactions Window**: Where all business-related transaction details are entered <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:35">[00:00:35]</a>.

## Start Here Section

This section is where you begin by entering relevant business information <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:40">[00:00:40]</a>.

### Accounts Setup

The accounts section categorizes financial items into major heads <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>:
*   Expenses: e.g., Purchase expense, Utility bills, Subscriptions, Salaries and wages <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>
*   Liabilities: e.g., Accounts payable, Taxes payable, Accrued expenses, Credit card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:03">[00:01:03]</a>
*   Income <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>
*   Assets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:54">[00:00:54]</a>

#### Managing Accounts
*   **Edit**: Hover over an item, click the pencil icon, edit details, and click outside <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>.
*   **Delete**: Click the three-dot icon next to an item and select "Delete" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:21">[00:01:21]</a>.
*   **Add**: Scroll down to the respective category and click "new page" to add a new item <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:28">[00:01:28]</a>.

#### Account Details
Each account item allows for detailed configuration <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:40">[00:01:40]</a>:
*   **Opening Balance**: Enter balances carried forward, especially for liabilities and assets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:44">[00:01:44]</a>.
*   **Classification**: Classify items as bills, debt, savings, or other relevant categories <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:51">[00:01:51]</a>. This classification is later used within the template <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:07">[00:02:07]</a>.
*   **Payment Frequency**: Select monthly, half-yearly, quarterly, or other frequencies for recurring payments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:12">[00:02:12]</a>.
*   **Opening Units**: Specify the starting units for any inventory or assets when beginning the business <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:27">[00:02:27]</a>.
*   **Asset Class**: Choose between "Inventory" or "Investment" for asset classification <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:42">[00:02:42]</a>.

## Transactions Window

This section is for [[tracking_business_activities | tracking business activities]] by entering individual financial transactions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:04">[00:03:04]</a>.

### Adding Transactions
You can add specific transaction types by clicking the respective buttons <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:08">[00:03:08]</a>:
*   Add Income <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:10">[00:03:10]</a>
*   Add Expense <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:02">[00:06:02]</a>
*   Add Asset <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:03">[00:06:03]</a>
*   Add Liability <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:04">[00:06:04]</a>

### Transaction Fields
When adding a transaction, the following details can be entered <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:12">[00:03:12]</a>:
*   **Date**: Automatically set to the current date, but can be changed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:16">[00:03:16]</a>.
*   **Period**: Select or add the relevant month and year (e.g., October 2024) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:24">[00:03:24]</a>.
*   **Description**: A brief explanation of the transaction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:42">[00:03:42]</a>.
*   **Category**: Automatically mapped (e.g., to Income if "Add Income" was clicked) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:45">[00:03:45]</a>.
*   **Account**: Choose from the accounts set up in the "Start Here" section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:48">[00:03:48]</a>. New accounts added here will also appear in the "Start Here" section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:05">[00:04:05]</a>.
*   **Payment Mode**: Specify how payment was received or made (e.g., Bank account, Cash) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:21">[00:04:21]</a>.
*   **Transaction Type**: Indicate if it's an "Inflow" (money coming in) or "Outflow" (money going out) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:51">[00:04:51]</a>.
*   **Amount**: The monetary value of the transaction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:07">[00:05:07]</a>.
*   **Document**: Attach any relevant documents <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:15">[00:05:15]</a>.
*   **Tax Rate and Tax Amount**: Enter the tax rate, and the tax amount will be calculated automatically <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:21">[00:05:21]</a>. Set to 0% for non-taxable transactions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:28">[00:05:28]</a>.
*   **Multiplier and Currency**: For multi-currency transactions, enter a multiplier to convert to the base currency (e.g., USD to Euro) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:36">[00:05:36]</a>.

### Special Transaction Types
*   **Investments**: [[Tracking transactions and profit and loss | Track investments]] including units, price, total value, and profit/loss upon sale <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:09">[00:06:09]</a>.
*   **Inventory**: [[Tracking transactions and profit and loss | Monitor inventory]] details and calculate profit/loss from sales <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:26">[00:06:26]</a>.

## Summary Section

Once transactions are entered, the summary section automatically updates to provide an [[overview_of_business_expense_tracker | overview]] of your business finances <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:49">[00:06:49]</a>.

### Finances Overview
This section offers an "entire glance of the business" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:56">[00:06:56]</a>, showing:
*   Total assets and their breakdown (e.g., accounts receivable, input taxes, cash value) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:58">[00:06:58]</a>.
*   Income sources <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:01">[00:07:01]</a>.
*   Liabilities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:01">[00:07:01]</a>.
*   Expenses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:03">[00:07:03]</a>.

### Debt
View debt payments, including past payments and upcoming payments based on entries on the liability side <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:23">[00:07:23]</a>.

### [[Budgeting and Tracking Business Expenses | Budgeting]]
This section displays:
*   Budgeted expenses and income <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:05">[00:08:05]</a>.
*   Percentage of expenses incurred compared to the budget <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:09">[00:08:09]</a>.

### [[Tracking savings transactions and overview | Savings]]
See how much savings have been made for particular funds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:16">[00:08:16]</a>.

### Bills and Subscriptions
These sections track past payments for bills and subscriptions, similar to the debt section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:22">[00:08:22]</a>.

### Tax
Provides a quarterly breakdown of taxes, showing:
*   Taxes payable <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:36">[00:08:36]</a>.
*   Tax credit available <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:40">[00:08:40]</a>.
*   Tax liability <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:41">[00:08:41]</a>.
These figures are calculated based on your income and expense entries <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:45">[00:08:45]</a>.

### Net Worth
The net worth section helps [[dashboard_analysis_for_personal_finance | analyze your overall financial health]]:
*   **Net Worth Goal**: Set a target net worth value <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:54">[00:08:54]</a>. This goal can be set at the top of the template <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:00">[00:09:00]</a>.
*   **Total Net Worth**: Calculated from all assets, liabilities, and entered transactions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:08">[00:09:08]</a>.
*   **Difference**: The variance between your net worth goal and total net worth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:14">[00:09:14]</a>.
*   **Total Growth**: The growth in net worth from the start of the financial year <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:19">[00:09:19]</a>.
*   **Percentage of Net Worth**: The percentage of your total net worth goal achieved <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:26">[00:09:26]</a>.

### Investments and Inventory Summaries
*   **Investments**: Summarizes the value and units held for all investments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:36">[00:09:36]</a>.
*   **Inventory**: Shows the status and profit/loss for inventory items <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:43">[00:09:43]</a>.

### Overall Business Summary
A concise summary of key financial performance indicators <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:47">[00:09:47]</a>:
*   Total income <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:48">[00:09:48]</a>.
*   Total expenses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:49">[00:09:49]</a>.
*   Gross profit <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:49">[00:09:49]</a>.
*   Taxes paid <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:51">[00:09:51]</a>.
*   Net profit of the business <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:51">[00:09:51]</a>.

## How to Use the Template

To effectively use this template and get a complete [[personal_finance_tracking | personal finance tracking]] overview, start by setting up your accounts under different heads in the "Start Here" section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:58">[00:09:58]</a>. Add opening balances for assets and liabilities as relevant <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:06">[00:10:06]</a>. Then, proceed to enter details for every business transaction in the "Transactions Window" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:11">[00:10:11]</a>. All information in the "Summary" section will automatically update, providing an entire overview of your business finances at a glance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:15">[00:10:15]</a>.
