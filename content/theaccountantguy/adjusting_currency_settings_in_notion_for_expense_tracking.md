## Changing Currency in Expense Records

All currencies within the tracker can be changed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:11">[00:06:11]</a>. To modify the currency format for expense records:

1.  Navigate to the "training expenses" section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:16">[00:06:16]</a>.
2.  Switch the view to "table view" if it's not already <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:17">[00:06:17]</a>.
3.  Click on the property that displays the expense amount <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:23">[00:06:23]</a>.
4.  Select "Edit property" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:24">[00:06:24]</a>.
5.  Under "Number format," which might currently show "US Dollars," click to reveal a dropdown menu <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:25">[00:06:25]</a>.
6.  Choose any currency of your preference from the list <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:28">[00:06:28]</a>.

This adjustment allows for precise [[expense_tracking_in_notion | expense tracking]] in your preferred local currency.

## Changing Currency in Dashboard Overview

The currency displayed in the dashboard's top overview section can also be adjusted <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:32">[00:06:32]</a>. For example, to change the currency symbol on the "Total Expense" or "Average Expense" displays:

1.  Click on any of the informational fields at the top of the dashboard <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:34">[00:06:34]</a>.
2.  The currency symbol, such as the dollar sign, is embedded within the formula used for that display <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:39">[00:06:39]</a>.
3.  You can directly edit this formula to change the currency symbol to your desired representation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:41">[00:06:41]</a>.
