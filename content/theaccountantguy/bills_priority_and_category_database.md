When [[creating_a_bills_tracker_in_notion | creating a bills tracker in Notion]], two key supplemental databases are the Bills Category Database and the Bills Priority Database. These databases are linked to the primary [[using_the_bills_details_database_in_notion | Bills Details Database]] to provide structured information and facilitate data presentation on the main dashboard <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:16:00">[03:16:00]</a>.

## Bills Category Database

The Bills Category Database divides bills into six distinct categories of expense <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:33:00">[02:33:00]</a>. These categories are used for analysis and distribution of bills <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:33:00">[01:33:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:44:00">[04:44:00]</a>.

### Defined Categories
The six categories for expenses include:
*   Entertainment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:35:00">[01:35:00]</a>
*   Food and Groceries <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:37:00">[01:37:00]</a>
*   Utility Bills <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:37:00">[01:37:00]</a>
*   Travel and Transportation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:39:00">[01:39:00]</a>
*   Loans and Debts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:40:00">[01:40:00]</a>
*   Others <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:42:00">[01:42:00]</a>

### Extracted Information
For each category, the database extracts three pieces of information to show total amount paid and percentage of bills paid:
*   Number of bills paid <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:42:00">[01:42:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:57:00">[04:57:00]</a>
*   Actual expenses incurred <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:43:00">[01:43:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:59:00">[04:59:00]</a>
*   Bills paid in percentage <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:44:00">[01:44:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:01:00">[05:01:00]</a>

## Bills Priority Database

The Bills Priority Database classifies bills into three priority levels: low, medium, or high <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:22:00">[01:22:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:38:00">[02:38:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:50:00">[04:50:00]</a>.

### Extracted Information
Similar to the category database, for each priority level, the database identifies:
*   Actual expenses incurred <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:10:00">[05:10:00]</a>
*   Bills paid in percentage <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:12:00">[05:12:00]</a>

These databases help in visualizing the distribution of bills by priority and category, along with their associated expenses and payment statuses, on the main Notion dashboard <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:27:00">[06:27:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:35:00">[06:35:00]</a>.
