## Content Generation Options

Wizzygen provides two primary options for generating content within [[notion_overview | Notion]]:

1.  **[[using_wizzygen_with_notion_databases | Notion Database]]** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:11">[00:00:11]</a>: A [[notion_overview | Notion]] database is structured in the form of rows and columns <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>.
2.  **[[using_wizzygen_with_notion_pages | Notion Page]]** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:11">[00:00:11]</a>: A [[notion_overview | Notion page]] is a simple blank page <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>.
