The [[using_employee_schedule_templates | employee schedule template]] in Notion helps organizations keep track of employee schedules and manage tasks efficiently <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:05">[00:00:05]</a>. This template offers four primary views to manage different aspects of employee scheduling and performance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:12">[00:00:12]</a>.

## Overview of Views

The template is organized into four distinct views:
*   **Employee Details** Lists information for all employees <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:14">[00:00:14]</a>.
*   **Task Schedule** Allows for entering tasks for employees on specific dates <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:19">[00:00:19]</a>.
*   **Monthly View** Shows monthly data divided into quarters, including hours worked and hours on leave <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:25">[00:00:25]</a>.
*   **Employee Performance** Displays detailed performance information broken down by individual employee <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:33">[00:00:33]</a>.

## Detailed Setup and Usage

### Employee Details

This section is where you manage employee records <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:39">[00:00:39]</a>.
*   **Adding New Employees** To add a new employee, click the "New" button <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>. You can also use the "Add Employee" quick action button at the top <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>.
*   **Categories/Departments** Employees can be categorized under relevant departments, such as operations, marketing, and finance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>. New categories can be added by clicking the plus icon <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:53">[00:00:53]</a>, and existing ones can be edited <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:57">[00:00:57]</a>.
*   **Employee Information Fields** When adding a new employee, you can enter their name, employee ID, and contact information <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:06">[00:01:06]</a>. Further details include department, position, and email ID <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:11">[00:01:11]</a>.

### Task Schedule

After employee details are entered, the task schedule can be utilized <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:24">[00:01:24]</a>.
*   **Shift Organization** Tasks are divided into shifts, such as first, second, and third shifts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:50">[00:01:50]</a>.
*   **Entering Task Information**
    *   **Start and End Time** Users can enter desired dates and times for tasks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:59">[00:01:59]</a>.
    *   **Task Details** Specific task information can be added <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:10">[00:02:10]</a>.
    *   **Employee Assignment** Tasks are assigned to employees based on the employee details entered previously <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:15">[00:02:15]</a>. Employees can also be added directly from this section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:21">[00:02:21]</a>.
    *   **Department Auto-computation** The respective department for each employee is automatically displayed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:24">[00:02:24]</a>.
    *   **Hours Worked Auto-computation** The hours an employee has worked on a specific task are automatically computed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:32">[00:02:32]</a>.
*   **Attendance Tracking** Employees can mark their attendance, indicating if they are present, on leave, or have a day off <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:40">[00:02:40]</a>.
*   **Task Status Updates** Employees can update the status of their tasks as "in progress," "completed," or "not started" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:47">[00:02:47]</a>. For days off or leave, the task status can be marked as "not applicable" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:57">[00:02:57]</a>.

### Monthly View

The monthly view provides an overview of performance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:16">[00:03:16]</a>.
*   **Quarter Division** Months are divided into different quarters <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:22">[00:03:22]</a>.
*   **Performance Overview** This view shows:
    *   Total hours worked for a specific month <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:28">[00:03:28]</a>.
    *   Total hours an employee has been on leave <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:30">[00:03:30]</a>.
    *   Total hours, which is the summation of working hours and leave hours <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:32">[00:03:32]</a>.
This view provides a quick overview of overall monthly performance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:38">[00:03:38]</a>.

### Employee Performance

For individual performance insights, the employee performance section is used <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:45">[00:03:45]</a>.
*   **Individual Employee Performance** This view displays the same information as the monthly view but is broken down by each employee <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:50">[00:03:50]</a>. You can see the hours worked, hours on leave, and total hours for each employee <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:56">[00:03:56]</a>.
