The credit card expenses tracker in Notion provides dedicated sections to manage and categorize your expenditures, offering both detailed entry and categorized overviews <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:51">[00:00:51]</a>.

## Overview of Expense Sections

The Notion tracker features two primary sections for expense management:
*   **Expense Details Section** This section is used for entering individual expense records with specific details <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:51">[00:00:51]</a>.
*   **Expenses Overview Section** This provides a summary of all expenses, organized into various categories <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:58">[00:00:58]</a>.

## [[entering_and_categorizing_transactions_in_notion | Entering and Categorizing Transactions]]

To begin [[tracking_expenses_with_notion | tracking expenses with Notion]], you first need to book expenses in the **Expense Details** section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:36">[00:01:36]</a>. This acts as the [[database_setup_for_tracking_expense_details_and_sources_in_notion | database setup for tracking expense details and sources in Notion]].

Each entry in the **Expense Details** section includes several key fields:
*   **Date** The date of the expense <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:42">[00:01:42]</a>.
*   **Expense Details** A description of the expense being entered <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:44">[00:01:44]</a>.
*   **Period** The designated period for which the expense is booked <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:46">[00:01:46]</a>.
*   **Year** The specific year of the expense <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:49">[00:01:49]</a>.
*   **Credit Card** The associated credit card used for the expense <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:51">[00:01:51]</a>. New credit cards can be added by simply typing them in <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:33">[00:02:33]</a>.
*   **Category** An assigned category for each expense <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:54">[00:01:54]</a>.
*   **Expense Amount** The monetary value of the expense <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:56">[00:01:56]</a>.
*   **Repayment Section** Where you can enter the amount repaid for a particular credit card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:00">[00:02:00]</a>.
*   **Opening Balance Due** For any balances carried forward from previous months <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:07">[00:02:07]</a>.
*   **Receipt Column** Allows you to attach bills or receipts for expenses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:33">[00:05:33]</a>.

When repaying a credit card, you can simply type "credit card repayment" in the details and enter the amount in the repayment column <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:57">[00:02:57]</a>. All entered details automatically update the overview sections <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:07">[00:03:07]</a>.

## [[expense_categorization_in_notion | Expense Categorization Overview]]

The **Expenses Overview** section provides a categorized summary of your spending, making it easier to manage your budget <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:41">[00:03:41]</a>. This section displays:
*   **Expense Categories** All categories you have added in the expense details <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:46">[00:03:46]</a>.
*   **Expense Amount** The total expenses incurred to date for each category <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:52">[00:03:52]</a>.
*   **Budgeted Amount** The budget you have set for each expense category <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:55">[00:03:55]</a>. You can [[setting_up_expense_categories_in_notion | set up expense categories in Notion]] and enter or adjust budgeted amounts by clicking on the budget amount field <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:59">[00:03:59]</a>.
*   **Budget Status** Indicates whether the expenses are "under budget" or "over budget" in comparison to the set budgeted amount <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:09">[00:01:09]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:26">[00:04:26]</a>.

## Detailed Views

The tracker offers various summarization views for [[bills_summary_and_categories_in_notion | bills summary and categories in Notion]]:
*   **Period View** Clicking on a particular period opens a new window showing transaction details for that period, including dates, details, amounts, and a "tag" (e.g., green for repayment, plain for expense) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:48">[00:05:48]</a>.
*   **Yearly View** Provides similar information to the period view but for a specific year <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:15">[00:06:15]</a>.
*   **Credit Card View** Clicking on a specific credit card displays all transactions related to that card, including its status and category <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:25">[00:06:25]</a>.
*   **Category View** Selecting any category shows all expenses incurred within that particular category with relevant details <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:51">[00:06:51]</a>.
