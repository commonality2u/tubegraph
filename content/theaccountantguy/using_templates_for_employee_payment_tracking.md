From: [[theaccountantguy]] <br/> 

The "employee paycheck tracker" is a simple template designed to [[payment_calculation_for_employees | keep track of all your employee payments]] in one place <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:06">[00:00:06]</a>.

## Template Sections

The template is divided into three main sections: Records, Payments, and Summary <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:11">[00:00:11]</a>.

### Records Section

This section contains all details about the employees <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>. It includes:
*   Whether employees are permanent <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:16">[00:00:16]</a>.
*   Payments due to them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>.
*   The number of months of payment due <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:19">[00:00:19]</a>.
*   How employees are paid (monthly or hourly basis) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:23">[00:00:23]</a>.
*   The amount of payment being paid to them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>.

This provides a complete record of all employees <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:29">[00:00:29]</a>.

### Payments Section

In this section, you can view:
*   The total paycheck that is due <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:48">[00:00:48]</a>.
*   The amount that has been paid <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>.
*   The balance paycheck that is still due <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>.
*   Any advance amount that has been paid <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:51">[00:00:51]</a>.

### Summary Section

The summary section provides an overview, showing:
*   The total paycheck that is due <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>.
*   The amount that has been paid <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:57">[00:00:57]</a>.
*   The outstanding balance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:58">[00:00:58]</a>.
*   The percentage of the total paycheck that is due <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>.

## Managing Employees and Departments

*   **Adding an Employee:** To add a new employee, click on the "new" option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:33">[00:00:33]</a>.
*   **Adding a Category:** To add a new category, click on the "plus" icon <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[00:00:37]</a>.
*   **Editing Departments:** Existing departments can be edited by clicking and modifying the details <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.

## Entering Payment Details

You can input all payment details by scrolling down to the bottom of the template <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:04">[00:01:04]</a>:
*   **Date:** Enter the date of the payment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>.
*   **Period:** Specify the period for which the payment is being made <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:09">[00:01:09]</a>.
*   **Description:** Add a description of the payment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:13">[00:01:13]</a>.
*   **Employee Selection:** Select the specific employee for whom the payment is being recorded <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a>.
*   **Pay Rate & Hours:**
    *   For hourly paid employees, specify the pay rate and the number of hours worked, and the payment amount will be calculated automatically <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:21">[00:01:21]</a>.
    *   For monthly paid employees, specify the pay rate and leave the hour column blank; this indicates a monthly payment basis <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:28">[00:01:28]</a>.
*   **Payment Status:** A column indicates if a payment has been made or not. To mark a payment as unpaid, simply untick the box <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:36">[00:01:36]</a>.

## Quarterly Section

The quarterly section displays the total paycheck due, amount paid, and net balance for each quarter <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:46">[00:01:46]</a>. This payment data is divided into "permanent" and "contractual" categories based on the employee details entered <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:53">[00:01:53]</a>.
