An asset planner template is available to help manage various aspects of assets, covering multiple sections <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:05">[00:00:05]</a>.

## Operational Management
The template includes an [[operational_management_for_assets | operational management]] section designed to oversee asset details, maintenance, financials, and performance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:08">[00:00:08]</a>.

### Asset Details
Key information tracked for each asset includes the asset ID, asset name, and asset type <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>.

### Financials
The financial details within the planner provide critical information for managing asset value. This includes the asset's current value, the depreciation method being used, and the depreciation rate <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:31">[00:00:31]</a>.

### Asset Performance
The template allows for tracking [[asset_performance_evaluation | asset performance]] from the moment an asset is created <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[00:00:37]</a>.

### Maintenance
A dedicated [[asset_maintenance_tracking | maintenance section]] covers details such as the maintenance ID and asset ID, alongside other relevant maintenance information <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>.

## Regulatory Management
The template features a regulatory management section that handles compliance-related information for assets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>.

## Documentation and Reviews
Users can add all relevant documents related to an asset in the documentation section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:48">[00:00:48]</a>. Additionally, a reviews section allows users to provide feedback on assets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:53">[00:00:53]</a>.

## Customizability
This asset planner template is fully customizable to meet specific user requirements <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:59">[00:00:59]</a>.
