This article describes a house rent payments tracker template, which can be utilized to [[setting_up_house_rent_payments_tracker_in_notion | keep track of house rental payments]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:04">[[00:00:04]]</a>.

## Template Overview

The template is structured with a "summary section" at the top, featuring four distinct views <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:10">[[00:00:10]]</a>, and a "transaction section" with three different views, plus a comprehensive view of all transactions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:20">[[00:01:20]]</a>.

### Summary Section

This section provides an overview of your rental properties and payments:

*   **Basic Information View**: This view facilitates the addition of tenants, categorized under different classifications of properties you own <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[[00:00:13]]</a>. Pre-added classifications include "apartment" and "duplex," with the option to add others <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:20">[[00:00:20]]</a>. New tenants can be added by clicking the "ADD tenant" button, which creates a new entry that can be dragged under the appropriate property category <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[[00:00:27]]</a>. Tenant details are entered by clicking a pencil icon <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[[00:00:37]]</a>.
*   **Property Section View**: This displays different property classifications <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[[00:00:46]]</a>. Underneath each property, you can see the tenant's name and key financial information, including:
    *   Total rent amount <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:54">[[00:00:54]]</a>
    *   Rent amount that has been paid <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:55">[[00:00:55]]</a>
    *   Balance rent amount that is due <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:57">[[00:00:57]]</a>
    *   Advanced rent that has been paid <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:58">[[00:00:58]]</a>
*   **Rental History View**: This shows the [[viewing_rental_transactions_and_summaries | rental history]] of all tenants who have made payments, based on transactions recorded at the bottom of the template <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:04">[[00:01:04]]</a>.
*   **Summary Section (Overall)**: Provides an overall summary of rent finances, including:
    *   Total amount due <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:14">[[00:01:14]]</a>
    *   Amount that has been paid <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:15">[[00:01:15]]</a>
    *   Outstanding rent amount <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[[00:01:16]]</a>
    *   Percentage of rent collected <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:17">[[00:01:17]]</a>

### Transaction Section

This section is dedicated to [[viewing_rental_transactions_and_summaries | managing rental transactions]]:

*   **Rent Paid View**: Displays all transactions for which rent has been paid, along with the respective tenants and amounts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:25">[[00:01:25]]</a>.
*   **Rent Due View**: Shows information for rent that is due and has not yet been paid <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[[00:01:31]]</a>. Clicking a checkbox in this view marks the rent as paid, moving it to the "Rent Paid" category <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:35">[[00:01:35]]</a>.
*   **Quarterly Summary View**: Provides a summary of rental payments and collections, showing the balance due for every month within each respective quarter <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:41">[[00:01:41]]</a>.

### Adding Payments

To quickly add a new rental payment, click the dedicated button provided in the template <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:50">[[00:01:50]]</a>.
