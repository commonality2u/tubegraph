## Content Creation Options in Notion

Wien offers two primary methods for content generation in Notion:
*   **Notion Page**: A simple, blank page in Notion <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>.
*   **Notion Database**: Content structured in rows and columns <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>.

## Generating Content on a Notion Page

To generate content on a Notion page:
1.  Name the connection <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:34">[00:00:34]</a>.
2.  Select "Notion page" as the content type <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:38">[00:00:38]</a>.
3.  Copy and paste the Notion page's URL into the designated section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.
4.  Provide a prompt, such as "write me a block of 100 words on Notion" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:48">[00:00:48]</a>.
Once the prompt is given, the [[ai_content_generation_for_notion | AI content]] will be automatically generated and appear on the Notion page <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:04">[00:01:04]</a>.

## Generating Content in a Notion Database for Specific Topics

For [[generating_ai_content_on_notion_pages_and_databases | AI content generation]] within a Notion database, which is particularly useful for [[bulk_document_generation_in_notion | generating content for multiple specific topics]]:
1.  Name the connection (e.g., "Notion database") <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:21">[00:01:21]</a>.
2.  Select "Notion database" from the options <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:25">[00:01:25]</a>.
3.  Copy the URL of the Notion database and paste it into the platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:28">[00:01:28]</a>.
4.  Formulate a prompt that incorporates the dynamic topic from the database rows, for instance, "write me a block of 100 words on the topic" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:48">[00:01:48]</a>. This allows the AI to generate unique content for each topic listed in the database rows <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:40">[00:01:40]</a>.

The AI will then populate the content for each row in the database, with every row being captured for content generation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:01">[00:02:01]</a>. This demonstrates how Wien can be utilized to generate [[generating_ai_content_on_notion_pages_and_databases | AI content]] for both Notion pages and databases <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:21">[00:02:21]</a>.
