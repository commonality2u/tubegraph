## Investment Dashboard Features

The investment dashboard in the [[using_a_personal_finance_tracker_spreadsheet|personal finance tracker spreadsheet]] provides a comprehensive overview of your investment portfolio <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:21">[00:05:21]</a>.

### Automatic Updates and Real-time Data
Once you enter the symbol for an investment, the price and graphs update automatically <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:36">[00:04:36]</a>. This data updates in real-time as trading occurs in the stock market <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:39">[00:04:39]</a>.

### Required Inputs
To utilize the investment dashboard, you need to provide the following details:
*   **Symbol** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:23">[00:04:23]</a>
*   **Number of Shares** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:24">[00:04:24]</a>
*   **Purchase Price** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:25">[00:04:25]</a>

For example, if you enter "Apple" as a symbol, followed by purchasing 15 shares at $200, all other related data will automatically update <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:27">[00:04:27]</a>.

### Overview and Analysis
The investment dashboard provides a quick representation of several key metrics <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:58">[00:04:58]</a>:
*   **Market Value**: The current market value of the symbols you hold <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:00">[00:05:00]</a>.
*   **Cost and Market Value Analysis**: Comparison of cost versus current market value <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:03">[00:05:03]</a>.
*   **Growth**: The growth of your investments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:04">[00:05:04]</a>.
*   **Symbol Representation**: Visual representation of the symbols <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:05">[00:05:05]</a>.
*   **Portfolio Value**: The total value of your investment portfolio <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:09">[00:05:09]</a>.
*   **Portfolio Gain**: The overall gain of your portfolio, both in absolute terms and as a percentage <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:10">[00:05:10]</a>.
*   **Dividend Income**: Income generated from dividends <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:12">[00:05:12]</a>.
*   **Total Holdings**: A summary of all your investment holdings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:15">[00:05:15]</a>.

All these parameters are updated in real-time as trading happens, allowing you to see your entire investments at a glance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:17">[00:05:17]</a>.
