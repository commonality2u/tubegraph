From: [[theaccountantguy]] <br/> 

The "employee paycheck tracker" is a simple template designed to help users [[using_notion_for_expense_tracking | keep track of all employee payments]] in one centralized location <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:04">[00:00:04]</a>.

## Template Sections

The template is organized into three main sections: Records, Payments, and Summary <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:11">[00:00:11]</a>.

### Records

This section provides a comprehensive record of all employees <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:31">[00:00:31]</a>. It includes details such as:
*   Whether employees are permanent <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:16">[00:00:16]</a>.
*   Payments due to them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>.
*   The number of months for which payment is due <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:19">[00:00:19]</a>.
*   How employees are paid (monthly or hourly basis) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>.
*   The amount of payment being disbursed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>.

Users can add new employees by clicking "new" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:33">[00:00:33]</a> and new categories by clicking the plus icon <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[00:00:37]</a>. Existing department details can also be edited <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.

### Payments

The payments section displays key financial metrics related to employee paychecks:
*   Total paycheck due <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:48">[00:00:48]</a>.
*   Amount paid <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>.
*   Balance paycheck due <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>.
*   Any advance amounts paid <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:51">[00:00:51]</a>.

### Summary

The summary section provides an overview of the financial status of employee payments:
*   Total paycheck due <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>.
*   Amount paid <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:57">[00:00:57]</a>.
*   Outstanding balance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:58">[00:00:58]</a>.
*   Percentage of the total paycheck due <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>.

## Detailed Payment Entry

At the bottom of the template, users can input specific payment details <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:04">[00:01:04]</a>:
*   **Date:** The date of the payment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>.
*   **Period:** The period for which the payment is being made <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:08">[00:01:08]</a>.
*   **Description:** A description of the payment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:13">[00:01:13]</a>.
*   **Employee Details:** Select the employee to whom the payment applies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a>.
*   **Pay Rate:** Specify the pay rate.
    *   For hourly employees, enter the pay rate and number of hours, and the payment amount will be automatically calculated <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:21">[00:01:21]</a>.
    *   For monthly employees, specify the pay rate and leave the hour column blank <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:28">[00:01:28]</a>.
*   **Payment Status:** A column indicates whether the payment has been made or is still due (can be unticked if unpaid) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:34">[00:01:34]</a>.

## Quarterly Section

The template also includes a quarterly section that shows the total paycheck due, amount paid, and net balance for each quarter <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:46">[00:01:46]</a>. Payments are divided into "permanent" and "contractual" categories based on the details entered <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:53">[00:01:53]</a>.
