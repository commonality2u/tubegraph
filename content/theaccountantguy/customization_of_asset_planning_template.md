## Template Sections

The asset planner template includes various sections to cover comprehensive asset management <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:05">[00:00:05]</a>.

### Operational Management
This section covers key operational aspects of assets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:08">[00:00:08]</a>.
*   **Asset Details**: Includes fields for asset ID, asset name, and asset type <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>.
*   **Maintenance Details**: Records information such as maintenance ID and asset ID, alongside other maintenance-related details <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>.
*   **Financial Details**: Displays the current value, depreciation method, and depreciation rate for assets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:31">[00:00:31]</a>.
*   **Performance**: Tracks and shows the performance of assets as they are created <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[00:00:37]</a>.

### Regulatory Management
Found at the bottom of the template, this section handles regulatory aspects <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.
*   **Compliance**: Contains all information related to compliance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:44">[00:00:44]</a>.
*   **Documentation**: Allows users to add all relevant documents required for the asset <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:47">[00:00:47]</a>.
*   **Reviews**: Provides a space for users to submit reviews about the assets being used <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>.
