From: [[theaccountantguy]] <br/> 

The "Employee Paycheck Tracker" is a Notion template designed to keep track of all employee payments in one location <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:04">[00:00:04]</a>. This template can assist with [[using_notion_for_financial_management | financial management]] related to employee compensation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:06">[00:00:06]</a>.

The template is structured into three main sections:
*   Records <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:11">[00:00:11]</a>
*   Payments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:12">[00:00:12]</a>
*   Summary <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>

## Records Section

This section provides detailed information about each employee <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>. It records:
*   Whether employees are permanent <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:16">[00:00:16]</a>
*   Payments due to them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>
*   The number of months of payment due <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:19">[00:00:19]</a>
*   How employees are paid (monthly or hourly) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:23">[00:00:23]</a>
*   The amount of payment being made to them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>

This section serves as a comprehensive record of all employees <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:29">[00:00:29]</a>. Users can add new employees by clicking "new" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:33">[00:00:33]</a> or add new categories by clicking the plus sign <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[00:00:37]</a>. Existing departments can also be edited <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.

## Payment Section

The payment section displays key financial figures, including:
*   Total paycheck due <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:48">[00:00:48]</a>
*   Amount paid <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>
*   Balance paycheck due <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>
*   Any advance amount paid <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:51">[00:00:51]</a>

## Summary Section

The summary section provides an overview of the payment status, showing:
*   Total paycheck due <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>
*   Amount paid <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:57">[00:00:57]</a>
*   Outstanding balance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:58">[00:00:58]</a>
*   Percentage of total paycheck due <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>

## Feeding in Payment Details

Users can input specific payment details by scrolling to the bottom of the template <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:04">[00:01:04]</a>. This includes:
*   **Date:** The date of the payment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>.
*   **Period:** The payment period can be entered by clicking a dedicated field <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>.
*   **Description:** A description of the payment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:13">[00:01:13]</a>.
*   **Employee Details:** Users can select the employee for whom the payment is being made <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a>.
*   **Pay Rate & Hours:** For hourly paid employees, the pay rate and number of hours can be specified, and the payment amount will be automatically calculated <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:21">[00:01:21]</a>. For monthly paid employees, only the pay rate is needed, and the hour column can be left blank <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:28">[00:01:28]</a>.
*   **Payment Status:** A column indicates if the payment has been made, which can be unticked if a payment is due but not yet paid <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:34">[00:01:34]</a>.

## Quarterly Section

The template also features a quarterly section that displays:
*   Total paycheck due per quarter <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:46">[00:01:46]</a>
*   Amount paid per quarter <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:49">[00:01:49]</a>
*   Net balance for each quarter <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:49">[00:01:49]</a>

This payment data is further categorized into "permanent" and "contractual" employees <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:53">[00:01:53]</a>.
