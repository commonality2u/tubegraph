## Setting Up a Bill Reminder Template

To create a new [[setting_up_bill_payment_reminders_on_whatsapp | bill payment reminder]]:
1.  Click on the drop-down menu and select "New template" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:20">[00:00:20]</a>.
2.  Specify the bill name, such as "Mobile bill" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>.
3.  Enter the bill amount, for example, $1500 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:31">[00:00:31]</a>.
4.  Once entered, click "Back" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:33">[00:00:33]</a>.

### Setting Recurring Payments

To set the bill to repeat on a recurring basis:
1.  Click the drop-down again and then the three dots next to the bill entry <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[00:00:37]</a>.
2.  Choose the recurrence frequency, for example, "Every month" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:43">[00:00:43]</a>.
3.  Specify the exact time and day when you want to receive the notification, such as 11:55 AM on the 20th of every month <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>.
4.  Click "Save" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>. This setup will send a notification to your [[setting_up_bill_payment_reminders_on_whatsapp | WhatsApp]] at the specified time each month <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>.

> [!example]
> An example shows a "Wi-Fi bills" reminder with a due date of September 20 and a payment amount of $3000 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:14">[00:00:14]</a>.

## Integrating with Pabbly Connect

For this automation to work, the help of [[stepbystep_guide_on_using_p_connect_for_automation | Pabbly Connect]] is required <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:06">[00:01:06]</a>.
*   [[stepbystep_guide_on_using_p_connect_for_automation | Pabbly Connect]] is used to set up the entire automation flow <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:11">[00:01:11]</a>.
*   A detailed explanation and guide are included when you download the template for this automation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:15">[00:01:15]</a>.

Once this automation is set up in [[stepbystep_guide_on_using_p_connect_for_automation | Pabbly Connect]], you will automatically receive notifications on your [[setting_up_bill_payment_reminders_on_whatsapp | WhatsApp]] every month for recurring bills <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:25">[00:01:25]</a>.

### Automatic Notifications in Action
When a new bill is added and the specified time approaches (e.g., 11:55 AM on the 20th of September), a new notification item will automatically be added and a message will be sent to [[setting_up_bill_payment_reminders_on_whatsapp | WhatsApp]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:44">[00:01:44]</a>. Notifications typically arrive within a minute of the set time <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:57">[00:01:57]</a>.
