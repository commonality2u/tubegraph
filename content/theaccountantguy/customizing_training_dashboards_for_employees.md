The employee training expenses tracker provides a comprehensive training dashboard with two primary views: Training Overview and Employee Records <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:06"></a>. This dashboard is highly customizable to suit specific organizational needs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37"></a>.

## Training Dashboard Overview

The **Training Overview** section provides a summary of all planned trainings, including:
*   **Training Date:** When employees are scheduled to attend <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21"></a>.
*   **Employees Count:** The total number of employees expected to attend <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:24"></a>.
*   **Percentage of Attendance:** Indicates the number of employees who have attended a specific training <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:29"></a>.
*   **Training Expense:** The cost associated with each training <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:34"></a>.

The **Employee Records** section lists employees, organized by their respective departments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:47"></a>. For each employee, the dashboard shows:
*   **Total Trainings Attended:** A quick overview of attended versus assigned trainings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:24"></a>.
*   **Percentage of Attendance:** The individual attendance rate for each employee <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:36"></a>.
*   **Next Training Date:** Allows for specifying the next scheduled training date, showing remaining days until the training <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:39"></a>.

### Expenses Overview
A dedicated expenses overview on the left side of the dashboard provides a summary of all training-related costs:
*   **Total Expense:** The aggregate cost incurred for all trainings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:08"></a>.
*   **Number of Trainings:** The total count of trainings conducted <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:11"></a>.
*   **Employees Count:** Total number of employees recorded <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:19"></a>.
*   **Average Expense:** The expense incurred per employee <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:25"></a>. This data automatically updates as transaction details are entered <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:29"></a>.

## Customizing Dashboard Content

### Managing Departments and Employees
*   **Editing Departments:** Departments can be directly edited by clicking on their names <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50"></a>.
*   **Adding New Departments:** New departments can be added by dragging a slider to the right and clicking the plus icon <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:01"></a>.
*   **Moving Employees:** Employees can be moved between departments by dragging their entry to the desired department <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:15"></a>, or by using the three-dot menu to shift them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:56"></a>.
*   **Adding Employee Records:** New employee records can be quickly added using the "add employee" quick action button, initially appearing under "No Department" before being assigned <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:47"></a>.
*   **Editing Employee Details:** Employee names and other details can be edited directly by clicking the edit button <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:00"></a>.

### Managing Training Records
The training records section allows for detailed management of individual training sessions:
*   **Setting Training Dates:** Assign dates for each training session <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:21"></a>.
*   **Adding Training Details:** Input specific details about each training <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:25"></a>.
*   **Selecting Employees:** Assign specific employees to trainings by selecting them from a list <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:29"></a>. Employees can also be edited or added directly from this selection menu, though using the quick action button is recommended <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:34"></a>.
*   **Tracking Attendance:** Use a checkbox to indicate if employees have attended a training <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:08"></a>. The attendance percentage for the training automatically updates based on these selections <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:30"></a>.
*   **Adding Training Records:** Use the "add training" quick action button to create new training records and add all relevant details <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:59"></a>.

### Managing Training Expenses
The training expenses section allows for editing cost information:
*   **Editing Expenses:** Training expense details can be modified using a pencil icon next to each entry <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:23"></a>. Updates are reflected immediately <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:32"></a>.
*   **Changing View:** The view can be switched to a table format for quicker editing of information <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:54"></a>.

## [[currency_customization_in_notion_dashboards | Currency Customization]]

The dashboard offers flexibility in [[customizing_dashboard_currency | currency customization]]:
*   **Changing Currency in Tables:** To change the currency displayed in table views (e.g., for training expenses), click on the number property, select "Edit Property," and change the "Number format" to any currency of your choice <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:11"></a>.
*   **Changing Currency in Dashboard Overview:** For currencies displayed in the top dashboard overview (e.g., total expense, average expense), the dollar sign is typically part of the formula and can be edited there to reflect a different currency symbol <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:32"></a>. This allows for complete customization to fit specific [[utilizing_notion_dashboards_for_financial_tracking | financial tracking]] requirements <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:42"></a>.
