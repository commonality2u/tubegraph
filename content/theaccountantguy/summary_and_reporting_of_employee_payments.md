From: [[theaccountantguy]] <br/> 

An Employee Paycheck Tracker template is a simple tool designed to [[paycheck_budget_tracker_overview | keep track of all employee payments]] in one centralized location <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:04">[00:00:04]</a>.

## Template Sections

The template is typically divided into three main sections:
*   **Records** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:11">[00:00:11]</a>
*   **Payments** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:12">[00:00:12]</a>
*   **Summary** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>

### Records Section
This section is dedicated to [[managing_employee_payment_records | maintaining details]] about each employee <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>. It includes:
*   Whether employees are permanent <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:16">[00:00:16]</a>
*   Payments due to them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>
*   Number of months payment is due <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:19">[00:00:19]</a>
*   Payment basis (monthly or hourly) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>
*   Amount of payment being paid <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>

You can add new employees by clicking a "new" button <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:33">[00:00:33]</a> or add new categories using a "plus" button <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[00:00:37]</a>. Existing departments can also be edited <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.

### Payment Section
This section provides an overview of payment statuses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>, displaying:
*   Total paycheck due <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:48">[00:00:48]</a>
*   Amount that has been paid <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>
*   Balance paycheck still due <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>
*   Any advance amount paid <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:51">[00:00:51]</a>

### Summary Section
The summary section offers a concise report <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:55">[00:00:55]</a>, showing:
*   Total paycheck due <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>
*   Amount paid <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:57">[00:00:57]</a>
*   Outstanding balance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:58">[00:00:58]</a>
*   Percentage of total paycheck that is due <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>

## Feeding Payment Details

Users can input specific payment details into the template <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:04">[00:01:04]</a>, including:
*   Date of payment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:06">[00:01:06]</a>
*   Period for which the payment is made <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:08">[00:01:08]</a>
*   Description of the payment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:13">[00:01:13]</a>
*   Employee details (selected from a list of added employees) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:15">[00:01:15]</a>
*   Pay rate <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:20">[00:01:20]</a>
*   Number of hours (for hourly paid employees, which automatically calculates the payment amount) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:22">[00:01:22]</a>
*   For monthly paid employees, the hour column can be left blank <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:28">[00:01:28]</a>.
*   A tickbox indicates if a payment has been made; unticking it marks the payment as unpaid <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:34">[00:01:34]</a>.

## Quarterly Section

The template also offers a [[quarterly_financial_summaries | quarterly section]] which breaks down payment information for each quarter and month <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:46">[00:01:46]</a>, showing:
*   Total paycheck due <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:47">[00:01:47]</a>
*   Amount paid <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:49">[00:01:49]</a>
*   Net balance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:49">[00:01:49]</a>

Payments within this section are further categorized into permanent and contractual <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:53">[00:01:53]</a>.
