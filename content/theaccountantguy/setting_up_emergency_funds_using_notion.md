This article explains how to utilize a Notion template to track emergency funds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:07">[00:00:07]</a>. The template offers a comprehensive system to manage your financial safety net <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:11">[00:00:11]</a>.

## Template Overview

The template includes three main sections for managing emergency funds:
*   **Fund Setup**: Where you define and categorize your emergency funds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>.
*   **Savings Overview**: Provides a summary of all savings made to date <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:15">[00:00:15]</a>.
*   **Summary Section**: Reflects all savings and required contributions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:20">[00:00:20]</a>.

## Fund Setup

The "Fund Setup" section is where you establish your emergency fund categories and individual funds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>.

### Categories
By default, there are three predefined categories:
*   Financial Safety <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:31">[00:00:31]</a>
*   Asset Protection <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:32">[00:00:32]</a>
*   Health and Wellness <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:34">[00:00:34]</a>

You can edit these existing categories or add new ones by clicking "Add a group" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:35">[00:00:35]</a>.

### Adding a New Fund
To add a new fund, click the "Add Fund" button <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:51">[00:00:51]</a>. This automatically creates a new fund under a "No Classification" tab <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:58">[00:00:58]</a>. You can then move it to the desired category using the three-dot menu <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:02">[00:01:02]</a>.

### Fund Details
For each fund, you can specify the following details by clicking on the pencil icon <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:11">[00:01:11]</a>:
*   **Fund Detail**: A descriptive name for the fund <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:14">[00:01:14]</a>.
*   **Starting Balance**: The initial amount available for the fund <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:17">[00:01:17]</a>.
*   **Goal Amount**: The targeted savings you aim to achieve for this emergency fund <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:23">[00:01:23]</a>.
*   **Start Date**: When you begin contributing money to this specific fund <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:32">[00:01:32]</a>.
*   **Savings Made Till Date**: Automatically calculated based on contributions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:42">[00:01:42]</a>.
*   **Target End Date**: The date by which you aim to have the total emergency fund available <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:48">[00:01:48]</a>.

## Contribution Section

The "Contribution" section is where you record the money you put into your emergency savings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:17">[00:02:17]</a>.

### Contributions Layout
Contributions are organized by month, with the latest month appearing at the top <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:35">[00:02:35]</a>. It also indicates the number of contributions made for each month <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:41">[00:02:41]</a>.

### Contribution Details
For each contribution entry, you can specify:
*   **Date**: The date of the contribution <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:45">[00:02:45]</a>.
*   **Description**: A brief description of the contribution <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:46">[00:02:46]</a>.
*   **Period**: The month and year of the contribution <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:48">[00:02:48]</a> (e.g., March 2024 can be added if not pre-defined <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:59">[00:02:59]</a>).
*   **Fund**: Select the specific emergency fund to which the contribution is being made <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:13">[00:03:13]</a>.
*   **Amount**: The amount of savings or contribution for the specific month towards the specific fund <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:25">[00:03:25]</a>.
*   **Requirement**: This column automatically calculates how much contribution is required each month to meet the targeted goal amount by the end date <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:35">[00:03:35]</a>.
*   **Check Mark**: A tick mark indicates that you have met or exceeded the required contribution for that fund in that month <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:02">[00:04:02]</a>.

### Quick Action Button
Using the "quick action" button automatically adds a new line item to the current month and date, populating it with formulas for convenience <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:31">[00:04:31]</a>.

## Quarterly Overview

The "Quarterly Overview" provides a glance view of all contributions across each quarter <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:09">[00:05:09]</a>.
*   Months are grouped into quarters (e.g., January-March is Quarter 1) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:17">[00:05:17]</a>.
*   For each month, it displays the total savings made and the total requirement <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:31">[00:05:31]</a>.
*   It also shows the percentage of the savings requirement met <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:04">[00:06:04]</a>.
*   Different colors provide a quick visual snapshot of your financial progress <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:29">[00:06:29]</a>.
*   The top of the section shows the total savings made for each quarter <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:41">[00:06:41]</a>.

## Savings Overview

The "Savings Overview" section re-categorizes your savings by the three main classifications, providing detailed insights for each emergency fund <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:58">[00:06:58]</a>.
*   **Total Savings**: The cumulative amount saved for each fund, including the starting balance and all contributions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:09">[00:07:09]</a>.
*   **Targeted Savings**: The goal amount set for each fund, pulled directly from the Fund Setup <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:35">[00:07:35]</a>.
*   **Achieved Percentage**: Shows what percentage of the total savings requirement has been met <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:46">[00:07:46]</a>.
*   **Targeted Savings Date**: The chosen end date for reaching the fund's goal <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:50">[00:07:50]</a>.
*   **Months Left**: Calculates the remaining time from the current date until the targeted end date <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:05">[00:08:05]</a>. This value updates automatically <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:03">[00:09:03]</a>.
*   **Contribution Required per Month**: Displays the monthly contribution needed to meet the targeted fund by the end date <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:31">[00:08:31]</a>.

## Summary Section

The "Summary Section" provides a high-level overview of your overall emergency fund progress <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:17">[00:09:17]</a>.
*   **Total Savings**: The sum of opening balances across all funds and all contributions made <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:20">[00:09:20]</a>.
*   **Savings Required**: The sum of all targeted savings amounts across all funds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:33">[00:09:33]</a>.
*   **Difference**: The total savings minus the total savings required <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:42">[00:09:42]</a>.
*   **Percentage**: The total savings as a percentage of the targeted savings amount <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:52">[00:09:52]</a>.
