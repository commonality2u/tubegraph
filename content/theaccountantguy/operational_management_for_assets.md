## Operational Management Section

Within the operational management section, key details regarding assets are covered <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:08">[00:00:08]</a>. This includes:

### Asset Details
The asset details section includes information such as asset ID, asset name, and asset type, among other categorized specifics <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>.

### Asset Maintenance
The [[asset_maintenance_tracking | maintenance section]] covers details like maintenance ID and asset ID, alongside all other relevant [[asset_maintenance_tracking | maintenance details]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>.

### Financial Management
The [[financial_management_of_assets | financials]] section displays the current value, depreciation method, and depreciation rate of the asset <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:32">[00:00:32]</a>.

### Asset Performance
The [[asset_performance_evaluation | performance]] section shows the [[asset_performance_evaluation | asset performance]] as it is created <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[00:00:37]</a>.

## Other Management Sections
