The Customer Payments Tracker is a Notion template designed to help users keep track of customer payments in one centralized location <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:04">[00:00:04]</a>. This template is highly [[customizing_notion_templates | customizable]] to fit specific requirements <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:54">[00:01:54]</a>.

## Template Sections

The tracker is divided into key sections:
*   **Summary Section** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:09">[00:00:09]</a>: Displays total payment due, amount received, the difference, and the percentage of total payments received <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:11">[00:00:11]</a>.
*   **Customer Section** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:20">[00:00:20]</a>: Allows users to set up and manage their customer database <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:24">[00:00:24]</a>.
*   **Overview Section** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>: Shows total payments received, payment due, and the percentage of payment received <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:15">[00:01:15]</a>.
*   **Transaction Section** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>: Used to enter and track individual transactions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:22">[00:01:22]</a>.

## [[customizing_notion_templates | Customizing Notion Templates]]

### Customer Section Customization
The Customer section is initially divided into "Regular," "Seasonal," and "Occasional" subsections <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:28">[00:00:28]</a>. Users have several [[customizing_notion_templates | customization]] options within this section:

*   **[[customizing_notion_templates | Editing]] Subsections**: To [[customizing_notion_templates | edit]] any of these subsections, simply click on them and start [[customizing_notion_templates | editing]] the details <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:33">[00:00:33]</a>.
*   **[[setting_up_templates_and_buttons_in_notion | Adding]] Subsections**: New subsections can be [[setting_up_templates_and_buttons_in_notion | added]] by clicking the plus icon <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:38">[00:00:38]</a>.
*   **[[customizing_notion_templates | Editing]] Customer Payment Amount**: For each customer, the total payment amount due can be [[customizing_notion_templates | changed]] by clicking on the amount and entering a new value <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>.
*   **[[customizing_notion_templates | Editing]] Customer Name**: Customer names can be [[customizing_notion_templates | edited]] by clicking the pencil icon next to the name <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:57">[00:00:57]</a>.
*   **[[setting_up_templates_and_buttons_in_notion | Adding]] New Customers**: Users can [[setting_up_templates_and_buttons_in_notion | add]] new customers to the database <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>.
*   **[[setting_up_templates_and_buttons_in_notion | Deleting]] Customers**: Existing customers can be [[setting_up_templates_and_buttons_in_notion | deleted]] by clicking the three-dot menu and selecting "delete" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:05">[00:01:05]</a>.

### Transaction Section Details
The Transaction section allows for detailed entry of payment activities:
*   **Date**: Record the date of the transaction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:24">[00:01:24]</a>.
*   **Description**: Add a description for the transaction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:25">[00:01:25]</a>.
*   **Customer Name**: Select the relevant customer from the existing database <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:27">[00:01:27]</a>.
*   **Amount**: Enter the transaction amount <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:33">[00:01:33]</a>.
*   **Document Section**: Attach specific invoices or related documents to the transaction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:35">[00:01:35]</a>. Filling these details updates the "payment received" amount in the overview section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:40">[00:01:40]</a>.
