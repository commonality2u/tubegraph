Wizzygen is a platform designed to facilitate [[creating_ai_generated_content_on_notion_pages_and_databases | creating AI generated content]] directly onto a [[setting_up_wizzygen_for_notion_workspace | Notion workspace]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:00">[00:00:00]</a>. It offers two primary methods for content creation: generating content on a Notion database or a Notion page <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:10">[00:00:10]</a>.

## Methods of Content Creation

*   **Notion Page**: This refers to a basic, blank page created within Notion <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>.
*   **Notion Database**: This is structured in rows and columns, typical of Notion databases <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>.

Wizzygen can [[integrating_ai_generated_content_into_notion | integrate AI-generated content]] into both Notion pages and databases <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>.

## Generating Content on a Notion Page

To generate content on a Notion page, a connection is built within Wizzygen <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:32">[00:00:32]</a>.

[[steps_for_using_wizzygen_with_notion | The process includes]]:
1.  Naming the connection, for example, "notion page" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:35">[00:00:35]</a>.
2.  [[defining_connection_databases_in_wizzygen | Selecting the "notion page"]] option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:38">[00:00:38]</a>.
3.  Copying and pasting the URL of the target Notion page into the designated section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.
4.  Clicking "next" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>.
5.  Providing a prompt for the AI, such as "write me a block of 100 words on notion" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>.

Upon execution, the AI-generated content will appear directly on the specified Notion page <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:04">[00:01:04]</a>. This demonstrates [[creating_content_on_notion_pages | creating content on Notion pages]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:12">[00:01:12]</a>.

## Generating Content on a Notion Database

For Notion databases, Wizzygen can [[populating_notion_databases_with_aigenerated_content | populate rows with AI-generated content]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:15">[00:01:15]</a>.

[[steps_for_using_wizzygen_with_notion | The steps are similar]]:
1.  Name the connection, for example, "notion database" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:21">[00:01:21]</a>.
2.  [[defining_connection_databases_in_wizzygen | Select the "notion database"]] option <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:25">[00:01:25]</a>.
3.  Copy and paste the URL of the Notion database <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:28">[00:01:28]</a>.
4.  Click "next" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:36">[00:01:36]</a>.
5.  Formulate a prompt, potentially referencing a column in the database, like "write me a block of 100 words on the topic" to generate content for each topic listed in the database <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:38">[00:01:38]</a>.

The content will then begin populating the respective rows within the Notion database <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:58">[00:01:58]</a>, capturing each row and generating content accordingly <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:17">[00:02:17]</a>.

Wizzygen simplifies the process of generating AI content for both Notion pages and databases <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:21">[00:02:21]</a>.
