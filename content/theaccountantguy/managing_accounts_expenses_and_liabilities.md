This article describes how to manage accounts, expenses, and liabilities using a small business bookkeeping template in Notion, offering a comprehensive overview of financial tracking from setup to summary reporting <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>.

## Template Structure

The Notion small business bookkeeping template is organized into three main sections <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>:
1.  **Start Here Section**: Located at the top, this section is for initial business information setup <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>.
2.  **Summary Section**: Displays a summary of all entered transactions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:50">[00:06:50]</a>.
3.  **Transactions Window**: Where all business transaction details are entered <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:35">[00:00:35]</a>.

## Setting Up Accounts

The "Start Here" section features an account setup area, crucial for initial configuration <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>.

### Account Categories
Accounts are categorized under four main heads <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>:
*   Expenses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a> (e.g., purchase expense, utility bills, subscriptions, salaries and wages <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:58">[00:00:58]</a>).
*   Liabilities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a> (e.g., accounts payable, taxes payable, accrued expenses, credit card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:04">[00:01:04]</a>).
*   Income <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>.
*   Assets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>.

### Managing Accounts
*   **Editing**: Hover over an item, click the pencil icon, edit details, and click outside to save <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>.
*   **Deleting**: Click the three-dot menu next to an item and select "delete" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:21">[00:01:21]</a>.
*   **Adding New**: Go to the respective category and click "new page" to add a new account <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:28">[00:01:28]</a>.

### Account Details
For each account, you can add various details <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:40">[00:01:40]</a>:
*   **Opening Balance**: For carried-forward balances, especially for liabilities and assets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:46">[00:01:46]</a>.
*   **Classification**: Classify items as bills, debt, savings, or other relevant categories <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:53">[00:01:53]</a>. Expenses like utility bills can be classified as "bills," and subscriptions as "subscription" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:01">[00:02:01]</a>.
*   **Payment Frequency**: Specify how often a payment occurs (e.g., monthly, half-yearly, quarterly) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:12">[00:02:12]</a>.
*   **Opening Units**: For inventory or assets at the start of the business <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:27">[00:02:27]</a>.
*   **Asset Class**: Choose between "inventory" or "investment" for assets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:42">[00:02:42]</a>.

## Entering Transactions

The transactions window allows you to record all financial activities.

### Adding Transactions
You can directly add income, expenses, assets, or liabilities by clicking the respective buttons <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:04">[00:03:04]</a>. For example, clicking "add income" automatically pre-fills the current date <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:10">[00:03:10]</a>.

### Transaction Details
Each transaction allows for the following details <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:19">[00:04:19]</a>:
*   **Date**: Automatically set to the current date when adding a new transaction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:16">[00:03:16]</a>.
*   **Period**: Define the month and year for the transaction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:24">[00:03:24]</a>.
*   **Description**: A brief explanation of the transaction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:42">[00:03:42]</a>.
*   **Category**: Automatically mapped based on the type of transaction added (e.g., "income" if "add income" was clicked) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:45">[00:03:45]</a>.
*   **Account**: Link the transaction to a predefined account from the "Start Here" section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:50">[00:03:50]</a>. New accounts added here will also reflect in the "Start Here" section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:05">[00:04:05]</a>.
*   **Payment Mode**: How the payment was received or made (e.g., bank account, cash account) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:21">[00:04:21]</a>. More accounts can be added as needed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:28">[00:04:28]</a>.
*   **Transaction Type**: Designate as "inflow" (money coming in) or "outflow" (money going out) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:51">[00:04:51]</a>.
*   **Amount**: The monetary value of the transaction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:07">[00:05:07]</a>.
*   **Document**: Attach any relevant documents <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:15">[00:05:15]</a>.
*   **Tax Rate and Tax Amount**: Enter a tax rate, and the tax amount will be calculated automatically <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:21">[00:05:21]</a>. Set to 0% for non-taxable transactions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:27">[00:05:27]</a>.
*   **Multiplier and Currency**: Useful for multi-currency transactions, allowing conversion to a base currency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:36">[00:05:36]</a>.

### Tracking Investments and Inventory
Within the transactions window, you can also track:
*   **Investments**: Details like units, price, total value, and investment value <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:07">[00:06:07]</a>, including profit or loss upon sale <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:17">[00:06:17]</a>.
*   **Inventory**: Track existing inventory, units, and profit or loss obtained from specific inventory items <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:26">[00:06:26]</a>.

## Summary and Reporting

All transactions entered in the "Transactions Window" automatically update the "Summary Section" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:49">[00:06:49]</a>.

### Finances Overview
The "Finances" section provides an overall glance at the business's financial health <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:54">[00:06:54]</a>. This includes:
*   [[assets_and_liabilities_breakdown | Assets]]: Detailed breakdown of assets like accounts receivable, input taxes, other receivables, and cash value <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:05">[00:07:05]</a>.
*   Income sources <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:01">[00:07:01]</a>.
*   Liabilities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:01">[00:07:01]</a>.
*   Expenses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:01">[00:07:01]</a>.

### Debt Management
The "Debt" section shows all debt payments, distinguishing between past and upcoming payments based on the liability entries <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:23">[00:07:23]</a>.

### [[budgeting_and_tracking_business_expenses | Budgeting]] and Savings
*   **Budgeting**: Displays budgeted expenses versus actual expenses incurred, and similar comparisons for income <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:03">[00:08:03]</a>.
*   **Savings**: Shows how much savings have been made for specific funds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:16">[00:08:16]</a>.

### Bills and Subscriptions
This section is similar to the debt section but specifically for bills and subscriptions, showing past payments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:22">[00:08:22]</a>.

### Tax Section
Provides a quarterly overview of taxes payable, tax credits available, and overall tax liability <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:35">[00:08:35]</a>, all based on income and expense entries <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:44">[00:08:44]</a>.

### Net Worth
The net worth section compares your set net worth goal to your total calculated net worth, showing the difference, total growth, and percentage of the goal achieved <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:51">[00:08:51]</a>. The net worth goal can be set in the "Start Here" section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:00">[00:09:00]</a>.

### Investment and Inventory Summaries
*   **Investments**: A summary of investments, including their value and units held <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:35">[00:09:35]</a>.
*   **Inventory**: Similar status updates for inventory <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:43">[00:09:43]</a>.

### Overall Profit and Loss Summary
At a glance, you can view total income, total expenses, gross profit, taxes paid, and the net profit of the business <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:47">[00:09:47]</a>.
