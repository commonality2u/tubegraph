## What is a Notion Page?

A Notion page is defined as a simple blank page created while [[using_notion_for_bulk_document_creation | using Notion]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>. In contrast, a Notion database is structured in rows and columns <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>.

## Generating AI Content on a Notion Page

To [[generating_content_using_ai_prompts_for_notion_pages_and_databases | generate AI content]] on a Notion page using Wizzygen, follow these steps:

1.  **Name the Connection**
    Begin by providing a name for the connection you are building, such as "notion page" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:32">[00:00:32]</a>.
2.  **Select Content Type**
    Choose "notion page" as the content type for generation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:36">[00:00:36]</a>.
3.  **Provide Notion Page URL**
    Copy the URL of your desired Notion page and paste it into the provided section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.
4.  **Input AI Prompt**
    Enter the prompt for the AI to generate content. For instance, a prompt could be: "write me a block of 100 words on notion" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:48">[00:00:48]</a>.
5.  **Generate Content**
    Once the prompt is entered, the AI will generate the content directly onto the specified Notion page <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:04">[00:01:04]</a>. The generated content will appear on the Notion page <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:08">[00:01:08]</a>.
