This article explores the customization options available for sales receipt templates and how to provide feedback or request further assistance.

## Customizing the Sales Receipt Tracker Template

The sales receipt tracker template provides several fields that can be customized to suit business needs:
*   **Business Information** You can input your business name <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:22">[00:00:22]</a> and address <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:25">[00:00:25]</a>.
*   **Payment Methods** The template offers a dropdown menu for various payment methods <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:19">[00:00:19]</a>.
*   **Product Details** For each sale, you can add multiple descriptions, quantities, and unit prices across different rows <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:45">[00:00:45]</a>.
*   **Formula Updates** If additional columns for descriptions and quantities are added, ensure that the formulas for subtotal, total amount, and quantity sold are updated accordingly for accurate calculations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:41">[00:01:41]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:04">[00:02:04]</a>.
*   **Client Summary** The client summary, which displays total sales, total quantity, and average sales price per client, can be customized to meet specific requirements <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:38">[00:02:38]</a>.

Further customization to the [[setting_up_and_customizing_a_sales_receipts_database | sales receipts database]] template is available by reaching out via email <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:42">[00:02:42]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:47">[00:02:47]</a>.

## Customizing PDF Sales Receipts

When [[generating_sales_receipts_as_pdf_documents | generating sales receipts as PDF documents]] using PDF output.com, several customization options are available:
*   **Template Selection** Users can select from a list of available templates <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:15">[00:03:15]</a>.
*   **Currency Selection** The currency displayed on the PDF can be changed, for example, from US Dollars to Euro, and the change will be automatically reflected <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:40">[00:04:40]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:07">[00:07:07]</a>.
*   **Bulk Export Database Customization** In the bulk export mode, the width of columns and the height of rows in the database can be freely resized to improve readability <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:40">[00:05:40]</a>.
