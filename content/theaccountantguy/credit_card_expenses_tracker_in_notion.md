The Credit Card Expenses Tracker in Notion, created by Sanat Biswal (The Accountant Guy), is designed to help users track credit card payments and manage expenses effectively <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:00">[00:00:00]</a>. This template can be customized to individual needs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:24">[00:07:24]</a>.

## Overview of the Tracker

The tracker provides several key sections for a comprehensive view of credit card activity and expenses:

*   **Credit Cards Overview** This section displays all added credit cards, such as Student Credit Card, Cash Back Credit Card, Travel Credit Card, and Rewards Credit Card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:10">[00:00:10]</a>. For each card, it shows the total amount due, the amount repaid, and the remaining balance due <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:23">[00:00:23]</a>.
*   **Monthly Overview** This customizable section details the amount due, repaid, and balance due for each month <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:32">[00:00:32]</a>.
*   **Yearly Overview** Provides a summary of the total amount due, repaid, and balance due as per different years <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.
*   **Expense Detail Section** Lists all expenses that have been added in detail <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:51">[00:00:51]</a>.
*   **Expenses Overview** Offers a high-level view of all expenses booked into different categories <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:58">[00:00:58]</a>. It displays total expenses, budgeted amounts, comparisons to the budget, and whether the budget is "under budget" or "over budget" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:05">[00:01:05]</a>.
*   **Notification Section** Shows the next repayment due date and the number of days remaining until that date for each added credit card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a>.

## Using the Tracker

To start [[tracking_transactions_and_expenses_in_notion | tracking transactions and expenses in Notion]], begin by booking expenses under the "Expense Details overview section" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>.

### Booking Expenses

The "Expense Details" section includes columns for <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:42">[00:01:42]</a>:
*   Date <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:42">[00:01:42]</a>
*   Expense Details (for which the expense is entered) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:44">[00:01:44]</a>
*   Period (for booking the expense) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:46">[00:01:46]</a>
*   Year <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:49">[00:01:49]</a>
*   Credit Card (related to the expense) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:51">[00:01:51]</a>
*   Category (to assign to each expense) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:54">[00:01:54]</a>
*   Expense Amount <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:57">[00:01:57]</a>
*   Repayment Section (for recording repayments) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:00">[00:02:00]</a>

You can also enter an "opening balance due" for any balances carried forward from previous months <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:07">[00:02:07]</a>.

When entering an expense:
1.  Assign the date <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:17">[00:02:17]</a>.
2.  Enter details (e.g., "opening balance") <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:20">[00:02:20]</a>.
3.  Select the designated period <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:24">[00:02:24]</a>.
4.  Enter the year <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:30">[00:02:30]</a>.
5.  Select an existing credit card or type to create a new one <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:33">[00:02:33]</a>.
6.  Assign a category <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:45">[00:02:45]</a>.
7.  Enter the expense amount <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:50">[00:02:50]</a>.

### Repaying Credit Cards

To record a repayment, simply type "credit card repayment" in the details and enter the repayment amount in the repayment column <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:58">[00:02:58]</a>. All entered details automatically update the overview sections <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:07">[00:03:07]</a>.

### Managing Budgets in Expenses Overview

In the "Expenses Overview" section, you can see how much you've spent for each category versus the budgeted amount <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:42">[00:03:42]</a>.
*   To set a budget amount, click on the "budget amount" field and enter your desired figure <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:58">[00:03:58]</a>.
*   The tracker will show "under budget" for expenses within the set limit and "over budget" if expenses exceed it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:26">[00:04:26]</a>.

### Setting Repayment Notifications

The "Notification section" helps you keep track of upcoming payment dates <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:35">[00:04:35]</a>.
*   Set your own repayment date by clicking on the date field <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:39">[00:04:39]</a>.
*   This will display how many days are pending until the next repayment for each credit card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:49">[00:04:49]</a>, helping you pay debts on time <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:05">[00:05:05]</a>.

## Additional Features

*   **Receipt Column** In the "Expense Details" section, there's a receipt column where you can attach bills or receipts for your expenses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:34">[00:05:34]</a>.
*   **Detailed Views** The tracker provides summarized views of your expenses:
    *   **By Period**: Clicking on a specific period opens a new window showing transaction details, dates, periods, transaction descriptions, amounts, and tags (green for repayment, regular for expense) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:44">[00:05:44]</a>.
    *   **By Year**: Similar detailed information is available for specific years <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:14">[00:06:14]</a>.
    *   **By Credit Card**: Clicking on a credit card shows all transactions related to that card, including transaction details and a status that can be changed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:24">[00:06:24]</a>. You can also assign and edit categories for each credit card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:40">[00:06:40]</a>.
    *   **By Category**: Selecting an expense category reveals all relevant expenses and their details under that category <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:48">[00:06:48]</a>.

## Customization and Support

You can edit existing credit card information using the pencil icon or add new credit cards and categories using the "new" button <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:16">[00:05:16]</a>. Categories can also be added directly by entering their details <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:24">[00:05:24]</a>.
