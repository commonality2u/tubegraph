## Related Sections within the Asset Planner

Beyond regulatory compliance, the asset planner template also features several other key sections:

*   **[[operational_management_of_assets | Operational Management]]**
    *   Asset details, including asset ID, name, and type <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>.
    *   [[asset_maintenance_tracking | Maintenance details]], covering maintenance ID and other related information <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>.
    *   [[financial_management_of_assets | Financial details]], showing current value, depreciation method, and rate <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:32">[00:00:32]</a>.
    *   Performance tracking, which shows asset performance as it is being created <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[00:00:37]</a>.
*   **Documentation**
    *   A section to add all relevant documents required for the asset <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:48">[00:00:48]</a>.
*   **Reviews**
    *   A section for users to provide reviews about the assets being used <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:53">[00:00:53]</a>.

The entire asset planner template is [[customization_of_asset_planning_template | customizable]] to meet specific user requirements <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:59">[00:00:59]</a>.
