This guide demonstrates how to [[creating_templates_for_recurring_payment_reminders | set up bill payment reminders]] directly on your WhatsApp, ensuring you receive timely notifications for recurring expenses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:05">[00:00:05]</a>.

## Example of a Bill Reminder
A reminder typically displays the bill name, due date, and payment amount. For instance, a Wi-Fi bill might show a due date of September 20th with a payment amount of $3,000 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:10">[00:00:10]</a>.

## Creating a New Bill Payment Reminder

To create a new reminder:
1.  Click on the dropdown menu <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:18">[00:00:18]</a>.
2.  Select "new template" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>.
3.  Enter the name of the bill, such as "Mobile bill" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:23">[00:00:23]</a>.
4.  Specify the bill amount, for example, $1,500 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:28">[00:00:28]</a>.
5.  Click "back" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:33">[00:00:33]</a>.

## Setting Recurring Payments

To [[creating_recurring_payment_reminders | set the bill to repeat on a recurring basis]]:
1.  Click the dropdown menu again <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:35">[00:00:35]</a>.
2.  Click on the three dots <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[00:00:37]</a>.
3.  Choose the option to "repeat on the recurring basis" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:39">[00:00:39]</a>.
4.  Select the frequency, such as "every month" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.
5.  Specify the exact time you wish to receive the notification (e.g., 11:55) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:44">[00:00:44]</a>.
6.  Set the specific day of the month for the notification (e.g., every 20th of every month) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>.
7.  Click "Save" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>.

## Automation for WhatsApp Notifications

This process requires automation, specifically with the help of Pabbly Connect <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:04">[00:01:04]</a>. Once the automation is set up in Pabbly Connect, you will automatically receive notifications on your WhatsApp every month for the recurring bills you've templated <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:17">[00:01:17]</a>.

## Receiving Notifications

After setting up the template and automation, new bill items will automatically appear, and you will receive a notification on WhatsApp <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:26">[00:01:26]</a>. The system is designed to send the notification within approximately one minute of the set time <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:54">[00:01:54]</a>.
