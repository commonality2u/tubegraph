## What is Transnistria?

[[transnistria_and_its_geopolitical_significance | Transnistria]] is a frozen conflict located on a thin slice of territory that was appended to the Moldavian Soviet Socialist Republic by the Soviet Union <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:58">[00:09:58]</a>. It unilaterally declared independence in 1991 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:03">[00:10:03]</a>.

Key characteristics:
*   **Population**: It has approximately half a million inhabitants <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:10">[00:10:10]</a>.
*   **Demographics**: About a third of its population is Russian <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:12">[00:10:12]</a>.
*   [[russian_influence_and_military_presence_in_transnistria | **Russian Support**]]: [[russian_influence_and_military_presence_in_transnistria | Transnistria]] is still supported by Moscow and is propped up by around 1500 Russian troops <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:14">[00:10:14]</a>.
