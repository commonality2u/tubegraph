*   **Data Analysis and Machine Learning**
    [[python_applications_and_frameworks | Python]] is the language of choice for [[python_applications_and_frameworks | Big Data analysis]] and [[aws_machine_learning_tools | machine learning]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:31">[00:00:31]</a>.
