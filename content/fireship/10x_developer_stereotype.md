The [[programmer_ego_and_competitiveness | 10x developer]] is a stereotype considered to be "actually real" within the programming community <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:38">[00:03:38]</a>. This individual is described as an "extremely rare unicorn" capable of performing the work of ten other developers combined <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:40">[00:03:40]</a>.

## Characteristics

*   **Exceptional Productivity**
    The core characteristic is the ability to accomplish the workload of ten developers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:40">[00:03:40]</a>. While some consider them a myth, others claim to have witnessed developers who code with the prowess of [[kevin_durant_brooklyn_nets | Durant]] playing basketball or Kasparov playing chess <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:44">[00:03:44]</a>.
*   **Natural Problem-Solving Ability**
    [[Programmer ego and competitiveness | 10x developers]] possess a natural problem-solving ability that significantly surpasses that of most others <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:52">[00:03:52]</a>.
*   **Impact on Others**
    When encountering a [[programmer_ego_and_competitiveness | 10x developer]], others often feel both incompetent and jealous <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:57">[00:03:57]</a>.
