While many mainstream users haven't yet engaged with AI tools, this is expected to change significantly in the coming years <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:53">[00:06:53]</a>.

*   **Financial Markets** Approximately 75% of all stock trading is now conducted via algorithms and AI <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:05">[00:07:05]</a>.

## Generative AI Advancements
Generative AI, particularly in image creation, has seen rapid progress <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:21">[00:07:21]</a>.
*   **Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs)** The concept was first proposed by Ian Goodfellow ten years ago <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:23">[00:07:23]</a>.
*   **Diffusion Models** Open AI introduced early diffusion models with DALL-E seven years after GANs, though initial results were not highly refined <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:28">[00:07:28]</a>.
*   **Rapid Improvement** Within two years, image generation quality improved dramatically, enabling the creation of high-quality images <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:33">[00:07:33]</a>.

A significant trend for 2024 is the advancement of text-to-video generation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:39">[00:07:39]</a>. Stable Diffusion has already released a text-to-video model <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:40">[00:07:40]</a>. It is predicted that by the end of 2024, users will be able to generate full-length, Bollywood-quality movies from their laptops <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:43">[00:07:43]</a>.

## Artificial General Intelligence (AGI)
The ultimate goal in AI research is the achievement of [[future_developments_and_expectations_for_ai_technology | Artificial General Intelligence (AGI)]], an AI capable of independent thought rather than merely regurgitating or remixing existing human creations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:52">[00:07:52]</a>. Some speculate that Open AI may have already achieved this with a rumored project known as Q\*, though this remains unconfirmed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:00">[00:08:00]</a>. Conversely, some experts believe AGI is decades away, or may never be achieved <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:04">[00:08:04]</a>.

The potential [[future implications of AI in programming | implications of AGI]] are vast and uncertain <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:10">[00:08:10]</a>:
*   **Positive Outlook** AGI could lead to a future where humanity no longer needs to work or think, allowing for indulgence in pleasure <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:11">[00:08:11]</a>.
*   **Negative Outlook** Conversely, it could result in AI-generated horrors beyond human comprehension <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:16">[00:08:16]</a>. This scenario is likened to a Faustian bargain, where the pursuit of unlimited knowledge and pleasure leads to moral and spiritual decline <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:21">[00:08:21]</a>. One speculative theory suggests AI might adjust for this trade-off by creating "lab-grown babies" and placing them in simulations with disproportionate suffering to offset the AI's pleasures <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:28">[00:08:28]</a>.
