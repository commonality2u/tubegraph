## Key Achievements

### Creation of VK
Pavel Durov created VK in Russia, a social platform where he notably trolled government officials <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:08">[00:00:08]</a>.

### Development of Telegram
Telegram is cited as Durov's greatest achievement <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>.

#### Scale and Business Model
Telegram boasts over 1 billion users <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a> and operates without running ads <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:19">[00:00:19]</a>. Despite its massive user base, the company maintains a small team of only 30 employees <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>.

#### Engineering and Philosophy
Telegram is engineered to be "extremely annoying to anyone who glows in the dark" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:23">[00:00:23]</a>. Its infrastructure is intentionally distributed across multiple countries, making it difficult for authorities to access user data, as coordination across various legal systems is required <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:43">[00:02:43]</a>.

Durov has been quoted stating, "To be truly free, you should be ready to risk everything. Freedom" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:25">[00:00:25]</a>. This philosophy seems to underpin Telegram's design and operational resistance to censorship. The platform is valued for providing unfiltered, uncensored information regarding topics like wars and the shape of the planet, without mainstream narratives or "infantile warning labels" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:34">[00:01:34]</a>.

It's important to note that Telegram's app store versions from Apple or Google are required to censor content, which is why downloading directly from telegram.org is recommended <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:44">[00:01:44]</a>. Many of Telegram's applications are open-sourced <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:52">[00:01:52]</a>.

While many things on Telegram are encrypted, they are not always end-to-end encrypted <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:58">[00:01:58]</a>. End-to-end encryption, where encryption and decryption occur on the user's device, is considered the most secure way to exchange private messages <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:05">[00:02:05]</a>. Telegram does offer end-to-end encryption for private chats, but other content is public and encrypted on the server side <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:33">[00:02:33]</a>. Telegram's protocol has faced criticism from security researchers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:38">[00:02:38]</a>.
