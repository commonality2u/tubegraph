## Hollywood vs. Reality

The common perception of a hacker, largely manufactured by Hollywood, involves opening a terminal, connecting to a remote mainframe, and breaking all its security protocols one by one with "awesome fancy animations" between each step <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:19:00">[03:19:00]</a>.

However, real hacking is described as "extremely tedious and boring" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:33:00">[03:33:00]</a>. It is primarily carried out by individuals or groups who possess significant resources and power <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:34:00">[03:34:00]</a>.
