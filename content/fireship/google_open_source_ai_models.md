In February 2024, Google introduced Gemma, a family of [[open_source_ai_models | open source models]] designed to compete with other models like Meta Llama 7B and Mistral <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:34">[00:01:34]</a>.

## Key Features and Performance

Based on Google's internal benchmarks, Gemma models are said to dominate the competition, especially concerning math and coding tasks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:41">[00:01:41]</a>. These models are free to use and can be utilized to generate revenue within your own applications <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:48">[00:01:48]</a>.

## Usage Limitations

Despite their open-source nature, Gemma models are subject to a prohibited use policy that users must follow <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:51">[00:01:51]</a>.
