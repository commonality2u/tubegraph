When applying for jobs in the tech industry, a company's decision to hire extends beyond a candidate's technical prowess. ## Understanding Cultural Fit
A company wants to ensure that a candidate will embrace their core values and fit into their management and leadership style <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:31">[00:10:31]</a>. This means that technical skill, while helpful, is not the sole determinant for being hired <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:26">[00:10:26]</a>.

### Preparation for Interviews
Before an interview, it is crucial to research and understand the company's core values <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:38">[00:10:38]</a>. Reviewing their social media profiles can provide insights into their messaging and help you determine if your values align with theirs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:41">[00:10:41]</a>. Aligning with a company's values is important to avoid disliking your job in the long term <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:44">[00:10:44]</a>. The goal is to mold yourself into the candidate they are seeking <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:48">[00:10:48]</a>.

## Handling Rejection
Job applications often result in silence or rejection after an initial phone interview <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:54">[00:09:54]</a>. It's common to feel sad, worthless, or angry, but rejection should be seen as part of life <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:58">[00:09:58]</a>.

### Learning from Rejection
Instead of dwelling on negative feelings, view rejection as an opportunity for personal growth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:11">[00:10:11]</a>. It is beneficial to ask for feedback or guidance to improve for future opportunities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:13">[00:10:13]</a>.

### The Importance of Persistence
Successful individuals are characterized by their persistence, failing repeatedly without giving up until they achieve their goals <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:17">[00:10:17]</a>.
