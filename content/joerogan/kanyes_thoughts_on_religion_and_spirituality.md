Kanye West, a globally influential artist and public figure, has shared his perspectives on religion and spirituality, often intertwining them with his personal and professional endeavors. His journey has involved moments of transformation and expression through different mediums including music, design, and public engagement.

## A Personal Calling

Kanye attributes his call to spirituality and religious life to a profound personal experience, describing it as a moment where God "knocked him off his horse" and invited him to serve a higher purpose <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="38:44">[38:44]</a>. He identifies as a "servant of God" and often speaks of his actions as being part of a larger divine plan. This spiritual calling has deeply influenced his creative output and life choices <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="53:53">[53:53]</a>.

## Religion and Public Life

Kanye's [presidential run](kanye_wests_presidential_run) was another expression of his religious convictions, where he emphasized the importance of being led by God in all aspects of life, including politics. He sees himself in a leadership role with the need to serve not just as an artist but in public life, envisioning a world where love and understanding can resolve conflicts both domestically and internationally <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="52:23">[52:23]</a>.

## Sunday Service and Music

Beginning in 2018, Kanye initiated the Sunday Service, which combined music and spirituality in a unique worship experience. This was part of his vision to make "going to church cool again," creating a sense of community and shared values <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="36:39">[36:39]</a>. His album "Jesus Is King" and other lyrics focus on themes of redemption, transformation, and praise, reflecting his commitment to using his talents in service of faith <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="26:02">[26:02]</a>.

## Spiritual and Personal Growth

Kanye emphasizes the intersection of [[spirituality and personal growth | spirituality and personal transformation]], articulating that a genuine relationship with God leads to personal growth and societal improvement <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="40:43">[40:43]</a>. His stance suggests that spiritual beliefs should inform and inspire actions in the community, pointing to how spiritual conviction can lead to creative and ethical solutions in various domains <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="37:27">[37:27]</a>.

## Critique and Message of Modern Christianity

Kanye has also publicly critiqued aspects of Christianity that he perceives as hypocritical or misaligned with the true teachings of Christ<b> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="42:46">[42:46]</a></b>. He advocates for a return to what he sees as fundamental Christian values, fostering a supportive and inclusive environment that highlights love, service, and the divine potential within every individual.

In summary, Kanye West's views on religion and spirituality not only influence his music and personal life but are also woven into his vision of leadership and societal transformation. He sees his journey as being divinely guided, aspiring to inspire others through his actions and artistry to reconsider the roles of faith and spirituality in modern life.
