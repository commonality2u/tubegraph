## The Rationality of Genocidal Programs

Weinstein argues that actions like those taken by Hitler can be seen as having a rationale through the lens of evolutionary biology. The dark latency within human nature for genocide or ethnic cleansing could be tied to evolutionary survival strategies, where homogenizing a population could theoretically reduce internal threats and competition <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:26:02">[02:26:02]</a>.
