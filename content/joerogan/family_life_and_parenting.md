## Parenting and COVID-19

The pandemic has brought unique challenges to family life and parenting. During the COVID-19 pandemic, concerns over health and safety have intensified as parents balance protecting their families with maintaining a semblance of normal life. The impact of quarantine and social distancing measures means families have had to adjust their routines and interactions significantly. This period has prompted many to reflect on personal and family dynamics and consider their approaches to parenting and family engagement in new ways <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:08">[00:02:08]</a>.

The pandemic also highlighted the value of family time, as shared activities like movie nights became a regular fixture during lockdowns. The simple pleasure of spending uninterrupted time together allowed families to bond over shared experiences, such as watching classic films or engaging in family activities, which helped maintain unity and morale in challenging times <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:27:28">[00:27:28]</a>.
