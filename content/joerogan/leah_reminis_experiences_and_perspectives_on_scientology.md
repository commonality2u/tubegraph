## Early Life and Introduction to Scientology

Leah Remini's journey into Scientology began at a young age of nine, following her mother's introduction to the church by Remini's stepfather. Her initial experiences were a stark contrast to her upbringing in a Catholic-Sicilian household where her father encouraged strict discipline and silence. Scientology presented a new world where children were seen as "Spiritual Beings" rather than mere kids, and this shift in environment made Remini feel empowered and important [<a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:11">[00:01:11]</a>]. 

## Life As a Scientologist

Embracing Scientology meant adhering to rigorous daily routines. Her contributions went beyond self-development; she believed she was playing a role in saving mankind, a belief instilled deeply by the church's teachings.

## Disconnection and Indoctrination

The disconnection policy, a tactic utilized by the church to separate members from perceived enemies, was a powerful tool that dictated Social relations within the church [<a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:56:08">[01:56:08]</a>].
