## Development of Patents

Howard claims to have developed a series of 97 patents. These patents cover various inventions, including an advanced propulsion system and a new form of flight he calls "tangential flight," which involves the ability for an object to rotate around its center of mass <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:03:01">[02:03:01]</a>.

### Key Innovations

1. **Tangential Flight**: This allows for an aircraft to move around its center of mass, a system Howard claims no other current technology can achieve <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:43:52">[01:43:52]</a>.
2. **Periodic Table Reimagining**: Inspired by the work of Walter Russell, Howard aims to redesign the periodic table to display the relationships between elements as expansions of a single substance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:00">[00:09:00]</a>.
3. **Lighting and Wave Conjugations**: His sculptures, such as transcendental lighting, demonstrate wave formations of energy fields in visual form <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:13:59">[02:13:59]</a>.
