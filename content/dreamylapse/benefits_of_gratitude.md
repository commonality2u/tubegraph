[[gratitude_meditation | Gratitude meditation]] focuses on [[cultivating_gratitude | cultivating a sense of gratitude]] and appreciation for the present moment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:10">[00:00:10]</a>. This practice encourages individuals to identify and feel gratitude for anything, regardless of its size or tangibility <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>.

## Immediate Effects of Gratitude

When engaging in gratitude practices, individuals are encouraged to fully feel the gratitude and appreciation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a> and to notice the physical sensations that accompany these feelings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:12">[00:01:12]</a>. An [[expressing_appreciation | expression of gratitude]], whether a silent thank you or a prayer, can lead to feelings of warmth and joy <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:48">[00:01:48]</a>. The aim is to embrace this feeling and allow it to permeate one's entire being <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>.

## Long-term Transformation

Gratitude is described as a powerful practice capable of transforming one's life <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:21">[00:02:21]</a>. By [[cultivating_gratitude | cultivating a sense of gratitude]], individuals can attract more abundance, joy, and positivity into their lives <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:26">[00:02:26]</a>.

> [!NOTE]
> Carrying a feeling of gratitude and appreciation throughout the day is encouraged to maintain these benefits <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:08">[00:02:08]</a>.
