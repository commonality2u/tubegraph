## Gratitude Meditation Techniques

To begin a gratitude meditation, find a comfortable position and close your eyes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:22">[00:00:22]</a>.

1.  **Breathwork**
    *   Take a deep breath in through your nose, filling your lungs with air <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>.
    *   Slowly exhale through your mouth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:38">[00:00:38]</a>.
    *   Allow your breath to become steady and calm <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>.

2.  **Identify Sources of Gratitude**
    *   As you continue to breathe, bring to mind something you are grateful for <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>.
    *   This can be something small or big, tangible or intangible <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:59">[00:00:59]</a>.
    *   Allow yourself to fully feel the gratitude and appreciation for it, noticing the sensations in your body <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>.

3.  **Express Gratitude**
    *   Take a moment to [[importance_of_expressing_gratitude | express your gratitude]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:22">[00:01:22]</a>.
    *   You can say a silent thank you or offer a prayer of gratitude <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:25">[00:01:25]</a>.
    *   Allow yourself to fully embrace this feeling and let it fill your entire being <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>.

4.  **Deepen the Practice**
    *   As you continue to breathe, think of more things you are grateful for <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:40">[00:01:40]</a>.
    *   Allow yourself to feel the warmth and joy that comes with each expression of gratitude <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:48">[00:01:48]</a>.

5.  **Conclusion**
    *   Take one final deep breath in, and as you exhale, slowly open your eyes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:58">[00:01:58]</a>.
    *   Carry this feeling of gratitude and appreciation with you throughout your day <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:08">[00:02:08]</a>.
