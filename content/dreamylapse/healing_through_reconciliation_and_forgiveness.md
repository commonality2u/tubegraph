## Core Principles

The practice centers around four simple phrases:
*   "I'm sorry" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:15">[00:00:15]</a>
*   "Please forgive me" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>
*   "Thank you" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>
*   "I love you" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:22">[00:00:22]</a>

These phrases are utilized to clear negative energy <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>, facilitate [[restoring_relationships_with_hooponopono | healing in relationships]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:29">[00:00:29]</a>, and foster [[achieving_inner_peace_and_happiness | inner peace]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:32">[00:00:32]</a>.

## How to Practice Ho'oponopono

To engage in Ho'oponopono, follow these steps:
1.  **Find a Quiet Space**
    Locate a quiet and comfortable place where you will not be disturbed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.
2.  **Relax and Breathe**
    Close your eyes and take a few deep breaths, allowing yourself to relax <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>.
3.  **Identify a Source of Negativity**
    Bring to mind a person or situation that is causing you stress, anxiety, or negativity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>. This could be:
    *   Someone you are in conflict with <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>
    *   A memory that brings up negative emotions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:02">[00:01:02]</a>
    *   A current situation causing you stress <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:05">[00:01:05]</a>
4.  **Repeat the Phrases**
    Repeat the four phrases ("I'm sorry," "please forgive me," "thank you," and "I love you") either silently or out loud <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>.
