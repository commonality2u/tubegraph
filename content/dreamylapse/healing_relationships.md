## Ho'oponopono: A Hawaiian Practice

Ho'oponopono is a Hawaiian practice focused on [[reconciliation_and_forgiveness | reconciliation and forgiveness]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:07">[00:00:07]</a>. It has recently gained popularity as a [[selfhelp_techniques | self-help technique]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:12">[00:00:12]</a>.

### The Four Phrases
The practice of Ho'oponopono involves four simple phrases <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:15">[00:00:15]</a>:
*   "I'm sorry" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>
*   "Please forgive me" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>
*   "Thank you" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>
*   "I [[practicing_self_love_and_self_esteem | love]] you" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:22">[00:00:22]</a>

These phrases are used to clear negative energy, heal relationships, and promote inner peace <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:32">[00:00:32]</a>.

## How to Practice Ho'oponopono

To use Ho'oponopono, follow these steps <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>:

1.  **Find a quiet place:** Locate a quiet and comfortable place where you will not be disturbed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:44">[00:00:44]</a>.
2.  **Relax:** Close your eyes and take a few deep breaths to allow yourself to relax <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:48">[00:00:48]</a>.
3.  **Identify the focus:** Bring to mind a person or situation that is causing you stress, anxiety, or negativity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:55">[00:00:55]</a>. This could be:
    *   Someone you are in conflict with <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:02">[00:01:02]</a>
    *   A memory that brings up negative emotions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:05">[00:01:05]</a>
    *   A current situation causing stress <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:08">[00:01:08]</a>
4.  **Repeat the phrases:** Repeat the four phrases ("I'm sorry," "Please forgive me," "Thank you," "I love you") either silently or out loud <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:13">[00:01:13]</a>.

## Benefits of Ho'oponopono

Ho'oponopono can serve as a powerful tool for healing and transformation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:30">[00:01:30]</a>, both for individuals and in relationships <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:32">[00:01:32]</a>. By taking responsibility for one's thoughts and actions, and by expressing love and gratitude, individuals can release negative energy and create a more positive and peaceful life <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:45">[00:01:45]</a>.
