Ho'oponopono is a Hawaiian practice focused on reconciliation and forgiveness, which has gained recognition as a self-help technique in recent years <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:04">[00:00:04]</a>. ## Core Phrases

The practice of Ho'oponopono revolves around four simple phrases <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:15">[00:00:15]</a>:
*   I'm sorry <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>
*   Please forgive me <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>
*   Thank you <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>
*   I love you <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:22">[00:00:22]</a>

These phrases are utilized to clear negative energy, heal relationships, and foster [[inner_peace_and_vitality_through_meditation | inner peace]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>.

## How to Practice Ho'oponopono

To engage in Ho'oponopono, follow these steps <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:36">[00:00:36]</a>:
1.  **Find a Quiet Space**: Locate a quiet and comfortable place where you will not be disturbed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.
2.  **Relax**: Close your eyes and take a few deep breaths, allowing yourself to relax <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>.
3.  **Identify the Issue**: Bring to mind a person or situation that is causing you [[Stress and anxiety relief through meditation | stress, anxiety]], or negativity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>. This could be someone with whom you are in conflict, a memory evoking negative emotions, or a current stressful situation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>.
4.  **Repeat the Phrases**: Repeat the four phrases ("I'm sorry," "Please forgive me," "Thank you," "I love you") either silently or out loud <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>.

## Benefits

Ho'oponopono is considered a powerful tool for both individual and relational healing and transformation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:27">[00:01:27]</a>. By taking responsibility for one's thoughts and actions, and by expressing love and gratitude, individuals can release negative energy <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:36">[00:01:36]</a>.
