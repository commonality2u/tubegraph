## How to Practice Gratitude

To begin, find a comfortable position <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:22">[00:00:22]</a> and close your eyes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>. Take a deep breath in through your nose, filling your lungs with air <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>, and slowly exhale through your mouth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:38">[00:00:38]</a>. Allow your breath to become steady and calm <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>.

As you continue to breathe, bring to mind something that you are grateful for <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>. This can be something small or big, tangible or intangible <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:59">[00:00:59]</a>. Whatever it is, allow yourself to fully feel the gratitude and appreciation for it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:04">[00:01:04]</a>. Notice the sensations in your body as you feel grateful <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:12">[00:01:12]</a>.

Next, take a moment to express your gratitude <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:22">[00:01:22]</a>. You can say a silent thank you or offer a prayer of gratitude <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:28">[00:01:28]</a>. Allow yourself to fully embrace this feeling of gratitude and let it fill your entire being <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>.

As you continue to breathe, think of more things that you are grateful for <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:40">[00:01:40]</a>. Allow yourself to feel the warmth and joy that comes with each expression of gratitude <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:48">[00:01:48]</a>.

To conclude the practice, take one final deep breath in and as you exhale, slowly open your eyes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:59">[00:01:59]</a>.
