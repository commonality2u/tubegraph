## Gratitude Meditation Practice

To engage in this [[gratitude_meditation_techniques | gratitude meditation]], follow these steps:

### Preparation
1.  Find a comfortable position and close your eyes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>.
2.  Take a deep breath in through your nose, filling your lungs with air <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[00:00:37]</a>.
3.  Slowly exhale through your mouth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:38">[00:00:38]</a>.
4.  Allow your breath to become steady and calm <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:45">[00:00:45]</a>.

### Identifying and Feeling Gratitude
1.  As you continue to breathe, bring to mind something you are grateful for <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:53">[00:00:53]</a>.
2.  This can be something small or big, tangible or intangible <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:01">[00:01:01]</a>.
3.  Allow yourself to fully feel the gratitude and appreciation for it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>.
4.  Notice the sensations in your body as you feel grateful <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:15">[00:01:15]</a>.

### Expressing Gratitude
1.  Take a moment to express your gratitude <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:25">[00:01:25]</a>.
2.  You can say a silent "thank you" or offer a prayer of gratitude <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:28">[00:01:28]</a>.
3.  Allow yourself to fully embrace this feeling of gratitude and let it fill your entire being <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>.

### Expanding Gratitude
1.  As you continue to breathe, think of more things for which you are grateful <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:48">[00:01:48]</a>.
2.  Allow yourself to feel the warmth and joy that comes with each expression of gratitude <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:55">[00:01:55]</a>.

### Concluding the Practice
1.  Take one final deep breath in <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:59">[00:01:59]</a>.
2.  As you exhale, slowly open your eyes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:00">[00:02:00]</a>.
3.  Carry this feeling of gratitude and appreciation with you throughout your day <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:13">[00:02:13]</a>.
