[[hooponopono_as_a_selfhelp_technique | Ho'oponopono]] is a Hawaiian practice focused on [[healing_through_reconciliation_and_forgiveness | reconciliation and forgiveness]] that has gained recognition as a [[hooponopono_as_a_selfhelp_technique | self-help technique]] in recent years <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:04">[00:00:04]</a>.

## Core Phrases

The practice is centered around four simple phrases <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:15">[00:00:15]</a>:
*   "I'm sorry" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>
*   "Please forgive me" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:18">[00:00:18]</a>
*   "Thank you" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:20">[00:00:20]</a>
*   "I love you" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>

These phrases are used to clear negative energy, heal relationships, and promote [[achieving_inner_peace_and_happiness | inner peace]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>.

## How to Practice

To apply [[practicing_hooponopono_for_stress_relief | Ho'oponopono]] for healing, follow these steps:

1.  **Find a quiet space:** Locate a comfortable place where you will not be disturbed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.
2.  **Relax:** Close your eyes and take a few deep breaths, allowing yourself to relax <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>.
3.  **Identify the issue:** Bring to mind a person or situation that is causing you [[practicing_hooponopono_for_stress_relief | stress]], anxiety, or negativity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>. This could be someone you are in conflict with, a negative memory, or a current stressful situation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>.
4.  **Repeat the phrases:** Silently or out loud, repeat the four phrases: "I'm sorry, please forgive me, thank you, I love you" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>. This is a form of [[incorporating_affirmations_into_daily_practice | affirmation practice]].

## Benefits of Ho'oponopono

[[hooponopono_as_a_selfhelp_technique | Ho'oponopono]] can be a powerful tool for [[spiritual_recovery | healing]] and transformation, both individually and in relationships <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:27">[00:01:27]</a>. By taking responsibility for one's thoughts and actions, and by expressing love and gratitude, individuals can release negative energy and create a more positive and [[achieving_inner_peace_and_happiness | peaceful life]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:36">[00:01:36]</a>. This practice fosters [[inner_calm_and_resilience | inner calm and resilience]].
