## The Four Phrases
The core of the practice involves four simple phrases:
*   "I'm sorry" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:15">[00:00:15]</a>
*   "Please forgive me" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>
*   "Thank you" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:20">[00:00:20]</a>
*   "I love you" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>

These phrases are used to clear negative energy, heal relationships, and promote inner peace <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>.

## How to Use [[hooponopono_practice | Ho'oponopono]]
To engage with the [[hooponopono_practice | Ho'oponopono]] practice, follow these steps:
1.  **Find a quiet space** Locate a comfortable place where you will not be disturbed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.
2.  **Relax** Close your eyes and take a few deep breaths to relax yourself <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>.
3.  **Identify the focus** Bring to mind a person or situation that is causing you stress, anxiety, or negativity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>. This could be someone you are in conflict with, a memory that evokes negative emotions, or a current stressful situation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>.
4.  **Repeat the phrases** Silently or aloud, repeat the four phrases: "I'm sorry," "Please forgive me," "Thank you," and "I love you" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>.

## Benefits of the Practice
[[hooponopono_practice | Ho'oponopono]] serves as a powerful tool for healing and transformation, benefiting both individuals and their relationships <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:27">[00:01:27]</a>. By embracing responsibility for our thoughts and actions, and by expressing love and gratitude, we can release negative energy <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:36">[00:01:36]</a>. This process contributes to creating a more [[rewiring_thought_patterns_for_positivity | positive]] and peaceful life <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:43">[00:01:43]</a>.
