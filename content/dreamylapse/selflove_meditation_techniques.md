## Preparation

To begin this [[meditation | meditation]], find a comfortable seated position in a quiet space <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:09">[00:00:09]</a>. Close your eyes and take a deep breath in through your nose, holding it for a few seconds before releasing it through your mouth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:29">[00:00:29]</a>. Repeat this breathing exercise a few times until your mind and body begin to relax <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:45">[00:00:45]</a>.

## Heart Center Visualization

Bring your attention to your heart center <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:59">[00:00:59]</a>. [[Visualization_techniques_for_relaxation | Visualize]] a warm and loving light radiating from your heart, spreading throughout your entire body <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>. As you inhale, imagine this light growing brighter and more intense, filling you with love and warmth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:27">[00:01:27]</a>. As you exhale, release any negative thoughts or feelings you may be holding on to <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:43">[00:01:43]</a>.
