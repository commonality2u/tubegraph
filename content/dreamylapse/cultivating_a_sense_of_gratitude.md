## Gratitude Meditation Practice

To begin this [[gratitude_meditation_practice | gratitude meditation]] session, it is recommended to find a comfortable position and close your eyes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:22">[00:00:22]</a>.

> [!meditation] Steps
> 1.  **Breathing**
>     *   Take a deep breath in through your nose, filling your lungs with air <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>.
>     *   Slowly exhale through your mouth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:38">[00:00:38]</a>.
>     *   Allow your breath to become steady and calm <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>.
> 2.  **Identify Sources of Gratitude**
>     *   As you continue to breathe, bring to mind something you are grateful for <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>.
>     *   This can be something small or big, tangible or intangible <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:59">[00:00:59]</a>.
>     *   Allow yourself to fully feel the gratitude and appreciation for it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:06">[00:01:06]</a>.
>     *   Notice the sensations in your body as you [[feeling_gratitude_in_the_body | feel grateful]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:12">[00:01:12]</a>.
> 3.  **Express Gratitude**
>     *   Take a moment to express your gratitude <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:20">[00:01:20]</a>.
>     *   This can be done by saying a silent "thank you" or offering a prayer of gratitude <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:28">[00:01:28]</a>.
>     *   Allow yourself to fully embrace this feeling of gratitude and let it fill your entire being <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>.
> 4.  **Deepen Gratitude**
>     *   As you continue to breathe, think of more things you are grateful for <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:40">[00:01:40]</a>.
>     *   Allow yourself to feel the warmth and joy that comes with each expression of gratitude <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:48">[00:01:48]</a>.
> 5.  **Conclusion**
>     *   Take one final deep breath in, and as you exhale, slowly open your eyes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:57">[00:01:57]</a>.
>     *   Carry this feeling of gratitude and appreciation with you throughout your day <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:08">[00:02:08]</a>.
