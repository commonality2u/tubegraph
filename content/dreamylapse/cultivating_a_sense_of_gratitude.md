This [[positive_mindset_cultivation_techniques | practice]] is designed to help you connect with positive feelings and draw more abundance, joy, and positivity into your life <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:26">[00:02:26]</a>.

## Beginning the Practice

To begin this practice, find a comfortable position <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:22">[00:00:22]</a>. Close your eyes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a> and take a deep breath in through your nose, filling your lungs with air <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>. Slowly exhale through your mouth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:38">[00:00:38]</a>. Allow your breath to become steady and [[inner_calm_and_resilience | calm]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>.

## Steps for Cultivating Gratitude

As you continue to breathe:

1.  **Identify Sources of Gratitude**
    *   Bring to mind something for which you are grateful <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>. This can be something small or big, tangible or intangible <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:59">[00:00:59]</a>.
    *   Allow yourself to fully feel the gratitude and appreciation for it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:04">[00:01:04]</a>.
    *   Notice the sensations in your body as you feel grateful <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:12">[00:01:12]</a>.

2.  **Express Gratitude**
    *   Take a moment to [[expressing_gratitude_and_appreciation | express your gratitude]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:22">[00:01:22]</a>. You can say a silent "thank you" or offer a prayer of gratitude <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:25">[00:01:25]</a>.
    *   Allow yourself to fully embrace this feeling of gratitude and let it fill your entire being <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>.

3.  **Expand Your Gratitude**
    *   As you continue to breathe, think of more things for which you are grateful <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:40">[00:01:40]</a>.
    *   Allow yourself to feel the warmth and joy that comes with each [[expressing_gratitude_and_appreciation | expression of gratitude]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:48">[00:01:48]</a>.

## Concluding the Practice

Take one final deep breath in, and as you exhale, slowly open your eyes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:58">[00:01:58]</a>. Take this feeling of gratitude and appreciation with you throughout your day <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:08">[00:02:08]</a>.
