## Core Phrases

The practice revolves around four simple phrases <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:15">[00:00:15]</a>:
*   "I'm sorry" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>
*   "Please forgive me" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>
*   "Thank you" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>
*   "I love you" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>

These phrases are utilized to clear negative energy, heal relationships, and [[practices_for_stress_relief_and_inner_peace | promote inner peace]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>.

## How to Practice Ho'oponopono

To engage in Ho'oponopono, follow these steps <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:36">[00:00:36]</a>:

1.  **Find a Quiet Space**: Locate a quiet and comfortable environment where you will not be disturbed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.
2.  **Relax**: Close your eyes and take a few deep breaths to allow yourself to relax <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>.
3.  **Identify the Source of Negativity**: Bring to mind a person or situation that is causing you stress, anxiety, or negativity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>. This could be someone you are in conflict with, a negative memory, or a current stressful situation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>.
4.  **Repeat the Phrases**: Repeat the four phrases — "I'm sorry," "Please forgive me," "Thank you," and "I love you" — either silently or out loud <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>.
