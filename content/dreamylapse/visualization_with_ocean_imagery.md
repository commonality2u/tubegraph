## How it Works

The process begins with conscious breathing and progressive imagery:

1.  **Preparation**: Close your eyes and take a deep breath in and out <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:05">[00:00:05]</a>.
2.  **Ocean Setting**:
    > Imagine yourself standing at the edge of the sea. Waves roll in slow and steady <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>.
    Connect the rhythm of the waves to your own breath <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>.
3.  **Inner Lighthouse**:
    > In the distance, a lighthouse glows <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:40">[00:00:40]</a>.
    This lighthouse is not external; it resides inside you <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>. It represents your innate light, which has always been present <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>.
4.  **Fueling the Light**: Each breath taken fuels this inner light <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:05">[00:01:05]</a>.
    *   Inhale: The light grows warmer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>.
    *   Exhale: The internal "storm" begins to fade <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a>.
