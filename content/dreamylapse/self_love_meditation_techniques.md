## Preparing for Meditation
To begin this [[guided_meditation_techniques | meditation]], find a comfortable seated position in a quiet space <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:06">[00:00:06]</a>. Close your eyes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:18">[00:00:18]</a>.

## Breathing and Relaxation
Take a deep breath in through your nose <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>. Hold it for a few seconds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>, then release it through your mouth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:29">[00:00:29]</a>. Repeat this [[Mindful breathing techniques | mindful breathing]] process several times until your mind and body begin to relax <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>.

## Heart Center Visualization
Bring your attention to your heart center <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:59">[00:00:59]</a>. Visualize a warm and loving light radiating from your heart <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:02">[00:01:02]</a>, spreading throughout your entire body <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:05">[00:01:05]</a>. As you inhale, imagine this light growing brighter and more intense, filling you with love and warmth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:20">[00:01:20]</a>. As you exhale, release any negative thoughts or feelings you may be holding on to <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>.

Repeat the following affirmations silently or out loud <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:52">[00:01:52]</a>:

*   "I love and accept myself exactly as I am" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:06">[00:02:06]</a>.
*   "I am deserving of love and respect" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:23">[00:02:23]</a>.
*   "I am worthy of happiness and success" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:30">[00:02:30]</a>.
*   "I am confident and capable" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:41">[00:02:41]</a>.
*   "I trust myself and my abilities" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:50">[00:02:50]</a>.

As you repeat these [[Selfesteem through meditation affirmations | affirmations]], visualize them becoming a part of your being <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:04">[00:03:04]</a>. See yourself radiating with [[Cultivating selflove and selfacceptance | self-love]] and confidence <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:16">[00:03:16]</a>.

## Releasing Negativity
Focus on any negative thoughts or self-doubt that may be holding you back <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:28">[00:03:28]</a>. Acknowledge them and then release them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:44">[00:03:44]</a>.
