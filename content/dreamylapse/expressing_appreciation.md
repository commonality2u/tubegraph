This practice focuses on [[cultivating_gratitude | cultivating a sense of gratitude]] and appreciation for the present moment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:10">[00:00:10]</a>. It is a form of [[gratitude_meditation | gratitude meditation]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:00">[00:00:00]</a>.

## How to Practice

1.  **Preparation**
    *   Begin by finding a comfortable sitting position <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:22">[00:00:22]</a>.
    *   Close your eyes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>.
    *   Take a deep breath in through your nose, filling your lungs with air, and slowly exhale through your mouth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>.
    *   Allow your breath to become steady and calm <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>.

2.  **Identify and Feel Gratitude**
    *   As you continue to breathe, bring to mind something you are grateful for <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>. This can be something small or big, tangible or intangible <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:59">[00:00:59]</a>.
    *   Allow yourself to fully feel the gratitude and appreciation for it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:04">[00:01:04]</a>.
    *   Notice the sensations in your body as you feel grateful <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:12">[00:01:12]</a>.

3.  **Express Gratitude**
    *   Take a moment to express your gratitude <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:22">[00:01:22]</a>.
    *   You can say a silent thank you or offer a prayer of gratitude <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:25">[00:01:25]</a>.
    *   Allow yourself to fully embrace this feeling of gratitude and let it fill your entire being <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>.

4.  **Expand and Carry Forward**
    *   As you continue to breathe, think of more things you are grateful for <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:40">[00:01:40]</a>.
    *   Allow yourself to [[visualizing_love_and_warmth | feel the warmth]] and joy that comes with each expression of gratitude <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:48">[00:01:48]</a>.
    *   Take one final deep breath in, and as you exhale, slowly open your eyes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:58">[00:01:58]</a>.
    *   Take this feeling of gratitude and appreciation with you throughout your day <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:08">[00:02:08]</a>.

## [[benefits_of_gratitude | Benefits of Gratitude]]

Gratitude is a powerful practice that can transform your life <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:21">[00:02:21]</a>. By cultivating a sense of gratitude, individuals can attract more abundance, joy, and positivity into their lives <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:26">[00:02:26]</a>.
