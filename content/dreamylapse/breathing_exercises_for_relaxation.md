## Introduction to Relaxation

To begin a relaxation practice, find a comfortable seated position in a quiet space <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:06">[00:00:06]</a>. Close your eyes and take a deep breath, focusing on the breath itself.

## Basic Breathing Technique

A fundamental [[breathing_exercises_in_meditation | breathing exercise]] for relaxation involves:
*   Inhaling deeply through your nose <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:18">[00:00:18]</a>.
*   Holding the breath for a few seconds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>.
*   Releasing the breath slowly through your mouth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:29">[00:00:29]</a>.

Repeat this sequence a few times until you feel your mind and body begin to relax <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>.
