## How to Practice Gratitude

To engage in a [[gratitude_meditation_practice|gratitude meditation practice]], follow these steps:

1.  **Preparation** Find a comfortable position and close your eyes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:22">[00:00:22]</a>.
2.  **Breathing** Take a deep breath in through your nose, filling your lungs, and slowly exhale through your mouth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>. Allow your breath to become steady and calm <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>.
3.  **Identify Gratitude** As you continue to breathe, bring to mind something for which you are grateful <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>. This can be something small or large, tangible or intangible <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>.
4.  **Feel the Gratitude** Allow yourself to fully feel the gratitude and appreciation for it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:06">[00:01:06]</a>. Notice the sensations in your body as you feel grateful <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:12">[00:01:12]</a>.
5.  **Express Gratitude** Take a moment to express your gratitude, either by saying a silent thank you or offering a prayer of gratitude <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:21">[00:01:21]</a>. Fully embrace this feeling and let it fill your entire being <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>.
6.  **Expand Gratitude** Continue to breathe and think of more things for which you are grateful <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:40">[00:01:40]</a>. Allow yourself to feel the warmth and joy that comes with each expression of gratitude <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:48">[00:01:48]</a>.
7.  **Conclusion** Take one final deep breath, and as you exhale, slowly open your eyes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:58">[00:01:58]</a>. Carry this feeling of gratitude and [[expressing_gratitude_and_appreciation|appreciation]] with you throughout your day <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:08">[00:02:08]</a>.
