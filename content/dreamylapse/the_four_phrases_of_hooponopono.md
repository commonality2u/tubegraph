[[introduction_to_hooponopono | Hooponopono]] is a Hawaiian practice focused on reconciliation and forgiveness <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:04">[00:00:04]</a>. It has recently gained popularity as a self-help technique <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:10">[00:00:10]</a>.

## Core Phrases

The practice revolves around four simple phrases <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:15">[00:00:15]</a>:
*   "I'm sorry" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>
*   "Please forgive me" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:19">[00:00:19]</a>
*   "Thank you" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:20">[00:00:20]</a>
*   "I love you" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>

These phrases are used to clear negative energy, [[benefits_of_hooponopono_in_stress_relief_and_relationship_healing | heal relationships]], and promote [[using_hooponopono_for_inner_peace | inner peace]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>.

## How to Use the Phrases

To [[steps_to_practice_hooponopono | practice Hooponopono]] and utilize its phrases, follow these [[steps_to_practice_hooponopono | steps]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:36">[00:00:36]</a>:
1.  **Find a Quiet Place**
    Locate a quiet and comfortable place where you will not be disturbed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.
2.  **Relax**
    Close your eyes and take several deep breaths, allowing yourself to relax <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>.
3.  **Identify a Situation**
    Bring to mind a person or situation that is causing you [[benefits_of_hooponopono_in_stress_relief_and_relationship_healing | stress]], anxiety, or negativity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>. This could be a conflict, a negative memory, or a current stressful situation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>.
4.  **Repeat the Phrases**
    Repeat the four phrases—"I'm sorry," "Please forgive me," "Thank you," "I love you"—either silently or out loud <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>.

## Benefits of the Practice

[[using_hooponopono_for_inner_peace | Hooponopono]] is considered a powerful tool for [[benefits_of_hooponopono_in_stress_relief_and_relationship_healing | healing]] and transformation, both for individuals and in relationships <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:27">[00:01:27]</a>. By taking responsibility for one's thoughts and actions, and by expressing love and gratitude, individuals can release negative energy and foster a more positive and peaceful life <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:36">[00:01:36]</a>.
