## Ho'oponopono

Ho'oponopono is a Hawaiian practice of reconciliation and forgiveness that has recently gained popularity as a self-help technique <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:04">[00:00:04]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:07">[00:00:07]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:10">[00:00:10]</a>.

### Core Phrases

The practice of Ho'oponopono involves four simple phrases <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:15">[00:00:15]</a>:
*   "I'm sorry" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>
*   "Please forgive me" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:18">[00:00:18]</a>
*   "Thank you" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:20">[00:00:20]</a>
*   "I love you" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:22">[00:00:22]</a>

These phrases are utilized to clear negative energy, [[healing_relationships_and_promoting_positive_energy | heal relationships]], and promote [[stress_relief_and_inner_peace | inner peace]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:29">[00:00:29]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:32">[00:00:32]</a>.

### How to Practice Ho'oponopono

To effectively use Ho'oponopono, follow these steps <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:36">[00:00:36]</a>:
1.  **Find a Quiet Space** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>: Locate a quiet and comfortable place where you will not be disturbed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:44">[00:00:44]</a>.
2.  **[[relaxation_techniques | Relax]]** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:48">[00:00:48]</a>: Close your eyes and take a few deep breaths, allowing yourself to [[relaxation_techniques | relax]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:48">[00:00:48]</a>.
3.  **Identify the Source of Negativity** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>: Bring to mind a person or situation that is causing you stress, anxiety, or negativity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:55">[00:00:55]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>. This could be:
    *   Someone with whom you are in conflict <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:02">[00:01:02]</a>
    *   A memory that triggers negative emotions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:04">[00:01:04]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:05">[00:01:05]</a>
    *   A current stressful situation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:08">[00:01:08]</a>
4.  **Repeat the Phrases** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>: Repeat the four phrases ("I'm sorry," "Please forgive me," "Thank you," "I love you") either silently or out loud <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:13">[00:01:13]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:15">[00:01:15]</a>.
