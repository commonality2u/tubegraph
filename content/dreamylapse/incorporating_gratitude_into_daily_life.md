This practice can be an effective way to integrate thankfulness into your daily routine.

## Guided Practice: Cultivating Gratitude

To begin, find a comfortable position and close your eyes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:22">[00:00:22]</a>.

### Breathing Foundation
Take a deep breath in through your nose, filling your lungs with air, and then slowly exhale through your mouth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>. Allow your breath to become steady and calm <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>. This step utilizes [[mindful_breathing_exercises | mindful breathing exercises]] to prepare the mind and body.

### Identifying Sources of Gratitude
As you continue to breathe, bring to mind something you are grateful for <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>. This can be something small or big, tangible or intangible <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:59">[00:00:59]</a>. Whatever it is, allow yourself to fully feel the gratitude and appreciation for it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:04">[00:01:04]</a>. Notice the sensations in your body as you feel grateful <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:12">[00:01:12]</a>.

### Expressing and Embracing Gratitude
Take a moment to express your gratitude <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:22">[00:01:22]</a>. This can be done by saying a silent thank you or offering a prayer of gratitude <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:25">[00:01:25]</a>. Allow yourself to fully embrace this feeling of gratitude and let it fill your entire being <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>.

As you continue to breathe, think of more things for which you are grateful <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:40">[00:01:40]</a>. Allow yourself to feel the warmth and joy that comes with each expression of gratitude <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:48">[00:01:48]</a>.

### Concluding the Practice
Take one final deep breath in, and as you exhale, slowly open your eyes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:56">[00:01:56]</a>.

## Carrying Gratitude Throughout the Day
The aim is to take this feeling of gratitude and appreciation with you throughout your day <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:08">[00:02:08]</a>.

Gratitude is a powerful practice that can [[transformative_power_of_gratitude | transform your life]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:18">[00:02:18]</a>. When a sense of gratitude is cultivated, it helps attract more abundance, joy, and positivity into one's life <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:26">[00:02:26]</a>.
