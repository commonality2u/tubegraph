This practice centers on [[gratitude_meditation_benefits | cultivating a sense of gratitude]] and appreciation for the present moment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>.

## Steps for Practice

The process begins by finding a comfortable position and closing your eyes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:22">[00:00:22]</a>.
1.  **Breathing** Take a deep breath in through your nose, filling your lungs with air, and slowly exhaling through your mouth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>. Allow your breath to become steady and calm <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>.
2.  **Identify Gratitude** As you continue to breathe, bring to mind something you are grateful for <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>. This can be something small or large, tangible or intangible <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:59">[00:00:59]</a>. Allow yourself to fully feel the gratitude and appreciation for it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>, noticing the sensations in your body <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:12">[00:01:12]</a>.
3.  **Express Gratitude** Take a moment to express your gratitude by saying a silent thank you or offering a prayer of gratitude <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:22">[00:01:22]</a>. Allow yourself to [[expressing_and_embracing_gratitude | fully embrace this feeling]] of gratitude and let it fill your entire being <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>.
4.  **Expand Gratitude** As you continue to breathe, think of more things you are grateful for <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:40">[00:01:40]</a>. Allow yourself to feel the warmth and joy that comes with each expression of gratitude <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:48">[00:01:48]</a>.
5.  **Conclude** Take one final deep breath in, and as you exhale, slowly open your eyes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:58">[00:01:58]</a>. Carry this feeling of gratitude and appreciation with you throughout your day <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:08">[00:02:08]</a>.

## Benefits of Gratitude

Gratitude is a powerful practice that can transform your life <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:18">[00:02:18]</a>. When we [[impact_of_gratitude_on_daily_life | cultivate a sense of gratitude]], we attract more abundance, joy, and positivity into our lives <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:26">[00:02:26]</a>.
