## Preparation

To begin, find a comfortable seated position in a quiet space <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:06">[00:00:06]</a>. Close your eyes and take deep breaths, inhaling through your nose and exhaling through your mouth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:18">[00:00:18]</a>. Repeat this breathing a few times until your mind and body begin to relax <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>.

## The Visualization Process

1.  **Focus on the Heart Center**
    Bring your attention to your heart center <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>.
2.  **Radiating Light**
    Visualize a warm and loving light radiating from your heart, spreading throughout your entire body <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:02">[00:01:02]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:05">[00:01:05]</a>.
3.  **Breathing in Love and Warmth**
    As you inhale, imagine this light growing brighter and more intense, filling you with love and warmth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:20">[00:01:20]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:24">[00:01:24]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:27">[00:01:27]</a>.
4.  **Releasing Negativity**
    As you exhale, consciously release any negative thoughts or feelings you may be holding onto <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:40">[00:01:40]</a>.
