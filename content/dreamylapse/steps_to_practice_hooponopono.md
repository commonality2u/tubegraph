## The Four Core Phrases of Ho'oponopono

The practice centers around repeating these [[the_four_phrases_of_hooponopono | four phrases]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:15">[00:00:15]</a>:
*   I'm sorry <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>
*   Please forgive me <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:19">[00:00:19]</a>
*   Thank you <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>
*   I love you <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>

## How to Practice Ho'oponopono

To effectively use Ho'oponopono, follow these steps <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:36">[00:00:36]</a>:
1.  **Find a Quiet Space**: Locate a quiet and comfortable place where you will not be disturbed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.
2.  **Relax**: Close your eyes and take a few deep breaths, allowing yourself to relax <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>.
3.  **Identify the Issue**: Bring to mind a person or situation that is causing you stress, anxiety, or negativity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>. This could be someone with whom you are in conflict, a memory that brings up negative emotions, or a current stressful situation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>.
4.  **Repeat the Phrases**: Repeat the [[the_four_phrases_of_hooponopono | four phrases]] ("I'm sorry," "Please forgive me," "Thank you," "I love you") either silently or out loud <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>.

## Benefits of Practicing Ho'oponopono

Ho'oponopono serves as a [[benefits_of_hooponopono_in_stress_relief_and_relationship_healing | powerful tool for healing and transformation]], benefiting individuals and relationships alike <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:29">[00:01:29]</a>. By taking responsibility for one's thoughts and actions, and by expressing love and [[importance_of_practicing_selflove_and_gratitude | gratitude]], practitioners can release negative energy and foster a more positive and peaceful life <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:36">[00:01:36]</a>.
