This practice combines [[Visualization and Breathing Exercises | visualization]] with positive affirmations to promote self-love and confidence. It is designed to help relax the mind and body, release negative thoughts, and foster a positive self-image.

## Preparation and Breathing
To begin, find a comfortable seated position in a quiet space <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:06">[00:00:06]</a>. Close your eyes and take a deep breath in through your nose, holding it for a few seconds, then releasing it through your mouth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:18">[00:00:18]</a>. Repeat this breathing exercise until your mind and body feel relaxed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>.

## Heart Center Visualization
Bring your attention to your heart center and [[Visualization and Breathing Exercises | visualize]] a warm and loving light radiating from it, spreading throughout your entire body <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>. As you inhale, imagine this light growing brighter and more intense, filling you with love and warmth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:20">[00:01:20]</a>. As you exhale, [[Releasing negative thoughts and selfdoubt | release any negative thoughts or feelings]] you may be holding onto <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>.

## Positive Affirmations
Once relaxed, repeat the following [[Incorporating affirmations into daily practice | affirmations]] silently or out loud <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:52">[00:01:52]</a>:

*   "I love and accept myself exactly as I am" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:06">[00:02:06]</a>.
*   "I am deserving of love and respect" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:23">[00:02:23]</a>.
*   "I am worthy of happiness and success" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:30">[00:02:30]</a>.
*   "I am confident and capable" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:41">[00:02:41]</a>.
*   "I trust myself and my abilities" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:50">[00:02:50]</a>.

As you repeat these [[Incorporating affirmations into daily practice | affirmations]], [[Visualization and Breathing Exercises | visualize]] them becoming a part of your being <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:01">[00:03:01]</a>, seeing yourself radiating with self-love and confidence <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:16">[00:03:16]</a>. This process contributes to [[Creating a positive and empowering selfimage | creating a positive and empowering self-image]] and [[how_affirmations_can_shift_mindset_and_beliefs | shifting your mindset and beliefs]].

## Releasing Self-Doubt
Next, focus on any negative thoughts or [[Releasing negative thoughts and selfdoubt | self-doubt]] that may be holding you back, then release them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:28">[00:03:28]</a>. See them dissipating like clouds in the sky, leaving behind a clear and peaceful mind <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:55">[00:03:55]</a>.

## Conclusion
Conclude the practice by taking a deep breath in and exhaling slowly <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:11">[00:04:11]</a>. When ready, open your eyes and take a moment to feel gratitude for yourself and your progress <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:24">[00:04:24]</a>. Carry this sense of self-love and [[Affirmations for self esteem and confidence | self-esteem]] with you throughout your day <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:30">[00:04:30]</a>. Remember, self-love is a practice that requires patience and dedication <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:48">[00:04:48]</a>, and with consistent practice, you will come to love and accept yourself fully <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:05">[00:05:05]</a>.
