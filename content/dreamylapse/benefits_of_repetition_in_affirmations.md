It is recommended to make it a daily practice, repeating affirmations preferably multiple times a day <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:25">[00:00:25]</a>. This consistent practice helps to:

*   Reinforce positive beliefs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:33">[00:00:33]</a>
*   Integrate these beliefs into the subconscious mind <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:36">[00:00:36]</a>
