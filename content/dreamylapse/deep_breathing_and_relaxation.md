[[breathing_techniques_for_relaxation | Deep breathing]] and [[Mindful breathing exercises | mindful breathing exercises]] are foundational elements of relaxation practices, often combined with [[Visualization and Breathing Exercises | visualization]] and affirmations to promote self-love and mental clarity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>.

## Getting Started

To begin, find a comfortable seated position in a quiet space <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:06">[00:00:06]</a>. Close your eyes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:18">[00:00:18]</a>.

## The Breathing Process

The core breathing exercise involves:
1.  Taking a deep breath in through your nose <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:18">[00:00:18]</a>.
2.  Holding it for a few seconds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>.
3.  Releasing it through your mouth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:29">[00:00:29]</a>.

Repeat this process several times until your mind and body begin to relax <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>. Finally, take a deep breath in and exhale slowly <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:11">[00:04:11]</a>.

## Visualization for Relaxation

Integrate visualization into your practice to enhance relaxation and self-connection:
*   Bring your attention to your heart center <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>.
*   Visualize a warm and loving light radiating from your heart, spreading throughout your entire body <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:02">[00:01:02]</a>.
*   As you inhale, imagine this light growing brighter and more intense, filling you with love and warmth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:20">[00:01:20]</a>.
*   As you exhale, consciously release any negative thoughts or feelings you may be holding onto <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>.

## Affirmations for Self-Love and Confidence

Incorporate affirmations to strengthen your self-perception. Repeat the following silently or out loud <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:52">[00:01:52]</a>:
*   "I love and accept myself exactly as I am" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:06">[00:02:06]</a>.
*   "Deserving of love and respect" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:23">[00:02:23]</a>.
*   "I am worthy of happiness and success" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:30">[00:02:30]</a>.
*   "I am confident and capable" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:41">[00:02:41]</a>.
*   "I trust myself and my abilities" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:50">[00:02:50]</a>.

As you repeat these affirmations, visualize them becoming a part of your being <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:01">[00:03:01]</a> and see yourself radiating with self-love and confidence <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:16">[00:03:16]</a>.

## Releasing Negative Thoughts

Next, focus on any negative thoughts or self-doubt that may be holding you back <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:28">[00:03:28]</a>. Acknowledge them, and then actively release them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:44">[00:03:44]</a>. See these thoughts dissipating like clouds in the sky, leaving behind a clear and peaceful mind <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:55">[00:03:55]</a>.
