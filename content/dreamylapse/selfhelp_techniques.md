Ho'oponopono is a Hawaiian practice of reconciliation and forgiveness that has gained popularity as a self-help technique in recent years <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:04">[00:00:04]</a>.

## Core Phrases
The practice involves four simple phrases <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:15">[00:00:15]</a>:
*   "I'm sorry" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>
*   "Please forgive me" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>
*   "Thank you" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>
*   "I love you" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>

## Purpose and Benefits
These phrases are used to clear negative energy, heal relationships, and promote inner peace <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>. Ho'oponopono can be a powerful tool for healing and transformation, both individually and in relationships <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:27">[00:01:27]</a>. By taking responsibility for one's thoughts and actions, and by expressing love and gratitude, individuals can release negative energy and create a more positive and peaceful life <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:36">[00:01:36]</a>.

## How to Practice Ho'oponopono
To use Ho'oponopono, follow these steps <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:36">[00:00:36]</a>:
1.  **Find a Quiet Place** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>: Locate a quiet and comfortable place where you will not be disturbed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.
2.  **Relax** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>: Close your eyes and take a few deep breaths, allowing yourself to relax <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>.
3.  **Identify the Source** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>: Bring to mind a person or situation that is causing you stress, anxiety, or negativity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>. This could be someone you are in conflict with, a memory that brings up negative emotions, or a current stressful situation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>.
4.  **Repeat the Phrases** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>: Repeat the four phrases ("I'm sorry," "Please forgive me," "Thank you," "I love you") either silently or out loud <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>.
