Ho'oponopono is a Hawaiian practice focused on [[reconciliation_and_forgiveness_techniques | reconciliation and forgiveness]] that has recently gained popularity as a self-help technique <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:04">[00:00:04]</a>.

## Core Phrases

The practice involves four simple phrases <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:15">[00:00:15]</a>:
*   I'm sorry <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:17">[00:00:17]</a>
*   Please forgive me <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:20">[00:00:20]</a>
*   Thank you <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:20">[00:00:20]</a>
*   I love you <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>

## Purpose and Benefits

These phrases are utilized to clear negative energy, [[healing_relationships_and_promoting_positive_energy | heal relationships]], and foster [[stress_relief_and_inner_peace | inner peace]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>. Ho'oponopono is considered a powerful tool for healing and transformation, both on an individual level and within relationships <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:29">[00:01:29]</a>. By taking responsibility for one's thoughts and actions and expressing love and gratitude, practitioners can release negative energy and cultivate a more positive and peaceful life <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:36">[00:01:36]</a>.

## How to Practice Ho'oponopono

To engage in the Ho'oponopono practice, follow these steps <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:36">[00:00:36]</a>:
1.  **Find a Quiet Space**: Locate a quiet and comfortable place where you will not be disturbed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.
2.  **Relax**: Close your eyes and take a few deep breaths, allowing yourself to relax <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>.
3.  **Identify the Issue**: Bring to mind a person or situation that is causing you stress, anxiety, or negativity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>. This could be:
    *   Someone you are in conflict with <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>
    *   A memory that evokes negative emotions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:03">[00:01:03]</a>
    *   A current stressful situation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:05">[00:01:05]</a>
4.  **Repeat the Phrases**: Repeat the four core phrases—"I'm sorry," "please forgive me," "thank you," and "I love you"—either silently or out loud <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>.
