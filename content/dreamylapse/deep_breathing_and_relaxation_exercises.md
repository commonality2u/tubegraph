Hold it for a few seconds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>, and then release it through your mouth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:29">[00:00:29]</a>. Visualize a warm and loving light radiating from your heart, spreading throughout your entire body <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:04">[00:01:04]</a>.
