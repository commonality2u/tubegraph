## The Power of Affirmations

Affirmations are positive statements designed to help shift your mindset, beliefs, and thoughts towards a more positive and empowering direction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:05">[00:00:05]</a>. They serve as tools to rewire your thinking patterns and to [[Building a Positive Self Image | cultivate a positive self-image]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:18">[00:00:18]</a>.

## Practicing Affirmations

Regular repetition is crucial for the effectiveness of affirmations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:24">[00:00:24]</a>. It is recommended to make it a daily practice, repeating your affirmations preferably multiple times a day <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>.
