## Cultivating Calm Through Breath

To begin, one should find a comfortable position and close their eyes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:23">[00:00:23]</a>. The core of this practice involves specific [[deep_breathing_and_relaxation | breathing techniques for relaxation]]:
*   **Inhalation**: Take a deep breath in through the nose, filling the lungs with air <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>.
*   **Exhalation**: Slowly exhale through the mouth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[00:00:37]</a>.

The goal is to allow the breath to become steady and calm <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>. This steady breathing helps to focus the mind and prepare for deeper meditative states, such as feeling gratitude <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>.

> [!tip] Practice
> Continue to breathe calmly and steadily throughout the practice <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:40">[00:01:40]</a>. Conclude the session by taking one final deep breath in and slowly opening the eyes upon exhale <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:58">[00:01:58]</a>.

Integrating mindful breathing into practices like [[gratitude_meditation_techniques | gratitude meditation]] enhances the experience by fostering deeper connection and focus <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>. It contributes to a sense of [[emotional_stability_and_mindfulness | emotional stability]] and allows individuals to fully embrace positive feelings like gratitude <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:34">[00:01:34]</a>.
