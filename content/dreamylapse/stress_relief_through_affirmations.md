Ho'oponopono is a Hawaiian practice of reconciliation and forgiveness that has gained popularity as a self-help technique for stress relief in recent years <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:04">[00:00:04]</a>. It is used to clear negative energy, heal relationships, and promote [[Inner peace techniques | inner peace]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>.

## The Four Phrases

The practice of Ho'oponopono involves four simple phrases that function as affirmations:
*   I'm sorry <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:15">[00:01:15]</a>
*   Please forgive me <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:18">[00:01:18]</a>
*   Thank you <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:20">[00:01:20]</a>
*   I love you <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:22">[00:01:22]</a>

## How to Practice Ho'oponopono for Stress Relief

To use Ho'oponopono for stress relief, follow these steps <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:36">[00:00:36]</a>:

1.  **Find a quiet place**: Locate a quiet and comfortable place where you will not be disturbed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.
2.  **Relax**: Close your eyes and take a few deep breaths, allowing yourself to relax <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>.
3.  **Identify the source of stress**: Bring to mind a person or situation that is causing you stress, anxiety, or negativity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>. This could be:
    *   Someone you are in conflict with <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>
    *   A memory that brings up negative emotions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:02">[00:01:02]</a>
    *   A current stressful situation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:05">[00:01:05]</a>
4.  **Repeat the phrases**: Repeat the four phrases ("I'm sorry," "Please forgive me," "Thank you," "I love you") either silently or out loud <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>.

## Benefits of Ho'oponopono

Ho'oponopono can be a powerful tool for healing and transformation, both individually and in relationships <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:30">[00:01:30]</a>. By taking responsibility for one's thoughts and actions and expressing love and gratitude, individuals can release negative energy and create a more positive and peaceful life <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:36">[00:01:36]</a>.
