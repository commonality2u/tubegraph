[[meditation | Gratitude meditation]] is a practice focused on [[cultivating_gratitude | cultivating a sense of gratitude]] and appreciation for the present moment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:10">[00:00:10]</a>.

## Practice Steps

This [[meditation techniques | practice]] involves several steps:
1.  **Preparation** Find a comfortable position and close your eyes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:22">[00:00:22]</a>.
2.  **Breathing** Take a deep breath in through your nose, filling your lungs with air, then slowly exhale through your mouth. Allow your breath to become steady and calm <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>.
3.  **Identify Gratitude** As you continue to breathe, bring to mind something for which you are grateful <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>. This can be something small or big, tangible or intangible <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:59">[00:00:59]</a>. Fully feel the gratitude and appreciation for it, noticing sensations in your body <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:06">[00:01:06]</a>.
4.  **Express Gratitude** Take a moment to [[expressing_appreciation | express your gratitude]] by saying a silent "thank you" or offering a prayer of gratitude <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:21">[00:01:21]</a>. Fully embrace this feeling and let it fill your entire being <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>.
5.  **Expand Gratitude** Continue to breathe and think of more things for which you are grateful <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:40">[00:01:40]</a>. Allow yourself to feel the warmth and joy that comes with each expression of gratitude <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:48">[00:01:48]</a>.
6.  **Conclude** Take one final deep breath in, and as you exhale, slowly open your eyes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:57">[00:01:57]</a>. Take this feeling of gratitude and appreciation with you throughout your day <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:08">[00:02:08]</a>.
