This meditation practice focuses on [[cultivating_gratitude_and_appreciation | cultivating a sense of gratitude and appreciation]] for the present moment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:10">[00:00:10]</a>.

## Practice Steps

1.  **Preparation**
    *   Find a comfortable position <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:22">[00:00:22]</a>.
    *   Close your eyes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>.
    *   Take a deep breath in through your nose, filling your lungs with air <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>.
    *   Slowly exhale through your mouth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:38">[00:00:38]</a>.
    *   Allow your breath to become steady and calm <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>.

2.  **Cultivating Gratitude**
    *   As you continue to breathe, bring to mind something you are grateful for <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:47">[00:00:47]</a>.
    *   This can be something small or big, tangible or intangible <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>.
    *   Allow yourself to fully feel the gratitude and appreciation for it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:04">[00:01:04]</a>.
    *   Notice the sensations in your body as you feel grateful <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:12">[00:01:12]</a>.

3.  **[[expressing_gratitude_and_its_benefits | Expressing Gratitude]]**
    *   Take a moment to [[expressing_gratitude_and_its_benefits | express your gratitude]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:19">[00:01:19]</a>.
    *   You can say a silent "thank you" or offer a prayer of gratitude <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:25">[00:01:25]</a>.
    *   Allow yourself to fully embrace this feeling and let it fill your entire being <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>.
    *   Continue to breathe and think of more things you are grateful for <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:40">[00:01:40]</a>.
    *   Allow yourself to feel the warmth and joy that comes with each expression of gratitude <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:48">[00:01:48]</a>.

4.  **Conclusion**
    *   Take one final deep breath in, and as you exhale, slowly open your eyes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:56">[00:01:56]</a>.
    *   Take this feeling of gratitude and appreciation with you throughout your day <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:08">[00:02:08]</a>.

Gratitude is a powerful practice that can [[transformative_power_of_gratitude | transform your life]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:18">[00:02:18]</a>.
