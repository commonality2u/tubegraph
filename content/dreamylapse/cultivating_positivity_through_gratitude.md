## Meditation Practice Steps

To begin, find a comfortable position and close your eyes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:22">[00:00:22]</a>.

1.  **Breathing**
    *   Take a deep breath in through your nose, filling your lungs with air <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>.
    *   Slowly exhale through your mouth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:38">[00:00:38]</a>.
    *   Allow your breath to become steady and calm <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>.

2.  **Focusing on Gratitude**
    *   As you continue to breathe, bring to mind something for which you are grateful <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>. This can be something small or large, tangible or intangible <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:59">[00:00:59]</a>.
    *   Allow yourself to fully feel the gratitude and appreciation for it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:04">[00:01:04]</a>.
    *   Notice the sensations in your body as you feel grateful <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:12">[00:01:12]</a>.

3.  **Expressing Gratitude**
    *   Take a moment to [[expressing_gratitude_and_appreciation | express your gratitude]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:19">[00:01:19]</a>. You can say a silent "thank you" or offer a prayer of gratitude <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:25">[00:01:25]</a>.
    *   Fully embrace this feeling of gratitude and let it fill your entire being <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>.

4.  **Expanding Gratitude**
    *   As you continue to breathe, think of more things for which you are grateful <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:40">[00:01:40]</a>.
    *   Allow yourself to feel the warmth and joy that comes with each expression of gratitude <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:48">[00:01:48]</a>.

5.  **Concluding the Practice**
    *   Take one final deep breath in, and as you exhale, slowly open your eyes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:58">[00:01:58]</a>.
    *   Carry this feeling of gratitude and appreciation with you throughout your day <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:08">[00:02:08]</a>.
