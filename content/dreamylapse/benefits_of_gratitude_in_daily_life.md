## Practicing Gratitude Meditation

To begin a [[gratitude_meditation_practice | gratitude meditation practice]]:
1.  Find a comfortable seat <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:22">[00:00:22]</a>.
2.  Close your eyes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>.
3.  Take a deep breath in through your nose, filling your lungs with air <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>.
4.  Slowly exhale through your mouth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:38">[00:00:38]</a>.
5.  Allow your breath to become steady and calm <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>.

### Identifying Gratitude
As you continue to breathe, bring to mind something you are grateful for <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>. This can be something small or big, tangible or intangible <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:59">[00:00:59]</a>. Allow yourself to fully feel the gratitude and appreciation for it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>.

### Feeling Gratitude in the Body
Notice the sensations in your body as you feel grateful <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:12">[00:01:12]</a>. Allow yourself to fully embrace this feeling of gratitude and let it fill your entire being <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>.

### Expressing Gratitude
Take a moment to [[expressing_gratitude_through_breathing | express your gratitude]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:22">[00:01:22]</a>. You can say a silent thank you or offer a prayer of gratitude <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:25">[00:01:25]</a>. As you continue to breathe, think of more things that you are grateful for <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:40">[00:01:40]</a>. Allow yourself to feel the warmth and joy that comes with each expression of gratitude <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:48">[00:01:48]</a>.

## Carrying Gratitude Through Your Day
Take one final deep breath in, and as you exhale, slowly open your eyes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:57">[00:01:57]</a>. Carry this feeling of gratitude and appreciation with you throughout your day <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:08">[00:02:08]</a>.
