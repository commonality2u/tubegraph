This practice focuses on [[Cultivating Self Love and Acceptance | cultivating self-love and acceptance]] and [[Building a Positive Self Image | building a positive self-image]] to enhance [[Selfesteem and energy channels | self-esteem]] and confidence.

## Setting the Scene

To begin, find a comfortable seated position in a quiet space <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:06">[00:00:06]</a>. Close your eyes and take a deep breath in through your nose, holding it for a few seconds before releasing it through your mouth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:18">[00:00:18]</a>. Repeat this breathing exercise a few times until your mind and body begin to relax <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>.

## Core Practice: Visualization and Affirmations

Bring your attention to your heart center <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:59">[00:00:59]</a>. [[Visualization techniques to cultivate selflove | Visualize a warm and loving light]] radiating from your heart, spreading throughout your entire body <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:02">[00:01:02]</a>. As you inhale, imagine this light growing brighter and more intense, filling you with love and warmth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:20">[00:01:20]</a>. With each exhale, release any negative thoughts or feelings you may be holding onto <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>.

Next, repeat the following [[Power of Positive Affirmations | affirmations]] to yourself silently or out loud <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:52">[00:01:52]</a>:

> [!quote] Affirmations for Self-Love and Confidence
> I love and accept myself exactly as I am <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:06">[00:02:06]</a>.
> I am deserving of love and respect <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:19">[00:02:19]</a>.
> I am worthy of happiness and success <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:30">[00:02:30]</a>.
> I am confident and capable <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:41">[00:02:41]</a>.
> I trust myself and my abilities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:50">[00:02:50]</a>.

As you repeat these [[Affirmations for confidence and balance | affirmations]], visualize them becoming a part of your being <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:04">[00:03:04]</a>. See yourself radiating with self-love and confidence <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:16">[00:03:16]</a>.

## Releasing Negative Thoughts

After the affirmations, focus on any [[Releasing negative thoughts and selfdoubt | negative thoughts or self-doubt]] that may be holding you back <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:28">[00:03:28]</a>. Acknowledge these thoughts and then consciously release them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:44">[00:03:44]</a>. Visualize them dissipating like clouds in the sky, leaving behind a clear and peaceful mind <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:55">[00:03:55]</a>. This step is crucial for [[Overcoming Challenges with Positive Thinking | overcoming challenges with positive thinking]].

## Conclusion and Continued Practice

Finally, take a deep breath in and exhale slowly <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:11">[00:04:11]</a>. When you are ready, open your eyes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:24">[00:04:24]</a>. Take a moment to feel gratitude for yourself and your progress <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:28">[00:04:28]</a>, and carry this sense of self-love and [[Selfesteem and energy channels | self-esteem]] with you throughout your day <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:30">[00:04:30]</a>.

Remember, self-love is a practice that requires patience and dedication <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:48">[00:04:48]</a>. With consistent effort, you will become able to love and accept yourself fully <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:05">[00:05:05]</a>.
