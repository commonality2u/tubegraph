Deception is rampant, with "Everyone had something to hide, including me" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:49">[00:02:49]</a>. The narrative explores [[Character trust and deceit | character trust and deceit]], emphasizing that "Trust is a currency in this town," a currency the narrator consistently lacks, always being "broke" in this regard <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:58">[00:02:58]</a>.

A key figure, Susan, embodies this deception: she claimed "she didn't know anything, but her eyes had already told the whole story" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:18">[00:04:18]</a>.
