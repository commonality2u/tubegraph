Moment is an "awesome platform" designed for creating full-stack AI applications without needing to code <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:51">[00:00:51]</a>. ## Core Capabilities
Moment allows users to build complex AI agents and integrate them into a front-end using its robust website/app builder <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:06">[00:01:06]</a>. The platform enables managing databases, API connections for tools, and building workflows, all within a single environment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>. Moment's own website was built using its app builder, demonstrating its capability for creating robust and visually appealing front ends with animations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:06">[00:05:06]</a>.

## Pricing and Cost-Effectiveness
A significant benefit of Moment is its predictable and affordable pricing model, especially when compared to platforms like Bubble <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>.
*   **Predictable Pricing**
    *   Moment charges for infrastructure (cores, memory, database storage) rather than "workload units" or per-operation fees <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:19">[00:03:19]</a>.
    *   Users are guaranteed a fixed monthly cost regardless of how many times pages are refreshed or APIs/databases are called <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:36">[00:03:36]</a>.
*   **Cost Savings**
    *   A comparative test showed operations that cost $155 and took 1 minute and 18 seconds on Bubble's free tier were achievable on Moment's free tier "really really quickly" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:20">[00:04:20]</a>.
    *   Moment can save users "thousands of thousands of dollars" compared to platforms with hidden fees and unpredictable pricing <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:52">[00:04:52]</a>.
