The Automator platform is hosting an [[ai_hackathon_competition_details | AI agent hackathon competition]] with $5,000 in cash prizes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:13">[00:00:13]</a>. This event is designed to engage the community with the Live Agent Studio in an exciting way, challenging participants to build impactful AI agents that solve real-world problems <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>.

### Registration Process
To [[participating_in_ai_hackathons_and_building_communitydriven_projects | participate]], individuals must build an AI agent for the Automator Live Agent Studio <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>. Registration is done via a link provided in the video description and pinned comment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>.

*   **Registration Benefits:** Registering for the hackathon before the competition starts grants participants a special link for free Voiceflow tokens and an extended trial on their platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:38">[00:02:38]</a>.
*   **Resources:** During the registration period, resources such as an in-depth developer guide and templates are provided to simplify the [[step_by_step_process_for_building_ai_agents | process of building an AI agent]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:51">[00:01:51]</a>.
*   **Community Support:** Participants are encouraged to join the Automator Think Tank Community, which has a dedicated forum section for the hackathon, to receive assistance while building their AI agents <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:16">[00:06:16]</a>.

### Key Timeline Dates
It is crucial for participants to be aware of the hackathon's timeline <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:46">[00:04:46]</a>.

*   **Registration Period:** Opens immediately and lasts for two weeks, concluding on January 8th <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:51">[00:04:51]</a>.
*   **Competition Period:** Officially begins on January 8th and runs for two weeks until January 22nd <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:57">[00:04:57]</a>. Submissions close on January 22nd <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:04">[00:05:04]</a>.
*   **Initial Judging Round:** After submissions close, the Automator team will take a few days to conduct the initial round of [[judging_criteria_for_ai_hackathon | judging]], selecting approximately the top 25% of agents <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:06">[00:05:06]</a>. These selected agents will be featured on the Live Agent Studio <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:17">[00:05:17]</a>.
*   **Community Voting:** The community will vote on the top agents from January 26th until February 1st <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:28">[00:05:28]</a>.
*   **Winners Announcement:** All winners will be announced during a live stream on February 1st at 9:00 AM Central Time <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:32">[00:05:32]</a>.

The competition aims to be straightforward and enjoyable, offering an opportunity to showcase AI Mastery to the world <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>.
