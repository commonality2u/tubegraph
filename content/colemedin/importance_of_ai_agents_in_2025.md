## Key Learning Areas for AI Agents

A critical skill to develop for 2025 is understanding [[advanced_architecture_for_ai_agents | agent architecture]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:55">[00:07:55]</a>. Anthropic's December 19, 2023, article on "Building Effective Agents" is highlighted as a valuable resource <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:11">[00:08:11]</a>. This article discusses various architectures and best practices for [[building_fullscale_ai_agents | powerful AI agents]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:09">[00:09:09]</a>.

### Core Components of an AI Agent
An [[defining_ai_agents | AI agent]] fundamentally includes:
*   A [[understanding_ai_agents | knowledge base]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:30">[00:09:30]</a>.
*   Retrieval tools to interact with services (e.g., Slack, Gmail) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:32">[00:09:32]</a>.
*   Chat memory <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:36">[00:09:36]</a>.

### Advanced Architectural Concepts
Anthropic's article further explores advanced [[advanced_architecture_for_ai_agents | architecture options]] for building powerful agents <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:07">[00:09:07]</a>, including:
*   **Prompt Chaining and Routing:** Beneficial for working with different LLMs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:43">[00:09:43]</a>.
*   **Parallelization:** Enables conquering tasks with multiple LLMs simultaneously <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:47">[00:09:47]</a>.
*   **Orchestration Workers:** A master agent can distribute work to various LLMs with an aggregator at the end <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:54">[00:09:54]</a>.
*   **Evaluator:** Crucial for reducing hallucinations in LLMs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:01">[00:10:01]</a>.

Understanding these architectural concepts is vital for developing effective [[ai_agents_and_their_development | AI agents]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:18">[00:10:18]</a>.

## Connection to Reasoning LLMs

[[specialized_ai_agents | Reasoning LLMs]] are considered the second most important area of focus for 2025, closely tied to [[ai_agents_and_their_development | AI agents]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:15">[00:11:15]</a>. These LLMs can reason with themselves about a prompt before providing a final answer, also known as Chain of Thought inference <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:29">[00:11:29]</a>.

[[advantages_of_ai_agents | Reasoning LLMs]] help solve critical generative AI problems such as hallucinations and poor decision-making by LLMs as agents <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:17">[00:12:17]</a>. The future will likely involve [[ai_agents_and_their_development | agentic workflows]] that combine [[specialized_ai_agents | reasoning LLMs]] (for power and complexity) with smaller, faster LLMs (for quick output on less complex tasks) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:32">[00:12:32]</a>.

> [!INFO]
> The ability to combine various LLMs, including [[specialized_ai_agents | domain-specific LLMs]], within [[advanced_architecture_for_ai_agents | agent architectures]] will drive significant innovation in 2025 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:03">[00:13:03]</a>.
