## Japanese Beetles

### Introduction to Japanese Beetles

Japanese beetles are often mistaken for ladybugs due to their similar appearance. However, unlike the more benign ladybugs, these beetles are aggressive and can pose a problem by biting humans and infesting homes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:00">[09:00]</a>.

### Characteristics of Japanese Beetles

- **Aggression:** Unlike ladybugs, which are passive and non-threatening, Japanese beetles are noted for their aggressive behavior. They can bite, which is a cause for concern for anyone dealing with an infestation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:51">[08:51]</a>.
- **Infestation:** These beetles can cause infestations in homes, and dealing with them can be a stressful experience for homeowners <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:58">[08:58]</a>.
