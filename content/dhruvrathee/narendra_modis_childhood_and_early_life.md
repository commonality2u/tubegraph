## Schooling and Early Interests
[[narendra_modis_political_journey_and_roles | Narendra Modi]] attended Bhagavatacharya Narayanacharya School <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:43:53">[01:43:53]</a>. His Sanskrit teacher, Prahlad Patel, described him as an average student <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:47:38">[01:47:38]</a>. An interesting aspect of his school days was his love for acting in school plays <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:56:06">[01:56:06]</a>. However, he would refuse to participate if he did not receive the main role <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:58:34">[01:58:34]</a>.

## Introduction to RSS and Political Ascendance
At the age of eight, [[narendra_modis_political_journey_and_roles | Narendra Modi]] came into contact with the RSS (Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:03:07">[02:03:07]</a>. In 1958, on Diwali, he became a child volunteer in the Vadnagar branch of the RSS <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:05:07">[02:05:07]</a>. His school classmate, Sudhir Joshi, noted that after school, he would typically drop off his books at home and then proceed to the RSS office <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:11:06">[02:11:06]</a>.

In 1972, at the age of 22, [[narendra_modis_political_journey_and_roles | Narendra Modi]] became a Pracharak (full-time worker) in RSS <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:21:49">[02:21:49]</a>. Fifteen years later, in 1987, he was brought into the BJP, where he was recognized as an exceptional organizer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:24:20">[02:24:20]</a>.

According to Andy Marino's political biography, [[narendra_modis_political_journey_and_roles | Narendra Modi]] played a crucial role in organizing the Gujarat portion of the 1990 Ram Rath Yatra <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:30:10">[02:30:10]</a>. By 1995, he was appointed as the National Secretary of BJP <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:45:00">[02:45:00]</a>. He was subsequently appointed as the Chief Minister of Gujarat in 2001 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:04:19">[03:04:19]</a>.
