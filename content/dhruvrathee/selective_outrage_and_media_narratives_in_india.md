## The "Brainwashing Mafia" and its Departments

A powerful "mafia" operates through WhatsApp and traditional media channels with a singular purpose: to "brainwash" Hindus and turn them into political "puppets" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:40:00">[01:40:00]</a>. This mafia operates through four key "departments":

1.  **Pride and Arrogance**: Instilling a sense of inherent greatness based solely on being Hindu, particularly for so-called upper-caste Hindus <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:06:00">[02:06:00]</a>.
2.  **Victimhood**: Portraying Hindus as historical victims who have suffered for centuries <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:30:00">[02:30:00]</a>.
3.  **Danger**: Convincing Hindus that they are under constant threat and identifying the perceived enemies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:35:00">[02:35:00]</a>.
4.  **Messiah (Narendra Modi)**: Presenting Narendra Modi as the sole savior who can protect Hindus, thus advocating for votes exclusively for him <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:41:00">[02:41:00]</a>.

The third and fourth departments, focusing on instilling fear and presenting a political savior, are particularly emphasized in the current environment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:59:00">[02:59:00]</a>.

### Manufacturing Fear: The "Danger" Narrative

The strategy of instilling fear within the majority community is not new.
*   **Pakistan**: In Pakistan, the Sunni Muslim majority was "brainwashed" through fake news that minorities (Hindus, Christians, Sikhs, Shia Muslims, Ahmadiyya Muslims, Sufi Muslims) posed a threat <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:28:00">[03:28:00]</a>. School books contained hateful propaganda, claiming Hindus and Sikhs killed millions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:43:00">[03:43:00]</a>. This led to a dire situation, with high poverty and unemployment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:30:00">[04:30:00]</a>.
*   **America (1919)**: Selective reporting by newspapers during the Washington riots inflamed racial tensions. An incident involving a black criminal attacking a black woman was suppressed, but when the same criminal attacked a white woman five days later, it became front-page news, leading to widespread riots and casualties <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:07:00">[05:07:00]</a>. The *Washington Post* later apologized for its racial past <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:58:00">[05:58:00]</a>.

*   The National Broadcasting and Digital Standards Authority (NBDSA) fined News18 and Times Now Nav Bharat for spreading hatred through their shows <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:30:00">[06:30:00]</a>.
*   Social media influencers and news channels used the incident to generalize, portraying "every Muslim man as a potential murderer," using the name "Abdul" as a blanket term for Muslims <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:18:00">[07:18:00]</a>.
*   The murder of Neha by Fayaz in Karnataka similarly activated right-wing influencers to spread hatred against all Muslims <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:34:00">[07:34:00]</a>.

This [[criticism_of_indian_media_and_fake_news | media bias and misinformation in Indian politics]] aims to "brainwash" Hindus, instill fear, and encourage votes for Modi <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:48:00">[07:48:00]</a>.
