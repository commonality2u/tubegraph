## Specific False Accusations and Their Impact

### The "Anti-National" Label
A common tactic used by certain groups, including those aligned with the BJP, is to label anyone criticizing them as "anti-national" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:21">[00:03:21]</a>. Elvish Yadav applied this label to Dhruv Rathee and others with opposing views <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:30">[00:03:30]</a>.

### The Aadil Aman Connection
Elvish Yadav falsely claimed that Dhruv Rathee created a Facebook group in 2020 and that an "important member" named Aadil Aman was part of it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:48">[00:03:48]</a>.
*   **Rebuttal**: Dhruv Rathee clarifies that the group was created in 2017 and deleted by 2020 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:01">[00:04:01]</a>. He appointed admins based on Facebook suggestions and activity, not personal acquaintance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:20">[00:04:20]</a>. One admin, Aadil Aman, made violent comments, which led to his removal from the admin position, and eventually, the group's closure around 2020-21 to avoid blame <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:37">[00:04:37]</a>.
*   **Further Misinformation**: Elvish Yadav fabricated stories, claiming that Aadil Aman and Dhruv Rathee were "best friends" and "old friends" who "worked for AAP together" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:12">[00:05:12]</a>. He then claimed that "this guy, Adil Aman, has moved to Germany" and was working for an NGO, Karwan-e-Mohabbat, since 2019 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:01">[00:23:01]</a>.
*   **Impact on an Innocent Third Party**: To support his claim, Elvish Yadav showed the Facebook profile of *another* Adil Aman, whose name was spelled differently <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:20">[00:23:20]</a>. This innocent individual, a researcher pursuing a Masters degree in Germany, confirmed he has "no affiliation with any political party" and the comment attributed to him was not made by him <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:07">[00:24:07]</a>. Due to this "deliberate defamation, and misrepresentation," this Adil Aman's "Facebook inbox [was] filled with abusive messages" and he received "threats to hurt me and my family" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:27">[00:24:27]</a>. Users on Twitter demanded his arrest based on this [[claims_of_spreading_hatred_and_misinformation | misinformation]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:40">[00:24:40]</a>. He has since sent a legal notice to Elvish Yadav and plans to file a legal case <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:52">[00:24:52]</a>.
