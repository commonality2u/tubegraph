The consumption of sugar can lead to an addiction similar to that of drugs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:53">[00:08:53]</a>. A 2017 study specifically compared [[impact_of_sugar_on_health_and_alternatives_to_sugary_drinks|sugar addiction]] to cocaine addiction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:02">[00:09:02]</a>.

## Characteristics of Sugar Addiction

Individuals addicted to sugar often exhibit regular consumption patterns <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:19">[00:09:19]</a>:
*   They will consume [[health_risks_of_soft_drinks|soft drinks]] regularly <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:21">[00:09:21]</a>.
*   If not regularly drinking [[health_risks_of_soft_drinks|soft drinks]], they will frequently consume other high-sugar items like candies, chocolates, sweets, or ice creams <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:23">[00:09:23]</a>.

The consistent intake of large amounts of added sugar is sufficient to cause severe health issues over time <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:10">[00:08:10]</a>. While immediate major consequences may not be seen after one or two days, prolonged daily consumption for years can lead to various ailments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:18">[00:08:18]</a>.

## Combating Sugar Addiction

It is crucial to fight [[impact_of_sugar_on_health_and_alternatives_to_sugary_drinks|sugar addiction]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:21:30">[00:21:30]</a>. While fresh fruit juices and lemonades avoid some harmful chemicals found in [[health_risks_of_soft_drinks|soft drinks]], they still contain significant amounts of sugar, often added sugar in lemonades or concentrated natural sugars in juices <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:21:39">[00:21:39]</a>.

The recommended solution is to replace sugary drinks with water <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:23">[00:22:23]</a>.
*   Regularly choosing water over [[health_risks_of_soft_drinks|soft drinks]], fruit juices, or lemonades <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:19">[00:22:19]</a>.
*   Over several weeks, taste buds will adjust to a lower sugar intake and may eventually dislike high sugar content <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:49">[00:22:49]</a>. This habit of drinking simple, pure water can be easily adopted <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:04">[00:23:04]</a>.
