Apertures is introduced as a social media management solution designed for busy professionals, aiming to give users their time back and unlock the true potential of their social media presence <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:12">[00:00:12]</a>. It functions as a "secret weapon" beyond a typical social media automation platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:24">[00:00:24]</a>.

## Accessing the Platform

Users can access the platform by directly signing in or signing up <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:47">[00:00:47]</a>. Account creation options include:
*   Email and password <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:51">[00:00:51]</a>
*   Sign-up with Google <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:53">[00:00:53]</a>

## Connecting Social Accounts

Upon signing up, users are prompted to connect their social media accounts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:19">[00:01:19]</a>.
*   **Currently Supported Platforms:**
    *   Twitter <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:21">[00:01:21]</a>
    *   LinkedIn <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:22">[00:01:22]</a>
    *   Lens Protocol <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:23">[00:01:23]</a>
*   [[technical_overview_of_github_integration_for_developers | GitHub]] can also be connected <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>.

## Creating and Managing Posts

The platform's dashboard allows users to create new posts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:44">[00:01:44]</a>.
*   **Post Creation:**
    *   Users can type their post content <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:47">[00:01:47]</a>.
    *   Ability to select specific social media accounts for publishing (e.g., Twitter, LinkedIn) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:56">[00:01:56]</a>.
*   **Version Control:** Users can create different versions of a post for each selected social media platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:01">[00:02:01]</a>. A "sync to default" option allows unifying content across platforms <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:32">[00:02:32]</a>.
*   **Post Actions:** Posts can be saved, published immediately, or scheduled for a later time <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:58">[00:01:58]</a>.
*   **Drafts Management:** Saved posts appear in a "drafts" section, where they can be edited or deleted <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:44">[00:02:44]</a>.

## [[technical_overview_of_github_integration_for_developers | GitHub Integration]] and AI Content Generation

A significant [[features_and_functionalities_of_the_new_application | feature]] of Apertures is its integration with [[technical_overview_of_github_integration_for_developers | GitHub]] for project-related social media content <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:03">[00:03:03]</a>.
*   **Connecting GitHub:** Users can connect their [[technical_overview_of_github_integration_for_developers | GitHub]] account and select specific repositories <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:07">[00:03:07]</a>.
*   **Adding Context:** Users can add context to their projects to help the [[openai_api_integration_for_social_media_content | AI]] generate more relevant and effective posts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:28">[00:03:28]</a>. This helps the [[openai_api_integration_for_social_media_content | AI]] understand the project better <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:34">[00:03:34]</a>.
*   **[[openai_api_integration_for_social_media_content | AI]] Post Generation from Commits:** The platform can generate social media posts directly from [[technical_overview_of_github_integration_for_developers | GitHub]] commit messages <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:41">[00:03:41]</a>.
    *   Users can view their commit messages <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:44">[00:03:44]</a>.
    *   Selecting a commit allows the [[openai_api_integration_for_social_media_content | AI]] to generate post suggestions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:51">[00:03:51]</a>.
    *   Generated posts can be saved, edited, or published <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:12">[00:04:12]</a>.
    *   Future posts generated from a project are visible in a dedicated section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:30">[00:04:30]</a>.
