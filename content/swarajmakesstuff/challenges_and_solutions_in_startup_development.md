Users can dedicate only one to two hours per week to plan and post content for the entire week <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:20">[00:02:20]</a>. This is achieved through the power of AI and various features designed to make the content creation and posting journey smoother <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:28">[00:02:28]</a>.
