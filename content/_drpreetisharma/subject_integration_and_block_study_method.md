For students preparing for the December 2024 FMG exam, a strategic [[creating_a_personalized_study_timetable | timetable]] has been developed, focusing on subject integration and a block study method. This [[60day_study_timetable_structure | 60-day timetable]], commencing on July 24th and concluding by September 21st, aims to ensure comprehensive syllabus coverage <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:38:00">[00:38:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:41:00">[00:41:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:03:00">[07:03:00]</a>. This initial phase will be followed by a dedicated revision plan <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:50:00">[00:50:00]</a>.

The overarching goal is to achieve thorough syllabus completion within these 60 days to facilitate subsequent revisions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:10:00">[02:10:00]</a>, as the key to cracking the exam lies in multiple revisions and consistent MCQ practice <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:21:00">[02:21:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:27:00">[02:27:00]</a>.

## Core Study Principles
The [[60day_study_timetable_structure | 60-day timetable]] emphasizes two main components:
*   **Theory and MCQs**: Students should aim to solve 100 to 120 MCQs per day, gradually increasing their daily practice if they are starting from a lower number <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:20:00">[01:20:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:39:00">[01:39:00]</a>.
*   **Study Hours**: An average of 8 to 10 hours of study per day is necessary to cover the syllabus effectively <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:54:00">[01:54:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:07:00">[02:07:00]</a>.
*   **Single Source**: It is crucial to maintain a single, consistent source of study material for each subject <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:34:00">[02:34:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:47:00">[02:47:00]</a>.
*   **Video Utilization**:
    *   For stronger subjects (where over 80% is scored in Q-banks), [[utilizing_rapid_revision_versus_main_videos_for_preparation | rapid revision videos]] can be used <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:55:00">[02:55:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:00:00">[03:00:00]</a>.
    *   For weaker subjects or topics (scoring less than 80% in Q-banks), main videos should be referred to <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:04:00">[03:04:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:09:00">[03:09:00]</a>.
    *   Specifically, for Pathology and Microbiology, only rapid revision videos are recommended <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:18:00">[03:18:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:07:00">[09:07:00]</a>.

## Subject Blocks for Integrated Study
The subjects are divided into four blocks to facilitate integrated learning and leverage their interconnections <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:39:00">[03:39:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:44:00">[03:44:00]</a>.

### Block 1: Foundational Clinical Subjects
This block focuses on subjects that are often taught together due to their anatomical and surgical relevance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:44:00">[03:44:00]</a>.
*   **Anatomy**: 4 days <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:50:00">[03:50:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:37:00">[07:37:00]</a>
*   **Surgery**: 6 days <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:53:00">[03:53:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:39:00">[07:39:00]</a>
*   **Orthopedics (Ortho)**: 2 days <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:57:00">[03:57:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:42:00">[07:42:00]</a>
*   **Radiology**: 2 days (to wrap up the block, as a lot of X-rays and CT scans are encountered in Surgery and Orthopedics) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:04:00">[04:04:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:43:00">[07:43:00]</a>

## Grand Tests (GTs)
The 60-day plan includes two dedicated days for Grand Tests:
*   **GT 1**: Around September 8th, even if all 19 subjects are not yet covered (e.g., after 15 subjects) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:17:00">[08:17:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:21:00">[08:21:00]</a>.
*   **GT 2**: On September 21st, marking the end of the 60-day timetable <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:36:00">[06:36:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:44:00">[08:44:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:47:00">[08:47:00]</a>.
Each GT day involves both giving the test and reviewing it thoroughly <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:17:00">[06:17:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:21:00">[06:21:00]</a>. These two GT days are added to the 58 days allocated for subject study, totaling 60 days <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:09:00">[06:09:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:50:00">[08:50:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:52:00">[08:52:00]</a>.

## Daily Study Schedule
A structured daily schedule is recommended to optimize [[time_management_and_daily_study_schedule | study hours]] and minimize fatigue:

*   **Morning Routine (1 hour)**: Enjoy personal morning activities, including 20 minutes outdoors for fresh air <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="11:00:00">[11:00:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="11:09:00">[11:09:00]</a>.
*   **Study Session 1 (8:00 AM - 11:00 AM)**: 3 hours of focused study <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="11:25:00">[11:25:00]</a>. This duration prepares students for the 2.5 to 3-hour exam sitting requirement <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="11:29:00">[11:29:00]</a>.
*   **Break 1 (11:00 AM - 11:30 AM)**: 30-minute break for relaxation, no social media or other screens <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="11:39:00">[11:39:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="12:19:00">[12:19:00]</a>.
*   **Study Session 2 (11:30 AM - 2:00 PM)**: 2.5 hours of study, bringing the total study time before lunch to 5.5 hours <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="12:22:00">[12:22:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="12:26:00">[12:26:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="12:28:00">[12:28:00]</a>.
*   **Mega Break (2:00 PM - 3:30 PM)**: 1.5 hours for lunch, power nap, or personal leisure (including social media/entertainment) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="12:35:00">[12:35:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="12:43:00">[12:43:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="12:51:00">[12:51:00]</a>.
*   **Study Session 3 (3:30 PM - 6:00 PM)**: 2.5 hours of study <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="12:57:00">[12:57:00]</a>.
*   **Break 2 (6:00 PM - 7:00 PM)**: 1 hour break, mixing relaxation with optional social interaction or media use <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="13:03:00">[13:03:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="13:07:00">[13:07:00]</a>.
*   **Study Session 4 (7:00 PM - 9:00 PM)**: 2 hours of study <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="13:19:00">[13:19:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="13:22:00">[13:22:00]</a>.
