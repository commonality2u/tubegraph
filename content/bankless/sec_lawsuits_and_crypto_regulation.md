## Wave of Lawsuit Dismissals

This includes:

*   **Opyn** Finser tweeted that the SEC was closing its investigation into Opyn, calling it a win for builders in the space and a step forward for innovation, preventing NFTs from being classified as securities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:26">[00:04:26]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:30">[00:04:30]</a>.
*   **Uniswap** The SEC officially closed its investigation into Uniswap Labs, which had previously received a Wells notice alleging operation as an unregistered broker and exchange, and issuance of unregistered securities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:43">[00:04:43]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:53">[00:04:53]</a>. This outcome was seen as a significant victory for DeFi, reaffirming the legality of the technology <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:57">[00:04:57]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:01">[00:05:01]</a>.
*   **Robinhood** Vlad Tenev announced the SEC's investigation into Robinhood Crypto was closed, emphasizing it as a win for justice and the rule of law, and a call for pro-innovation policies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:07">[00:05:07]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:10">[00:05:10]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:17">[00:05:17]</a>. Robinhood had previously engaged with the SEC multiple times to understand how to operate legally, only to be met with a Wells notice <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:12">[00:05:12]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:20">[00:05:20]</a>.
*   **ConsenSys (MetaMask)** Joseph Lubin confirmed an agreement in principle with the SEC to dismiss the securities enforcement case concerning MetaMask, pending commission approval <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:23">[00:05:23]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:26">[00:05:26]</a>.
