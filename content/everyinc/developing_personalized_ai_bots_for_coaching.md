## The "Codex V" Approach

The core idea behind personalized AI coaching is to provide the AI with extensive self-knowledge, often compiled into a "Codex V" (a reference to Buster Benson's "Book of Life") <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:32:21">[00:32:21]</a>. This document acts as the AI's foundational understanding of the user.

Key information to include in a Codex V:
*   Ideal weekly calendar <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:22">[00:00:22]</a>
*   Energetic "black holes" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:23">[00:00:23]</a>
*   Reactivity triggers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:25">[00:00:25]</a>
*   Burnout warning signs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>
*   Decision-making principles <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:28">[00:00:28]</a>
*   Biomarkers and biometrics <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:29">[00:00:29]</a>
*   Prior experiment results <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>
*   Core personality traits (e.g., Enneagram type) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:39:40">[00:39:40]</a>
*   Core values <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:39:57">[00:39:57]</a>
*   Intentions and goals (e.g., preparing for fatherhood, meditation access, personal hobbies, business goals) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:41:43">[00:41:43]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:41:55">[00:41:55]</a>
*   Ideal daily/weekly architecture <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:42:29">[00:42:29]</a>

The larger context window of modern AI models makes it feasible to upload extensive self-knowledge into a document, eliminating previous bottlenecks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:33:10">[00:33:10]</a>.

### Case Study: AI for Jhana Meditation Practice

An example of [[using_ai_for_personal_coaching | using AI for personal coaching]] is in Jhana meditation practice <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:09">[00:10:09]</a>. Jhanas are meditation states characterized by bliss or joy, achieved by reliably directing attention to pleasurable bodily sensations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:09">[00:13:09]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:25">[00:13:25]</a>.

**How AI is used for Jhana practice:**
*   **Experiment Design:** Users maintain a journal of their Jhana practice experiments, trying different frames or directions for their awareness <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:59">[00:15:59]</a>.
*   **Contextual Feedback:** The journal is uploaded to a dedicated ChatGPT project, along with a Jhana instruction manual <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:27">[00:16:27]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:51">[00:16:51]</a>.
*   **Personalized Advice:** When a user describes a struggle (e.g., distraction hindering Jhana access), the AI provides tailored advice by referencing the uploaded notes and manual <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:51">[00:17:51]</a>. This advice might include relaxing expectations or starting from a place of humility <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:27">[00:19:27]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:54">[00:19:54]</a>.
*   **Regular Reflection:** Users record small journaling entries after practices and then re-upload updated PDFs weekly for more meta-reflections on how to approach the practice the next week <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:21:20">[00:21:20]</a>.
*   **Voice Memos:** Voice recording memos into ChatGPT while on walks allows for real-time reflection and advice, fostering "thinking on the move" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:17">[00:23:17]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:24">[00:24:24]</a>.

The AI's ability to provide immediate, context-aware feedback makes it a valuable complement to human coaching, especially for detailed personal practices <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:59">[00:22:59]</a>.

## The Future of AI Coaching

AI coaches are currently valuable, and their potential continues to grow. Key innovations to make them even better include:
*   **Centaur Teams:** The concept of "centaurs" (human + AI teams) applies strongly to coaching. Coaches can create AI bots for clients, enabling continuous engagement and practice between human sessions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:57:57">[00:57:57]</a>. This allows for deeper insight and more consistent progress <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:57:11">[00:57:11]</a>.
*   **Integrated Data:** Automatically pulling in data from various sources (e.g., Google, to-do list apps, health APIs) into the AI's "Codex V" will unlock immense usefulness by providing even richer, more real-time context <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:58:51">[00:58:51]</a>.
*   **Voice and Uncanny Valley:** As AI voice crosses the "uncanny valley," it will unlock a new mode of interaction, making the coaching experience more natural and engaging <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:58:43">[00:58:43]</a>.
*   **Three-Way Chat Threads:** Future iterations could involve three-way chat threads where clients, AI, and human coaches can interact, allowing the human coach to monitor conversations and intervene if the AI hallucinates or provides unhelpful advice <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:58:18">[00:58:18]</a>. This fosters [[developing_ai_products_with_user_feedback | developing AI products with user feedback]] and oversight.
*   **Predictive Capabilities:** Leveraging quantified self-data to predict personal states (e.g., likelihood of achieving a Jhana state based on sleep, stress, and past success) would enable proactive adjustments and interventions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:59:31">[00:59:31]</a>. This aligns with [[improving_personal_practices_with_guided_ai_assistance | improving personal practices with guided AI assistance]].

Ultimately, AI coaching aims to provide a low-cost alternative for those who cannot access human coaches, while also serving as a powerful augmentation for human coaches, allowing for deeper engagement and more consistent progress with clients <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:56:10">[00:56:10]</a>.
