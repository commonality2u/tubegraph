*   **Strategic decision-making**: ChatGPT can help evaluate where different strategic perspectives agree and disagree, guiding users to identify "fault lines of disagreement" and different "vectors of strategy" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="42:40:00">[42:40:00]</a>.
