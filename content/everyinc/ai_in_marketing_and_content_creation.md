AI can role-play as multiple characters to simulate a focus group or an entire audience <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:02">[00:04:02]</a>. This capability allows users to:
*   Test assumptions about content or marketing messages <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:25">[00:00:25]</a>.
*   Predict how an audience might react to different options <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:14">[00:03:14]</a>.
*   Receive "wisdom of the crowds" feedback <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:45">[00:00:45]</a>.

## [[Role of AI in creative work | AI]] as a Judge and Evaluator

*   **Binary Classification:** They excel at binary (0/1) classification based on specific criteria. For example, an [[AI in filmmaking | AI]] can check if an article is concise or has a compelling hook <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:57">[00:15:57]</a><a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:04">[00:16:04]</a>.

This capability can be used to generate specific criteria for evaluation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:40">[00:17:40]</a>.
