## [[Using AI for Research and Interview Preparation | AI for Research and Interview Preparation]]

AI plays a crucial role in preparing for podcast interviews by helping hosts to deeply ingest and understand complex topics <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:30">[00:04:30]</a>.

### Understanding Complex Topics
A primary use of AI, particularly Claude, is to develop a robust "mental model" of a topic <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:44:00">[00:44:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:45:00">[00:45:00]</a>.
This is achieved by:
*   **Uploading Content** Podcasters can upload entire books, articles, or transcripts (e.g., converting ePub to text) into an AI project <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:25">[00:10:25]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:44">[00:10:44]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:50">[00:10:50]</a>. This provides the AI with all the necessary context to answer questions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:55">[00:10:55]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:04">[00:14:04]</a>.
*   **Contextual Questioning** Instead of vague queries, specific questions related to the uploaded content can be asked, such as "how does he explain what groups made up modern Europeans?" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:00">[00:11:00]</a>. This allows for focused interrogation of the material <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:52:11">[00:52:11]</a>.
*   **Clarifying Concepts** For topics that are vaguely understood, chatting with Claude helps to clarify confusing aspects and ensure the user is "on the right track" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:29">[00:00:29]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:03">[00:17:03]</a>. This includes asking "wait, step back, why is this necessary, what's going on?" or "how do I think about the broader context of what's happening here?" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[00:00:37]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:41">[00:17:41]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:57">[00:17:57]</a>.
*   **Addressing Blind Spots** By going through an author's writings with Claude, podcasters can identify potential blind spots in their own thinking or areas of confusion about the author's ideas, which can then be directly addressed during the interview <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:22:25">[00:22:25]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:52:50">[00:52:50]</a>.

### Crafting Interview Questions
While AI is not yet capable of generating the specific, nuanced questions that a human host can <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:24">[00:04:24]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:02:27">[01:02:27]</a>, it assists in the research-intensive process of question formulation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:40:46">[00:40:46]</a>:
*   **Developing a "Playbook"**: AI can analyze past interview questions to identify patterns in how a host likes to ask questions and conduct interviews <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:55:11">[00:55:11]</a>. This allows for the creation of guidelines for another AI to generate questions for new guests <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:59:01">[00:59:01]</a>.
*   **Scaffolding Knowledge**: For difficult texts, AI can provide a condensed explanation of key ideas, acting as a "useful scaffold" for understanding where pieces fit together <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:04">[00:10:04]</a>. This allows the host to start interviews at a deeper level <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:39">[00:23:39]</a>.

AI streamlines several [[PostProduction Processes in Podcasting with AI | post-production processes in podcasting]], automating repetitive tasks and improving efficiency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:04:54">[01:04:54]</a>.
