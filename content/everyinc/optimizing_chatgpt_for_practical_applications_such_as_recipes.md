## Recipe Generation and Modification

ChatGPT is highly effective for generating and modifying recipes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:30">[00:19:30]</a>. Users can provide ingredients they have on hand, desired cuisine style, and other preferences to receive a tailored recipe <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:31">[00:19:31]</a>.

Key benefits for recipes include:
*   **Direct Recipes** ChatGPT provides a recipe directly without requiring users to navigate through lengthy blog posts or personal stories, unlike traditional recipe websites <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:07">[00:20:07]</a>.
*   **On-the-Fly Modifications** If an ingredient is missing, ChatGPT can instantly modify the recipe to suggest substitutes or alternative instructions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:29">[00:20:29]</a>. For example, hoisin sauce can be replaced with brown sugar <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:33">[00:20:33]</a>.
*   **Vision Capabilities** It is anticipated that ChatGPT's vision capabilities could allow users to upload a photo of a cookbook recipe and ask it to extract and modify it for missing ingredients <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:54">[00:20:54]</a>.
