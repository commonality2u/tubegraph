## Using AI to Spark Ideas and Anecdotes

One way [[enhancing_creativity_and_idea_development_with_ai | AI assists creative work]] is by prompting contextual memory to unearth interesting anecdotes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:36">[00:01:36]</a>. For instance, to write a piece about New York City, ChatGPT can be prompted to list "most beautiful and distinctive buildings built between 1910 and 1933" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:31">[00:00:31]</a>. The goal is to find a building that triggers a personal memory <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>.

Memory is described as being "layered and sort of behind doors," often outsourced to "little checkpoints" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>. Just as returning to a childhood home can bring back forgotten memories, associating ideas with physical spaces can prompt recall <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>. By providing a list of buildings, AI can act as a catalyst for these memory associations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:36">[00:01:36]</a>.

A practical example of this is when the mention of the "Woolworth Building" from ChatGPT's list instantly brought an anecdote to mind <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:47">[00:01:47]</a>. This anecdote involved a college meeting at the building where the concept of "hiring and dating is a Max function" was discussed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:12">[00:02:12]</a>. This concept explains that in certain situations, only the top value matters (e.g., marrying one person, hiring one CEO), unlike others where a broad range of elements needs to play together <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:40">[00:02:40]</a>. The recollection of this conversation, prompted by the building, provided a strong starting point for a written piece <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:47">[00:02:47]</a>.

## The Non-Linear Nature of AI-Assisted Creativity

The [[writing_with_and_about_ai | writing process with AI]] often involves an iterative and sometimes unpredictable journey <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:14">[00:04:14]</a>. What starts as an intention to write about one topic—such as New York City and its influence on taste—can pivot dramatically based on the AI's prompts and the writer's subsequent memory recall <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:06">[00:04:06]</a>. In the example above, the focus shifted from New York architecture to the abstract concept of "Max functions" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:09">[00:04:09]</a>.

This fluidity is not a mistake but a fundamental aspect of how the [[benefits_of_using_ai_for_creative_work | creative process]] works, especially when [[challenges_and_benefits_of_aiassisted_writing | collaborating with AI]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:17">[00:04:17]</a>. [[using_ai_to_improve_writing_taste_and_style | AI is effective]] at "pushing your brain into new areas that you wouldn't have thought of" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:42">[00:04:42]</a>. A key skill for creators is to allow these unexpected connections to emerge without filtering them out as "off-topic" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:48">[00:04:48]</a>. Following the "spark" of inspiration, even if it leads in an unforeseen direction, is crucial for unlocking new ideas <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:55">[00:04:55]</a>.
