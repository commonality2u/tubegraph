## Practical Applications & Examples

### Jhana Meditation Practice
An individual uses an AI coach for their Jhana meditation practice. Jhanas are meditation states involving sustained attention to pleasure in the body, leading to bliss or joy <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:12:54">[01:12:54]</a>. The AI coach helps by:
*   **Journaling and Experimentation:** The user keeps a journal of their Jhana exploration, experimenting with different frames and prompts, which is then uploaded to the AI <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:51:00">[01:51:00]</a>.
*   **Personalized Feedback:** The AI, using the "Journey instruction booklet" and the user's logs, provides personalized advice. For instance, when the user struggled to access Jhana 1 due to distraction, the AI offered gentle suggestions, such as relaxing expectations and approaching practice with curiosity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:17:07">[01:17:07]</a>.
*   **Emotional Block Recognition:** The AI can help identify "J-blocked" states, signaling underlying emotions that need to be felt for progress <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:09:00">[02:09:00]</a>.

## Setting up an AI Coach
Users can customize their AI coach by:
*   **Custom Instructions:** Defining the AI's role (e.g., "world-class coach"), purpose (e.g., "provide insightful wise reflections"), and access to personal documents like the Codex V <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:34:06">[00:34:06]</a>.
*   **Communication Style:** Specifying desired tone (e.g., "direct honesty, playful reverence, compassionate challenge") and prompt style (e.g., "potent questions," "laconic but impactful") <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:34:19">[00:34:19]</a>. This can be tweaked for individual preferences <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:34:36">[00:34:36]</a>.
*   **Project-Specific Chats:** Organizing different coaching topics into separate projects within the AI (e.g., "Jhana coaching," "Costa Rica move") with distinct instructions and files helps manage context and prevents confusion <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:16:00">[02:16:00]</a>. This [[adaptive_use_of_ai_for_personal_and_creative_tasks | adaptive use of AI]] improves overall organization.
*   **Voice Memos:** Recording voice memos into the AI during walks or other activities allows for "thinking on the move," facilitating reflections on personal or business matters <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:17:00">[02:17:00]</a>.

## AI Coach vs. Human Coach
AI coaches offer a "low-cost alternative" for those who might not otherwise access coaching or therapy <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:56:15">[00:56:15]</a>. They can also act as a "complement or augmentation" to human coaches, providing constant availability and deeper insights into a client's ongoing experience through continuous touchpoints <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:56:28">[00:56:28]</a>. This supports the "centaur" model, where human plus AI teams outperform AI alone <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:57:39">[00:57:39]</a>.
