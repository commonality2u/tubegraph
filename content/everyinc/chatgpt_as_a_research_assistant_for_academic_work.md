### [[Using ChatGPT for decision making and research | Research and Understanding Complex Information]]
As a neuroscientist, ChatGPT is used to interact with research papers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:54">[00:03:54]</a>. The process involves:
1.  Receiving paper alerts in an email inbox <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:06">[00:04:06]</a>.
2.  Quickly assessing relevance based on the title <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:15">[00:04:15]</a>.
3.  Downloading the PDF (accessible with an academic email, or via other methods) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:20">[00:04:20]</a>.
4.  Uploading the PDF to ChatGPT <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:33">[00:04:33]</a>.
5.  Asking questions, explicitly stating "based on the PDF attached" to ensure the AI uses the provided text <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:31">[00:05:31]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:39">[00:05:39]</a>.

This allows for a quick understanding of key considerations from a paper, such as measuring cognitive load in ADHD, by providing bullet points rather than requiring a full 30-page read <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:51">[00:05:51]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:11">[00:08:11]</a>. It helps determine if a paper is specifically relevant to one's research without reading the entire document <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:19">[00:07:19]</a>.

ChatGPT also acts as a translator for complex academic language, especially from adjacent research fields where different terminology is used <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:51">[00:09:51]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:24">[00:10:24]</a>. Prompts like "explain me like I'm five" or "explain me like I'm an undergraduate psychology student" can simplify concepts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:11">[00:10:11]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:01">[00:13:01]</a>. It can clarify findings, limitations, or even translate specific reviewer comments on academic papers, addressing "ungoogleable" blind spots <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:18">[00:10:18]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:10">[00:14:10]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:53">[00:14:53]</a>.

### [[ChatGPT in writing and content creation | Writing and Outlining]]
In [[using_chatgpt_in_writing | writing]], ChatGPT transforms a "gardener" writer (one whose story unfolds as they write) into an "architect" (one who plans and outlines) during the later phases of writing <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:44:51">[00:44:51]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:45:04">[00:45:04]</a>.

The process for writing an article involves:
1.  Collecting "curiosity attractors" (ideas, creative projects, questions that keep resurfacing) and adding them to a note-taking system like Roam <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:45:45">[00:45:45]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:49:52">[00:49:52]</a>.
2.  Organizing these notes into a rough outline (introduction, body, conclusion) on the same page <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:50:35">[00:50:35]</a>.
3.  Exporting and uploading this outline to ChatGPT <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:51:10">[00:51:10]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:51:13">[00:51:13]</a>.
4.  Asking ChatGPT to act as an editor, identifying missing aspects, and referring to research from psychology and cognitive neuroscience <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:51:29">[00:51:29]</a>. This acts as a developmental editor, helping to look around corners and identify blind spots or missing arguments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:53:51">[00:53:51]</a>.
5.  Integrating relevant suggestions into the Google Doc draft <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:53:29">[00:53:29]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:53:31">[00:53:31]</a>.
6.  For the final draft, re-uploading the complete article to ChatGPT and asking for specific suggestions on improving clarity, flow, or argument accessibility <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:02:40">[01:02:40]</a>. This provides an "extra pair of eyes" to ensure the article is as good as possible, especially useful without a human editor <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:03:42">[01:03:42]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:03:47">[01:03:47]</a>.

ChatGPT is particularly good at identifying research fields, areas, and theories, even if it might hallucinate specific papers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:54:28">[00:54:28]</a>. This helps in discovering different angles and perspectives that might be outside one's immediate field of expertise <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:54:36">[00:54:36]</a>.

### General Utility
*   **"Model Manager" Approach**: Users who are effective at using ChatGPT act like "model managers." They have a sense of when the AI's output isn't quite right and will nudge it in different directions, ask about related concepts to build their own knowledge, or try different approaches (e.g., asking for results in another language) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:28:42">[00:28:42]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:29:14">[00:29:14]</a>. Being polite to the AI, by saying "thank you," is also recommended <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:28:28">[00:28:28]</a>.
*   **Upleveling Skills**: [[ChatGPTs impact on programming and data visualization | ChatGPT]] can help individuals who are new to a skill (e.g., coding, thumbnail design) get to a "pretty good level" quickly, acting as an "upleveling" tool <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:42:50">[00:42:50]</a>.
*   **Encouraging Exploration**: For curious individuals, ChatGPT is "magical" because it provides quick answers to questions that previously seemed impossible to figure out, facilitating deeper exploration of diverse topics <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:00:26">[01:00:26]</a>. This shift suggests a future where humans rely less on rote memory and more on creativity, as answers are readily available <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:01:47">[01:01:47]</a>.

## Importance of Data for AI
The quality of ChatGPT's output is highly dependent on the specificity of the input prompt ("you get out what you put in") <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:39:10">[00:39:10]</a>. Additionally, individuals who maintain journaling or note-taking practices (like daily journaling or collecting notes in Roam) have a significant advantage <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:26:17">[01:26:17]</a>. This personal data provides a rich context for the AI to work with, allowing for highly tailored and useful advice that wouldn't be possible with generic prompts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:26:22">[01:26:22]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:26:56">[01:26:56]</a>.
