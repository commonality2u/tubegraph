Optimizing contributions to your Registered Education Savings Plan (RESP) and Registered Disability Savings Plan (RDSP) before year-end is a key tax strategy. These accounts offer significant government grants and bonds to help you save for education or support individuals with disabilities.

## Registered Education Savings Plan (RESP)

Contributing to your RESP by December 31st ensures your child receives government grants for the current year <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:13">[00:05:13]</a>.

*   **Canada Education Savings Grant (CESG)**: The government matches 20% of contributions up to $2,500 annually per child, meaning you can receive up to $500 in CESG per child each year <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:17">[00:05:17]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:19">[00:05:19]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:25">[00:05:25]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:27">[00:05:27]</a>.
*   **Deadline Importance**: Missing the December 31st deadline means losing this year's grant <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:30">[00:05:30]</a>.
*   **Carry Forward**: Unused CESG room can be carried forward, allowing you to catch up on missed grants in future years <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:32">[00:05:32]</a>.

## Registered Disability Savings Plan (RDSP)

The [[benefits_of_rdsp_for_canadians_with_disabilities | Registered Disability Savings Plan (RDSP)]] is a crucial tool for long-term financial security for individuals with disabilities. Contributing to your RDSP by December 31st can significantly boost its value through government contributions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:34">[00:05:34]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:37">[00:05:37]</a>.

*   **Canada Disability Savings Grant (CDSG)**: You can earn up to $3,500 in grants annually <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:41">[00:05:41]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:46">[00:05:46]</a>.
*   **Canada Disability Savings Bond (CDSB)**: You can also receive up to $1,000 in bonds each year, even without personal contributions, if eligible <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:46">[00:05:46]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:49">[00:05:49]</a>.
*   **Carry Forward**: Unused entitlements for the CDSG and CDSB can be carried forward for up to 10 years <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:51">[00:05:51]</a>.
*   **Maximizing Benefits**: Contributing now ensures you maximize your annual benefits and receive your grants and bonds quicker <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:55">[00:05:55]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:57">[00:05:57]</a>.
