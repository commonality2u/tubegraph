## Key Factors When Moving Abroad

When considering moving abroad, it's crucial to prioritize what matters most to you <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>. The speaker suggests focusing on three to five factors to evaluate potential countries <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:06">[00:02:06]</a>. Key factors often considered include:

*   **Healthcare**: Access to good doctors and hospitals, and insurance coverage <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:01">[00:01:01]</a>.
*   **Cost of Living**: Understanding expenses for housing, groceries, and daily living to avoid financial stress <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>.
*   **Safety**: Crime rates, political stability, and concerns about natural disasters <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a>.
*   **Job Market**: Crucial for those seeking employment or planning to work remotely <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:24">[00:01:24]</a>.
*   **Visa and Citizenship**: Ease of obtaining and maintaining a visa, and the long-term citizenship process <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>.
*   **Taxes**: What you'll owe on income and property <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:42">[00:01:42]</a>.
*   **Weather**: Climate preferences, especially for Canadians looking for warmer places <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:47">[00:01:47]</a>.

For example, Homer, considering a move abroad, prioritized healthcare, cost of living, and safety, leading him to consider Spain, Portugal, and New Zealand due to their great healthcare, lower costs than Canada, and solid safety records <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:14">[00:02:14]</a>.

## Country-Specific Cost of Living Comparisons

Here's a breakdown of 11 countries Canadians might consider, with insights into their cost of living:

### Thailand
Thailand is a strong choice for Canadians, offering affordable living and excellent healthcare <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:00">[00:03:00]</a>. The overall cost of living is much lower than in Canada, enabling a comfortable lifestyle on a modest budget <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:13">[00:03:13]</a>.

### Portugal
Portugal provides a somewhat comfortable lifestyle, particularly outside major cities, where the cost of living is more manageable <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:57">[00:03:57]</a>.

### Mexico
Mexico offers a culturally rich and affordable lifestyle close to Canada <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:24">[00:04:24]</a>. The cost of living is significantly lower than in Canada, with budget-friendly housing, food, and transportation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:36">[00:04:36]</a>.

### Vietnam
Vietnam is a fantastic choice for an affordable lifestyle <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:16">[00:05:16]</a>. The cost of living, especially in coastal cities like Da Nang, is incredibly low <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:26">[00:05:26]</a>.

### Malaysia
Malaysia is an up-and-coming choice for Canadians, offering affordability and modern amenities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:10">[00:06:10]</a>. The low cost of living allows for a comfortable lifestyle <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:32">[00:06:32]</a>.

### Panama
