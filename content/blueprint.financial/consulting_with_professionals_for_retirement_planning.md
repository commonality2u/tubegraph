## Types of Professionals and Their Contributions

They ensure you are maximizing your resources and investments to meet your retirement goals <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:12">[00:11:12]</a>.

### Accountants and Tax Advisors
Accountants and tax advisers play a vital role in helping you navigate changes in your tax situation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:16">[00:11:16]</a>. Their expertise can help you keep more money in your pocket during retirement <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:18">[00:11:18]</a>.

### Legal Experts
