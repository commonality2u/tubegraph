## What is the CPP Disability Benefit?

The [[The CPP disability benefit and its importance | CPP disability benefit]] is designed to provide financial support if you become severely disabled before retirement and are unable to work <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:39">[00:09:39]</a>. It can serve as a lifeline for individuals who are unable to work due to a serious health issue <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:48">[00:10:48]</a>.

## Key Considerations

*   **Application Required**: To receive this benefit, you must apply for it and meet specific criteria <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:52">[00:10:52]</a>.
*   **Crucial Support**: Not understanding or knowing about the [[The CPP disability benefit and its importance | CPP disability benefit]] could mean missing out on crucial financial support during a time of need, especially if an unexpected health event occurs close to retirement <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:57">[00:10:57]</a>.
