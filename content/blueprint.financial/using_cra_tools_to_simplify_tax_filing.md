## Autofill My Return

One key tool provided by the CRA is "Autofill My Return," designed to streamline tax preparation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:02">[00:09:02]</a>. This feature is integrated into NetFile-certified tax software programs like TurboTax and Wealthsimple Tax <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:07">[00:09:07]</a>.

### How Autofill My Return Works

Autofill My Return is designed to prevent errors and ensure accurate tax filing <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:57">[00:09:57]</a>.

1.  **Instantly Pull Tax Slips** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:12">[00:09:12]</a>
    By connecting your tax software to your My CRA account, Autofill can automatically import essential tax documents <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:15">[00:09:15]</a>. This includes T4s, T5s, RRSP contributions, and other tax slips directly from the CRA <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:20">[00:09:20]</a>.
2.  **Prevent Audit Triggers** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:27">[00:09:27]</a>
    A common [[common_tax_filing_mistakes_to_avoid | tax filing mistake to avoid]] is forgetting to report all income sources, such as secondary jobs, dividends, or side hustle earnings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:30">[00:09:30]</a>. Since the CRA already possesses this information, any discrepancies can lead to reassessments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:34">[00:09:34]</a>. Autofill ensures that your tax return accurately matches the information the CRA expects <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:39">[00:09:39]</a>.
3.  **Save Time and Find Missed Credits** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:44">[00:09:44]</a>
    The tool eliminates the need for manual data entry, reducing the risk of typos and the time spent gathering paperwork <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:47">[00:09:47]</a>. It also helps identify and claim missed credits and deductions, ensuring you [[maximizing_tax_deductions_and_credits | maximize your tax deductions and credits]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:52">[00:09:52]</a>.

### How to Use It

To utilize Autofill My Return, log into your My CRA account before filing, connect it to your tax software, and import your tax slips with a single click <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:58">[00:10:04]</a>. This process removes the need for manual entry and minimizes the chance of mistakes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:06">[00:10:06]</a>.

Using tools like Autofill My Return can help you simplify your [[tax_filing_tips_and_software_recommendations | tax filing process]] and avoid costly tax mistakes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:56">[00:01:56]</a>.
