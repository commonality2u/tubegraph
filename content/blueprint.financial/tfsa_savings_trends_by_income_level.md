*   Those earning $5,000 to $9,999 per year hold an average of $16,000 in their [[taxfree_savings_account_tfsa_strategies | TFSAs]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:26">[00:07:26]</a>.

### Middle-Income Earners ($20,000 - $74,999 Annually)
*   In the middle-income range ($20,000 to $49,999 per year), [[taxfree_savings_account_tfsa_strategies | TFSA]] balances remain fairly consistent, ranging from about $28,000 to $30,000 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:47">[00:07:47]</a>.
