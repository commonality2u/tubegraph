Unlike couples, single retirees do not have a built-in caregiver, making [[financial_planning_for_single_retirees | planning for health-related costs]] crucial <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:36">[00:07:36]</a>. Health care expenses can accumulate rapidly, with single retirees often facing higher long-term care costs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:51">[00:06:51]</a>.

## Costs of Care in Canada

The typical costs for various types of care in Canada can be substantial:
*   **Home Care:** Ranges from $25 to $50 per hour <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:57">[00:06:57]</a>.
*   **Assisted Living:** Can range from $3,000 to $5,000 per month <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:05">[00:07:05]</a>.
*   **Government-Subsidized Nursing Home:** Costs typically fall between $2,000 and $3,000 per month <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:09">[00:07:09]</a>.
*   **Private Care:** Can exceed $5,000 per month <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:13">[00:07:13]</a>.

## Strategies for Planning Health Costs

To mitigate the impact of these costs, single retirees should consider several proactive steps:

### Budgeting for Care
It is essential to budget specifically for potential care needs. This includes keeping a reserve fund or investigating long-term care insurance options <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:17">[00:07:17]</a>.

### Assigning a Power of Attorney
A critical step is to assign a power of attorney to a trusted individual. This ensures that someone can make medical and [[financial_planning_for_canadian_retirees | financial decisions]] on your behalf if you are unable to <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:23">[00:07:23]</a>.

### Considering Insurance Policies
Disability and critical illness insurance policies can provide a buffer against major medical expenses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:30">[00:07:30]</a>. These policies can help cover significant costs that might otherwise deplete savings.

## Overall Retirement Planning

Incorporating potential long-term care costs into a comprehensive [[financial_planning_for_single_retirees | retirement plan]] is vital for single individuals to achieve peace of mind and [[financial_stability_and_retirement_income | financial stability]] throughout their retirement <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:14:03">[00:14:03]</a>.
