## Key Factors for Moving Abroad
When considering a move abroad, it's crucial to prioritize what truly matters to you <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>. Here are common factors people consider:

*   **Healthcare**: Access to good doctors and hospitals, and health insurance coverage <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:01">[00:01:01]</a>.
*   **Cost of Living**: Understanding expenses for housing, groceries, and daily living <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>.
*   **Safety**: Crime rates, political stability, and concerns about natural disasters <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a>.
*   **Job Market**: Crucial for those job hunting or planning to work remotely <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:24">[00:01:24]</a>.
*   **Visa and Citizenship**: Ease of obtaining and maintaining a visa, and the citizenship process <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>.
*   **Taxes**: What you'll owe on income and property <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:42">[00:01:42]</a>.
*   **Weather**: Canadians often prefer warmer, sunnier climates <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:47">[00:01:47]</a>.

It's recommended to pick your top three to five factors and use them to evaluate potential countries <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:06">[00:02:06]</a>.

## Top Countries for Canadians to Consider Moving Abroad
Here are some of the [[top_countries_for_canadian_retirees | top countries]] for Canadians to consider, often offering a lower [[cost_of_living_comparison_for_canadian_retirees_abroad | cost of living]] compared to Canada <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:29">[00:02:29]</a>:

### Thailand
Thailand offers affordable living, excellent healthcare, and a vibrant culture <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:00">[00:03:00]</a>.
*   **Healthcare**: Top-notch medical and dental care available in cities like Bangkok and Chiang Mai <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:08">[00:03:08]</a>.
*   **Cost of Living**: Significantly lower, allowing for a comfortable lifestyle on a modest budget <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:13">[00:03:13]</a>.
*   **Visas**: Special visa for those over 50, the convenient (but expensive) Thai Elite Visa, and a new digital nomad visa allowing up to 5 years of stay with renewals every 6 months <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:20">[00:03:20]</a>.
*   **Safety**: Generally safe, though occasional political unrest and natural disasters like tsunamis and floods occur <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:38">[00:03:38]</a>.
*   **Other**: A significant time difference can make staying in touch with Canada challenging <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:46">[00:03:46]</a>.

*   **Cost of Living**: Allows for a comfortable lifestyle, particularly outside major cities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:58">[00:03:58]</a>.
