## Country-Specific Visa Options for Canadians

This section outlines various visa and residency options for Canadians considering different countries.

### Thailand
Thailand offers a great visa situation for Canadians <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:19:00">[03:19:00]</a>:
*   **Special Visa for those over 50:** This visa makes it very easy to stay in Thailand <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:23:00">[03:23:00]</a>.
*   **Thai Elite Visa:** While convenient, this option can be expensive <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:27:00">[03:27:00]</a>.
*   **Digital Nomad Visa:** A newer option that is easy to qualify for, allowing stays of up to 5 years with renewals every 6 months <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:31:00">[03:31:00]</a>.

### Portugal
Portugal provides relatively straightforward visa options for Canadians <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:10:00">[04:10:00]</a>. This includes the popular **Golden Visa**, which is available for investors <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:12:00">[04:12:00]</a>.

### Mexico
Visa options in Mexico are straightforward <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:42:00">[04:42:00]</a>. Mexico's proximity to Canada also makes it easy to stay connected with family <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:46:00">[04:46:00]</a>.

### Vietnam
Visas in Vietnam are generally easy to get, but they do require regular renewals, which can be a minor hassle <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:40:00">[05:40:00]</a>.

### Malaysia
Malaysia makes long-term stays easy through its **Malaysia My Second Home (MM2H) Visa program** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:38:00">[06:38:00]</a>. Additionally, a new **Digital Nomad Visa** is available <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:44:00">[06:44:00]</a>.

### Panama
Panama offers easy residency through visa options like the **Friendly Nations Visa** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:22:00">[07:22:00]</a>. A significant financial perk for many is that you are only taxed on income earned within Panama <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:26:00">[07:26:00]</a>.

### Dubai (UAE)
Dubai provides visa options such as the **Dubai Golden Visa** for investors and professionals <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:05:00">[08:05:00]</a>.

### Serbia
Visas are generally easy to obtain in Serbia <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:48:00">[08:48:00]</a>.

### Montenegro
Residency and citizenship are straightforward in Montenegro, particularly through property investment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:28:00">[09:28:00]</a>.

### Costa Rica
Residency in Costa Rica is attainable through programs like the popular **Pensionado program** for retirees <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:10:00">[10:10:00]</a>.

### Colombia
Residency in Colombia is easy to obtain, especially through investment or retirement visas <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:43:00">[10:43:00]</a>.
