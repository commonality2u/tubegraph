## [[strategies_to_increase_personal_income | Strategies to Increase Income in Canada]]

Understanding the correlation between income and [[importance_of_net_worth_over_income_for_financial_freedom | net worth]] can guide smarter [[strategies_for_increasing_personal_net_worth_using_data | financial decisions]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:44">[00:05:44]</a>. 1.  **Location Matters**: Relocating to a province with higher average wages can boost income and [[importance_of_net_worth_over_income_for_financial_freedom | net worth]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:50">[00:05:50]</a>. 2.  **Invest in Education**: Higher education significantly boosts earning potential in Canada <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:06">[00:06:06]</a>.
    *   A bachelor's degree yields a median income of approximately $69,000 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:10">[00:06:10]</a>.
    *   A college diploma brings in about $47,000 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:17">[00:06:17]</a>.
    *   A high school diploma results in about $35,000 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:19">[00:06:19]</a>.
    *   No certificate or diploma results in only about $26,000 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:22">[00:06:22]</a>.
    Investing in education, especially in high-demand fields, can substantially increase income <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:27">[00:06:27]</a>.
3.  **Choose High-Income Careers**: Specialized industries typically offer higher wages <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:32">[00:06:32]</a>:
    *   Senior Management: Pays on average about $91 per hour <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:38">[00:06:38]</a>.
    *   Engineering: Close to $50 per hour <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:43">[00:06:43]</a>.
    *   Finance, Health, and Science Roles: Earn about $42 to $44 hourly <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:46">[00:06:46]</a>.
    *   Trades and Transports: Offer solid wages at about $32 per hour <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:51">[00:06:51]</a>.
4.  **Start a Business**: Many of Canada's wealthiest individuals built their fortunes by starting businesses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:57">[00:06:57]</a>. Business ownership provides the potential for significant wealth through growth, profits, and eventual sale, making it a powerful pathway to financial success <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:02">[00:07:02]</a>.

Increasing income directly enhances the ability to save more, which in turn accelerates the power of [[impact_of_compounding_returns_on_net_worth_growth | compounding returns]] and helps achieve financial milestones faster <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:44">[00:07:44]</a>.
