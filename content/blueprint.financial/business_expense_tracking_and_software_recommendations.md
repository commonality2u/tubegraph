Tracking business expenses is crucial for small business owners in Canada, especially during tax season, to ensure that all eligible deductions are claimed and to avoid issues with the CRA <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:38">[00:05:38]</a>.

## Why Track Business Expenses?

Accurate expense tracking can help maximize your tax deductions, allowing you to keep more of your hard-earned cash <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:23">[00:00:23]</a>. It can also provide insights into specific areas, such as your marketing return on investment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:17">[00:05:17]</a>. Without careful tracking, you might feel like you're leaving money on the table when tax season arrives <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:00">[00:00:00]</a>.

Keeping meticulous records is also important for advanced tax strategies, as these often require deeper analysis and more meticulous record-keeping <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:05">[00:09:05]</a>.
