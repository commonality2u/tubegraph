## Country-Specific Visa and Residency Options

### Thailand
Thailand offers several visa options for those looking to relocate:
*   **Special Visa:** Individuals over the age of 50 may qualify for a special visa that simplifies long-term stays <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:21:00">[03:21:00]</a>.
*   **Thai Elite Visa:** This visa offers convenience but comes at a high cost <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:27:00">[03:27:00]</a>.
*   **Digital Nomad Visa:** A newer option that is easy to qualify for, allowing stays of up to 5 years with renewals every 6 months <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:31:00">[03:31:00]</a>.

### Portugal
Visa options in Portugal are generally straightforward <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:10:00">[04:10:00]</a>. One popular choice is the Golden Visa, which is available for investors <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:12:00">[04:12:00]</a>.

### Mexico
Mexico offers straightforward visa options for Canadians <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:42:00">[04:42:00]</a>.

### Vietnam
Visas for Vietnam are relatively easy to obtain, though they do necessitate regular renewals, which can be somewhat inconvenient <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:42:00">[05:42:00]</a>.

### Malaysia
Malaysia provides several programs for long-term stays:
*   **Malaysia My Second Home (MM2H) Program:** This visa program facilitates extended residency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:38:00">[06:38:00]</a>.
*   **Digital Nomad Visa:** A new digital nomad visa is also available <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:44:00">[06:44:00]</a>.

### Panama
Panama makes residency simple through visa options like the Friendly Nations Visa <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:21:00">[07:21:00]</a>. A significant benefit for many is that residents are only taxed on income earned within Panama <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:26:00">[07:26:00]</a>.

### Dubai
Dubai offers visa options such as the Dubai Golden Visa, suitable for investors and professionals <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:05:00">[08:05:00]</a>.

### Serbia
Visas for Serbia are easy to acquire <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:46:00">[08:46:00]</a>.

### Montenegro
Residency and citizenship in Montenegro are straightforward, especially for those who invest in property <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:26:00">[09:26:00]</a>.

### Costa Rica
Residency in Costa Rica is attainable through programs like the popular *pensionado* program, which is designed for retirees <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:10:00">[10:10:00]</a>.

### Colombia
Residency in Colombia is generally easy to obtain, particularly through investment or retirement visas <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:43:00">[10:43:00]</a>.
