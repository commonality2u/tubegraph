## [[importance_of_tracking_net_worth | Importance of Tracking Net Worth]]

[[importance_of_tracking_net_worth | Tracking your net worth]] is akin to stepping on a scale after holiday overindulgence; you might not like the immediate result, but it is essential <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:23">[00:00:23]</a>. It serves as your "financial fitness check" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:32">[00:00:32]</a>. Year-over-year tracking provides clarity on whether you are progressing towards your financial goals or moving away from them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:35">[00:00:35]</a>. Understanding your exact financial standing empowers you to refocus and make informed decisions for the year ahead <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:43">[00:00:43]</a>.

## How to Calculate Your Net Worth

Calculating your net worth is straightforward <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:54">[00:00:54]</a>:
1.  **List all your assets** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:58">[00:00:58]</a>: This includes cash, investments, real estate, and any other valuable holdings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:59">[00:00:59]</a>.
2.  **Subtract your liabilities** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:04">[00:01:04]</a>: This encompasses mortgages, loans, and credit card debt <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:06">[00:01:06]</a>.

You should [[alternative_tools_for_tracking_net_worth_in_canada | use a method that works best for you]] for tracking, whether it's a budgeting app, a notebook, or a spreadsheet <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>.

A spreadsheet allows you to categorize your finances, track trends over time, and gain a deeper understanding of your financial situation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:18">[00:01:18]</a>. ## Responding to Net Worth Changes

*   **If your net worth grew**: Take time to celebrate your achievements, such as paying down debt, increasing savings, growing investments, or taking a trip <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:36">[00:01:36]</a>. Equally important is to understand *why* it grew, allowing you to replicate those successes in the future <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:47">[00:01:47]</a>.
*   **If your net worth didn't grow**: Use this as an opportunity to reflect on what went wrong and make a plan to course-correct <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:52">[00:01:52]</a>. Every step forward is progress, and knowing your current standing is the initial step towards improvement <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:58">[00:01:58]</a>.
