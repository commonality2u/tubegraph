The Canada Pension Plan (CPP) is structured to ensure its viability for future generations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:06">[00:06:06]</a>. A frequently asked question is whether the CPP will eventually run out of funds and cease payments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:02">[00:06:02]</a>.

> [!INFO] Designed for Sustainability
> The CPP is specifically designed to be [[cpp_management_and_sustainability | sustainable]] over the long term <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:06">[00:06:06]</a>. A recent report indicated that the plan will remain sustainable for the next 75 years <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:09">[00:06:09]</a>.
