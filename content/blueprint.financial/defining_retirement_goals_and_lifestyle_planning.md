## Defining Your Retirement Goals
The second essential step before retiring is to define your retirement goals <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:49">[00:01:49]</a>. This involves envisioning what your "dream retirement" looks like <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:52">[00:01:52]</a>. For example, Pam and Jim Helper aimed to maintain a comfortable lifestyle, incorporating plans for travel and various activities with their family <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:54">[00:01:54]</a>.

## Housing Decisions
A significant part of defining retirement goals involves making decisions about housing <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:03">[00:02:03]</a>:
*   **Staying in Your Current Home**
    *   If you plan to stay in your home, you need to factor in maintenance costs, potential home modifications, and real estate taxes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:06">[00:02:06]</a>. This allows for more accurate budgeting in later years <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:16">[00:02:16]</a>.
*   **Downsizing**
    *   Moving to a smaller home can free up equity, reduce maintenance costs, and lower property taxes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:21">[00:02:21]</a>.
*   **Relocating**
    *   If you intend to move closer to family or to a more affordable location, you should account for new living costs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:28">[00:02:28]</a>.

Regardless of whether you stay put or move, factoring in housing costs is critical for creating a realistic retirement budget <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:45">[00:02:45]</a>.

## Calculating Retirement Expenses
The third step is to calculate your retirement expenses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:51">[00:02:51]</a>. Pam and Jim set their annual retirement spending at $90,000, which covered basic living costs and discretionary spending for travel and hobbies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:56">[00:02:56]</a>. Planning for these expenses is crucial for a smooth retirement <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:04">[00:03:04]</a>. It's often a wise strategy to overestimate expenses when budgeting <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:09">[00:03:09]</a>. Regularly reviewing these expenses against [[inflation_and_retirement_planning | inflation]] and potential unexpected costs ensures long-term financial preparedness <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:33">[00:03:33]</a>. This step is considered very crucial for your [[financial_planning_for_retirement | retirement planning]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:11">[00:04:11]</a>.

## Lifestyle Planning Beyond Finances
While managing finances is a significant part of retirement, it's equally important to plan for how you will live your life <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:12">[00:08:12]</a>.

### Create a Bucket List
Creating a bucket list is a highly recommended step where you can dream big <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:27">[00:08:27]</a>. It serves as an opportunity to prioritize experiences and goals that you've always wanted to pursue but lacked the time for <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:35">[00:08:35]</a>. Examples include visiting national parks, learning an instrument, writing a memoir, starting a community garden, or volunteering <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:44">[00:09:03]</a>. Writing these ideas down makes them more tangible and provides motivation and something to anticipate in retirement <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:05">[00:09:05]</a>.

### Prioritize Health and Regular Checkups
Your health is the foundation of a happy retirement, as "without it, money means nothing" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:08">[00:10:08]</a>. Make it a priority to:
*   Schedule annual checkups with your doctor to monitor vital signs like blood pressure and cholesterol <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:16">[00:10:16]</a>.
*   Stay up-to-date with essential cancer screenings for early detection <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:22">[00:10:22]</a>.
*   Not overlook dental and vision appointments, as maintaining these can significantly enhance your quality of life <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:28">[00:10:28]</a>.
*   Work closely with your healthcare team to effectively manage any chronic conditions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:33">[00:10:33]</a>.

Being proactive about your health by exercising, eating well, and sleeping adequately will help you enjoy a full and worry-free retirement <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:39">[00:10:39]</a>.
