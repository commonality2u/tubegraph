## Setting Annual Spending Expenses

Jim and Pam, for example, set their annual retirement spending expenses at $90,000 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:56:00">[02:56:00]</a>. This figure included their basic living costs and discretionary spending for activities like travel and hobbies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:59:00">[02:59:00]</a>.

It can be a wise move to overestimate your expenses, as it's always beneficial to [[planning_a_sustainable_retirement_budget | budget]] for more than you anticipate spending <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:09:00">[03:09:00]</a>. Jim and Pam's projected expenses started around $90,000 in 2024 and remained consistent in real dollars as they approached retirement, helping them maintain their desired lifestyle <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:17:00">[03:17:00]</a>.

## Factors to Consider

### Housing Costs
When defining your [[Defining Retirement Goals | retirement goals]], decisions about housing significantly impact your budget <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:03:00">[02:03:00]</a>.

*   **Staying in Your Home**
    If you plan to remain in your current home, like Pam and Jim, you must factor in maintenance costs, potential home modifications, and real estate taxes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:06:00">[02:06:00]</a>. This allows for more accurate budgeting in later years <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:16:00">[02:16:00]</a>.

*   **Downsizing**
    Moving to a smaller home can free up equity, reduce maintenance costs, and lower property taxes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:21:00">[02:21:00]</a>.

*   **Relocating**
    If you intend to move closer to family or to a more affordable location, you should account for new living costs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:28:00">[02:28:00]</a>. Many retirees in Canada find that adjusting their housing situation can significantly impact their [[planning_a_sustainable_retirement_budget | retirement budget]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:37:00">[02:37:00]</a>. Factoring in housing costs, whether you stay put or move, is key to a realistic [[planning_a_sustainable_retirement_budget | retirement budget]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:45:00">[02:45:00]</a>.

### Inflation and Unexpected Costs
Consistently reviewing expenses against inflation and potential unexpected costs ensures you are financially prepared for the long term <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:31:00">[03:31:00]</a>.

Whether you're planning a modest or extravagant lifestyle, calculating your annual expenses like Jim and Pam provides a clear picture of your savings needs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:40:00">[03:40:00]</a>. This step is crucial for your [[comprehensive_financial_planning_for_retirement | retirement planning]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:09:00">[04:09:00]</a>.
