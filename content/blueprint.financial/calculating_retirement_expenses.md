A crucial step in planning for a stress-free retirement in Canada is to calculate your anticipated expenses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:51">[00:02:51]</a>. This provides a clear picture of how much you need to save to maintain your desired lifestyle <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:47">[00:03:47]</a>.

## Example: Pam and Jim Helper

Pam and Jim Helper, a couple using financial planning software, serve as a helpful example <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:21">[00:00:21]</a>.

Their approach to calculating expenses:
*   They set their annual retirement spending at $90,000 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:53">[00:02:53]</a>.
*   This figure includes their basic living costs and discretionary spending for travel and hobbies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:57">[00:02:57]</a>.
*   They wisely overestimated their expenses, which is recommended to budget for more than initially planned <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:09">[00:03:09]</a>.
*   Their projected expenses start around $90,000 in 2024 and remain consistent in real dollars as they near retirement <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:16">[00:03:16]</a>. This consistency helps them maintain their lifestyle without fearing they will run out of money <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:27">[00:03:27]</a>.
*   They regularly review their expenses against inflation and potential unexpected costs to ensure long-term financial preparedness <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:31">[00:03:31]</a>.

## Importance of Calculating Expenses

Planning for your expenses is vital for a smooth retirement <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:04">[00:03:04]</a>. Whether you envision a modest or extravagant lifestyle, realistically assessing what you'll spend helps you stay on track for a stress-free retirement <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:41">[00:03:41]</a>.

It is important to note that the example provided is a simplified one <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:56">[00:03:56]</a>. Calculating your actual retirement spending can be complex <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:01">[00:04:01]</a>, and this step is crucial for your overall [[financial_planning_for_retirement_in_canada | retirement planning]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:09">[00:04:09]</a>.
