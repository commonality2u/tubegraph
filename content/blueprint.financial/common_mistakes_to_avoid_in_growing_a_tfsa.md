## [[common_mistakes_with_tfsa_usage | Common Mistakes]] with TFSA Investments

Here are some [[common_mistakes_in_tfsa_investing | common mistakes]] seen in TFSA investing that can be very costly <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:12:00">[06:12:00]</a>:

### [[risks_of_highrisk_investments_in_a_tfsa | Taking on Too Much Risk]]
Some investors aim for extreme growth by chasing speculative investments such as meme stocks or cryptocurrencies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:19:00">[06:19:00]</a>.
> While these can offer massive returns in a short period, they often come with significant [[risks_of_highrisk_investments_in_a_tfsa | risks]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:27:00">[06:27:00]</a>.

Why it's a problem:
*   [[risks_of_highrisk_investments_in_a_tfsa | High-risk investments]] are unpredictable and often result in larger losses rather than sustained growth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:03:00">[07:03:00]</a>.
*   The chances of losing all your money are much higher than achieving consistently high rates of return <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:48:00">[06:48:00]</a>.
*   Recovering from significant losses can take years and derail TFSA growth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:08:00">[07:08:00]</a>.

### Playing it Too Safe
On the other end of the spectrum, some investors stick with low-interest savings accounts, GICs, or even just cash accounts, thinking it's the safest route for their TFSA <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:14:00">[07:14:00]</a>.
> While these options protect your principal, they significantly limit growth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:27:00">[07:27:00]</a>. For example, at a 2% rate of return, $10,000 would take a staggering 66 years to grow to $100,000 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:31:00">[07:31:00]</a>.

Why it's a problem:
*   Low returns barely outpace inflation, meaning purchasing power diminishes over time <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:42:00">[07:42:00]</a>.
*   Investors miss out on the compounding potential of higher growth investments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:49:00">[07:49:00]</a>.

### Trying to Time the Market
Many investors attempt to buy low and sell high, believing they can predict market movements <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:54:00">[07:54:00]</a>.
> While this strategy sounds appealing, it's very difficult to execute consistently <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:02:00">[08:02:00]</a>.

Why it's a problem:
*   Most people miss out on the best performing market days by waiting on the sidelines <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:08:00">[08:08:00]</a>.
*   Even professionals struggle to time the market accurately <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:12:00">[08:12:00]</a>.
*   The cost of getting it wrong can far outweigh the potential benefits <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:15:00">[08:15:00]</a>. Staying invested through ups and downs typically outperforms trying to predict market swings for most retail investors <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:18:00">[08:18:00]</a>.

### Ignoring Fees
High fees, such as those associated with mutual funds, can significantly eat into returns over time <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:26:00">[08:26:00]</a>.

Why it's a problem:
*   A 2% management fee on a $100,000 portfolio can cost tens of thousands of dollars over decades <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:34:00">[08:34:00]</a>.
*   Low-cost options like ETFs offer similar market exposure with significantly lower fees, allowing more of your money to grow <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:42:00">[08:42:00]</a>.

### Not Understanding Your Investments and Risk Tolerance
Before committing to any investment, it's essential to understand how it works and whether it aligns with your risk tolerance and financial goals <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:50:00">[08:50:00]</a>.
> It's easy to be tempted by trendy assets like Bitcoin hitting $100,000 or hot tech stocks like Nvidia, but if you don't fully understand the investment and can't handle the risk, it could derail your progress <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:00:00">[09:00:00]</a>.

Why it's important:
*   Many investors overestimate their ability to handle risk, leading to poor decisions when markets become volatile and investments drop <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:15:00">[09:15:00]</a>.
*   Be honest with yourself and build a portfolio that works with your comfort level <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:24:00">[09:24:00]</a>.
