## Key Factors to Prioritize

When considering [[steps_to_consider_before_moving_abroad | moving abroad]], there are seven common factors people consider <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:59">[00:00:59]</a>:

### Healthcare
It's important to know you'll have access to good doctors and hospitals, and that your health insurance will provide adequate coverage <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:05">[00:01:05]</a>.

### Cost of Living
Understanding what you'll spend on housing, groceries, and daily expenses is crucial to avoid financial stress <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:14">[00:01:14]</a>. ### Safety
Feeling safe is key, so research crime rates, political stability, and the concern for natural disasters <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:24">[00:01:24]</a>.

### Job Market
Whether you are [[ways_to_make_money_while_living_overseas | job hunting]] or planning to [[ways_to_make_money_while_living_overseas | work remotely]], knowing the local job market is crucial <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:28">[00:01:28]</a>.

### Visa and Citizenship
Consider the ease of obtaining and maintaining a [[visa_options_for_retiring_abroad | visa]] in your chosen country, and if long-term plans include citizenship, understand that process <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:39">[00:01:39]</a>.

### Taxes
Investigate the income and property taxes you would owe <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:47">[00:01:47]</a>.

### Weather
Many Canadians prefer a sunnier and warmer climate when [[steps_to_consider_before_moving_abroad | moving abroad]], making climate a significant consideration <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:55">[00:01:55]</a>.
