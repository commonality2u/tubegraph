### Prioritizing Your Needs

No country is perfect <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>, as Voltaire famously said, "Perfect is the enemy of good" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>. When considering [[steps_for_moving_abroad_from_canada | moving abroad]], it's essential to identify what truly matters to you <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>.

Here are seven key factors people often consider:

*   **Healthcare**
    *   Access to good doctors and hospitals <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:03">[00:01:03]</a>.
    *   Adequate health insurance coverage <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:05">[00:01:05]</a>.
*   **Cost of Living**
    *   Understanding expenses for housing, groceries, and daily life <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:09">[00:01:09]</a>.
    *   Ensuring financial comfort and avoiding stress <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:14">[00:01:14]</a>.
*   **Safety**
    *   Evaluating crime rates and political stability <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:19">[00:01:19]</a>.
    *   Considering concerns about natural disasters <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:22">[00:01:22]</a>.
*   **Job Market**
    *   Crucial for those seeking employment or planning to [[making_money_while_living_overseas | work remotely]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:26">[00:01:26]</a>.
*   **Visa and Citizenship**
    *   Ease of obtaining and maintaining a [[visa_and_residency_options_for_expatriates | visa]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>.
    *   Understanding the long-term citizenship process <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>.
*   **Taxes**
    *   Assessing income and property tax obligations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:42">[00:01:42]</a>.
*   **Weather**
    *   Considering the climate, especially for Canadians seeking warmer, sunnier places <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:48">[00:01:48]</a>.

Other factors to consider include proximity to family, language barriers, expat community, internet speed, political stability, and education system <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:00">[00:02:00]</a>. It's recommended to pick your top three to five factors and use them to evaluate potential countries <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:06">[00:02:06]</a>.

*Example:* Homer prioritized healthcare, cost of living, and safety. His research led him to consider Spain, Portugal, and New Zealand, while he steered clear of Brazil, South Africa, and Mexico due to concerns in these areas <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:14">[00:02:14]</a>.
