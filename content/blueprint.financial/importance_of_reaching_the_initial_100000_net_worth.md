As [[strategies_to_increase_net_worth_using_financial_data | wealth]] accumulates, the balance between contributions and [[impact_of_investing_and_compounding_returns_on_net_worth_growth | investment returns]] shifts dramatically <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:55">[00:02:55]</a>:

This process is akin to the launch phase of a rocket: most effort is required to break free from gravity (reaching the first $100,000), but once achieved, the rest of the journey becomes faster and easier <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:42">[00:03:42]</a>.
