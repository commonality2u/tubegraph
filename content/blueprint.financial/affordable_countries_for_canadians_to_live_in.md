## Key Factors When Considering a Move Abroad
When thinking about moving abroad, there is no "perfect" country, and it's essential to prioritize what truly matters to you <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>. Here are common considerations:

*   **Healthcare**: Access to good doctors and hospitals, and adequate health insurance coverage <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:01">[00:01:01]</a>.
*   **Cost of Living**: Understanding expenses for housing, groceries, and daily life to avoid financial stress <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>.
*   **Safety**: Assessment of crime rates, political stability, and natural disaster concerns <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a>.
*   **Job Market**: Crucial for those seeking employment or planning to work remotely <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:24">[00:01:24]</a>.
*   **Visa and Citizenship**: Ease of obtaining and maintaining a visa, and the long-term citizenship process <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>.
*   **Taxes**: What you'll owe on income and property <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:42">[00:01:42]</a>.
*   **Weather**: For Canadians, a sunnier and warmer climate is often a significant draw <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:47">[00:01:47]</a>.

It's recommended to select your top three to five factors and use them to evaluate potential countries <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:06">[00:02:06]</a>. For example, Homer, planning to move abroad, prioritized healthcare, [[cost_of_living_considerations_for_canadian_retirees | cost of living]], and safety. Based on these, he considered Spain, Portugal, and New Zealand due to their strong healthcare systems, lower costs than Canada, and solid safety records <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:14">[00:02:14]</a>. Conversely, he avoided Brazil, South Africa, and Mexico due to inconsistent healthcare, varying [[cost_of_living_considerations_for_canadian_retirees | living costs]], and safety concerns <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:35">[00:02:35]</a>.

Research tools like Nomad List and Numbeo are helpful for comparing places to visit and their associated [[cost_of_living_considerations_for_canadian_retirees | cost of living]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:48">[00:02:48]</a>.

## Top Countries for Canadians to Consider

### Thailand
Thailand is an excellent choice for Canadians, offering affordable living, good healthcare, and a vibrant culture <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:58">[00:02:58]</a>.
*   **Healthcare**: Cities like Bangkok and Chiang Mai provide top-notch medical and dental care <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:10">[00:03:10]</a>.
*   **Cost of Living**: Significantly lower than Canada, allowing for a comfortable lifestyle on a modest budget <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:13">[00:03:13]</a>.
*   **Visas**: A special visa is available for those over 50 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:23">[00:03:23]</a>. The Thai Elite Visa is convenient but expensive <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:27">[00:03:27]</a>. A new Digital Nomad Visa allows up to five years of stay with renewals every six months <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:31">[00:03:31]</a>.
*   **Safety**: Generally safe, but experiences occasional political unrest and natural disasters like tsunamis and floods <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:38">[00:03:38]</a>.
*   **Logistics**: Time difference can make staying in touch with Canada challenging <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:46">[00:03:46]</a>.

### Portugal
Portugal is an appealing option for Canadians, known for its safety and mild climate <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:50">[00:03:50]</a>.
*   **Healthcare**: A strong system, particularly in urban areas <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:53">[00:03:53]</a>.
*   **Cost of Living**: Allows for a comfortable lifestyle, especially outside major cities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:57">[00:03:57]</a>.
*   **Safety**: One of the safest countries in Europe <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:04">[00:04:04]</a>.
*   **Visas**: Relatively straightforward options, including the popular Golden Visa for investors <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:08">[00:04:08]</a>.
*   **Weather**: Mild with warm summers and mild winters, ideal for escaping Canadian cold <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:15">[00:04:15]</a>.

*   **Safety**: While a concern, choosing the right area can mitigate risks, with some neighborhoods feeling as safe or safer than many in Canada <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:52">[00:04:52]</a>.
