When considering a move abroad, finances are a critical component that can determine the success of your dream <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>. Nearly 40% of new Canadians are contemplating leaving their province or the country due to skyrocketing living expenses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:05">[00:00:05]</a>.

## Key Financial Considerations

To avoid financial stress, it is essential to prioritize and research specific financial aspects of any potential new country <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:14">[00:01:14]</a>:

*   **Cost of Living**
    Understanding what you will spend on housing, groceries, and daily living expenses is crucial <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>. Tools like Nomad List and Numbeo can help research the cost of living <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:48">[00:02:48]</a>.
*   **Taxes**
    It is important to investigate what you will owe on income and property in the new country <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:42">[00:01:42]</a>.
*   **Healthcare Costs**
    Assess the cost of healthcare services and whether your health insurance will provide sufficient coverage <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:01">[00:01:01]</a>. Many countries offer more affordable or comparable healthcare options than Canada <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:31">[00:04:31]</a>.
*   **Job Market**
    Whether seeking employment or planning remote work, understanding the local job market is crucial <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:25">[00:01:25]</a>.
*   **Visa and Citizenship Expenses**
    Consider the ease and cost of obtaining and maintaining a visa, as well as the long-term citizenship process if applicable <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>.

## Country-Specific Financial Overviews

### Thailand
Thailand offers affordable living and a much lower overall cost of living, allowing for a comfortable lifestyle on a modest budget <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:02">[00:03:02]</a>. Healthcare facilities in cities like Bangkok and Chiang Mai provide top-notch medical and dental care <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:10">[00:03:10]</a>. While the Thai Elite Visa is convenient, it can be expensive <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:27">[00:03:27]</a>.

### Portugal
Portugal has a strong healthcare system, especially in urban areas <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:55">[00:03:55]</a>. The cost of living supports a comfortable lifestyle, particularly outside major cities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:58">[00:03:58]</a>. Visa options include the Golden Visa for investors <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:12">[00:04:12]</a>.

### Mexico
Mexico is known for its affordability and culturally rich lifestyle <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:24">[00:04:24]</a>. Healthcare, particularly dental work, is excellent and much more affordable than in Canada <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:29">[00:04:29]</a>. The cost of living is significantly lower, with housing, food, and transportation being budget-friendly <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:35">[00:04:35]</a>.

### Vietnam
Vietnam is an excellent choice for an affordable lifestyle with incredibly low living costs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:16">[00:05:16]</a>. Visas are relatively easy to acquire but require regular renewals <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:40">[00:05:40]</a>.

### Malaysia
Malaysia is an emerging choice offering affordability and modern amenities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:10">[00:06:10]</a>. Healthcare is top-notch, especially in Kuala Lumpur and Penang, with many English-speaking doctors <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:17">[00:06:17]</a>. The low cost of living allows for a comfortable lifestyle <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:30">[00:06:30]</a>. The Malaysia My Second Home (MM2H) visa program and a new digital nomad visa make long-term stays easy <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:38">[00:06:38]</a>.

### Panama
Panama is affordable and offers a high quality of life <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:05">[00:07:05]</a>. Living costs are reasonable <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:15">[00:07:15]</a>. Visa options like the Friendly Nations Visa simplify residency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:21">[00:07:21]</a>. A significant financial draw is that you are only taxed on income earned within Panama <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:26">[00:07:26]</a>.

### Dubai
Dubai is ideal for those seeking a modern lifestyle with financial perks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:44">[00:07:44]</a>. While living costs can be high, especially for housing, the very low income tax helps balance it out <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:00">[00:08:00]</a>. Visa options include the Dubai Golden Visa for investors and professionals <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:05">[00:08:05]</a>. There is also an amazing job market <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:53">[00:07:53]</a>.

### Serbia
Serbia is a great choice for affordability <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:31">[00:08:31]</a>. The cost of living is low, with cheap housing, food, and transport <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:40">[00:08:40]</a>. Healthcare is decent, with many private options preferred by expats <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:36">[00:08:36]</a>. Visas are easy to obtain <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:47">[00:08:47]</a>.

### Montenegro
Montenegro offers a low cost of living, much lower than in Canada, especially outside of tourist areas <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:15">[00:09:15]</a>. Residency and citizenship are straightforward, particularly through property investment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:28">[00:09:28]</a>. Private healthcare is often preferred by expats <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:20">[00:09:20]</a>.

### Costa Rica
Costa Rica has a moderate cost of living, with affordable housing, especially outside of tourist hotspots <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:06">[00:10:06]</a>. Healthcare is well-regarded and affordable, with both public and private options <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:59">[00:10:01]</a>. Residency is attainable through the popular pensionado program for retirees <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:10">[00:10:10]</a>.

### Colombia
Colombia offers very low living costs, allowing for a comfortable lifestyle on a modest budget <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:36">[00:10:38]</a>. Residency is also easy to obtain, particularly through investment or retirement visas <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:43">[00:10:46]</a>.
