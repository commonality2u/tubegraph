## Advanced Tax Write-offs for International Operations

When expanding or maintaining foreign operations, several expenses can often be deducted <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:27"></a>. These include:
*   Travel expenses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:31"></a>
*   Legal fees <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:31"></a>
*   International marketing costs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:33"></a>

### Navigating Double Taxation

If your business earns income in another country, it might be subject to taxes in both that country and Canada <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:35"></a>. To prevent double taxation, tax treaties often provide mechanisms like tax credits or exemptions for taxes paid abroad <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:43"></a>. A detailed understanding of these treaties is very important <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:51"></a>.
