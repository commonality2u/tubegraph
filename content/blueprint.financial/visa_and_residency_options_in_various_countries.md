## Country-Specific Visa and Residency Options

### Thailand
Thailand offers a great visa situation, especially for certain demographics and work types <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:19:00">[03:19:00]</a>:
*   **Age 50+ Visa**: Individuals over the age of 50 can qualify for a special visa that facilitates extended stays <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:23:00">[03:23:00]</a>.
*   **Thai Elite Visa**: This option is convenient but can be expensive <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:27:00">[03:27:00]</a>.
*   **[[becoming_a_digital_nomad | Digital Nomad Visa]]**: A new visa category is available for [[becoming_a_digital_nomad | digital nomads]], easy to qualify for, and allows stays of up to five years with renewals every six months <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:31:00">[03:31:00]</a>.

### Portugal
Visa options in Portugal are relatively straightforward <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:10:00">[04:10:00]</a>. The popular Golden Visa program is available for investors <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:12:00">[04:12:00]</a>.

### Mexico
Mexico provides straightforward visa options <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:42:00">[04:42:00]</a>. Its proximity to Canada also simplifies staying connected with family <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:46:00">[04:46:00]</a>.

### Vietnam
Visas for Vietnam are generally easy to obtain, though they require regular renewals, which can be a minor inconvenience <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:40:00">[05:40:00]</a>.

### Malaysia
Malaysia offers programs that simplify long-term stays <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:38:00">[06:38:00]</a>:
