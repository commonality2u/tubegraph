## Importance of Defining Goals

Defining your retirement goals is a key part of [[importance_of_retirement_planning | retirement planning]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:49:10">[01:49:10]</a>. It sets the stage for [[retirement_planning_and_financial_strategies | financial strategies]] and ensures you can maintain your desired lifestyle <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:53:08">[01:53:08]</a>.

## Key Aspects of Retirement Goals

### Lifestyle
For individuals like Pam and Jim, a primary goal was to maintain a comfortable lifestyle, including plans for travel and other activities with their family <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:53:08">[01:53:08]</a>. This includes accounting for both basic living costs and discretionary spending on hobbies and travel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:00:00">[03:00:00]</a>.

### Housing Decisions
A significant part of defining retirement goals involves decisions about housing <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:01:03">[02:01:03]</a>. Factoring in housing costs is essential for creating a realistic [[planning_a_sustainable_retirement_budget | retirement budget]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:45:00">[02:45:00]</a>.

Considerations include:
*   **Staying in your current home** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:03:07">[02:03:07]</a>: If planning to stay, factor in maintenance costs, potential home modifications, and real estate taxes to [[planning_a_sustainable_retirement_budget | budget]] accurately <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:08:00">[02:08:00]</a>.
*   **Downsizing** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:18:00">[02:18:00]</a>: Moving to a smaller home can free up equity, reduce maintenance costs, and lower property taxes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:21:00">[02:21:00]</a>.
*   **Relocating** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:25:00">[02:25:00]</a>: If moving closer to family or to a more affordable location, new living costs must be accounted for <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:28:00">[02:28:00]</a>. Many retirees in Canada find that adjusting their housing situation can significantly impact their [[planning_a_sustainable_retirement_budget | retirement budget]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:37:00">[02:37:00]</a>.

By clearly defining what you want your retirement to look like, you lay the groundwork for [[comprehensive_financial_planning_for_retirement | comprehensive financial planning]] and help ensure a stress-free future <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:51:00">[03:51:00]</a>.
