## [[factors_to_consider_when_moving_abroad | Factors to Consider When Moving Abroad]]

No country is perfect <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>, so when thinking about moving overseas, it's essential to prioritize what truly matters to you <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>. Here are key [[factors_to_consider_when_moving_abroad | factors often considered]] by those moving abroad:

*   **Healthcare**: Access to good doctors and hospitals, and reliable health insurance coverage <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:01">[00:01:01]</a>.
*   **[[cost_of_living_comparison_in_canada_and_abroad | Cost of Living]]**: Understanding expenses for housing, groceries, and daily life to avoid financial stress <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>.
*   **Safety**: Evaluating crime rates, political stability, and concerns about natural disasters <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a>.
*   **Job Market**: Crucial for those job hunting or planning to work remotely <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:24">[00:01:24]</a>.
*   **Visa and Citizenship**: Ease of obtaining and maintaining a visa, and long-term citizenship processes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>.
*   **Taxes**: Understanding income and property tax obligations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:42">[00:01:42]</a>.
*   **Weather**: For Canadians, a warmer, sunnier climate is often a significant draw <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:47">[00:01:47]</a>.

While many other factors exist, it's recommended to pick your top three to five priorities to evaluate potential countries <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:06">[00:02:06]</a>.
