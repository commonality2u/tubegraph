## Key Factors When Considering a Move Abroad

When exploring the possibility of [[steps_to_consider_before_moving_abroad | moving abroad]], it's essential to prioritize what truly matters to you, as no country is perfect <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>. Key factors often considered include:

*   **Healthcare:** Access to good doctors and hospitals, and sufficient health insurance coverage <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:01">[00:01:01]</a>.
*   **Cost of Living:** Understanding expenses for housing, groceries, and daily life to avoid financial stress <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>.
*   **Safety:** Assessing crime rates, political stability, and natural disaster concerns <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a>.
*   **Job Market:** Crucial for those job hunting or planning to [[ways_to_make_money_while_living_overseas | work remotely]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:24">[00:01:24]</a>.
*   **Visa and Citizenship:** How easy it is to obtain and maintain a visa, and the long-term citizenship process <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>.
*   **Taxes:** Understanding income and property tax obligations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:42">[00:01:42]</a>.
*   **Weather:** The climate is a significant consideration, especially for Canadians seeking warmer environments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:47">[00:01:47]</a>.

It's recommended to pick your top three to five priorities to evaluate potential countries <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:06">[00:02:06]</a>.

## Countries and Their Visa Options for Canadians

This list, informed by research using Nomad List and Numbeo <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:48">[00:02:48]</a>, highlights 11 top countries and their visa considerations for Canadians.

### Thailand
Thailand offers affordable living, excellent healthcare, and a vibrant culture <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:00">[00:03:00]</a>.
*   **Special Visa (over 50):** Available for individuals aged 50 and above, making it easy to stay <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:21">[00:03:21]</a>.
*   **Thai Elite Visa:** Convenient but can be expensive <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:27">[00:03:27]</a>.
*   **Digital Nomad Visa:** Easy to qualify for, allowing stays up to 5 years with renewals every 6 months <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:31">[00:03:31]</a>.

### Portugal
Portugal is appealing due to its strong healthcare system, reasonable [[cost_of_living_comparison_for_canadian_retirees_abroad | cost of living]], and high safety <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:53">[00:03:53]</a>.
*   **Golden Visa:** A popular option for investors, making residency relatively straightforward <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:10">[00:04:10]</a>.

### Mexico
Mexico provides an affordable, culturally rich lifestyle close to Canada <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:24">[00:04:24]</a>.
*   **Visa options:** Straightforward for Canadians <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:42">[00:04:42]</a>. Proximity to Canada is beneficial for staying connected and for remote workers needing Canadian time zones <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:46">[00:04:46]</a>.

### Vietnam
Vietnam is an excellent choice for an affordable lifestyle and rich cultural experiences, with an incredibly low [[cost_of_living_comparison_for_canadian_retirees_abroad | cost of living]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:16">[00:05:16]</a>.
*   **Visas:** Pretty easy to get, but require regular renewals, which can be a hassle <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:40">[00:05:40]</a>.

### Malaysia
Malaysia is an up-and-coming choice, offering affordability, modern amenities, and a rich culture <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:10">[00:06:10]</a>.
*   **Malaysia My Second Home (MM2H) VISA program:** Makes long-term stays easy <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:38">[00:06:38]</a>.
*   **Digital Nomad Visa:** Also available <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:44">[00:06:44]</a>.

### Panama
Panama is affordable, easy to settle into, and offers a high quality of life <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:03">[00:07:03]</a>.
*   **Friendly Nations Visa:** Makes residency simple <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:21">[00:07:21]</a>.
*   **Taxation:** Only income earned in Panama is taxed, which is a major draw <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:26">[00:07:26]</a>.

### Dubai
Dubai is ideal for Canadians seeking a modern lifestyle with financial perks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:42">[00:07:42]</a>.
*   **Dubai Golden Visa:** Available for investors and professionals <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:03">[00:08:03]</a>.
*   **Taxation:** Very low income tax helps balance the high cost of living <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:58">[00:07:58]</a>.

### Serbia
Serbia is a great choice for affordability and cultural experiences <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:30">[00:08:30]</a>.
*   **Visas:** Easy to get <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:47">[00:08:47]</a>.

### Montenegro
Montenegro is a hidden gem with stunning natural beauty, a relaxed lifestyle, and a low [[cost_of_living_comparison_for_canadian_retirees_abroad | cost of living]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:10">[00:09:10]</a>.
*   **Residency and Citizenship:** Straightforward, particularly through property investment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:27">[00:09:27]</a>.

### Costa Rica
Costa Rica is a top pick for warm weather, natural beauty, and a strong expat community <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:52">[00:09:52]</a>.
*   **Pensionado Program:** Residency is attainable through this popular program for retirees <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:10">[00:10:10]</a>.

### Colombia
Colombia is a vibrant option offering culture, low [[cost_of_living_comparison_for_canadian_retirees_abroad | living costs]], and stunning landscapes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:31">[00:10:31]</a>.
*   **Residency:** Easy to obtain, particularly through investment or retirement visas <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:43">[00:10:43]</a>.
*   **Safety:** While improved, safety is a primary concern, and careful location choice is advised <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:51">[00:10:51]</a>.
