## The Financial Impact of Health Care Costs

Health care costs can accumulate rapidly for single retirees, often leading to higher long-term care expenses compared to couples <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:51">[00:06:51]</a>.

Typical costs in Canada include:
*   **Home Care** ranges from $25 to $50 per hour <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:57">[00:06:57]</a>.
*   **Assisted Living** can range from $3,000 to $5,000 per month <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:05">[00:07:05]</a>.
*   **Government-subsidized Nursing Homes** may cost between $2,000 to $3,000 per month <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:09">[00:07:09]</a>.
*   **Private Care** can exceed $5,000 per month <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:13">[00:07:13]</a>.

## Strategies for Planning Health Costs

[[retirement_planning_strategies_for_single_retirees | Smart planning]] is essential to manage these significant expenses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:16">[00:07:16]</a>:

*   **Budget for Care** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:18">[00:07:18]</a>: Set aside a reserve fund specifically for care or consider long-term care insurance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:20">[00:07:20]</a>.
*   **Assign a Power of Attorney** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:22">[00:07:22]</a>: Designate a trusted individual to make medical and financial decisions on your behalf if necessary <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:25">[00:07:25]</a>.
*   **Consider Disability and Critical Illness Insurance** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:31">[00:07:31]</a>: These policies can provide coverage for major medical costs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:32">[00:07:32]</a>.
*   **Explore [[exploring_alternative_retirement_living_arrangements | Co-Living Arrangements]]** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:42">[00:04:42]</a>: Options like senior co-housing or shared housing can help cut fixed costs and provide social support <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:17">[00:06:17]</a>.
