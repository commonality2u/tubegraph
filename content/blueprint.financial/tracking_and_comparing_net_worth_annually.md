The start of a new year is an ideal time to assess and improve your financial health <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:04">[00:00:04]</a>. A crucial first step is to compare your net worth from the beginning of the previous year to its end <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:18">[00:00:18]</a>.

## Why Tracking Your Net Worth is Important

[[benefits_of_tracking_your_net_worth | Tracking your net worth]] serves as a financial fitness check, similar to stepping on a scale to understand your current state <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:23">[00:00:23]</a>. Year-over-year tracking reveals whether you are progressing towards your financial goals or drifting away from them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:35">[00:00:35]</a>. Knowing if your net worth grew, shrank, or remained stable empowers you to refocus and make [[strategies_for_increasing_personal_net_worth_using_financial_data | informed decisions]] for the upcoming year <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:40">[00:00:40]</a>.

## [[steps_to_set_up_a_net_worth_tracking_system | How to Calculate Your Net Worth]]

Calculating your net worth is a straightforward process <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:54">[00:00:54]</a>:
1.  **List all assets**: Include cash, investments, real estate, and any other valuable holdings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:58">[00:00:58]</a>.
2.  **Subtract liabilities**: This includes mortgages, loans, and credit card debt <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:04">[00:01:04]</a>.

## Methods for Tracking

For tracking your net worth, use a method that suits you best <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:08">[00:01:08]</a>. [[alternative_tools_for_tracking_net_worth | Options]] include budgeting apps, notebooks, or spreadsheets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:12">[00:01:12]</a>. Spreadsheets are often recommended due to their flexibility and customization, allowing you to categorize finances, track trends, and gain a deeper understanding of your financial situation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a>.

## Motivation and Action Based on Comparison

*   **If your net worth grew**: Celebrate your achievements, whether from paying down debt, increasing savings, or growing investments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:36">[00:01:36]</a>. Crucially, take time to understand *why* it grew so you can replicate those successes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:47">[00:01:47]</a>.
*   **If your net worth didn't grow**: Use this as an opportunity to reflect on what went wrong and create a plan for course correction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:52">[00:01:52]</a>. Recognizing your current standing is the first step towards improvement <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:59">[00:01:59]</a>.
