## Why Track Your Net Worth?
[[importance_of_tracking_net_worth | Tracking your net worth]] serves as a crucial financial fitness check, similar to stepping on a scale after the holidays <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:24">[00:00:24]</a>. This year-over-year comparison reveals whether you are progressing towards your financial goals or moving away from them <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:35">[00:00:35]</a>. Understanding if your net worth grew, shrank, or remained the same provides the clarity needed to refocus and make [[benefits_of_tracking_financial_trends_and_making_decisions | informed decisions]] for the upcoming year <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:40">[00:00:40]</a>. This analysis is often the first financial task undertaken at the start of a new year <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>.

## How to Calculate Net Worth
[[steps_to_update_and_maintain_a_net_worth_tracker | Calculating your net worth]] is a straightforward process:
1.  **List all assets:** Include cash, investments, real estate, and any other valuable holdings you possess <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:58">[00:00:58]</a>.
2.  **Subtract liabilities:** Deduct all your debts, such as mortgages, loans, and credit card balances <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:04">[00:01:04]</a>.

## Motivation and Action Based on Your Net Worth
**If your net worth grew:** Take time to celebrate your achievements, whether it's paying down debt, increasing savings, or growing investments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:38">[00:01:38]</a>. Crucially, analyze *why* it grew so you can replicate those successes in the future <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:48">[00:01:48]</a>.

**If your net worth did not grow or shrank:** Use this as an opportunity for reflection <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:52">[00:01:52]</a>. Understand what went wrong and develop a plan for course correction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:54">[00:01:54]</a>. Recognizing your current financial standing is the essential first step toward improvement <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:59">[00:01:59]</a>.
