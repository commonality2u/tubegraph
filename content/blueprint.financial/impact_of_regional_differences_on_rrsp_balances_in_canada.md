A 2023 survey provides insights into how different regions across Canada manage their Registered Retirement Savings Plans (RRSPs), indicating that provinces approach RRSP management in varied ways <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:05">[00:04:05]</a>.

## Regional Breakdown of RRSP Holdings

The survey highlights significant regional disparities in RRSP account balances and contributions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:11">[00:04:11]</a>:

*   **Ontario** leads with the highest average RRSP balance, reported at $162,900 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:22">[00:04:22]</a>.
*   **Atlantic Canada** stands out for having the highest average contribution, at $8,155 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:58">[00:04:58]</a>.

Despite these regional differences, Canadians nationwide remain engaged with their RRSPs, focusing on either accumulating larger account balances or making consistent contributions year over year <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:05">[00:05:05]</a>.
