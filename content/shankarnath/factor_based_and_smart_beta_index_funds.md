[[Strategy Indices and FactorBased Investing | Factor-based index funds]], also known as [[Advantages of Smart Beta Funds | smart beta index funds]], are a type of investment fund that allocates capital to stocks based on specific criteria or attributes, rather than solely on [[market_capitalization_based_index_funds | market capitalization]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:39">[00:09:39]</a>. While traditional indices often use [[market_capitalization_based_index_funds | market capitalization]] as a primary criterion, [[Ways to Invest in FactorBased Strategies | factor funds]] are designed around other variables <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:46">[00:09:46]</a>.

## Defining Attributes
These funds identify attractively valued companies for an index portfolio using a scientific combination of financial attributes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:46">[00:09:46]</a>. Key attributes include:
*   P/B ratio <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:54">[00:09:54]</a>
*   P/E ratio <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:57">[00:09:57]</a>
*   Dividend yield <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:57">[00:09:57]</a>
*   Book value <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:57">[00:09:57]</a>
*   Cash flow <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:59">[00:09:59]</a>
*   Sales <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:59">[00:09:59]</a>

## Key Factors
The most significant factors utilized in these funds include:
*   **Value** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:13">[00:10:13]</a>: Often captured by metrics like the P/B ratio, P/E ratio, and dividend yield <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:15">[00:10:15]</a>.
*   **Momentum** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:17">[00:10:17]</a>: Based entirely on price and volume data <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:19">[00:10:19]</a>.
*   **Quality** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:21">[00:10:21]</a>: Encompasses a range of attributes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:22">[00:10:22]</a>.
*   **Low Volatility** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:24">[00:10:24]</a>

## Methodology
Once the factors and their methodologies are defined, extensive computing power is employed to convert financial metrics and complex data streams into a [[Investment Opportunities in Smart Beta Indices | smart beta portfolio]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:26">[00:10:26]</a>. [[Smart Beta ETF Strategies | Smart beta funds]] can be based on a single factor, or they can combine two or even three factors <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:38">[00:10:38]</a>.

## Growing Popularity
Over the last few years, there has been a significant increase in the number of fund launches in this category, reflecting their growing popularity globally <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:43">[00:10:43]</a>. [[FactorBased Investing | Factor indexing]] is an area of increasing interest in the investment community <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:50">[00:10:50]</a>.
