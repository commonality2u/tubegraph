### Goal-Based Investing
INDmoney facilitates goal-based investing, helping users plan for future needs like purchasing a home or car, funding children's education, or planning for retirement <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:21:00">[04:21:00]</a>. The app features an expense tracker that, once synced with a savings bank account, shows exactly where money is spent across different categories without requiring manual data input <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:36:00">[04:36:00]</a>.
