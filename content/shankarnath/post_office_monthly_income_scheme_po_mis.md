The Post Office Monthly Income Scheme (POMIS) is a government-backed savings scheme offered by the Department of Post. It is designed to provide regular income payouts, making it a suitable investment option for [[fixed_deposit_schemes_for_senior_citizens | senior citizens]] who seek consistent returns and may not be comfortable with equity investments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:39">[00:06:39]</a>.

## Key Features

*   **Tenure** The scheme has a five-year maturity period <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:35">[00:06:35]</a>.
*   **Payout Frequency** Interest is paid on a monthly basis <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:41">[00:06:41]</a>.
*   **Investment Limit** As per current rules, an individual can invest up to 9 lakh rupees in this scheme <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:52">[00:06:52]</a>.
*   **Interest Rate** The interest rate for POMIS is set every quarter <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:56">[00:06:56]</a>. As of the video's recording, the interest rate is 7.4 percent <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:58">[00:06:58]</a>. Once an investment is made, the interest rate prevailing at that time is fixed for the entire five-year tenure of the investment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:01">[00:07:01]</a>. This makes the POMIS function like a [[fixed_deposit_schemes_for_senior_citizens | fixed deposit]] with a monthly payout <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:06">[00:07:06]</a>.
