Despite reaching 300,000 subscribers, he reveals that he is his "own worst critic" and is rarely satisfied with his content <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="16:15:00">[16:15:00]</a>.

## Imposter Syndrome and Audience Pressure
Johnson confesses to feeling "vulnerable" despite discussing his experiences on his channel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="16:11:00">[16:11:00]</a>. He admits to having [[selfcriticism_and_growth_on_youtube | imposter syndrome and growth on YouTube]] and questioning whether he deserves his large audience <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="16:27:00">[16:27:00]</a>.

He highlights the following concerns regarding his [[audience_impact_and_reception_of_inspirational_content | audience reception]]:
*   **Fear of Disappointment** He worries about disappointing his new, large audience because he doesn't know what they want <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="16:22:00">[16:22:00]</a>.
*   **Loyalty to Original Audience** He is concerned about disappointing his original tech audience, who supported him before his channel gained significant traction <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="16:31:00">[16:31:00]</a>.

## The Fear of Not Being Accepted
Johnson traces his deep-seated fear of not living up to others' expectations to his childhood, where he felt he had to excel at everything on the first try <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="14:27:00">[14:27:00]</a>. He states that trying new things earnestly can be "scary" due to the fear of not being accepted, but it is "necessary for growth" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="16:55:00">[16:55:00]</a>.

## YouTube as a Platform for Growth
Despite these personal struggles, Johnson views YouTube as a crucial platform for him to experiment and grow creatively <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="16:44:00">[16:44:00]</a>. He emphasizes that growth requires trying, even when it's daunting <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="16:49:00">[16:49:00]</a>. He holds onto the mantra from his first video: "Regardless of how this video performs, there's gonna be another one. It's just baby steps" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="17:15:00">[17:15:00]</a>.
