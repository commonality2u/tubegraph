## Overview of Five Minute Crafts

[[five_minute_crafts | Five-Minute Crafts]] is described as a very large craft and DIY operation, well-known for its "nonsensical life hacks" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:38">[00:01:38]</a>. The channel has a substantial audience on Instagram and Facebook, but is most recognized on YouTube <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:53">[00:01:53]</a>. Their YouTube videos are compilations of older Instagram videos, often haphazardly put together to reach 10 minutes in length, with three such videos uploaded daily <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:00">[00:02:00]</a>.

### Social Media Reach and Other Channels

[[the_rise_and_reach_of_5minute_crafts_on_social_media | Five-Minute Crafts]] has seen significant growth, reaching 50 million subscribers on YouTube, placing them third behind PewDiePie and T-Series <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:57">[00:00:57]</a>. This channel is one of many owned by the same entity, including offshoots designed for extremely broad demographics such as [[five_minute_crafts | Five-Minute Crafts]] Kids, Girly, Family, Teens, and [[five_minute_crafts_men_channel | Men]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>. Despite the varied demographics, these channels are noted for being "exactly the same" in content <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:25">[00:01:25]</a>.

## [[analysis_of_5minute_crafts_video_format_and_content | Analysis of Five Minute Crafts Men Channel]]

The content on [[five_minute_crafts_men_channel | Five Minute Crafts Men]] often bears a coincidental relationship to its presumed intended audience <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:12">[00:02:12]</a>. The titles and thumbnails of these videos are frequently misleading <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:09">[00:02:09]</a>.

### Examples of Questionable "Life Hacks"

Several examples from [[five_minute_crafts_men_channel | Five Minute Crafts Men]] highlight the impracticality and absurdity of the "hacks":

*   **Power Drill Toothbrush Scrubber**: A video titled "22 Handy Hacks for Men" features using a power drill with a toothbrush to clean, raising questions about its necessity when a sponge would suffice and the wastefulness of using new toothbrushes for this purpose <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:21">[00:02:21]</a>.
*   **Wrinkled T-shirt Hack**: A "lazy life hack" suggests lying on a wrinkled t-shirt to remove wrinkles, which is shown to take an hour <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:19">[00:03:19]</a>.
*   **Dirty Chips Packet**: This "hack" suggests folding a chip bag to avoid getting dust on hands, but ultimately results in dust on the hands anyway <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:59">[00:03:59]</a>.
*   **Crazy Hair Growth**: This involves using an old mascara wand with "crazy growth oils" applied to a girlfriend's eyelashes, another woman's eyebrows, and a full beard for rapid hair growth, which is presented as something "every real man do[es]" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:13">[00:04:13]</a>.
*   **"What happens when men are home alone"**: This segment depicts men trying "funny hairstyles" and having "fun in front of the mirror," though the actions portrayed seem disconnected and odd <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:03">[00:05:03]</a>.
*   **"Each man has his own little joys"**: This segment includes unrelated clips of men talking about changing tires, waking up, and throwing something into a trash can from a distance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:23">[00:05:23]</a>.
*   **"Men's haircuts reveal the true personality"**: This features drawings of famous people with different hairstyles, associating "long hair" with a "creative personality," which is humorously questioned for its implications for those not on the list <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:13">[00:06:13]</a>. The segment also humorously defines "vertical bangs" and other hair parts as "bangs" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:35">[00:06:35]</a>.
*   **"How to understand a woman"**: This segment features a book titled "How to understand a woman" and a narrative about a man dating three women, giving each $5,000 for an "experiment" to choose a wife <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:47">[00:06:47]</a>. The women spend the money on self-beautification, gifts for him, or stock market investment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:44">[00:07:44]</a>. The narrative concludes with the suggestion that "men are always men" and would choose the most beautiful one, implying a sexist viewpoint <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:35">[00:08:35]</a>.
*   **Tie-Tying Instructions**: Nearly all [[five_minute_crafts_men_channel | Five Minute Crafts Men]] videos include the exact same clips on how to tie a tie <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:05">[00:09:05]</a>.
*   **Peanut Butter in Hair**: A video titled "25 simple life hacks to be attractive" includes putting peanut butter in hair without explanation, which was personally experienced as messy and difficult to remove <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:36">[00:10:36]</a>.
*   **Kitchen Hacks**: Examples include not washing dirty dishes but eating from something else, stacking items with chopsticks instead of cleaning the fridge or using Tupperware, and needing assistance to get toast out of a toaster <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:51">[00:11:51]</a>.

## [[criticism_of_unrealistic_and_staged_life_hacks | General Criticism]]

The main [[critique_of_5minute_crafts_life_hacks | criticism]] of these life hacks is their pretense of being practical solutions to everyday problems, despite often being impractical, wasteful, or even dangerous <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:50">[00:12:50]</a>. The videos present unrealistic scenarios, such as being able to execute complex drill hacks but not being able to use a toaster safely <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:11">[00:13:11]</a>.

Reviews include statements like "I freaking love it," "I tried the DIYs and it went well," "[[five_minute_crafts_men_channel | Five Minute Crafts Men]] loved your crafts," and "best app for good knowledge" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:30">[00:13:30]</a>.
