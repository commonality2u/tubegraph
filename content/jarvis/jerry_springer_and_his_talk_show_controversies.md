## Controversies of *The Jerry Springer Show*
The show was widely criticized for its content, which included:
*   Homophobia <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:42:00">[03:42:00]</a>
*   Misogyny <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:45:00">[03:45:00]</a>
*   Constant fighting among guests, which was a hallmark of the show <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:47:00">[03:47:00]</a>. One of Springer's bodyguards even gained enough fame from these confrontations to get his own "trash TV show" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:50:00">[03:50:00]</a>.

Springer himself would often make an entrance by sliding down a stripper pole <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:57:00">[03:57:00]</a>. The show often featured bizarre scenarios, such as a random man spinning plates in the background during a fight <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:06:00">[05:06:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:30:00">[05:30:00]</a>. This contributed to the perception of the show as a "circus" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:32:00">[05:32:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:35:00">[05:35:00]</a>. An example episode described involved a woman concerned about her boyfriend cheating, which escalated into a fight and the alleged mistress flashing the crowd, all airing at 1 PM <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:31:00">[04:31:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:16:00">[05:16:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:39:00">[05:39:00]</a>. The show also included instances of casual racism <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:32:00">[06:32:00]</a>.

The show's formula involved "cherry-picking very irregular people in unusual circumstances and exploit[ing] them and mak[ing] fun of them for profit" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:43:00">[08:43:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:48:00">[08:48:00]</a>. ## Jerry Springer's Reaction to Controversies
When asked about his show's controversial past, Jerry Springer initially offered a satirical apology, saying he was "so sorry" for having "ruined the culture" and hoping "hell isn't that hot" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:25:00">[07:25:00]</a>. This was later revealed to be a joke <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:47:00">[07:47:00]</a>.

In a 2019 interview on the *Today Show*, Springer defensively stated he would not change anything about the show <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:11:00">[08:11:00]</a>. He argued that his show was groundbreaking for featuring "regular people" on television <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:23:00">[08:23:00]</a>. He attempted to compare the behavior of his guests to celebrities who discuss their personal lives, relationships, and drug use on late-night shows, suggesting that celebrities are given a "pass" because they are "rich," "famous," or "good looking" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:02:00">[09:02:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:12:00">[09:12:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:29:00">[09:29:00]</a>. Critics countered that the issue was not with the people on the show, but with the show itself being "trash" and making "moral compromises for cash" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:56:00">[09:56:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:27:00">[10:27:00]</a>.
