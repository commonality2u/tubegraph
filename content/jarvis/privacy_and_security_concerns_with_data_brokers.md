*   Email address <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:44:00">[02:44:00]</a>

## Risks and Concerns
The information collected by data brokers is sold to various parties, leading to several privacy and security concerns:
*   **Recipients of data**: Data is sold to scammers, telemarketers, and robocallers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:58:00">[02:58:00]</a>.
*   **Exposure**: This practice exposes individuals' information, making it vulnerable <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:04:00">[03:04:00]</a>.
*   **Data Breaches**: Personal information like emails and passwords can be included in [[how_cookies_and_user_data_profiles_work | data breaches]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:21:00">[03:21:00]</a>.
*   **[[Identity Theft and Social Media Issues | Identity Theft]]**: There is a risk of [[identity_teft_and_social_media_issues | identity theft]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:34:00">[03:34:00]</a>.
