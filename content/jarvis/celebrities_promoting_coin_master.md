## Funding for Endorsements

[[coin_master_advertising_strategies | Coin Master]] is able to afford these celebrity endorsements due to its financial success <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:54">[00:07:54]</a>. Released in 2016, it became the top-grossing mobile game in the UK and Germany, and is a top-five grossing game in the US <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:58">[00:07:58]</a>. This revenue is primarily generated through microtransactions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:12">[00:08:12]</a>. The advertisements featuring celebrities are highly misleading about the actual [[coin_master_game_mechanics_and_gameplay_experience | gameplay experience]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:15:41">[00:15:41]</a>. They portray scenarios of direct confrontation and interaction, but the core of the game is based on spinning a slot machine and raiding villages, with little to no player customization or real-time interaction with other players <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:40">[00:09:40]</a><a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:09">[00:10:09]</a>.
