## Dropbox's Response: A More "Enlightened Way of Working"

Recognizing these fundamental shifts and internal productivity challenges, Dropbox sought to design a "more enlightened way of working" [01:05:54](<a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:05:54">[01:05:54]</a>.

Post-COVID, Dropbox adopted a "virtual first" model, becoming 90% remote [01:07:56](<a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:07:56">[01:07:56]</a>. This approach was informed by studying successful remote-first companies and synthesizing a common playbook, which Dropbox subsequently [[cultural_practices_in_rapidly_scaling_companies | open-sourced]] as its "virtual first toolkit" [01:08:20](<a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:08:20">[01:08:20]</a>. This model has shown positive results, significantly increasing employee satisfaction, retention, engagement, and offer acceptance rates [01:08:48](<a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:08:48">[01:08:48]</a>.

### New Product Stack for Distributed Work
Central to this new mission is building a product stack specifically for distributed work. One key innovation is [[impact_of_ai_on_developer_productivity | Dropbox Dash]] [01:11:11](<a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:11:11">[01:11:11]</a>:
*   **Universal Search** Dash connects to various applications, providing universal search capabilities [01:11:14](<a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:11:14">[01:11:14]</a>.
*   **AI-Powered Q&A** Leveraging advances in AI (e.g., similar to ChatGPT), Dash allows users to ask natural language questions and receive answers based on their personalized, connected information, which standard AI models cannot provide [01:11:18](<a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:11:18">[01:11:18]</a>.
*   **Organized Working Life** Dash also introduces "stacks" or smart collections to help organize disparate types of content (e.g., Google Docs, 4K videos, AirTable) into common containers, simplifying organization and sharing [01:12:57](<a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:12:57">[01:12:57]</a>.

Dash aims to address the fundamental problem of finding, organizing, sharing, and securing information in the cloud era, adapting the core value proposition of Dropbox to a new distributed, multi-tool environment [01:12:12](<a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:12:12">[01:12:12]</a>.
