## The Rise of Agents and Digital Labor

A key concept in the future workforce is the emergence of "agents," which are defined as software or robotic entities that work with individuals, understand their preferences, have an institutional memory, and provide advice and assistance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:32:50">[00:32:50]</a>. This concept was envisioned in films like *Minority Report*, where personalized digital interactions in retail stores showcased early ideas of agents <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:32:11">[00:32:11]</a>.

The development of agent technology is accelerating rapidly <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:44:00">[00:44:00]</a>. Salesforce is heavily investing in "Agent Force," a product designed to enable digital labor <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:41:40">[00:41:40]</a>. The company aims to move from automating customer touchpoints and aggregating data to building an agentic platform on top of this foundation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:38:18">[00:38:18]</a>. The ultimate vision includes a future "robotic drone layer" that feeds off these platforms and capabilities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:38:40">[00:38:40]</a>.

This shift represents a fundamental movement in computer science: from data to automation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:39:04">[00:39:04]</a>. The goal is to continuously make technology lower cost, easier to use, and more automated <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:39:10">[00:39:10]</a>.

## Impact on the Workforce

The integration of [[the_evolving_capabilities_and_future_potential_of_ai | AI]] and agents will lead to a rebalancing of the workforce <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:40:40">[00:40:40]</a>.

### Specific Role Transformations
*   **Support Engineers:** Roles for support engineers may decrease due to the implementation of robotic support layers through agent force <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:40:46">[00:40:46]</a>. Salesforce, for example, moved its entire help infrastructure to Agent Force, resulting in a 50% reduction in human escalation and 83% of inquiries resolved robotically <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:32">[00:19:32]</a>.
*   **Account Executives/Sales:** Conversely, there is a current trend of increasing demand for account executives and sales roles to drive company growth <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:40:57">[00:40:57]</a>.

### Broader Industry Impacts
AI and agents are expected to create new job categories that currently lack sufficient personnel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:41:16">[00:41:16]</a>. Many existing jobs, particularly blue-collar roles in areas like restaurants, trucking, construction, and gardening, may not be significantly impacted by automation for a long time <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:41:38">[00:41:38]</a>. This suggests a "Tale of Two Cities," with different impacts on jobs in urban and rural areas <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:42:13">[00:42:13]</a>.

### Healthcare as an Example
The healthcare sector stands to benefit significantly from agents. Routine tasks like scheduling appointments, providing pre- and post-procedure instructions, and relaying lab results can be automated by agents <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:34:40">[00:34:40]</a>. This could alleviate burnout among medical professionals by reducing administrative burden <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:33:57">[00:33:57]</a>, allowing them to focus on more complex, empathetic, face-to-face interactions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:34:31">[00:34:31]</a>. Studies even suggest that AI, like ChatGPT, can provide more accurate diagnoses than human doctors in some cases, partly due to the elimination of human bias <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:35:50">[00:35:50]</a>. This highlights the potential for [[impact_of_ai_on_healthcare_and_other_industries | AI in healthcare]].

## Adapting to the Future

Entrepreneurs and companies must adopt a "beginner's mind" (Shoshin) to continually clear out preconceptions and remain open to new possibilities <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:23">[00:23:23]</a>. This mindset allows for continuous experimentation and the discovery of winning tactics that can evolve into winning strategies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:21:09">[00:21:09]</a>.

Companies should embrace Kaizen, the Japanese motto for continuous improvement <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:52:07">[00:52:07]</a>. This means constantly evolving and learning from both successes and failures <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:55:11">[00:55:11]</a>. The entrepreneurial journey is not linear; it's a roller coaster of continuous challenges and [[transformations_in_engineering_roles_with_ai | transformations]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:50:24">[00:50:24]</a>. The goal is to anticipate and welcome the future, leading customers towards it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:55:45">[00:55:45]</a>. This proactive approach is crucial for navigating the [[future_of_startups_and_product_development_with_ai | future of AI in product development]] and its impact on the workforce.
