## What is an AI Agent?

An [[future_of_autonomous_agents_and_ai_in_collaboration | AI agent]] is envisioned as a system that works *with* you, possessing institutional memory and understanding preferences to provide advice and assistance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:32:58">[00:32:58]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:33:16">[00:33:16]</a>. It can be a piece of software on a phone, a robot in a home, or a car that understands its driver <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:33:04">[00:33:04]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:33:07">[00:33:07]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:33:10">[00:33:10]</a>. This concept was previously explored in films like *Minority Report*, where digital environments adapted to a user's interests and engaged in conversation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:32:11">[00:32:11]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:32:43">[00:32:43]</a>.

## Salesforce's "Agent Force"

Salesforce is making a significant bet on AI agents with its "Agent Force" product <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:32">[00:01:32]</a>.
Mark Benioff, co-founder and CEO of Salesforce, considers himself a "startup CEO" focused on this new digital labor industry <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:31:04">[00:31:04]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:31:40">[00:31:40]</a>.

### Capabilities and Impact
*   **Customer Service Automation**: Salesforce has moved its entire help infrastructure to Agent Force <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:32">[00:19:32]</a>. This has led to a 50% reduction in human escalation for support inquiries and 83% of inquiries being resolved robotically <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:41">[00:19:41]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:45">[00:19:45]</a>.
*   **Cost Reduction**: The adoption of AI agents is seen as an opportunity to lower costs and improve efficiency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:21:49">[00:21:49]</a>.
*   **Digital Labor Provider**: Salesforce aims to be a provider of digital labor, not just a data management company <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:21:56">[00:21:56]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:21:58">[00:21:58]</a>.

### Development and Strategy
The Agent Force product was accelerated by the acquisition of Airkit, a company built by a former Salesforce entrepreneur who had previously sold "Relate IQ" to Salesforce <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:44:11">[00:44:11]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:44:19">[00:44:19]</a>. The production code for Agent Force was released at the end of October <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:44:48">[00:44:48]</a>. Salesforce is driving a massive internal and external push for Agent Force, including:
*   Focusing the entire company on the product <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:19:15">[00:19:15]</a>.
*   Training salespeople and expanding the sales organization with 1,000-2,000 new account executives focused solely on Agent Force <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:21:41">[00:21:41]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:21:36">[00:21:36]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:40:57">[00:40:57]</a>.
*   Running aggressive marketing campaigns, including ads with Matthew McConaughey and Woody Harrelson <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:06">[00:20:06]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:08">[00:20:08]</a>.
*   Demonstrating capabilities through real-world examples like help.salesforce.com <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:20:36">[00:20:36]</a>.
*   Activating the product for all 135,000 Salesforce customers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:26">[00:25:26]</a>.
*   Encouraging their ecosystem of "Trailblazers" to become "Agent Blazers" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:16">[00:25:16]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:21">[00:25:21]</a>.

## Examples and Use Cases for AI Agents

*   **Healthcare**: Agents can manage pre- and post-procedure tasks, such as reminding patients to drink water after a CT scan or take medications <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:34:40">[00:34:40]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:35:12">[00:35:12]</a>. They can reduce the administrative burden on doctors and nurses, freeing them for more empathetic, face-to-face conversations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:33:57">[00:33:57]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:34:11">[00:34:11]</a>. Studies even suggest [[user_experiences_with_ai_and_evolving_ai_capabilities | AI]] can provide more accurate diagnoses than doctors in some cases, due to less bias <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:35:50">[00:35:50]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:35:59">[00:35:59]</a>.
*   **Customer Touchpoints**: Agents can automate and enhance customer interactions across various channels, including sales, service, marketing, analytics, and integrations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:38:00">[00:38:00]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:38:02">[00:38:02]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:38:04">[00:38:04]</a>.

## [[future_of_autonomous_agents_and_ai_in_collaboration | Future implications of AI for product management]] and the Workforce

The emergence of AI agents will lead to workforce rebalancing <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:40:40">[00:40:40]</a>. For example, Salesforce anticipates needing fewer support engineers due to robotic support layers, while simultaneously hiring more account executives to grow the company <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:40:46">[00:40:46]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:40:57">[00:40:57]</a>.

However, many jobs, particularly in blue-collar sectors, may not be impacted for a long time <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:41:36">[00:41:36]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:41:56">[00:41:56]</a>. [[future_implications_of_ai_for_product_management | AI]] is also expected to create new jobs, filling needs that currently lack sufficient human resources <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:41:20">[00:41:20]</a>. This creates a "Tale of Two Cities" scenario, with different impacts in different places <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:42:13">[00:42:13]</a>.
