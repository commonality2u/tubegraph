## How AI is Shifting Developer Work
AI-enabled tools like GitHub Copilot or Tab9 are leading to fundamental shifts in how people work <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:51:47">[00:51:47]</a>.
*   **Enhanced Efficiency** Top engineers, sometimes referred to as 10x engineers, are finding these tools make them 100% more effective and efficient <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:50:24">[00:50:24]</a>. Specific tasks, such as building an HTTP server, can be completed 50% faster <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:52:25">[00:52:25]</a>.
*   **Shift in Focus** Developers now spend more time reviewing code than writing it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:51:59">[00:51:59]</a>. Research indicates that approximately 50% of a developer's time is spent on reviewing compared to writing <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:52:10">[00:52:10]</a>.
*   **Cognitive Repurposing** The primary benefit of AI is not merely cutting task time in half, but rather freeing up cognitive space for developers to focus on more complex and challenging problems <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:52:43">[00:52:43]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:52:54">[00:52:54]</a>.
