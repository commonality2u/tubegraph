[[top_attributes_of_a_successful_product_manager | High agency]] is a crucial trait for [[the_role_of_product_managers | product managers]], enabling them to achieve goals despite obstacles <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:25:55">[01:25:55]</a>. The concept, coined by Eric Weinstein, describes the ability to find a way to get what you want without waiting for perfect conditions or blaming circumstances <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:25:06">[01:25:06]</a>. High-agency individuals either push through adverse conditions or manage to reverse them to achieve their objectives <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:25:46">[01:25:46]</a>.

## Why High Agency is Vital for PMs
[[the_role_of_product_managers | Product managers]] constantly face adverse conditions, such as insufficient resources, [[challenges_and_responsibilities_in_product_management | challenges]] with legacy infrastructure, staffing issues, and customer problems <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:26:08">[01:26:08]</a>. There is no shortage of problems for a [[the_role_of_product_managers | product manager]] to solve <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:26:20">[01:26:20]</a>.

## Components of High Agency
High agency in [[the_role_of_product_managers | product managers]] is characterized by:
*   **Strong Ownership Mindset** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:27:42">[01:27:42]</a>
*   **Creative Execution** through challenges <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:27:52">[01:27:52]</a>
*   **High Degree of Resilience** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:28:04">[01:28:04]</a>

## Impact on Product Managers
This trait differentiates [[the_role_of_product_managers | product managers]] who have a large [[thinking_big_and_driving_impact_as_a_product_manager | impact]] and exceed expectations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:26:30">[01:26:30]</a>. Some PMs, despite initial disadvantages or perceived [[top_attributes_of_a_successful_product_manager | capabilities]] on paper, achieve significant results due to their high agency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:26:52">[01:26:52]</a>. Conversely, PMs with tremendous potential may not achieve it if they lack high agency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:27:09">[01:27:09]</a>. Recognizing high agency provides a valuable vocabulary to understand team dynamics and individual performance in [[the_art_and_role_of_product_management | product management]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:28:28">[01:28:28]</a>.
