## Impact on Engineering Work
[[The impact of AI on software and business | Productivity AI engineering]] is a top-of-mind concern for many <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:50:14">[00:50:14]</a>. Even highly effective engineers, sometimes referred to as "10x engineers," find that [[The role of AI in software development | AI-enabled tools]] like GitHub Copilot make them 100% more effective and efficient <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:50:24">[00:50:24]</a>.

Key shifts observed due to [[The role of AI in software development | AI-enabled tools]]:
*   **Shift in task focus** Developers spend more time reviewing code generated by AI rather than writing it from scratch <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:51:59">[00:51:59]</a>. A paper by an MSR team indicates that roughly 50% of a developer's time is now spent reviewing versus writing <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:52:10">[00:52:10]</a>.
*   **Cognitive freedom** [[Impact of AI on skills and products | AI allows engineers to complete certain tasks faster]], such as building an HTTP server, freeing up cognitive space to tackle more challenging problems <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:52:25">[00:52:25]</a>.
*   **Changed mental models** The adoption of AI tools alters developers' expectations regarding friction models, cognitive load, and reliance on code <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:53:09">[00:53:09]</a>.

## Challenges and Future Considerations
The [[Impact and challenges of AI in product management | impact and challenges of AI]] are still being explored <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:51:06">[00:51:06]</a>. While AI can accelerate tasks, it's crucial to understand that [[AI and its impact on jobs | productivity isn't simply about cutting task time in half]] to reduce workforce size <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:52:41">[00:52:41]</a>.

Important open questions and areas for future research and focus include:
*   **Measuring productivity** How should productivity be measured in an [[The role of AI in shaping product management skills | AI-augmented environment]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:53:27">[00:53:27]</a>?
*   **Trust and reliability** A potential new dimension to measurement frameworks, such as the SPACE framework, could be trust or reliability, considering over-reliance on AI tools <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:51:29">[00:51:29]</a>.
*   **Learning and onboarding** How does AI affect the learning process for new codebases or languages, especially for novices versus experts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:53:51">[00:53:51]</a>?
*   **Holistic metrics** It is essential to use holistic, balanced metrics to avoid oversimplification and ensure a comprehensive understanding of [[Future implications of AI for product management | AI's impact]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:53:36">[00:53:36]</a>.

Ultimately, the goal is to leverage AI to enable developers to do more complex work, requiring careful consideration of how these new tools influence their mental models and overall experience <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:52:50">[00:52:50]</a>.
