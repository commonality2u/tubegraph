## AI as a Management Technology

At its core, [[the_impact_of_ai_on_software_and_business | AI]] functions as a management technology, adept at managing intelligence, whether human or artificial <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:20:39">[01:20:39]</a>. While individuals express fear regarding [[future_implications_of_ai_for_product_management | AI]], the concern is often not with the technology itself but with the corporations deploying it, especially given that [[interactions_and_implications_of_ai_in_enterprise_settings | AI]] must be embodied and deployed by humans who may have misaligned objectives <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:19:25">[01:19:25]</a>. This necessitates a serious re-evaluation of governance and alignment structures within companies, areas where the tech industry has historically underinvested <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:20:28">[01:20:28]</a>.

Observing the struggles with [[interactions_and_implications_of_ai_in_enterprise_settings | AI]] agents reveals common organizational pitfalls like passing the buck or infinite regress in tasks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:20:45">[01:20:45]</a>. However, [[the_impact_of_ai_on_software_and_business | AI]]'s strength in summarization promises to revolutionize management by drastically reducing the need for hierarchical structures to process information. This means CEOs will soon be able to get summaries of their entire company's activities directly, bypassing numerous layers of middle management <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:23:25">[01:23:25]</a>.

## Ethical Considerations and Future Implications
As [[future_implications_of_ai_for_product_management | AI]] develops, the future presents an incredibly wide aperture of possibilities, making it difficult to predict outcomes with certainty <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:25:32">[01:25:32]</a>. Navigating this uncertainty requires taking ethical action today that makes sense across a wide variety of future scenarios <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:26:01">[01:26:01]</a>. Key actions include:
*   Increasing transparency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:26:18">[01:26:18]</a>
*   Building more competent leadership <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:26:22">[01:26:22]</a>
*   Founding companies committed to strong values <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:26:24">[01:26:24]</a>

This approach is analogous to the lean methodology for dealing with business uncertainty, advocating for a scientific and experimental approach to coping with the unknown <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:25:05">[01:25:05]</a>. [[Role of AI in startups and entrepreneurship | AI]] is forcing a reckoning with whether companies truly want to "change the world for the better" and whether their internal structures support this mission over mere profit maximization <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:32:30">[01:32:30]</a>. It demands that organizations embody promises of trustworthiness in their very structure, not just rely on individual good intentions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:35:10">[01:35:10]</a>.

> "If you want your organization to be trustworthy, the promises it makes, if you want to win the Public's trust by making promises they can believe, you have to embody those promises in the structure of the organization itself so that even if you weren't there the promise would be kept" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:35:05">[01:35:05]</a>

This shift can lead to a competitive advantage for companies committed to "human flourishing," as they can attract top talent and foster unwavering customer loyalty <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:41:07">[01:41:07]</a>. Founders are encouraged to work with legal counsel to implement structural changes from the outset, such as specific legal designations or mission pledges, to ensure their companies remain aligned with their long-term values and avoid being "steamrolled" by purely financial interests <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:43:41">[01:43:41]</a>.

Ultimately, the goal is to build companies where "human flourishing is the thing at the beating heart," creating environments where people want to work on awesome products and drive positive change in the world <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:39:41">[01:39:41]</a>.
