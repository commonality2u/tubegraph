From: [[lennyspodcast]] <br/> 

AI has the potential to significantly impact education, particularly through personalized tutoring. Kevin Weil, Chief Product Officer at OpenAI, highlights this as one of the most important things AI could achieve <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:06:21">[01:06:21]</a>.

## Potential of Personalized Tutoring

Personalized AI tutoring is considered "uncontroversial" and "good for kids" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:07:34">[01:07:34]</a>. Studies show that combining classroom education with personalized tutoring can lead to "multiple standard deviation improvements in learning speed" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:07:25">[01:07:25]</a>.

While existing products like Khan Academy are doing great things in this space <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:06:47">[01:06:47]</a>, and organizations like Vinod Khosla's nonprofit are making an impact <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:06:54">[01:06:54]</a>, Weil expresses surprise that a "two billion kid, AI personalized tutoring thing" doesn't yet exist <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:07:02">[01:07:02]</a>. He believes the models are already capable enough to provide this <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:07:11">[01:07:11]</a>.

## Accessibility and Impact

The models are free, and with the widespread availability of Android devices, personalized AI tutoring could be accessible globally, potentially changing the world for children who might not otherwise have access to a solid education <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:07:36">[01:07:36]</a>.

The ability of AI models to understand the complexities and nuances of human speech is crucial to making chat a versatile interface for learning <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:42:08">[01:42:08]</a>. This allows for an open-ended, flexible communication medium, akin to how humans interact and learn from each other <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:42:48">[01:42:48]</a>.

## AI as a Reskilling Tool

Beyond formal education, AI tools like ChatGPT can also serve as powerful "reskilling app[s]" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:35:05">[01:35:05]</a>. They possess a vast amount of knowledge and can teach new things to individuals interested in acquiring new skills <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:39:39">[01:39:39]</a>.

This [[humanai_interaction_and_personalization | human-AI interaction and personalization]] approach allows models to reason and adapt in ways that mimic human thought processes, offering a new dimension to learning and skill development <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:03:37">[01:03:37]</a>. By understanding how a person would solve a problem, or by giving the AI a "mindset" (e.g., "you are the world's greatest marketer"), the models can provide more effective and relevant responses <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:28:47">[01:28:47]</a>. This means the future of education and skill acquisition can leverage AI to provide personalized and highly adaptable learning experiences.
