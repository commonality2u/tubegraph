## Chatbot in Action

The [[setting_up_and_using_a_chatbot | chatbot]] is designed to answer questions by extracting factual information from the Substack posts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:02:00">[01:02:00]</a>. This means all responses provided by the [[setting_up_and_using_a_chatbot | chatbot]] are directly sourced from the creator's published content <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:05:00">[02:05:00]</a>.
