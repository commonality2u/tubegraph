## Applications of AI in Video Ad Content

AI can be utilized to produce visual elements within a video ad concept, specifically for:

*   **Visual Hooks** A video concept typically starts with a text hook, followed by a visual hook <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>. This visual hook can be [[ai_generated_visuals_in_video_advertising | AI generated]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:44">[00:00:44]</a>. An example includes a visual transition from an overweight person to a fit person <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>.
*   **Value Proposition Visuals** When showcasing value propositions in a video ad, visual elements can also be [[ai_assets_and_video_generation | AI generated]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:03">[00:01:03]</a>. An instance given is the use of [[ai_assets_and_video_generation | AI generated]] visuals of "yummy food" to convey a value <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>.
