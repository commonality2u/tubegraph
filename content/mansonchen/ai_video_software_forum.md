Using Forum, a video can be generated in approximately 15 minutes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:26">[00:01:26]</a>. This speed is based on information from the creator's posts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:29">[00:01:29]</a>.

## Related AI Tools

### ControlNet

ControlNet is another open-source software that operates on top of Stable Diffusion <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>. It functions as a text-to-image or image-to-image tool <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:43">[00:01:43]</a>. With ControlNet, users can [[animation_and_video_generation | generate images and videos]] while maintaining additional controls for elements like poses or compositions for text-to-image conversion <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:55">[00:01:55]</a>.
