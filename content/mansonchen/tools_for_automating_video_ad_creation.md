### Key Features of [[automating_video_ad_creation_with_sovran | Sovereign]]

[[automating_video_ad_creation_with_sovran | Sovereign]] facilitates a [[modular_approach_to_video_ad_creation | modular approach to video ad creation]], adhering to direct response video best practices by breaking down ads into "building blocks":
*   **Hooks** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:36:00">[02:36:00]</a>
*   **Body** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:36:00">[02:36:00]</a>
*   **Call to Action (CTA)** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:37:00">[02:37:00]</a>

This [[script_frameworks_for_video_ad_creation | script framework]] allows for extensive testing of different hooks with various body videos and CTA end cards <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:35:00">[03:35:00]</a>. Given that people typically watch a maximum of 5 seconds of a video ad, the tool emphasizes focusing time and attention on optimizing the hook <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:50:00">[03:50:00]</a>.

Other notable features include:
*   **Automatic Naming Convention** The tool automatically names each variation, eliminating manual naming errors and ensuring organized data tracking <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:37:00">[02:37:00]</a>. Users can also disable this feature if they prefer their own naming convention <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:47:00">[07:47:00]</a>.
*   **Bulk Download** All video ads from a batch can be downloaded simultaneously <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:44:00">[02:44:00]</a>.
*   **Audio Layering** An audio layer (music or voice-over) can be added to each variation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:51:00">[02:51:00]</a>.
*   **Unlimited Storage** Provides unlimited clip and video ad storage <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:56:00">[02:56:00]</a>.
*   **Multiple Aspect Ratios** Exports in any standard aspect ratio, including 1x1, 4x5, 9x16 (for stories, Reels, TikTok), and 16x9 (for YouTube, Facebook) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:59:00">[02:59:00]</a>.
*   **Bulk Copy Addition** Allows for bulk addition of copy to visual hooks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:11:00">[03:11:00]</a>.

The interface allows users to upload clips into different modules, choosing up to four hooks, two body videos, and two call-to-action end cards to generate up to 16 ad variations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:36:00">[06:36:00]</a>.
