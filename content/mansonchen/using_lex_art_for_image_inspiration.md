## How to Use lex.art for [[creating_visual_hooks_with_ai | Creating Visual Hooks with AI]]

The platform offers the ability to copy the specific text prompts used to create the images <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:16">[00:00:16]</a>. This feature is crucial for adapting or replicating desired visual styles. The overall process for [[generating_visual_hooks_for_creatives | generating visual hooks for creatives]] is as follows:

1.  **Browse and Find Inspiration**
    Visit lex.art to scroll through numerous image visuals and gather inspiration for your creative concepts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:11">[00:00:11]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:14">[00:00:14]</a>.
2.  **Copy Prompts**
    If an image is appealing, its prompt can be copied directly from lex.art <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:16">[00:00:16]</a>.
3.  **Generate Images with AI**
    Take the copied prompt to an [[ai_tools_for_creative_production_and_testing | AI image generation tool]] such as Midjourney, DALL-E 3, or Stable Diffusion XL to create your own images <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:22">[00:00:22]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:29">[00:00:29]</a>. Select the preferred image from the generated options <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:31">[00:00:31]</a>.
4.  **Create [[ai_generated_video_content | AI-Generated Video Content]]**
    Use RunwayML.com, specifically their Gen-2 model, to transform the chosen image into a video <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:35">[00:00:35]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:39">[00:00:39]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>.
5.  **Add Effects and Transitions**
    Apply custom effects and transitions to the short video clip (approximately 4 seconds) to finalize the [[visual_hooks_and_modular_creative_strategy | visual hook]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:45">[00:00:45]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:48">[00:00:48]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>.
