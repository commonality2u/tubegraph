## Key Trends in Creative Production

### Low-Fi Ads
Low-fi ads are unpolished creatives that often don't resemble traditional advertisements <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:18:00">[08:18:00]</a>. Examples include Post-it notes, writing on whiteboards, or simulated iMessage and Slack conversations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:30:00">[08:30:00]</a>. Their "sneaky" appearance helps them perform well by not immediately signaling they are ads <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="08:40:00">[08:40:00]</a>.

### AI-Generated Content (AI UGC)
[[using_ai_tools_to_increase_ad_creatives | AI-generated User-Generated Content (UGC)]] is a hot topic that significantly reduces the cost of creative production <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="09:43:00">[09:43:00]</a>. This allows for greater [[Optimizing Creative Production and Tracking | creative diversity]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:22:00">[10:22:00]</a>.

The benefit of these tools is their low production cost, enabling more iterative testing <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="11:17:00">[11:17:00]</a>.

### AI Statics
AI tools like Midjourney and Flux (an open-source text-to-image model) enable rapid testing and iteration of static ad concepts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="11:44:00">[11:44:00]</a>.
*   **Pro Tip:** Websites like replicate.com/ai offer open-source text-to-image models that can be used with API calls to generate hundreds of images at once, significantly faster than Discord-based interfaces <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="11:59:00">[11:59:00]</a>. These can then be quickly overlaid with text in tools like Canva <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="12:19:00">[12:19:00]</a>.

### AI Video
The leading AI video tool currently is Gen 3 from Runway <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="12:41:00">[12:41:00]</a>. A common strategy involves using AI to create "visual hooks" that lead into a pre-existing body video <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="12:52:00">[12:52:00]</a>. This modular approach allows for efficient testing of different hooks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="13:13:00">[13:13:00]</a>.

### Podcast-Style Ads
These ads leverage the authenticity and trust associated with podcasts, especially in the US <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="15:17:00">[15:17:17]</a>. They often don't look like ads and can even feature AI voices, as seen in Headway's ads <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="14:59:00">[14:59:00]</a>. This format immediately attracts attention, and users often don't realize it's an ad, thinking it's just a conversation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="15:44:00">[15:44:00]</a>.

## Leveraging AI for Creative Testing Strategy

The rise of AI tools supports the broader need for rapid and diverse creative testing. The fastest-growing advertisers test significantly more ads per month (45 vs. 4) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:39:00">[05:39:00]</a>. This high volume of testing requires [[utilizing_ai_tools_for_marketing_and_content_creation | efficient tools]] to keep the cost per creative low, as a vast majority of ads will not meet benchmarks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="17:51:00">[17:51:00]</a>.

> "You need a high volume of ad variations and just let your audience vote on what the best ad is. Bottom performing ads won't get any spend. Your best ads are are going to get a lot of spend so just let your audience vote." <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="17:30:00">[17:30:00]</a>

AI tools facilitate the continuous testing of new ideas and concepts, essential for scaling ad accounts and preventing ad fatigue <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="18:04:00">[18:04:00]</a>. They allow advertisers to:
*   **Research Messaging:** [[utilizing_ai_tools_for_marketing_and_content_creation | AI tools]] like ChatGPT can analyze customer reviews to identify concise, five-star testimonials for use in ads <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="19:40:00">[19:40:00]</a>. This helps find the best social proof or messaging <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="20:29:00">[20:29:00]</a>.
*   **Generate Diverse Creative Formats:** Once a core message or angle is identified, AI allows for an "explosion" of formats like Post-it note statics, Reddit post screenshots, iMessage conversations, AI UGC videos, and talking head UGC <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="25:06:00">[25:06:00]</a>.

By integrating these [[tools_for_automating_video_ad_creation | AI tools]], advertisers can maintain an "always-on" [[importance_of_dedicated_creative_testing_campaign | creative testing campaign]], constantly pumping fresh creatives into their Evergreen ad sets <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="34:03:00">[34:03:00]</a>. This proactive approach helps combat ad fatigue and ensures a steady supply of winning ads <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="18:30:00">[18:30:00]</a>.
