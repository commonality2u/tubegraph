[[creative_testing_for_facebook_ads | Creative testing]] is a crucial aspect of scaling [[Facebook creative testing strategies | Facebook ads]] and improving Cost Per Acquisition (CAC) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:12:00">[03:12:00]</a>. It prevents a situation where winning ads become ineffective due to ad fatigue and there are no new creatives ready to launch <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:13:00">[03:13:00]</a>.

## Key Principles of Effective Creative Testing

*   **Continuous Iteration** It's essential to keep iterating on ads that are performing well <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:18:00">[03:18:00]</a>.
*   **Testing New Concepts** Regularly test new creative concepts to discover high-performing "home run" ads <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:21:00">[03:21:00]</a>.
*   **Repeatable Process** The goal is to establish a repeatable process that consistently yields one to three winning ads each month <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:26:00">[03:26:00]</a>.

## Role in Campaigns

Creatives that have successfully passed the [[creative_testing_for_facebook_ads | creative testing]] criteria are then utilized in evergreen broad targeting campaigns with bid caps and cost caps <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:49:00">[04:49:00]</a>. This approach minimizes risk, allowing for more aggressive spending on proven creatives <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:54:00">[04:54:00]</a>.
