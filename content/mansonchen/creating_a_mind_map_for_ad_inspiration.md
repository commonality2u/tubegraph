## Benefits of Using a Mind Map

Developing this type of [[mind_maps_for_creative_strategy | mind map]] is particularly useful for individuals aiming to create many different static images or [[creating_video_ad_concepts | video ads]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:42">[00:00:42]</a>.
