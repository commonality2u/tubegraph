## Key Universal Human Motivators

People are generally motivated by the desire to:
*   Avoid effort <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:16:00">[02:16:00]</a>
*   Save time <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:18:00">[02:18:00]</a>
*   Avoid mental or physical pain <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:20:00">[02:20:00]</a>
*   Increase comfort <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:23:00">[02:23:00]</a>
*   Improve their health <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:25:00">[02:25:00]</a>
*   Improve their status or luxury <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:27:00">[02:27:00]</a>
*   Feel love <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:29:00">[02:29:00]</a>
*   Be praised and appreciated <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:30:00">[02:30:00]</a>

Understanding these motivators helps in creating diverse and effective ad messages <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:39:00">[02:39:00]</a>. This approach contributes to a healthy [[creative_diversity_in_advertising | creative diversity]] mix in campaigns <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:13:00">[06:13:00]</a>.
