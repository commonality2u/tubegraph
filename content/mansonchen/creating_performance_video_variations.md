*   Hooks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:11">[00:00:11]</a>
*   Body videos <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:12">[00:00:12]</a>

1.  **Upload Assets**

    *   Upload body videos <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:06">[00:01:06]</a>.
    *   Upload call-to-action end cards <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:08">[00:01:08]</a>.
    *   Upload audio files, which can include music or voiceovers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:17">[00:01:17]</a>.

2.  **Select Components**
    *   Choose the specific hooks desired to stitch together with body videos <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:28">[00:01:28]</a>.

    *   Adjust volume levels for all selected video and audio files <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:41">[00:01:41]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:03">[00:02:03]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:06">[00:02:06]</a>.
    *   Apply these settings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:48">[00:01:48]</a>.

4.  **Choose Aspect Ratio**
