## Challenges of Integration

Currently, a key question for platforms is how to rank and recommend content when a portion of it is AI-generated, especially compared to camera-based content <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:29">[00:00:29]</a>. This highlights the [[challenges_of_integrating_ai_generated_content_with_traditional_content | challenges of integrating AI-generated content with traditional content]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:25">[00:00:25]</a>.

## Impact on Existing Creators

If the ratio of AI-generated content increases significantly on a platform—for instance, reaching 50%—it could lead to existing content creators receiving fewer views and less customer attention <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.

## The Future of Platforms

Should AI-generated content become a dominant force, there may be a necessity to develop entirely new platforms specifically for AI-generated content <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:51">[00:00:51]</a>. This scenario points towards a potential shift in [[the_future_of_ai_platforms_in_content_creation | the future of AI platforms in content creation]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:51">[00:00:51]</a>.
