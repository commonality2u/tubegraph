## [[challenges_in_evolving_ai_technologies_in_education | Challenges and Opportunities in AI Adoption]]

While the potential of [[role_of_ai_in_education | AI in education]] is vast, several [[challenges_in_evolving_ai_technologies_in_education | challenges]] exist.

### Engagement and Proactive AI

A significant hurdle is student engagement, as only a small percentage of students proactively seek out AI assistance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:47:00">[07:47:00]</a>. The shift to proactive AI in Khan Academy Classroom aims to address this by making the AI a more central and interactive part of the learning journey <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:54:00">[07:54:00]</a>. The most effective approach involves teachers assigning AI-driven tutoring sessions and holding students accountable, which significantly boosts engagement <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="34:05:00">[34:05:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="34:40:00">[34:40:00]</a>.

### Policy and District-Level Adoption

Schools are expected to be among the first places to see mainstream adoption of [[role_of_ai_in_education | AI for productivity]] and learning <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="19:12:00">[19:12:00]</a>.
*   **Cost-Effectiveness** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="19:47:00">[19:47:00]</a>: [[impact_of_ai_on_education_and_unconventional_applications | AI solutions]] are dramatically cheaper than traditional interventions like live tutoring (e.g., $10-$15 per year compared to $25-$50 per hour) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="19:48:00">[19:48:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="20:18:00">[20:18:00]</a>.
*   **Teacher Benefits** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="21:03:00">[21:03:00]</a>: Districts are reporting that AI saves teachers at least 5 hours per week, making it a valuable recruiting and retention tool <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="21:03:00">[21:03:00]</a>.

Building effective [[role_of_ai_in_education | AI for education]] requires significant development beyond simple prompting layers <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="21:32:00">[21:32:00]</a>.
*   **Guardrails and Moderation** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="21:48:00">[21:48:00]</a>: Implementing robust safety and moderation features, including managing false positives, is crucial for educational settings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="21:48:00">[21:48:00]</a>.
*   **Math Accuracy** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="22:07:00">[22:07:00]</a>: Ensuring high accuracy in math, particularly with complex evaluation errors, is an ongoing area of focus <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="22:07:00">[22:07:00]</a>.
*   **User Interface** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="22:35:00">[22:35:00]</a>: Developing natural and intuitive user interfaces that integrate AI seamlessly into various aspects of learning (e.g., brainstorming tools, outlining tools, drafting assistance) is vital <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="22:37:00">[22:37:00]</a>.
*   **Desired Capabilities** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="23:46:00">[23:46:00]</a>:
    *   **Improved Memory** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="23:58:00">[23:58:00]</a>: Enabling AI models to retain context and memory over longer interactions is key for personalized learning <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="24:07:00">[24:07:00]</a>.
    *   **Advanced Voice Integration** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="24:26:00">[24:26:00]</a>: Integrating advanced voice capabilities for more natural student-AI interactions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="24:34:00">[24:34:00]</a>.
    *   **Higher Quality Questions** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="24:58:00">[24:58:00]</a>: Models need to generate more reliable and high-quality questions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="25:01:00">[25:01:00]</a>.
    *   **Vision Capabilities** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="25:30:00">[25:30:00]</a>: Allowing AI to "see" and understand student work (e.g., handwritten math problems on a tablet) to provide immediate, natural feedback <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="25:34:00">[25:34:00]</a>.

### Model Evaluation

Khan Academy uses a rigorous evaluation framework for AI models <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="29:08:00">[29:08:00]</a>:
*   **Tough Test Cases** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="29:16:00">[29:16:00]</a>: A battery of several hundred "tough test cases" (e.g., specific math problems, nuanced evaluation scenarios) are used to assess model performance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="29:24:00">[29:24:00]</a>. Initially, models failed on 70% of these, but this has improved to under 10% <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="29:46:00">[29:46:00]</a>.
*   **Machine and Human Labeling** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="30:10:00">[30:10:00]</a>: AI is used to flag potential errors in interactions, which are then verified by human labelers to determine accuracy and conversation productivity (e.g., student engagement vs. disengagement) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="30:13:00">[30:13:00]</a> <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="30:44:00">[30:44:00]</a>.
