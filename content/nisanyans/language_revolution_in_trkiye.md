The Language Revolution in Trkiye is a distinct historical event, separate from the [[alphabet_revolution_in_trkiye | Alphabet Revolution]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:24:26">[01:24:26]</a>.

### Distinction from the [[alphabet_revolution_in_trkiye | Alphabet Revolution]]

The Language Revolution and the [[alphabet_revolution_in_trkiye | Alphabet Revolution]] are "two completely independent events" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:24:26">[01:24:26]</a>. While the [[alphabet_revolution_in_trkiye | Alphabet Revolution]] occurred in 1927-1928 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:28:33">[01:28:33]</a>, the Language Revolution began later <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:06:11">[10:06:11]</a>.

### Timeline

The Language Revolution commenced in 1932 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:33:36">[01:33:36]</a> and reached its peak in 1935 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:36:35">[01:36:35]</a>. This means there was a difference of approximately four to five years between it and the [[alphabet_revolution_in_trkiye | Alphabet Revolution]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:40:45">[01:40:45]</a>.

### Objectives and Nature

The primary aim of the Language Revolution was "to eliminate all the words of Arabic and Persian origin in Turkish" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:20:22">[10:20:22]</a>. It is described as "a whole different madness" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="10:22:26">[10:22:26]</a>.
