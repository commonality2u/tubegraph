Societies establish various norms to protect their peace, tranquility, and comfort <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:46">[00:03:46]</a>. A key debate arises regarding the extent of individual freedom versus societal expectations. While traditional liberal thought asserts that adults should be free to do anything with mutual consent as long as it doesn't harm third parties <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:50">[00:02:50]</a>, ignoring potential social harms when considering mutual consent is seen as problematic <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:01">[00:04:01]</a>. Societal norms and practices, whether traditional or modern, can significantly impact population dynamics.
