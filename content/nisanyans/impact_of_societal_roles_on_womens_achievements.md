## Perceived Lack of Achievement

Similarly, in politics, there is an implied limited representation or success <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:10">[00:01:10]</a>. Despite instances of women exhibiting genius in certain fields, it is argued that "as a rule it does not come out when opportunities are given" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:33">[00:01:33]</a>.

## Rejection of External Factors

The idea that oppression and [[male_dominance_and_its_impact_on_womens_opportunities | male domination]] explain this pattern is dismissed as incorrect <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:44">[00:01:44]</a>.

## Biological and Psychological Differences

A central argument posits that women and men have different biological functions and genetic structures, which lead to distinct psychological advantages and disadvantages <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:00">[00:02:00]</a>. The primary biological function of women is stated to be giving birth, ensuring the child's safety, care, and feeding <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:45">[00:02:45]</a>. In contrast, men are described as compensating for their biological "dysfunctions" or weaknesses in the biological field through skills and ambitions in other areas <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:11">[00:03:11]</a>.

This perspective is reinforced by comparing human behavior to that of mammals like monkeys, where the functions of males and females are described as "very obvious and very logical" due to biological evolution <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:40">[00:03:40]</a>. The fundamental nature of a human being is described as an animal whose primary function is sexual intercourse and reproduction, with differing capabilities between women and men in these roles <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:20">[00:05:20]</a>.

## Modern Society and Equality

In contemporary society, where "tactile functions have acquired such complex, unnatural qualities," it is acknowledged that women should be granted certain rights and that equality is a necessary societal arrangement <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:00">[00:04:00]</a>. However, the underlying premise remains that human beings are fundamentally animals, and societal structures are akin to "decorations" over this basic nature <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:25">[00:04:25]</a>.

The speaker concludes that these facts are often disregarded in an age that prioritizes "ideological lies more respected than objective truths" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:46">[00:05:46]</a>.
