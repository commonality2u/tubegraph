*   **Difficulty with Complex Cognition** As individuals age, they may slowly lose the ability to:
    *   Comprehend complex events simultaneously <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:43">[00:01:43]</a>.
    *   Connect the beginning and end of ideas <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:47">[00:01:47]</a>.

    *   Quickly extract possible answers and examples from memory <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:10">[00:02:10]</a>.
*   **Impaired Thinking Quality** The conditions for "thinking correctly" and "lively thinking" are slowly lost <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:17">[00:02:17]</a>.

## Experience as Armor

However, this "shell" might hide an internal softness or diminished capacity <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:37">[00:02:37]</a>. The speaker expresses uncertainty about whether relying on this "armor" is the right approach <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:50">[00:02:50]</a>.
