Abandoning these territories at the negotiation table is considered impossible, as it would lead to the disintegration of Russia <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:23:51">[00:23:51]</a>. These are declared historically Russian lands with overwhelmingly Russian populations, and their belonging to Ukraine is attributed to Lenin's past donation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:15">[00:24:15]</a>. Russia has issued passports and made investments in these regions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:39">[00:24:39]</a>.
