## Underlying Issues: Bureaucratic Rationality and Education
The speaker links the issues in media reporting to "bureaucratic rationality" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:27">[00:24:27]</a> and a lack of comprehensive education, falling under the broader topic of [[reflection_on_science_reason_and_propaganda_in_modern_society | reflection on science, reason and propaganda in modern society]].

### Bureaucratic Rationality
Propaganda, such as that concerning Ukraine, is seen as a "successful operation" from a bureaucratic perspective <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:24:44">[00:24:44]</a>. Bureaucratic rationality focuses on setting a target and finding efficient methods to reach it, like an "algorithmic thought" or a "process diagram" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:54">[00:25:54]</a>. However, this logic "does not concern" human, moral, social, cultural, economic, or political factors <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:26:24">[00:26:24]</a>. Its focus is solely on fulfilling a "certain task" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:27:13">[00:27:13]</a>.

### Deficiencies in Education
The speaker argues that bureaucratic logic's limitations stem from a lack of "educational equipment" to understand events comprehensively <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:27:33">[00:27:33]</a>. Universities are criticized for being "completely directed to train bureaucrats" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:27:54">[00:27:54]</a>, failing to train individuals who "can comprehend and care about such things" as broader societal impacts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:28:44">[00:28:44]</a>.

For "quality education," the speaker suggests a return to fundamental disciplines:
*   **History:** To understand past mistakes and long-term trends <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:28:48">[00:28:48]</a>.
*   **Philosophy:** To teach "reverse thinking" and challenge simple inferences and algorithms <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:29:24">[00:29:24]</a>, increasing "flexibility" and "acrobatics" of the brain <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:30:20">[00:30:20]</a>.
*   **Theology:** To foster thinking about basic and moral principles <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:30:26">[00:30:26]</a>.

This type of comprehensive education is "gone," and the world no longer raises such people <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:11:14">[01:11:14]</a>. Consequently, society is "rapidly advancing towards a world whose algorithmic rationality is condemned to bureaucratic and technocratic minds," which the speaker believes "will be a disaster for humanity" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:31:33">[00:31:33]</a>.
