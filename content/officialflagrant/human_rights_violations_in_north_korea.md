## Government Control and Oppression

The North Korean regime maintains absolute control over its citizens through a brutal system.

*   **Prison System**: North Korea operates three types of prisons:
    *   **Re-education camps**: Sentences are typically less than two years <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:18:27">[01:18:27]</a>.
    *   **Labor camps**: Sentences can be over ten years <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:18:32">[01:18:32]</a>.
    *   **Political prison camps (Concentration Camps)**: These are lifetime sentences, but prisoners rarely last more than three months, often worked to death and forced to clean nuclear weapon debris, leading to high cancer rates <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:18:39">[01:18:39]</a>. The entire country is described as a prison camp, with the ocean sealed by guards <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:35:00">[01:35:00]</a>.
*   **Targeting of Defectors**: Defectors like Yeonmi Park are placed on killing lists, and their families back in North Korea are denounced and "disappeared" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:08:10">[01:08:10]</a>.
*   **Propaganda and Image Management**:
