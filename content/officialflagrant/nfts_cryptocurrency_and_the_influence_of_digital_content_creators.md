### Shifting Influence and Value
The landscape of [[fame_and_influence_of_internet_creators | fame]] is undergoing a significant shift, with the value of the "influencer" and "online entrepreneur" becoming increasingly recognized <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:35:08">[00:35:08]</a>. *   **Direct Engagement:** Unlike traditional TV shows that sell advertisements for a product months after shooting, [[the_influence_of_streaming_platforms_and_content_creation | digital content]] can be produced and monetized with ads within days <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:35:45">[00:35:45]</a>.

Paul mentions Emma Chamberlain as an example of a YouTube star who has become a "massive Global star" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:34:02">[00:34:02]</a>.
