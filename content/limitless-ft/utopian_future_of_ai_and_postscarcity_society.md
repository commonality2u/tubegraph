## Post-Scarcity Paradigm

In this optimistic scenario, [[ai_and_technological_advancements | AI models]], specifically large language models (LLMs), could [[impact_of_ai_on_job_replacement_and_human_labor | replace a significant portion of human jobs and work currently performed]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:05">[00:00:05]</a>. This replacement is envisioned to generate widespread value for everyone <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:11">[00:00:11]</a>.

The core benefit of this outcome is the elimination of the necessity for human beings to labor purely for survival <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:14">[00:00:14]</a>. For the first time in history, humanity could transition to a "post scarcity" world paradigm <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:22">[00:00:22]</a>. In such a society, the constant struggle to merely exist would cease to be a driving force <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:24">[00:00:24]</a>, allowing individuals the freedom to choose their pursuits after simply "being alive" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>.
