Developing a high-functioning, highly effective team is a lengthy process that cannot be rushed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:03">[00:01:03]</a>. It requires a significant investment of time to identify, test, and integrate individuals into a cohesive unit.

## Assembling Elite Talent

A key component of an effective organization is the assembly of a core group of exceptionally talented individuals <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:23">[00:00:23]</a>. These individuals are rare, with only a few potentially being encountered each year <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:32">[00:00:32]</a>.

Examples of such [[examples_of_talented_individuals_in_musks_companies | talented individuals in Musk's companies]] include:
*   [[examples_of_talented_individuals_in_musks_companies | Mark Josa]] at SpaceX <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>
*   [[examples_of_talented_individuals_in_musks_companies | Steve Davis]] at The Boring Company <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>

Each of Elon Musk's companies typically features around five individuals who are considered "out of this world talented" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>.

## The Long Vetting Process

Identifying true potential and reliability in an individual takes approximately a decade of testing and observation <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:36">[00:00:36]</a>. This extensive vetting period is crucial for ensuring that individuals can consistently perform and contribute to the team's objectives.

## Cultivating a "Mind Meld"

Once identified and integrated, highly effective team members develop a "mind meld" where they understand how to work together without needing explicit communication <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:58">[00:00:58]</a>. This level of [[the_role_of_teamwork_in_elon_musks_success | teamwork]] and intuitive understanding significantly enhances the collective's efficiency.

## The Overall Timeframe

The creation of a self-sustaining and maximally effective organizational "organism" can take around 20 years <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:09">[00:01:09]</a>. This timeframe underscores that building a top-tier [[the_evolution_of_a_highfunctioning_organizational_structure | organizational structure]] and team is a long-term developmental process rather than an overnight achievement.

> "It literally takes like 20 years to kind of build an organism almost that is self- sustaining and maximally evolved to be effective." <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:07">[00:01:07]</a>
