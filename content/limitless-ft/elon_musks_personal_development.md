## The Power of Team Assembly

A significant, yet often "underappreciated," aspect of [[elon_musks_influential_superteam | Elon Musk]]'s effectiveness is his ability to assemble a highly skilled team <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:14">[00:00:14]</a>. Over 20 to 30 years, he has built a collective of approximately 20 individuals who are regarded as "some of the most talented humans on the planet" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>. Finding such individuals is rare, with only "a couple of these people every year" typically encountered <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:32">[00:00:32]</a>.

### Cultivating Talent

The process of vetting and integrating these top-tier talents is extensive, often taking "a decade of... testing someone and testing what they can do to really learn their true potential and learn that they're reliable" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:39">[00:00:39]</a>. Examples of these key individuals include Mark Josa at [[spacexs_impact_on_reducing_the_cost_of_launching_to_orbit | SpaceX]] and Steve Davis at The Boring Company <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>. Each of [[elon_musks_influential_superteam | Elon Musk]]'s companies reportedly features "five or so people that are just out of this world talented" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:52">[00:00:52]</a>.

These core team members possess a unique "mind meld" that enables them to collaborate seamlessly, often without explicit communication, knowing intuitively "what needs to get done" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:58">[00:00:58]</a>. This level of organizational synergy "can't rush" and is estimated to require about 20 years to forge into a "self-sustaining and maximally evolved" operational organism <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:06">[00:01:06]</a>.
