## Building Talented Teams

A key, often "underappreciated" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:16">[00:00:16]</a>, aspect of Musk's effectiveness is his ability to assemble highly talented teams <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:23">[00:00:23]</a>. Over 20 to 30 years, he has cultivated a collective of approximately 20 individuals who are considered among the "most talented humans on the planet" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>.

### Characteristics of Musk's Teams
*   **Rarity of Talent**: Such individuals are rare, encountered perhaps only "a couple... every year" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:34">[00:00:34]</a>.
*   **Long-term Vetting**: Identifying and building trust with these individuals requires extensive vetting, taking "a decade of like testing someone and testing what they can do to really learn their true potential and learn that they're reliable" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:36">[00:00:36]</a>.
*   **Exceptional Talent**: Each of his companies typically features about five "out of this world talented" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a> individuals. [[examples_of_talented_individuals_in_musks_companies | Examples of talented individuals in Musks companies]] include Mark Josa at [[spacexs_impact_on_lowering_space_travel_costs_with_starship | SpaceX]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:43">[00:00:43]</a> and Steve Davis at Boring Company <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>.
*   **Seamless Collaboration**: These core team members possess a "mind meld" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:58">[00:00:58]</a>, allowing them to work effectively together, often without needing verbal communication to understand what needs to be done <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>.

### The Time Investment in Team Building
Building such a highly functional and self-sustaining "organism" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:09">[00:01:09]</a> of talent cannot be rushed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:06">[00:01:06]</a>. It literally takes "like 20 years to kind of build" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:06">[00:01:06]</a> a maximally evolved and effective collective <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:11">[00:01:11]</a>. This long-term commitment to developing and integrating top talent is a critical component of [[the_role_of_teamwork_in_elon_musks_success | Elon Musks success]].
