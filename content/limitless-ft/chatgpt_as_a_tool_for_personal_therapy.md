### A Safe Space for Difficult Conversations

One of the key benefits identified is ChatGPT's utility in opening up about subjects that are otherwise hard to discuss <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:51">[00:00:51]</a>. This is particularly noted for men, who may find it easier to articulate challenging thoughts to a robot than to another human <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:55">[00:00:55]</a>. This function provides a "safe space" for individuals to explore difficult topics when they lack the "optionality," "surface area," or a suitable person to engage with <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:02">[00:01:02]</a>.

While interacting with this "weird social genie in a bottle" about personal afflictions might seem "outside of [[social_etiquette_and_talking_to_ai|social etiquette]]" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:34">[00:00:34]</a>, its role as a confessional tool is significant.
