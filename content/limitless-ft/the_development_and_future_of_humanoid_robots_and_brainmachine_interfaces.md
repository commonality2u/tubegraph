Deviating too much from the human form factor makes it significantly harder for [[advancements_in_energy_and_robotics | robots]] to interact with this existing environment <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:11:06">[01:11:06]</a>.
2.  **Psychological Comfort**: Humans tend to react better when [[advancements_in_energy_and_robotics | robots]] resemble humans <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:11:34">[01:11:34]</a>.
