J. Paul Getty, widely recognized as one of the wealthiest private citizens in history, often attributed a significant portion of his success to the influence and guidance of his father, George Getty. Throughout his life, Getty acknowledged the profound impact his father had on him both personally and professionally.

## Early Influence and Entry into the Oil Business

George Getty, a successful attorney turned oilman, was the catalyst for J. Paul Getty's entry into the oil industry. According to Getty, his father introduced him to the oil business when J. Paul was quite young. With the guidance and support of his father, Getty's path in life was significantly shaped:

> "Without the love and training of my father, I would not be the man I became" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:09">[00:02:09]</a>.

George Getty's decision to involve his son in the intricate facets of the oil industry from a young age provided J. Paul with invaluable hands-on experience. Starting as early as age 11, Getty observed oil operations and performed laborious tasks in the oil fields by 15, laying the foundation for his future success in the industry <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:50">[00:05:50]</a>.

## Cultivating a Strong Work Ethic

J. Paul Getty credited his father with instilling a strong work ethic and the principles of self-reliance and hard work:

> "My father believed in work ethic... he made me learn that money must be earned" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:35">[00:07:35]</a>.

George Getty's approach was to ensure that his son earned his keep, even when working in his own company. This experience was instrumental in developing a resilient and determined mindset in J. Paul Getty, which was essential in overcoming future business challenges.

## A Role Model and Mentor

The respect with which J. Paul Getty spoke of his father reflects the lasting impact George Getty had on him as a role model and mentor. Repeatedly throughout his life, J. Paul Getty expressed immense admiration for his father's intelligence and character:

> "His loving kindness and great heart combined with a charming simplicity of manner made George Getty the idol of all who knew him" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:50">[00:16:50]</a>.

George Getty's influence extended beyond professional guidance. His father imparted on him the values of honesty, integrity, and resilience, which shaped Getty's approach to both business and life challenges <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:16:53">[00:16:53]</a>.

## Legacy and Responsibility

The death of George Getty was a profound moment for J. Paul Getty, who spoke of the immense responsibility he felt to carry on the work his father had begun:

> "I can only strive to carry on to the best of my ability the life work of an abler man" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:17:01">[00:17:01]</a>.

This sense of obligation propelled J. Paul Getty to new heights, driven by a desire to not only live up to his father's legacy but to expand it into a global business empire <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:40:51">[00:40:51]</a>.

## Reflections on Fatherhood

Despite his professional success, J. Paul Getty's complex relationship with his own children highlighted the contrast between his upbringing and that of his sons. While Getty was able to benefit from his father's direct mentorship, his fractured personal life made it difficult for him to provide the same level of guidance to his children:

> "I had hoped and desired that my sons would enter the family business, eventually taking over for me... I experienced many disappointments" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:54:34">[00:54:34]</a>.

Though J. Paul Getty strived to involve his sons in his businesses, he recognized the impact his own father's influence had on his success, contrasting his experiences with those of his children <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:52:56">[00:52:56]</a>.

In summary, George Getty's influence on J. Paul Getty was profound and enduring. His father's teachings and example were not only pivotal in Getty's career choices but also in weaving the fabric of values that underpinned his life and legacy.
