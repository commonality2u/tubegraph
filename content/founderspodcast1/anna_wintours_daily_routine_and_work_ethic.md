Anna Wintour is renowned for her meticulous approach to her daily routine and her rigorous work ethic, which have played a significant role in her sustained influence and leadership in the fashion industry.

## Early Morning Routine

Anna Wintour begins her day with a disciplined morning routine that sets the tone for her workload. She rises by 5:00 a.m. and exercises at 5:30 a.m. Following her workout, she sits for 30 minutes of professional hair and makeup before being chauffeured to her office, where her three assistants are already waiting for her arrival <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:00">[00:00:00]</a>.

## Work Schedule and Management Style

Wintour's work schedule is meticulously planned, reflecting her preference for order and precision. Her requests to her assistants are constant and span day and night, weekdays and weekends, typically delivered through emails with no subject lines. This reflects her emphasis on directness and efficiency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:23">[00:00:23]</a>.

## Authority and Influence

Since taking over as editor-in-chief of Vogue in 1988, Anna Wintour has significantly expanded the power and influence the position holds within the fashion world. Her authority is such that her support can make a substantial difference for fashion designers, often securing them opportunities to lead bigger labels and providing them with vital coverage in the magazine <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:33">[00:00:33]</a>.

Wintour's rigorous stewardship is evident in every aspect of Vogue, from editorial decisions down to her influence over high-profile events like the Met Gala. She oversees every detail, believing firmly that "God is in the details" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:13:55">[00:13:55]</a>.

## Decisiveness

Anna Wintour's decisiveness is one of her most admired qualities. Asked about the number one thing she hoped people would learn from her, Wintour cited decisiveness and clarity as indispensable traits <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:57">[00:10:57]</a>. This approach is reflected in how she runs Vogue, insisting on maintaining high standards and reinforcing the necessity of excellence among her team <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:00:31">[01:00:31]</a>.

## Work Environment

Despite the rigorous demands she places on her team, many who work closely with Wintour praise her directness. It creates an environment where expectations are clear, and everyone knows where they stand <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:45">[00:01:45]</a>.

For Anna, work is not merely a professional obligation but a passion that drives her every action. Her engaged and sometimes relentless approach ensures that she remains not just a leader at Vogue but an influential figure whose impact extends far beyond the magazine <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:02:24">[01:02:24]</a>.

Through her disciplined daily routine and unwavering commitment to her work, Anna Wintour stands as a paragon of success and influence in the fashion world.
