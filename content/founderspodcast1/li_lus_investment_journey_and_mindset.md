Central to Li Lu’s investment philosophy is the focus on understanding a business from an owner’s perspective—seeing through to its operational core rather than merely speculating on market movements. This approach aligns closely with [[charlie_mungers_investment_philosophy | Charlie Munger’s investment philosophy]] of concentrating investments on enduringly great businesses purchased at fair prices <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="07:00:00">[07:00:00]</a>.
