The 0.8.0 update for [[Minecraft Pocket Edition 080 update features | Minecraft Pocket Edition]], available on iOS and Android, is described as the biggest update seen for the game thus far <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>. This update introduces numerous new items and blocks, significantly expanding gameplay possibilities, particularly in [[enhancements_in_creative_mode | Creative Mode]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:54">[00:00:54]</a>.

## Transportation and Rails
A major addition to the game is the [[introduction_of_rails_and_minecarts | introduction of rails and mine carts]], including powered rails <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>. Players can now ride inside mine carts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:25">[00:03:25]</a>.

## Building Materials and Structures
The update expands the variety of building blocks available:
*   **Different Colored Wood** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:27">[00:01:27]</a>
*   **Iron Bars** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:56">[00:01:56]</a>
*   **Stone Fences** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:03">[00:02:03]</a>
*   **Bedrock** – can now be used for building in [[enhancements_in_creative_mode | Creative Mode]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:12">[00:02:12]</a>

## Farming and Plants
New agricultural items and seeds have been introduced:
*   **Hay Stacks** – useful for storing hay efficiently in small amounts of space <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>.
*   **New Seeds and Plants** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:19">[00:02:19]</a>:
    *   Pumpkin <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:26">[00:02:26]</a>
    *   Melon <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:30">[00:02:30]</a>
    *   Carrots <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:30">[00:02:30]</a>
    *   Potato <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:32">[00:02:32]</a>
    *   Beetroot Seeds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:37">[00:02:37]</a>

## Miscellaneous [[ingame_technology_and_items | Items]]
Other notable additions include:
*   **Sponges** – though they do not have a functional purpose within the game <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:07">[00:02:07]</a>.
*   **Ink Sacks** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:37">[00:02:37]</a>
*   **More Dyes** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:40">[00:02:40]</a>
*   **TNT** – can now be used in [[enhancements_in_creative_mode | Creative Mode]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:46">[00:02:46]</a>.

The update also features significant graphical improvements, making the game look more like its PC counterpart, and includes a much higher render distance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:04">[00:01:04]</a>. The update is free for existing owners of the application <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:14">[00:03:14]</a>.
