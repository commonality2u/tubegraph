### Packaging and Physical Feel
Japanese [[yugioh_structure_decks_and_starter_decks_comparison | structure decks]] come in plastic holders instead of the regular cardboard packaging used for US decks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:03">[00:03:03]</a>. Additionally, the physical Japanese cards are noted to "feel really good in the hand," suggesting a difference in card stock or finish <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:16">[00:04:16]</a>.

### Additional Items
Beyond the cards, the Japanese [[yugioh_structure_decks_and_starter_decks_comparison | Synchron Extreme Structure Deck]] includes a playmat featuring the deck's cover artwork <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:57">[00:01:57]</a>.
