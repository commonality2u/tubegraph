Chain Crowd Control (CC) is a [[league_of_legends_gameplay_strategy | gameplay strategy]] in [[league_of_legends | League of Legends]] that involves applying multiple crowd control abilities in sequence to keep an opponent immobilized or otherwise incapacitated for an extended period.

## Effectiveness and Impact
Chain CC can be highly effective, allowing players to "chain CC them so hard" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:12">[00:01:12]</a>. The power of chain CC, particularly from champions like [[brand_gameplay_strategies_in_league_of_legends | Brand]], has been noted to be so significant that it could potentially lead to a champion being disabled in [[league_of_legends_gameplay | gameplay]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:18">[00:01:18]</a>.
