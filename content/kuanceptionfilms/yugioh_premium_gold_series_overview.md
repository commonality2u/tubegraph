The Yu-Gi-Oh! Premium Gold series, specifically "Premium Gold Infinite Gold," is a product known for its desirable reprints <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:49">[00:00:49]</a>. The set's box art in 2016 appeared "quite red" and "very royal" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:18">[00:00:18]</a>, with the cards themselves featuring a "gold infinitely golden" aesthetic that was highly regarded <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:10">[00:00:10]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:18">[00:02:18]</a>.

## Release Information

The Premium Gold series was released around March 18th or 19th, 2016, as stated by the video creator making the video on "Saturday, March 19th" following its release "yesterday" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:29">[00:00:29]</a>.

## Product Contents

Each box of Premium Gold typically contains three packs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:49">[00:01:49]</a>. A single box contains 35 cards in total <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:00">[00:02:00]</a>.
