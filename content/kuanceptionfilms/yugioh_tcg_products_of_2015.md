The year 2015 saw the release of highly anticipated [[Yugioh card reprints and meta cards of 2015 | YuGiOh TCG products]], notably the 2015 Dark Rebellion Xyz Dragon Mega Tin <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:09">[00:00:09]</a>. This product was considered by some to be the most anticipated [[Yugioh card reprints and meta cards of 2015 | YuGiOh TCG product]] of 2015 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:14">[00:00:14]</a>.

## 2015 Dark Rebellion Xyz Dragon Mega Tin

The 2015 Dark Rebellion Xyz Dragon Mega Tin features eye-catching artwork <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:32">[00:01:32]</a>. Each tin includes:
*   A promo pack containing super rare cards <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:47">[00:00:47]</a>.
*   The Dark Rebellion Xyz Dragon card itself <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:51">[00:00:51]</a>.
*   Three 2015 Mega Tin Mega Packs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:54">[00:00:54]</a>.

### Promo Cards

The promo pack included in the 2015 Dark Rebellion Xyz Dragon Mega Tin typically contains:
*   Dark Rebellion Xyz Dragon (in a platinum rare reprint) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:42">[00:01:42]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:45">[00:01:45]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:55">[00:01:55]</a>
*   Nekroz of Gungnir <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:59">[00:01:59]</a>
*   Ptolemy M7 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:00">[00:02:00]</a>
*   Elder Entity Hastur <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:01">[00:02:01]</a>
*   Castel the Skyblaster Musketeer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:08">[00:02:08]</a>

These promo cards are considered "fantastic pulls" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:09">[00:02:09]</a> and are included with every Dark Rebellion Xyz Dragon 2015 Mega Tin <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:15">[00:02:15]</a>.
