The [[value_and_rarity_of_yugioh_cards | collectibility and value of YuGiOh cards]] are significantly influenced by factors such as age, rarity, and individual card utility. Products like the Yu-Gi-Oh 2012 Premium Collection 10 become particularly sought after over time.

## Yu-Gi-Oh 2012 Premium Collection 10

The Yu-Gi-Oh 2012 Premium Collection 10 is a tin that contains 14 foil cards in every pack <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:06">[00:00:06]</a>. Released more than three years prior to 2015, this tin is considered "pretty rare nowadays to find" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:14">[00:00:14]</a>, making it a desirable item for [[chasing_rare_yugioh_cards | collectors]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:11">[00:00:11]</a>.
