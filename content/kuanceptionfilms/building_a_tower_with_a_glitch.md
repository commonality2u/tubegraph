This guide explains a glitch in Minecraft Pocket Edition on iOS that allows players to instantly teleport to the top of a tower using a door and specific building techniques <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:01">[00:02:01]</a>.

## Materials Needed

To perform this glitch, you will need:
*   A wooden door or a trap door <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:10">[00:02:10]</a>
*   Building materials such as cobblestone or wood <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:16">[00:02:16]</a>

## How to Perform the Glitch

1.  **Build a Tower:** Construct a tower that is "exactly straight" with a central column, described as a "pull straight down the middle" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:19">[00:02:19]</a>.
2.  **Place the Door:** Position a door underneath the tower <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:34">[00:02:34]</a>.
3.  **Enter and Close:** Walk up to the door and close it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:36">[00:02:36]</a>. You should become stuck inside the door <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:44">[00:02:44]</a>.
4.  **Exit the Game:** While stuck, close out of the game <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:46">[00:02:46]</a>.
5.  **Re-enter the Game:** Go back into the game <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:48">[00:02:48]</a>.
6.  **Teleportation:** Upon re-entering, you should find yourself on top of the tower <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:51">[00:02:51]</a>.

*   **Emergency Teleportation:** If attacked by zombies or other hostile mobs, you can quickly [[using_glitches_for_teleportation_in_minecraft_pe|teleport]] to safety on top of a tower <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:06">[00:03:06]</a>.
