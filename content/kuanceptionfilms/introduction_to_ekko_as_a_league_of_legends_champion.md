### Resonance (Passive)

### W - Parallel Convergence
This ability has a passive effect where Ekko's basic attacks deal extra magic damage to all low-health enemies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:35">[00:00:35]</a>.

### Phase Dive
