The "Become the Turtle" concept originated from a video by Drifter, specifically his "EXO Shield inep video" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:20">[00:00:20]</a>, which inspired Conception Films to adopt this playstyle <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>.

## Gameplay Application

This concept is applied in *Call of Duty: Advanced Warfare*, where players embody a "turtle" playstyle <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:34">[00:00:34]</a>. The video showcases a montage of "greatest moments" achieved while "being the turtle" in the game <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:31">[00:00:31]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:34">[00:00:34]</a>.

## Purpose of the Video

The video serves as a [[creating_gaming_tributes | tribute to Drifter]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:36">[00:00:36]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:39">[00:00:39]</a>, encouraging viewers who are also fans to share it with him <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.
