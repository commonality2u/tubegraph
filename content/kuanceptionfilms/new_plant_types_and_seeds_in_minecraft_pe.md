The [[minecraft_pocket_edition_080_update_features | Minecraft Pocket Edition 0.8.0 update]], available on iOS and Android, introduced significant changes and additions to the game, including an expansion of agricultural content <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:25:00">[00:25:00]</a>.

## Expanded Plant and Seed Variety

The update brought several new types of seeds and plants to the game, diversifying farming options <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:18:41">[02:18:41]</a>:

*   **Pumpkin** (New) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:28:44">[02:28:44]</a>
*   **Melon** (New) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:30:26">[02:30:26]</a>
*   **Carrots** (New) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:31:37">[02:31:37]</a>
*   **Potato** (New) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:33:04">[02:33:04]</a>
*   **Beetroot Seeds** (New) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:35:46">[02:35:46]</a>

Existing plant types like sugar cane and wheat seeds remained available <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:23:01">[02:23:01]</a>.

## New Storage and Utility Items

In addition to new plants, the update also introduced items beneficial for farming and resource management:

*   **Hay Stacks**: These allow for more efficient storage of hay in smaller amounts of space, enabling players to keep more hay in chests <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:33:48">[01:33:48]</a>.
*   **Ink Sacks and More Dyes**: The update included ink sacks and an expanded variety of dyes <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:37:37">[02:37:37]</a>.
