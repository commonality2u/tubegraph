## Puzzles and Quests

Puzzles are a central element of [[paradigm_game_overview | Paradigm]]. Early challenges include:
*   **Powering a Computer** The player must figure out how to plug in a computer character named "John" to make it functional <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:35">[00:03:35]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:00">[00:04:00]</a>.
*   **Operating System Repair** A computer character (reminiscent of HAL 9000, displaying [[parody_elements_in_gaming_videos | parody elements]]) tasks the player with fixing its bugged operating system, requiring them to "reinstall" and find "lines every flaw piggies" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:04">[00:05:04]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:09">[00:05:09]</a>. This quest involves acquiring specific items, such as "fixie singles" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:21">[00:05:21]</a>.
*   **Mailbox Puzzle** A multi-step puzzle requires the player to find a hidden "babushka mailbox" and retrieve a map from it <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:46">[00:10:46]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:50">[00:10:50]</a>. This map is then used to complete the current objective <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:42">[00:11:42]</a>.

Players are encouraged to pick up and utilize various items found in the environment to solve these challenges <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:47">[00:06:47]</a>. The game's demo concludes upon the completion of these interconnected puzzles <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:24">[00:12:24]</a>.
