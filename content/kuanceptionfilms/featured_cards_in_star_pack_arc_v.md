## Highly Sought After Cards

### Dark Rebellion XYZ Dragon
The [[galaxy_eyes_photon_dragon_and_other_valuable_cards | Dark Rebellion XYZ Dragon]] is considered one of the best cards in the Star Pack Arc V set, making it a primary target for players opening packs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:14">[00:00:14]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:29">[00:01:29]</a>. Despite its desirability, pulling this card can be challenging <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:10">[00:06:10]</a>.

### Constellar Pleiades
[[stardust_warrior_and_other_featured_cards | Constellar Pleiades]] is another highly valued card within the set <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:15">[00:00:15]</a>. It is regarded as one of the best cards available <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:26">[00:01:26]</a> and can be difficult to acquire outside of [[unboxing_star_pack_arc_v | Star Pack Arc V]] booster packs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:38">[00:06:38]</a>. Obtaining multiple copies of this card is considered a very good outcome <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:51">[00:01:51]</a>.

### Odd-Eyes Pendulum Dragon
[[stardust_warrior_and_other_featured_cards | Odd-Eyes Pendulum Dragon]] is also a desirable card to pull from the set <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:33">[00:01:33]</a>.
