The Yu-Gi-Oh 2012 Premium Collection Tin is a highly sought-after item within the trading card game community, containing 14 foil cards in each tin <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:06">[00:00:06]</a>. As of 2015, this tin was considered pretty rare to find, being a product released more than three years prior <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:14">[00:00:14]</a>.

## Contents of the Tin

Upon [[unboxing_yugioh_premium_collection_tin | opening]] the tin, collectors will find the 14 foil cards along with a "Premium Collection Beginner's Guide" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:59">[00:00:59]</a>.

### Guaranteed Cards

Two specific cards are guaranteed to be included in every Yu-Gi-Oh 2012 Premium Collection Tin:
*   Elemental Hero The Shining <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:14">[00:01:14]</a>
*   Number 34: Terror-Byte <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:31">[00:01:31]</a>
