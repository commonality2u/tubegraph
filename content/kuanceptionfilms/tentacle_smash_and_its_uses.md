Tentacle Smash is an ability in League of Legends, specifically the Q ability of the champion Illaoi <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:34">[00:00:34]</a>. It is part of Illaoi's broader kit, which includes abilities like Test of Spirit (E) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:57">[00:00:57]</a> and Leap of Faith (ultimate) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:00">[00:01:00]</a>. These [[champion_abilities_and_combos | champion abilities and combos]] define much of her [[ingame_strategies_and_tactics | ingame strategies and tactics]].

## Mechanics
When activated, Illaoi slams tentacles down, damaging all enemies caught in the area of effect <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[00:00:37]</a>.

## Strategic Uses
Tentacle Smash can be utilized to harass an opponent in the lane from a safe distance <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:43">[00:00:43]</a>. This [[ingame_strategies_and_tactics | ingame strategy]] helps subdue opponents, particularly when they move to attack Illaoi's tentacles <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>.
