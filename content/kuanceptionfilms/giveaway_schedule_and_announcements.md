The channel announced a giveaway for the [[emperor_of_darkness_structure_deck|Emperor of Darkness Structure Deck]] to show appreciation for active subscribers and the Yu-Gi-Oh! community, especially after a period of less activity due to school commitments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:24">[00:00:24]</a>.

## Giveaway Item
Three copies of the Emperor of Darkness Structure Deck will be given away <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:17">[00:01:17]</a>. This deck, described as the "new monarch stack," was considered a top-tier meta deck at the time, and three copies could be combined to build a competitive deck for approximately $30 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:23">[00:01:23]</a>.

## How to Enter
To be eligible for the giveaway, participants must:
*   Subscribe to the channel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:47">[00:01:47]</a>.
*   Like the giveaway video <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:06">[00:02:06]</a>.
*   Leave a genuine comment explaining why they want the deck, why they like Yu-Gi-Oh!, or why they like the channel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:16">[00:02:16]</a>.
*   Like the channel's Facebook page <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:50">[00:02:50]</a>.
These steps are described as "four really easy steps" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:02">[00:03:02]</a>. More detailed instructions can be found in [[how_to_enter_the_yugioh_giveaway|How to Enter the Yugioh Giveaway]].
