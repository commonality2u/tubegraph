The *Star Pack ARC V* set contains several notable cards that collectors and players aim to acquire <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:12"></a>. Each pack in this set contains three cards <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:32"></a>.

## Highly Sought-After Cards

The most desired cards in *Star Pack ARC V* include:
*   **Dark Rebellion XYZ Dragon** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:14"></a>. This card is consistently targeted during pack openings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:45"></a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:29"></a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:38"></a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:16"></a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:03"></a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:50"></a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:55"></a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:10"></a>.
*   [[constellar_pleiades_cards_in_star_pack_arc_v | Constellar Pleiades]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:15"></a>. It is considered one of the best cards in the set <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:26"></a>. This card is also noted as being "pretty hard to pull nowadays" unless it's from a *Star Pack* <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:38"></a>.
