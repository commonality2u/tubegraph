## [[YuGiOh Legendary Collection 3 Yugis World | Legendary Collection 3 Yugi's World]] The Game Board Edition

The product opened is the [[YuGiOh Legendary Collection 3 Yugis World | Legendary Collection 3 Yugi's World]] The Game Board Edition. <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:08">[00:00:08]</a> It includes the Seal of Orichalcos, other ultra rare cards, and tokens. <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:14">[00:00:14]</a> It also contains "dank mega packs" featuring reprints from Generation 1 Yu-Gi-Oh!, such as Dark Magician and Blue-Eyes White Dragon. <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:23">[00:00:23]</a>

### Unboxing Experience
The host expressed excitement, noting that they had never before opened a [[YuGiOh Legendary Collection 3 Yugis World | Legendary Collection 3 Yugi's World]] product. <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:51">[00:00:51]</a>

The box itself features "the game board edition" artwork and the Seal of Orichalcos artwork. <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:09">[00:01:09]</a>

#### Box Contents
Upon opening, the contents were revealed:
*   **Promo Pack**: A promo pack was found, topped with the Seal of Orichalcos. <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:22">[00:01:22]</a> This pack typically contains three ultra rares, the Seal of Orichalcos, and tokens. <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:07">[00:02:07]</a>
*   **Mega Packs**: Five "dank" mega packs were included in the box. <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:29">[00:01:29]</a> The artwork on the mega packs features Pharaoh Yami Yugi. <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:39">[00:01:39]</a>
