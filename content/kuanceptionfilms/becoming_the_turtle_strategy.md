The "Becoming the Turtle" strategy is a gameplay approach inspired by Drifter's EXO Shield inep video <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>. It advocates for players to adopt a defensive and resilient playstyle, embodying the characteristics of a turtle <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:02">[00:00:02]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:05">[00:00:05]</a>, <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:12">[00:00:12]</a>.

## Origin and Inspiration
The strategy was conceived by Conception Films, who was inspired by Drifter's content, specifically his EXO Shield video <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:26">[00:00:26]</a>. This inspiration led to the direct implementation of the "become the turtle" mindset in gameplay <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:29">[00:00:29]</a>.

## Application
This strategy has been applied in Call of Duty: Advanced Warfare <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:36">[00:00:36]</a>. A video montage showcases "greatest moments" achieved while employing this particular playstyle <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:31">[00:00:31]</a>.
