[[reprints_of_classic_yugioh_cards | Yugioh card reprints]] are a significant aspect of the Yugioh Trading Card Game (TCG), often appearing in special sets or tins designed to make popular or powerful cards more accessible. These products can be highly anticipated by the community.

## 2015 Dark Rebellion XYZ Dragon Mega Tins

The 2015 Dark Rebellion XYZ Dragon Mega Tin was a highly anticipated product of 2015 in the Yugioh TCG <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:14">[00:00:14]</a>. Each tin includes:
*   A [[promotional_yugioh_cards | promo pack]] containing super rares and the Dark Rebellion XYZ Dragon card itself <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:45">[00:00:45]</a>.
*   Three 2015 Mega Packs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:53">[00:00:53]</a>.

These Mega Packs are essentially [[reprints_of_classic_yugioh_cards | reprints]] of all the sets that were printed during the year, including cards from popular archetypes like Shadoll and Qliphort <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>.
