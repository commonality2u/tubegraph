## Box Contents and Opening
The booster box contains 50 packs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:37">[00:00:37]</a>, with each pack holding three cards <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:39">[00:00:39]</a>. The unboxing process involves manually opening the sealed box <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:10">[00:00:10]</a>. The box is described as "shiny" and "perfection" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>. Upon opening, the packs are found to be split into two sections of 25 packs each <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:13">[00:01:13]</a>. While the box may not contain many "good reprints," the presenter states they "work with what we got" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:51">[00:00:51]</a>. The overall [[yugioh_pack_opening_experience | pack opening experience]] is described as beautiful <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:55">[00:00:55]</a>.
