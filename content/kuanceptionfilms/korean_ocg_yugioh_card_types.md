This article details the opening of a Primal Origin Korean OCG (Original Card Game) booster box, showcasing various card types, rarities, and the overall experience of [[Opening Yugioh Card Packs | opening Yu-Gi-Oh! card packs]] from this region <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:33">[00:00:33]</a>.

## Booster Box Contents and Goals

The booster box contained approximately 40 packs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:17">[00:02:17]</a>. The primary goal for this unboxing was to pull Bujin cards and Galaxy-Eyes Photon cards <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:22">[00:01:22]</a>.

The Korean OCG packs were noted for their "above average" build quality <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:38">[00:07:38]</a> and the "art background" being "too strong" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:05">[00:10:05]</a>.
