Understanding the market value of Yu-Gi-Oh! cards involves considering factors like rarity, desirability, and individual card prices <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:54">[00:02:54]</a>.

## Product Rarity and Value
The [[yugioh_2012_premium_collection_tin | Yu-Gi-Oh! 2012 Premium Collection Tin]] is considered rare to find as of 2015, being a product released more than three years prior <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:14">[00:00:14]</a>. Each tin is advertised to contain 14 foil cards <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:06">[00:00:06]</a>.
