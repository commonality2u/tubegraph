The 2015 Dark Rebellion Xyz Dragon Mega Tin was one of the most anticipated Yu-Gi-Oh! TCG products of 2015 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:14">[00:00:14]</a>. This tin typically includes a promo pack and three 2015 Mega Tin Mega Packs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:47">[00:00:47]</a>. The Mega Packs are essentially reprints of all sets released that year, featuring cards from archetypes such as Stellarknight and Qliphort <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>.

## Featured Promo Cards
Each [[2015_dark_rebellion_xyz_dragon_mega_tin_opening|2015 Dark Rebellion Xyz Dragon Mega Tin opening]] comes with a set of promotional cards <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:15">[00:01:15]</a>.
The [[featured_cards_in_the_2015_mega_tin|featured cards in the 2015 Mega Tin]] include:
*   [[featured_cards_in_the_2015_mega_tin|Dark Rebellion Xyz Dragon]] (a platinum [[prismatic_rare_and_artifact_card_pulls|rare]] reprint) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:42">[00:01:42]</a>
*   Picked-Old Entity Hastur (a [[rare_and_super_rare_card_pulls|Secret Rare]] card) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:01">[00:02:01]</a>
*   [[featured_cards_in_the_2015_mega_tin|Castel, the Skyblaster Musketeer]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:05">[00:02:05]</a>
