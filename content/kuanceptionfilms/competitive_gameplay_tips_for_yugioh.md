## Core Deck Strategy: Anti-Meta, Burn, and Lockdown

The deck's primary strategy revolves around being a "troll, anti-meta, burn, and lockdown" deck <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:59">[00:00:59]</a>. This approach aims to disrupt opponent strategies, inflict steady damage, and prevent them from making plays.

### Key Competitive Elements

*   **Anti-Meta**: Targets popular strategies by running cards that counter common tactics, like special summoning or graveyard effects <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>.
*   **Burn Damage**: Utilizes cards that inflict direct life point damage to the opponent, contributing to a quicker win condition <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:02">[00:01:02]</a>.
*   **Lockdown**: Implements cards and combos that restrict the opponent's ability to summon monsters, activate effects, or attack, effectively "locking" them out of the game <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:03">[00:01:03]</a>.
*   **Troll**: Employs cards with effects that are particularly annoying or frustrating for opponents, often leading to concession <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:59">[00:00:59]</a>.

## Essential Monsters for Competitive Play

The monster lineup is crucial for establishing lockdown and generating advantage.

*   **Trap Trix Myrmeleo** (2 copies): Essential for the deck. When Normal Summoned, it adds any "Hole" Trap Card from the deck to the hand <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:20">[00:01:20]</a>. When Special Summoned, it can destroy an opponent's Spell or Trap Card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:29">[00:01:29]</a>. This ensures access to crucial traps like "Bottomless Trap Hole" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:25">[00:01:25]</a>.
*   **Trap Trix Dionaea** (2 copies): Special Summons Trap Trix monsters from the graveyard when Normal Summoned <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>. When Special Summoned, it adds a "Hole" Trap Card from the graveyard to the hand, allowing for reuse of cards like "Bottomless Trap Hole" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:48">[00:01:48]</a>.
*   **Trap Trix Trick Nepenthes** (2 copies): Features high defense and a good effect <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:59">[00:01:59]</a>.
*   **Trap Trix Atrax** (1 copy): Has high attack and a cool effect <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:05">[00:02:05]</a>.
*   **Fossil Dyna Pachycephalo** (1 copy): A highly essential card for this deck <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:11">[00:02:11]</a>. Its effect prevents either player from Special Summoning monsters <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:20">[00:02:20]</a>, and when flipped face-up, it destroys all Special Summoned monsters on the field <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:22">[00:02:22]</a>. Strategies are in place to protect this monster from attacks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:27">[00:02:27]</a>.
*   **Gravekeeper's Spy** (2 copies): Considered the best Level 4 Gravekeeper monster for the deck <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:33">[00:02:33]</a>. When flipped face-up, it can Special Summon another Gravekeeper's monster with 1500 or less ATK from the deck, enabling easy Rank 4 Xyz summons <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:40">[00:02:40]</a>.
*   **Summoner Monk** (1 copy): Useful for quickly getting Level 4 monsters onto the field for Xyz plays <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:55">[00:02:55]</a>. Can be used to Special Summon "Fossil Dyna Pachycephalo" to create a lockdown <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:05">[00:03:05]</a>.
*   **Kagetokage** (1 copy): Can be Special Summoned from the hand when a Level 4 monster is Normal Summoned <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:13">[00:03:13]</a>, aiding in Xyz summons.
*   **Malefic Stardust Dragon** (1 copy): Provides extra attack power <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:24">[00:03:24]</a>. It can be Special Summoned by banishing "Stardust Dragon" from the Extra Deck, meaning it doesn't take up a Main Deck slot <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:27">[00:03:27]</a>. It works well with the abundance of field cards in the deck <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:43">[00:03:43]</a>.
*   **Santa Claws** (1 copy): A valuable card for removing powerful opponent monsters like "Quasar" or "Cyber Dragon Infinity" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:46">[00:03:46]</a>. Its Tribute Summon cannot be negated if using the opponent's monsters <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:55">[00:03:55]</a>.

## Strategic Spell Cards

Spell cards support the lockdown, field control, and burn strategies.

*   **Black Garden** (1 copy): An "insane" field card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:09">[00:04:09]</a>. When a monster is Normal or Special Summoned (excluding Flip Summons), its ATK is halved, and the opposing player Special Summons an 800 ATK/DEF Level 2 token <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:12">[00:04:12]</a>. This can lead to building a "token army" for strategic plays <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:29">[00:04:29]</a>.
*   **Necrovalley** (2 copies): Really essential for this deck <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:43">[00:04:43]</a>. It boosts the ATK and DEF of Gravekeeper's monsters <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:47">[00:04:47]</a> and prevents opponents from Special Summoning monsters from the graveyard <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:50">[00:04:50]</a>, effectively shutting down decks like Shaddoll and Stellarknight <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:57">[00:04:57]</a>.
*   **Terraforming** (2 copies): Provides draw power for field cards, ensuring access to "Black Garden" or "Necrovalley" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:07">[00:05:07]</a>.
*   **Dark Hole** (1 copy): A destruction card that can be useful as some monsters have beneficial effects when sent to the graveyard <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:15">[00:05:15]</a>. "Raigeki" can be used as an alternative <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:17">[00:05:17]</a>.
*   **Swords of Revealing Light** (2 copies): Excellent for stalling time in a lockdown/troll deck <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:34">[00:05:34]</a>. Preferred over "Gravity Bind" because it affects Xyz monsters, which are common in Rank 4 builds <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:46">[00:05:46]</a>.
*   **Burden of the Mighty** (1 copy): A "trolling" card that causes each face-up opponent monster to lose 100 ATK times its own level <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:59">[00:05:59]</a>.
*   **Mystical Space Typhoon** (1 copy): Essential for backrow removal, especially for continuous Spell/Trap Cards <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:07">[00:06:07]</a>. "Night Beam" can be an alternative <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:12">[00:06:12]</a>.

## Defensive and Burn-Oriented Trap Cards

The core of the deck's lockdown and burn strategy lies in its trap cards.

*   **Bottomless Trap Hole** (1 copy): Essential for the deck as it removes strong Special Summoned monsters from play <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:22">[00:06:22]</a>. Trap Trix cards easily bring this card out <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:31">[00:06:31]</a>. It is currently limited to one <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:36">[00:06:36]</a>.
*   **Trap Hole** (2 copies): While often overlooked due to prevalence of Special Summons, it becomes powerful when "Fossil Dyna Pachycephalo" is on the field, forcing opponents to Normal Summon <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:41">[00:06:41]</a>. This creates a strong lockdown <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:57">[00:06:57]</a>.
*   **Trap Trix Trap Hole Nightmare** (1 copy): A very good trap card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:17">[00:07:17]</a>. When a monster Special Summoned this turn activates its effect on the opponent's field, this card negates that effect and destroys the monster <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:22">[00:07:22]</a>.
*   **Deep Dark Trap Hole** (1 copy): A backup for "Bottomless Trap Hole" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:32">[00:07:32]</a>. When a Level 5 or higher Effect Monster is Special Summoned, it banishes that monster <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:35">[00:07:35]</a>.
*   **Solemn Warning** (1 copy): A good and "trolly" card that negates the summoning of any monster <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:46">[00:07:46]</a>.
*   **Nightmare Wheel** (2 copies): Preferred over "Fiendish Chain" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:55">[00:07:55]</a>. It prevents the opponent's monster from attacking and inflicts 500 life points damage to the opponent during each of your Standby Phases <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:59">[00:07:59]</a>, contributing to the burn strategy <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:10">[00:08:10]</a>.
*   **Coffin Seller** (1 copy): Provides extra burn power <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:20">[00:08:20]</a>. Each time a monster is sent to the opponent's graveyard, it inflicts 300 damage <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:22">[00:08:22]</a>.
*   **Mirror Force** (1 copy): An essential trap card for obvious reasons <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:31">[00:08:31]</a>.
*   **Scrap-Iron Scarecrow** (1 copy): A "trolly" defensive card that re-sets itself face-down instead of going to the graveyard after use <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:36">[00:08:36]</a>.
*   **Magic Cylinder** (1 copy): Provides extra defensive power and burn damage <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:45">[00:08:45]</a>, dealing significant damage if the opponent has a high ATK monster <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:49">[00:08:49]</a>.
*   **Wall of Disruption** (1 copy): A highly effective card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:08:58">[00:08:58]</a>. When an opponent's monster declares an attack, all opponent's ATK position monsters lose 800 ATK for each monster they control <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:01">[00:09:01]</a>. For example, with three attack position monsters, each would lose 2400 ATK <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:12">[00:09:12]</a>. It serves as an alternative to "Mirror Force" and is quite "trolly" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:27">[00:09:27]</a>.
*   **Compulsory Evacuation Device** (1 copy): A pretty essential Spell/Trap card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:32">[00:09:32]</a>.
*   **Ceasefire** (2 copies): Provides extra burn power <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:37">[00:09:37]</a>. With six monsters on the field (three on each side), activating two "Ceasefire" cards can inflict 6000 life points of damage <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:09:43">[00:09:43]</a>.

## Extra Deck: Essential Xyz Monsters

The Extra Deck should primarily consist of Rank 4 Xyz monsters, with two being particularly essential.

*   **Lavalval Chain**: Essential for competitive play <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:21">[00:10:21]</a>. Used to place "Fossil Dyna Pachycephalo" on top of the deck, ensuring access to the lockdown <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:31">[00:10:31]</a>. Also good for milling <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:35">[00:10:35]</a>.
*   **Number 101: Silent Honor ARK**: Considered to have a "broken" effect and is an overall great defensive Rank 4 Xyz <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:23">[00:10:23]</a>.
*   **Number 50: Blackship of Corn**: Has a good effect, dealing 1000 damage each turn if the opponent has a monster with less attack <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:47">[00:10:47]</a>.
*   **Number 55: Gogogo Goliath**: A temporary Rank 4 Xyz with a good effect <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:10:57">[00:10:57]</a>.
*   **Number 39: Utopia**: An essential Rank 4 Xyz card for any deck <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:06">[00:11:06]</a>.
*   **Stardust Dragon**: Included solely to enable the Special Summon of "Malefic Stardust Dragon" by removing it from play <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:15">[00:11:15]</a>. Since the deck doesn't have tuners, "Stardust Dragon" itself won't be summoned, making it a "free" way to get a powerful monster onto the field if a field card is active <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:21">[00:11:21]</a>.

The Extra Deck is generally flexible, encouraging players to include a variety of Rank 4 Xyz cards that suit their playstyle, with "Lavalval Chain" and "Number 101" being indispensable <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:12:03">[00:12:03]</a>. "Dark Rebellion Xyz Dragon" is also a recommended addition once it becomes more accessible <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:11:47">[00:11:47]</a>.
