The 2015 Blue-Eyes White Dragon deck profile features a variety of crucial Spell and Trap Cards designed to support the deck's primary strategy of summoning and protecting [[notable_cards_and_their_effects | Blue-Eyes White Dragon]] and enabling powerful plays <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:06">[00:00:06]</a>.

## Spell Cards

Key Spell Cards in the deck focus on milling, drawing, and special summoning to quickly establish a dominant board presence.

*   **Dragon Shrine** (x2) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:39">[00:03:39]</a>
    *   This card is effective for milling <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:42">[00:03:42]</a>.
    *   It can potentially send two [[notable_cards_and_their_effects | Blue-Eyes White Dragon]]s to the graveyard <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:44">[00:03:44]</a>.
    *   Dragon Shrine can also mill a White Stone of Legend to add a [[notable_cards_and_their_effects | Blue-Eyes White Dragon]] to your hand <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:49">[00:03:49]</a>.
    *   Running two copies is recommended <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:54">[00:03:54]</a>.
*   **One for One** (x2) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:57">[00:03:57]</a>
    *   A great card that serves as an alternative method to summon Maiden with Eyes of Blue without relying on Shining Angel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:03">[00:04:03]</a>.
    *   At least one copy should be included <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:09">[00:04:09]</a>.
*   **Swords of Revealing Light** (x1) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:13">[00:04:13]</a>
    *   Useful for desperate situations <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:17">[00:04:17]</a>.
*   **Pot of Duality** (x1) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:19">[00:04:19]</a>
    *   Helps to draw better cards during critical moments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:20">[00:04:20]</a>.
*   **Trade-In** (x1) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:24">[00:04:24]</a>
    *   A powerful drawing card that allows you to discard a Level 8 monster to draw two cards <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:39">[00:04:39]</a>.
*   **Cards of Consonance** (x1) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:27">[00:04:27]</a>
    *   Its effect allows you to discard a Dragon-type Tuner monster with 1000 or less attack to draw two cards <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:27">[00:04:27]</a>.
    *   It functions as an alternative to Pot of Duality <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:33">[00:04:33]</a>.
*   **Ancient Rules** (x1) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:43">[00:04:43]</a>
    *   Though not widely used, this card is effective <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:46">[00:04:46]</a>.
    *   Its effect allows for the special summon of a Level 5 or higher Normal Monster from your hand <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:48">[00:04:48]</a>, which can lead to an easy first-turn [[notable_cards_and_their_effects | Blue-Eyes White Dragon]] summon <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:52">[00:04:52]</a>.
*   **Burst Stream of Destruction** (x1) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:00">[00:05:00]</a>
    *   Used as an alternative to Raigeki <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:03">[00:05:03]</a>.
    *   Good for clearing the opponent's monsters from the field <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:11">[00:05:11]</a>.
*   **Monster Reborn** (x1) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:14">[00:05:14]</a>
    *   A great card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:14">[00:05:14]</a>.
*   **Mystical Space Typhoon (MST)** (x1) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:22">[00:05:22]</a>
    *   Considered an essential card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:22">[00:05:22]</a>.
*   **Silver's Cry** (x3) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:26">[00:05:26]</a>
    *   An alternative to Monster Reborn or Call of the Haunted for official tournaments <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:28">[00:05:28]</a>.
    *   Definitely run these cards to get your [[notable_cards_and_their_effects | Blue-Eyes White Dragon]]s onto the field from the graveyard <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:33">[00:05:33]</a>.
