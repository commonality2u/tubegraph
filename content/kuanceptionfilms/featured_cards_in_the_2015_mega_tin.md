## Promo Cards
Each [[2015 Dark Rebellion Xyz Dragon Mega Tin opening | 2015 Dark Rebellion Xyz Dragon Mega Tin]] includes a promo pack containing super rare cards <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:47">[00:00:47]</a>. The specific promo cards featured are:

*   **Dark Rebellion Xyz Dragon** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:51">[00:00:51]</a>: This card is prominently featured on the tin's artwork <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:32">[00:01:32]</a> and appears in a platinum rare reprint <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:45">[00:01:45]</a>.
*   **Pictol Entity Hastur** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:01">[00:02:01]</a>
*   **Castel the Skyblaster Musketeer** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:05">[00:02:05]</a>
