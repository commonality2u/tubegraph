The [[Yu Gi Oh Traptrix deck strategy | Traptrix deck]] for December 2015 emphasizes building a very strong back row defense using [[Trap Card Strategies in YuGiOh | trap cards]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:36">[00:00:36]</a>. This defensive focus is a core component of the deck's overall strategy, allowing it to control the opponent's plays while setting up its own board.

### Key Monsters Supporting Defense

Several [[Key monster cards in Traptrix deck 2015 | Traptrix monsters]] are crucial for establishing and maintaining the deck's defensive back row:

*   **Traptrix Myrmeleo**
    *   This card is run in three copies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>.
    *   It can be used to search for "Bottomless Trap Hole" or any other [[Trap Card Strategies in YuGiOh | "Trap Hole" card]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:51">[00:00:51]</a>.
    *   When special summoned from the graveyard, it can destroy any spell or [[Trap Card Strategies in YuGiOh | trap card]] on the field, acting like a "Mystical Space Typhoon" (MST) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>. This provides valuable back-row removal.
*   **Traptrix Dionaea**
    *   Run in three copies <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:02">[00:01:02]</a>.
    *   Can special summon any [[Key monster cards in Traptrix deck 2015 | Traptrix monster]] from the graveyard <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:05">[00:01:05]</a>.
    *   When special summoned from the graveyard, it allows the player to reuse their [[Trap Card Strategies in YuGiOh | "Trap Hole" cards]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:09">[00:01:09]</a>. This recycling ability is vital for sustained defensive pressure.

### Essential [[Important Spell and Trap Cards | Trap Cards]] for Defense

The deck incorporates a variety of [[Important Spell and Trap Cards | trap cards]] to disrupt opponent's plays and protect the field:

*   **Solemn Warning**: A very standard card in the format, used for negating summons and effects <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:44">[00:02:44]</a>.
*   **Bottomless Trap Hole**: One copy is included <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:49">[00:02:49]</a>.
*   **Traptrix Trap Hole Nightmare**: Two copies are run for consistency <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:52">[00:02:52]</a>. This card negates monster effects and acts similarly to "Bottomless Trap Hole" by banishing special summoned monsters that use their effects <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:54">[00:02:54]</a>.
*   **Compulsory Evacuation Device**: A "very sexy" and standard card used to return cards to the opponent's hand <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:16">[00:03:16]</a>.
*   **Mirror Force**: Included for anti-attacking measures, destroying opponent's monsters when they attack <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:19">[00:03:19]</a>.
*   **Dimensional Prison**: Another anti-attacking card used to banish opponent's attacking monsters <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:21">[00:03:21]</a>.
*   **Vanity's Emptiness**: A very standard card in the format that prevents opponents from special summoning, effectively stopping them from playing Yu-Gi-Oh and considered "pretty cheap" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:33">[00:03:33]</a>.
*   **Call of the Haunted**: A very versatile card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:04">[00:04:04]</a>. It can special summon "Traptrix Myrmeleo" to destroy a spell/trap, or special summon "Traptrix Dionaea" to reuse a [[Trap Card Strategies in YuGiOh | "Trap Hole" card]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:46">[00:03:46]</a>. This provides additional opportunities for [[Rank 4 and Rank 5 Xyz cards in Traptrix deck | Rank 4 plays]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:52">[00:03:52]</a>.
*   **Breakthrough Skill** (referred to as "fish chain"): Essential for negating monster effects <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:06">[00:04:06]</a>.

The deck's defensive strategy is bolstered by the synergy between its monsters and [[Trap Card Strategies in YuGiOh | trap cards]]:
*   [[Key monster cards in Traptrix deck 2015 | Traptrix Myrmeleo]] can search for [[Trap Card Strategies in YuGiOh | "Trap Hole" cards]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:51">[00:00:51]</a>, ensuring access to defensive measures.

*   "Call of the Haunted" can revive either [[Key monster cards in Traptrix deck 2015 | Myrmeleo]] for spell/trap removal or [[Key monster cards in Traptrix deck 2015 | Dionaea]] to bring back a [[Trap Card Strategies in YuGiOh | Trap Hole]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:46">[00:03:46]</a>, enhancing the deck's ability to constantly [[tricking_opponents_and_making_plays | trick opponents]] and react.

This combination of searching, recycling, and a robust lineup of disruptive [[Important Spell and Trap Cards | trap cards]] ensures the [[Yu Gi Oh Traptrix deck strategy | Traptrix deck]] maintains a formidable back row defense throughout the duel.
