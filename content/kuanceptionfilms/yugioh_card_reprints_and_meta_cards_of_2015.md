## 2015 Dark Rebellion XYZ Mega Tin Contents

Each 2015 Dark Rebellion XYZ Mega Tin includes:
*   A promo pack containing super rare cards <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:45">[00:00:45]</a>.
*   The Dark Rebellion XYZ Dragon <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:51">[00:00:51]</a>.
*   Three 2015 Mega Packs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:53">[00:00:53]</a>.

The Mega Packs are characterized as [[impact_of_reprints_on_yugioh_card_market | reprints]] of all sets released throughout 2015 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>. These [[reprints_of_classic_yugioh_cards | reprints]] include cards from meta-defining archetypes like Shaddoll ("Stradella cards") and Qliphort ("CLE for cars") <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:01">[00:01:01]</a>.

## Notable Card Reprints and Meta Cards

Among the highly desired cards from the 2015 Mega Tins were:
