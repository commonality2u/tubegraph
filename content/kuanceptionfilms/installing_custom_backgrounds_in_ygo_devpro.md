This guide details how to install custom backgrounds and wallpapers within [[tutorial_on_adding_custom_sleeves_in_ygo_devpro | Yu-Gi-Oh DevPro]], allowing for a personalized gameplay experience <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:15">[00:00:15]</a>. The process involves adding images and installing them within the game's customization settings.

## Navigating to Customization Settings
To begin customizing your DevPro visuals, navigate to the "game list" within [[tutorial_on_adding_custom_sleeves_in_ygo_devpro | DevPro]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:23">[00:00:23]</a>. From there, select the "customize" tab <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:25">[00:00:25]</a> and then click on "content" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>.

## Adding Custom Wallpapers/Backgrounds (Menu)
To change the main menu background:
1.  Go to the "backgrounds" section within the customization menu <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:49:00">[01:49:00]</a>.
2.  Click "ADD" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:53:00">[01:53:00]</a>.
3.  Select the desired image file from your computer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:56:00">[01:56:00]</a>.
4.  If prompted, confirm to [[how_to_resize_images_for_custom_sleeves_and_backgrounds | resize the image]] to the correct size <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:05:00">[02:05:00]</a>.
5.  Right-click on your newly added image and select "install success" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:12:00">[02:12:00]</a>.
6.  To [[previewing_and_formatting_custom_sleeves_and_backgrounds | preview]] the applied background, click "preview" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:16:00">[02:16:00]</a>.

> [!INFO] Note on Resizing
> It is important to have a correctly sized image, or [[how_to_resize_images_for_custom_sleeves_and_backgrounds | it may appear distorted]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:48">[00:00:48]</a>. Yu-Gi-Oh DevPro will typically prompt you to resize the image to the correct dimensions <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:15:00">[01:15:00]</a>.

## Adding Custom In-Game Backgrounds (Duel)
To change the background that appears during a duel:
1.  Within the customization menu, go to "game backgrounds" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:36:00">[02:36:00]</a>.
2.  Click "add" to select your desired wallpaper <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:38:00">[02:38:00]</a>.
3.  Right-click on the selected image and choose "install success" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:57:00">[02:57:00]</a>.
4.  You can [[previewing_and_formatting_custom_sleeves_and_backgrounds | preview]] the in-game background to ensure it is correctly installed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:02:00">[03:02:00]</a>.

## Verification
To confirm that both your [[tips_for_personalizing_yugioh_gameplay_experience_with_visuals | custom backgrounds]] and [[tutorial_on_adding_custom_sleeves_in_ygo_devpro | custom sleeves]] are correctly formatted and applied, enter an actual duel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:36:00">[03:36:00]</a>. The changes should be visible during gameplay <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:46:00">[03:46:00]</a>.
