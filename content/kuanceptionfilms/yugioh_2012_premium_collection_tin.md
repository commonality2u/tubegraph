The YuGiOh 2012 Premium Collection Tin is a highly sought-after Yu-Gi-Oh! product, containing 14 foil cards in each tin <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:02">[00:00:02]</a>. As of 2015, it was considered a rare find, having been released more than three years prior <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:11">[00:00:11]</a>.

## Contents

Each tin includes:
*   A Premium Collection Beginner's Guide <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:59">[00:00:59]</a>.
*   Fourteen foil cards <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:05">[00:01:05]</a>.

## Notable Cards and Pulls

Certain cards are guaranteed within every tin, while others are rare pulls:

*   **Elemental Hero The Shining** <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:14">[00:01:14]</a>
