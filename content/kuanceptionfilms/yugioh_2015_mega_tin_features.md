The 2015 Dark Rebellion XYZ Dragon Mega Tin was a highly anticipated product of 2015 for the Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game (TCG) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:14">[00:00:14]</a>. The artwork on the Mega Tin itself was described as "fantastic" and "marvelous" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:32">[00:01:32]</a>.

## Contents

Each 2015 Dark Rebellion XYZ Dragon Mega Tin typically includes:
*   A promo pack containing super rare cards and the featured [[Dark Rebellion XYZ Dragon]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:45">[00:00:45]</a>.
*   Three [[2015_mega_tin_pull_results | 2015 Mega Packs]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:53">[00:00:53]</a>.

## Features

### Promo Cards
The promo pack accompanying the tin includes:
*   [[Dark Rebellion XYZ Dragon]] (in a platinum rare [[yugioh_card_reprints | reprint]]) <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:42">[00:01:42]</a>
*   Ptolemy Abyss <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:55">[00:01:55]</a>
*   Elder Entity Hastorr <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:01">[00:02:01]</a>
*   Castel, the Skyblaster Musketeer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:05">[00:02:05]</a>
