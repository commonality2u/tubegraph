This tutorial outlines the process for adding custom card sleeves in Yu-Gi-Oh! DevPro, allowing players to personalize their gameplay experience with unique visuals <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:15">[00:00:15]</a>.

## Accessing Customization Settings

To begin customizing, navigate to the DevPro game list and select the "Customize" tab <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:23">[00:00:23]</a>. Within this tab, locate and click on the "Content" section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>.

## Adding Custom Sleeves

To customize your sleeves:

1.  **Select Sleeve Option**: Go to "Player One Covers" within the Content section <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:34">[00:00:34]</a>.
2.  **Prepare Image**: Ensure you have an image ready that you wish to use as your sleeve <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:46">[00:00:46]</a>. While it's beneficial to have a [[how_to_resize_images_for_custom_sleeves_and_backgrounds | correctly sized image]] to avoid distortion, the program will prompt you to resize it to the correct dimensions if needed <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:48">[00:00:48]</a><a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:15">[00:01:15]</a>.
3.  **Add Image**: Click the "ADD" button and browse to find the desired picture on your computer <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:53">[00:00:53]</a><a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>.
4.  **Confirm Resize**: If prompted to resize the image, confirm by pressing "Yes" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:15">[00:01:15]</a>.
5.  **Install Sleeve**: Once the image is added, click on your newly added sleeve, right-click, and select "Install" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:24">[00:01:24]</a>. A "success" prompt will appear <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:28">[00:01:28]</a>.
6.  **Preview**: To see your new sleeves in action, click "Preview" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:34">[00:01:34]</a>. The preview will show your customized sleeves <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:37">[00:01:37]</a>.

## Verifying in Duel

To ensure your custom sleeves are correctly applied in a duel, start an actual duel <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:36">[00:03:36]</a>. You will observe that your custom sleeves are correctly formatted and visible during gameplay <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:46">[00:03:46]</a>.
