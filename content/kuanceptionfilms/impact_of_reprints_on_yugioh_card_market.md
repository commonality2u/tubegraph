### Effect on Market Value

The practice of Konami reprinting cards, especially highly sought-after ones, has a direct impact on the [[yugioh_card_market_value | market value]] of those cards <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:39:09">[01:39:09]</a>. While this can be viewed negatively by those holding the original, more expensive versions, it makes the cards more accessible to a wider player base. This phenomenon is often described as "killing the market value" of the existing printings <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:39:09">[01:39:09]</a>.

Despite the potential for [[yugioh_card_market_value | market value]] depreciation, the overall sentiment towards the [[reprints_of_classic_yugioh_cards | reprints]] in [[reprints_in_premium_gold_infinite_gold | Premium Gold Infinite Gold]] was positive, with many viewing them as "sexy" and beneficial for deck building <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:52:00">[05:52:00]</a>.
