## Legendary Collection 3: Yugi's World Game Board Edition

The *Legendary Collection 3: Yugi's World Game Board Edition* comes with a game board, a promo pack, and five mega packs <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:09">[00:01:09]</a>. The promo pack contains three [[card_types_and_rarities_like_secret_rare_and_promo_cards | ultra rare cards]] and tokens <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:07">[00:02:07]</a>. The mega packs include "really great reprints" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:23">[00:00:23]</a>, featuring [[card_types_and_rarities_like_secret_rare_and_promo_cards | classic normal monsters]] like [[card_types_and_rarities_like_secret_rare_and_promo_cards | Dark Magician]] and [[card_types_and_rarities_like_secret_rare_and_promo_cards | Blue-Eyes White Dragon]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:29">[00:00:29]</a>.
