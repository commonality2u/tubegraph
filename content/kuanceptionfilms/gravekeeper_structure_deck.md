## Packaging and Contents

The packaging for the Gravekeeper structure deck is noted as misleading <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:25">[00:00:25]</a>. While it suggests the inclusion of 25 cards, 2 boosters, and 5 foil cards, the deck actually contains 40 cards <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:27">[00:00:27]</a>.

A typical value cube containing the Gravekeeper structure deck also includes:
*   A styrofoam pack <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:53">[00:00:53]</a>
*   A promo pack <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:56">[00:00:56]</a>
*   Booster packs, such as "Hidden Arsenal 4" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:05">[00:01:05]</a> and "Return of the Duelist" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:41">[00:01:41]</a>
