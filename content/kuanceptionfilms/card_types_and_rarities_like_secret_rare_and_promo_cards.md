This article details the various card types and rarities encountered when opening the 2015 Odd-Eyes Pendulum Dragon Megatin, including [[Booster pack and promo cards | promo cards]], [[Ultra Rare and Secret Rare Cards | secret rare]], and [[Rare and Super Rare Card Pulls | super rare]] cards.

## 2015 Odd-Eyes Pendulum Dragon Megatin Contents

The 2015 Odd-Eyes Pendulum Dragon Megatin contains a promo pack with two [[Rare and Super Rare Card Pulls | Super Rare]] cards and a Platinum [[Ultra Rare and Secret Rare Cards | Secret Rare]] Odd-Eyes Pendulum Dragon card itself <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:12:00">[01:12:00]</a>. The Odd-Eyes Pendulum Dragon card is described as "real beautiful" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:45:00">[02:45:00]</a>. The tin also includes a reprint of Majesty's Fiend <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:57:00">[02:57:00]</a> and Galaxy Tomahawk <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:42:00">[03:42:00]</a>.

## Notable Card Pulls and Rarities

During the opening, several cards of different rarities were pulled:
*   A [[Ultra Rare and Secret Rare Cards | Secret Rare]] Jewel Thief was pulled <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:31:00">[02:31:00]</a>.
*   An Elder Entity Norden, described as a "really cool level 4 card," was pulled <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:51:00">[02:51:00]</a>.
*   [[Commentary on the rarity and playability of cards | El Shaddoll Winda]] was pulled, which was also obtained in a previous video <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="03:37:00">[03:37:00]</a>.
*   A "revealing light" card was pulled, which the opener had never pulled before <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="04:22:00">[04:22:00]</a>.
*   A second Dante card was pulled, following one from a previous 2015 Megatin opening <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:12:00">[05:12:00]</a>. This was considered "insane" luck <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="06:18:00">[06:18:00]</a>.
*   A Magical Spring [[Ultra Rare and Secret Rare Cards | Secret Rare]] card was pulled <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:48:00">[05:48:00]</a>.
*   A Number 39: Utopia Beyond and Utopia Prime were also pulled <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="05:52:00">[05:52:00]</a>.
