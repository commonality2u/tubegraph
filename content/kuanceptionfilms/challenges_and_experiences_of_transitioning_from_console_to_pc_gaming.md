## Initial Impressions and Performance
Upon its release, *Call of Duty: Black Ops 3* garnered significant attention, with many players enjoying the game <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:16">[00:00:16]</a>. While the PlayStation 4 version was noted to be running very well and was fun to play <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:38">[00:00:38]</a>, the PC version presented immediate [[gaming_performance_and_technical_challenges | challenges]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:22">[00:00:22]</a>.

### PC Version Specifics
The PC version of *Black Ops 3* exhibited a noticeable [[challenges_faced_during_gameplay | learning curve]] and optimization issues at launch <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:22">[00:00:22]</a>. The player expressed hope that these issues would be resolved in the weeks following release <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:31">[00:00:31]</a>. Despite the technical problems, the player had high hopes for the game's future <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:34">[00:00:34]</a>.

> [!NOTE] Release Platforms
> *Call of Duty: Black Ops 3* was released for Xbox One, PlayStation 4, PC, and last-generation consoles <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:45">[00:00:45]</a>. However, the player did not recommend purchasing the game on last-gen consoles (Xbox 360 and PlayStation 3) due to poor investment value <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:50">[00:00:50]</a>.

## Transitioning from Console to PC
The player, originally a PlayStation 3 enthusiast, acquired the PC version of *Black Ops 3* because they did not yet own a PlayStation 4 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:59">[00:00:59]</a>. This transition led to [[experiences_with_gameplay_on_pc | struggles]] on the PC platform <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:09">[00:01:09]</a>.

### Gameplay and Competitive Landscape
The PC version of *Black Ops 3* was observed to have a highly competitive demographic <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:16">[00:01:16]</a>. During gameplay, the player experienced significant [[gaming_performance_and_technical_challenges | frame drops]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:08">[00:02:08]</a>, which were described as "terrible" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:42">[00:04:42]</a>. Despite these technical issues, the player managed to secure kills <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:44">[00:04:44]</a>.

The experience highlights the typical [[call_of_duty_console_vs_pc_gameplay | differences between console and PC gameplay]], particularly concerning performance and the competitive environment. The player notes a need to improve their skills <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:52">[01:52]</a>, suggesting a steeper [[challenges_faced_during_gameplay | learning curve]] for those accustomed to console controls.
