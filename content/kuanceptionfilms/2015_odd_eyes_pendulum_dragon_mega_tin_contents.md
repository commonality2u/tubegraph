The 2015 Odd-Eyes Pendulum Dragon Mega Tin is a Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game product released in 2015 <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:30">[00:00:30]</a>. It is part of the [[yugioh_tcg_products_of_2015 | 2015 YuGiOh TCG products]] lineup.

### Tin Contents

The [[2015_odd_eyes_pendulum_dragon_mega_tin_contents | Mega Tin]] includes:
*   One promo pack <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:12">[00:01:12]</a>.
*   Two Super Rares <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:15">[00:01:15]</a>.
*   One Platinum Secret Rare [[2015_odd_eyes_pendulum_dragon_mega_tin_contents | Odd-Eyes Pendulum Dragon]] card <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:01:17">[00:01:17]</a>.

The tin itself features the [[2015_odd_eyes_pendulum_dragon_mega_tin_contents | Odd-Eyes Pendulum Dragon]] artwork <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:14">[00:02:14]</a>.
