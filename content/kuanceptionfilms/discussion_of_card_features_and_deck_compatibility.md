This article details observations and opinions on various cards pulled from a Yu-Gi-Oh! Star Pack 2013 booster box, focusing on their features, effects, and potential [[yugioh_deck_building_tips | deck compatibility]].

## Noteworthy Cards and Their Features

Many cards received specific commentary regarding their utility, rarity, or general appeal.

### Elemental Hero Escuridao
Elemental Hero Escuridao is highlighted as a foil card with a "really nice effect" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:34">[00:02:34]</a>. A second copy of this card was also pulled <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:06:24">[00:06:24]</a>.

### Goblin Burg
Goblin Burg is considered "pretty useful" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:08">[00:02:08]</a>.
*   **Deck Compatibility:** It is noted as working "pretty well with Mass Heroes" and being useful for [[yugioh_card_game_strategy_insights | Xyz summoning level four]] (rank four Xyz) monsters <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:58">[00:05:58]</a>. The presenter intends to include it in their [[yugioh_deck_building_tips | Elemental Hero deck]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:02:11">[00:02:11]</a>.

### Gagaga Magician
Gagaga Magician was a card the opener was "looking for" and is described as a "really nice card" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:16">[00:03:16]</a>.

### One-for-One
The spell card One-for-One is described as a "really nice card" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:31">[00:03:31]</a> and later reiterated as a "really good card" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:31">[00:07:31]</a>.

### Barbaroid the Ultimate Battle Machine

### Seven Swords Warrior
Seven Swords Warrior is described as "actually pretty cool" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:04:28">[00:04:28]</a>.

### Number 10: Illumiknight
This card was specifically desired by the presenter <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:19">[00:05:19]</a>.

### Galaxy-Eyes Photon Dragon
Pulled as a foil card, Galaxy-Eyes Photon Dragon is deemed "sweet" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:05:21">[00:05:21]</a> and "cool because [it's] spoiler and that's shiny" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:41">[00:07:41]</a>.

### Number 17: Leviathan Dragon
This Xyz monster is described as a "pretty cool card" <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:07:04">[00:07:04]</a>.

### Gandora the Dragon of Destruction
