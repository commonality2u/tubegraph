## Key Features

### Rails and Minecarts
A major change in the 0.8.0 update is the inclusion of rails and minecarts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:39">[00:00:39]</a>. This feature allows players to build transportation systems within their worlds, enabling faster movement across distances <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.

### Powered Rails
In addition to standard rails, the update also brings powered rails, which are essential for maintaining momentum and speed for minecarts <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:00:41">[00:00:41]</a>.

### In-Minecart View
Players can experience riding inside a minecart, offering a unique perspective of the game world as they travel along the tracks <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="00:03:25">[00:03:25]</a>.
