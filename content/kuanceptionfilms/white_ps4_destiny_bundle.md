## Bundle Contents and Release

This bundle marked the introduction of the first-ever white [[playstation_4_excitement | PlayStation 4]] <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:30:03">[01:30:03]</a>. It was made available when the game *Destiny* released on September 9th <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:46:03">[01:46:03]</a>.

The bundle includes:
*   A 500 GB glacier white [[playstation_4_excitement | PS4]] console <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:50:03">[01:50:03]</a>
*   A white DualShock 4 controller <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:54:03">[01:54:03]</a>
*   A 30-day voucher for PlayStation Plus <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="01:56:03">[01:56:03]</a>

The creation of *Destiny* itself, and its presentation on [[playstation_4_excitement | PS4]], was praised, with thanks extended to Bungie and Activision <a class="yt-timestamp" data-t="02:01:03">[02:01:03]</a>.
